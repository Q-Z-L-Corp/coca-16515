epitome 
英[ɪˈpɪtəmi] 美[ɪˈpɪtəmi]
 
n.缩影；典型的人或事物；（书、演讲等的）摘要；
 "

N-SING
典型;缩影
If you say that a person or thing is the epitome of something, you are emphasizing that they are the best possible example of a particular type of person or thing.
例：Maureen was the epitome of sophistication...莫琳是个典型的老油条。
例：The Dorchester Hotel is the epitome of luxury in the heart of London.多切斯特酒店是伦敦市中心豪华奢侈的缩影。

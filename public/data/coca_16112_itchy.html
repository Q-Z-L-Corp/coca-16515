itchy 
英[ˈɪtʃi] 美[ˈɪtʃi]
 
adj.（使）发痒的；渴望旅行（或搬家、换工作等）；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
发痒的;令人感到瘙痒的
If a part of your body or something you are wearing is itchy, you have an unpleasant feeling on your skin that makes you want to scratch.
例：...itchy, sore eyes...又痒又痛的眼睛
例：Wigs are itchy and uncomfortable.假发让人感觉发痒,很不舒服。

PHRASE
漫游的渴望;旅行的渴望
If you have itchy feet, you have a strong desire to leave a place and to travel.
例：The trip gave me itchy feet and I wanted to travel more.这次旅行让我渴望出游,想去更多的地方。

rounded 
英[ˈraʊndɪd] 美[ˈraʊndɪd]
 
adj.圆形的；丰满的；全面的；[语]圆唇的；vi.&vt.round的过去式和过去分词；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
圆形的;圆润的
Something that is rounded is curved in shape, without any points or sharp edges.
例：...a low rounded hill...低矮的圆形小山
例：The barge had a rounded bow and stern.这艘驳船的两端都是圆形的。

ADJ-GRADED
全面发展的;多方面的;完善的
You describe something or someone as rounded or well-rounded when you are expressing approval of them because they have a personality which is fully developed in all aspects.
例：...his carefully organised narrative, full of rounded, believable and interesting characters.他的叙述结构严谨，不乏形象丰满、真实有趣的人物
例：...a well-rounded, well-educated and highly intelligent man...全面发展、受过良好教育的聪明过人的男子

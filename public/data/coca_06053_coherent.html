coherent 
英[kəʊˈhɪərənt] 美[koʊˈhɪrənt]
 
adj.一致的；连贯的；条理分明的；清楚明白的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
一致的;连贯的
If something is coherent, it is well planned, so that it is clear and sensible and all its parts go well with each other.
例：He has failed to work out a coherent strategy for modernising the service...他未能制定出一条连贯的策略来实现服务的现代化。
例：The President's policy is perfectly coherent.总统的政策环环相扣，浑然一体。

ADJ-GRADED
清楚明白的;有条理的
If someone is coherent, they express their thoughts in a clear and calm way, so that other people can understand what they are saying.
例：He's so calm when he answers questions in interviews. I wish I could be that coherent.他在面试中回答问题时非常从容镇静。我希望自己也能那样条理清晰。

rhythm 
英[ˈrɪðəm] 美[ˈrɪðəm]
 
n.[诗]节奏，韵律；[医]节律，规律；[乐]节拍；[艺]调和；
 "

N-VAR
(声音或运动的)节奏，韵律，节拍
A rhythm is a regular series of sounds or movements.
例：His music of that period fused the rhythms of Jazz with classical forms...他那个时期的音乐将爵士乐的韵律和古典音乐的形式融合在了一起。
例：He had no sense of rhythm whatsoever...他没有任何节奏感。

N-COUNT
(身体、季节、潮汐等的)规律性变化，节奏
A rhythm is a regular pattern of changes, for example changes in your body, in the seasons, or in the tides.
例：Begin to listen to your own body rhythms.开始聆听你自己身体的节奏。
例：...the seasonal rhythm of the agricultural year.一年中农业的季节性

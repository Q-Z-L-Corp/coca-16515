hysteria 
英[hɪˈstɪəriə] 美[hɪˈstɪriə]
 
n.[医]癔病；（特指女人的）歇斯底里；病态兴奋；脏躁；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（一群人的）情绪失控，狂野情绪的爆发
Hysteria among a group of people is a state of uncontrolled excitement, anger, or panic.
例：No one could help getting carried away by the hysteria...所有人都不由自主地受到狂热情绪的感染。
例：Several were hurt in the panic. 'It was mass hysteria,' said Rev John Borders.数人在恐慌中受了伤。“人们都疯了，”约翰·博德斯牧师说。

N-UNCOUNT
癔病;歇斯底里
A person who is suffering from hysteria is in a state of violent and disturbed emotion as a result of shock.
例：By now, she was screaming, completely overcome with hysteria.现在她正在尖叫，完全处于癔病发作状态。

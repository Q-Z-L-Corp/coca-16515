rethink 
英[ˌri:ˈθɪŋk] 美[riˈθɪŋk]
 
vt.& vi.重新考虑或再想；n.重新考虑，反思，新想法；
 "

VERB
重新考虑;反思
If you rethink something such as a problem, a plan, or a policy, you think about it again and change it.
例：Both major political parties are having to rethink their policies...两个主要政党都必须反思其政策。
例：I think all of us need to rethink our attitudes toward health and sickness.我想我们都需要反思自己对待健康和疾病的态度。

N-SING
重新考虑;反思
If you have a rethink of a problem, a plan, or a policy, you think about it again and change it.
例：There must be a rethink of government policy towards this vulnerable group.必须重新考虑政府对待这一弱势群体的政策。

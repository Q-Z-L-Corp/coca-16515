eccentricity 
英[ˌeksenˈtrɪsəti] 美[ˌɛksɛnˈtrɪsɪti]
 
n.古怪；反常，怪癖；[物]偏心距；[数]离心率；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
古怪
Eccentricity is unusual behaviour that other people consider strange.
例：He was known as Mad Shelley partly because of his eccentricity and partly because of his violent temper...大家都叫他“疯子谢利”，部分因为他的古怪，部分因为他火爆的脾气。
例：She is unusual to the point of eccentricity.她特立独行到了古怪的地步。

N-COUNT
古怪行为(或想法);怪癖
Eccentricities are ways of behaving that people think are strange, or habits or opinions that are different from those of most people.
例：We all have our eccentricities.我们每个人都有自己的怪癖。
例：...the eccentricities of British life.英国人生活中的种种怪癖

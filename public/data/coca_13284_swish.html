swish 
英[swɪʃ] 美[swɪʃ]
 
vt.& vi.鞭打；（使）发飒飒声；adj.时髦的；漂亮的；n.嗖嗖声；漂亮；搞同性恋的男子；
 "

V-ERG
(使)刷地(或嗖地、呼地)快速移动
If something swishes or if you swish it, it moves quickly through the air, making a soft sound.
例：A car swished by heading for the coast...一辆车嗖地驶过朝海岸开去。
例：He swished his cape around his shoulders...他刷地一下披上斗篷。

ADJ-GRADED
好看的;时髦的;高档的
If you describe something as swish, you mean that it is smart and fashionable.
例：...a swish cocktail bar.时髦的鸡尾酒酒吧

ADJ
作为症状的;作为征候的;表明…的
If something is symptomatic of something else, especially something bad, it is a sign of it.
例：The city's problems are symptomatic of the crisis that is spreading throughout the country.该市的问题是正在全国蔓延的危机的征兆显现。

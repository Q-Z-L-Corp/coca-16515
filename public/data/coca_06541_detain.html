detain 
英[dɪˈteɪn] 美[dɪˈten]
 
vt.留住，阻住；耽搁；拘留，扣留；
 "

VERB
拘留;扣押
When people such as the police detain someone, they keep them in a place under their control.
例：The act allows police to detain a suspect for up to 48 hours...该法令允许警方将嫌疑犯扣押最多48小时。
例：He was arrested and detained for questioning.他遭到逮捕、拘留以接受问讯。

VERB
阻留;耽搁
To detain someone means to delay them, for example by talking to them.
例：Thank you. We won't detain you any further.非常感谢。我们就不再耽搁您的时间了。

英[ˌju:nɪ'fɔ:mətɪ] 美[ˌjunɪ'fɔmətɪ]
 
n.均匀性；单调，无变化；同样，一样；

N-UNCOUNT
统一（性);一致（性）；规则（性）
If there is uniformity in something such as a system, organization, or group of countries, the same rules, ideas, or methods are applied in all parts of it.
例：Spanish liberals sought to create linguistic as well as administrative uniformity.西班牙自由主义者寻求语言及行政上的统一。
例：...the strength of the ideology is seen in the remarkable uniformity of attitudes and beliefs.从态度和信仰上的高度一致可以看出意识形态的力量

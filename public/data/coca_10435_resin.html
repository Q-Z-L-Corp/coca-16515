树脂
Resin is a sticky substance that is produced by some trees.
例：The resin from which the oil is extracted comes from a small, tough tree.用于提取这种油的树脂取自于一种矮小坚韧的树。

例：Two of the lorries, carrying industrial resin and paint thinner, burst into flames.其中运载工业树脂和涂料稀释剂的两辆卡车起火了。

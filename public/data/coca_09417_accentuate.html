VERB
强调;加重;使更明显
To accentuate something means to emphasize it or make it more noticeable.
例：His shaven head accentuates his large round face...光头使他的大圆脸更加突出。
例：The whole air of menace was accentuated by the fact that he was so cordial and soft-voiced.他如此亲切，说话轻声细语，这更加重了威胁的语气。

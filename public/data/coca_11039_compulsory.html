ADJ
强制性的;强迫的；义务的
If something is compulsory, you must do it or accept it, because it is the law or because someone in a position of authority says you must.
例：In East Germany learning Russian was compulsory...在东德，俄语是必修课。
例：Many young men are trying to get away from compulsory military conscription.很多年轻人试图逃避强制征兵的政策。

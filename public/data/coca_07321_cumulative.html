ADJ
聚积的;积累的;渐增的
If a series of events have a cumulative effect, each event makes the effect greater.
例：It is simple pleasures, such as a walk on a sunny day, which have a cumulative effect on our mood...一些简单的娱乐，譬如在一个阳光明媚的日子散步，就能使我们的心情更愉快。
例：The benefits from eating fish are cumulative.吃鱼的好处要长期才能显现。

implore 
英[ɪmˈplɔ:(r)] 美[ɪmˈplɔr, -ˈplor]
 
vt.恳求或乞求（某人）；vi.恳求，乞求；
 "

VERB
恳求;乞求;哀求
If you implore someone to do something, you ask them to do it in a forceful, emotional way.
例：Opposition leaders this week implored the president to break the deadlock...反对党领袖本周恳求总统打破僵局。
例：'Tell me what to do!' she implored him.她哀求他说：“求你告诉我该怎么办！”

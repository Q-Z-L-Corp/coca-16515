cynical 
英[ˈsɪnɪkl] 美[ˈsɪnɪkəl]
 
adj.怀疑的；愤世嫉俗的；冷嘲的；见利忘义的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
认为世人皆自私的;愤世嫉俗的
If you describe someone as cynical, you mean they believe that people always act selfishly.
例：...his cynical view of the world.他那愤世嫉俗的世界观

ADJ-GRADED
(对成功或人的真善)怀疑的，悲观的
If you are cynical about something, you do not believe that it can be successful or that the people involved are honest.
例：It's hard not to be cynical about reform...很难相信改革会成功。
例：It has also made me more cynical about relationships.这也使得我愈加不相信爱情。

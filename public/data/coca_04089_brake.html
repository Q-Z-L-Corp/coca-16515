brake 
英[breɪk] 美[brek]
 
n.制动器，闸；刹车；阻碍；vt.& vi.刹（车）；
 "

N-COUNT
制动器；刹车；车闸
Brakes are devices in a vehicle that make it go slower or stop.
例：The brakes began locking...制动器开始锁定。
例：A seagull swooped down in front of her car, causing her to slam on the brakes.一只海鸥突然扑到她的车前，她急忙猛踩刹车。

VERB
刹车；用刹车使（车）减速；刹住（车）
When a vehicle or its driver brakes, or when a driver brakes a vehicle, the driver makes it slow down or stop by using the brakes.
例：He heard tires squeal as the car braked to avoid a collision...为避免相撞而刹车时，他听到了轮胎与地面刺耳的摩擦声。
例：She braked sharply to avoid another car...她为了躲另一辆车而紧急刹车。

N-COUNT
抑制因素；约束
You can use brake in a number of expressions to indicate that something has slowed down or stopped.
例：Illness had put a brake on his progress...疾病使他的进展放缓。
例：You can take the financial brakes off in June.6月份可以放开金融管制。

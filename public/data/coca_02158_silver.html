silver 
英[ˈsɪlvə(r)] 美[ˈsɪlvɚ]
 
n.银；银币；银制品；银色；adj.银制的；像银的；银色的；银白色的；vt.（在某物上）镀银；使具有银色光泽，使变成银色；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
银
Silver is a valuable pale grey metal that is used for making jewellery and ornaments.
例：...a hand-crafted brooch made from silver.一枚手工银制胸针
例：...amber earrings set in silver.镶琥珀的银耳环

N-UNCOUNT
银币
Silver consists of coins that are made from silver or that look like silver.
例：...the basement where £150,000 in silver was buried.埋有 150,000 英镑银币的地下室

N-UNCOUNT
银器(尤指餐具)
You can use silver to refer to all the things in a house that are made of silver, especially the cutlery and dishes.
例：He beat the rugs and polished the silver.他拍打去地毯上的灰尘，还擦拭了银器。

COLOUR
银色;银灰色;银白色
Silver is used to describe things that are shiny and pale grey in colour.
例：He had thick silver hair which needed cutting.他浓密的银发该理理了。
例：...a silver sports car...一辆银色跑车

N-VAR
同 silver medal
A silver is the same as a silver medal .
例：Britain went on to take bronze and then followed it up by winning silver in the World Cup.英国斩获了铜牌，接下来又赢得了世界杯的银牌。

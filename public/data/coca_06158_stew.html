stew 
英[stju:] 美[stu]
 
vt.& vi.炖，煨；闷热；担忧；n.炖煮的菜肴；烦恼，焦虑；
 "

N-VAR
炖煮的菜肴
A stew is a meal which you make by cooking meat and vegetables in liquid at a low temperature.
例：She served him a bowl of beef stew...她为他端上了一碗炖牛肉。
例：They made a stew.他们做了一道炖菜。

VERB
炖;煨;焖
When you stew meat, vegetables, or fruit, you cook them slowly in liquid in a closed dish.
例：Stew the apple and blackberries to make a thick pulp.将苹果和黑莓炖成稠浆。
例：...stewed prunes.炖李子

PHRASE
深感不安的;焦虑的;担心的
If you are in a stew, you feel very worried.
例：He's been in a stew since early this morning...他从今天一大早就焦虑不安。
例：Highly charged emotions have you in a stew.高度紧张的情绪会让人陷入不安。

PHRASE
(故意)让…独自烦恼(而不加劝慰)
If you let a person stew or if you leave them to stew, you deliberately allow them to worry about something for a while, rather than telling them something which would make them feel better.
例：I'd rather let him stew...我宁可让他一个人去烦恼。
例：Leave them to stew in their own juice.让他们自食其果吧。

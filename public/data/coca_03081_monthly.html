ADJ
每月的;每月一次的
A monthly event or publication happens or appears every month.
例：Many people are now having trouble making their monthly house payments...现在很多人在支付房款月供上都有困难。
例：Kidscape runs monthly workshops for teachers.基德斯克普负责组织每月一次的教师研讨班。

ADJ
每月的;按月的
Monthly quantities or rates relate to a period of one month.
例：The monthly rent for a two-bedroom flat would be £953.33...双卧室的公寓每月房租是953.33英镑。
例：Monthly interest costs vary.每个月的利息费用都不一样。

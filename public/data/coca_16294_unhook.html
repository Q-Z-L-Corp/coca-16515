VERB
解开（衣服）的钩扣
If you unhook a piece of clothing that is fastened with hooks, you undo the hooks.
例：She unhooked her dress.她解开裙子的钩扣。

VERB
把…从钩上取下
If you unhook something that is held in place by hooks, you open it or remove it by undoing the hooks.
例：Chris unhooked the shutters and went out on the balcony.克里斯拉开百叶窗，走到了阳台上。

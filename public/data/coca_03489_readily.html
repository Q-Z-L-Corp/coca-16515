readily 
英[ˈredɪli] 美[ˈrɛdəli, ˈrɛdli]
 
adv.乐意地；快捷地；轻而易举地；便利地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
乐意地；欣然地
If you do something readily, you do it in a way which shows that you are very willing to do it.
例：I asked her if she would allow me to interview her, and she readily agreed...我问她是否允许我采访她，她欣然同意了。
例：When I was invited to the party, I readily accepted.当被邀请参加聚会的时候，我一口答应了。

ADV-GRADED
迅速地；容易地；轻松地
You also use readily to say that something can be done or obtained quickly and easily. For example, if you say that something can be readily understood, you mean that people can understand it quickly and easily.
例：The components are readily available in hardware shops...这些零件很容易在五金商店买到。
例：I don't readily make friends.我不轻易交友。

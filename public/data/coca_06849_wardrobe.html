wardrobe 
英[ˈwɔ:drəʊb] 美[ˈwɔrdroʊb]
 
n.衣柜，衣橱；藏衣室；（个人，戏团的）全部服装；行头；
 "

N-COUNT
衣柜;衣橱
A wardrobe is a tall cupboard or cabinet in which you can hang your clothes.

N-COUNT
(某人的)全部服装
Someone's wardrobe is the total collection of clothes that they have.
例：Her wardrobe consists primarily of huge cashmere sweaters and tiny Italian sandals.她的衣物主要是宽大的开司米毛衣和小巧的意大利凉鞋。

N-UNCOUNT
(剧团演员的)全部戏装，行头
The wardrobe in a theatre company is the actors' and actresses' costumes.
例：In the wardrobe department were rows of costumes.戏装部有一排排的戏装。

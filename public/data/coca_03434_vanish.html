vanish 
英[ˈvænɪʃ] 美[ˈvænɪʃ]
 
vi.消失；突然不见；消亡，消灭；[数]化为零；vt.使消失，使不见；n.[语音学]消失音，弱化音；
 "

VERB
消失;失踪;突然不见
If someone or something vanishes, they disappear suddenly or in a way that cannot be explained.
例：He just vanished and was never seen again...他就这么消失了，再也没人见到过他。
例：The aircraft vanished without trace...飞机消失得无影无踪。

VERB
(物种）灭绝;（传统）消亡
If something such as a species of animal or a tradition vanishes, it stops existing.
例：Near the end of Devonian times, thirty percent of all animal life vanished...在泥盆纪末期，30％的动物物种都灭绝了。
例：...all the species of birds that had vanished from Iowa.艾奥瓦州所有消失了的鸟类物种

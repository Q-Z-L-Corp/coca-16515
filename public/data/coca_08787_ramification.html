N-COUNT
（决定、计划、事件产生的）后果，影响
The ramifications of a decision, plan, or event are all its consequences and effects, especially ones which are not obvious at first.
例：The book analyses the social and political ramifications of AIDS for the gay community.这本书分析了艾滋病给同性恋群体所带来的社会和政治影响。

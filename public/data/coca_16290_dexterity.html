N-UNCOUNT
(双手的)灵巧;(思维的)敏捷
Dexterity is skill in using your hands, or sometimes your mind.
例：...Reid's dexterity on the guitar.里德弹吉他的娴熟指法
例：...the wit and verbal dexterity of the script.剧本的风趣幽默和机敏的语言

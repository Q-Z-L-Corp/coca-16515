meal 
英[mi:l] 美[mil]
 
n.餐，饭；进餐（时间）；〈英〉一次挤奶量；谷物粗粉（用作饲料或加工面粉）；vi.进餐；
 "

N-COUNT
一餐;一顿饭
A meal is an occasion when people sit down and eat, usually at a regular time.
例：She sat next to him throughout the meal...用餐时她一直坐在他身旁。
例：It's rare that I have an evening meal with my children.我难得跟孩子们一起吃晚饭。

N-COUNT
一餐所吃的食物
A meal is the food you eat during a meal.
例：The waiter offered him red wine or white wine with his meal...侍者为他端上佐餐的红葡萄酒或者白葡萄酒。
例：Fresh fish makes a delicious meal.新鲜的鱼做菜非常美味。

N-UNCOUNT
（谷物的）粗磨粉
Meal is a rough powder made of crushed grain. It is used to make flour or animal food.

PHRASE
花费过多的时间或精力做…;对…小题大做
If you think someone is taking more time and energy to do something than is necessary, you can say that they are making a meal of it.
例：Lawyers always make such a meal of the simplest little thing.律师总是死抠细节。

PHRASE
健康丰盛的一餐
If you have a square meal, you have a large healthy meal.

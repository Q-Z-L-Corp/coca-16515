ADJ
(湖)淡水的;(生物)淡水中生长的
A freshwater lake contains water that is not salty, usually in contrast to the sea. Freshwater creatures live in water that is not salty.
例：...Lake Balaton, the largest freshwater lake in Europe...巴拉顿湖，欧洲最大的淡水湖
例：The perch is a freshwater fish.鲈鱼是淡水鱼。

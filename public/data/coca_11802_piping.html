piping 
英[ˈpaɪpɪŋ] 美[ˈpaɪpɪŋ]
 
n.（某种或某长度的）管道；笛声；（食品上装饰用的）奶油花饰；（衣服、椅罩等的）滚边；adj.尖声的；高声的；平静的；平和的；

N-UNCOUNT
（金属、塑料等的）管子，管材
Piping is metal, plastic, or another substance made in the shape of a pipe or tube.
例：...rolls of bright yellow plastic piping.成卷的明黄色塑料管

N-UNCOUNT
（衣服、垫子等的）滚边
Piping is cloth made into a narrow tube. Piping is used to decorate the edges of clothing and things such as cushions.
例：The red dress had slim black piping around the neck.这条红裙的领口有细细的黑色滚边。

ADJ
尖声的；高声的
A piping voice is high-pitched.
例：As she was about to follow there came a piping voice from upstairs. 'Is that Daddy back?'她刚要跟上，楼上便有人高声问道：“是爸爸回来了吗？”

v.漂洗( rinse的过去式和过去分词 )；冲洗；用清水漂洗掉（肥皂泡等）；（用清水）冲掉；

VERB
清洗;冲洗
When you rinse something, you wash it in clean water in order to remove dirt or soap from it.
例：After shampooing, always rinse the hair several times in clear water...每次用完洗发剂后都要用清水将头发冲洗几次。
例：It's important to rinse the rice to remove the starch.一定要淘米以洗掉淀粉。

VERB
漱（口）
If you rinse your mouth, you wash it by filling your mouth with water or with a liquid that kills germs, then spitting it out.
例：Use a toothbrush on your tongue as well, and rinse your mouth frequently.舌头也要用牙刷清洁，并且经常漱口。

N-COUNT
染发剂
A hair rinse is a dye whose colour gradually disappears after you have washed your hair a number of times, rather than being permanent.

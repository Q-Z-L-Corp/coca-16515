ADV-GRADED
安全地;无危害地
If something is done safely, it is done in a way that makes it unlikely that anyone will be harmed.
例：The waste is safely locked away until it is no longer radioactive...废料被安全储存起来，直到其不再有辐射性。
例：'Drive safely,' he said and waved goodbye...“小心开车，”他说着挥手告别。

ADV
安稳地;稳妥地;确定地
You also use safely to say that there is no risk of a situation being changed.
例：Once events are safely in the past, this idea seems to become less alarming...一旦那些事件尘封为历史，这个想法似乎就变得不那么可怕了。
例：The number two seed is safely through to the second round of the tournament.二号种子顺利地进入了锦标赛第二轮。

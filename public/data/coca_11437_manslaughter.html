N-UNCOUNT
过失杀人;非预谋杀人罪
Manslaughter is the illegal killing of a person by someone who did not intend to kill them.
例：A judge accepted her plea that she was guilty of manslaughter, not murder.她申诉说自己并非蓄意谋杀，而是过失杀人，法官接受了她的申诉。

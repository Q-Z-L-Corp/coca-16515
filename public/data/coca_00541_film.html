film 
英[fɪlm] 美[fɪlm]
 
n.影片；电影；胶片；薄层；vt.& vi.（把…）拍摄（成）电影[电视等]；上镜头；vt.（给…）覆上一薄层；生薄膜；变得朦胧；
 "

N-COUNT
电影;影片
A film consists of moving pictures that have been recorded so that they can be shown at the cinema or on television. A film tells a story, or shows a real situation.
例：Everything about the film was good. Good acting, good story, good fun.这部片子各个方面都很不错：表演出色，故事精彩，情节有趣。
例：...a government health film about the dangers of smoking.有关吸烟危害的公益健康教育影片

VERB
拍摄;把…拍成电影(或电视)
If you film something, you use a camera to take moving pictures which can be shown on a screen or on television.
例：He had filmed her life story...他把她一生的经历拍成了电影。
例：Considering the restrictions under which she filmed, I think she did a commendable job.考虑到她拍摄时所受的各种限制，我觉得她交出的答卷值得嘉许。

N-UNCOUNT
纪实镜头;记录画面
Film of something is moving pictures of a real event that are shown on television or on a screen.
例：They have seen news film of families queueing in Russia to buy a loaf of bread.他们已经看过那段俄罗斯许多家庭为买面包排起长队的新闻画面。

N-VAR
胶卷
A film is the narrow roll of plastic that is used in a camera to take photographs.
例：The photographers had already shot a dozen rolls of film.摄影师们已经拍光了一打胶卷。

N-UNCOUNT
电影艺术;电影业
The making of cinema films, considered as a form of art or a business, can be referred to as film or films .
例：Film is a business with limited opportunities for actresses...电影行业对女演员来说机会有限。
例：She wanted to set up her own company to invest in films.她想成立自己的公司投资电影。

N-COUNT
薄层
A film of powder, liquid, or oil is a very thin layer of it.
例：The sea is coated with a film of raw sewage.海面上漂浮着一层未经处理的污水。

N-UNCOUNT
(塑料)薄膜
Plastic film is a very thin sheet of plastic used to wrap and cover things.
例：Cover with plastic film and refrigerate for 24 hours.覆上保鲜膜，冷藏 24 小时。

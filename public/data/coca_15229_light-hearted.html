ADJ-GRADED
轻松愉快的；无忧无虑的
Someone who is light-hearted is cheerful and happy.
例：They were light-hearted and prepared to enjoy life.他们无忧无虑，随时准备享受生活。

ADJ-GRADED
不严肃的；随便的；为了好玩的
Something that is light-hearted is intended to be entertaining or amusing, and not at all serious.
例：There have been many attempts, both light-hearted and serious, to locate the Loch Ness Monster.有很多人或随意或认真地尝试过，想要找到尼斯湖水怪。

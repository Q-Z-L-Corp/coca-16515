masterpiece 
英[ˈmɑ:stəpi:s] 美[ˈmæstərpis]
 
n.杰作，名作；杰出的事；
 "

N-COUNT
杰作;名著
A masterpiece is an extremely good painting, novel, film, or other work of art.
例：His book, I must add, is a masterpiece.我必须补充一点：他的书是一部杰作。
例：...masterpieces by artists like Rembrandt, Raphael and Ingres.伦勃朗、拉斐尔和安格尔这些画家的杰作

N-COUNT
(画家、作家或作曲家的)最佳作品，代表作
An artist's, writer's, or composer's masterpiece is the best work that they have ever produced.

N-COUNT
典范
A masterpiece is an extremely clever or skilful example of something.
例：The whole thing was a masterpiece of crowd management.整件事情就是人群管理的典范。

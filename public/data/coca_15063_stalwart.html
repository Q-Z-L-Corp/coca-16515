N-COUNT
(尤指某个政党的)忠实拥护者，坚定分子
A stalwart is a loyal worker or supporter of an organization, especially a political party.
例：His free-trade policies aroused suspicion among Tory stalwarts...他的自由贸易政策在保守党的忠实拥护者中间引起了怀疑。
例：Moving to Germany, he became a stalwart of the revered Kurt Edelhagen Orchestra.搬到德国后，他成了著名的库尔特·埃德尔哈根管弦乐队的一名忠实听众。

ADJ-GRADED
忠实的;坚定的;完全可靠的
A stalwart supporter or worker is loyal, steady, and completely reliable.
例：...a stalwart supporter of the colonial government...殖民政府的忠实拥护者
例：The stalwart volunteers marched in this morning ready to go to work.忠实的志愿者们今天上午已就位，随时准备开始工作。

ADJ
强壮的;雄健的
A stalwart man is strong.
例：I knew I was never in any danger with my stalwart bodyguard around me.我知道有强壮的保镖在我的周围，我永远不会有什么危险。

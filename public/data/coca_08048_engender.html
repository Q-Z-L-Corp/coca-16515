VERB
导致，造成（某种感觉、气氛、状况等）
If someone or something engenders a particular feeling, atmosphere, or situation, they cause it to occur.
例：It helps engender a sense of common humanity...这有助于营造一种博爱的感觉。
例：Mr Bowles could engender delight in students and musicians alike.鲍尔斯先生能够让学生和音乐家都感到快乐。

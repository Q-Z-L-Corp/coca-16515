nasal 
英[ˈneɪzl] 美[ˈnezəl]
 
adj.鼻的；鼻音的；n.鼻音字母，鼻音；鼻骨；
 "

ADJ
鼻的
Nasal is used to describe things relating to the nose and the functions it performs.
例：...inflamed nasal passages.鼻腔发炎
例：...nasal decongestant sprays.减轻鼻充血的喷雾剂

ADJ
带鼻音的;有鼻音的
If someone's voice is nasal, it sounds as if air is passing through their nose as well as their mouth while they are speaking.
例：Her voice was nasal and penetrating...她的声音带点儿鼻音，富有穿透力。
例：She talked in a deep nasal monotone.她说话鼻音很重，语调单一。

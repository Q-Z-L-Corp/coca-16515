ceramics 
英[sɪ'ræmɪks] 美[sə'ræmɪks]
 
n.制陶术；陶器制造；陶瓷工艺；陶瓷制品( ceramic的名词复数 )；陶瓷器；制陶艺术；陶瓷装潢艺术；
 "

N-MASS
陶瓷
Ceramic is clay that has been heated to a very high temperature so that it becomes hard.
例：...ceramic tiles.瓷砖
例：...items made from hand-painted ceramic.手绘瓷砖制品

N-COUNT
陶瓷装饰品;陶瓷制品
Ceramics are ceramic ornaments or objects.
例：...a collection of Chinese ceramics.一批中国陶瓷

N-UNCOUNT
制陶术
Ceramics is the art of making artistic objects out of clay.

frailty 
英[ˈfreɪlti] 美[ˈfrelti]
 
n.脆弱；虚弱；（性格或行为上的）弱点；缺点；
 "

N-VAR
弱点;脆弱
If you refer to the frailties or frailty of people, you are referring to their weaknesses.
例：...the frailties of human nature.人性的弱点
例：...a triumph of will over human frailty.意志战胜人性弱点

N-UNCOUNT
(体质的)虚弱
Frailty is the condition of having poor health.
例：She died after a long period of increasing frailty.在很长一段时间里，她身体日渐虚弱，最后离开人世。

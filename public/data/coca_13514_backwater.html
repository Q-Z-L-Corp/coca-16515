backwater 
英[ˈbækwɔ:tə(r)] 美[ˈbækˌwɔtɚ, -ˌwɑtɚ]
 
n.回水，回流；滞水，死水；闭塞的地方；不受外界事件或新思想影响的地方；v.倒开船；食言；
 "

N-COUNT
闭塞的地方；与世隔绝的地方
A backwater is a place that is isolated.
例：...a quiet rural backwater.僻静的乡村

N-COUNT
停滞（或落后）的地方；一潭死水
If you refer to a place or institution as a backwater, you think it is not developing properly because it is isolated from ideas and events in other places and institutions.
例：Britain could become a political backwater with no serious influence in the world...英国有变成在世界上没有真正影响力的政治死水之虞。
例：This agency will be relegated to the backwaters of Washington...这个机构将沦落为华盛顿闭塞落后之地。

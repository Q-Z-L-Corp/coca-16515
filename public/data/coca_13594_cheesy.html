ADJ-GRADED
有干酪味的
Cheesy food is food that tastes or smells of cheese.
例：...cheesy biscuits...芝士饼干
例：The sauce was too runny and not cheesy enough.酱汁太稀，奶酪味不足。

ADJ-GRADED
廉价的;劣质的;虚伪的
If you describe something as cheesy, you mean that it is cheap, unpleasant, or insincere.
例：...a cheesy Baghdad hotel...巴格达的廉价旅馆
例：The King's Road was getting increasingly cheesy...国王路越来越俗气了。

N-COUNT
读者（人数）
The readership of a book, newspaper, or magazine is the number or type of people who read it.
例：Its readership has grown to over 15,000 subscribers...它的订阅人数上升到了15,000多人。
例：A new format would alienate its ageing readership without attracting young readers.新的版式会疏远上了年纪的读者群，并且也不会吸引年轻读者。

N-COUNT
（英国大学的）高级讲师职位
In Britain, a readership is the post of a reader at a university.

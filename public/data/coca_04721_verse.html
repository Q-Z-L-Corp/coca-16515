verse 
英[vɜ:s] 美[vɜrs]
 
n.诗，韵文；诗节，诗行；诗篇；圣经中的节；v.作诗或者从事于作诗；使熟练或者精通；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
诗;诗体;韵文
Verse is writing arranged in lines which have rhythm and which often rhyme at the end.
例：...a slim volume of verse...一本薄薄的诗集
例：I have been moved to write a few lines of verse.我因感动而写下了几句诗。

N-COUNT
诗节;诗行；（歌曲的）段落;（《圣经》、《古兰经》等中的）节
A verse is one of the parts into which a poem, a song, or a chapter of the Bible or the Koran is divided.
例：This verse describes three signs of spring...这节诗描绘了春天来临的三个征兆。
例：The choir has sung only two verses of the last hymn.合唱团只唱了最后一首赞美诗的两个段落。

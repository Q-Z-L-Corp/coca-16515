VERB
往（肉类食品）上浇热油及肉汁
If you baste meat, you pour hot fat and the juices from the meat itself over it while it is cooking.
例：Pam was in the middle of basting the turkey...帕姆正在往烤鸡上淋油。
例：Bake for 15-20 minutes, basting occasionally.烘烤15至20分钟，不时地浇点汁。

triangle 
英[ˈtraɪæŋgl] 美[ˈtraɪˌæŋɡəl]
 
n.三角形；三人一组；三角铁；三角板；

N-COUNT
三角(形);三角形物体
A triangle is an object, arrangement, or flat shape with three straight sides and three angles.
例：This design is in pastel colours with three rectangles and three triangles…这一设计由淡色的 3 个长方形和 3 个三角形构成。
例：Its outline roughly forms an equilateral triangle.它的轮廓大致形成一个等边三角形。

N-COUNT
三角铁(一种打击乐器)
The triangle is a musical instrument that consists of a piece of metal shaped like a triangle. You play it by hitting it with a short metal bar.

N-COUNT
三角关系
If you describe a group of three people as a triangle, you mean that they are all connected with each other in a particular situation, but often have different interests.
例：She plays a French woman in a love triangle with Jonathan Pryce and Christopher Walken.她扮演一位与乔纳森·普赖斯和克里斯托弗·沃肯有三角恋关系的法国女子。
例：…the classic triangle of husband, wife and mistress.丈夫、妻子与情妇的典型三角关系

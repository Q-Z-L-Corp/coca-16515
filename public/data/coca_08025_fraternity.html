N-UNCOUNT
兄弟情谊;友爱;互助
Fraternity refers to friendship and support between people who feel they are closely linked to each other.
例：Bob needs the fraternity of others who share his mission.鲍勃需要与他共同肩负这项使命的其他人的友爱互助。

N-COUNT
同业者;具有相同职业(或兴趣)的人
You can refer to people who have the same profession or the same interests as a particular fraternity .
例：...the spread of stolen guns among the criminal fraternity.盗窃枪支流入犯罪团伙手中
例：...the sailing fraternity.帆船运动爱好者们

(美国男大学生)联谊会，兄弟会

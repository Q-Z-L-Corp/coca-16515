peck 
英[pek] 美[pɛk]
 
vt.啄，啄食；啄出，啄穿；〈非〉轻吻，以迅速敷衍的方式亲吻；vi.啄；小口地吃；不断地批评，吹毛求疵；n.啄；啄痕，（啄出的）小洞；轻吻；
 "

VERB
啄；啄食
If a bird pecks at something or pecks something, it moves its beak forward quickly and bites at it.
例：It was winter and the sparrows were pecking at whatever they could find...时值冬季，麻雀啄食着能够找到的任何东西。
例：Chickens pecked in the dust...鸡在尘土中啄食。

VERB
匆匆轻吻
If you peck someone on the cheek, you give them a quick, light kiss.
例：Elizabeth walked up to him and pecked him on the cheek...伊丽莎白走到他面前，在他脸颊上匆匆吻了一下。
例：She pecked his cheek.她轻轻地吻了一下他的脸。

dam 
英[dæm] 美[dæm]
 
n.水坝，堰；水库；（牙科用的）橡皮障；〈比喻〉障碍；vt.用水坝阻止；阻塞，抑制；
 "

N-COUNT
坝;堤
A dam is a wall that is built across a river in order to stop the water flowing and to make a lake.
例：...plans to build a dam on the Danube River.在多瑙河上建坝的计划
例：...the Aswan Dam.阿斯旺大坝

VERB
在（河）上筑坝
To dam a river means to build a dam across it.
例：...plans to dam the nearby Delaware River...在附近的特拉华河上筑坝的计划
例：This reservoir was formed by damming the River Blith.这个水库是通过在布莱斯河上筑坝形成的。

N-COUNT
雌亲;母畜
In the breeding of farm animals, an animal's dam is its mother.

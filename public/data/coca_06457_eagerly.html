eagerly 
英['i:gəlɪ] 美[ˈiɡɚlɪ]
 
adv.渴望地，热切地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
热切的;渴望的
If you are eager to do or have something, you want to do or have it very much.
例：Robert was eager to talk about life in the Army...罗伯特很想谈谈陆军生活。
例：When my own son was five years old, I became eager for another baby...我家儿子5岁的时候，我特别想再要一个孩子。

ADJ-GRADED
（看起来或听上去）急切的，期盼的
If you look or sound eager, you look or sound as if you expect something interesting or enjoyable to happen.
例：Arty sneered at the crowd of eager faces around him...阿蒂对他身边那些期盼的面孔嗤之以鼻。
例：Her voice was girlish and eager.她的声音像个小姑娘似的，而且带着一股急不可耐。

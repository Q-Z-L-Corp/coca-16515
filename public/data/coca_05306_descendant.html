descendant 
英[dɪˈsendənt] 美[dɪˈsɛndənt]
 
n.后代；后裔；（由过去类似物发展来的）派生物；弟子；adj.下降的；祖传的；
 "

N-COUNT
后代;后裔;子孙
Someone's descendants are the people in later generations who are related to them.
例：They are descendants of the original English and Scottish settlers.他们是最初的英格兰和苏格兰定居者的后代。
例：...Lord Cochrane and his descendants.科克伦勋爵及其后裔

N-COUNT
派生物;衍生物;后代产物
Something modern which developed from an older thing can be called a descendant of it.
例：His design was a descendant of a 1956 device...他的设计是从一个 1956 年的装置衍生而来的。
例：They are the descendants of plants imported by the early settlers.它们是早期移民带来的一些植物的后代。

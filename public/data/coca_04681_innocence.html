innocence 
英[ˈɪnəsns] 美[ˈɪnəsəns]
 
n.清白；天真无邪；无罪的人；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
天真;单纯
Innocence is the quality of having no experience or knowledge of the more complex or unpleasant aspects of life.
例：...the sweet innocence of youth...青春时代的可爱与单纯
例：Youngsters are losing their childhood innocence too quickly.青少年正过早地失去他们童年的纯真。

N-UNCOUNT
清白;无罪
If someone proves their innocence, they prove that they are not guilty of a crime.
例：He claims he has evidence which could prove his innocence.他声称有证据证明自己是无辜的。

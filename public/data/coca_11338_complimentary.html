complimentary

adj.表示敬意的；赞美的；恭维的；赠送的；adv.崇敬地，赞美地，赞许地；

ADJ-GRADED
赞美的;赞扬的
If you are complimentary about something, you express admiration for it.
例：The staff have been very complimentary, and so have the customers...员工们对此大加赞扬，顾客们也一样。
例：We often get complimentary remarks regarding the cleanliness of our patio.我们经常听人称赞我们的露台非常干净。

ADJ
(座位、票或书)免费赠送的
A complimentary seat, ticket, or book is given to you free.
例：He had complimentary tickets to take his wife to see the movie.他有几张赠票，可以带妻子去看电影。
例：...a complimentary copy of Dr Sherwood's recently published book.舍伍德博士最近出版的作品的赠书

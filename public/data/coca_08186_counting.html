counting 
英['kaʊntɪŋ] 美['kaʊntɪŋ]
 
n.计算；v.（按顺序）数( count的现在分词)；有价值；数出总数；算得上；

PREP
包括…在内
Not counting a particular thing means not including that thing. Counting a particular thing means including that thing.
例：...an average operating profit of 15% to 16% of sales, not counting administrative expenses.不包括管理开支在内，销售营业利润平均为15%到16%

PHRASE
(数量)继续增长的
If you say and counting after a number or an amount of something, you mean that the number or amount is continuing to increase.
例：There is a 1,700-year-old tea tree still living in southern China which is more than 100 feet tall and counting.中国南方生长着一棵已有1,700年历史的茶树，高100多英尺，而且还在不断长高。

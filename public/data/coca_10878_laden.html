ADJ
装满的；满载的
If someone or something is laden with a lot of heavy things, they are holding or carrying them.
例：I came home laden with cardboard boxes...我抱着一大堆纸箱回了家。
例：The following summer the peach tree was laden with fruit...第二年夏天桃树就结满了桃子。

ADJ-GRADED
充满…的；饱受…的
If you describe a person or thing as laden with something, particularly something bad, you mean that they have a lot of it.
例：We're so laden with guilt...我们深感愧疚。
例：Many of their heavy industries are laden with debt.他们的重工业很多负债累累。

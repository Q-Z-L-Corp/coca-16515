dazzling 
英[] 美[]
 
adj.耀眼的，光彩夺目的；灿烂的；v.（使）眼花(dazzle的ing形式)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
辉煌的;灿烂的;艳丽的;动人的
Something that is dazzling is very impressive or beautiful.
例：He gave Alberg a dazzling smile.他冲着阿尔伯格粲然一笑。

ADJ
（光）炫目的，耀眼的
A dazzling light is very bright and makes you unable to see properly for a short time.
例：He shielded his eyes against the dazzling declining sun.他用手遮挡眼前落日耀眼的余晖。

N-COUNT
地区;区域
A district is a particular area of a town or country.
例：I drove around the business district.我开车在商业区转了转。
例：...Nashville's shopping district.纳什维尔的购物区

N-COUNT
行政区;辖区;管区
A district is an area of a town or country which has been given official boundaries for the purpose of administration.
例：...the home of the governor of the district.该区行政长官的家
例：...the continuing support of Glasgow District Council.格拉斯哥行政区政务委员会一直以来给予的支持

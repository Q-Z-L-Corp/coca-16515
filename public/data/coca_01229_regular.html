regular 
英[ˈregjələ(r)] 美[ˈrɛɡjəlɚ]
 
adj.有规律的；规则，整齐的；不变的；合格的；n.正规军；主力（或正式）队员；常客；adv.定期地；经常地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
定时的;定期的;有规律的
Regular events have equal amounts of time between them, so that they happen, for example, at the same time each day or each week.
例：Take regular exercise...定期进行锻炼。
例：Now it's time for our regular look at the world of international sport...现在我们再来关注一下国际体坛的消息。

ADJ-GRADED
经常的;频繁的
Regular events happen often.
例：...after a morning punctuated by regular volleys of gunfire...过了枪声阵阵的早上之后
例：This condition usually clears up with regular shampooing.这种情形通常多用香波洗洗头即可消除。

ADJ-GRADED
经常光顾的;经常去的
If you are, for example, a regular customer at a shop or a regular visitor to a place, you go there often.
例：'Tell me, Mr Mentakis, was Mrs Savalas one of your regular customers?'...“告诉我，门塔基斯先生，萨瓦拉斯夫人是您的常客吗？”
例：She has become a regular visitor to Houghton Hall.她已经成了霍顿府的常客。

N-COUNT
常客;老顾客;固定成员;正式成员
The regulars at a place or in a team are the people who often go to the place or are often in the team.
例：Regulars at his local pub have set up a fund to help out...经常光顾他的酒吧的人伸出援手成立了一个基金会。
例：I wasn't one of their regulars.我并不是他们的正式成员。

ADJ
经常使用的;固定的
You use regular when referring to the thing, person, time, or place that is usually used by someone. For example, someone's regular place is the place where they usually sit.
例：The man sat at his regular table near the window.这个人坐在他固定的靠窗的位置。
例：...samples from one of their regular suppliers.他们的一个固定供货商提供的样品

ADJ-GRADED
（声音或动作）规律的，规则的
A regular rhythm consists of a series of sounds or movements with equal periods of time between them.
例：...a very regular beat...非常规律的节拍
例：He stood in the doorway, listening to her quiet, regular breathing.他站在走道里，听着她那沉静而规律的呼吸声。

ADJ
正常的;常见的;普通的
Regular is used to mean 'normal'.
例：The product looks and burns like a regular cigarette...这种产品看起来和点燃后都与常见的香烟没什么两样。
例：He describes himself as just a regular guy from suburban Chicago.他说自己就是一个从芝加哥郊区来的普通人。

ADJ
(饮品或食物)分量适中的，中份的
In some restaurants, a regular drink or quantity of food is of medium size.
例：...a cheeseburger and regular fries.一个奶酪汉堡和中份薯条

ADJ-GRADED
整齐排列的;（空距）均匀的
A regular pattern or arrangement consists of a series of things with equal spaces between them.
例：...strange small rounded sandy hillocks, that look as if they've been scattered in a regular pattern on the ground.奇怪的圆形小沙丘，看上去好像是被整齐地散放在地上似的
例：...regular rows of wooden huts.整齐的一排排小木屋

ADJ-GRADED
匀称的;对称的
If something has a regular shape, both halves are the same and it has straight edges or a smooth outline.
例：...some regular geometrical shape.一些对称的几何图形

ADJ
（军队）常规的，常备的
Regular troops are professional soldiers who are a permanent part of an official national army.
例：Most schemes attempt to reduce the cost of defence through a smaller regular army...大多数方案都试图通过缩减常备军的规模来降低国防支出。
例：Only about a third of the reinforcements will be regular troops.增援部队中仅有约1/3是常备军。

ADJ
(语法中)按规则变化的，规则的
In grammar, a regular verb, noun, or adjective inflects in the same way as most verbs, nouns, or adjectives in the language.

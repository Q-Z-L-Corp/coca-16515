pump 
英[] 美[]
 
n.抽水机；打气筒；泵；轻软舞鞋；v. 用泵输送 ；涌出；急速摇晃；盘问；
 "

N-COUNT
泵；抽水机；打气筒
A pump is a machine or device that is used to force a liquid or gas to flow in a particular direction.
例：...pumps that circulate the fuel around in the engine...使燃料在发动机内循环的泵
例：There was no water in the building, just a pump in the courtyard...楼里没有水，只是在庭院里有个水泵。

VERB
抽吸；抽运；泵送
To pump a liquid or gas in a particular direction means to force it to flow in that direction using a pump.
例：It's not enough to get rid of raw sewage by pumping it out to sea...将未经处理的污水排入海中了事是不够的。
例：The money raised will be used to dig bore holes to pump water into the dried-up lake.所筹资金将用于钻孔打水以抽水到干涸的湖中。

N-COUNT
（汽油）加油泵
A petrol or gas pump is a machine with a tube attached to it that you use to fill a car with petrol.
例：There are already long queues of vehicles at petrol pumps.加油泵边车辆已经排起了长队。
例：...gas pumps.汽油加油泵

VERB
洗（胃）
If someone has their stomach pumped, doctors remove the contents of their stomach, for example because they have swallowed poison or drugs.
例：She was released from hospital yesterday after having her stomach pumped.洗胃之后，她昨天出了院。

VERB
大量投入；大量注入
If you pump money or other resources into something such as a project or an industry, you invest a lot of money or resources in it.
例：The Government needs to pump more money into community care.政府需要将更多资金投入到社区护理这一块。

VERB
不停地追问
If you pump someone about something, you keep asking them questions in order to get information.
例：He ran in every five minutes to pump me about the case...他每隔五分钟就跑来追问我这件事。
例：He must have pumped Janey for details...他肯定追问过珍妮详细的情况。

VERB
接连发射；快速连射
To pump bullets into someone means to fire a lot of bullets into them very quickly.
例：A gunman burst in and pumped five bullets into her head.一个持枪歹徒闯进来，朝她的脑袋连射5枪。

N-COUNT
（运动或休闲时穿的）橡胶底帆布鞋
Pumps are canvas shoes with flat rubber soles which people wear for sports and leisure.

N-COUNT
（通常皮制的）女式浅口鞋
Pumps are women's shoes that do not cover the top part of the foot and are usually made of plain leather.

PHRASE
刺激（经济等的）发展
To prime the pump means to do something to encourage the success or growth of something, especially the economy.
例：...the use of tax money to prime the pump of the state's economy.用税收收入来刺激该国经济发展

functional 
英[ˈfʌŋkʃənl] 美[ˈfʌŋkʃənəl]
 
adj.功能的；[数]函数的；有多种用途的；机能性；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
实用的;为实用而设计的
Functional things are useful rather than decorative.
例：...modern, functional furniture...实用的现代家具
例：The decor is functional.装潢风格追求实用。

ADJ
工作(或运转)方式的;功能性的
Functional means relating to the way in which something works or operates, or relating to how useful it is.
例：...rules defining the territorial boundaries and functional limits of the local state.划定本州地界并明确政府职权的章程

ADJ
在工作的;在起作用的;运转正常的
Functional equipment works or operates in the way that it is supposed to.
例：We have fully functional smoke alarms on all staircases.我们在各层楼梯都安装有完全可用的烟雾警报器。

finally 
英[ˈfaɪnəli] 美[ˈfaɪnəli]
 
adv.最后，终于；总算；末后；结果；
 "

ADV
终于;总算
You use finally to suggest that something happens after a long period of time, usually later than you wanted or expected it to happen.
例：The word was finally given for us to get on board...终于通知我们登乘了。
例：The food finally arrived at the end of last week and distribution began...食品总算在上周末运到并开始分发。

ADV
最后地;末尾地
You use finally to indicate that something is last in a series of actions or events.
例：The action slips from comedy to melodrama and finally to tragedy.剧情从喜剧逐渐发展为情节剧，最后又演变为悲剧。

ADV
(用于引出观点等)最后，最后要说的是
You use finally in speech or writing to introduce a final point, question, or topic.
例：Finally, who needs the theatre?...最后要说的是，谁还需要剧院呢？
例：And finally, a word about the winner and runner-up.最后，来谈一谈冠亚军。

N-VAR
迹象;暗示
An indication is a sign which suggests, for example, what people are thinking or feeling.
例：All the indications are that we are going to receive reasonable support from abroad...所有迹象都表明，我们将从国外获得有力支持。
例：He gave no indication that he was ready to compromise.他没有妥协的意思。

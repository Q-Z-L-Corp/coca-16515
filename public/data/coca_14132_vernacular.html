vernacular 
英[vəˈnækjələ(r)] 美[vərˈnækjələ(r)]
 
n.白话；行话；本国语；动植物的俗名；adj.本国语的；白话的；方言的；本国的；
 "

N-COUNT
方言;土语;地方话
The vernacular is the language or dialect that is most widely spoken by ordinary people in a region or country.
例：...books or plays written in the vernacular...用方言写就的书或戏剧
例：To use the vernacular of the period, Peter was square...用那时的土话讲，彼得是个老古板。

ADJ
（建筑）民间风格的
Vernacular architecture is the style of architecture in which ordinary people's houses are built in a particular region.
例：...the island's vernacular architecture.岛上具有当地风格的建筑
例：...the untouched vernacular buildings in superb limestone.用上乘石灰石建造的原汁原味的民间建筑

ADJ
内置的；嵌入式的
Built-in devices or features are included in something as a part of it, rather than being separate.
例：...modern cameras with built-in flash units...有内置闪光灯的新式相机
例：We're going to have built-in cupboards in the bedrooms.我们将在卧室安装嵌入式衣橱。

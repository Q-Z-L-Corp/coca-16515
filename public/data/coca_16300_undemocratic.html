ADJ-GRADED
不民主的；非民主的；独裁的
A system, process, or decision that is undemocratic is one that is controlled or made by one person or a small number of people, rather than by all the people involved.
例：...the undemocratic rule of the former political establishment...原统治集团的独裁统治
例：Opponents denounced the decree as undemocratic and unconstitutional.反对派抨击该法令是不民主的、违反宪法的。

ADJ-GRADED
更好的；更可取的；更合适的
If you say that one thing is preferable to another, you mean that it is more desirable or suitable.
例：A big earthquake a long way off is preferable to a smaller one nearby...远处发生大地震总比在附近发生小地震好。
例：The hazards of the theatre seemed preferable to joining the family paint business...经营剧院的风险似乎比加入家族涂料产业的风险要小一些。

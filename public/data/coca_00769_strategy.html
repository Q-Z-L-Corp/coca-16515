N-VAR
(尤指长期的)战略，策略，行动计划
A strategy is a general plan or set of plans intended to achieve something, especially over a long period.
例：Next week, health ministers gather in Amsterdam to agree a strategy for controlling malaria...下周，卫生部长们将在阿姆斯特丹聚首共商防治疟疾的策略。
例：What should our marketing strategy have achieved?...我们的销售策略应该取得了哪些成果呢？

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指战争中的)战略，战略学
Strategy is the art of planning the best way to gain an advantage or achieve success, especially in war.
例：I've just been explaining the basic principles of strategy to my generals.我刚刚一直在向各位将军解释战略学的基本原理。

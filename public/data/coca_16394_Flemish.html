Flemish means belonging or relating to the region of Flanders in northern Europe, or to its people, language, or culture.
例：...a splendid collection of Dutch, French and Flemish art.一批收藏的荷兰、法国和佛兰德地区的精美艺术品
例：...this picturesque Flemish town.风景如画的佛兰德城镇

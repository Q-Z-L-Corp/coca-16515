VERB
取代，替代(陈旧或令人不满意的事物)
If something is superseded by something newer, it is replaced because it has become old-fashioned or unacceptable.
例：Hand tools are relics of the past that have now been superseded by the machine.手工工具是历史的遗物，现在已经被机器所取代。

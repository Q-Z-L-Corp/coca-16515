N-COUNT
部分
A portion of something is a part of it.
例：Damage was confined to a small portion of the castle...城堡仅有一小部分受损。
例：I have spent a fairly considerable portion of my life here...我生命中相当长的一段时光是在这里度过的。

N-COUNT
（饭菜的）一份，一客
A portion is the amount of food that is given to one person at a meal.
例：Desserts can be substituted by a portion of fresh fruit...餐后甜点可以用一份新鲜水果代替。
例：The portions were generous.每份饭菜分量都很足。

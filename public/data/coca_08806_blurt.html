VERB
脱口而出；说漏嘴
If someone blurts something, they say it suddenly, after trying hard to keep quiet or to keep it secret.
例：'I was looking for Sally', he blurted, and his eyes filled with tears.“我当时正在在找萨莉”，他脱口而出，眼里噙满了泪水。

seventh 
英[ˈsevnθ] 美[ˈsɛvənθ]
 
n.第七（个）；七分之一；[乐]七度音程；adj.第七的；七分之一的；adv.居第七位地；
 "

ORD
第七
The seventh item in a series is the one that you count as number seven.
例：I was the seventh child in a family of 11.我在家里的 11 个孩子中排行老七。

FRACTION
七分之一
A seventh is one of seven equal parts of something.
例：A million people died, a seventh of the population.共有 100 万人丧生，占总人口的七分之一。

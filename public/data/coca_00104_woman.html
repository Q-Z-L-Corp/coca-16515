woman 
英[ˈwʊmən] 美[ˈwʊmən]
 
n.女人，妇女；成年女子；女拥人或女下属；女人本能；
 "

N-COUNT
成年女子;妇女
A woman is an adult female human being.
例：...a young Lithuanian woman named Dayva.名叫戴娃的立陶宛年轻女子
例：...men and women over 75 years old.75岁以上的男女

N-UNCOUNT
(泛指)女性，女子
You can refer to women in general as woman .
例：...the oppression of woman.对妇女的压迫

N-COUNT
爱好…的女人;喜欢做…的女子
If you say that a woman is, for example, a gambling woman or an outdoors woman, you mean that she likes gambling or outdoor activities.
例：She is an avid outdoors woman...她酷爱户外运动。
例：I'm too old to have a dog now. I'm a cat woman.我太老了，养不了狗。我喜欢养猫。

N-COUNT
来自…的女人;在…上大学的女子
If you say that a woman is, for example, a London woman or an Oxford woman, you mean that she comes from London or Oxford, or went to university there.
例：...a 38-year-old London woman...38岁的伦敦女子
例：The headmistress was an Oxford woman.女校长毕业于牛津大学。

N-COUNT
妻子;情人;女友
Some people refer to a man's wife, lover, or girlfriend as his woman .
例：I know my woman will never leave me.我知道我的女人永远不会离开我。

N-COUNT
(公司或组织的)女代表，女代理人
You can refer to a female representative of a company or organization as that company or organization's woman .
例：Yet another successful Labour woman took her seat...又有一位成功的工党女代表就任议员。
例：That's Judith Croft, the CND woman.那个就是朱迪丝·克罗夫特, 核裁军运动女代表。

N-VOC
(命令性的或生气、不耐烦时的称呼)婆娘，娘儿们
People sometimes address a woman as woman when they are ordering her to do something or when they are angry or impatient with her. This use could cause offence.
例：Do you realize, woman, the scandal and publicity that will be involved?臭婆娘，你知不知道这会恶名远扬？

PHRASE
独立自主;做独立女性
If you say that a woman is her own woman, you approve of the fact that she makes her plans and decisions herself, and does not depend on other people.
例：She knew she had made the right decision. She was her own woman again.她知道她作了一个正确的决定。她又独立自主了。

VERB
反攻；反击
If you counter-attack, you attack someone who has attacked you.
例：The security forces counter-attacked the following day and quelled the unrest...第二天安全部队发起反击，镇压了骚动。
例：Marion's father counter-attacked by saying that Blaze's claims were cruel and untrue.玛丽昂的父亲反击说布拉兹的说法冷酷无情，根本是在撒谎。

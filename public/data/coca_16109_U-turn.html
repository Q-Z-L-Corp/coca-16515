N-COUNT
U型转弯；180度转弯；掉头
If you make a U-turn when you are driving or cycling, you turn in a half circle in one movement, so that you are then going in the opposite direction.
例：Making a sharp U-turn, she headed back...她猛地掉转车头，往回开。
例：Eventually, he turned off the main route and suddenly did a U-turn.最后，他驶离主道，来了一个急掉头。

N-COUNT
（政治家在政策、计划、行动方面的）180度大转弯，彻底转变
If you describe a change in a politician's policy, plans, or actions as a U-turn, you mean that it is a complete change and that they made the change because they were weak or were wrong.
例：...a humiliating U-turn by the Prime Minister.首相极不光彩的180度大转变
例：...the U-turns he made on economic policy and pit closures.他在经济政策和煤窑关闭方面的那些180度大转变

resolution 
英[ˌrezəˈlu:ʃn] 美[ˌrɛzəˈluʃən]
 
n.决心；解决；坚决；分辨率；
 "

N-COUNT
正式决定;决议
Aresolution is a formal decision taken at a meeting by means of a vote.
例：He replied that the UN had passed two major resolutions calling for a complete withdrawal.他答复说联合国已经通过了两项号召全面撤军的重要决议。
例：...a draft resolution on the occupied territories.针对被占领领土的决议草案

N-COUNT
决心;决定
If you make a resolution, you decide to try very hard to do something.
例：They made a resolution to lose all the weight gained during the Christmas period.他们下定决心要减去圣诞期间增加的体重。

N-UNCOUNT
坚定;坚决;果断
Resolution is determination to do something or not do something.
例：'I think I'll try a hypnotist,' I said with sudden resolution.“我会找催眠师试试，”我突然坚决地说。

N-SING
解决
The resolution of a problem or difficulty is the final solving of it.
例：...the successful resolution of a dispute involving UN inspectors in Baghdad.涉及联合国驻巴格达观察员的争端的成功解决
例：...in order to find a peaceful resolution to the crisis.为了和平解决此次危机

N-UNCOUNT
分辨率;清晰度
The resolution of an image is how clear the image is.
例：Now this machine gives us such high resolution that we can see very small specks of calcium.现在这台机器可以呈现很高的分辨率，我们可以看见非常小的钙微粒。

dictatorial 
英[ˌdɪktəˈtɔ:riəl] 美[ˌdɪktəˈtɔriəl, -ˈtor-]
 
adj.独裁的；专横傲慢的；盛气凌人的；独断；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
独裁的;专政的
Dictatorial means controlled or used by a dictator.
例：He suspended the constitution and assumed dictatorial powers.他搁置宪法，实行独裁统治。

ADJ-GRADED
专横的;霸道的;喜欢发号施令的
If you describe someone's behaviour as dictatorial, you do not like the fact that they tell people what to do in a forceful and unfair way.
例：If you are too strict with them, your children will see you as dictatorial.如果对孩子要求太严，他们会觉得你独断专行。
例：...his dictatorial management style.他专横霸道的管理风格

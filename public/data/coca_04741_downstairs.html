downstairs 
英[ˌdaʊnˈsteəz] 美[ˌdaʊnˈsterz]
 
adv.在楼下；向[往]楼下；adj.楼下的；
 "

ADV
往楼下;顺楼梯而下;至楼下
If you go downstairs in a building, you go down a staircase towards the ground floor.
例：Denise went downstairs and made some tea.丹尼丝下楼泡了茶。

ADV
在楼下
If something or someone is downstairs in a building, they are on the ground floor or on a lower floor than you.
例：The telephone was downstairs in the entrance hall...电话在楼下的门厅里。
例：Everybody was downstairs watching a movie.大家都在楼下看电影。

ADJ
在楼下的;在一楼的
Downstairs means situated on the ground floor of a building or on a lower floor than you are.
例：She repainted the downstairs rooms and closed off the second floor.她重新粉刷了楼下的房间，并把二楼封上了。

N-SING
楼下;底层
The downstairs of a building is its lower floor or floors.
例：The downstairs of the two little houses had been entirely refashioned.两栋小楼的底层已经彻底翻修过。

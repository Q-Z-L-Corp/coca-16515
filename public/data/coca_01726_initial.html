initial 
英[ɪˈnɪʃl] 美[ɪˈnɪʃəl]
 
adj.最初的；开始的；首字母的；n.首字母；[语音学]声母；特大的大写字母；vt.用姓名的首字母签名；
 "

ADJ
开始的;最初的
You use initial to describe something that happens at the beginning of a process.
例：The initial reaction has been excellent...初期的反应非常好。
例：The aim of this initial meeting is to clarify the issues.本次初步会议的目标是澄清这些问题。

N-COUNT
(姓名的)首字母(例如 Michael Dennis Stocks 的首字母缩写为 M.D.S.)
Initials are the capital letters which begin each word of a name. For example, if your full name is Michael Dennis Stocks, your initials will be M.D.S.
例：...a silver Porsche car with her initials JB on the side.车身有她姓名缩写JB的银色保时捷跑车

VERB
用姓名的首字母签名于
If someone initials an official document, they write their initials on it, for example to show that they have seen it or that they accept or agree with it.
例：Would you mind initialing this voucher?...请在凭单上用首字母签名。
例：The agreement was initialled in June.这份协议是6月份草签的。

cautionary 
英[ˈkɔ:ʃənəri] 美[ˈkɔʃəneri]
 
adj.警告的；提醒注意的；告诫的；<英>担保的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(故事或其标注)警戒的，警世的
A cautionary story or a cautionary note to a story is one that is intended to give a warning to people.
例：Barely fifteen months later, it has become a cautionary tale of the pitfalls of international mergers and acquisitions...不过15个月，它就成了告诫人们留心跨国并购中陷阱的警世故事。
例：An editorial in The Times sounds a cautionary note.《泰晤士报》的一篇社论读起来颇有警诫意味。

rug 
英[rʌg] 美[rʌɡ]
 
n.小块地毯；〈英〉（围盖膝的）围毯，车毯；〈美俚男子假发；
 "

N-COUNT
小地毯;垫子
A rug is a piece of thick material that you put on a floor. It is like a carpet but covers a smaller area.
例：A Persian rug covered the hardwood floors.硬木地板上铺着一张波斯地毯。

N-COUNT
(盖肩膀或膝盖以保暖的)围毯，小毯子
A rug is a small blanket which you use to cover your shoulders or your knees to keep them warm.
例：The old lady was seated in her chair at the window, a rug over her knees.老太太坐在窗边的椅子上，膝盖上盖着一条小毯子。
例：...a travel rug.旅行毛毯

PHRASE
不再支持;停止帮助
If someone pulls the rug from under a person or thing or pulls the rug from under someone's feet, they stop giving their help or support.
例：If the banks opt to pull the rug from under the ill-fated project, it will go into liquidation.如果这些银行不再对这个倒霉的项目提供支持，它也就破产了。

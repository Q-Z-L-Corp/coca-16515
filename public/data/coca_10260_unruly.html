adj.不守规矩的；任性的；难驾驭的；难控制的；

ADJ-GRADED
（尤指孩子）没教养的，难控制的，难管束的
If you describe people, especially children, as unruly, you mean that they behave badly and are difficult to control.
例：It's not good enough just to blame the unruly children.对难管教的孩子只是责备是不够的。
例：...unruly behaviour.没教养的行为

ADJ-GRADED
（头发）凌乱的，不整洁的
Unruly hair is difficult to keep tidy.
例：The man had a huge head of remarkably black, unruly hair.那个人有一头乱蓬蓬的黑发。

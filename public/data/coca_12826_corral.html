corral 
英[kəˈrɑ:l] 美[kəˈræl]
 
n.畜栏；vt.把…赶入畜栏；
 "

N-COUNT
(北美的)畜栏，围栏
In North America, a corral is a space surrounded by a fence where cattle or horses are kept.

VERB
把(动物)关进畜栏;把(人)关起来
To corral a person or animal means to capture or confine them.
例：Within hours, police corralled the three men Lewis had named.几小时之内，警察就抓到了刘易斯提到的那三个人。

rump

n.臀部，尾部；残余部分；牛臀肉；余党；

N-SING
残余分子;残存力量
The rump of a group, organization, or country consists of the members who remain in it after the rest have left.
例：The rump of the party does in fact still have considerable assets...事实上，这个党派的残余分子确实仍然拥有可观的资产。
例：He finds himself heading a rump party largely out of tune with his own views.他发现自己领导的这个残存的党派和自己的观点大多不符。

N-COUNT
（动物的）尾部，臀部
An animal's rump is its rear end.
例：The cows' rumps were marked with a number.奶牛的臀部标着一个数字。

N-UNCOUNT
臀肉牛排
Rump or rump steak is meat cut from the rear end of a cow.

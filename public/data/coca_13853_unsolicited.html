ADJ
未经请求的；自发的
Something that is unsolicited has been given without being asked for and may not have been wanted.
例：'If I were you,' she adds by way of some unsolicited advice, 'I'd watch out for that girl of yours.'“如果我是你，”她主动建议说，“我会密切注意自己的女儿。”

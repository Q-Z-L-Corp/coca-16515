ADJ
拜占庭的；拜占庭帝国的
Byzantine means related to or connected with the Byzantine Empire.
例：...Byzantine civilisation...拜占庭文明
例：There are also several well-preserved Byzantine frescoes.也有几处保存完好的拜占庭时期的壁画。

ADJ-GRADED
（体制、程序）拜占庭式的，错综复杂的，诡秘的
If you describe a system or process as byzantine, you are criticizing it because it seems complicated or secretive.
例：...a byzantine system of rules and trading arrangements.包含各种规则和贸易协定的错综复杂的体系

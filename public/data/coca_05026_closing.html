ADJ
结束的;结尾的
The closing part of an activity or period of time is the final part of it.
例：He entered RAF service in the closing stages of the war...战争快要结束的时候，他加入了英国皇家空军。
例：They almost won the match in the closing minutes...最后几分钟的时候他们差一点赢了这场比赛。

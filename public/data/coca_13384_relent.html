relent 
英[rɪˈlent] 美[rɪˈlɛnt]
 
vi.动怜悯心；发慈悲心；vt.收敛；变温和；
 "

VERB
变宽厚;变温和;心软
If you relent, you allow someone to do something that you had previously refused to allow them to do.
例：Finally his mother relented and gave permission for her youngest son to marry.最后他母亲作出让步，允许她的小儿子结婚。

VERB
(天气)转晴，缓和，转好
If bad weather relents, it improves.
例：If the weather relents, the game will be finished today.如果天气转好的话，比赛将在今天结束。

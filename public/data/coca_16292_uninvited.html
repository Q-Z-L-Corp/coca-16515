ADJ
未经邀请的；不请自到的；未经要求的
If someone does something or goes somewhere uninvited, they do it or go there without being asked, often when their action or presence is not wanted.
例：He came uninvited to one of Stein's parties.在斯坦举办的一个聚会上他不请自到。
例：...a hundred invited guests and many more who were uninvited.100个受邀嘉宾和更多没被邀请的客人

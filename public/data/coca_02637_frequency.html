N-UNCOUNT
频率;发生次数
The frequency of an event is the number of times it happens during a particular period.
例：The frequency of Kara's phone calls increased rapidly...卡拉的电话飞速增多。
例：The tanks broke down with increasing frequency.坦克越来越频繁地熄火。

N-VAR
(声波或无线电波的)频率，周率
In physics, the frequency of a sound wave or a radio wave is the number of times it vibrates within a specified period of time.
例：You can't hear waves of such a high frequency.如此高频率的声波是无法听到的。
例：...a frequency of 24 kilohertz.24 千赫的频率

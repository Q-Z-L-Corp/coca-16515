abhor 
英[əbˈhɔ:(r)] 美[æbˈhɔr]
 
vt.憎恶；（厌恶地）回避；拒绝；淘汰；n.厌恶者，拒绝者；
 "

VERB
（尤指出于道德原因）憎恨，憎恶
If you abhor something, you hate it very much, especially for moral reasons.
例：He was a man who abhorred violence and was deeply committed to reconciliation...他是一个憎恶采用暴力而坚决主张和解的人。
例：If nature abhors a vacuum, journalists abhor a transition, when there is little news to cover.好比自然界拒绝真空一样，新闻工作者则厌恶没有多少新闻可供报道的过渡时期。

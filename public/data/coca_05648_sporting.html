ADJ
与体育运动有关的;体育运动用的
Sporting means relating to sports or used for sports.
例：...major sporting events, such as Wimbledon and the World Cup finals.主要的体育赛事，如温布尔登网球赛及世界杯决赛
例：...a huge sporting goods store.大型体育用品商店

PHRASE
极大的可能
If you have a sporting chance of doing something, it is quite likely that you will do that thing.
例：There was a sporting chance they would meet, but not necessarily at the party.他们很有可能会见面，但不一定在聚会上。

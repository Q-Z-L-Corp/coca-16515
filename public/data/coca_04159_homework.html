N-UNCOUNT
（学生的）家庭作业
Homework is school work that teachers give to pupils to do at home in the evening or at the weekend.
例：Have you done your homework, Gemma?杰玛，你做完家庭作业了吗？

N-UNCOUNT
必要的准备工作
If you do your homework, you find out what you need to know in preparation for something.
例：Before you go near a stockbroker, do your homework.找股票经纪人之前，先要做好必要的准备工作。

bulk 
英[bʌlk] 美[bʌlk]
 
n.（大）体积；大块，大量；大多数，大部分；主体；vt.& vi.变得越来越大（或重要）；vi.显得庞大；形成大块；堆积起来；vt.使凝聚成一团或形成一堆；adj.大批的，大量的；散装的；
 "

N-SING
（大）体积；（大）量
You can refer to something's bulk when you want to emphasize that it is very large.
例：The truck pulled out of the lot, its bulk unnerving against the dawn.卡车从空地上开出来，庞大的车身小心翼翼地避让着草坪。
例：...the shadowy bulk of an ancient barn.一座古老谷仓的巨大阴影

N-SING
庞大的身躯；巨大的体重；肥硕的体形
You can refer to a large person's body or to their weight or size as their bulk .
例：Bannol lowered his bulk carefully into the chair...班诺尔小心翼翼地将肥硕的身躯落座到椅子上。
例：Despite his bulk he moved lightly on his feet.尽管他身形庞大，移动起来还是很灵巧。

QUANT
主体；大部分
The bulk of something is most of it.
例：The bulk of the text is essentially a review of these original documents...文章的主要部分基本是对这些原始文件的回顾。
例：The vast bulk of imports and exports are carried by sea.大部分的进出口货物都是通过海路运输的。

N-UNCOUNT
大宗，大批（买卖）
If you buy or sell something in bulk, you buy or sell it in large quantities.
例：Buying in bulk is more economical than shopping for small quantities.批量采购要比零买实惠很多。
例：...bulk purchasing.大宗采购

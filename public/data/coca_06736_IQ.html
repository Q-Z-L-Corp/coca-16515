Your IQ is your level of intelligence, as indicated by a special test that you do. IQ is an abbreviation for (缩略=) 'intelligence quotient'.
例：He possesses an IQ of only 77...他的智商只有 77。
例：His IQ is above average.他的智商属于中等以上。

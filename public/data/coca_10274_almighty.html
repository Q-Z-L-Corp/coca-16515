almighty 
英[ɔ:lˈmaɪti] 美[ɔlˈmaɪti]
 
adj.全能的；万能的；有无限权力的；非常的；adv.非常；n.全能的神；
 "

N-PROPER
全能者（指上帝）；全能的上帝
The Almighty is another name for God. You can also refer to Almighty God .
例：Adam sought guidance from the Almighty...亚当寻求上帝的指引。
例：Let us now confess our sins to Almighty God.现在让我们向上帝忏悔我们的罪恶。

EXCLAM
（表示惊奇、愤怒或恐惧，可能具冒犯意味）上帝呀，耶稣呀
People sometimes say God Almighty or Christ Almighty to express their surprise, anger, or horror. These expressions could cause offence.

ADJ-GRADED
极大的；极严重的
Almighty means very serious or great in extent.
例：I had the most almighty row with the waitress...我和女招待大吵了一架。
例：I heard an almighty bang.我听到一声巨响。

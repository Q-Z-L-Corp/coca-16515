VERB
找到；探明；确定…的位置
If you locate something or someone, you find out where they are.
例：The scientists want to locate the position of the gene on a chromosome...科学家们希望找出该基因在染色体中的位置。
例：We've simply been unable to locate him.我们就是没办法找到他。

放置；设立；建立

例：Atlanta was voted the best city in which to locate a business by more than 400 chief executives...逾400名行政总裁投票推选亚特兰大为最适宜开办公司的城市。

迁居；开办公司

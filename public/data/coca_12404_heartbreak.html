N-VAR
（尤指感情破裂引起的）心碎，极度伤心
Heartbreak is very great sadness and emotional suffering, especially after the end of a love affair or close relationship.
例：...suffering and heartbreak for those close to the victims...那些受害者亲人的痛苦和伤心
例：Recent events had obviously been a heartbreak for him.最近发生的一系列事件显然让他伤心不已。

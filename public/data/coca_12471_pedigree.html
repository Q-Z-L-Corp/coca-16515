pedigree 
英[ˈpedɪgri:] 美[ˈpɛdɪˌɡri]
 
n.血统；家谱；血统表；adj.纯种的；有血统来源的；
 "

N-COUNT
（狗、猫等动物的）血统记录，纯种谱系
If a dog, cat, or other animal has a pedigree, its ancestors are known and recorded. An animal is considered to have a good pedigree when all its known ancestors are of the same type.
例：60 per cent of dogs and ten per cent of cats have pedigrees.60%的狗和10%的猫有血统记录。

ADJ
有纯种谱系证明的；纯种的
A pedigree animal is descended from animals which have all been of a particular type, and is therefore considered to be of good quality.
例：...a pedigree dog.纯种狗
例：...pedigree horses.纯种马

N-COUNT
背景；出身；门第；世系
Someone's pedigree is their background or their ancestors.
例：Hammer's business pedigree almost guaranteed him the acquaintance of U.S. presidents...哈默的商业背景几乎保证了他能够结识美国总统。
例：She had an impeccable aristocratic pedigree.她有纯正的贵族血统。

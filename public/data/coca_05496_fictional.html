ADJ
虚构的;非真实的
Fictional characters or events occur only in stories, plays, or films and never actually existed or happened.
例：It is drama featuring fictional characters...这部戏剧中的人物是虚构的。
例：Ulverton is a fictional village on the Wessex Downs.阿尔弗顿是一个虚构的地处西撒克斯丘陵的村庄。

helm 
英[helm] 美[hɛlm]
 
n.舵，舵柄；掌舵，掌管；（国家，企业等的）机要部门，领；头盔；vt.指挥；给…掌舵；给…戴上头盔；
 "

N-COUNT
舵柄;舵轮
The helm of a boat or ship is the part that is used to steer it.
例：I got into our dinghy while Willis took the helm and positioned the boat for the photograph.我钻进我们的小艇，威利斯掌舵，将船摆好位置准备拍照。

N-SING
（政府或组织中的）领导位置
You can say that someone is at the helm when they are leading or running a country or organization.
例：He has been at the helm of Lonrho for 31 years.他执掌伦罗公司已达31年。
例：...Yutaka Kume, who took the helm as Nissan's president in June 1985.1985年6月成为日产汽车社长的久米丰

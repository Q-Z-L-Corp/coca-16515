boyish 
英[ˈbɔɪɪʃ] 美[ˈbɔɪɪʃ]
 
adj.男孩的；孩子气的；（女孩）像男孩的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（外貌或举止）男孩子气的，稚气的
If you describe a man as boyish, you mean that he is like a boy in his appearance or behaviour, and you find this characteristic quite attractive.
例：She was relieved to see his face light up with a boyish grin...看到他脸上现出孩子气的笑容，她总算松了口气。
例：He loves to learn, and has a boyish enthusiasm for life.他热爱学习，对生活有一种少年般纯真的热情。

ADJ-GRADED
（女子）长得像男孩子的，假小子的
If you describe a girl or woman as boyish, you mean that she looks like a boy, for example because she has short hair or small breasts.
例：...her tall, boyish figure.她高挑的男孩子般的身材

pathway

N-COUNT
小路；路径
A pathway is a path which you can walk along or a route which you can take.
例：Richard was coming up the pathway.理查德正沿着小路走过来。
例：...a pathway leading towards the nearby river.通往附近河流的小路

N-COUNT
途径；行动路线
A pathway is a particular course of action or a way of achieving something.
例：Diplomacy will smooth your pathway to success.为人圆通会让你成功的道路通畅无阻。
例：... the call to arms that opened the pathway to freedom for the Brazilian people.为巴西人民开启了自由之路的武装起来的号召

featured 
英['fi:tʃəd] 美['fitʃəd]
 
adj.有…的面貌特征的，作为号召物的；作为特色的；
 "

N-COUNT
特征;特点;特色
A feature of something is an interesting or important part or characteristic of it.
例：Patriotic songs have long been a feature of Kuwaiti life...爱国歌曲长期以来一直是科威特生活的一个特色。
例：The spacious gardens are a special feature of this property...大花园是这处房产的特色。

N-PLURAL
面容;五官
Your features are your eyes, nose, mouth, and other parts of your face.
例：His features seemed to change...他的容貌似乎有所改变。
例：Her features were strongly defined.她的五官轮廓分明。

VERB
由…主演;以…为主要内容;重点介绍
When something such as a film or exhibition features a particular person or thing, they are an important part of it.
例：It's a great movie and it features a Spanish actor who is going to be a world star within a year...这是一部精彩的影片，主演是一位西班牙演员，他不出一年就会成为国际巨星。
例：The hour-long programme will be updated each week and feature highlights from recent games...这个时长一小时的节目将每周更新，重点介绍最近比赛的精彩片断。

VERB
出演重头戏;担任主演;(在展览、杂志中)为重点推出的部分
If someone or something features in something such as a show, exhibition, or magazine, they are an important part of it.
例：Jon featured in one of the show's most thrilling episodes.乔恩主演剧中最引人入胜的剧集之一。

N-COUNT
(报刊或杂志的)特写，专题;(电台或电视台的)特别节目
A feature is a special article in a newspaper or magazine, or a special programme on radio or television.
例：We are delighted to see the Sunday Times running a long feature on breast cancer.我们高兴地看到《星期天时报》发表了有关乳腺癌的长篇专题文章。
例：...a special feature on the fund-raising project.有关集资项目的专题报道

N-COUNT
正片;影片;故事片
A feature or a feature film or movie is a full-length film about a fictional situation, as opposed to a short film or a documentary.
例：...the first feature-length cartoon, Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.第一部卡通长片《白雪公主和七个小矮人》

N-COUNT
地理特征;地形地物;地貌
A geographical feature is something noticeable in a particular area of country, for example a hill, river, or valley.

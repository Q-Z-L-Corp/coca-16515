ADV
渐增地;越来越多地
You can use increasingly to indicate that a situation or quality is becoming greater in intensity or more common.
例：He was finding it increasingly difficult to make decisions...他发现越来越难以作出决定。
例：There is an increasingly popular alternative...有了越来越受欢迎的替代品。

oyster

n.牡蛎；<俚>守秘密的人；鸡背肉；沉默寡言的人；
 "

N-COUNT
牡蛎；蚝
An oyster is a large flat shellfish. Some oysters can be eaten and others produce valuable objects called pearls.
例：He had two dozen oysters and enjoyed every one of them.他吃了两打牡蛎，每一只都吃得津津有味。

PHRASE
世界是属于…的；尽可做想做之事（或去想去之处）
If you say that the world is someone's oyster, you mean that they can do anything or go anywhere that they want to.
例：You're young, you've got a lot of opportunity. The world is your oyster.你还年轻，机会有的是。世界是属于你的。

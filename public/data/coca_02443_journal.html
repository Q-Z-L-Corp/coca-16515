journal 
英[ˈdʒɜ:nl] 美[ˈdʒɜrnl]
 
n.日报，日志，日记；定期刊物，期刊，杂志；[会计]分类账；
 "

N-COUNT
（尤指某专门学科的）杂志，刊物
A journal is a magazine, especially one that deals with a specialized subject.
例：All our results are published in scientific journals.我们的研究结果全都发表在科学刊物上了。

N-COUNT
日报，周报，…报（常用于报纸名称中）
A journal is a daily or weekly newspaper. The word journal is often used in the name of the paper.
例：He was a newspaperman for The New York Times and some other journals.他是个记者，为《纽约时报》和其他一些报刊撰稿。
例：...The Wall Street Journal.《华尔街日报》

N-COUNT
日记；日志
A journal is an account which you write of your daily activities.
例：Sara confided to her journal...萨拉在日记中倾诉心事。
例：On the plane he wrote in his journal.他在飞机上写日记。

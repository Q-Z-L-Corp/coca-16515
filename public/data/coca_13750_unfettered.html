ADJ
自由的；不受拘束的
If you describe something as unfettered, you mean that it is not controlled or limited by anyone or anything.
例：...unfettered free trade...不受限制的自由贸易
例：Unfettered by the bounds of reality, my imagination flourished.跳出现实的束缚，我的想象力天马行空。

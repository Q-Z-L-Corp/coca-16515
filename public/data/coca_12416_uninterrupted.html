uninterrupted 
英[ˌʌnˌɪntəˈrʌptɪd] 美[ˌʌnˌɪntəˈrʌptɪd]
 
adj.不间断的，连续的；未受干扰的；联绵；
 "

ADJ
不间断的；连续不断的
If something is uninterrupted, it is continuous and has no breaks or interruptions in it.
例：This enables the healing process to continue uninterrupted...这样能使恢复过程不间断地进行下去。
例：His hearing remained good, so that his contact with the world was uninterrupted.他的听力尚佳，因此他与外界的联系没有中断。

ADJ
（景色）不受阻挡的
An uninterrupted view of something is a clear view of it, without any obstacles in the way.
例：Diners can enjoy an uninterrupted view of the garden.用餐者对花园的景色一览无余。

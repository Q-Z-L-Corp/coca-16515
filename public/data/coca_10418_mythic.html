mythic 
英[ˈmɪθɪk] 美[ˈmɪθɪk]
 
adj.神话的，虚构的；
 "

ADJ
神话的;只存在于神话中的
Someone or something that is mythic exists only in myths and is therefore imaginary.
例：...the mythic figure of King Arthur.亚瑟王这一传奇人物

ADJ-GRADED
很著名的;很重要的
If you describe someone or something as mythic, you mean that they have become very famous or important.
例：...a team whose reputation has achieved mythic proportions...声名显赫的球队
例：His rapid rise to power has given him a near-mythic status in the industry.他迅速升任要职，这让他在业界近乎被神化了。

sculpt 
英[skʌlpt] 美[skʌlpt]
 
vt.雕刻；雕塑；使成雕塑状；vi.雕刻，雕塑；
 "

VERB
雕刻;雕塑
When an artist sculpts something, they carve or shape it out of a material such as stone or clay.
例：An artist sculpted a full-size replica of her head...一位艺术家按实际大小雕刻了一个她的头部模型。
例：When I sculpt, my style is expressionistic.我的雕刻风格是表现主义。

VERB
使成形
If something is sculpted, it is made into a particular shape.
例：More familiar landscapes have been sculpted by surface erosion...还有更多熟悉的地貌是因地表侵蚀形成的。
例：Michael smoothed and sculpted Jane's hair into shape.迈克尔把简的头发梳理平整并做出发型。

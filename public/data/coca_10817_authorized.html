VERB
批准；准许；认可
If someone in a position of authority authorizes something, they give their official permission for it to happen.
例：It would certainly be within his power to authorize a police raid like that...他绝对有权批准那样的一次警方突袭行动。
例：We are willing to authorize the president to use force if necessary.我们愿意授权总统在必要的时候使用武力。

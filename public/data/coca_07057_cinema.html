cinema 
英[ˈsɪnəmə] 美[ˈsɪnəmə]
 
n.电影院；电影艺术；电影业；电影；
 "

N-COUNT
电影院
A cinema is a place where people go to watch films for entertainment.
例：The country has relatively few cinemas.相对来讲，这个国家几乎没有几家电影院。

N-SING
（电影院的）电影
You can talk about the cinema when you are talking about seeing a film in a cinema.
例：I can't remember the last time we went to the cinema...我记不得上次我们去看电影是什么时候了。
例：They decided to spend an evening at the cinema.他们决定晚上去看电影。

N-UNCOUNT
电影业;电影艺术
Cinema is the business and art of making films.
例：Contemporary African cinema has much to offer in its vitality and freshness.当代非洲电影在活力和新颖性上颇足称道。
例：...in the early days of cinema.电影业的早期

socialization 
英[ˌsəʊʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃn] 美[ˌsoʊʃələˈzeɪʃn]
 
n.社会化，社会主义化；公有化；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指儿童的)社会化，合群
Socialization is the process by which people, especially children, are made to behave in a way which is acceptable in their culture or society.
例：Female socialization emphasizes getting along with others.女性的社会化强调与人相处。

N-UNCOUNT
社会所有化;社会主义化
Socialization is the process by which something is made to operate on socialist principles.

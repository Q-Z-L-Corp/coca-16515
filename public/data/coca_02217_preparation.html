preparation 
英[ˌprepəˈreɪʃn] 美[ˌprɛpəˈreʃən]
 
n.准备，预备；准备工作；配制品；制剂；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
准备；预备；筹备
Preparation is the process of getting something ready for use or for a particular purpose or making arrangements for something.
例：Rub the surface of the wood in preparation for the varnish...打磨木头的表面，为刷清漆做准备。
例：Few things distracted the Pastor from the preparation of his weekly sermons...几乎没有什么事情能使牧师在准备每周的布道时分神。

N-PLURAL
准备工作；预备工作；筹备工作
Preparations are all the arrangements that are made for a future event.
例：The United States is making preparations for a large-scale airlift of 1,200 American citizens...美国正在为运送1,200名美国公民的大规模空运做准备。
例：Final preparations are under way for celebrations to mark German unification.纪念德国统一的庆典活动正在做最后的准备工作。

N-COUNT
（食物、医药、化妆品的）制剂，配制品
A preparation is a mixture that has been prepared for use as food, medicine, or a cosmetic.
例：...anti-ageing creams and sensitive-skin preparations.抗衰老面霜和敏感性皮肤适用的护肤制剂

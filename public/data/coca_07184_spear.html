spear 
英[spɪə(r)] 美[spɪr]
 
n.矛；枪；（某些植物的）嫩枝；貌，标枪；vt.用矛刺；用鱼叉捉；〈美俚〉得到，捕到；刺，戳；adj.父系的，右边的；
 "

N-COUNT
矛;投枪;梭镖
A spear is a weapon consisting of a long pole with a sharp metal point attached to the end.

VERB
用矛(投)刺;叉住
If you spear something, you push or throw a pointed object into it.
例：Spear a piece of fish with a carving fork and dip it in the batter...用切肉叉叉一片鱼肉，在面糊里蘸一下。
例：A police officer was speared to death.一名警官被刺死了。

N-COUNT
(芦笋、花椰菜等的)嫩茎
Asparagus or broccoli spears are individual stalks of asparagus or broccoli.

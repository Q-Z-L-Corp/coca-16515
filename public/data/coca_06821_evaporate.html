evaporate 
英[ɪˈvæpəreɪt] 美[ɪˈvæpəˌret]
 
vt.& vi.（使某物）蒸发掉；vi.消失；发散气体；蒸发；vt.使脱水；使蒸发；使挥发；使沉淀；
 "

V-ERG
（使）蒸发;（使）挥发
When a liquid evaporates, or is evaporated, it changes from a liquid state to a gas, because its temperature has increased.
例：Moisture is drawn to the surface of the fabric so that it evaporates...湿气被吸到织物表面从而蒸发。
例：The water is evaporated by the sun.水在阳光照射下蒸发了。

VERB
（情绪）逐渐消失；（计划）逐渐落空；（活动）逐渐停止
If a feeling, plan, or activity evaporates, it gradually becomes weaker and eventually disappears completely.
例：My anger evaporated and I wanted to cry...我的怒气渐渐消失，想大哭一场。
例：Your dreams always seem to evaporate, and nothing ever quite matches expectations...你的梦想似乎总是逐渐破灭，从来没有什么事情能完全符合你的预期。

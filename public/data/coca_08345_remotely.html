ADV
(用于否定句)丝毫，根本
You use remotely with a negative statement to emphasize the statement.
例：We had never seen anything remotely like it before...我们以前从来没有见过任何类似的事情。
例：Nobody was remotely interested.根本没有人感兴趣。

ADV-GRADED
遥远地;偏远地
If someone or something is remotely placed or situated, they are a long way from other people or places.
例：...the remotely situated, five bedroom house.地处边远地段的5居室住宅

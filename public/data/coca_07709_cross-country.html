N-UNCOUNT
越野比赛(如赛跑、自行车赛、滑雪赛等)
Cross-country is the sport of running, riding, or skiing across open countryside rather than along roads or around a running track.
例：She finished third in the world cross-country championships in Antwerp.她在安特卫普的世界越野锦标赛上获得了第3名。

ADJ
横越全国的;遍及全国的
A cross-country journey involves less important roads or railway lines, or takes you from one side of a country to the other.
例：...cross-country rail services...遍及全国的铁路服务网络
例：The group made a 13-concert cross-country tour, including a stop in New York.该乐队举行了13场全国巡回演唱会，其中包括在纽约的一站。

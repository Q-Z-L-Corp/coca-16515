dissident 
英[ˈdɪsɪdənt] 美[ˈdɪsɪdənt]
 
n.持异议者，公开大唱反调者，持不同政见者；adj.持不同意见的（人）；
 "

N-COUNT
持不同政见者;异议者
Dissidents are people who disagree with and criticize their government, especially because it is undemocratic.
例：...political dissidents.持不同政见者
例：...former Soviet dissident Natan Sharansky.前苏联持不同政见者纳坦·夏兰斯基

ADJ
有不同意见的;持不同政见的
Dissident people disagree with or criticize their government or a powerful organization they belong to.
例：...a dissident Russian novelist...持不同政见的俄罗斯小说家
例：She was suspected of having links with a dissident group.她被怀疑与一个持不同政见的政治团体有关联。

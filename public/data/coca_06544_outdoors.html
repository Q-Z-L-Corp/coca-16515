ADV
在户外；在室外；在野外
If something happens outdoors, it happens outside in the fresh air rather than in a building.
例：It was warm enough to be outdoors all afternoon...天气很暖和，整个下午都可以呆在户外。
例：The ceremony was being held outdoors.该仪式正在户外举行。

N-SING
户外工作；野外活动；户外休闲
You refer to the outdoors when talking about work or leisure activities which take place outside away from buildings.
例：I'm a lover of the outdoors...我喜爱户外运动。
例：Life in the great outdoors isn't supposed to be luxurious.美好的户外活动不必很奢侈。

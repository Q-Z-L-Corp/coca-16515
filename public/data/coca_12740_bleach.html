bleach 
英[bli:tʃ] 美[blitʃ]
 
vt.& vi.漂白；使褪色；n.漂白剂；漂白；
 "

VERB
漂白；使变白
If you bleach something, you use a chemical to make it white or pale in colour.
例：These products don't bleach the hair.这些产品不会使头发变白。
例：Commercial flour is bleached artificially.市面上的面粉是经过人工漂白的。

V-ERG
晒白；（使）褪色；（使）变白
If the sun bleaches something, or something bleaches, its colour gets paler until it is almost white.
例：The tree's roots are stripped and hung to season and bleach...树根剥皮后挂起来风干晒白。
例：The sun will bleach the hairs on your face...太阳会使你脸上的汗毛发白。

N-MASS
漂白剂
Bleach is a chemical that is used to make cloth white, or to clean things thoroughly and kill germs.

VERB
是…的典型;代表
If something or someone typifies a situation or type of thing or person, they have all the usual characteristics of it and are a typical example of it.
例：These two buildings typify the rich extremes of Irish architecture.这两座建筑物体现了爱尔兰建筑风格的极致多变。
例：…a reaction against the achievements of science, as typified by the development of nuclear weapons.对以核武器发展为代表的科学成就的反对

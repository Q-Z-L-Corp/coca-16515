hellish 
英[ˈhelɪʃ] 美[ˈhɛlɪʃ]
 
adj.很坏的，很讨厌的；adv.很坏地，很讨厌地；n.很坏，很讨厌；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
地狱般的;讨厌的;可憎的
You describe something as hellish to emphasize that it is extremely unpleasant.
例：The atmosphere in Washington is hellish...华盛顿的气氛令人憎恶。
例：I'm sure you will remember from the hellish years of your boyhood.我相信你一定记得自己梦魇般的童年时代的事情。

ADV
…得要命;…得够呛
You use hellish to emphasize an unpleasant quality or its great extent.
例：It's hellish cold up here in winter.这里的冬天冷得要命。

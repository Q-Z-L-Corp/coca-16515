worthy 
英[ˈwɜ:ði] 美[ˈwɜrði]
 
adj.应得某事物；值得做某事；可尊敬的；配得上的，相称的；n.杰出人物，伟人；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
值得…的;应该获得…的
If a person or thing is worthy of something, they deserve it because they have the qualities or abilities required.
例：The bank might think you're worthy of a loan...银行也许会认为你具备贷款的条件。
例：The Minister says the idea is worthy of consideration...部长说这个想法值得考虑。

ADJ-GRADED
值得尊敬的;可敬的；令人称许的
A worthy person or thing is approved of by most people in society and considered to be morally respectable or correct.
例：...worthy members of the community...团体中德高望重者
例：They thought the feminist label was too worthy.他们觉得女权主义者这个称谓太抬举她们了。

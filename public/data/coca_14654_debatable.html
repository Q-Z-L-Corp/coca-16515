debatable 
英[dɪˈbeɪtəbl] 美[dɪˈbetəbəl]
 
adj.可争辩的；成问题的；未定的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有争议的;有疑问的;未定的
If you say that something is debatable, you mean that it is not certain.
例：Whether we can stay in this situation is debatable...在这种情况下我们能否留下还很难说。
例：It is debatable whether or not antibiotics would make any difference...抗生素能否起到什么作用还无法定论。

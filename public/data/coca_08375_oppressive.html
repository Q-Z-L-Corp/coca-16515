oppressive 
英[əˈpresɪv] 美[əˈprɛsɪv]
 
adj.沉重的；压迫的；压抑的；沉闷；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（社会、法律、习俗等）压迫的，压制的，暴虐的，不公的
If you describe a society, its laws, or customs as oppressive, you think they treat people cruelly and unfairly.
例：The new laws will be just as oppressive as those they replace.新法律只会与原来的法律一样苛严。
例：...refugees from the oppressive regime.逃离暴政的难民

ADJ-GRADED
（天气、空气等）闷热的，难以忍受的
If you describe the weather or the atmosphere in a room as oppressive, you mean that it is unpleasantly hot and damp.
例：The oppressive afternoon heat had quite tired him out...下午闷热的天气使他疲惫不堪。
例：The little room was windowless and oppressive.这个小房间没有窗户，使人感到闷热难耐。

ADJ-GRADED
令人心情沉重的；让人感到压抑的；使人苦恼的
An oppressive situation makes you feel depressed and uncomfortable.
例：...the oppressive sadness that weighed upon him like a physical pain.如肉体上的疼痛一样压在他心头的令人抑郁的悲伤

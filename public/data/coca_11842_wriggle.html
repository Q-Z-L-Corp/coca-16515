wriggle 
英[ˈrɪgl] 美[ˈrɪɡəl]
 
vi.扭动，蠕动，蜿蜒行进；摆脱，用计逃脱；vt.（使身体某一部位）扭动；耍滑不做，逃避（应做的事等）；n.蠕动，扭动；
 "

VERB
蠕动，扭动(身体的一部分)
If you wriggle or wriggle part of your body, you twist and turn with quick movements, for example because you are uncomfortable.
例：The babies are wriggling on their tummies...婴儿们趴着，扭来扭去。
例：They were fidgeting and wriggling in their seats...他们在座位上焦躁不安，扭来扭去。

VERB
扭动着行进;蠕动着行进
If you wriggle somewhere, for example through a small gap, you move there by twisting and turning your body.
例：He clutched the child tightly as she again tried to wriggle free...孩子一扭一扭地又想挣脱，他紧紧地抓住了她。
例：Bauman wriggled into the damp coverall.鲍曼扭着身子穿上潮湿的工作服。

ADJ-GRADED
（话语、描述或选择）合适的，恰当的，贴切的
An apt remark, description, or choice is especially suitable.
例：The words of this report are as apt today as in 1929.这份报告的措词用在今天和用在1929年一样贴切。
例：...an apt description of the situation.对这种情况恰如其分的描述

ADJ-GRADED
易于…的；有…倾向的
If someone is apt to do something, they often do it and so it is likely that they will do it again.
例：She was apt to raise her voice and wave her hands about...她动不动就拔高嗓门，并挥舞双手。
例：This type of weather is apt to be more common in winter.这种天气在冬季往往更为常见。

rematch 
英[ˈri:mætʃ] 美[riˈmætʃ, ˈriˌmætʃ]
 
v.（运动竞赛、桥牌等的）重赛；
 "

N-COUNT
(因为平局或有争议而进行的)复赛，再赛，重赛
A rematch is a second game that is played between two people or teams, for example because their first match was a draw or because there was a dispute about some aspect of it.
例：Duff said he would be demanding a rematch.达夫说他将要求重赛。

N-COUNT
(比赛或竞赛对手之间的)复赛，二次交手
A rematch is a second game or contest between two people or teams who have already faced each other.
例：Stanford will face UCLA in a rematch...斯坦福队将与加利福尼亚大学洛杉矶分校队展开第二回合较量。
例：The debate this week was a rematch of last year's.这个星期的辩论还是在去年的两个对手之间进行。

enthuse 
英[ɪnˈθju:z] 美[ɪnˈθuz]
 
vt.使热情；vi.对…极热心；
 "

VERB
兴奋地说;津津乐道地讲述
If you enthuse about something, you talk about it in a way that shows how excited you are about it.
例：Elizabeth David enthuses about the taste, fragrance and character of Provencal cuisine...伊丽莎白·戴维对普罗旺斯菜肴的色香味津津乐道。
例：'I've found the most wonderful house to buy!' she enthused.“我已经找到想买的最棒的房子啦！”她兴奋地说。

VERB
使兴奋;使有热情
If you are enthused by something, it makes you feel excited and enthusiastic.
例：I was immediately enthused...我立即兴奋起来。
例：Conference participants were clearly enthused by their presence, and the two women responded by listening intently...她们的出席令与会者明显兴奋起来，作为回应这两位女士也专心倾听起来。

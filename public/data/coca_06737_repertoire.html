repertoire 
英[ˈrepətwɑ:(r)] 美[ˈrepərtwɑ(r)]
 
n.全部节目；全部本领；（计算机的）指令表；
 "

N-COUNT
（一个演员所能表演的）全部节目，曲目
A per-former's repertoire is all the plays or pieces of music that he or she has learned and can perform.
例：Meredith D'Ambrosio has thousands of songs in her repertoire.梅雷迪思·丹布罗西奥会唱几千首歌曲。

N-SING
全部技能（或才能）
The repertoire of a person or thing is all the things of a particular kind that the person or thing is capable of doing.
例：...Mike's impressive repertoire of funny stories...迈克会讲大量有趣故事的非凡才能
例：This has been one of the most successful desserts in my repertoire.这是我会做的甜点中做得最成功的一种。

N-SING
（某个种类的）全部剧目(或曲目)
You can refer to all the plays or music of a particular kind as, for example, the classical repertoire or the jazz repertoire .
例：It is no coincidence that the works in the 'standard repertoire' tend to have names.在“标准剧目”中的作品有各自的名字并非偶然。

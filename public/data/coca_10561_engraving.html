engraving 
英[ɪnˈgreɪvɪŋ] 美[ɛnˈɡrevɪŋ]
 
n.版画；雕刻（作品）；雕刻艺术；镌版术；v.在（硬物）上雕刻（字，画等）( engrave的现在分词 )；将某事物深深印在（记忆或头脑中）；
 "

N-COUNT
雕版；图版
An engraving is a picture or design that has been cut into a surface.

N-COUNT
版画
An engraving is a picture that has been printed from a plate on which designs have been cut.
例：...a color engraving of oranges and lemons.橙子和柠檬的彩色版画

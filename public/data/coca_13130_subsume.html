subsume 
英[səbˈsju:m] 美[səbˈsum]
 
vt.归入，包括；
 "

VERB
将…归入，将…纳入(更大的组织或范畴)
If something is subsumed within a larger group or class, it is included within it, rather than being considered as something separate.
例：After that the two alliances might be subsumed into a new European security system...从那以后，这两个联盟将可能会被纳入一个新的欧洲安全体系之中。
例：Scottish football would be subsumed by a British League.苏格兰足球将被纳入某个英国足球联赛。

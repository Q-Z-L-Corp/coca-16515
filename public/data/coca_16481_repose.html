repose 
英[rɪˈpəʊz] 美[rɪˈpoʊz]
 
n.休息，安眠，宁静；vt.将（手臂等）靠在某人（某物）上；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
休息;休憩;闲适
Repose is a state in which you are resting and feeling calm.
例：He had a still, almost blank face in repose...他休息时面部平静，几乎毫无表情。
例：Its atmosphere is one of repose rather than excitement.那里气氛安宁而不热闹。

VERB
位于;安置于;坐落于
If something reposes somewhere, it is there.
例：Exquisite china soup dishes reposed on silver plates.银盘子上面摆放着精制的陶瓷汤盘。

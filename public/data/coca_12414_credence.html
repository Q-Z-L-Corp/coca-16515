N-UNCOUNT
(理论或故事的)可信性，真实性
If something lends or gives credence to a theory or story, it makes it easier to believe.
例：Good studies are needed to lend credence to the notion that genuine progress can be made in this important field.需要进行认真的研究，以让人们相信在这一重要领域可以取得真正的进展。

N-UNCOUNT
信任;相信
If you give credence to a theory or story, you believe it.
例：You're surely not giving any credence to this story of Hythe's?你肯定不会相信海斯的说法吧？

ADJ
不正确的;错误的;不真实的
Something that is incorrect is wrong and untrue.
例：He denied that his evidence about the telephone call was incorrect...他否认自己关于电话的证词是虚假的。
例：People often have incorrect information about food.人们对食物经常会有不正确的认识。

ADJ-GRADED
不适当的;不合适的
Something that is incorrect is not the thing that is required or is most suitable in a particular situation.
例：...injuries caused by incorrect posture.由不当姿势造成的损伤
例：...incorrect diet.不当的饮食

VERB
撤销对…的管制规定;解除对…的控制
To deregulate something means to remove controls and regulations from it.
例：...the need to deregulate the US airline industry...撤销对美国航空业管制的需要
例：Once wholesale prices are deregulated, consumer prices will also rise.一旦批发价格放开，零售价格也会随之上涨。

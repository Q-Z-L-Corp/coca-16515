N-UNCOUNT
士气;精神面貌
Morale is the amount of confidence and cheerfulness that a group of people have.
例：Many pilots are suffering from low morale...很多飞行员目前士气低落。
例：They hope to boost the morale of their troops.他们希望提升部队的士气。

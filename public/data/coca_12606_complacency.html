complacency 
英[kəmˈpleɪsnsi] 美[kəmˈplesənsi]
 
n.自满，满足；自鸣得意；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
自满;沾沾自喜
Complacency is being complacent about a situation.
例：...a worrying level of complacency about the risks of infection from AIDS...对感染艾滋病的危险毫不在意，实在堪忧
例：She warned that there was no room for complacency on inflation.她警告说对待通货膨胀不能有一丝的懈怠。

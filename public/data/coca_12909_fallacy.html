 "

N-VAR
谬论;谬见
A fallacy is an idea which many people believe to be true, but which is in fact false because it is based on incorrect information or reasoning.
例：It's a fallacy that the affluent give relatively more to charity than the less prosperous...富人比穷人对慈善事业的捐助更多些;这种观点是一种谬见。
例：It exposes the fallacy of short-term industrial gain at long-term environmental expense.这暴露了以长久的环境破坏为代价换取短期工业利益的错误。

N-COUNT
演唱会；演奏会；现场演出
A gig is a live performance by someone such as a musician or a comedian.
例：The two bands join forces for a gig at the Sheffield Arena on November 28...11月28日，这两支乐队将携手在谢菲尔德剧场举办一场演唱会。
例：He supplemented his income with occasional comedy gigs.他偶尔会表演单口喜剧赚取外快。

VERB
（公开）表演，演奏
When musicians or other performers gig, they perform live in public.
例：By the time he was 15, Scott had gigged with a handful of well-known small bands.到15岁的时候，斯科特已经与几支闻名遐迩的小乐队同台演出过。
例：...ten years of gigging in bars and clubs all over Kentucky.足迹遍及肯塔基州各酒吧和夜总会的10年驻唱生涯

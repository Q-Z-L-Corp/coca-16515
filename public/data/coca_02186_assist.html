assist 
英[əˈsɪst] 美[əˈsɪst]
 
n.帮助；援助；机器助手；辅助装置；vt.帮助；援助；帮助某人做某事；搀扶（某人）上下车；vi.援助；出席；参加；
 "

VERB
帮助，协助(完成工作)
If you assist someone, you help them to do a job or task by doing part of the work for them.
例：Julia was assisting him to prepare his speech...朱莉娅正在帮他准备讲演稿。
例：The family decided to assist me with my chores...全家人决定帮助我做家务。

VERB
（提供信息、意见或金钱）帮助，援助
If you assist someone, you give them information, advice, or money.
例：The public is urgently requested to assist police in tracing this man...紧急要求公众帮助警方追踪此人。
例：Foreign Office officials assisted with transport and finance problems...外交部官员帮助解决运输和经费问题。

VERB
有助于;促进
If something assists in doing a task, it makes the task easier to do.
例：...a chemical that assists in the manufacture of proteins...有助于生成蛋白质的一种化学物质
例：Here are some good sources of information to assist you in making the best selection...这里是一些有价值的信息来源，可以帮助你作出最佳选择。

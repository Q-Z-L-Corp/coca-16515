cynicism 
英['sɪnɪsɪzəm] 美[ˈsɪnɪˌsɪzəm]
 
n.讥笑，讥讽的言词；愤世嫉俗；玩世不恭；犬儒哲学，犬儒主义；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
认为世人皆自私的观念;愤世嫉俗
Cynicism is the belief that people always act selfishly.
例：I found Ben's cynicism wearing at times.我有时觉得本的愤世嫉俗令人厌烦。

N-UNCOUNT
(对成功或人的真善的）怀疑
Cynicism about something is the belief that it cannot be successful or that the people involved are not honourable.
例：This talk betrays a certain cynicism about free trade.这番讲话流露出一种对自由贸易不看好的态度。

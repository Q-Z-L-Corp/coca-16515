exposition 
英[ˌekspəˈzɪʃn] 美[ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃən]
 
n.博览会；展览会；阐述；
 "

N-COUNT
阐述;解释;说明
An exposition of an idea or theory is a detailed explanation or account of it.
例：The fullest exposition of Coleridge's thought can be found in the States-man's Manual.柯尔律治的思想可以在《政治家手册》中找到最全面的阐述。

N-COUNT
展览会;博览会
An exposition is an exhibition in which something such as goods or works of art are shown to the public.
例：...an art exposition.艺术博览会

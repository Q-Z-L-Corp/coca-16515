weightless 
英[ˈweɪtləs] 美[ˈwetlɪs]
 
adj.没有重量的，失重的，无重力的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
无重量的;轻的
Something that is weightless weighs nothing or seems to weigh nothing.
例：Photons have no mass—they are weightless.光子没有质量——它们是无重的。
例：...weightless silk curtains.轻柔的丝质窗帘

ADJ
失重的
A person or object is weightless when they are in space and the earth's gravity does not affect them, so that they float around.
例：Helen described life in a weightless environment during her period in space.海伦描述了她身处太空时失重状态下的生活。

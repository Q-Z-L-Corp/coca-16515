N-COUNT
（通常指集体）远足，短途旅行
An outing is a short enjoyable trip, usually with a group of people, away from your home, school, or place of work.
例：One evening, she made a rare outing to the local discotheque.她有天晚上破天荒地去了一次当地的迪斯科舞厅。
例：...families on a Sunday afternoon outing.星期天下午远足的家庭

N-COUNT
体育比赛
In sport, an outing is an occasion when a player competes in a particular contest or competition.
例：Playing against Zebre in England's first outing, he suffered a whiplash injury to his neck.在英格兰队首次对阵热布里的比赛中，他颈部受了挥鞭伤。

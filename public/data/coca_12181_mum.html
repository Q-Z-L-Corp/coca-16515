N-FAMILY
妈妈
Your mum is your mother.
例：He misses his mum...他想念他的妈妈。
例：Mum and Dad are coming for lunch...爸爸和妈妈要来吃午饭。

PHRASE
保守秘密;闭口不谈
If you keep mum or stay mum about something, you do not tell anyone about it.
例：He is keeping mum about his feelings on the matter.他闭口不谈他对此事的个人感受。

ADJ
屈从的;顺从的;勉强接受的;逆来顺受的
If you are resigned to an unpleasant situation or fact, you accept it without complaining because you realize that you cannot change it.
例：He is resigned to the noise and mess...他勉强接受了这里的喧嚣和脏乱。
例：Pauline was already resigned to losing her home.保利娜已经无奈地接受了失去家园的事实。

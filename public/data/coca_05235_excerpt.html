N-COUNT
摘录;节选;选段
An excerpt is a short piece of writing or music which is taken from a larger piece.
例：...an excerpt from Tchaikovsky's Nutcracker.柴可夫斯基的《胡桃夹子》选段

V-PASSIVE
摘录;节选
If a long piece of writing or music is excerpted, short pieces from it are printed or played on their own.
例：The readings you heard earlier were excerpted from a new oral history of Pearl Harbor.你之前听到的朗读片段节选自一部新的珍珠港口述史。

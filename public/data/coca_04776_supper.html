supper 
英[ˈsʌpə(r)] 美[ˈsʌpɚ]
 
n.晚饭，晚餐；夜点心；晚餐会；
 "

N-VAR
晚餐;晚饭
Some people refer to the main meal eaten in the early part of the evening as supper .
例：Some guests like to dress for supper.一些客人喜欢穿礼服去赴晚宴。Y See Usage Note at meal. 见 meal 的用法说明

N-VAR
夜宵;宵夜
Supper is a simple meal eaten just before you go to bed at night.
例：She gives the children their supper, then puts them to bed.她让孩子们吃了点夜宵，然后让他们上床睡觉。

PHRASE
打点;疏通
If someone has to sing for their supper, they have to do a job before they are allowed to do something they want to do.
例：They would have to 'sing for their supper' during the exhausting official round of duties.他们不得不在那些耗人精力的官方手续的办理过程中进行疏通。

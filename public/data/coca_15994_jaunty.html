ADJ-GRADED
信心十足的；精力充沛的
If you describe someone or something as jaunty, you mean that they are full of confidence and energy.
例：...a jaunty little man...得意洋洋的小个子男人
例：Tremain's novel is altogether jauntier, more various and energetic than these quotations imply.与这些引文给人的印象相比，特里曼的小说总的来说更加充满自信、富于变化，也更有活力。

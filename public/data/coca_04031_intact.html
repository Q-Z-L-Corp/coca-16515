ADJ
完整无缺的;未受损伤的
Something that is intact is complete and has not been damaged or changed.
例：Most of the cargo was left intact after the explosion...大部分货物在爆炸发生后都完好无损。
例：If the family unit is still intact, the patient frequently does very well.如果家庭仍然完整,病人常常恢复得很好。

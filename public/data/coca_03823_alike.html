alike 
英[əˈlaɪk] 美[əˈlaɪk]
 
adj.同样的，相似的；adv.同样地；两者都，同样地；类似于；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
相同的；相似的
If two or more things are alike, they are similar in some way.
例：We looked very alike.我们长得很像。

ADV-GRADED
相同地；相似地
Alike means in a similar way.
例：They even dressed alike.他们甚至连穿戴都相似。
例：...their assumption that all men and women think alike.他们关于所有男人和女人思考问题的方式都类似的假定

ADV
同等地；同样地
You use alike after mentioning two or more people, groups, or things in order to emphasize that you are referring to both or all of them.
例：The techniques are being applied almost everywhere by big and small firms alike.无论在大公司还是小公司，这些技术的应用几乎无处不在。

ADJ
铭记于心的；刻在脑海中的
If you say that something is engraved on your mind or memory or on your heart, you are emphasizing that you will never forget it, because it has made a very strong impression on you.
例：Her image is engraved upon my heart...她的形象铭刻在我心中。
例：The drowning at Chappaquiddick, now more than 20 years ago, is still engraved in the collective memory of America.直至今日，二十几年前的查帕奎迪克溺亡事件仍然铭记在全体美国人心中。

ineffectual 
英[ˌɪnɪˈfektʃuəl] 美[ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəl]
 
adj.无效的，不起作用的；徒劳无益的；n.无用的人；无一技之长者；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不起作用的;徒劳无益的
If someone or something is ineffectual, they fail to do what they are expected to do or are trying to do.
例：The mayor had become ineffectual in the struggle to clamp down on drugs.市长打击毒品的努力未见成效。
例：...the well-meaning but ineffectual jobs programs of the past.过去的一些出发点很好但却无成效的就业计划

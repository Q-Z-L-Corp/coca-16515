consistency 
英[kənˈsɪstənsi] 美[kənˈsɪstənsi]
 
n.连贯；符合；前后一致；浓度；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
连贯性;一致性
Consistency is the quality or condition of being consistent.
例：He scores goals with remarkable consistency...他的进球一气呵成。
例：There's always a lack of consistency in matters of foreign policy.外交政策总是缺乏连贯性。

N-UNCOUNT
稠度;平滑度
The consistency of a substance is how thick or smooth it is.
例：Dilute the paint with water until it is the consistency of milk...用水把涂料稀释到牛奶般的稠度。
例：I added a little milk to mix the dough to the right consistency.我加了一点儿牛奶，好让面团稠度适中。

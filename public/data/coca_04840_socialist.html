ADJ
社会主义的
Socialist means based on socialism or relating to socialism.
例：...members of the ruling Socialist party...执政的社会主义党党员
例：...low-inflation policies practised by the socialist government...社会主义政府执行的低通货膨胀政策

N-COUNT
社会主义者;社会主义党人
A socialist is a person who believes in socialism or who is a member of a socialist party.
例：The French electorate voted out the socialists.法国大选中社会主义党人落选。

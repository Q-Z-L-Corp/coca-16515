fatten 
英[ˈfætn] 美[ˈfætn]
 
vt.喂肥；养肥（牲畜）；使（钱）增多；使（公司）升值；
 "

V-ERG
(动物)长膘，变肥;催肥，养肥
If an animal is fattened, or if it fattens, it becomes fatter as a result of eating more.
例：The cattle are being fattened for slaughter...喂肥牛群以宰杀。
例：The creature continued to grow and fatten.这个家伙继续长大、变肥。

VERB
使(企业)发财;使(利润)增长;为…牟利
If you say that someone is fattening something such as a business or its profits, you mean that they are increasing the value of the business or its profits, in a way that you disapprove of.
例：They have kept the price of sugar artificially high and so fattened the company's profits.他们一直人为地使糖的价格居高不下，以此为公司牟利。

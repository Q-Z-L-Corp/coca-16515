purchasing 
英[ˈpɜ:tʃəsɪŋ] 美[ˈpɜrtʃəsɪŋ]
 
n.购买，采购；v.购买( purchase的现在分词 )；购买东西；
 "

VERB
买；购买；采购
When you purchase something, you buy it.
例：He purchased a ticket and went up on the top deck...他买了一张票，然后上到顶层甲板。
例：Most of those shares were purchased from brokers.大部分的股份是从经纪人手中购得的。

N-UNCOUNT
购买行为
The purchase of something is the act of buying it.
例：Some of the receipts had been for the purchase of cars.其中一些收据是购买汽车时开的。

N-COUNT
所购之物；购置物
A purchase is something that you buy.
例：She opened the tie box and looked at her purchase. It was silk, with maroon stripes.她打开领带盒，看着她买回的东西：一条带有褐红色条纹的丝制领带。

N-UNCOUNT
紧握；牢牢抓住
If you get a purchase on something, you manage to get a firm grip on it.
例：I got a purchase on the rope and pulled...我紧抓住绳子，用力去拉。
例：I couldn't get any purchase with the screwdriver on the damn screws.我用螺丝刀拧那些该死的螺丝钉时一点也使不上劲儿。

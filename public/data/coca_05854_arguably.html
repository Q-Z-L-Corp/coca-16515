ADV
可论证地;可以说;按理说
You can use arguably when you are stating your opinion or belief, as a way of giving more authority to it.
例：They are arguably the most important band since The Rolling Stones...他们无疑是继滚石之后最出色的乐队。
例：Arguably, 1932 was the year of greatest achievement at the Laboratories.可以说1932年是该实验室最有收获的一年。

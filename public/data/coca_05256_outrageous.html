ADJ-GRADED
令人无法容忍的；骇人的
If you describe something as outrageous, you are emphasizing that it is unacceptable or very shocking.
例：I must apologise for my outrageous behaviour...我必须为自己极端无礼的行为道歉。
例：Charges for local telephone calls are particularly outrageous.本地电话资费贵得出奇。

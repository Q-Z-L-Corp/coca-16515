shore 
英[ʃɔ:(r)] 美[ʃɔr, ʃor]
 
n.岸；滨；国家（尤指濒海国家）；支柱；vt.支撑；支持；
 "

N-COUNT
海岸;河岸;湖滨
The shores or the shore of a sea, lake, or wide river is the land along the edge of it. Someone who is on shore is on the land rather than on a ship.
例：They walked down to the shore.他们走到了湖边。
例：...elephants living on the shores of Lake Kariba...生活在卡里巴湖滨的大象

N-PLURAL
(国家或大洲的)区域，境内
When someone or something reaches the shores of a country or continent, they arrive in that country or continent.
例：It is feared that a similar epidemic will soon reach the shores of Europe...人们担心类似的流行病很快会传到欧洲。
例：This youngster is another destined to leave these shores.这个年轻人是又一个注定要离开这个国家的人。

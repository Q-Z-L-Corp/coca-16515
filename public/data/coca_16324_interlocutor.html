N-COUNT
对话者
Your interlocutor is the person with whom you are having a conversation.
例：Owen had the habit of staring motionlessly at his interlocutor.欧文谈话时有一动不动、目不转睛地盯着对方的习惯。

N-COUNT
(会谈或谈判的)参加者，代表
If a person or organization has a role as an interlocutor in talks or negotiations, they take part or act as a representative in them.
例：...Judge Thomas Pigot QC, recommended that cross examination could be conducted through an interlocutor and as part of the video interview process.王室法律顾问托马斯·皮戈特法官建议盘诘可以通过一位代表进行,并作为录像采访过程的一部分。
例：...key interlocutors in the Middle East conference.中东会议的主要与会代表

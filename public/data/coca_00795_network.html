network 
英[ˈnetwɜ:k] 美[ˈnetwɜrk]
 
n.网；（电视与计算机）网络；网状物；广播网；vt.& vi.将…连接成网络；建立工作关系；vt.把（地方节目）编排到广播网（或电视网）联播；使（全国）联播；（如）用网覆盖；广泛分布；vi.沟通，互助；
 "

N-COUNT
网状物;网状系统
A network of lines, roads, veins, or other long thin things is a large number of them which cross each other or meet at many points.
例：...Strasbourg, with its rambling network of medieval streets...中世纪街道纵横交织的斯特拉斯堡
例：The uterus is supplied with a rich network of blood vessels and nerves.密布的血管和神经网向子宫输送养料。

N-COUNT
(人或机构组成的)网络
A network of people or institutions is a large number of them that have a connection with each other and work together as a system.
例：Distribution of the food is going ahead using a network of local church people and other volunteers...食品正通过当地教会人士和其他志愿者形成的网络分发下去。
例：He is keen to point out the benefits which the family network can provide.他很乐意指出家庭网络可以带来的好处。

N-COUNT
网络;联动系统
A particular network is a system of things which are connected and which operate together. For example, a computer network consists of a number of computers that are part of the same system.
例：...a computer network with 154 terminals...有154个终端的计算机网络
例：Huge sections of the rail network are out of action.铁路网的很多区段停运。

N-COUNT
广播网;电视网
A radio or television network is a company or group of companies that broadcasts radio or television programmes throughout an area.
例：An American network says it has obtained the recordings.美国一家广播网声称已经得到了那些录音材料。
例：...Fuji Television Network, a highly successful commercial station.富士电视网,一家非常成功的商业电视台

VERB
对(电视或广播节目)进行联播
When a television or radio programme is networked, it is broadcast at the same time by several different television companies.
例：Lumsdon would like to see his programme sold and networked...拉姆斯敦希望看到自己的节目卖出，并进行联播。
例：He had once had his own networked chat show.他曾经拥有自己的电视联播访谈节目。

VERB
建立工作关系网;交际
If you network, you try to meet new people who might be useful to you in your job.
例：In business, it is important to network with as many people as possible on a face to face basis.在生意场上，通过与尽可能多的人面对面地打交道来建立工作关系很重要。

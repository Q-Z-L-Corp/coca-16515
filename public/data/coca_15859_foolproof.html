ADJ-GRADED
(计划、机器等)十分简单的，容易操作的，万无一失的
Something such as a plan or a machine that is foolproof is so well designed, easy to understand, or easy to use that it cannot go wrong or be used wrongly.
例：The system is not 100 per cent foolproof...这个系统并非是万无一失。
例：I spent the day working out a foolproof plan to save him.我花了一天的时间想出了一个营救他的周全方案。

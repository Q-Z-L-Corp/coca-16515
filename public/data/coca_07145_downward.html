downward 
英[ˈdaʊnwəd] 美[ˈdaʊnwərd]
 
adj.向下的，往下的；adv.向下地，下行地；
 "

ADJ
下降的;向下的
A downward movement or look is directed towards a lower place or a lower level.
例：...a firm downward movement of the hands.双手有力的向下摆动

ADJ
趋向没落的;衰退的;局势变坏的
If you refer to a downward trend, you mean that something is decreasing or that a situation is getting worse.
例：The downward trend in home ownership is likely to continue.拥有房屋所有权者人数减少的趋势可能会持续。
例：...a decline in the economy, resulting in a general downward spiral.经济上的衰退导致了总体上的螺旋式下降。

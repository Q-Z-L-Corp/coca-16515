individualism 
英[ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəm] 美[ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəˌlɪzəm]
 
n.个人主义，利己主义；自由放任主义；个性，独特性；不干涉主义；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
个性;特立独行
You use individualism to refer to the behaviour of someone who likes to think and do things in their own way, rather than imitating other people.
例：He is struck by what he calls the individualism of American officers.他称其为美国军官的个性给他留下了很深的印象。

N-UNCOUNT
(国家对经济、政治的)不干涉主义，自由放任主义
Individualism is the belief that economics and politics should not be controlled by the state.
例：...the strong individualism in their political culture.他们的政治文化中浓厚的个人自由主义

infinitely 
英[ˈɪnfɪnətli] 美[ˈɪnfənɪtlɪ]
 
adv.无限地，无穷地；极其；
 "

ADJ
极多的;极度的
If you describe something as infinite, you are emphasizing that it is extremely great in amount or degree.
例：...an infinite variety of landscapes...变化万千的风景
例：With infinite care, John shifted position...约翰小心翼翼地挪动了位置。

ADJ
无限的;无穷的;无边的
Something that is infinite has no limit, end, or edge.
例：...an infinite number of atoms...无穷多的原子
例：Obviously, no company has infinite resources.显然,没有哪个公司的资源是无限的。

N-UNCOUNT
自尊(心)
Self-respect is a feeling of confidence and pride in your own ability and worth.
例：They have lost not only their jobs, but their homes, their self-respect and even their reason for living.他们不仅丢掉了工作，还失去了居所、自尊乃至生存的理由。

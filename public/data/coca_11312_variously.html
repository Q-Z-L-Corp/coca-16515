ADV
不同地;以各种方式
You can use variously to introduce a number of different ways in which something can be described.
例：...the crowds, which were variously estimated at two to several thousand...人数估计不一、从两千人到数千人不等的人群
例：The family has been described variously as crass, bigoted, racist and plain boring.人们对这家人有各种不同的评价：愚蠢、顽固、有种族偏见和无聊透顶，不一而足。

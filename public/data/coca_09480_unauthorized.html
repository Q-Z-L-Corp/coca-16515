ADJ
未经授权的；未经许可的
If something is unauthorized, it has been produced or is happening without official permission.
例：...a new unauthorized biography of the Russian President.一本新的未经授权的俄罗斯总统传记
例：...the unauthorized use of a military vehicle...未经许可使用军车

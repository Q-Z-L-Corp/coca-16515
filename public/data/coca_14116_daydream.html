daydream 
英[ˈdeɪdri:m] 美[ˈdeˌdrim]
 
n.白日梦；幻想，空想；vi.做白日梦；
 "

VERB
做白日梦;幻想;空想
If you daydream, you think about pleasant things for a period of time, usually about things that you would like to happen.
例：Do you work hard for success rather than daydream about it?...你是否付出艰苦努力以取得成功，而不是只在那里做白日梦？
例：He daydreams of being a famous journalist...他幻想着自己成为著名记者。

N-COUNT
白日梦;幻想;空想
A daydream is a series of pleasant thoughts, usually about things that you would like to happen.
例：He escaped into daydreams of beautiful women...他躲进白日梦中幻想美女。
例：The idea for the story came to him in a daydream.这个故事的念头是他做白日梦的时候突然想到的。

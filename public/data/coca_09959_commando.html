commando 
英[kəˈmɑ:ndəʊ] 美[kəˈmændoʊ]
 
n.突击队，突击队员；[史]（南非布尔战争时代的）义勇队；
 "

N-COUNT
突击队
A commando is a group of soldiers who have been specially trained to carry out surprise attacks.
例：...a small commando of marines...海军陆战队突击小组
例：The hostages were freed in the commando raid.在突击队的突袭行动中，人质获释。

N-COUNT
突击队员
A commando is a soldier who is a member of a commando.
例：Captain David Clement and 150 commandos stormed the port this morning.戴维·克莱门特上尉和150名突击队员今天早上对港口展开突袭。

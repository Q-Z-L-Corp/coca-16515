beloved 
英[bɪˈlʌvd] 美[bɪˈlʌvɪd, -ˈlʌvd]
 
adj.被热爱的；亲爱的；为…所爱的；n.心爱的人，可爱的人；情人；亲爱的教友；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
深爱的；钟爱的
A beloved person, thing, or place is one that you feel great affection for.
例：He lost his beloved wife last year...他去年痛失爱妻。
例：The rose is the most romantic of flowers, beloved of poets, singers, and artists.玫瑰是最浪漫的花，深受诗人、歌手和艺术家的喜爱。

N-SING
爱人；心爱的人
Your beloved is the person that you love.
例：He takes his beloved into his arms.他将心爱之人拥入怀中。

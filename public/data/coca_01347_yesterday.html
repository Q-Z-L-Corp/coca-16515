ADV
昨天
You use yesterday to refer to the day before today.
例：She left yesterday...她昨天走了。
例：Yesterday she announced that she is quitting her job.昨天她宣布打算辞职。

N-UNCOUNT
过去；（尤指）不久前，近日，日前
You can refer to the past, especially the recent past, as yesterday .
例：The worker of today is different from the worker of yesterday.如今的工人和以往的不同了。
例：...a world without yesterdays or tomorrows.没有过去和未来的世界

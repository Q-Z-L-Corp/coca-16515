circulate 
英[ˈsɜ:kjəleɪt] 美[ˈsɜrkjəleɪt]
 
vt.& vi.（使）循环；（使）流通；（使）流传；散布；vi.传送，传递，传阅；
 "

V-ERG
(文章等)传递，传阅，散发
If a piece of writing circulates or is circulated, copies of it are passed round among a group of people.
例：The document was previously circulated in New York at the United Nations...这份文件先前在纽约的联合国总部传阅过。
例：Public employees, teachers and liberals are circulating a petition for his recall...公务员、教师和自由派人士正在散发请愿书，要求将他召回。

V-ERG
(谣言等)流传;散布
If something such as a rumour circulates or is circulated, the people in a place tell it to each other.
例：Rumours were already beginning to circulate that the project might have to be abandoned...已传出谣言说，该项目可能会被迫取消。
例：I deeply resented those sort of rumours being circulated at a time of deeply personal grief.我特别憎恨那种在人家伤心透顶的时候还在传播的谣言。

VERB
(在密闭场所或系统内)循环，流转，传播
When something circulates, it moves easily and freely within a closed place or system.
例：...a virus which circulates via the bloodstream and causes ill health in a variety of organs...通过血液循环传播、造成多种器官病变的病毒
例：Cooking odors can circulate throughout the entire house.做饭的香味可以传到家里的各个角落。

VERB
(在聚会上)周旋，往来应酬
If you circulate at a party, you move among the guests and talk to different people.
例：Let me get you something to drink, then I must circulate.我给你找点喝的，然后我得去应酬一下。

artistry 
英[ˈɑ:tɪstri] 美[ˈɑrtɪstri]
 
n.艺术之性质；技艺；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
艺术才能；艺术造诣
Artistry is the creative skill of an artist, writer, actor, or musician.
例：...his artistry as a cellist.他作为大提琴演奏家的艺术造诣
例：...portrait sculptors of considerable skill and artistry.具有高超技巧和艺术造诣的人像雕塑家

N-UNCOUNT
技能；技巧
You can use artistry to refer to a high level of skill in a sport or activity.
例：John McEnroe beat Jimmy Connors with the most complete display of artistry the game had ever seen...约翰·麦肯罗将球技发挥得淋漓尽致，从而击败了吉米·康纳斯，这在比赛中前所未见。
例：Tea processing requires both technique and artistry.茶叶加工对工艺和手法都有要求。

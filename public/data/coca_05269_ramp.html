n.土堤斜坡；斜道；敲诈；（装车或上下飞机的）活动梯；vt.&vi.敲诈；vi.蔓延；

N-COUNT
斜面；斜坡；坡面
A ramp is a sloping surface between two places that are at different levels.
例：Lillian was coming down the ramp from the museum.莉莲正从博物馆那边的斜坡上走下来。
例：...a ramp to facilitate entry into the pool from a wheelchair.为方便乘轮椅者进入泳池而设的斜面

N-COUNT
（车辆进出主路时用的）匝道，坡道
An entrance ramp is a road which cars use to drive onto a major road, and an exit ramp is a road which cars use to drive off a major road.

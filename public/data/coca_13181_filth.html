filth 
英[fɪlθ] 美[fɪlθ]
 
n.污物；污秽；猥亵；淫猥；

N-UNCOUNT
肮脏;污秽;污物
Filth is a disgusting amount of dirt.
例：The living-room floor was littered with filth and tin cans...客厅地上丢满了脏东西和罐头听。
例：Thousands of tons of filth and sewage pour into the Ganges every day.每天都有成千上万吨污物和废水排入恒河。

N-UNCOUNT
淫秽;秽亵;脏话
People refer to words or pictures, usually ones relating to sex, as filth when they think they are very disgusting and rude.
例：The dialogue was all filth and innuendo.这段对话全是下流的言辞和性暗示。
例：...pornographic filth.色情淫秽出版物

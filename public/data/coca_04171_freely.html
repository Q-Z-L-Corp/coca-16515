freely 
英[ˈfri:li] 美[ˈfrili]
 
adv.自由地，随意地，免费地；直率地；免费地；慷慨地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
经常地;大量地
Freely means many times or in large quantities.
例：We have referred freely to his ideas...我们频繁地提到了他的观点。
例：George was spending very freely.乔治花钱大手大脚。

ADV-GRADED
坦率地;无顾忌地
If you can talk freely, you can talk without needing to be careful about what you say.
例：She wondered whether he had someone to whom he could talk freely.她不知道他是否有可以倾心交谈的对象。

ADV-GRADED
自愿地;心甘情愿地
If someone gives or does something freely, they give or do it willingly, without being ordered or forced to do it.
例：Danny shared his knowledge freely with anyone interested...丹尼十分乐意地和所有感兴趣的人分享他的知识。
例：Williams freely admits he lives for racing.威廉斯坦承他为赛车而活。

ADV-GRADED
无阻碍地;顺畅地
If something or someone moves freely, they move easily and smoothly, without any obstacles or resistance.
例：The clay court was slippery and he was unable to move freely...红土网球场很滑，他不能自如地移动。
例：You must allow the clubhead to swing freely.必须让杆头一气呵成地挥出。

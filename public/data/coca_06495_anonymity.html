anonymity 
英[ˌænəˈnɪməti] 美[ˌænəˈnɪmɪti]
 
n.匿名；作者不详；匿名者；无名者；
 "

ADJ
匿名的;无名的;不知名的
If you remain anonymous when you do something, you do not let people know that you were the person who did it.
例：You can remain anonymous if you wish...你愿意的话可以不透露姓名。
例：An anonymous benefactor stepped in to provide the prize money.一个匿名捐助者施以援手提供了奖金。

ADJ
不记名的；不具名的
Something that is anonymous does not reveal who you are.
例：Of course, that would have to be by anonymous vote.当然，那必须通过不记名投票的方式。

ADJ-GRADED
无特色的;平淡无奇的
If you describe a place as anonymous, you dislike it because it has no unusual or interesting features and seems unwelcoming.
例：...the most anonymous part of north-west Washington...华盛顿西北部最无特色的地区
例：It's nice to stay in a home rather than in an anonymous holiday villa.呆在家里比呆在单调乏味的度假别墅里要惬意得多。

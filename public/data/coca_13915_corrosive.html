corrosive 
英[kəˈrəʊsɪv] 美[kəˈroʊsɪv]
 
adj.腐蚀性的；侵蚀性的；（对社会、个人情感等）有害的；（语言）激烈的；n.腐蚀性物品；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
腐蚀性的;侵蚀性的
A corrosive substance is able to destroy solid materials by a chemical reaction.
例：Sodium and sulphur are highly corrosive.钠和硫磺腐蚀性很强。

ADJ-GRADED
逐渐起破坏作用的;损害性的
If you say that something has a corrosive effect, you mean that it gradually causes serious harm.
例：...the corrosive effects of inflation.通货膨胀的破坏作用

VERB
弹奏，拨弄(吉他等弦乐器)
If you strum a stringed instrument such as a guitar, you play it by moving your fingers backwards and forwards across the strings.
例：In the corner, one youth sat alone, softly strumming a guitar...一个青年独自坐在角落里，轻轻地弹着吉他。
例：Vaska strummed away on his guitar.瓦斯卡弹拨着自己的吉他。

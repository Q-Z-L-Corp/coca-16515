announcer 
英[əˈnaʊnsə(r)] 美[əˈnaʊnsɚ]
 
n.宣告者；播音员；
 "

N-COUNT
（广播电台或电视台的）广播员，播音员
An announcer is someone who introduces programmes on radio or television or who reads the text of a radio or television advertisement.
例：The radio announcer said it was nine o'clock...电台播音员报时9点整。
例：In the commercial an announcer interviews a Texan.在这则广告中播音员采访了一个得克萨斯人。

N-COUNT
（火车站、机场等的）广播员，播报员
The announcer at a railway station or airport is the person who makes the announcements.
例：The announcer apologised for the delay.播报员就晚点表示歉意。

festoon 
英[feˈstu:n] 美[fɛˈstun]
 
n.[建]垂花饰；花彩装饰物，彩旗；v.给…结彩，在…饰以花彩；使成花彩形；
 "

VERB
给…饰花彩;结彩于
If something is festooned with, for example, lights, balloons, or flowers, large numbers of these things are hung from it or wrapped around it, especially in order to decorate it.
例：The temples are festooned with lights.灯彩装饰着庙宇。
例：...a lamppost festooned in political stickers.贴满政治招贴画的电线杆

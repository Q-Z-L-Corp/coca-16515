justified 
英[ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪd] 美[ˈdʒʌstəˌfaɪd]
 
adj.有正当理由的，合理的；事出有因的；v.调整；证明…有道理( justify的过去式和过去分词)；

ADJ-GRADED
（决定、行为或想法）正当的，合理的
If you describe a decision, action, or idea as justified, you think it is reasonable and acceptable.
例：In my opinion, the decision was wholly justified.我认为，这个决定完全合理。

ADJ
（做…）有正当理由的
If you think that someone is justified in doing something, you think that their reasons for doing it are good and valid.
例：He's absolutely justified in resigning. He was treated shamefully.他完全有理由辞职。他受到了侮辱。

integrity 
英[ɪnˈtegrəti] 美[ɪnˈtɛɡrɪti]
 
n.正直，诚实；完整；[计算机]保存；健全；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
正直;诚实
If you have integrity, you are honest and firm in your moral principles.
例：I have always regarded him as a man of integrity...我一直认为他正直诚实。
例：He was praised for his fairness and high integrity.他的公正和正直操守受人称道。

N-UNCOUNT
完整;完全;完善
The integrity of something such as a group of people or a text is its state of being a united whole.
例：Separatist movements are a threat to the integrity of the nation.分离主义运动对国家统一是个威胁。

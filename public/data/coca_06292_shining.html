ADJ
(成就)辉煌的，值得钦佩的;(品质)出色的,卓越的
A shining achievement or quality is a very good one which should be greatly admired.
例：She is a shining example to us all...她是我们所有人的光辉榜样。
例：The Ariane space-rocket project has had a shining success.阿里安航天火箭项目取得了辉煌的成功。

constellation 
英[ˌkɒnstəˈleɪʃn] 美[ˌkɑnstəˈleɪʃn]
 
n.星座；一群杰出人物；一系列（相关的想法、事物）；一群（相关的人）；
 "

N-COUNT
星座
A constellation is a group of stars which form a pattern and have a name.
例：...a planet orbiting a star in the constellation of Cepheus.围绕仙王座中一颗恒星旋转的行星

N-COUNT
一群，一组(相似的事物)
A constellation of similar things is a group of them.
例：Most patients have a constellation of diseases, with few clear-cut distinctions between them...大多数患者的病症都很相似，鲜有明显的区别。
例：The largest gallery contains at its centre a constellation of photographs called My Wishes.最大的画廊在中心位置展出了一组名为《我的心愿》的摄影作品。

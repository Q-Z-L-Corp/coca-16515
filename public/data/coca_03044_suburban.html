suburban 
英[səˈbɜ:bən] 美[səˈbɜrbən]
 
adj.城郊的，在郊区的，城郊住宅区的；土气的；见闻不广的；n.郊区居民；
 "

ADJ
郊区的;城外的
Suburban means relating to a suburb.
例：...a comfortable suburban home.城外舒适的住所
例：...a suburban shopping centre in Sydney.悉尼城郊的购物中心

ADJ-GRADED
平淡乏味的;呆板的;传统的
If you describe something as suburban, you mean that it is dull and conventional.
例：His clothes are conservative and suburban.他衣着保守而传统。
例：...ghastly good taste and suburban gentility.令人厌恶的高级品位和无聊呆板的绅士风度

N-COUNT
（尤指政党或政府的）宣言，声明
A manifesto is a statement published by a person or group of people, especially a political party, or a government, in which they say what their aims and policies are.
例：The Tories are currently drawing up their election manifesto...保守党人目前正在起草竞选宣言。
例：His manifesto promised measures to protect them.他在宣言里保证要为他们采取保护措施。

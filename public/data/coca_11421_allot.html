allot 
英[əˈlɒt] 美[əˈlɑt]
 
vt.分配，摊派给；分派，派给；拨给；指定（款项等）作某种用途；

VERB
分配;分派;划拨
If something is allotted to someone, it is given to them as their share.
例：The seats are allotted to the candidates who have won the most votes...席位被分配给了赢得选票最多的候选人。
例：We were allotted half an hour to address the committee.给了我们半个小时向委员会作陈述。

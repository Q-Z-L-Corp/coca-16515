self 
英[self] 美[sɛlf]
 
n.自己；本人；本性；私利；adj.同一的；纯净的；单一的；vt.使自花授精；使近亲繁殖；vi.自花授精；
 "

N-COUNT
自身;本性;本我
Your self is your basic personality or nature, especially considered in terms of what you are really like as a person.
例：You're looking more like your usual self...你现在看上去较像你平时的样子了。
例：She was back to her old self again.她又恢复了老样子。

N-COUNT
个性;自我
A person's self is the essential part of their nature which makes them different from everyone and everything else.
例：I want to explore and get in touch with my inner self...我想认真探寻、了解内心的自我。
例：The face is the true self visible to others.这副面孔是他人所能见到的自我真实的一面。

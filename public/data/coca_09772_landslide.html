N-COUNT
（选举中）大获全胜，一面倒的胜利
A landslide is a victory in an election in which a person or political party gets far more votes or seats than their opponents.
例：He won last month's presidential election by a landslide...他以压倒性多数赢得上月的总统选举。
例：The NLD won a landslide victory in the elections five months ago.全国民主联盟在5个月前的选举中获得压倒性胜利。

N-COUNT
塌方；山崩；滑坡
A landslide is a large amount of earth and rocks falling down a cliff or the side of a mountain.
例：The storm caused landslides and flooding in Savona.风暴致使萨沃纳发生塌方和洪灾。

ADJ
火山的;由火山引发的
Volcanic means coming from or created by volcanoes.
例：Over 200 people have been killed by volcanic eruptions...火山喷发已导致200多人丧生。
例：Mount Unzen has been spewing out volcanic ash, gas, and rock today...云仙山今天一直在向外喷涌火山灰、气体和火山岩。

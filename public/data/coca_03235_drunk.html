drunk 
英[drʌŋk] 美[drʌŋk]
 
adj.醉的；陶醉的；沉醉；飘飘然；n.醉汉，酒鬼，酗酒者；v.喝（酒）( drink的过去分词)；饮；喝酒；（尤指）酗酒；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
喝醉的
Someone who is drunk has drunk so much alcohol that they cannot speak clearly or behave sensibly.
例：Stewart could not remember exactly why he had done it because he was so drunk...斯图尔特记不清他为什么要那样做了,因为他烂醉如泥。
例：I got drunk and had to be carried home...我喝醉了，只好让人把我送回家。

N-COUNT
醉汉,酒鬼;酗酒者
A drunk is someone who is drunk or frequently gets drunk.
例：A drunk lay in the alley.一个酒鬼躺在小胡同里。

ADJ-GRADED
陶醉的;兴奋的;沉醉的
If you are drunk with a strong emotion or an experience, you are in a state of great excitement because of it.
例：They are currently drunk with success...他们现在正陶醉于成功之中。
例：I felt drunk with the excitement of life.当时我沉醉于生活中的精彩刺激。

Drunk is the past participle of drink.

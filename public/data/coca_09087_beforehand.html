ADV
预先；事先；提前
If you do something beforehand, you do it earlier than a particular event.
例：How could she tell beforehand that I was going to go out?...她怎么会事先知道我要出去？
例：Saunas can be hazardous if misused. Avoid a big meal beforehand.洗桑拿浴如果方法不当会有危险，浴前切忌饱食。

enunciate 
英[ɪˈnʌnsieɪt] 美[ɪˈnʌnsiˌet]
 
vi.&vt.（清晰地）发音；确切地说明；
 "

VERB
（清晰地）念（词）；（清晰地）发（音）
When you enunciate a word or part of a word, you pronounce it clearly.
例：His voice was harsh as he enunciated each word carefully...他一字一顿，听上去很生硬。
例：She enunciates very slowly and carefully.她缓慢、仔细而又清晰地读着。

VERB
阐明；清楚而准确地阐释
When you enunciate a thought, idea, or plan, you express it very clearly and precisely.
例：He was ever ready to enunciate his views to all who would listen.他总是乐意向所有愿意倾听的人阐明他的观点。

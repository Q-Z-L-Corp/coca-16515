N-VAR
绣花;刺绣品
Embroidery consists of designs stitched into cloth.
例：The shorts had blue embroidery over the pockets...这条短裤的口袋上有蓝色刺绣。
例：The panel contains an embroidery.这块饰片上有刺绣。

N-UNCOUNT
刺绣
Embroidery is the activity of stitching designs onto cloth.
例：She learned sewing, knitting and embroidery.她学了缝纫、编织和刺绣。

devour 
英[dɪˈvaʊə(r)] 美[dɪˈvaʊr]
 
vt.狼吞虎咽地吃光；吞没，毁灭；全神贯注地看；
 "

VERB
狼吞虎咽地吃;吞食
If a person or animal devours something, they eat it quickly and eagerly.
例：A medium-sized dog will devour at least one can of food per day...中型犬每天至少要吃掉一罐狗粮。
例：She devoured half an apple pie.她狼吞虎咽地吃下了半个苹果派。

VERB
如饥似渴地阅读;热切地看
If you devour a book or magazine, for example, you read it quickly and with great enthusiasm.
例：She began devouring newspapers when she was only 12.年仅 12 岁时，她就开始如饥似渴地阅读报纸。

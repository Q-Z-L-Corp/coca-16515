cluster 
英[ˈklʌstə(r)] 美[ˈklʌstɚ]
 
n.丛；簇，串；群；[语言]辅音群；vi.丛生；群聚；vt.使密集，使聚集；
 "

N-COUNT
(人或物的)组，群，簇
A cluster of people or things is a small group of them close together.
例：...clusters of men in formal clothes...一群群身着正装的男人
例：There's no town here, just a cluster of shops, cabins and motels at the side of the highway.这儿没有城镇，只在公路边上有一片商店、小木屋和汽车旅馆。

VERB
(人)聚集
If people cluster together, they gather together in a small group.
例：The passengers clustered together in small groups...乘客们三五成群聚在一起。
例：The children clustered around me.孩子们聚集在我的周围。

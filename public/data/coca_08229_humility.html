N-UNCOUNT
谦逊;谦虚;谦恭
Someone who has humility is not proud and does not believe they are better than other people.
例：...a deep sense of humility...极为谦虚
例：For a long time he still thought like a millionaire but he has humility now.在很长一段时间里，他仍以一个百万富翁的方式思维，但他现在变得谦恭了。

backfire 
英[ˌbækˈfaɪə(r)] 美[ˈbækˌfaɪr]
 
vi.发生回火；适得其反，事与愿违的结果；n.逆火；
 "

VERB
事与愿违；适得其反
If a plan or project backfires, it has the opposite result to the one that was intended.
例：The President's tactics could backfire...总统的策略可能会适得其反。
例：It all backfired on me!一切都与我的愿望背道而驰！

VERB
（车辆或其发动机）逆火，回火
When a motor vehicle or its engine backfires, it produces an explosion in the exhaust pipe.
例：The car backfired.车回火了。

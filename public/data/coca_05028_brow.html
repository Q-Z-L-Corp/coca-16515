brow 
英[braʊ] 美[braʊ]
 
n.眉，眉毛；前额；容貌，表情；山脊；
 "

N-COUNT
额；额头
Your brow is your forehead.
例：He wiped his brow with the back of his hand...他用手背擦了擦前额。
例：She wrinkled her brow inquisitively.她好奇地皱起了眉头。

N-COUNT
眉；眉毛
Your brows are your eyebrows.
例：He had thick brown hair and shaggy brows.他一头浓密的棕色头发，眉毛乱糟糟的。

N-COUNT
山脊；坡顶
The brow of a hill is the top part of it.
例：He was on the look-out just below the brow of the hill.他就在山脊下负责警戒。

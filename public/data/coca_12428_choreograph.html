choreograph 
英[ˈkɒriəgrɑ:f] 美[ˈkɔriəgræf]
 
v.设计舞蹈动作；
 "

VERB
为(芭蕾舞等)设计舞蹈动作;为…编舞
When someone choreographs a ballet or other dance, they invent the steps and movements and tell the dancers how to perform them.
例：Achim had choreographed the dance in Act II himself...阿希姆自己设计了第2幕的舞蹈动作。
例：She has danced, choreographed, lectured and taught all over the world.她在世界各地跳过舞、编过舞、讲过学、也教过书。

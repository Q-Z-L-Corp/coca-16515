 "

N-SING
一致看法;共识
A consensus is general agreement among a group of people.
例：The consensus amongst the world's scientists is that the world is likely to warm up over the next few decades...世界各国科学家一致认为在未来几十年里地球有可能变暖。
例：The question of when the troops should leave would be decided by consensus.部队应该何时撤离的问题将由大家共同决定。

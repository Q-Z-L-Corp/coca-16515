PREP
除了…之外
You use except to introduce the only thing or person that a statement does not apply to, or a fact that prevents a statement from being completely true.
例：I wouldn't have accepted anything except a job in Europe...除了到欧洲工作外，我本来什么都不会接受。
例：I don't take any drugs whatsoever, except aspirin for colds...我什么药都不吃，除了感冒时吃阿司匹林。

PREP-PHRASE
除了;除…之外;唯有
You use except for to introduce the only thing or person that prevents a statement from being completely true.
例：He hadn't eaten a thing except for one forkful of salad...除了一餐叉色拉，他什么都没吃。
例：Everyone was late, except for Richard.除了理查德，其他所有人都迟到了。

whisker 
英[ˈwɪskə(r)] 美[ˈhwɪskɚ, ˈwɪs-]
 
n.细须；连鬓胡子；〈美俚〉腮帮子；[化]须晶；
 "

N-COUNT
(猫、鼠等动物的)胡须
The whiskers of an animal such as a cat or a mouse are the long stiff hairs that grow near its mouth.

N-PLURAL
连鬓胡子;络腮胡子
You can refer to the hair on a man's face, especially on the sides of his face, as his whiskers .
例：...wild, savage-looking fellows, with large whiskers, unshaven beards, and dirty faces.长着浓密的络腮胡子，胡须未刮且脸上脏兮兮的看起来很粗野的家伙

N-SING
微小的数量；一丝；一点儿
You can use whisker in expressions such as by a whisker or within a whisker of to indicate that something happened or is true, but only by a very small amount or degree.
例：A new pet census showed that cats now outnumber dogs by a whisker (7 million to 6.9 million)...新的宠物普查显示出猫的数量略微超过了狗的数量(700万比690万)。
例：She came within a whisker of taking a gold medal...她离金牌只有一步之遥。

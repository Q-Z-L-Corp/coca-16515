incomparable 
英[ɪnˈkɒmprəbl] 美[ɪnˈkɑmprəbl]
 
adj.无双的；无敌的，无比的；无可比拟；无与伦比；
 "

ADJ
极其好的;令人叫绝的
If you describe someone or something as incomparable, you mean that they are extremely good or impressive.
例：...a play starring the incomparable Edith Evans...由演技精湛的伊迪丝·埃文斯主演的戏剧
例：The views from the house are incomparable.从房子里向外看到的景色非常优美。

ADJ
绝伦的;无比的;无双的
You use incomparable to emphasize that someone or something has a good quality to a great degree.
例：It was a performance of incomparable brilliance.那是场精彩绝伦的演出。
例：...an area of incomparable beauty.有绝世美景的地区

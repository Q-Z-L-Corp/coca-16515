continually 
英[kən'tɪnjʊəlɪ] 美[kənˈtɪnjʊəlɪ]
 
adv.不停地；持续地；屡屡地；一再地；
 "

ADJ
不停的;不间断的
A continual process or situation happens or exists without stopping.
例：The school has been in continual use since 1883...自1883年以来，该校从未停办过。
例：They felt continual pressure to perform well...他们不断感受到压力，促使他们好好表演。

ADJ
一再的;反复的;频繁的
Continual events happen again and again.
例：...the government's continual demands for cash to finance its chronic deficit...政府为补贴长期的财政赤字而对资金的持续需求
例：She suffered continual police harassment.她不断受到警方的骚扰。

ADJ
唯一的；独一无二的
Something that is unique is the only one of its kind.
例：Each person's signature is unique...每个人的签名都是独一无二的。
例：The area has its own unique language, Catalan.这个地区有自己单独的语言，加泰罗尼亚语。

ADJ-GRADED
独特的；无与伦比的
You can use unique to describe things that you admire because they are very unusual and special.
例：Brett's vocals are just unique...布雷特的歌声无与伦比。
例：Kauffman was a woman of unique talent and determination.考夫曼是一个有着超群才智和决断力的女子。

ADJ
独有的；特有的
If something is unique to one thing, person, group, or place, it concerns or belongs only to that thing, person, group, or place.
例：No one knows for sure why adolescence is unique to humans...没有人确切地知道为什么只有人类才会经历青春期。
例：This interesting and charming creature is unique to Borneo.这种有趣迷人的动物是婆罗洲独有的。

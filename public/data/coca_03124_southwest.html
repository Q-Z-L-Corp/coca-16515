southwest 
英[ˌsaʊθ'west] 美[saʊθˈwɛst, saʊˈwɛst]
 
n.西南，西南方，；西南部；adv.向西南；adj.西南的，西南部的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
西南;西南方
The south-west is the direction which is halfway between south and west.
例：...the village of Popplewell, some six miles to the south-west.位于西南方约6英里处的波普尔维尔村

N-SING
(地方、国家或地区的)西南部
The south-west of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the south-west.
例：...the mountains in the south west of the USA.美国西南部的山脉

ADV
向西南方;朝西南
If you go south-west, you travel towards the south-west.
例：We took a plane south-west across the Anatolian plateau to Cappadocia.我们乘飞机向着西南方向越过安纳托利亚高原，前往卡帕多西亚。

ADV
位于…的西南方;在…的西南
Something that is south-west of a place is positioned to the south-west of it.
例：...a gold mine at Orkney, south-west of Johannesburg.位于约翰内斯堡西南奥克尼地区的一座金矿

ADJ
西南部的；西南方的
The south-west part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the south-west.
例：...a light aircraft crash near Stranraer in South-West Scotland.在苏格兰西南部斯特兰拉尔附近发生的一次轻型飞机的坠机事故
例：...in the south-west corner of my garden.在我家花园的西南角

ADJ
(风)来自西南方的
A south-west wind is a wind that blows from the south-west.

COCA TOP 20000 words - same 




same 
英[seɪm] 美[sem]
 
adj.同一的；相同的，同样的；pron.同一事物；同样的人；（和…）同样的事物；（数目、颜色、大小、质量等）相同；adv.同样，类似；
 "

ADJ
同样的;相同的
If two or more things, actions, or qualities are the same, or if one is the same as another, they are very like each other in some way.
例：The houses were all the same — square, close to the street, needing paint...房子都一模一样——方方正正、临近街道、需要粉刷。
例：In essence, all computers are the same...所有的电脑其实都一样。

PHR-CONJ-SUBORD
与…相似;与…一样
If something is happening the same as something else, the two things are happening in a way that is similar or exactly the same.
例：I mean, it's a relationship, the same as a marriage is a relationship...我的意思是它是一种人际关系，就如同婚姻也是一种人际关系一样。
例：I want to go home having won a game of football the same as you leave the ground and you want to go away with your team having won...你希望离开赛场的时候自己的球队取得了胜利，同样我也想赢了一场球赛再回家。

ADJ
同一的;同样的
You use same to indicate that you are referring to only one place, time, or thing, and not to different ones.
例：Bernard works at the same institution as Arlette...伯纳德和阿莱特在同一家机构工作。
例：It's impossible to get everybody together at the same time...让所有人同时聚在一起是不可能的。

ADJ
无变化的;一成不变的;千篇一律的
Something that is still the same has not changed in any way.
例：Taking ingredients from the same source means the beers stay the same...取材自同一原材料意味着啤酒的口味始终如一。
例：Only 17% said the economy would improve, but 25% believed it would stay the same.仅 17% 的人说经济状况会改善，却有25% 的人认为经济会维持老样子。

PRON
刚才提到的;上述的
You use the same to refer to something that has previously been mentioned or suggested.
例：We made the decision which was right for us. Other parents must do the same...我们作出了对于我们而言正确的决定。其他父母也必须这么做。
例：In the United States small specialised bookshops survive quite well. The same applies to small publishers...美国的小型专业书店经营状况不错。小型出版社也是如此。

PRON
(用于商业信件、法案等文件中)上述事物，上述情况
You use same to refer to something that has already been mentioned in a document such as a business letter or bill.
例：Wrist watches: £100. Inscription of same: £15.腕表: 100英镑。在其上刻字: 15英镑。

CONVENTION
我也是;我也一样
You say 'same here' in order to suggest that you feel the same way about something as the person who has just spoken to you, or that you have done the same thing.
例：'Nice to meet you,' said Michael. 'Same here,' said Mary Ann...“很高兴见到你，”迈克尔说。“我也是，”玛丽·安说。
例：'I hate going into stores.' — 'Same here,' said William.“我讨厌进商店。”——“我也一样，”威廉说。

CONVENTION
你也一样，你也是(用于致以同样的祝愿)
You say 'same to you' in response to someone who wishes you well with something.
例：'Have a nice Easter.' — 'And the same to you Bridie.'“复活节快乐。”——“你也一样，布赖迪。”
例：'Goodbye, then, and thanks. Good luck.' — 'The same to you.'“那么，再见，谢谢。祝你好运。”——“也祝你好运。”

PHRASE
同样的(饮品)再来一份
You say 'same again' when you want to order another drink of the same kind as the one you have just had.
例：Give Roger another pint, Imogen, and I'll have the same again.再给罗杰来一杯，伊莫金，我也再来一份同样的。

PHRASE
仍然;依然;照样
You can say all the same or just the same to introduce a statement which indicates that a situation or your opinion has not changed, in spite of what has happened or what has just been said.
例：I arranged to pay him the dollars when he got there, a purely private arrangement. All the same, it was illegal...我准备等他到那儿时付给他美元，纯属私人安排。不过同样也是非法的。
例：He was unable to pay attention to the papers on his desk. Just the same, he pulled over the stack of papers and started to examine them...他的心不在桌子上的那一摞试卷上，尽管如此，他还是拽过来开始评阅。

PHRASE
对我完全一样;对我毫无区别;我都无所谓
If you say 'It's all the same to me', you mean that you do not care which of several things happens or is chosen.
例：Whether I've got a moustache or not it's all the same to me...我留不留胡子都无所谓。
例：What's the difference between a white lie and a lie? I mean, it's all the same to me.善意的谎言和谎言之间有什么不同？我是说，对于我而言二者完全一样。

PHRASE
同一事物;同一个人
When two or more people or things are thought to be separate and you say that they are one and the same, you mean that they are in fact one single person or thing.
例：Luckily, Nancy's father and her attorney were one and the same person...幸运的是，南希的父亲就是她的律师。
例：I'm willing to work for the party because its interests and my interests are one and the same.我愿意为该党效力，因为它的利益和我的利益完全一致。

PHRASE
正是这个;同一个人(或事物)
You say 'the same' or 'the very same' in reply to someone's question when you are saying that they have identified a person or thing correctly.
例：'This Sawtry guy, he is John Sawtry?' — 'Yes, sir. The very same.'“这个叫索特里的小伙子，他是约翰·索特里吗？”——“是的，先生。正是他。”

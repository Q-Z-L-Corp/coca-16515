ADV-GRADED
近来；最近
If you have done something recently or if something happened recently, it happened only a short time ago.
例：The bank recently opened a branch in Germany...这家银行最近在德国开设了分行。
例：It is only fairly recently that historians have begun to investigate the question...只是到最近历史学家才开始探究这个问题。

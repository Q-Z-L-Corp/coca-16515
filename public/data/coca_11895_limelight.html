limelight 
英[ˈlaɪmlaɪt] 美[ˈlaɪmˌlaɪt]
 
n.众人注意的中心；（不）是公众关注的中心，（不）引人注目；石灰光，石灰光灯；白炽灯；vt.使显露头角，使受到注目；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
公众注意的中心
If someone is in the limelight, a lot of attention is being paid to them, because they are famous or because they have done something very unusual or exciting.
例：Tony has now been thrust into the limelight, with a high-profile job.托尼现在一下子成了众人关注的中心，因为他有一份经常出镜、见报的工作。

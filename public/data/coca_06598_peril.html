peril 
英[ˈperəl] 美[ˈpɛrəl]
 
n.危险；冒险；vt.置…于危险中；危及；
 "

N-VAR
重大危险
Perils are great dangers.
例：...the perils of the sea...海洋的凶险
例：In spite of great peril, I have survived...虽然处境非常危险,我还是挺过来了。

N-PLURAL
（某活动或行为的）危险，问题
The perils of a particular activity or course of action are the dangers or problems it may involve.
例：...the perils of starring in a television commercial.拍电视广告的危险

PHRASE
（用于警告）自冒风险
If you say that someone does something at their peril, you are warning them that they will probably suffer as a result of doing it.
例：Ignore it at your peril...无视该问题要自担风险。
例：Anyone who breaks the law does so at their peril.违法者要自担后果。

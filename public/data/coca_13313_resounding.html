resounding 
英[rɪˈzaʊndɪŋ] 美[]
 
adj.反响的，共鸣的；响亮的，宏亮的；彻底的，完全的；夸张的，虚夸的；v.回响resound的现在分词；
 "

ADJ
响亮的;洪亮的;嘹亮的
A resounding sound is loud and clear.
例：There was a resounding slap as Andrew struck him violently across the face...安德鲁狠狠掴了他一巴掌，声音响亮干脆。
例：She got a resounding round of applause...她赢得一片热烈掌声。

ADJ
(成功)彻底的，完全的，辉煌的，巨大的
You can refer to a very great success as a resounding success.
例：The good weather helped to make the occasion a resounding success.天公作美，使得这次活动获得了巨大成功。

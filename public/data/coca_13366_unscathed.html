ADJ-GRADED
未受伤的；未受损害的
If you are unscathed after a dangerous experience, you have not been injured or harmed by it.
例：Tony emerged unscathed apart from a severely bruised finger...托尼除了一根手指严重淤青外没有受到其他伤害。
例：East Los Angeles was left relatively unscathed by the riots...相对来说，洛杉矶东部基本没有受到暴乱的影响。

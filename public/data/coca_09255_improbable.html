improbable 
英[ɪmˈprɒbəbl] 美[ɪmˈprɑbəbl]
 
adj.不大可能是真实的，不大可能的；未必确实的；未必会发生的；非概然；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不大可能的;未必会(发生)的
Something that is improbable is unlikely to be true or to happen.
例：...a highly improbable coincidence...几率非常小的巧合
例：It seems improbable that this year's figure will fall much below last year's 75,000.看来今年的数字不大可能比去年的 75,000 差太多。

ADJ-GRADED
奇异的;荒谬的
If you describe something as improbable, you mean it is strange, unusual, or ridiculous.
例：On the face of it, their marriage seems an improbable alliance.表面上看,他们的婚姻似乎是个荒谬的结合。
例：...her improbable accent.她奇怪的口音

N-UNCOUNT
作者身份
The authorship of a piece of writing is the identity of the person who wrote it.
例：Its authorship has been disputed.谁是作者尚无定论。

N-UNCOUNT
写作活动；作家职业
Authorship is the activity or job of writing books or articles.
例：It was her first try at authorship and proved a great success.这是她第一次尝试写作，结果证明非常成功。

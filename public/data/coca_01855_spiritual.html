spiritual 
英[ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl] 美[ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl]
 
adj.精神的；心灵的；高尚的，崇高的；宗教的；n.圣歌（尤指美国南部黑人的）；教会事物；
 "

ADJ
精神(上)的;心灵(上)的
Spiritual means relating to people's thoughts and beliefs, rather than to their bodies and physical surroundings.
例：She lived entirely by spiritual values, in a world of poetry and imagination.她追求精神上的满足，完全生活在一个诗歌和想象的世界里。

ADJ
宗教(上)的；宗教信仰(上)的
Spiritual means relating to people's religious beliefs.
例：The spiritual leader of Ireland's 3.7 million Catholics...爱尔兰 370 万天主教徒的宗教领袖
例：A man in priestly clothes offered spiritual guidance.一位身着牧师袍服的男子进行了宗教布道。

N-COUNT
灵歌(原为美国黑奴所唱的一种宗教歌曲)
A spiritual is a religious song of the type originally sung by black slaves in America.

PHRASE
精神家园;精神故乡
Your spiritual home is the place where you feel that you belong, usually because your ideas or attitudes are the same as those of the people who live there.

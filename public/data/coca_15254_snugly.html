ADJ-GRADED
(尤指寒冷天气中)暖烘烘的，温暖舒适的
If you feel snug or are in a snug place, you are very warm and comfortable, especially because you are protected from cold weather.
例：They lay snug and warm amid the blankets.他们舒舒服服、暖暖和和地躺在毯子里。
例：...a snug log cabin.舒适温暖的小木屋

ADJ-GRADED
(衣服等)紧身的，贴身的
Something such as a piece of clothing that is snug fits very closely or tightly.
例：...a snug black T-shirt and skin-tight black jeans...贴身的黑色T恤和紧身的黑色牛仔裤
例：Every dress is lined, ensuring a snug, firm fit.每条裙子都加了衬里，确保穿起来贴身而挺括。

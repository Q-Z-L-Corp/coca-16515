moisture 
英[ˈmɔɪstʃə(r)] 美[ˈmɔɪstʃɚ]
 
n.水分；湿气；潮湿；降雨量；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
潮气;湿气;水分
Moisture is tiny drops of water in the air, on a surface, or in the ground.
例：When the soil is dry, more moisture is lost from the plant...土壤干燥时，植物会流失更多的水分。
例：Rainfall affects the moisture content of the atmosphere.降雨会影响空气湿度。

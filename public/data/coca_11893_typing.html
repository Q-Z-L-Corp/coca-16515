N-UNCOUNT
打字
Typing is the work or activity of typing something by means of a typewriter or word processor.
例：She didn't do any typing till the evening.她直到晚上才开始打字。

N-UNCOUNT
打字技术
Typing is the skill of using a typewriter or keyboard quickly and accurately.
例：My typing is quite dreadful...我的打字水平非常差。
例：Ginny tried to sharpen up some rusty typing skills.金尼试图重拾生疏了的打字技术。

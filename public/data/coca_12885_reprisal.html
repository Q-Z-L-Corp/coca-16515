reprisal 
英[rɪˈpraɪzl] 美[rɪˈpraɪzəl]
 
n.报复（行为）；（特指国家间的）暴力性报复，报复行为，[史]（对敌国人民，财产的）报复性劫掠；〈罕〉赔偿，赔款；
 "

N-VAR
报复（行为）
If you do something to a person in reprisal, you hurt or punish them because they have done something violent or unpleasant to you.
例：There were fears that some of the Western hostages might be killed in reprisal...有人担心一些西方人质可能会受到报复性杀害。
例：Witnesses are unwilling to testify through fear of reprisals.由于害怕报复，目击者不愿作证。

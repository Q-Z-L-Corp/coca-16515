ADV-GRADED
笼统地；概括地
You use generally to give a summary of a situation, activity, or idea without referring to the particular details of it.
例：University teachers generally have admitted a lack of enthusiasm about their subjects...高校教师大体上承认对其所教专业缺乏热情。
例：Speaking generally, the space enterprise has served astronomy well.总的说来，航空企业很好地促进了天文学的发展。

ADV
通常地；一般地；普遍地
You use generally to say that something happens or is used on most occasions but not on every occasion.
例：As women we generally say and feel too much about these things...作为女性，我们通常在这些事情上谈得过多，过于敏锐。
例：It is generally true that the darker the fruit the higher its iron content...一般来说，水果颜色越深，含铁量就越高。

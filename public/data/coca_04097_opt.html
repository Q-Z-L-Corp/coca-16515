VERB
选择；作出抉择
If you opt for something, or opt to do something, you choose it or decide to do it in preference to anything else.
例：Depending on your circumstances you may wish to opt for one method or the other...依据自身情况的不同，你可能希望选择这种或那种方法。
例：Our students can also opt to stay in residence.我们的学生也可以选择住校。

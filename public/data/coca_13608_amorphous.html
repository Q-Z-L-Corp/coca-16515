ADJ-GRADED
无固定形状（或结构）的；不规则的；无组织的
Something that is amorphous has no clear shape or structure.
例：A dark, strangely amorphous shadow filled the room.一团形状怪异的黑影笼罩了整个房间。
例：...the amorphous mass of the unemployed.散漫的失业人群

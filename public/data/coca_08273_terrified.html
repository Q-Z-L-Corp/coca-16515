VERB
吓坏;使极度恐慌;使非常害怕
If something terrifies you, it makes you feel extremely frightened.
例：Flying terrifies him...坐飞机让他害怕得要命。
例：The thought of dying slowly and painfully terrified me.缓慢而痛苦地死去的想法使我恐惧不已。

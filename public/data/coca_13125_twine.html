twine 
英[twaɪn] 美[twaɪn]
 
n.盘绕；麻线；捻；缠绕在一起的东西；vt.& vi.搓；编；（使）盘绕；vi.蜿蜒；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤用于园艺和农事的)麻线，合股线
Twine is strong string used especially in gardening and farming.

V-ERG
(使)缠绕;(使)盘绕;(使)环绕
If you twine one thing around another, or if one thing twines around another, the first thing is twisted or wound around the second.
例：He had twined his chubby arms around Vincent's neck...他胖乎乎的双臂搂住文森特的脖子。
例：He twined his fingers into hers…他和她十指相扣。

virtual 
英[ˈvɜ:tʃuəl] 美[ˈvɜrtʃuəl]
 
adj.实质上的，事实上的；（计算机）虚拟的；<物>有效的，虚像的；（粒子）实际存在的；
 "

ADJ
几乎的;实际上的;事实上的
You can use virtual to indicate that something is so nearly true that for most purposes it can be regarded as true.
例：Argentina came to a virtual standstill while the game was being played...在比赛进行的时候，阿根廷全国几乎陷入了停顿状态。
例：He claimed to be a virtual prisoner in his own home.他说自己在家里简直就像是个囚犯。

ADJ
虚拟的;模拟的
Virtual objects and activities are generated by a computer to simulate real objects and activities.
例：Up to four players can compete in a virtual world of role playing...最多可以有4个玩家在一个虚拟世界中扮演角色互相拼杀。
例：This is a virtual shopping centre offering visitors entry to a clutch of well-known etailers without going to their different websites.这是一个虚拟的购物中心，访问者在此就可以浏览很多著名的网上零售店铺，而无需分别访问它们各自的网站。

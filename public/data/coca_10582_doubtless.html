ADV
很可能地;几乎肯定地
If you say that something is doubtless the case, you mean that you think it is probably or almost certainly the case.
例：He will doubtless try and persuade his colleagues to change their minds...他很可能会试图说服同事们改变主意。
例：Doubtless he was justified in some of his criticism of the media...他对媒体的一些批评性言论无疑是合乎情理的。

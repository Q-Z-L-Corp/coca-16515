noisily 
英['nɔɪzɪlɪ] 美['nɔɪzɪlɪ]
 
adv.吵闹地；喧喧嚷嚷；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
喧闹的;嗓门大的;聒噪的
A noisy person or thing makes a lot of loud or unpleasant noise.
例：...my noisy old typewriter...我那台噪音极大的旧打字机
例：His daughter was very active and noisy in the mornings.他的女儿一到上午就很好动，吵吵闹闹的。

ADJ-GRADED
充满噪音的;嘈杂的
A noisy place is full of a lot of loud or unpleasant noise.
例：It's a noisy place with film clips showing constantly on one of the cafe's giant screens...这个咖啡馆很嘈杂，室内的一个大屏幕上不停地播放着电影片断。
例：The baggage hall was crowded and noisy...行李大厅拥挤嘈杂。

ADJ-GRADED
(为引起注意)吵吵嚷嚷的;哗众取宠的
If you describe someone as noisy, you are critical of them for trying to attract attention to their views by frequently and forcefully discussing them.
例：It might, at last, silence the small but noisy intellectual clique.它可能最终会使这个聒噪的知识分子小圈子安静下来。
例：...the noisy and unpopular fringe groups that are attempting to change the culture of their society.企图变革社会文化的哗众取宠、不受欢迎的边缘群体

ADJ-GRADED
充满生气的;精力充沛的;活跃的

例：Tom felt himself being drawn towards her vibrant personality...汤姆感觉自己被她充满朝气的个性所吸引。
例：...Shakespeare's vibrant language...莎士比亚鲜活的语言

ADJ-GRADED
（色彩）明亮的，鲜亮的
Vibrant colours are very bright and clear.
例：Horizon Blue, Corn Yellow and Pistachio Green are just three of the vibrant colours in this range...天际蓝、玉米黄、淡草绿色只是这一色系内的3种亮色。

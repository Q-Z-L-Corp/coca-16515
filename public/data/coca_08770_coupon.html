N-COUNT
(购物的)赠券，优惠券
A coupon is a piece of printed paper which allows you to pay less money than usual for a product, or to get it free.
例：Bring the coupon below to any Tecno store and pay just £10.99.持下面的优惠券到任意一家Tecno专卖店，只需付10.99英镑。
例：...a 50p money-off coupon.可以便宜50便士的优惠券

N-COUNT
(剪自报刊的)咨询单，订货单，参赛表
A coupon is a small form, for example in a newspaper or magazine, which you send off to ask for information, to order something, or to enter a competition.
例：Send the coupon with a cheque for £18.50, made payable to 'Good Housekeeping'...将订单连同一张18.50英镑的支票一同寄出，支票抬头为“《好管家》杂志”。
例：He was filling in his pools coupon.他正在填写足球博彩的彩票。

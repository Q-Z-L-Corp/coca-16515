stubble 
英[ˈstʌbl] 美[ˈstʌbəl]
 
n.作物收割后遗留在地里的残茎，茬子；短而硬的胡茬子；短粗硬毛；楂；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(作物收割后的)茬
Stubble is the short stalks which are left standing in fields after corn or wheat has been cut.
例：The stubble was burning in the fields.地里的麦茬正在燃烧。

N-UNCOUNT
胡子茬
The very short hairs on a man's face when he has not shaved recently are referred to as stubble .
例：His face was covered with the stubble of several nights.他几天没刮胡子，脸上胡子拉碴。

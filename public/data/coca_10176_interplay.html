N-UNCOUNT
相互作用;相互影响
The interplay between two or more things or people is the way that they have an effect on each another or react to each other.
例：...the personal interplay between great entertainers and a live public.演艺巨星与现场观众之间的直接互动
例：...the interplay of political, economic, social and cultural factors.政治、经济、社会及文化因素之间的相互作用

jumble 
英[ˈdʒʌmbl] 美[ˈdʒʌmbəl]
 
vt.（使）混乱，杂乱；使困惑；vi.纷乱；n.杂乱的一堆；一团乱；<英>待义卖的杂旧物；
 "

N-COUNT
杂乱的一堆；混乱的一团
A jumble of things is a lot of different things that are all mixed together in a disorganized or confused way.
例：The shoreline was made up of a jumble of huge boulders.海岸线上堆着一大堆杂乱的巨石。
例：...a meaningless jumble of words.一连串毫无意义的胡言乱语

V-ERG
（使）混乱；（使）杂乱
If you jumble things or if things jumble, they become mixed together so that they are untidy or are not in the correct order.
例：He's making a new film by jumbling together bits of his other movies...他将自己的几部影片东一块西一块地拼凑成一部新电影。
例：...a number of animals whose remains were jumbled together by scavengers and floods...被食腐动物丢弃又被洪水冲积在一起的一些动物残骸

N-UNCOUNT
用于义卖的废旧物品
Jumble is old or unwanted things that people give away to charity.
例：She expects me to drive round collecting jumble for the church.她期望我能开车去四处收集旧物用于教堂义卖。

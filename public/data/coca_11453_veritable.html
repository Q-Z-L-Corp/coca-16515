ADJ
真正的，十足的，不折不扣的（用于强调大小、数量或性质等）
You can use veritable to emphasize the size, amount, or nature of something.
例：...a veritable feast of pre-match entertainment.尽情的赛前娱乐享受
例：...a veritable army of security guards.一支真正的警卫部队

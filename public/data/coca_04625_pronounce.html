pronounce 
英[prəˈnaʊns] 美[prəˈnaʊns]
 
vt.& vi.发音，读；vt.宣布，宣称；演讲，讲述；vi.宣判；
 "

VERB
发…的音；读
To pronounce a word means to say it using particular sounds.
例：Have I pronounced your name correctly?...你的名字我念得对吗？
例：He pronounced it Per-sha, the way the English do.他像英格兰人那样将它读成Per-sha。

VERB
宣称；宣告；宣布；断言
If you pronounce something to be true, you state that it is the case.
例：A specialist has now pronounced him fully fit...一位专家已宣布他的身体完全恢复了。
例：I now pronounce you man and wife.我现在宣布你们结为夫妻。

VERB
宣称；宣布
If someone pronounces a verdict or opinion on something, they give their verdict or opinion.
例：The authorities took time to pronounce their verdicts...当局斟酌权衡再三才宣布他们的决定。
例：'As for me,' he pronounced, 'I can recognize a good deal when I see one'...“至于我，”他宣称，“买卖好坏我一看便知。”

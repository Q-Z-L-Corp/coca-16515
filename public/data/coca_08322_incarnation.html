incarnation 
英[ˌɪnkɑ:ˈneɪʃn] 美[ˌɪnkɑrˈneɪʃn]
 
n.化身；前身；典型体现；
 "

N-COUNT
(某种品质的)典型体现，化身
If you say that someone is the incarnation of a particular quality, you mean that they represent that quality or are typical of it in an extreme form.
例：The regime was the very incarnation of evil...该政权正是罪恶的化身。
例：She is a perfect incarnation of glamour.她是魅力的完美化身。

N-COUNT
(在世间的)化身，前身
An incarnation is an instance of being alive on earth in a particular form. Some religions believe that people have several incarnations in different forms.
例：She began recalling a series of previous incarnations...她开始回忆起前世的种种情形。
例：His industry and persistence suggest that he was an ant in a previous incarnation.他的勤奋与坚持表明他前世是只蚂蚁。

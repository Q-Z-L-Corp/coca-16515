sunrise 
英[ˈsʌnraɪz] 美[ˈsʌnˌraɪz]
 
n.日出（时分），黎明；<诗>日出的地方，东方；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
日出(时分);黎明;拂晓
Sunrise is the time in the morning when the sun first appears in the sky.
例：The rain began before sunrise.日出前下起了雨。

N-COUNT
朝霞;晨曦
A sunrise is the colours and light that you see in the eastern part of the sky when the sun first appears.
例：There was a spectacular sunrise yesterday.昨天的朝霞很绚烂。

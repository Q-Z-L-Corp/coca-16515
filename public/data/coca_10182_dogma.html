dogma 
英[ˈdɒgmə] 美[ˈdɔgmə]
 
n.教义，教条，信条；武断的意见；adj.教义的，教条的，信条的；
 "

N-VAR
教条;信条;教义
If you refer to a belief or a system of beliefs as a dogma, you disapprove of it because people are expected to accept that it is true, without questioning it.
例：Their political dogma has blinded them to the real needs of the country...他们的政治信条让他们看不到国家真正的需要。
例：He stands for freeing the country from the grip of dogma.他主张将国家从教条的控制下解放出来。

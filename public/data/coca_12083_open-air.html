open-air

 "

ADJ
露天的；户外的；室外的
An open-air place or event is outside rather than in a building.
例：...the Open Air Theatre in Regents Park.摄政公园里的露天剧场
例：...an open air concert in brilliant sunshine.沐浴在明媚阳光中的露天音乐会

N-SING
露天；户外；野外
If you are in the open air, you are outside rather than in a building.
例：We sleep out under the stars, and eat our meals in the open air.我们在星空下露宿，在野外用餐。

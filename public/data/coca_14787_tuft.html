tuft 
英[tʌft] 美[tʌft]
 
n.丛生植物；（头发、羽毛、草等）一簇；<古>髭；逢迎权贵的人；vt.用丛毛装饰；使分成簇（或束）；vi.形成一簇，丛生；
 "

N-COUNT
一丛;一簇;一撮
A tuft of something such as hair or grass is a small amount of it which is growing together in one place or is held together at the bottom.
例：He had a small tuft of hair on his chin.他下巴上有一小撮胡须。
例：…tufts of wool torn from sheep by bushes and brambles.被矮丛林与荆棘粘下来的簇簇羊毛

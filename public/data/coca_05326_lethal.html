lethal 
英[ˈli:θl] 美[ˈliθəl]
 
adj.致命的，致死的；能致命的；破坏性的；有害的；n.致死因子；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
致命的；可致死的
A substance that is lethal can kill people or animals.
例：...a lethal dose of sleeping pills.剂量足以致命的安眠药
例：...chemicals lethal to fish and aquatic mammals.会杀死鱼类和水生哺乳动物的致命化学品

ADJ-GRADED
危害极大的；毁灭性的
If you describe something as lethal, you mean that it is capable of causing a lot of damage.
例：High-powered cars are lethal weapons in the hands of inexperienced drivers...马力大的汽车到了缺乏经验的司机手中便成了毁灭性的武器。
例：Frost and wet are the lethal combination for plants.霜雨天气对植物危害极大。

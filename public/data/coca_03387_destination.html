N-COUNT
目的地;终点
The destination of someone or something is the place to which they are going or being sent.
例：Spain is still our most popular holiday destination...西班牙仍是我们最喜爱的度假去处。
例：Only half of the emergency supplies have reached their destination.仅有一半的紧急救援物资运抵了目的地。

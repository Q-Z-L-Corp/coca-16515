congestion 
英[kənˈdʒestʃən] 美[kənˈdʒestʃən]
 
n.拥挤，堵车；阻塞；充血；（人口）过剩，稠密；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
堵塞;拥堵
If there is congestion in a place, the place is extremely crowded and blocked with traffic or people.
例：The problems of traffic congestion will not disappear in a hurry...交通拥堵问题不会一下子就消失。
例：Energy consumption, congestion and pollution have increased.能源消耗、交通堵塞以及污染状况愈加严重。

N-UNCOUNT
(身体部位的)堵塞，充血，淤血
Congestion in a part of the body is a medical condition in which the part becomes blocked.
例：...nasal congestion.鼻塞

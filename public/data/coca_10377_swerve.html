 "

V-ERG
(使)突然转向;(使)急转弯
If a vehicle or other moving thing swerves or if you swerve it, it suddenly changes direction, often in order to avoid hitting something.
例：Drivers coming in the opposite direction swerved to avoid the bodies...从对面来的汽车司机急忙转向以免撞到那些尸体。
例：Her car swerved off the road into a 6ft high brick wall...她的车突然转向冲出了马路，撞向 6 英尺高的一面砖墙。

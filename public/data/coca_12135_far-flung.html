far-flung 
英[ˈfɑ:ˈflʌŋ] 美[ˈfɑrˈflʌŋ]
 
adj.广布的；广泛的；蔓延的；辽阔的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
遥远的;偏僻的
Far-flung places are a very long distance away from where you are or from important places.
例：Ferries are a lifeline to the far-flung corners of Scotland.渡船是苏格兰边远地区的生命线。
例：...one of the farthest-flung outposts of the old Roman Empire.古罗马帝国最偏远的一个边境

ADJ
庞大的;涉及广泛的
If you describe something such as organization or system as far-flung, you mean that it extends over a very large area.
例：...a far-flung network of conspirators.庞大的阴谋颠覆网络

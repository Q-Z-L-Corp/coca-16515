EXCLAM
啊哈（书面语中用于表示满意、惊讶等）
Aha is used in writing to represent a noise that people make in conversation, for example to express satisfaction or surprise.
例：Aha! Here at last, the answer to my question.啊哈，我的问题到这儿终于有了答案。

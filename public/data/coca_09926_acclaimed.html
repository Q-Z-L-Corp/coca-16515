VERB
称赞;赞扬
If someone or something is acclaimed, they are praised enthusiastically.
例：She has been acclaimed for the TV drama 'Prime Suspect'...她凭借电视剧《头号嫌疑犯》而受到好评。
例：He was acclaimed as England's greatest modern painter...他被誉为英格兰现代最伟大的油画家。

N-UNCOUNT
公开赞扬;称誉
Acclaim is public praise for someone or something.
例：Angela Bassett has won critical acclaim for her excellent performance...安吉拉·贝塞特凭借其出色的表演赢得了评论界的好评。
例：All this equipment has received international acclaim from the specialist hi-fi press.这套设备受到全球高保真音响专业报刊的赞誉。

walk 
英[wɔ:k] 美[wɔk]
 
vt.& vi.走；步行；散步；n.步行；步态；人行道；步行的路径；vi.行走；陪伴…走；徒步旅行；不翼而飞；vt.牵着（动物）走，遛，赶着…走；
 "

VERB
走;行走;走路
When you walk, you move forward by putting one foot in front of the other in a regular way.
例：Rosanna and Forbes walked in silence for some while...罗莎娜和福布斯默不作声地走了一会儿。
例：We walked into the foyer...我们走进门厅。

N-COUNT
漫步;散步
A walk is a journey that you make by walking, usually for pleasure.
例：I went for a walk...我去散步了。
例：He often took long walks in the hills.他经常在山中久久漫步。

N-SING
步行的距离(或路程)
A walk of a particular distance is the distance which a person has to walk to get somewhere.
例：It was only a three-mile walk to Kabul from there...从那里到喀布尔只需走上3英里。
例：The church is a short walk from Piazza Dante.教堂离但丁广场只有几步之遥。

N-COUNT
步行小径;(适宜散步的)小径，道路
A walk is a route suitable for walking along for pleasure.
例：There is a 2 mile coastal walk from Craster to Newton.从克拉斯特到牛顿有一条两英里的海滨小径。

N-SING
走;行走;步行
A walk is the action of walking rather than running.
例：She slowed to a steady walk.她放慢脚步，徐徐而行。

N-SING
步态;走路的样子
Someone's walk is the way that they walk.
例：George, despite his great height and gangling walk, was a keen dancer.乔治尽管长得过高，走路姿势难看，但却热衷于跳舞。

VERB
陪同;陪伴护送
If you walk someone somewhere, you walk there with them in order to show politeness or to make sure that they get there safely.
例：She walked me to my car...她陪我走到我的车旁。
例：'What a nice gentleman you are, to walk Hilary home,' her mother said.“您能送希拉里走回家，真是一位绅士，”她的母亲说道。

VERB
遛(狗);带(狗)溜达
If you walk your dog, you take it for a walk in order to keep it healthy.
例：I walk my dog each evening around my local streets.我每晚在家附近的街道上遛狗。

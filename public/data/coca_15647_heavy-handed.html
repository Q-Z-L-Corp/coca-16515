heavy-handed 
英[ˈhevi:ˈhændɪd] 美[ˈhɛviˈhændɪd]
 
adj.笨手笨脚的，拙劣的；严厉的；暴虐的；手辣的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
粗暴的;暴虐的
If you say that someone's behaviour is heavy-handed, you mean that they are too forceful or too rough.
例：...heavy-handed police tactics...警方的粗暴手段
例：The demonstration had been dealt with in a violent and heavy-handed way...示威遭到了残暴的镇压。

ADJ-GRADED
用量太多的;重手的;笨拙的
If someone is heavy-handed with something, they use too much of it or use it in a clumsy way.
例：It all depends on how heavy-handed you are with the paprika...这要看你下多重的辣椒粉了。
例：In fact she tends to be a little heavy-handed when she wears make-up.实际上她的妆往往化得有点过浓。

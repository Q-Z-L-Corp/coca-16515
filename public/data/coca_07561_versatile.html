adj.（指工具、机器等）多用途的；多才多艺的；有多种学问、技能或职业的；多功能的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有多种技能的;多才多艺的
If you say that a person is versatile, you approve of them because they have many different skills.
例：He had been one of the game's most versatile athletes.他是这项运动中技术最全面的运动员之一。

ADJ-GRADED
有多种用途的;多功能的
A tool, machine, or material that is versatile can be used for many different purposes.
例：Never before has computing been so versatile.计算机的用途从没像现在这么广泛过。

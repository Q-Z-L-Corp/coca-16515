conciliatory 
英[kənˈsɪliətəri] 美[kənˈsɪliətɔri]
 
adj.安抚的；说服的；调和的；调解的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
愿意和解的; 调停的;安抚的
When you are conciliatory in your actions or behaviour, you show that you are willing to end a disagreement with someone.
例：The next time he spoke he used a more conciliatory tone...他再次开口时使用了更具安抚性的语气。
例：The President's speech was hailed as a conciliatory gesture toward business.总统的讲话被视为对商界的安抚性姿态，受到了欢迎。

ADJ-GRADED
自相矛盾的；似矛盾而（可能） 正确的
If something is paradoxical, it involves two facts or qualities which seem to contradict each other.
例：Some sedatives produce the paradoxical effect of making the person more anxious...一些镇静剂适得其反，加重了患者的焦虑。
例：We were a team of individuals — as paradoxical as that sounds.我们是一个保持了个体的团队——尽管这听起来自相矛盾。

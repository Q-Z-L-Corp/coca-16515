profession 
英[prəˈfeʃn] 美[prəˈfɛʃən]
 
n.职业，专业；同行；宣称；信念，信仰；
 "

N-COUNT
（需要高等教育或训练的）职业
A profession is a type of job that requires advanced education or training.
例：Harper was a teacher by profession...哈珀的职业是教师。
例：Only 20 per cent of jobs in the professions are held by women.在这些职业中，只有20%的岗位是由女性担任的。

N-COUNT-COLL
（某一专业工作的）全体人员，业界人士，专业界
You can use profession to refer to all the people who have the same profession.
例：The attitude of the medical profession is very much more liberal now.现在，医务人员的态度开明多了。

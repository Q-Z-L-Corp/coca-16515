ascension 
英[əˈsenʃn] 美[əˈsɛnʃən]
 
n.上升，升高；
 "

N-SING
（一些宗教中的）升天
In some religions, when someone goes to heaven, you can refer to their ascension to heaven.
例：...the crucifixion, resurrection and ascension of Jesus Christ.耶稣基督的受难、复活和升天
例：...the two-day holiday marking the Prophet's ascension to heaven.纪念先知穆罕默德升天的双休假日

N-SING
升迁；升职；（地位的）提高
The ascension of a person to a high rank or important position is the act of reaching this position.
例：...50 years after his ascension to the Cambodian throne.在他登上柬埔寨王位50年后

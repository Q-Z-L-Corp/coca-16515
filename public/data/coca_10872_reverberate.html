reverberate 
英[rɪˈvɜ:bəreɪt] 美[rɪˈvɜrbəreɪt]
 
vi.回响；弹回；反射；vt.使回响；使反射；使回弹；
 "

VERB
发出回声;回响;回荡
When a loud sound reverberates through a place, it echoes through it.
例：The sound of the tank guns reverberated through the little Bavarian town...坦克炮声在巴伐利亚小镇回荡。
例：A woman's shrill laughter reverberated in the courtyard.一个女人的尖笑声在院子里回荡。

VERB
(事情、思想等)产生巨大反响，引起轰动，影响深远
You can say that an event or idea reverberates when it has a powerful effect which lasts a long time.
例：The controversy surrounding the take-over yesterday continued to reverberate around the television industry...昨天围绕接管展开的争论在电视业仍然有巨大的反响。
例：The news sent shock waves through the community that have continued to reverberate to this day.这则消息震惊了整个社区，直到现在还波澜未息。

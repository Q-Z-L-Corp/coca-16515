ADJ-GRADED
（气味或味道）辛辣的，苦的，刺激的
An acrid smell or taste is strong and sharp, and usually unpleasant.
例：The room filled with the acrid smell of tobacco...房间里弥漫着刺鼻的烟草味。
例：The plant has an unpleasant odour and an acrid taste.这种植物闻着刺鼻，吃着辣口。

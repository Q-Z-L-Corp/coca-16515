pilgrimage 
英[ˈpɪlgrɪmɪdʒ] 美[ˈpɪlɡrəmɪdʒ]
 
n.朝圣之旅；参拜圣地；人生的旅程，一生；漫游；vi.去朝圣，去参拜圣地；漫游；
 "

N-COUNT
朝圣；朝觐
If you make a pilgrimage to a holy place, you go there for a religious reason.
例：...the pilgrimage to Mecca.去麦加的朝圣

N-COUNT
拜谒；重要旅程
A pilgrimage is a journey that someone makes to a place that is very important to them.
例：...a private pilgrimage to family graves...私下前往家族墓地的拜谒
例：His father took him on a sentimental pilgrimage to Ireland.他爸爸带他踏上了去爱尔兰的情感之旅。

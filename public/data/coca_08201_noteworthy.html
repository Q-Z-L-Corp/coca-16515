ADJ-GRADED
值得注意的;显著的;有趣的
A fact or event that is noteworthy is interesting, remarkable, or significant in some way.
例：It is noteworthy that the programme has been shifted from its original August slot to July...值得注意的是该节目已从原来8月份的档期换到了7月份。
例：I found nothing particularly noteworthy to report...我并无特别重要的事情要报告。

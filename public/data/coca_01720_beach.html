beach 
英[bi:tʃ] 美[bitʃ]
 
n.海滩，海滨；岸上的沙子和卵石；vt.将…拖上岸；将滑艇拖到小屋前面的岸边；
 "

N-COUNT
海滩；海滨
A beach is an area of sand or stones beside the sea.
例：...a beautiful sandy beach...美丽的沙滩
例：I just want to lie on the beach in the sun.我只想躺在海滩上晒晒太阳。

V-ERG
把（船）拖上岸；（船等）搁浅
If something such as a boat beaches, or if it is beached, it is pulled or forced out of the water and onto land.
例：We beached the canoe, running it right up the bank...我们把独木舟径直划到岸边，并拖上岸。
例：The boat beached on a mud flat...船搁浅在一片淤泥滩上。

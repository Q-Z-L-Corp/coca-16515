N-COUNT
问答竞赛;智力竞赛
A quiz is a game or competition in which someone tests your knowledge by asking you questions.
例：We'll have a quiz at the end of the show.节目的最后我们要举行一次智力竞赛。

VERB
询问;查问;盘问
If you are quizzed by someone about something, they ask you questions about it.
例：He was quizzed about his income, debts and eligibility for state benefits...他被问及收入、债务以及是否有资格享受政府发放的救济金等问题。
例：Sybil quizzed her about life as a working girl.西比尔询问了她当女工的生活。

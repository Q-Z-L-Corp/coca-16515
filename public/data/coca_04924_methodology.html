（进行教学、研究等的）方法论,方法学，一套方法
A methodology is a system of methods and principles for doing something, for example for teaching or for carrying out research.
例：Teaching methodologies vary according to the topic...题目不同，教学法也不一样。
例：In their own work they may have favored the use of methodology different from mine.他们在做自己的工作时，也许更喜欢使用和我不一样的一套方法。

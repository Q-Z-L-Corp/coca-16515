injured 
英[ˈɪndʒəd] 美[ˈɪndʒərd]
 
adj.受伤的；受损害的；损伤感情的；v.损害(injure的过去式)；伤害（名誉、自尊等）；（尤指在事故中）伤害；使受伤；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
受伤的;有伤的
An injured person or animal has physical damage to part of their body, usually as a result of an accident or fighting.
例：The other injured man had a superficial stomach wound...另一名受伤男子是腹部的皮外伤。
例：Many of them will have died because they were so badly injured.他们中很多人会因伤势过重而死亡。

ADJ-GRADED
(感情)受到伤害的
If you have injured feelings, you feel upset because you believe someone has been unfair or unkind to you.
例：...a look of injured pride.自尊心受到伤害的表情
例：...compensation for injured feelings.精神损失赔偿

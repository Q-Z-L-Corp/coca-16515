celebrity 
英[səˈlebrəti] 美[səˈlɛbrɪti]
 
n.名人，知名人士；名流；名声；名誉；
 "

N-COUNT
(尤指娱乐界的)名人，明星
A celebrity is someone who is famous, especially in areas of entertainment such as films, music, writing, or sport.
例：In 1944, at the age of 30, Hersey suddenly became a celebrity.1944年，30岁的赫西一夜成名。
例：...a host of celebrities.群星荟萃

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指娱乐界的)名声，名誉，名气
If a person or thing achieves celebrity, they become famous, especially in areas of entertainment such as films, music, writing, or sport.
例：Joanna has finally made it to the first rank of celebrity after 25 years as an actress.乔安娜当了25年演员后，终于跻身一线女明星之列。

VERB
猛冲；扑
If you lunge in a particular direction, you move in that direction suddenly and clumsily.
例：He lunged at me, grabbing me violently...他向我扑过来，使劲把我抓住。
例：I lunged forward to try to hit him.我猛冲向前要打他。

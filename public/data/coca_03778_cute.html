cute 
英[kju:t] 美[kjut]
 
adj.漂亮的；娇小可爱的；机灵的，精明的；矫揉造作的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
可爱的;漂亮迷人的
Something or someone that is cute is very pretty or attractive, or is intended to appear pretty or attractive.
例：Oh, look at that dog! He's so cute.哦，看看那条狗!它太可爱了。
例：...a cute little house.一栋漂亮的小房子

ADJ-GRADED
妩媚的;性感的
If you describe someone as cute, you think they are sexually attractive.
例：There was this girl, and I thought she was really cute.就是这个女孩，我认为她非常性感。

ADJ-GRADED
精明的;机灵的
If you describe someone as cute, you mean that they deal with things cleverly.
例：The President, trying to be cute, said his liberal arts education had not really prepared him for such complicated issues as this...总统故作精明地说道，自己所接受的文科教育并不能使自己有能力应付如此复杂的问题。
例：That's a cute trick.这是个设计巧妙的骗局。

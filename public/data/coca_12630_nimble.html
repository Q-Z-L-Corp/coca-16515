nimble 
英[ˈnɪmbl] 美[ˈnɪmbəl]
 
adj.灵活的；敏捷的；（头脑）思路敏捷的，机智的；机敏的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
敏捷的;灵活的;灵巧的
Someone who is nimble is able to move their fingers, hands, or legs quickly and easily.
例：Everything had been stitched by Molly's nimble fingers...每一件东西都是莫莉灵巧的双手缝制出来的。
例：Val, who was light and nimble on her feet, learnt to dance the tango.脚步轻盈灵活的瓦尔学会了跳探戈舞。

ADJ-GRADED
敏锐的;聪明的;机智的
If you say that someone has a nimble mind, you mean they are clever and can think very quickly.
例：A nimble mind backed by a degree in economics gave him a firm grasp of financial matters...机敏的头脑再加上经济学学位作后盾使他对金融问题把握非常准。
例：Elderly people are told that if they want to keep their minds nimble, they must use them.老年人被告知，要想保持头脑灵敏，就必须要多动脑。

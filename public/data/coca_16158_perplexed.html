ADJ-GRADED
困惑的；迷惑不解的
If you are perplexed, you feel confused and slightly worried by something because you do not understand it.
例：She is perplexed about what to do for her daughter...她不知道该为女儿做些什么。
例：Japan's perplexed bankers need not worry.迷惑不解的日本银行家不必担心。

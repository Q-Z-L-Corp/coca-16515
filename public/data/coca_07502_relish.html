relish 
英[ˈrelɪʃ] 美[ˈrɛlɪʃ]
 
vt.品尝，品味；给…加佐料，调味；欣赏；vi.有滋味；有…的味道；n.味道，滋味；调味品；引起兴趣的东西；
 "

VERB
喜爱;玩味;欣赏;品味
If you relish something, you get a lot of enjoyment from it.
例：I relish the challenge of doing jobs that others turn down...我喜欢挑战别人拒绝做的工作。
例：He ate quietly, relishing his meal.他安静地吃着，细细品味着食物。

VERB
盼望;期待;享受
If you relish the idea, thought, or prospect of something, you are looking forward to it very much.
例：Jacqueline is not relishing the prospect of another spell in prison...杰奎琳可不想再蹲一阵子监狱。
例：He relished the idea of getting some cash.他盼望得到一些现金。

N-MASS
开胃小菜;调味品
Relish is a sauce or pickle that you eat with other food in order to give the other food more flavour.

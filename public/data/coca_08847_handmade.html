ADJ
手工制的
Handmade objects have been made by someone using their hands or using tools rather than by machines.
例：As they're handmade, each one varies slightly.因为是手工制作，所以每件都有细微差别。
例：...handmade chocolates.手工制作的巧克力

V-PASSIVE
手工制作
If something is handmade, it is made by someone using their hands or using tools rather than by machines.
例：The beads they use are handmade in the Jura mountains in central France...他们用的珠子是法国中部的侏罗山山民手工制作的。
例：These exquisitely ornate boxes and cabinets have been handmade and hand-painted by Indian craftspeople.这些精美华丽的箱子和柜子都是印度工匠手工制作并彩绘的。

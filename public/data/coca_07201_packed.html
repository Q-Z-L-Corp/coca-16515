adj.充满…的；塞满了…的；v.包装(pack的过去式和过去分词)；

ADJ-GRADED
非常拥挤的
A place that is packed is very crowded.
例：From 3.30 until 7pm, the shop is packed...从下午3点30分到7点，那家商店非常拥挤。
例：...a packed meeting at Westminster...在威斯敏斯特召开的与会人数众多的会议

ADJ-GRADED
充满…的；富含…的
Something that is packed with things contains a very large number of them.
例：The Encyclopedia is packed with clear illustrations and over 250 recipes...那本百科全书有很多清晰的插图和250多个食谱。
例：Fish and chips are packed with protein.炸鱼薯条富含蛋白质。

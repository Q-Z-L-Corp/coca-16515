ADJ
城外的；市郊的
Out-of-town shops or facilities are situated away from the centre of a town or city.
例：...shopping at cheaper, out-of-town supermarkets.在价格低廉的城郊超市购物

ADJ
外来的；非本地的
Out-of-town is used to describe people who do not live in a particular town or city, but have travelled there for a particular purpose.
例：...a deluxe hotel for out-of-town visitors.供外地来客下榻的豪华旅馆

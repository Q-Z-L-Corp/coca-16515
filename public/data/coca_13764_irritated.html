irritated 
英[ˈɪrɪteɪtɪd] 美[]
 
adj.恼怒的，生气的；v.使发怒( irritate的过去式和过去分词 )；使急躁；使感到不适；使疼痛；
 "

VERB
激怒;使恼怒;使烦躁
If something irritates you, it keeps annoying you.
例：Their attitude irritates me...他们的态度激怒了我。
例：Perhaps they were irritated by the sound of crying.也许他们被哭声惹烦了。

VERB
刺激(身体部位);使发痒;使疼痛
If something irritates a part of your body, it causes it to itch or become sore.
例：Wear rubber gloves while chopping chillies as they can irritate the skin.切辣椒时要戴橡胶手套,因为辣椒会刺激皮肤。

N-VAR
变形;变质;彻底的变化
When a metamorphosis occurs, a person or thing develops and changes into something completely different.
例：...his metamorphosis from a republican to a democrat...他从共和党人到民主党人的倒戈
例：The employment department has undergone several metamor-phoses.就业部已经经历了数次改革。

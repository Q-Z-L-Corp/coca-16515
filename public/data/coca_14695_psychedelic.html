psychedelic 
英[ˌsaɪkəˈdelɪk] 美[ˌsaɪkɪˈdɛlɪk]
 
adj.引起幻觉的，致幻觉的；迷幻的，幻觉的；n.迷幻剂；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
迷幻药的；引起幻觉的；致幻的
Psychedelic means relating to drugs such as LSD which have a strong effect on your mind, often making you see things that are not there.
例：Grof describes his research with psychedelic drugs and the experiences they triggered.格罗夫描述了他对迷幻药物及其带来的体验的研究情况。
例：...his first real, full-blown psychedelic experience.他第一次真正意义上的致幻体验

ADJ-GRADED
（艺术）光怪陆离的，造成迷幻效果的
Psychedelic art has bright colours and strange patterns.
例：...psychedelic patterns.光怪陆离的图案

ADJ
（音乐）迷幻的（尤指20世纪60年代末70年代初与迷幻药物密切相关的流行音乐）
Psychedelic music is pop music, especially of the late 1960s and early 1970s, which is closely associated with drugs such as LSD.
例：...at a time when, you know, everyone was doing kind of trippy, psychedelic music.你知道，当人人都在那种神游的迷幻音乐的那段时间

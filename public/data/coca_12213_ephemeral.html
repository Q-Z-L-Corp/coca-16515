ephemeral 
英[ɪˈfemərəl] 美[ɪˈfɛmərəl]
 
adj.短暂的，瞬息的；朝露；一年生；朝生暮死；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
短暂的;瞬间的
If you describe something as ephemeral, you mean that it lasts only for a very short time.
例：He talked about the country's ephemeral unity being shattered by the defeat...他谈到战败使得该国短暂的统一彻底破灭。
例：These paintings are in some ways a reminder that earthly pleasures are ephemeral.这些画在某种意义上提醒人们世俗的享乐是短暂的。

ADJ
确信的;深信的;坚信的
If you are convinced that something is true, you feel sure that it is true.
例：He was convinced that I was part of the problem...他确信我就是问题的一部分。
例：He became convinced of the need for cheap editions of good quality writing...他开始相信大众对优秀作品的普及本有需求。

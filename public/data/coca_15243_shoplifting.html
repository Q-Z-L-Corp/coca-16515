N-UNCOUNT
商店偷窃
Shoplifting is stealing from a shop by hiding things in a bag or in your clothes.
例：The grocer accused her of shoplifting and demanded to look in her bag...食品杂货店老板说她行窃并要求检查她的手提袋。
例：He admitted five shoplifting offences.他承认了 5 次商店行窃的罪行。

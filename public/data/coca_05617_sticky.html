sticky 
英[ˈstɪki] 美[ˈstɪki]
 
adj.粘性的；热湿的，闷热的；极不愉快的；不动的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
黏性的;涂有黏性物质的
A sticky substance is soft, or thick and liquid, and can stick to other things. Sticky things are covered with a sticky substance.
例：...sticky toffee...黏牙的太妃糖
例：If the dough is sticky, add more flour...如果面团太黏，就再加点面粉。

ADJ-GRADED
(情况)棘手的，令人为难的
A sticky situation involves problems or is embarrassing.
例：Inevitably the transition will yield some sticky moments...过渡时期难免会出现一些困难的时候。
例：Her research was going through a sticky patch.她的研究工作正处于一个艰难的阶段。

ADJ-GRADED
(天气)湿热的，闷热的
Sticky weather is unpleasantly hot and damp.
例：...four desperately hot, sticky days in the middle of August.8 月中旬极其闷热的 4 天

PHRASE
下场很悲惨;死得很惨
If someone comes to a sticky end or meets a sticky end, they suffer very badly or die in an unpleasant way.
例：Arminius also came to a sticky end, murdered by his own troops.阿米尼乌斯也死得很惨，被自己的部下害死了。

ADJ-GRADED
与多人发生性关系的；淫乱的；滥交的
Someone who is promiscuous has sex with many different people.
例：She is perceived as vain, spoilt and promiscuous...她被认为是个被人惯坏了的、既虚荣又淫乱的女人。
例：You know the risks of promiscuous sex.你知道滥交的危险。

ADJ-GRADED
混杂的
Promiscuous means including a wide range of different things.
例：...the dazzling, promiscuous display of new styles.各种新的风格混杂在一起，令人眼花缭乱

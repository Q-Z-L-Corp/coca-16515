英[ʌnˈnætʃrəl] 美[ʌnˈnætʃərəl]
 
adj.不自然的，做作的；奇异的；不真诚的；违背人性的，变态的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不自然的；诡异的；反常的
If you describe something as unnatural, you mean that it is strange and often frightening, because it is different from what you normally expect.
例：The aircraft rose with unnatural speed on take-off...那架飞机以反常的速度起飞升空。
例：The altered landscape looks unnatural and weird.改造后的景观看起来很不自然，极其怪异。

ADJ-GRADED
不自然的；做作的
Behaviour that is unnatural seems artificial and not normal or genuine.
例：She gave him a bright, determined smile which seemed unnatural.她对他开朗、坚定地一笑，但笑得有点不自然。

wage 
英[weɪdʒ] 美[wedʒ]
 
n.工资；报应；vt.& vi.实行，进行，作（战等）；〈方〉雇佣；〈古〉打赌，抵押，担保；
 "

N-COUNT
工资;工钱
Someone's wages are the amount of money that is regularly paid to them for the work that they do.
例：His wages have gone up...他涨工资了。
例：This may end efforts to set a minimum wage well above the poverty line.这可能会使将最低工资定得远高于贫困线的努力告吹。

VERB
开展，发动(运动或战争)
If a person, group, or country wages a campaign or a war, they start it and continue it over a period of time.
例：...the three factions that had been waging a civil war...发动内战的三个派别
例：They waged a price war.他们打起了价格战。

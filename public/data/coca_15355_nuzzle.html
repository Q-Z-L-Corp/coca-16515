nuzzle 
英[ˈnʌzl] 美[ˈnʌzəl]
 
vt.（用鼻）掘；擦；依偎；（用头或脸）紧挨；
 "

VERB
(用鼻子和嘴表示亲热地)轻擦，轻触
If you nuzzle someone or something, you gently rub your nose and mouth against them to show affection.
例：She nuzzled me and I cuddled her...她用嘴轻轻蹭蹭我，我搂住了她。
例：The dog came and nuzzled up against me.小狗跑过来，依偎在我身边。

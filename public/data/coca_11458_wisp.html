wisp 
英[wɪsp] 美[wɪsp]
 
n.小捆；小束；小缕；vt.把…卷成小把；vi.一缕缕地飘动；
 "

N-COUNT
（毛发的）缕，绺，小把
A wisp of hair is a small, thin, untidy bunch of it.
例：She smoothed away a wisp of hair from her eyes.她将眼睛上的一缕头发拨开。

N-COUNT
(烟、云等的)缕
A wisp of something such as smoke or cloud is an amount of it in a long thin shape.
例：A thin wisp of smoke straggled up through the trees.一缕轻烟在林间袅袅升起。
例：...an occasional wisp of white cloud.偶尔出现的一缕白云

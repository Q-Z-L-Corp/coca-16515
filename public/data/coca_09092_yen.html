N-COUNT
日元（日本货币单位）
The yen is the unit of currency used in Japan.
例：She's got a part-time job for which she earns 2,000 yen a month.她有一份月收入2,000日元的兼职工作。

N-SING
渴望;强烈的欲望;热望
If you have a yen to do something, you have a strong desire to do it.
例：Mike had a yen to try cycling...迈克非常想试试骑自行车。
例：He's a natural with any kind of engine but he has an unfortunate yen for speed.他天生对各种发动机就非常在行，但却过分追求速度。

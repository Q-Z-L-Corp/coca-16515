mixing 
英['mɪksɪŋ] 美['mɪksɪŋ]
 
n.混合，混频；v.混合( mix的现在分词 )；（使）结交；相容；平安相处；
 "

V-RECIP-ERG
(使)混合;(使)调和;(使)掺和
If two substances mix or if you mix one substance with another, you stir or shake them together, or combine them in some other way, so that they become a single substance.
例：Oil and water don't mix...油和水不相溶。
例：It mixes easily with cold or hot water to make a tasty, filling drink...它极易溶于冷水或热水，冲成既可口又充饥的饮料。

VERB
调制;配制
If you mix something, you prepare it by mixing other things together.
例：He had spent several hours mixing cement...他花了几个小时搅拌水泥。
例：Are you sure I can't mix you a drink?你确定不用我为你调一杯饮料吗？

N-VAR
(制作糕点、调味汁等的)混合原料，混合配料
A mix is a powder containing all the substances that you need in order to make something such as a cake or a sauce. When you want to use it, you add liquid.
例：For speed we used packets of pizza dough mix...为了节约时间，我们用了几包做比萨饼的混合面粉。
例：It was a packet mix.这是一小包混合配料。

N-COUNT
结合体；混合体
A mix of different things or people is two or more of them together.
例：The story is a magical mix of fantasy and reality...这个故事是幻想与现实的奇妙结合。
例：We get a very representative mix of people.我们现在有了非常具有代表性的一群人。

V-RECIP-ERG
相协调;相容
If two things or activities do not mix or if one thing does not mix with another, it is not a good idea to have them or do them together, because the result would be unpleasant or dangerous.
例：Politics and sport don't mix.政治与体育不可混为一谈。
例：...some of these pills that don't mix with drink...一些不能用饮料冲服的药片

V-RECIP
交际;交往;结交
If you mix with other people, you meet them and talk to them. You can also say that people mix .
例：I ventured the idea that the secret of staying young was to mix with older people...我大胆地提出了这样的观点：保持年轻的秘诀就在于和年长的人交往。
例：People are supposed to mix, do you understand?...人们应该互相交际，你明白吗？

VERB
混录;混合录制
When a record producer mixes a piece of music, he or she puts together the various sounds that have been recorded in order to make the finished record.
例：They've been mixing tracks for a new album due out later this year.他们一直在为定于今年稍后推出的新专辑录制歌曲。

PHRASE
经常大吵大闹，动不动就大打出手(美国英语用 mix it up)
If you say that someone mixes it, or in American English mixes it up, you mean that they often fight or argue strongly about things.
例：Stewart has developed a tendency to mix it verbally with the opposition.斯图尔特现在变得只要和别人意见不合，就准备大吵一架。

N-COUNT
预防措施；防备
A precaution is an action that is intended to prevent something dangerous or unpleasant from happening.
例：Could he not, just as a precaution, move to a place of safety?...就算仅仅是为了以防万一，难道他就不能挪到安全的地方吗？
例：I had taken the precaution of doing a little research before I left London...我已在离开伦敦前进行了准备，做了些调查研究。

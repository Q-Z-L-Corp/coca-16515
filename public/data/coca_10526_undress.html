undress 
英[ʌnˈdres] 美[ʌnˈdrɛs]
 
vt.脱…衣服；使卸下装饰；暴露；从（例如伤员）脱去绷带；vi.脱衣服；n.便服；裸体；衣服未穿完整；
 "

VERB
脱衣服；脱去…的衣服
When you undress or undress someone, you take off your clothes or someone else's clothes.
例：She went out, leaving Rachel to undress and have her shower...她出去了，留下雷切尔一个人除衣淋浴。
例：She undressed the child before putting her in the tin bath.她给孩子脱下衣服，然后把她放进锡铁澡盆里。

PHRASE
一丝不挂；赤身裸体
If someone is in a state of undress, they do not have all their clothes on.
例：Every cover showed a woman in a state of undress.每个封面上都有一名裸体女子。

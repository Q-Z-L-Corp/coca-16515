ADJ
(事件或时间)即将到来的，即将发生的
A coming event or time is an event or time that will happen soon.
例：This obviously depends on the weather in the coming months...这显然取决于未来几个月的天气。
例：They talk of the coming battle.他们谈论着即将到来的战斗。

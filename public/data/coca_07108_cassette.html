N-COUNT
(盒式)磁带，录像带
A cassette is a small, flat, rectangular plastic case containing magnetic tape which is used for recording and playing back sound or film.
例：His two albums released on cassette have sold more than 10 million copies.他的两张专辑的磁带销量突破了1,000万。

N-COUNT
(胶卷)暗盒;底片盒
A cassette is the container or case for the film that you load into a camera.

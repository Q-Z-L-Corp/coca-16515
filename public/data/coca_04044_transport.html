transport

N-UNCOUNT
交通工具;运输工具
Transport refers to any vehicle that you can travel in or carry goods in.
例：Have you got your own transport?...你有自己的交通工具吗？
例：Which type of transport do you prefer?你更喜欢哪种类型的代步工具？

N-UNCOUNT
交通运输系统;公共交通(网)
Transport is a system for taking people or goods from one place to another, for example using buses or trains.
例：The extra money could be spent on improving public transport...多余的资金可以用于改善公共交通系统。
例：The sudden onset of winter caused havoc with rail and air transport…冬天的突然降临让铁路和航空运输系统陷入混乱。

N-UNCOUNT
运输;运送
Transport is the activity of taking goods or people from one place to another in a vehicle.
例：Local production virtually eliminates transport costs.本地生产实际上消除了运输成本。

VERB
运输;运送
To transport people or goods somewhere is to take them from one place to another in a vehicle.
例：There's no petrol, so it's very difficult to transport goods...没有汽油，因此很难运输货物。
例：They use tankers to transport the oil to Los Angeles...他们用油轮把石油输送到洛杉矶。

VERB
使身处他境
If you say that you are transported to another place or time, you mean that something causes you to feel that you are living in the other place or at the other time.
例：Dr Drummond felt that he had been transported into a world that rivalled the Arabian Nights...德拉蒙德博士觉得自己仿佛被带入了一个可与《天方夜谭》相媲美的世界。
例：In a dream you can be transported back in time…在梦里你可以回到过去。

N-COUNT
(军用)运输工具;(尤指)运输机
A military or troop transport is a military vehicle, especially a plane, that is used to carry soldiers or equipment.

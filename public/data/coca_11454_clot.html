N-COUNT
(血液的)凝块
A clot is a sticky lump that forms when blood dries up or becomes thick.
例：He needed emergency surgery to remove a blood clot from his brain.他需要进行紧急手术以清除脑部的一处血块。

VERB
(血液)凝结成块
When blood clots, it becomes thick and forms a lump.
例：The patient's blood refused to clot...病人的血液无法凝结。
例：Aspirin apparently thins the blood and inhibits clotting.阿司匹林能显著地稀释血液、抑制血液凝结。

insane 
英[ɪnˈseɪn] 美[ɪnˈsen]
 
adj.疯狂的；精神病的；非常愚蠢的；
 "

ADJ
精神错乱的;精神失常的;疯狂的
Someone who is insane has a mind that does not work in a normal way, with the result that their behaviour is very strange.
例：Some people simply can't take it and they just go insane...一些人完全承受不了，结果就精神失常了。
例：Agnes was a battered woman who had killed, in self-defense, while temporarily insane.阿格尼丝是个备受虐待的女人,她在一时精神错乱下出于自我防卫杀了人。

ADJ-GRADED
蠢极的;荒唐的
If you describe a decision or action as insane, you think it is very foolish or excessive.
例：I said, 'Listen, this is completely insane.'我说：“听着,这简直荒唐之极。”
例：...insane jealousy.不可理喻的嫉妒心

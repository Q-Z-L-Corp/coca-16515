didactic 
英[daɪˈdæktɪk] 美[daɪˈdæktɪk]
 
adj.教学的；教导的；（指人）学究式的；迂腐的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
教导的;教诲的;(尤指)道德说教的
Something that is didactic is intended to teach people something, especially a moral lesson.
例：In totalitarian societies, art exists for didactic purposes.在极权社会里，艺术只为教化的目的而存在。

ADJ-GRADED
好灌输的;好说教的
Someone who is didactic tells people things rather than letting them find things out or discussing things.
例：He is more didactic in his approach to the learning process.他在教学过程中侧重于灌输。

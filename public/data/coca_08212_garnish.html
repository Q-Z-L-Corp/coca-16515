garnish 
英[ˈgɑ:nɪʃ] 美[ˈgɑrnɪʃ]
 
vt.装饰，文饰；给…加配菜；[法]传讯；n.装饰，装饰品；华丽的词藻；配菜；<俚>勒索金；
 "

N-VAR
（菜肴的)装饰菜，配菜
A garnish is a small amount of salad, herbs, or other food that is used to decorate cooked or prepared food.
例：...a garnish of chopped raw onion, tomato and fresh coriander...用生洋葱末、西红柿和新鲜香菜切好后搭配的装饰菜
例：Reserve some watercress for garnish.留点豆瓣菜作配菜。

VERB
给（菜肴）加装饰菜
If you garnish cooked or prepared food, you decorate it with a garnish.
例：She had finished the vegetables and was garnishing the roast.她已经做好了蔬菜，正在给烤肉添加饰菜。

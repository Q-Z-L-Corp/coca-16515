melodic 
英[məˈlɒdɪk] 美[məˈlɑdɪk]

 "

ADJ
旋律(上)的
Melodic means relating to melody.
例：...Schubert's effortless gift for melodic invention.舒伯特驾轻就熟的谱曲天分

ADJ-GRADED
旋律优美的;悦耳动听的
Music that is melodic has beautiful tunes in it.
例：Wonderfully melodic and tuneful, his songs have made me weep.他的歌旋律优美、悦耳动听，让我潸然泪下。

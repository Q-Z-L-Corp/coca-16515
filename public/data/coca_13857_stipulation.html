VERB
规定;明确要求
If you stipulate a condition or stipulate that something must be done, you say clearly that it must be done.
例：She could have stipulated that she would pay when she collected the computer...她本可以要求她来取电脑时付钱。
例：International rules stipulate the number of foreign entrants.国际规则规定了外国参赛者的人数。

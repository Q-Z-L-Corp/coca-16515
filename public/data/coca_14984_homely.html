homely 
英[ˈhəʊmli] 美[ˈhoʊmli]
 
adj.<尤英>家常的；平凡的；<美>（人）不好看的；相貌平庸的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
像家一样舒适的
If you describe a room or house as homely, you like it because you feel comfortable and relaxed there.
例：I wanted a homely room but I wanted it to look smart, too...我想要一个像家一样舒适的房间，但也希望它看起来很漂亮。
例：We try and provide a very homely atmosphere.我们试图营造一种非常舒适放松的气氛。

ADJ-GRADED
（食物）简单的，普通的，家常的
Homely food is simple and ordinary.
例：Scottish baking is homely, comforting and truly good.苏格兰烘烤食品简单、温馨，吃起来十分美味可口。

ADJ-GRADED
（女子）热情亲切的，家庭主妇型的
If you describe a woman as homely, you mean that she has a warm, comforting manner and looks like someone who would enjoy being at home and running a family.
例：Mrs Jones was a pleasant, homely person with a ready smile.琼斯夫人和蔼亲切，总是面带微笑。

ADJ-GRADED
相貌平平的
If you say that someone is homely, you mean that they are not very attractive to look at.
例：The man was homely and overweight.该男子其貌不扬，身材肥胖。

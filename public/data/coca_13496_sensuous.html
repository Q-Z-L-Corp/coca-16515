sensuous 
英[ˈsenʃuəs] 美[ˈsɛnʃuəs]
 
adj.感觉（上）的；感官的；（引起）美感的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
愉悦感官的;给人以美感的
Something that is sensuous gives pleasure to the mind or body through the senses.
例：The film is ravishing to look at and boasts a sensuous musical score...这部电影画面迷人，配乐悦耳动听。
例：It is a sensuous but demanding car to drive.这车坐上去很舒服，但需要高超的驾驶技术。

ADJ-GRADED
性感的;耽于肉欲的
Someone or something that is sensuous shows or suggests a great liking for sexual pleasure.
例：...his sensuous young mistress, Marie-Therese.他年轻性感的情妇玛丽－特蕾泽
例：...wide sensuous lips...丰阔而性感的嘴唇

chest 
英[tʃest] 美[tʃɛst]
 
n.胸部，胸腔；箱子；金库；资金；
 "

N-COUNT
胸;胸部;胸口;胸膛
Your chest is the top part of the front of your body where your ribs, lungs, and heart are.
例：He crossed his arms over his chest...他双手交叉，放在胸前。
例：He was shot in the chest...他胸部中弹。

N-COUNT
大箱子；大柜子
A chest is a large, heavy box used for storing things.
例：At the very bottom of the chest were his carving tools.箱子最底下是他的雕刻工具。
例：...a treasure chest.藏宝箱

PHRASE
倾诉胸中的不快;吐苦水
If you get something off your chest, you talk about something that has been worrying you.
例：I feel it's done me good to get it off my chest.我感觉吐吐苦水对我有好处。

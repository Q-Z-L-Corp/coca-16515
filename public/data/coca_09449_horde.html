N-COUNT
（熙攘、纷扰的）一大帮人，一大伙人
If you describe a crowd of people as a horde, you mean that the crowd is very large and excited and, often, rather frightening or unpleasant.
例：This attracts hordes of tourists to Las Vegas.这吸引了成群结队的游客到拉斯韦加斯来。
例：...a horde of people was screaming for tickets.一大群人在嚷着要票。

N-COUNT
鸿沟；严重分歧；巨大的差距
A gulf is an important or significant difference between two people, things, or groups.
例：Within society, there is a growing gulf between rich and poor.社会上的贫富差距正在逐渐加大。
例：...the gulf between rural and urban life.城乡生活的巨大差异

N-COUNT
海湾
A gulf is a large area of sea which extends a long way into the surrounding land.
例：Hurricane Andrew was last night heading into the Gulf of Mexico.昨晚，飓风“安德鲁”进入了墨西哥湾。

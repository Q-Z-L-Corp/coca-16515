N-COUNT
改变；变更
An alteration is a change in or to something.
例：Making some simple alterations to your diet will make you feel fitter...对日常饮食做些简单的调整就会让你身体更健康。
例：The structural alterations made to the house were planned with Gail's help.对房屋结构调整的规划是在盖尔的帮助下完成的。

N-UNCOUNT
更改；改动
The alteration of something is the process of changing it.
例：Her jacket was at the boutique waiting for alteration.她的短上衣放在时装店里，要做些改动。

exist 
英[ɪgˈzɪst] 美[ɪɡˈzɪst]
 
vi.存在；生存；生活；继续存在；
 "

VERB
存在;实际上有
If something exists, it is present in the world as a real thing.
例：He thought that if he couldn't see something, it didn't exist...他认为自己看不见的东西就不存在。
例：Research opportunities exist in a wide range of pure and applied areas of entomology...纯理论昆虫学和应用昆虫学的众多领域都存在着研究机会。

VERB
（尤指在逆境中）生存，生活
To exist means to live, especially under difficult conditions or with very little food or money.
例：I exist from one visit to the next...我的生活就是一个接一个的访问。
例：I was barely existing.我勉强维持生计。

parched 
英[pɑ:tʃt] 美[pɑrtʃt]
 
adj.炎热的；v.（使）焦干， （使）干透( parch的过去式和过去分词 )；使（某人）极口渴；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（尤指地面或植物）焦干的，焦枯的
If something, especially the ground or a plant, is parched, it is very dry, because there has been no rain.
例：The clouds gathered and showers poured down upon the parched earth.乌云聚涌，倾盆大雨落在焦干的大地上。
例：...a hill of parched brown grass.满山的褐色枯草

ADJ-GRADED
（嘴、喉咙或嘴唇）干燥的
If your mouth, throat, or lips are parched, they are unpleasantly dry.

ADJ-GRADED
干渴的；焦渴的
If you say that you are parched, you mean that you are very thirsty.

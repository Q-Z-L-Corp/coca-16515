tidy 
英[ˈtaɪdi] 美[ˈtaɪdi]
 
adj.整洁的，整齐的；（数量）相当大的；相当好的；健康的；n.盛零碎物品的容器；椅子的背罩；vt.& vi.使整洁；弄整齐；使有条理；整理，收拾；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
整洁的;整齐的
Something that is tidy is neat and is arranged in an organized way.
例：Having a tidy desk can seem impossible if you have a busy, demanding job...如果工作忙、难度大，要想保持桌面整洁似乎不太可能。
例：I'll do your garden, I'll keep that tidy for you.我会帮你弄弄花园，帮你保持它的整洁。

ADJ-GRADED
整洁的;有条理的
Someone who is tidy likes everything to be neat and arranged in an organized way.
例：She's obsessively tidy, always hoovering and polishing.她有洁癖，整天不是用吸尘器吸尘，就是擦来擦去。

VERB
收拾，整理(房间、橱柜等)
When you tidy a place such as a room or cupboard, you make it neat by putting things in their proper places.
例：She made her bed, and tidied her room.她整理了床铺，收拾了自己的房间。

ADJ-GRADED
一大笔(钱)
A tidy amount of money is a large amount.
例：The opportunities are there to make a tidy profit.赚大钱的机会就在那儿摆着。

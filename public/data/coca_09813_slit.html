slit 
英[slɪt] 美[slɪt]
 
vt.切开，撕开；使有狭缝；n.狭长切口；裂缝；投币口；
 "

VERB
切开;剖开;割开
If you slit something, you make a long narrow cut in it.
例：They say somebody slit her throat...他们说有人割断了她的喉咙。
例：He began to slit open each envelope...他开始裁开每个信封。

N-COUNT
狭长的切口;裂口
A slit is a long narrow cut.
例：Make a slit in the stem about half an inch long.在树干上切一道大约半英寸长的口子。

N-COUNT
狭缝
A slit is a long narrow opening in something.
例：She watched them through a slit in the curtains.她透过窗帘上的一道窄缝注视着他们。

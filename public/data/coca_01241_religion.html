religion 
英[rɪˈlɪdʒən] 美[rɪˈlɪdʒən]
 
n.宗教；支配自己生活的大事；教派；心爱的事物；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
宗教信仰;宗教活动
Religion is belief in a god or gods and the activities that are connected with this belief, such as praying or worshipping in a building such as a church or temple.
例：...his understanding of Indian philosophy and religion...他对印度哲学和宗教信仰的理解
例：Do avoid potentially contentious subjects such as religion, sex or politics.一定要避免可能引起争议的话题，如宗教、性和政治。

N-COUNT
宗教;教派
A religion is a particular system of belief in a god or gods and the activities that are connected with this system.
例：...the Christian religion.基督教

PHRASE
(突然)信教，皈依（某种）宗教
If you say that someone has got religion, you are referring in a mocking way to the fact that they have suddenly decided to follow a particular religion.
例：This guy got religion about a year back.这家伙一年前信了教。

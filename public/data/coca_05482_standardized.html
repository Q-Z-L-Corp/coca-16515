VERB
使标准化;使合乎标准
To standardize things means to change them so that they all have the same features.
例：There is a drive both to standardise components and to reduce the number of models on offer...正在为实现零部件标准化和减少推出的型号数量而努力。
例：He feels standardized education does not benefit those children who are either below or above average intelligence.他认为标准化教育对那些智力水平低于或超出平均水平的儿童没有好处。

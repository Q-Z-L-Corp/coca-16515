ADJ
一代的；两代之间的
Generational means relating to a particular generation, or to the relationship between particular generations.
例：People's lifestyles are usually fixed by generational habits and fashions.人们的生活方式通常取决于那一代的生活习惯和流行时尚。
例：...a generational conflict within the Asian community.亚裔群体内部的代际冲突

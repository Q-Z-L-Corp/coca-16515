N-COUNT
（外部的）屋顶
A rooftop is the outside part of the roof of a building.
例：Below us you could glimpse the rooftops of a few small villages.我们可以瞥见脚下一些小村庄的屋顶。

PHRASE
公开宣布;使尽人皆知
If you shout something from the rooftops, you say it or announce it in a very public way.
例：When we have something definite to say, we shall be shouting it from the rooftops.如果有确切的消息要宣布，我们会让大家都知道。

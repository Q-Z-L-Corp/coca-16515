homage 
英[ˈhɒmɪdʒ] 美[ˈhɑmɪdʒ]
 
n.敬意；效忠；顺从；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
尊敬;崇敬;敬意
Homage is respect shown towards someone or something you admire, or to a person in authority.
例：Palace has released two marvellous films that pay homage to our literary heritage...皇宫影业公司发行了两部颂扬我们文学遗产的精彩影片。
例：At his coronation he received the homage of kings from Wales, Northumbria and Scotland.在他的加冕典礼上，威尔士、诺森布里亚和苏格兰的国王向他宣誓效忠。

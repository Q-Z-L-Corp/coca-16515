affiliation 
英[əˌfɪliˈeɪʃn] 美[əˌfɪliˈeɪʃn]
 
n.加入；附属；联系或关系；接纳；
 "

N-VAR
（组织之间的）密切联系，隶属关系
If one group has an affiliation with another group, it has a close or official connection with it.
例：The group has no affiliation to any political party...该团体不隶属于任何政党。
例：The officer cited the federation's rule on affiliation.该官员引用了联邦政府对于隶属关系的规定。

N-VAR
（个人之间或个人与组织之间的）联系，从属关系
If you have an affiliation with a group or another person, you have a close or official connection with them.
例：...Johnson's affiliation with shoe company Nike...约翰逊就职于耐克鞋业公司
例：They asked what her political affiliations were.他们询问她属于哪个政党。

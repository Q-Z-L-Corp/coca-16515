VERB
使厌恶；使惊骇
If something appals you, it disgusts you because it seems so bad or unpleasant.
例：His ignorance appals me...他的无知让我震惊。
例：My wife now looks like her mother, which appals me.我老婆现在看着就跟丈母娘似的，可吓人了。

delineate

 "

VERB
刻画;描述;阐明
If you delineate something such as an idea or situation, you describe it or define it, often in a lot of detail.
例：Biography must to some extent delineate characters...传记必须在一定程度上描绘人物。
例：The relationship between Church and State was delineated in a formal agreement.教会和政府之间的关系在一份正式的协议中作了阐明。

VERB
划出，划定(边界)
If you delineate a border, you say exactly where it is going to be.
例：...an agreement to delineate the border.划界协议

adj.油腻的；肥胖的；多脂肪的；n.胖子；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(食物)含脂肪多的,肥的,高脂的
Fatty food contains a lot of fat.
例：Don't eat fatty food or chocolates...不要吃高脂食物或巧克力。
例：The report dispels the myth that Northerners have a fattier diet than people in the south.该报告消除了北方人比南方人膳食脂肪含量高的谬论。

ADJ
(酸、组织等)含脂肪的
Fatty acids or fatty tissues, for example, contain or consist of fat.
例：...fatty acids...脂肪酸
例：The woman lost about 1.8kg of fatty tissue during the week's fast.那个女人在禁食一星期后减脂约 1.8 公斤。

N-COUNT
胖子;胖墩;肥仔
If you call someone a fatty, you are criticizing or insulting them for being fat.
例：Consuming this amount of food could turn these fit players into fatties...这些体格健壮的运动员吃这么多会变成胖子的。
例：'Get another one, Fatty,' said Jerry.“再拿一个，胖子。”杰里说。

diseased 
英[dɪˈzi:zd] 美[dɪˈzizd]
 
adj.患病的；有弊病的；[植]有病害的；不健全的；v.使生病(disease的过去分词)；传染；
 "

ADJ
有病的;病变的;害病的
Something that is diseased is affected by a disease.
例：The arteries are diseased and a transplant is the only hope...动脉已经发生病变，移植是唯一的希望。
例：Clear away dead or diseased plants.清除掉枯死和染上病虫害的植物。

ADJ
(思想)病态的，不健康的
If you say that someone's mind is diseased, you are emphasizing that you think it is not normal or balanced.
例：Gardner describes the book as 'the product of a diseased and evil mind'.加德纳称这本书为“一个病态恶魔的作品”。

adj.无能的，笨的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
一无所知的;一窍不通的
If you describe someone as clueless, you are showing your disapproval of the fact that they do not know anything about a particular subject or that they are incapable of doing a particular thing properly.
例：I came into adult life clueless about a lot of things that most people take for granted...我已成年，却对许多大部分人认为理所当然的事情一窍不通。
例：We have found some drivers clueless as to the law.我们发现有些司机对这一法规一无所知。

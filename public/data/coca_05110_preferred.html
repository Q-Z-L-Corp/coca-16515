VERB
更喜欢；偏好
If you prefer someone or something, you like that person or thing better than another, and so you are more likely to choose them if there is a choice.
例：Does he prefer a particular sort of music?...他有特别喜欢的音乐吗？
例：I became a teacher because I preferred books and people to politics...我当了老师，因为我更喜欢书本和人而不是权术。

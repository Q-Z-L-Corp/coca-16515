lucid 
英[ˈlu:sɪd] 美[ˈlusɪd]
 
adj.清楚的，明白的；神志清醒的；清澈的，透明的；[动，植]光滑的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
明晰的；明了易懂的
Lucid writing or speech is clear and easy to understand.
例：...a lucid account of the history of mankind...对人类历史的清楚记述
例：His prose is always lucid and compelling.他的散文素来简洁、有力。

ADJ-GRADED
（病后或糊涂过后）清醒的，头脑清楚的，思路清晰的
If someone is lucid, they are thinking clearly again after a period of illness or confusion.
例：He wasn't very lucid, he didn't quite know where he was.他神志不是很清醒，不太知道自己在哪里。

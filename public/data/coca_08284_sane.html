sane 
英[seɪn] 美[sen]
 
adj.心智健全的；神志正常的；明智的；稳健的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
心智健全的;神志正常的
Someone who is sane is able to think and behave normally and reasonably, and is not mentally ill.
例：He seemed perfectly sane...他似乎神志完全正常。
例：It wasn't the act of a sane person.心智健全的人做不出这种事来。

ADJ-GRADED
清醒的;明智的;合情合理的
If you refer to a sane person, action, or system, you mean one that you think is reasonable and sensible.
例：No sane person wishes to see conflict or casualties.理智的人都不愿意看到冲突或伤亡。
例：...a sane and safe energy policy.安全合理的能源政策

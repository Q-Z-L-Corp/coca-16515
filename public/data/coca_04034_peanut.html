peanut 
英[ˈpi:nʌt] 美[ˈpiˌnʌt]
 
n.花生；微不足道的人，小人物；很少的钱；adj.微不足道的；
 "

N-COUNT
花生
Peanuts are small nuts that grow under the ground. Peanuts are often eaten as a snack, especially roasted and salted.
例：...a packet of peanuts...一包花生
例：Add 2 tablespoons of peanut oil.加两汤匙花生油。

N-PLURAL
少得可怜的钱；小钱
If you say that a sum of money is peanuts, you mean that it is very small.
例：The cost was peanuts compared to a new kitchen...与建新厨房相比，那笔费用微不足道。
例：The jobs they offer pay peanuts.他们提供的工作报酬很低。

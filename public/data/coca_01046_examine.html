examine 
英[ɪgˈzæmɪn] 美[ɪɡˈzæmɪn]
 
vt.检查，调查；考试；诊察；审问；vi.检查；调查；
 "

VERB
仔细检查;细查
If you examine something, you look at it carefully.
例：He examined her passport and stamped it...他仔细检查了她的护照，然后在上面盖了章。
例：Forensic scientists are examining what police believe to have been the bombers' car.法医学家正在对一辆警方怀疑属于投弹者的汽车进行仔细检查。

VERB
（医生）诊察，检查
If a doctor examines you, he or she looks at your body, feels it, or does simple tests in order to check how healthy you are.
例：Another doctor examined her and could still find nothing wrong...另外一名医生对她进行了诊察，仍旧没有发现任何问题。
例：He was examined again and then prescribed a different herbal medicine.医生又对他诊察了一遍，然后开了另外一种草药。

VERB
仔细研究;审查;剖析
If an idea, proposal, or plan is examined, it is considered very carefully.
例：I have given the matter much thought, examining all the possible alternatives...这个问题我已经考虑了很多，分析了各种可能的选择。
例：The plans will be examined by EU environment ministers.欧盟各国环境部部长将仔细研究这些计划。

VERB
测验;考核
If you are examined, you are given a formal test in order to show your knowledge of a subject.
例：...learning to cope with the pressures of being judged and examined by our teachers.学会应对老师评判和测验时的压力

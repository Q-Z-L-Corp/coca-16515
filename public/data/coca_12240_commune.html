commune 
英[ˈkɒmju:n] 美[ˈkɑmjun]
 
n.公社；亲密的会谈；vi.交谈，谈心；密切联系；<美>接受圣餐；
 "

N-COUNT
群居团体;社群;公社
A commune is a group of people who live together and share everything.
例：Mack lived in a commune.麦克生活在一个群居团体里。

N-COUNT
(法国等国家有自己的地方议会的)市镇
In France and some other countries, a commune is a town, village, or area which has its own council.

VERB
与(动物、精灵或大自然)交流;与…融为一体
If you say that someone is communing with an animal or spirit, or with nature, you mean that they appear to be communicating with it.
例：He was so happy communing with the dolphin in Dingle Bay...他在丁格尔湾与海豚交流的时候特别开心。
例：She would happily trot behind him as he set off to commune with nature.他出去亲近大自然的时候，她就会一路小跑，快活地跟在他后面。

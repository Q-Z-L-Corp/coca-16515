chafe 
英[tʃeɪf] 美[tʃef]
 
vt.& vi.擦热（尤指皮肤）；擦痛；发怒；惹怒；n.擦伤；
 "

V-ERG
(皮肤)擦破；（使）擦伤
If your skin chafes or is chafed by something, it becomes sore as a result of something rubbing against it.
例：My shorts were chafing my thighs...我的短裤把大腿磨得生疼。
例：She turned him in the bed so that the sheets wouldn't chafe his skin into sores...她帮他翻了个身，免得他生褥疮。

VERB
(因…)生气，恼怒
If you chafe at something such as a restriction, you feel annoyed about it.
例：He had chafed at having to take orders from another...他对于不得不听命于人很恼火。
例：He was chafing under the company's new ownership.他在新老板的手下工作得很不开心。

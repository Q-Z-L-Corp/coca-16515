endless 
英[ˈendləs] 美[ˈɛndlɪs]
 
adj.无尽的，无边的；没完没了的；永久的；环形的，无端的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
无止境的;无边无际的;无休止的
If you say that something is endless, you mean that it is very large or lasts for a very long time, and it seems as if it will never stop.
例：They turned into an endless street...他们转入一条看不到尽头的街道。
例：The war was endless.那是场无休止的战争。

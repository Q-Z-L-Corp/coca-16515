newcomer 
英[ˈnju:kʌmə(r)] 美[ˈnukʌmə(r)]
 
n.新来的人；新手；新生事物；
 "

N-COUNT
新来的人;新参加的人;新手
A newcomer is a person who has recently arrived in a place, joined an organization, or started a new activity.
例：He must be a newcomer to town and he obviously didn't understand our local customs...他一定是刚到镇上来的，明显不了解我们这儿的风俗。
例：The candidates are both relative newcomers to politics.两位候选人相对而言都是政坛新人。

N-COUNT
新近出现的事物;新得到的东西
A newcomer is something which has not existed before or been available before.
例：The company's latest newcomer is a 4 x 4 estate with a 2.2 litre petrol engine.公司最近新到了一辆有2.2升汽油发动机的4 x 4客货两用车。
例：...last year's aggressive price slashing by campsite newcomer, Airtours.野营服务新军英国空中旅游公司去年咄咄逼人的大幅削价

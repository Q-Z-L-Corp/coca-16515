N-COUNT
大船;轮船;舰
A ship is a large boat which carries passengers or cargo.
例：Within ninety minutes the ship was ready for departure...90分钟之内轮船准备出发。
例：We went by ship over to America.我们搭轮船去美国。

VERB
运送;运输;船运
If people or things are shipped somewhere, they are sent there on a ship or by some other means of transport.
例：Food is being shipped to drought-stricken Southern Africa.食物正被运往遭受旱灾的非洲南部。

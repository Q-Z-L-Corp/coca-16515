英[rɪf'rɪdʒəreɪtɪd] 美[rɪf'rɪdʒəreɪtɪd]

VERB
（常指为了保存食物而将其）冷藏
If you refrigerate food, you make it cold, for example by putting it in a fridge, usually in order to preserve it.
例：Refrigerate the dough overnight.将面团冷藏过夜。
例：...a lorry with refrigerated cargo.装有冷藏货物的卡车

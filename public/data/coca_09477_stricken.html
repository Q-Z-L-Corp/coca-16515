受煎熬的;患病的;遭受挫折的;受灾的
If a person or place is stricken by something such as an unpleasant feeling, an illness, or a natural disaster, they are severely affected by it.
例：...a family stricken by genetically inherited cancer...受遗传性癌症折磨的一家
例：Foreign aid workers will not be allowed into the stricken areas.国外救援人员不准进入灾区。

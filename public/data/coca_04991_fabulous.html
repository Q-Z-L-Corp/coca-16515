fabulous 
英[ˈfæbjələs] 美[ˈfæbjələs]
 
adj.极好的，极妙的；（美貌）惊人的；寓言般的；难以置信的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
极好的;绝妙的
If you describe something as fabulous, you are emphasizing that you like it a lot or think that it is very good.
例：This is a fabulous album. It's fresh, varied, fun...这张专辑真棒，新颖、有趣且富于变化。
例：The scenery and weather were fabulous.景色和天气都好极了。

ADJ-GRADED
(成功)极大的;(美貌)惊人的
If you talk about someone's fabulous beauty, for example, or their fabulous success, you are emphasizing that they are extremely beautiful or extremely successful.
例：Despite his fabulous wealth, he is very much a man of the people.他虽然腰缠万贯，却非常亲民。

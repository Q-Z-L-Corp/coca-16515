barometer 
英[bəˈrɒmɪtə(r)] 美[bəˈrɑmɪtə(r)]
 
n.气压计；晴雨表；显示变化的事物；<比喻>（舆论等的）标记；
 "

N-COUNT
气压计；气压表；晴雨表
A barometer is an instrument that measures air pressure and shows when the weather is changing.

N-COUNT
变化的标志；晴雨表
If something is a barometer of a particular situation, it indicates how things are changing or how things are likely to develop.
例：In past presidential elections, Missouri has been a barometer of the rest of the country...在过去的总统选举中，密苏里州一直是国内其他州的晴雨表。
例：Our sleep pattern is a barometer to our psychological well-being.我们的睡眠模式是心理健康的晴雨表。

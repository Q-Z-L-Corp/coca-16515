admire 
英[ədˈmaɪə(r)] 美[ædˈmaɪr]
 
vt.赞赏；称赞；欣赏；<美口>想要；
 "

VERB
钦佩；欣赏；羡慕
If you admire someone or something, you like and respect them very much.
例：I admired her when I first met her and I still think she's marvellous...初次见面时，我就非常佩服她，时至今日我仍然认为她很出色。
例：He admired the way she had coped with life...他欣赏她处世的方式。

VERB
欣赏；观赏
If you admire someone or something, you look at them with pleasure.
例：We took time to stop and admire the view.我们特意驻足欣赏风景。

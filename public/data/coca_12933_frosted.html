frosted 
英[ˈfrɒstɪd] 美[ˈfrɔstɪd]
 
adj.被霜覆盖的，受霜害的；
 "

ADJ
(玻璃)磨砂的，毛面的
Frosted glass is glass that you cannot see through clearly.
例：The top half of the door to his office was of frosted glass.他办公室门的上半部分是磨砂玻璃。

ADJ
结霜的;被霜覆盖的
Frosted means covered with frost.
例：...the frosted trees.被霜覆盖的树木

ADJ
覆有霜状物的
Frosted means covered with something that looks like frost.
例：...frosted blue eye shadow.蓝色霜状眼影

ADJ
覆有糖霜的
Frosted means covered with icing.
例：...a plate of frosted cupcakes.一盘撒有糖霜的纸杯蛋糕

ADJ-GRADED
震耳欲聋的
A deafening noise is a very loud noise.
例：...the deafening roar of fighter jets taking off.喷气式战斗机起飞时震耳欲聋的轰鸣声

ADJ
(沉默）没有任何回应的，死一般的
If you say there was a deafening silence, you are emphasizing that there was no reaction or response to something that was said or done.
例：When we ask people for suggestions we get a deafening silence.当我们向大家寻求建议时，得到的只是一片死寂的沉默。

halo 
英[ˈheɪləʊ] 美[ˈheɪloʊ]
 
n.光环；荣光；（日月等的）晕；vt.使有晕轮；围以光环；vi.成晕轮；
 "

N-COUNT
（绘画中圣人、天使头上的）光环，光轮
A halo is a circle of light that is shown in pictures round the head of a holy figure such as a saint or angel.

N-COUNT
晕;晕状物
A halo is a circle of light round a person or thing, or something that looks like a circle of light.
例：The sun had a faint halo round it...环绕太阳有圈淡淡的光晕。
例：She angrily slung a scarf around her head, flattening her halo of hair.她气鼓鼓地把头巾裹在头上，发髻都被压扁了。

n.肥皂；肥皂剧；[化]皂，脂肪酸盐；vt.抹肥皂；用肥皂擦洗；<俚>拍…的马屁，吹捧（某人）；vi.用肥皂擦洗；

N-MASS
肥皂
Soap is a substance that you use with water for washing yourself or sometimes for washing clothes.
例：...a bar of lavender soap.一块薰衣草香皂
例：...a large packet of soap powder.一大盒肥皂粉

VERB
用肥皂擦洗;往…上面抹肥皂
If you soap yourself, you rub soap on your body in order to wash yourself.
例：She soaped herself all over.她把全身涂满肥皂。

ADJ
近海的;沿海的
Coastal is used to refer to things that are in the sea or on the land near a coast.
例：Local radio stations serving coastal areas often broadcast forecasts for yachtsmen...服务沿海地区的地方广播电台经常为驾驶游艇出海的人播报天气预报。
例：The fish are on sale from our own coastal waters.出售的鱼来自我们自己的近海水域。

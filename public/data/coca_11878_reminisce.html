reminisce 
英[ˌremɪˈnɪs] 美[ˌrɛməˈnɪs]
 
vi.& vt.追忆；
 "

VERB
追忆;回忆;叙旧
If you reminisce about something from your past, you write or talk about it, often with pleasure.
例：Ray and I ate our meal and reminisced about the trip...雷和我吃着饭，回忆起那次旅行。
例：I don't like reminiscing because it makes me feel old.我不喜欢追忆往事，因为那会让我感觉变老了。

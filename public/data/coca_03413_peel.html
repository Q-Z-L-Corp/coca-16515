peel 
英[pi:l] 美[pil]
 
vt.剥皮；覆盖层脱落，剥落；vi.剥落；脱落；揭掉；表面起皮；n.果皮，蔬菜皮；长柄木铲；[英史]堡寨，堡宅；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（水果）皮，外皮
The peel of a fruit such as a lemon or an apple is its skin.
例：...grated lemon peel.擦碎的柠檬皮

VERB
剥皮；去皮；削皮
When you peel fruit or vegetables, you remove their skins.
例：She sat down in the kitchen and began peeling potatoes.她坐在厨房里，开始削土豆皮。

V-ERG
剥掉；剥落
If you peel off something that has been sticking to a surface or if it peels off, it comes away from the surface.
例：One of the kids was peeling plaster off the wall...其中一个孩子在剥墙上的灰泥。
例：It took me two days to peel off the labels...我花了两天时间才揭下这些标签。

VERB
掉漆
If a surface is peeling, the paint on it is coming away.
例：Its once-elegant white pillars are peeling.曾经典雅的白色立柱正在掉漆。

VERB
（通常因为晒伤而）脱皮，起皮
If you are peeling or if your skin is peeling, small pieces of skin are coming off, usually because you have been burned by the sun.
例：His face, at the moment, was peeling from sunburn.那时他的脸因为晒伤而脱皮。

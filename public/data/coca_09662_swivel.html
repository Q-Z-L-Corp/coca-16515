swivel 
英[ˈswɪvl] 美[ˈswɪvəl]
 
n.转节；转环；旋轴；旋转接头；vt.& vi.（使）旋转；在枢轴上转动；（把身子或脸等）转向另一方；转身；
 "

V-ERG
(使)旋转;(使)转动
If something swivels or if you swivel it, it turns around a central point so that it is facing in a different direction.
例：She swivelled her chair round and stared out across the back lawn...她把椅子转过来，向外凝视着屋后的草坪。
例：His chairs can swivel, but they can't move up or down.他的椅子能旋转，但是不能升降。

VERB
(突然)转身朝向
If you swivel in a particular direction, you turn suddenly in that direction.
例：He swivelled round to face Sarah.他转过身面对着萨拉。

V-ERG
(使)(头或眼睛)转动
If your head or your eyes swivel in a particular direction or if you swivel them in a particular direction, you quickly look in that direction.
例：Roger swivelled his head to look at her...罗杰转过头看着她。
例：His eyes swivelled from one girl to the other.他的目光在两个女孩身上打转。

VERB
尊敬;崇敬;敬重
If you revere someone or something, you respect and admire them greatly.
例：The Chinese revered corn as a gift from heaven...中国人将谷物奉为上天的恩赐。
例：Today he's still revered as the father of the nation.现在，他仍被尊为国父。

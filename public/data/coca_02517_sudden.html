sudden 
英[ˈsʌdn] 美[ˈsʌdn]
 
adj.突然的，未预见到的；急躁的，仓促的；快的，迅速的；急剧；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
突然的;骤然的;忽然的
Sudden means happening quickly and unexpectedly.
例：He had been deeply affected by the sudden death of his father-in-law...岳父的猝死使他十分伤心。
例：'I hope,' the stranger said, 'that the sudden change of venue did not inconvenience you.'...那位陌生人说：“我希望突然改变地点不会给您带来不便。”

PHRASE
突然地;出乎意料地
If something happens all of a sudden, it happens quickly and unexpectedly.
例：All of a sudden she didn't look sleepy any more.她突然看起来一点都不困了。

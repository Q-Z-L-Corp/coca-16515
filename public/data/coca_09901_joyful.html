joyful 
英[ˈdʒɔɪfl] 美[ˈdʒɔɪfəl]
 
adj.快乐的，高兴的；令人开心的，使人喜悦的；快意；融融；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人愉快的；使人高兴的
Something that is joyful causes happiness and pleasure.
例：Giving birth to a child is both painful and joyful...生小孩是件既痛苦又令人高兴的事。
例：A wedding is a joyful celebration of love.婚礼就是快乐的爱情庆典。

ADJ-GRADED
高兴的；快乐的；欣喜的
Someone who is joyful is extremely happy.
例：We're a very joyful people; we're very musical people and we love music.我们是非常快乐的民族；我们能歌善舞，热爱音乐。

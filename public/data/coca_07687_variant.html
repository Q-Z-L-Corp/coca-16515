N-COUNT
变体;变种;变化形式
A variant of a particular thing is something that has a different form to that thing, although it is related to it.
例：The quagga was a strikingly beautiful variant of the zebra...斑驴是一种极美丽的斑马变种。
例：An intriguing variant to pipelines would be to carry the water using the existing system of canals...除用管道外，利用现有的运河系统来输送水也很值得一试。

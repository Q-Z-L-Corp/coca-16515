padding 
英[ˈpædɪŋ] 美[ˈpædɪŋ]
 
n.衬料；衬垫；赘语；废话；v.给…装衬垫，加垫子( pad的现在分词 )；步行，放轻脚步走；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（柔软的）垫料，衬料
Padding is soft material which is put on something or inside it in order to make it less hard, to protect it, or to give it a different shape.
例：...the foam rubber padding on the headphones...头戴式耳机上的泡沫橡胶垫料
例：Players must wear padding to protect them from injury.运动员必须戴防护垫以免受伤。

N-UNCOUNT
冗词赘句；凑篇幅的废话
Padding is unnecessary words or information used to make a piece of writing or a speech longer.
例：Of the sonnet eleven of the lines are mere padding and say nothing.那首十四行诗中有11行都是废话，毫无意义。
例：...the kind of subject that politicians put in their speeches for a bit of padding.政客在演讲中凑篇幅的那类话题

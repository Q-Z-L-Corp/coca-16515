susceptible 
英[səˈseptəbl] 美[səˈsɛptəbəl]
 
adj.易受影响的；易受感染的；善感的；可以接受或允许的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
易受…影响的;易为…左右的
If you are susceptible to something or someone, you are very likely to be influenced by them.
例：Young people are the most susceptible to advertisements...年轻人最容易受广告的影响。
例：James was extremely susceptible to flattery...詹姆斯听几句奉承话就忘乎所以。

ADJ-GRADED
易受(伤)的;易患(病)的;体质过敏的
If you are susceptible to a disease or injury, you are very likely to be affected by it.
例：Walking with weights makes the shoulders very susceptible to injury...负重行走时肩膀很容易受伤。
例：Diesel exhaust is particularly aggravating to many susceptible individuals.许多体质敏感的人尤其反感柴油废气。

ADJ-GRADED
感情丰富的;善变的
A susceptible person is very easily influenced emotionally.
例：Hers was a susceptible nature.她很情绪化。

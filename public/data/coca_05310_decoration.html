decoration 
英[ˌdekəˈreɪʃn] 美[ˌdɛkəˈreʃən]
 
n.装饰，装潢；装饰品；装饰图案，装饰风格；奖章；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(室内的)装饰，装潢
The decoration of a room is its furniture, wallpaper, and ornaments.
例：The decoration and furnishings had to be practical enough for a family home...房子的装潢和家具都必须很实用，适合家居生活。
例：With its simple decoration, the main bedroom is a peaceful haven.主卧室装饰简单，像一处宁静的港湾。

N-VAR
装饰（的特定方面）
Decorations are features that are added to something in order to make it look more attractive.
例：The only wall decorations are candles and a single mirror...墙上唯一的装饰是几支蜡烛和一面镜子。
例：The planting of winter and spring-flowering bulbs will supply colourful decoration at a time when most gardens are looking fairly desolate.栽种冬春季节开花的鳞茎植物可以在大多数花园看上去还比较萧索的时候提供多彩的装点。

N-COUNT
(彩纸、气球等)装饰品，点缀物
Decorations are brightly coloured objects such as pieces of paper and balloons, which you put up in a room on special occasions to make it look more attractive.
例：Festive paper decorations had been hung from the ceiling.喜庆的彩纸已经挂在天花板上。

N-COUNT
勋章;奖章;荣誉称号
A decoration is an official title or honour which is given to someone, usually in the form of a medal, as a reward for military bravery or public service.
例：He was awarded several military decorations.他被授予了多枚军功章。

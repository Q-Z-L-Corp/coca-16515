ADV-GRADED
小心谨慎地；战战兢兢地
If you do something gingerly, you do it in a careful manner, usually because you expect it to be dangerous, unpleasant, or painful.
例：She was touching the dressing gingerly with both hands...她双手小心翼翼地抚摸着伤口处的绷带。
例：I drove gingerly past the security check points.我战战兢兢地开车经过安检站。

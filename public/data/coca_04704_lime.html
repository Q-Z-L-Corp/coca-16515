N-VAR
酸橙
A lime is a green fruit that tastes like a lemon. Limes grow on trees in tropical countries.
例：...peeled slices of lime...剥了皮的酸橙片
例：Oranges, lemons and limes were found to cure scurvy...人们发现橙子、柠檬和酸橙能治疗坏血病。

N-UNCOUNT
酸橙味饮料
Lime is a drink that tastes of limes.
例：...a pint of lager and lime.一品脱莱姆淡啤酒

N-COUNT
欧椴树
A lime is a large tree with pale green leaves. It is often planted in parks in towns and cities.
例：...dilapidated avenues of limes.破败不堪的欧椴树林阴道

N-UNCOUNT
石灰
Lime is a substance containing calcium. It is found in soil and water.
例：If your soil is very acid, add lime.如果土壤酸性太强，就加些石灰。

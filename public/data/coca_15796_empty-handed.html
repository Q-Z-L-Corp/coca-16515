ADJ
两手空空的;一无所获的
If you come away from somewhere empty-handed, you have failed to get what you wanted.
例：Delegates from the warring sides held a new round of peace talks but went away empty-handed...交战各方的代表进行了新一轮的和谈，但是毫无成果。
例：Shirley returned home empty-handed from her shopping trip.雪莉出去购物，回家时却两手空空。

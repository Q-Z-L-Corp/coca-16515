noticeably 
英['nəʊtɪsəblɪ] 美[ˈnotɪsəblɪ]
 
adv.显著地，明显地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
容易注意到的;显著的;明显的
Something that is noticeable is very obvious, so that it is easy to see, hear, or recognize.
例：It is noticeable that women do not have the rivalry that men have...显而易见，女性不像男人们那样好勇斗狠。
例：The most noticeable effect of these changes is in the way people are now working together.这些变化带来的最明显效果体现在人们现在一起工作的方式上。

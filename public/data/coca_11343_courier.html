courier 
英[ˈkʊriə(r)] 美[ˈkʊriɚ, ˈkə-, ˈkʌr-]
 
n.旅游团的服务员；导游；（传递信息或重要文件的）信使；通讯员；
 "

N-COUNT
快递员;信使;快递公司
A courier is a person who is paid to take letters and parcels direct from one place to another.
例：He worked as a motorcycle courier...他的工作是骑摩托车送快递。
例：The cheques were delivered to the bank by a private courier firm.支票由私人快递公司送到了银行。

N-COUNT
(旅游公司的)导游
A courier is a person employed by a travel company to look after people who are on holiday.

VERB
让快递员送;快递
If you courier something somewhere, you send it there by courier.
例：I couriered it to Darren in New York.我把它快递给纽约的达伦。

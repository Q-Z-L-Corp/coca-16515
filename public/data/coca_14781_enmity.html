N-VAR
敌意;仇恨;愤恨
Enmity is a feeling of hatred towards someone that lasts for a long time.
例：I think there is an historic enmity between them...我觉得他们之间的仇恨由来已久。
例：President Mitterrand arrived in Hanoi yesterday to bury old colonial and cold war enmities.密特朗总统昨天到达河内，以期结束长久以来殖民战争和冷战造成的敌意。

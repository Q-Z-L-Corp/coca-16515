bead 
英[bi:d] 美[bid]
 
n.有孔小珠，念珠；水珠，露珠；珠子项链；vt.用小珠装饰；使成串珠状；vi.形成珠状；起泡；
 "

N-COUNT
珠子；小珠
Beads are small pieces of coloured glass, wood, or plastic with a hole through the middle. Beads are often put together on a piece of string or wire to make jewellery.
例：...a string of beads.一串珠子
例：...a multicoloured bead necklace.一串彩珠项链

N-COUNT
（液体的）小珠，小滴
A bead of liquid or moisture is a small drop of it.
例：...beads of blood...血滴
例：He wiped away the beads of sweat on his forehead.他擦去了额头上的汗珠。

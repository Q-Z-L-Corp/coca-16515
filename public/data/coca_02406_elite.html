N-COUNT
精英;杰出人物
You can refer to the most powerful, rich, or talented people within a particular group, place, or society as the elite .
例：...a government comprised mainly of the elite...主要由精英组成的政府
例：We have a political elite in this country.我们国家有一群政治精英。

精英的;精锐的;最优秀的

ADJ
组织的；安排的；筹备的
Organizational abilities and methods relate to the way that work, activities, or events are planned and arranged.
例：Evelyn's excellent organisational skills were soon spotted by her employers...伊夫琳出色的组织能力很快就被老板发现。
例：Because we took the whole class for a complete afternoon session, organisational problems were minimal.我们带着全班上了整整一个下午的课，所以课堂安排上基本没什么问题。

ADJ
（组织或机构）结构的
Organizational means relating to the structure of an organization.
例：The police now recognise that big organisational changes are needed.警方现在意识到需要进行大幅度的机构调整。
例：...the organizational structure of British trade unions.英国工会的组织结构

ADJ
有关组织（或机构）的
Organizational means relating to organizations, rather than individuals.
例：This problem needs to be dealt with at an organizational level...这个问题需要在机构层面上解决。
例：There was no strong organizational base on which to build.缺乏一个坚实的组织基础。

nothingness 
英[ˈnʌθɪŋnəs] 美[ˈnʌθɪŋnɪs]
 
n.无；虚无；不存在；无生命；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
不存在;无
Nothingness is the fact of not existing.
例：There might be something beyond the grave, you know, and not nothingness.坟墓之外也许还有些什么，你知道，并非万事皆空。

N-UNCOUNT
虚无;空虚
Nothingness means complete emptiness.
例：Her eyes, glazed with the drug, stared with half closed lids at nothingness.她因吸食毒品而目光呆滞，双眼半睁半闭，茫然呆视。

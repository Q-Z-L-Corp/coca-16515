thirst 
英[θɜ:st] 美[θɜrst]
 
n.（口）渴；渴感；长期的干渴；vi.渴望；口渴；
 "

N-VAR
渴;口渴
Thirst is the feeling that you need to drink something.
例：Coca is well-known for reducing hunger, thirst and fatigue...人们都知道可乐能消饿、止渴和解乏。
例：Instead of tea or coffee, drink water to quench your thirst...渴了喝水，别喝茶或咖啡。

N-UNCOUNT
干渴;缺水
Thirst is the condition of not having enough to drink.
例：They died of thirst on the voyage.他们在航行途中因缺水而死亡。

N-SING
渴望;渴求
A thirst for something is a very strong desire for that thing.
例：Children show a real thirst for learning.孩子们对于学习表现出真诚的渴望。
例：...their ever-growing thirst for cash.他们对于金钱越来越强烈的渴求

VERB
渴望;渴求
If you say that someone thirsts for something, you mean that they have a strong desire for it.
例：We all thirst for the same things.我们都渴望得到同样的东西。

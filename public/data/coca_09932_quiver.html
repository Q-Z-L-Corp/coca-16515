quiver 
英[ˈkwɪvə(r)] 美[ˈkwɪvɚ]
 
vt.& vi.微颤，抖动；vt.使震动；n.抖动；颤音；箭筒；箭筒；
 "

VERB
颤动;抖动
If something quivers, it shakes with very small movements.
例：Her bottom lip quivered and big tears rolled down her cheeks.她的下唇颤抖着，豆大的泪珠顺着面颊滚落下来。

VERB
(人或声音由于愤怒、激动等)颤抖，发抖，打哆嗦
If you say that someone or their voice is quivering with an emotion such as rage or excitement, you mean that they are strongly affected by this emotion and show it in their appearance or voice.
例：Cooper arrived, quivering with rage...库珀来了，气得直打哆嗦。
例：Mack made his voice quiver with fear on these last two words.麦克说出最后这两个字时，吓得声音颤抖。

N-COUNT
箭袋;箭筒;箭囊

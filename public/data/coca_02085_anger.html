N-UNCOUNT
怒；愤怒
Anger is the strong emotion that you feel when you think that someone has behaved in an unfair, cruel, or unacceptable way.
例：He cried with anger and frustration...他愤怒而又沮丧得哭了起来。
例：Ellen felt both despair and anger at her mother.埃伦对她的母亲感到既绝望又生气。

VERB
使发怒；使生气；激怒
If something angers you, it makes you feel angry.
例：The decision to allow more offshore oil drilling angered some Californians.允许进行更多近海石油钻探的决定激怒了一些加利福尼亚人。

ADV
要不然；或者
You use alternatively to introduce a suggestion or to mention something different to what has just been stated.
例：Allow about eight hours for the drive from Calais. Alternatively, you can fly to Brive.从加来开车去，要留出大约8小时的时间。或者，你可以坐飞机前往布里夫。

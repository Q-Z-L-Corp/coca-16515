tapestry 
英[ˈtæpəstri] 美[ˈtæpɪstri]
 
n.挂毯；绣帷；织锦；vt.用挂毯装饰；在一块挂毯中制织出或描绘；罩上绣花罩毯；
 "

N-VAR
挂毯;壁毯;织锦;花毯
A tapestry is a large piece of heavy cloth with a picture sewn on it using coloured threads.
例：The seats of the chairs had been recovered in tapestry...椅座上又铺回了花毯。
例：He sat on a tapestry cushion next to the hearth...他坐在壁炉边上的一块绣花软垫上。

N-COUNT
各式各样的人(或物)
You can refer to something as a tapestry when it is made up of many varied types of people or things.
例：Hedgerows and meadows are thick with a tapestry of wild flowers.灌木树篱和草地中开满了五彩缤纷的野花。
例：...Chicago's political tapestry.芝加哥政坛的丰富多彩

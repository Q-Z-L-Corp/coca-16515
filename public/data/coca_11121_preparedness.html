N-UNCOUNT
（尤指对战争或灾难）有准备，做好准备
Preparedness is the state of being ready for something to happen, especially for war or a disaster.
例：The situation in the capital forced them to maintain military preparedness.首都的形势迫使他们时刻做好军事准备。
例：...red alert, the maximum state of preparedness.红色警戒，一级准备状态

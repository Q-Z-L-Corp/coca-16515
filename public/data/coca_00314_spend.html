spend 
英[spend] 美[spɛnd]
 
vt.& vi.用钱，花钱；vt.花费；消耗；花（时间）；度过；n.（为某目的或某段时间内的）花销，花费，开销；
 "

VERB
花(钱);用(钱)
When you spend money, you pay money for things that you want.
例：By the end of the holiday I had spent all my money...到假期结束时，我已经花光了自己所有的钱。
例：Businessmen spend enormous amounts advertising their products...商人们花费巨额资金为自己的商品做广告。

VERB
花费(时间或精力)
If you spend time or energy doing something, you use your time or effort doing it.
例：Engineers spend much time and energy developing brilliant solutions...工程师们花费很多时间和精力来开发完美的解决方案。
例：This energy could be much better spent taking some positive action.这些精力若用于采取某些积极行动的话，可能会更值得。

VERB
(在某处)度过(一段时间)
If you spend a period of time in a place, you stay there for a period of time.
例：We spent the night in a hotel.我们在一家旅馆过夜。

N-COUNT
开支;费用
The spend on a particular thing is the amount of money that is spent on it, or will be spent.
例：...the marketing and advertising spend.营销和广告开支

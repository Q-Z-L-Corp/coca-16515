termination 
英[ˌtɜ:mɪˈneɪʃn] 美[ˌtɜrmɪˈneɪʃn]
 
n.终止妊娠；结束；终止处；结局；
 "

V-ERG
(使)终止;(使)结束
When you terminate something or when it terminates, it ends completely.
例：Her next remark abruptly terminated the conversation...她接下来说的话使谈话戛然而止。
例：His contract terminates at the end of the season.他的合同在本赛季末终止。

VERB
终止(妊娠)
To terminate a pregnancy means to end it.
例：After a lot of agonizing she decided to terminate the pregnancy...经历一番痛苦挣扎后，她决定终止妊娠。
例：In the world as a whole, about ten per cent of all pregnancies are terminated.从全世界看来，所有怀孕的人中约 10% 的妊娠被终止了。

VERB
(火车、公共汽车)到达终点
When a train or bus terminates somewhere, it ends its journey there.
例：This train will terminate at Taunton.这列火车的终点站是汤顿。

feminine 
英[ˈfemənɪn] 美[ˈfɛmənɪn]
 
adj.有女性气质的；女子气的；[语]阴性的；阴柔的；n.女性；温柔的女性；[语]阴性，阴性词；
 "

ADJ
女性的;女人的
Feminine qualities and things relate to or are considered typical of women, in contrast to men.
例：...male leaders worrying about their women abandoning traditional feminine roles.男性领导人担心自己的妻子放弃传统的女性角色
例：...a manufactured ideal of feminine beauty.虚构的女性美典范

ADJ-GRADED
女子气的;女人味的;(尤指)柔美的
Someone or something that is feminine has qualities that are considered typical of women, especially being pretty or gentle.
例：I've always been attracted to very feminine, delicate women...我总是被十分娇柔纤弱的女子吸引。
例：The bedroom has a light, feminine look.卧室布置得很雅致，充满女人味。

ADJ
(名词、代词或形容词)阴性的
In some languages, a feminine noun, pronoun, or adjective has a different form from a masculine or neuter one, or behaves in a different way.

petition 
英[pəˈtɪʃn] 美[pəˈtɪʃən]
 
n.请愿书，；请愿，请求；上诉状；vi.祈求，请求；请愿；vt.（向法庭）申诉；
 "

N-COUNT
请愿书
A petition is a document signed by a lot of people which asks a government or other official group to do a particular thing.
例：We recently presented the government with a petition signed by 4,500 people.最近我们向政府递交了有4,500人签名的请愿书。

N-COUNT
上诉；（对法庭的）请求
A petition is a formal request made to a court of law for some legal action to be taken.
例：His lawyers filed a petition for all charges to be dropped...他的律师提请撤销所有指控。
例：The court rejected their petition.法庭驳回了他们的上诉。

VERB
祈求；请求；请愿
If you petition someone in authority, you make a formal request to them.
例：...couples petitioning for divorce...申请离婚的夫妇
例：All the attempts to petition the Congress had failed...所有向国会请愿的努力均告失败。

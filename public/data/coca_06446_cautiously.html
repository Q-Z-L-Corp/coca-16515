cautiously 
英['kɔ:ʃəslɪ] 美[ˈkɔʃəslɪ]
 
adv.小心地，谨慎地；小心翼翼；翼翼；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
谨慎的;慎重的;小心的
Someone who is cautious acts very carefully in order to avoid possible danger.
例：The scientists are cautious about using enzyme therapy on humans...科学家们对在人身上使用酶疗法非常慎重。
例：He is a very cautious man.他做事很谨慎。

ADJ-GRADED
(态度或反应)谨慎的，小心的
If you describe someone's attitude or reaction as cautious, you mean that it is limited or careful.
例：He has been seen as a champion of a more cautious approach to economic reform.人们一直将他视为主张更为谨慎的经济改革措施的提倡者。

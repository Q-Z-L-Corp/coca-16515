ADJ-GRADED
不能忍受的;无法容忍的
If you describe something as intolerable, you mean that it is so bad or extreme that no one can bear it or tolerate it.
例：They felt this would put intolerable pressure on them...他们觉得这会给他们带来无法承受的压力。
例：Human rights abuses by any party are intolerable.任何党派践踏人权的行为都不能容忍。

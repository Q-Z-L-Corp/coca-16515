foolishly 
英['fu:lɪʃlɪ] 美[ˈfulɪʃlɪ]
 
adv.愚蠢地，可笑地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(举止或行为)愚蠢的，傻的，笨的
If someone's behaviour or action is foolish, it is not sensible and shows a lack of good judgment.
例：It would be foolish to raise hopes unnecessarily...无谓地燃起人们的希望是愚蠢的。
例：It is foolish to risk skin cancer.冒患上皮肤癌的风险是愚蠢的。

ADJ-GRADED
傻的;蠢的;显得难堪的
If you look or feel foolish, you look or feel so silly or ridiculous that people are likely to laugh at you.
例：I just stood there feeling foolish and watching him...我只是傻傻地站在那里看着他。
例：I didn't want him to look foolish and be laughed at.我不希望见到他出丑，被别人嘲笑。

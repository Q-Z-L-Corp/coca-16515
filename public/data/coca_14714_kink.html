kink 
英[kɪŋk] 美[kɪŋk]
 
n.（绳、头发等的纽）结，（道路等的）弯曲，肌肉抽筋；v.（使）纠结，（使）纽绞；

N-COUNT
（直线物体上的）弯，结
A kink is a curve or twist in something which is otherwise or normally straight.
例：...a tiny black kitten with tufted ears and a kink in her tail.长着毛茸茸的耳朵、尾巴卷曲的小黑猫

V-ERG
（使）弯曲；（使）扭结；（使）绞缠
If something kinks or is kinked, it has, or it develops a curve or twist in it.
例：...her wet hair kinking in the breeze...她那在微风中缠结的湿发
例：Care is needed when loading the roll to prevent twisting or kinking the film...装胶卷时要小心，别使胶片扭结。

N-COUNT
（尤指古怪）念头;奇想，怪癖
A kink is a particular quality or feature of a person's mind or character, especially one which is thought to be unusual or abnormal.
例：What kink did he have in his character?他性格中有哪些怪癖？

PHRASE
解决问题
If someone works out the kinks in a situation, they resolve the problems associated with it.
例：...working out the kinks of a potential trade agreement.解决有望达成的一个贸易协定的若干问题
例：...joint sessions with her, to work out the kinks in the marriage.为解决婚姻中存在的问题而与她进行的交谈

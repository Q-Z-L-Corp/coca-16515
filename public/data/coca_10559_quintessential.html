ADJ-GRADED
典范的;典型的
Quintessential means representing a perfect or typical example of something.
例：This was quintessential Midwestern farming country...这就是典型的中西部农村。
例：Everybody thinks of him as the quintessential New Yorker.大家都视他为典型的纽约人。

ADJ
本质的;精髓的
Quintessential means representing the central nature of something.
例：...the quintessential charm of his songs.他的歌声中最本质的魅力

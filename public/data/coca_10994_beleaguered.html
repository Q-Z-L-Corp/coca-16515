beleaguered 
英[bɪˈli:gəd] 美[bɪˈligərd]
 
adj.受到围困[围攻]的；包围的；v.围攻( beleaguer的过去式和过去分词)；困扰；骚扰；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
处境艰难的；饱受非议的；备受指责的
A beleaguered person, organization, or project is experiencing a lot of difficulties, opposition, or criticism.
例：There have been seven coup attempts against the beleaguered government.风雨飘摇的政府已经遭到了7次未遂政变的打击。

ADJ
被敌人包围的；四面楚歌的；陷入重围的
A beleaguered place or army is surrounded by its enemies.
例：The rebels continue their push towards the beleaguered capital.叛军继续向四面被围的首都推进。

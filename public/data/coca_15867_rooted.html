rooted 
英[ˈru:tɪd] 美[]
 
adj.生根的，有根的；固定的，根深蒂固的；v.生根；使（某事物）深深扎根；
 "

ADJ
起源于…的;根源于…的;由…产生的
If you say that one thing is rooted in another, you mean that it is strongly influenced by it or has developed from it.
例：The crisis is rooted in deep rivalries between the two groups.这场危机的根源是这两个群体之间积怨已深的敌对情绪。
例：...powerful songs rooted in traditional African music.起源于传统非洲音乐的打动人心的歌曲

ADJ-GRADED
（观点、感受）根深蒂固的，难以改变的
If someone has deeply rooted opinions or feelings, they believe or feel something extremely strongly and are unlikely to change.
例：Racism is a deeply rooted prejudice which has existed for thousands of years.种族主义是存在了几千年的根深蒂固的偏见。

PHRASE
（因害怕或吃惊）呆住，不能动弹
If you are rooted to the spot, you are unable to move because you are very frightened or shocked.
例：We just stopped there, rooted to the spot.我们一下子就停在了那里，呆若木鸡。

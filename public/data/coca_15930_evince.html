VERB
（经常间接地）表明，显示
If someone or something evinces a particular feeling or quality, they show that feeling or quality, often indirectly.
例：The entire production evinces authenticity and a real respect for the subject matter...整部作品表现出真实性以及对主题的真正尊重。
例：The new president has so far evinced no such sense of direction.新总统到目前尚未表现出对未来发展的判断力。

n.黏质物；黏液；黏泥；软泥；v.（用稀泥等）涂，糊；分泌黏液使黏滑；清除黏液[稀泥等]；〈英俚〉滑脱，用狡猾手段脱身溜掉 (away through etc.)；

N-UNCOUNT
黏滑的污垢;烂泥
Slime is a thick, wet substance which covers a surface or comes from the bodies of animals such as snails.
例：There was an unappealing film of slime on top of the pond.池塘表面有一层黏腻腻的污泥。

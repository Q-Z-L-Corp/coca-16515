scattered 
英[ˈskætəd] 美[ˈskætərd]
 
adj.分散的；零散的；疏落的；到处都是…的；
 "

ADJ
分散的;七零八落的;疏疏落落的
Scattered things are spread over an area in an untidy or irregular way.
例：He picked up the scattered toys...他收拾好扔得到处都是的玩具。
例：Tomorrow there will be a few scattered showers...明天局部地区将会有阵雨。

ADJ
遍布着…的;到处都是…的
If something is scattered with a lot of small things, they are spread all over it.
例：Every surface is scattered with photographs.每面都贴满了照片。

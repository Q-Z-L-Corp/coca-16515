goodwill 
英[ˌgʊdˈwɪl] 美[ˈɡʊdˈwɪl]
 
n.友好，亲善；好感，青睐；（企业的）信誉，声誉；商誉；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
友善；善意；亲善
Goodwill is a friendly or helpful attitude towards other people, countries, or organizations.
例：I invited them to dinner, a gesture of goodwill...我请他们吃饭以示友善。
例：They depend on the goodwill of visitors to pick up rubbish.他们相信游客会自觉捡起垃圾。

N-UNCOUNT
信誉；商誉
The goodwill of a business is something such as its good reputation, which increases the value of the business.
例：We do not want to lose the goodwill built up over 175 years.我们不想让历经175年树立起来的良好信誉毁于一旦。

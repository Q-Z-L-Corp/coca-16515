profane 
英[prəˈfeɪn] 美[proˈfen, prə-]
 
adj.不敬（神）的；渎神的；亵渎的；世俗的；vt.不敬；亵渎，玷污；n.未受秘传的人；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
亵渎的；渎神的
Profane behaviour shows disrespect for a religion or religious things.
例：...profane language.亵渎的话语

ADJ
非宗教的；世俗的
Something that is profane is concerned with everyday life rather than religion and spiritual things.
例：Cardinal Daly has said that churches should not be used for profane or secular purposes.戴利主教已经说过，教堂不应该用于非宗教或世俗的目的。

VERB
亵渎；不敬；玷污；渎神
If someone profanes a religious belief or institution, they treat it with disrespect.
例：They have profaned the long upheld traditions of the Church.他们亵渎了教会长期以来的传统。

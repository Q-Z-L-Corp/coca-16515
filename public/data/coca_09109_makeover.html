例：She received a cosmetic makeover at a beauty salon as a birthday gift.她得到了一份生日礼物，可以在美容院享受一次美容。

重大改造；革新
If an organization or system is given a makeover, important changes are made in order to improve it.
例：The biggest makeover has been in TV drama.最大的变革发生在电视剧行业。

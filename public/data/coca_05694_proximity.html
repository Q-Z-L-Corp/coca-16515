N-UNCOUNT
接近；临近；邻近
Proximity to a place or person is nearness to that place or person.
例：Part of the attraction is Darwin's proximity to Asia...达尔文市的魅力一部分在于它靠近亚洲。
例：He became aware of the proximity of the Afghans...他意识到阿富汗人近在咫尺。

ADJ
法医的
Forensic is used to describe the work of scientists who examine evidence in order to help the police solve crimes.
例：They were convicted on forensic evidence alone...仅凭法医的证据他们便被定了罪。
例：Forensic experts searched the area for clues.法医专家勘查了这个区域，以寻找线索。

ADJ
法院的;法庭的
Forensic means relating to the legal profession.
例：He won admiration for his forensic skills in cross-examining ministers.他在盘问几位部长时表现出的问案技巧为他赢得了赞赏。
例：...a forensic psychiatrist.司法精神病学家

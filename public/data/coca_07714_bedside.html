bedside 
英[ˈbedsaɪd] 美[ˈbɛdˌsaɪd]
 
n.床边，床侧；adj.床旁的；关于病人的，临床的；
 "

N-SING
床旁；床边；床侧
Your bedside is the area beside your bed.
例：She put a cup of tea down on the bedside table...她在床头柜上放了一杯茶。
例：He drew a chair up to the bedside and sat down.他拉了把椅子到床边坐了下来。

N-SING
（某人的）病床边
If you talk about being at someone's bedside, you are talking about being near them when they are ill in bed.
例：She kept vigil at the bedside of her critically ill son...她整夜都守在病重的儿子床边。
例：She was called to her brother's bedside.她被叫到了哥哥的病榻前。

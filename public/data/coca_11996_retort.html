VERB
反驳;回嘴
To retort means to reply angrily to someone.
例：Was he afraid, he was asked. 'Afraid of what?' he retorted...有人问他是否害怕。“害怕什么？”他反问道。
例：Others retort that strong central power is a dangerous thing in Russia.其他人反驳说强大的中央集权在俄罗斯是很危险的。

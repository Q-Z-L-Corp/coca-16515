neat 
英[ni:t] 美[nit]
 
adj.整洁的，干净的；灵巧的；匀整的；未搀水的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
整洁的;整齐的
A neat place, thing, or person is tidy and smart, and has everything in the correct place.
例：So they left her in the neat little house, alone with her memories...于是他们把她一个人留在整洁的小房子里，只有记忆陪着她。
例：She undressed and put her wet clothes in a neat pile in the corner.她脱掉衣服，把湿衣服整齐地码成一堆放在角落里。

ADJ-GRADED
爱整洁的;会收拾的
Someone who is neat keeps their home or possessions tidy, with everything in the correct place.
例：'That's not like Alf,' he said, 'leaving papers muddled like that. He's always so neat.'他说道：“这不像是阿尔夫的风格，他不会把文件弄得那么乱。他一向很爱整洁。”

ADJ-GRADED
匀整的;秀气的;小巧优雅的
A neat object, part of the body, or shape is quite small and has a smooth outline.
例：...a faded woman with neat features.姿色虽衰但五官清秀的女人
例：...neat handwriting.娟秀的笔迹

ADJ-GRADED
(行动等)干净利落的，利索的
A neat movement or action is done accurately and skilfully, with no unnecessary movements.
例：A neat move between Black and Keane left Nigel Clough in the clear, but his shot skimmed wide of the far post.奈杰尔·克拉夫凭借一次漂亮的跑位在布莱克和基恩之间扯出了空当儿，但是他的射门却大大偏出了远门柱。

ADJ-GRADED
巧妙的;简洁的;简便易行的
A neat way of organizing, achieving, explaining, or expressing something is clever and convenient.
例：It had been such a neat, clever plan...这曾是个如此巧妙、机智的计划。
例：Neat solutions are not easily found to these issues...很难找到解决这些问题的巧妙办法。

ADJ-GRADED
很好的;很棒的
If you say that something is neat, you mean that it is very good.
例：'Oh, those new apartments are really neat,' the girl babbled on...那个女孩赞不绝口地说：“噢，那些新公寓真是非常棒。” 
例：It'll be neat to have a father and son playing on the same team...爸爸和儿子同队效力，那真是太棒了。

ADJ
(烈性酒)未掺水的，纯的
When someone drinks strong alcohol neat, they do not add a weaker liquid such as water to it.
例：He poured himself a brandy and swallowed it neat...他给自己倒了一杯白兰地，没兑水就一饮而尽。
例：He took a mouthful of neat whisky, and coughed.他喝了一口纯威士忌，咳嗽了起来。

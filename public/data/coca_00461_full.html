full 
英[fʊl] 美[fʊl]
 
adj.满的，装满的；完全的，完整的；丰富的；详尽的；adv.极其，十分；充分地，完全地；整整；直接地；n.充分，完全；全部；完整；极盛时；v.把衣服缝得宽大；把裙子缝出皱褶；[印]蒸洗，漂洗；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
满的;装满的
If something is full, it contains as much of a substance or as many objects as it can.
例：Once the container is full, it stays shut until you turn it clockwise.容器一旦装满它会保持关闭，想要打开须按顺时针方向转动。
例：...a full tank of petrol.一满箱汽油

ADJ-GRADED
有大量…的;满是…的
If a place or thing is full of things or people, it contains a large number of them.
例：The case was full of clothes...箱子里塞满了衣服。
例：The streets are still full of debris from two nights of rioting.街道上仍到处可见连续两晚的骚乱留下的残迹。

ADJ-GRADED
充满(某一情感或特质)的
If someone or something is full of a particular feeling or quality, they have a lot of it.
例：I feel full of confidence and so open to possibilities...我感到信心十足，愿意考虑各种可能。
例：Mom's face was full of pain.妈妈的脸上写满了痛苦。

ADJ-GRADED
满员的;没有空位(或空间)的
You say that a place or vehicle is full when there is no space left in it for any more people or things.
例：The main car park was full when I left about 10.45...我 10 点 45 分左右离开时，主停车场已经没有车位了。
例：They stay here a few hours before being sent to refugee camps, which are now almost full...他们在这儿呆了几个小时后被送往难民营，而那里现在几乎已经满员了。

ADJ-GRADED
(手或胳膊)占着的，拿满了的，挂满了的
If your hands or arms are full, you are carrying or holding as much as you can carry.
例：Sylvia entered, her arms full of packages...西尔维娅走了进来，怀里抱满了包裹。
例：People would go into the store and come out with their arms full.人们到这家商店来总是满载而归。

ADJ-GRADED
饱的;吃(或喝)胀的
If you feel full, you have eaten or drunk so much that you do not want anything else.
例：It's healthy to eat when I'm hungry and to stop when I'm full.饿了就吃，饱了就停，这样很健康。

ADJ
(置于名词前)所有的，全部的
You use full before a noun to indicate that you are referring to all the details, things, or people that it can possibly include.
例：Full details will be sent to you once your application has been accepted...所有细节会在申请接受后马上寄出。
例：May I have your full name?...请问您的全名是什么？

ADJ
(声音、光线、力气等)最大(强度)的
Full is used to describe a sound, light, or physical force which is being produced with the greatest possible power or intensity.
例：From his study came the sound of Mahler, playing at full volume...从他的书房里传出马勒的乐曲，音量开到了最大。
例：Officials say the operation will be carried out in full daylight...官员们说行动将在大白天进行。

ADJ
完全的;充分的;彻底的
You use full to emphasize the completeness, intensity, or extent of something.
例：We should conserve oil and gas by making full use of other energy sources...我们应该通过充分利用其他能源来节省石油和天然气。
例：Television cameras are carrying the full horror of this war into homes around the world...电视摄像机把这场战争彻头彻尾的恐怖传送到全世界的千家万户。

ADJ-GRADED
(内容)详尽的，丰富的
A full statement or report contains a lot of information and detail.
例：Mr Primakov gave a full account of his meeting with the President.普里马科夫先生详细叙述了他和总统的会面。
例：...the enormous detail in this very full document.在这份内容十分全面的文件里包含的海量细节

ADJ-GRADED
(生活)多姿多彩的，充实的
If you say that someone has or leads a full life, you approve of the fact that they are always busy and do a lot of different things.
例：You will be successful in whatever you do and you will have a very full and interesting life.你不管做什么都会成功，并且会过着非常充实有趣的生活。

ADV
恰好;正好;直接地
You use full to emphasize the force or directness with which someone or something is hit or looked at.
例：The burning liquid hit him full in the right eye...滚烫的液体正好溅进了他的右眼。
例：She kissed him full on the mouth...她直接吻上了他的嘴唇。

ADJ
(所拥有的权利、地位或重要性)完整的，正式的
You use full to refer to something which gives you all the rights, status, or importance for a particular position or activity, rather than just some of them.
例：How did the meeting go, did you get your full membership?...面谈进行得怎么样，你成为正式成员了吗？
例：She sent her provisional licence with the test certificate to have it upgraded to a full licence.她将临时执照连同考试证书一起提交上去以申请升级为正式执照。

ADJ
(味道)浓烈的，浓郁的
A full flavour is strong and rich.
例：Italian plum tomatoes have a full flavour, and are best for cooking.意大利李形番茄味道很重，最适合烹饪。

ADJ-GRADED
(身体某一部位)丰满的，圆润的
If you describe a part of someone's body as full, you mean that it is rounded and rather large.
例：The Juno Collection specialises in large sizes for ladies with a fuller figure.朱诺系列专门针对身材较为丰满的女士推出大号女装。
例：...his strong chin, his full lips, his appealing mustache.他坚挺的下巴、饱满的双唇和迷人的八字胡

ADJ-GRADED
(裙摆或袖子)宽大的，宽松的
A full skirt or sleeve is wide and has been made from a lot of fabric.
例：My wedding dress has a very full skirt.我结婚礼服的裙摆很宽松。

ADJ
(月)满的，圆的
When there is a full moon, the moon appears as a bright, complete circle.

PHRASE
所有地;全部地;全数地
You say that something has been done or described in full when everything that was necessary has been done or described.
例：The medical experts have yet to report in full...医学专家尚未提出完整的报告。
例：We guarantee to reply in full within 10 working days.我们保证在 10 个工作日内悉数回复。

PHRASE
(尤指对令人不快的事)很清楚地知道(或了解)，一清二楚
If you say that a person knows full well that something is true, especially something unpleasant, you are emphasizing that they are definitely aware of it, although they may behave as if they are not.
例：He knew full well he'd be ashamed of himself later.他很清楚地知道自己过后会无地自容。

PHRASE
尽量;在最大程度上;充分地;彻底地
Something that is done or experienced to the full is done to as great an extent as is possible.
例：She probably has a good mind, which should be used to the full...她可能很有头脑，应该充分运用。
例：There's only one thing we should do with love; experience it to the full for as long as it lasts.对于爱情我们只应有一种态度：趁其尚未消失时尽情享受。

PHRASE
洋洋得意;自以为是
If you say to someone 'you're full of yourself', you disapprove of them because they appear very pleased with themselves, thinking that they are very clever, special, or important.
例：He's full of himself, sharp and aggressive and sometimes he comes over badly.他是个自以为是的家伙，说话尖酸又爱挑衅，有时会给人留下极差的印象。

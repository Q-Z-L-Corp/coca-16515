N-VAR
傍晚;晚上
The evening is the part of each day between the end of the afternoon and the time when you go to bed.
例：All he did that evening was sit around the flat...那天晚上他一直在公寓里无所事事地坐着。
例：Supper is from 5.00 to 6.00 in the evening...晚餐在傍晚5点到6点之间。

delivery 
英[dɪˈlɪvəri] 美[dɪˈlɪvəri, -ˈlɪvri]
 
n.传送，投递；[法]（正式）交付；分娩；讲演；
 "

N-VAR
(信件、包裹等的)递送，投递，运送
Delivery or a delivery is the bringing of letters, parcels, or other goods to someone's house or to another place where they want them.
例：Please allow 28 days for delivery...递送将需要 28 天的时间。
例：It is available at £108, including VAT and delivery.其价格为 108 英镑，包括增值税和送货费在内。

N-COUNT
递送之物;投递之物;运送之物
A delivery of something is the goods that are delivered.
例：I got a delivery of fresh eggs this morning.我今天早上收到了送来的新鲜鸡蛋。

ADJ
递送的;投递的;运送的
A delivery person or service delivers things to a place.
例：...a pizza delivery man.比萨饼递送员
例：...Interflora, the flower delivery service.国际花商联盟，鲜花速递服务机构

N-UNCOUNT
发言(或演说)的方式
You talk about someone's delivery when you are referring to the way in which they give a speech or lecture.
例：His speeches were magnificently written but his delivery was hopeless.他的发言稿写得非常精彩，但他的演讲风格真是无可救药。

N-VAR
分娩;生产
Delivery is the process of giving birth to a baby.
例：In the end, it was an easy delivery: a fine baby boy...分娩过程最终非常顺利，生了个健康的男婴。
例：Premature birth is three times more likely for twins, and delivery at 36 to 38 weeks is normal.双胞胎的早产率要比一般的高出两倍，在36到38周之间分娩很正常。

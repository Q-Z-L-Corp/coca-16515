N-COUNT
罕见的人（或物）；奇物;珍品
If someone or something is a rarity, they are interesting or valuable because they are so unusual.
例：Motorized wheelchairs are a rarity here...电动轮椅在这里很罕见。
例：He was a rarity among Wall Street lawyers...他在华尔街的律师当中是个奇人。

N-UNCOUNT
稀有;罕见
The rarity of something is the fact that it is very uncommon.
例：This indicates the rarity of such attacks...这显示出这种疾病发作的罕见性。
例：It was a real prize due to its rarity and good condition...因为稀有并且品相完好，它价值连城。

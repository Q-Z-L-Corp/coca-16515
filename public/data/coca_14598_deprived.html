ADJ-GRADED
贫困的;贫穷的
Deprived people or people from deprived areas do not have the things that people consider to be essential in life, for example acceptable living conditions or education.
例：...probably the most severely deprived children in the country.可能是这个国家里最贫困的儿童
例：...the problems associated with life in a deprived inner city area.与市中心贫民区生活联系在一起的问题

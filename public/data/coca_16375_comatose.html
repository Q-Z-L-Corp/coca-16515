comatose 
英[ˈkəʊmətəʊs] 美[ˈkoʊmətoʊs]
 
adj.昏迷的； 不省人事的；昏昏沉沉的； 昏昏欲睡的；
 "

ADJ
昏迷的
A person who is comatose is in a coma.
例：The right side of my brain had been so severely bruised that I was comatose for a month.由于右脑遭受重创，我昏迷了一个月。

ADJ
(通常指因劳累或饮酒过量而)酣睡的
A person who is comatose is in a deep sleep, usually because they are tired or have drunk too much alcohol.
例：Grandpa lies comatose on the sofa.爷爷躺在沙发上，睡得很沉。

demolish 
英[dɪˈmɒlɪʃ] 美[dɪˈmɑlɪʃ]
 
vt.摧毁，拆毁（建筑物等）；毁坏，破坏；推翻；驳倒（论点、理论等）；
 "

VERB
摧毁，拆毁，拆除(建筑物等)
To demolish something such as a building means to destroy it completely.
例：A storm moved directly over the island, demolishing buildings and flooding streets...暴风雨径直席卷该岛，摧毁了建筑，淹没了街道。
例：The building is now being demolished to make way for a motorway.为修建高速公路，现正拆除这栋建筑。

VERB
驳倒，推翻(某人的观点或论点)
If you demolish someone's ideas or arguments, you prove that they are completely wrong or unreasonable.
例：Our intention was to demolish the rumours that have surrounded him...我们的目的是要彻底击破围绕他的那些谣言。
例：The myth that Japan is not open to concerns from outside has, I think, been demolished at a stroke.关于日本对外界的关注不予理会的谬论，我认为，一下子就被驳倒了。

VERB
大败，打垮(对手)
If a person or team demolishes their opponents, they defeat them by a great amount.
例：Millwall demolished Notts County 6-0 on Saturday.周六米尔沃尔队以 6 比 0 大败诺茨郡队。

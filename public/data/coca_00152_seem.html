seem 
英[si:m] 美[sim]
 
v.好像，仿佛；似乎；装作；看来好像；
 "

V-LINK
好像;似乎;看来
You use seem to say that someone or something gives the impression of having a particular quality, or of happening in the way you describe.
例：We heard a series of explosions. They seemed quite close by...我们听到一连串的爆炸声，似乎就在附近。
例：Everyone seems busy except us...除了我们，大家好像都很忙碌。

V-LINK
(在描述自己的感觉、想法、经历时用于缓和语气)好像，似乎
You use seem when you are describing your own feelings or thoughts, or describing something that has happened to you, in order to make your statement less forceful.
例：I seem to have lost all my self-confidence...我好像已经自信全无。
例：I seem to remember giving you very precise instructions...我好像记得给过你非常明确的指示。

PHRASE
似乎不能;好像无法
If you say that you cannot seem or could not seem to do something, you mean that you have tried to do it and were unable to.
例：No matter how hard I try I cannot seem to catch up on all the bills...无论怎么努力，我好像就是无法付清所有的账单。
例：Kim's mother couldn't seem to stop crying.金的母亲似乎要哭个没完。

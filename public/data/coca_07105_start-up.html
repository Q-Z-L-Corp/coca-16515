start-up 
英[stɑ:t ʌp] 美[ˈstɑrtˌʌp]
 
n.启动；adj.（新企业或工程）开办阶段的；
 "

ADJ
(费用)创办所需的，起始阶段的，启动的
The start-up costs of something such as a new business or new product are the costs of starting to run or produce it.
例：That is enough to pay the start-up costs for fourteen research projects...那足够用来支付 14 个科研项目的启动费用了。
例：The minimum start-up capital for a Pizza franchise is estimated at $250,000 to $315,000.申请比萨饼店的品牌特许经营权的最低启动资金估计为 25 万到 31.5 万美元。

ADJ
(公司)新成立的;刚创办的
A start-up company is a small business that has recently been started by someone.
例：Thousands and thousands of start-up firms have poured into the computer market.成千上万家新成立的公司已涌进了计算机市场。

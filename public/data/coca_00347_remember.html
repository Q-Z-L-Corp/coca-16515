remember 
英[rɪˈmembə(r)] 美[rɪˈmɛmbɚ]
 
vt.& vi.记得；牢记；vt.记着；纪念；回想起；送钱[礼物]给…；
 "

VERB
记得;记起;想起
If you remember people or events from the past, you still have an idea of them in your mind and you are able to think about them.
例：You wouldn't remember me. I was in another group...你不会记得我。我那时在另外一组。
例：I certainly don't remember talking to you at all...我当然完全不记得和你讲过话。

VERB
(经过一段时间后)忆起，回忆起
If you remember that something is the case, you become aware of it again after a time when you did not think about it.
例：She remembered that she was going to the social club that evening...她记起来那晚她正要去社交俱乐部。
例：Then I remembered the cheque, which cheered me up.然后我想起了那张支票，这让我精神大振。

VERB
（用于否定）记起，想起
If you cannot remember something, you are not able to bring it back into your mind when you make an effort to do so.
例：If you can't remember your number, write it in code in a diary...如果你记不住你的号码，用代码把它记在记事本上。
例：I couldn't remember ever having felt so safe and secure...我从未有过如此安全的感觉。

VERB
记住，记得(做某事)
If you remember to do something, you do it when you intend to.
例：I did remember to take the present...我的确记得拿了礼物。
例：Please remember to enclose a stamped addressed envelope when writing.请记得写信时在信封里放一个贴好邮票、写明地址的信封。

VERB
记住，牢记(重要的事)
You tell someone to remember that something is the case when you want to emphasize its importance. It may be something that they already know about or a new piece of information.
例：It is important to remember that each person reacts differently...重要的是要记住每个人的反应不同。
例：It is worth remembering that children tend to copy their parents in this respect...值得记住的是孩子们在这一方面往往会效仿父母。

VERB
(因为某事)记住（某人）
If you say that someone will be remembered for something that they have done, you mean that people will think of this whenever they think about the person.
例：At his grammar school he is remembered for being bad at games...在文法学校他因不善于玩游戏为大家所熟知。
例：He will always be remembered as one of the great Chancellors of the Exchequer.作为最伟大的财政大臣之一他将永远被铭记。

VERB
代(某人)问候(某人)
If you ask someone to remember you to a person who you have not seen for a long time, you are asking them to pass your greetings on to that person.
例：'Remember me to Lyle, won't you?' I said...“代我问候莱尔，好吗？”我说。
例：She asked to be remembered to you.她要我转达对你的问候。

VERB
值得记忆；令人难忘
If you make a celebration an occasion to remember, you make it very enjoyable for all the people involved.
例：We'll give everyone a night to remember...我们将带给大家一个难忘的夜晚。
例：I'll make it a birthday to remember.我将使这成为一个难忘的生日。

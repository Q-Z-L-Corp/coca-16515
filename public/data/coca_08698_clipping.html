N-COUNT
剪报
A clipping is an article, picture, or advertisement that has been cut from a newspaper or magazine.
例：...bulletin boards crowded with newspaper clippings.贴满报纸剪报的布告栏

N-COUNT
剪下物
Clippings are small pieces of something that have been cut from something larger.
例：Having mown the lawn, there are all those grass clippings to get rid of.修剪草坪后有很多剪下的碎草需要清理。
例：...nail clippings.剪下的指甲

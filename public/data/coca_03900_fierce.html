fierce 
英[fɪəs] 美[fɪrs]
 
adj.凶猛的，残忍的；猛烈的；狂热的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
凶猛的;狂怒的
A fierce animal or person is very aggressive or angry.
例：They look like the teeth of some fierce animal.它们看上去像某种猛兽的牙齿。

ADJ-GRADED
(感情或行动)激烈的，狂热的
Fierce feelings or actions are very intense or enthusiastic, or involve great activity.
例：Competition has been fierce to win a stake in Skoda...争夺斯柯达股份的竞争极为激烈。
例：The town was captured after a fierce battle with rebels at the weekend...该镇在周末与叛乱分子一番激战后被攻占。

ADJ-GRADED
极度的;巨大的;强烈的
Fierce conditions are very intense, great, or strong.
例：The climbers were trapped by a fierce storm which went on for days.登山者被一连几天的强风暴雨给困住了。

adequacy 
英['ædɪkwəsɪ] 美['ædɪkwəsɪ]
 
n.足够；充分；胜任；适当；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
适当;恰当
Adequacy is the quality of being good enough or great enough in amount to be acceptable.
例：I am very critical of the adequacy of Britain's race laws...我对英国种族法律的适当性颇有微词。
例：Several studies point to a real cause for concern over the adequacy of the diet eaten by British children.针对英国儿童膳食质量的担忧，数份研究将矛头指向了同一根源。

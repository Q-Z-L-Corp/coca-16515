triangular 
英[traɪˈæŋgjələ(r)] 美[traɪˈæŋɡjəlɚ]
 
adj.三角（形）的；由三个部分构成的；三方面的；
 "

ADJ
三角(形)的
Something that is triangular is in the shape of a triangle.
例：...cottages around a triangular green.坐落在一块三角形绿地周围的小别墅
例：…triangular bandages to make slings.用作吊腕带的三角绷带

ADJ
三角关系的;三人间的;三方面的
You can describe a relationship or situation as triangular if it involves three people or things.
例：One particular triangular relationship became the model of Simone's first novel.一段特殊的三角关系成了西蒙娜第一本小说的原型。

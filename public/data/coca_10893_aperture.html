aperture 
英[ˈæpətʃə(r)] 美[ˈæpətʃɚ]
 
n.孔，洞；（照相机，望远镜等的）光圈；孔径；隙缝；
 "

N-COUNT
孔；缝隙；缺口
An aperture is a narrow hole or gap.
例：Through the aperture he could see daylight.透过缝隙，他能看见日光。

N-COUNT
（照相机的）光圈，孔径
In photography, the aperture of a camera is the size of the hole through which light passes to reach the film.
例：Use a small aperture and position the camera carefully...用小光圈，仔细调整相机位置。
例：The camera has a 32mm lens with a maximum aperture of f/4.5 and a fixed shutter speed of 1/100sec.这架相机有一个32毫米的镜头，最大孔径为f/4.5，固定快门速度为1/100秒。

ADV
往(或在)城外;往(或在)城镇的非商业区
If you go uptown, or go to a place uptown, you go away from the centre of a town or city towards the edge. Uptown sometimes refers to a part of the city other than the main business district.
例：He rode uptown and made his way to Bob's apartment...他乘车前往城外鲍勃的公寓。
例：Susan continued to live uptown...苏珊继续住在市郊。

headlong 
英[ˈhedlɒŋ] 美[ˈhedlɔŋ]
 
adv.头向前地；急速地；轻率地；adj.头向前的；急速的；轻率的；险峻的；
 "

ADV
快速地
If you move headlong in a particular direction, you move there very quickly.
例：He ran headlong for the open door.他飞快地朝敞开着的门跑去。

ADV
头朝下地
If you fall or move headlong, you fall or move with your head furthest forward.
例：She missed her footing and fell headlong down the stairs.她一脚踩空，头朝下摔下了楼梯。

ADV
莽撞地;轻率地
If you rush headlong into something, you do it quickly without thinking carefully about it.
例：Do not leap headlong into decisions...不要轻率地作决定。
例：The country, they say, will inevitably now plunge headlong into decadence.他们说，现在整个国家会不可避免地一头陷入衰退中。

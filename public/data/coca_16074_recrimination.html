N-UNCOUNT
反诉;相互指责;反唇相讥
Recriminations are accusations that two people or groups make about each other.
例：The bitter rows and recriminations have finally ended the relationship...激烈的争吵和相互指责最终为他们的感情画上了句号。
例：The war sweeps up everyone in hatred and recrimination.战争让大家充满仇恨并相互指责。

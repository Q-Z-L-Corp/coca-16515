rampage 
英[ˈræmpeɪdʒ] 美[ˈræmˌpedʒ]
 
n.（因发怒等）暴跳，暴怒；狂暴行为；乱闹；v.暴跳，暴怒；狂暴地乱冲；
 "

VERB
横冲直撞
When people or animals rampage through a place, they rush about there in a wild or violent way, causing damage or destruction.
例：Hundreds of youths rampaged through the town, shop windows were smashed and cars overturned...数百名年轻人在镇上横冲直撞，砸碎商店玻璃，掀翻车辆。
例：He used a sword to try to defend his shop from a rampaging mob.他拿了一把剑试图保护自己的店不受闹事暴徒的打砸。

PHRASE
横冲直撞;撒野
If people go on the rampage, they rush about in a wild or violent way, causing damage or destruction.
例：The prisoners went on the rampage destroying everything in their way.囚犯撒野闹事，砸毁了所有挡路的东西。
例：...a bull on the rampage.横冲直撞的公牛

idiot 
英[ˈɪdiət] 美[ˈɪdiət]
 
n.傻瓜，笨蛋；白痴；
 "

N-COUNT
白痴;傻子;笨蛋
If you call someone an idiot, you are showing that you think they are very stupid or have done something very stupid.
例：I knew I'd been an idiot to stay there...我知道我呆在这里很蠢。
例：You're an idiot!你是个白痴！

ADJ
愚蠢的;白痴似的
Idiot means stupid.
例：...a bunch of idiot journalists waiting to ask me stupid questions.一帮白痴似的记者等着问我愚蠢的问题

N-COUNT
弱智者;低能儿
In the past, people who had something wrong with their brains that made them seem less intelligent, or different from other people, were sometimes called idiots .
例：...the village idiot.村里的那个智障者

loll 
英[lɒl] 美[lɑl]
 
vi.懒洋洋地躺着[坐着、站着]；（头、舌等耷拉，下垂）；懒洋洋地坐着（或躺着、站着）；无所事事；
 "

VERB
懒洋洋地坐（或躺）
If you loll somewhere, you sit or lie in a very relaxed position.
例：He was lolling on the sofa in the shadows near the fire...他懒洋洋地躺在炉火边一个背光的沙发上。
例：He lolled back in his comfortable chair.他懒洋洋地靠在舒适的椅子上。

VERB
（尤指头或舌）耷拉，垂下
If something fairly heavy, especially someone's head or tongue, lolls, it hangs down in a loose, uncontrolled way.
例：When he let go the head lolled sideways...他一放手，脑袋便垂向一侧。
例：His tongue was lolling out of the side of his mouth...他的舌头从嘴角耷拉出来。

ADJ
（商店）一站式的，综合性的，一应俱全的
A one-stop shop is a place where you can buy everything you need for a particular purpose.
例：A marvellous discovery for every bride-to-be, The Wedding Centre is the ultimate one-stop shop.每位准新娘都会惊喜地发现，婚庆中心提供全方位的一站式服务。

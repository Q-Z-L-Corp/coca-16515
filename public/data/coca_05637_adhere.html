adhere 
英[ədˈhɪə(r)] 美[ədˈhɪr]
 
vi.黏附；附着；坚持；追随；vt.使粘附；遵循，坚持；追随，依附；
 "

VERB
遵守，遵循（规定或协议）
If you adhere to a rule or agreement, you act in the way that it says you should.
例：All members of the association adhere to a strict code of practice...协会的所有成员都遵守一套严格的行为规范。
例：She adhered to the strict Islamic dress code.她恪守伊斯兰教严格的着装规定。

VERB
支持，拥护，持有（观点或信仰）
If you adhere to an opinion or belief, you support or hold it.
例：He urged them to adhere to the values of Islam which defend the dignity of man.他鼓励他们坚守伊斯兰教维护人类尊严的价值观。

VERB
黏附；附着
If something adheres to something else, it sticks firmly to it.
例：Small particles adhere to the seed...小颗粒牢牢附着在种子上。
例：This sticky compound adheres well on this surface.这种黏性的化合物能牢牢地粘在这个表面上。

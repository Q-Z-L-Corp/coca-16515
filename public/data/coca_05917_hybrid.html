hybrid 
英[ˈhaɪbrɪd] 美[ˈhaɪbrɪd]
 
n.杂种；杂交生成的生物体；混合物；混合词；adj.混合的；杂种的；
 "

N-COUNT
（动物或植物的）杂（交）种，混（合）种
A hybrid is an animal or plant that has been bred from two different species of animal or plant.
例：All these brightly coloured hybrids are so lovely in the garden.花园里所有这些色彩鲜艳的杂交花真美丽。
例：...a hybrid between watermint and spearmint.水生薄荷和留兰香的杂交种

N-COUNT
（尤指两种不同物质的）混合物，合成物
You can use hybrid to refer to anything that is a mixture of other things, especially two other things.
例：...a hybrid of solid and liquid fuel.固体和液体燃料的混合物

混合动力车；油电混合车
A hybrid or a hybrid car is a car that has both an electric motor and an ordinary car engine. It uses the ordinary engine when it needs extra power.
例：Hybrids, unlike pure electric cars, never need to be plugged in.与纯电动汽车不同，混合动力车永远不需要接电源。
例：Hybrid cars can go almost 600 miles between refueling.混合动力车加一次油可以跑几乎600英里。

N-SING
损害;伤害;危害
If you do someone or something a disservice, you harm them in some way.
例：He said the protesters were doing a disservice to the nation...他说抗议者正对国家造成危害。
例：You could do yourself a grave disservice by revealing all to a potential rival.将一切都透露给潜在的竞争对手只会给自己帮倒忙。

untitled 
英[ˌʌnˈtaɪtld] 美[ʌnˈtaɪtld]
 
adj.无标题的，无称号的，无权利的；
 "

ADJ
无标题的
If something such as a book, film, or song is untitled, it does not have a title.
例：The full-length feature, as yet untitled, will include interviews plus footage of their live gigs.这部尚未定名的正规长度影片将包括对他们的采访和他们现场表演的片段。

ADJ
无头衔的；无爵位的
Someone who is untitled does not have a title such as 'Sir' or 'Lord'.

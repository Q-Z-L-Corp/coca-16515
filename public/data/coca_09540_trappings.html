N-PLURAL
(权力、财富、工作等带来的)装点之物，额外实惠
The trappings of power, wealth, or a particular job are the extra things, such as decorations and luxury items, that go with it.
例：The family were in government for several generations and evidently loved the trappings of power.那个家族好几代人都曾在政府任职，显然对随权力而来的各种额外实惠情有独钟。

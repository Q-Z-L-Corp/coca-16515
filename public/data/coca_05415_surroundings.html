N-PLURAL
环境
When you are describing the place where you are at the moment, or the place where you live, you can refer to it as your surroundings .
例：Schumacher adapted effortlessly to his new surroundings.舒马赫非常轻松地适应了新环境。
例：...a peaceful holiday home in beautiful surroundings.环境优美宁静的度假屋

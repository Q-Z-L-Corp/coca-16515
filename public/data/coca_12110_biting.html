biting 
英[ˈbaɪtɪŋ] 美[ˈbaɪtɪŋ]
 
adj.尖锐刺人的；刺痛的；辛辣的；嘲讽的；v.咬，叮( bite的现在分词 )；刺痛；有咬（或叮）的习性；（人）上当；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
寒冷的；刺骨的；凛冽的
Biting wind or cold is extremely cold.
例：...a raw, biting northerly wind...阴冷刺骨的北风
例：Antarctic air brought biting cold to southern Chile on Thursday.周四，南极气流给智利南部带来了刺骨的严寒。

ADJ-GRADED
刻薄的；尖刻的；辛辣的
Biting criticism or wit is very harsh or unkind, and is often caused by such feelings as anger or dislike.
例：...a furore caused by the author's biting satire on the Church...作者对教会尖刻的讽刺引发的公愤
例：This was the most biting criticism made against her.这是对她最尖锐的批评。

ADJ
余下的;剩余的
The remaining things or people out of a group are the things or people that still exist, are still present, or have not yet been dealt with.
例：The three parties will meet next month to work out remaining differences...三方将在下个月会面，以解决余下的分歧。
例：The United States has withdrawn the remaining staff from its embassy...美国已经撤回了大使馆的剩余人员。

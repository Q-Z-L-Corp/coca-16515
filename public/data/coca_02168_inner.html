ADJ
内部的;里面的
The inner parts of something are the parts which are contained or are enclosed inside the other parts, and which are closest to the centre.
例：She got up and went into an inner office...她起身进了里间办公室。
例：Wade stepped inside and closed the inner door behind him.韦德走进去，随手带上了里面的门。

ADJ
内心的
Your inner feelings are feelings which you have but do not show to other people.
例：Loving relationships will give a child an inner sense of security...亲昵关系会给予孩子内心一种安全感。
例：Michael needed to express his inner tensions.迈克尔需要宣泄一下他内心的紧张。

denomination 
英[dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃn] 美[dɪˌnɑmɪˈneɪʃn]
 
n.宗派；教派；面额；<正>名称；
 "

N-COUNT
(同一宗教中的)教派，宗派
A particular denomination is a particular religious group which has slightly different beliefs from other groups within the same faith.
例：Acceptance of women preachers varies greatly from denomination to denomination.在接受女性传教士的问题上，不同教派的态度大相径庭。

N-COUNT
(钞票或硬币的)面额，面值
The denomination of a banknote or coin is its official value.
例：...a pile of bank notes, mostly in small denominations.其中大部分都是小面额的一沓钞票

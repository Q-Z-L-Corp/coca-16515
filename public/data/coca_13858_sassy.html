ADJ-GRADED
年少粗鲁的;轻狂无礼的
If an older person describes a younger person as sassy, they mean that they are disrespectful in a lively, confident way.
例：Are you that sassy with your parents, young lady?小姑娘，你对父母那样无礼吗？

ADJ-GRADED
时髦的;漂亮的
Sassy is used to describe things that are smart and stylish.
例：...his sharp sassy style.他那极为时髦的风格
例：...colourful and sassy fashion accessories.各种五颜六色的精美时尚饰品

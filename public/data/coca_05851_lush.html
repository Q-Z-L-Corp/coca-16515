lush 
英[lʌʃ] 美[lʌʃ]
 
adj.葱翠的；豪华的；丰富的；n.醉汉；vi.喝醉；

ADJ-GRADED
葱郁的；葱茏的；草木茂盛的
Lush fields or gardens have a lot of very healthy grass or plants.
例：...the lush green meadows bordering the river...河畔郁郁葱葱的青草地
例：The beautifully landscaped gardens sprawl with lush vegetation.一个个花园打扮得煞是好看，到处是绿茵茵的植被。

ADJ-GRADED
豪华的；奢侈的
If you describe a place or thing as lush, you mean that it is very luxurious.
例：...a mirrored bathroom done in soft pink tiles with a lush, plush carpet...铺粉色瓷砖和豪华地毯、装有镜子的卫生间
例：The fabrics were lush.布料奢华。

N-COUNT
醉鬼；酒鬼
If you describe someone as a lush, you mean that they drink too much alcohol.

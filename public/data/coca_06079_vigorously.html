vigorously 
英['vɪɡərəslɪ] 美[ˈvɪɡərəslɪ]
 
adv.精神旺盛地，活泼地；大力；蓬蓬勃勃；猛力；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（体育活动）剧烈的，强度大的，强劲的
Vigorous physical activities involve using a lot of energy, usually to do short and repeated actions.
例：Very vigorous exercise can increase the risk of heart attacks...运动太过剧烈会增大心脏病发作的风险。
例：African dance is vigorous, but full of subtlety.非洲舞蹈强劲奔放，但又处处透着细腻。

ADJ-GRADED
活跃的;积极的;热烈的
A vigorous person does things with great energy and enthusiasm. A vigorous campaign or activity is done with great energy and enthusiasm.
例：Sir Robert was a strong and vigorous politician.罗伯特爵士是位坚定而又活跃的政治家。
例：...the most vigorous critics of the government...言辞最为激烈的政府批评家们

ADJ-GRADED
精力旺盛的;充满活力的
A vigorous person is strong and healthy and full of energy.
例：He was a vigorous, handsome young man.他是个精力充沛、年轻帅气的小伙子。

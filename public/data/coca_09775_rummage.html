rummage 
英[ˈrʌmɪdʒ] 美[ˈrʌmɪdʒ]
 
v.翻查；搜出；翻箱倒柜；n.翻查，搜查；杂物，七零八碎的东西；
 "

VERB
搜寻;翻找;乱翻
If you rummage through something, you search for something you want by moving things around in a careless or hurried way.
例：They rummage through piles of second-hand clothes for something that fits...他们在一堆堆的二手衣服里翻找着合身的衣服。
例：Marianne went to rummage in the refrigerator.玛丽安娜在冰箱里翻了翻。

N-UNCOUNT
捐赠品;捐赠物
Rummage is old or unwanted things that people give away to charities.
例：...loads of pitiful rummage.大量慈善捐赠品

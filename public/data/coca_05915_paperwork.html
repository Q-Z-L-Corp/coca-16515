N-UNCOUNT
文书工作
Paperwork is the routine part of a job which involves writing or dealing with letters, reports, and records.
例：At every stage in the production there will be paperwork — forms to fill in, permissions to obtain, letters to write.在生产的每个阶段都有文书工作——填表格，报批，写信。

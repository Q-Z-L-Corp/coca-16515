bread 
英[bred] 美[brɛd]
 
n.面包；食物；营养，营养物；生计；vt.在…上撒面包屑；
 "

N-MASS
面包
Bread is a very common food made from flour, water, and yeast.
例：...a loaf of bread...一条面包
例：...bread and butter...抹黄油的面包

N-UNCOUNT
（通过工作等挣得的）钱财，金钱
If you earn your bread doing a particular job or activity, you earn your money doing it.
例：There's not a living soul in Colorado who doesn't depend for his bread on silver.科罗拉多的每一个人都是靠着开采银矿养家糊口。

VERB
给（鱼或肉）抹面包屑
If food such as fish or meat is breaded, it is covered in tiny pieces of dry bread called breadcrumbs. It can then be fried or grilled.
例：It is important that food be breaded just minutes before frying.把食物抹上面包屑后过几分钟就炸，这一点很重要。

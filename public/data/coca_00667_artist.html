artist 
英[ˈɑ:tɪst] 美[ˈɑrtɪst]
 
n.艺术家；画家；能手；骗子；
 "

N-COUNT
画家;雕塑家;艺术家
An artist is someone who draws or paints pictures or creates sculptures as a job or a hobby.
例：...the studio of a great artist...一个伟大的艺术家的工作室
例：Each poster is signed by the artist...每幅海报都有这个艺术家的签名。

N-COUNT
艺术家（如小说家、诗人、导演等）
An artist is a person who creates novels, poems, films, or other things which can be considered as works of art.
例：His books are enormously easy to read, yet he is a serious artist...虽然他是一个不折不扣的艺术家，但是他的作品极其通俗易懂。
例：Engel is quoted as saying that balanced people do not become artists.引用恩格尔的话说就是理性的人是成不了艺术家的。

N-COUNT
表演艺术家;艺人
An artist is a performer such as a musician, actor, or dancer.
例：...a popular artist who has sold millions of records.唱片销量达到数百万张的当红音乐家

N-COUNT
能手;高手;大师
If you say that someone is an artist at a particular activity, you mean they are very skilled at it.
例：He is an exceptional footballer — an artist.他是一名出色的足球运动员，一个足球高手。

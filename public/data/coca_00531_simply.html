simply 
英[ˈsɪmpli] 美[ˈsɪmpli]

ADV
简单地;只不过;仅仅;纯粹
You use simply to emphasize that something consists of only one thing, happens for only one reason, or is done in only one way.
例：The table is simply a chipboard circle on a base...这张桌子只不过是在底座上安了一块圆形刨花板。
例：Most of the damage that's occurred was simply because of fallen trees...目前大部分损坏都是因为倒下的树木造成的。

ADV
(用于强调)简直，根本
You use simply to emphasize what you are saying.
例：This sort of increase simply cannot be justified...这种增长根本就不合理。
例：So many of these questions simply don't have answers...这些问题有很多根本就没有答案。

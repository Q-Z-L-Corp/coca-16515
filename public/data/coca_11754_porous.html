porous 
英[ˈpɔ:rəs] 美[ˈpɔrəs, ˈpor-]
 
adj.能穿透的，能渗透的；有毛孔或气孔的；漏洞多的；易穿过的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
多孔的；能渗透的
Something that is porous has many small holes in it, which water and air can pass through.
例：The local limestone is very porous.当地石灰岩渗透性很强。

ADJ-GRADED
多漏洞的；松懈的
If something such as a defence or a barrier is porous, it can be penetrated or crossed easily.
例：Mr Balaguer is deploying more soldiers along his country's porous border with Haiti...巴拉格尔先生正向本国和海地之间驻守不严的边界部署更多的士兵。
例：Once a tough defensive team, the Saints now have the most porous defence in the league.圣徒队一度防御能力很强，现在却成了联盟里防守漏洞最多的队伍。

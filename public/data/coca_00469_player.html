N-COUNT
运动员；选手；参加运动者
A player in a sport or game is a person who takes part, either as a job or for fun.
例：...his greatness as a player...他作为运动员的伟大之处
例：She was a good golfer and tennis player.她是一名高尔夫球和网球好手。

N-COUNT
（乐器）演奏者
You can use player to refer to a musician. For example, a piano player is someone who plays the piano.
例：...a professional trumpet player.职业小号手

N-COUNT
重要参与者
If a person, country, or organization is a player in something, they are involved in it and important in it.
例：Big business has become a major player in the art market...大公司已成为艺术市场上的重要力量。
例：America is not a party to the negotiations, yet it is a key player...美国不是谈判的一方，但却扮演着关键的角色。

N-COUNT
演员；表演者
A player is an actor.
例：...a company of players...一群演员
例：Oscar nominations went to all five leading players.5名主演均获得了奥斯卡提名。

tug 
英[tʌg] 美[tʌɡ]
 
vt.& vi.用力拉，使劲拉；使劲，挣扎；[航海]用拖船拖曳；竞争；n.猛拉，推力；绳索链条；[航海] 拖船；竞争，斗争；
 "

VERB
使劲拉;拽
If you tug something or tug at it, you give it a quick and usually strong pull.
例：A little boy came running up and tugged at his sleeve excitedly...一个小男孩跑上来，非常激动地拽着他的袖子。
例：She kicked him, tugging his thick hair.她一边踢他，一边扯着他那浓密的头发。

N-COUNT
拖船;拖轮
A tug or a tug boat is a small powerful boat which pulls large ships, usually when they come into a port.

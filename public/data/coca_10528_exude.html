exude 
英[ɪgˈzju:d] 美[ɪgˈzud]
 
vt.使渗出；使流出；充分显示，显露；发出，散发；vi.渗出；缓慢流出；发出，发散；
 "

V-ERG
充分显露
If someone exudes a quality or feeling, or if it exudes, they show that they have it to a great extent.
例：The guerrillas exude confidence. Every town, they say, is under their control...游击队员显得信心十足，他们说每座城镇都在他们的控制之下。
例：She exudes an air of relaxed calm...她表现出一副气定神闲的样子。

V-ERG
（使）渗出;（使）逸出
If something exudes a liquid or smell or if a liquid or smell exudes from it, the liquid or smell comes out of it slowly and steadily.
例：Nearby was a factory which exuded a pungent smell.旁边是一家散发出刺鼻气味的工厂。
例：...the fluid that exudes from the cane toad's back.海蟾蜍背部分泌的液体

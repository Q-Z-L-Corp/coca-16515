passion 
英[ˈpæʃn] 美[ˈpæʃn]
 
n.激情，热情；热心，爱好；热恋；酷爱；vt.&vi.[诗歌用语]表露强烈感情，显示巨大热情；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
强烈的情欲；热恋
Passion is strong sexual feelings towards someone.
例：...my passion for a dark-haired, slender boy named James.我对一个名叫詹姆斯的黑头发高挑个儿男孩的热恋
例：...the expression of love and passion.爱和情欲的表达

N-UNCOUNT
热情；激情；强烈的感情
Passion is a very strong feeling about something or a strong belief in something.
例：He spoke with great passion.他发表了热情洋溢的讲话。
例：...the passion and commitment of the Republican candidate.共和党候选人的激情和奉献精神

N-COUNT
强烈爱好；酷爱
If you have a passion for something, you have a very strong interest in it and like it very much.
例：She had a passion for gardening...她酷爱园艺。
例：His other great passion was Italy.他酷爱的另一个国家是意大利。

ADJ-GRADED
（任务）繁重的，艰巨的，麻烦的
If you describe a task as onerous, you dislike having to do it because you find it difficult or unpleasant.
例：...parents who have had the onerous task of bringing up a very difficult child.曾经辛辛苦苦地养育过任性难缠的孩子的父母亲

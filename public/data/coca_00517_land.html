COCA TOP 20000 words - land 




land 
英[lænd] 美[lænd]
 
n.陆地；国家；地产；土地；vt.& vi.（使）登岸；降临；使陷于（困境）；使不得不应付；vt.自船上卸下；获得；捕到；钓到（鱼）；vi.跳落，跌落，被抛落（地面）；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
土地；田地；（尤指）耕作用地，建筑用地
Land is an area of ground, especially one that is used for a particular purpose such as farming or building.
例：Good agricultural land is in short supply.良田短缺。
例：...160 acres of land.160英亩土地

N-COUNT
（属于某人的）地产，地皮
You can refer to an area of land which someone owns as their land or their lands.
例：Their home is on his father's land...他们的家建在他父亲拥有的地皮上。
例：His lands were poorly farmed.他的田地耕种不当。

N-SING
农耕；农村生活
If you talk about the land, you mean farming and the way of life in farming areas, in contrast to life in the cities.
例：Living off the land was hard enough at the best of times.即使在年景最好的时候，务农为生也是相当艰辛的。

N-UNCOUNT
陆地；大地
Land is the part of the world that consists of ground, rather than sea or air.
例：It isn't clear whether the plane went down over land or sea.不清楚飞机是坠毁在陆地上还是海里。
例：...a stretch of sandy beach that was almost inaccessible from the land.从陆路几乎难以到达的一片沙滩

N-COUNT
国度；国土
You can use land to refer to a country in a poetic or emotional way.
例：Her husband's body lies buried 2,000 miles away in a strange land.她丈夫的遗体埋葬在两千英里外一个陌生的国度。
例：...blessed lands of sun and sea and olive trees.有着阳光、海水和橄榄树的美丽国度

VERB
落下；跌落
When someone or something lands, they come down to the ground after moving through the air or falling.
例：He was sent flying into the air and landed 20ft away...他被抛到空中，落在20英尺外的地方。
例：Three mortar shells had landed close to a crowd of people.3枚迫击炮弹落在人群旁。

V-ERG
（使）着陆；（使）登陆；抵达
When someone lands a plane, ship, or spacecraft, or when it lands, it arrives somewhere after a journey.
例：The jet landed after a flight of just under three hours...这架喷气式飞机在飞行了将近3小时后降落了。
例：He landed his troops on the western shore...他率部队在西岸登陆。

VERB
（尤指船只）卸（货）
To land goods somewhere means to unload them there at the end of a journey, especially by ship.
例：The vessels will have to land their catch at designated ports.船只必须在指定的港口把捕到的鱼卸下。
例：...a five-man gang which landed the huge shipment on the Cornwall coast.在康沃尔海岸卸下这一大批货的五人团伙

V-ERG
（使）落入；（使）陷于
If you land in an unpleasant situation or place or if something lands you in it, something causes you to be in it.
例：He landed in a psychiatric ward...他被关进了精神病房。
例：This is not the first time his exploits have landed him in trouble.这已经不是他第一次因自己的冒险行为而惹上麻烦了。

VERB
使遭遇（困境）
If someone or something lands you with a difficult situation, they cause you to have to deal with the difficulties involved.
例：The other options simply complicate the situation and could land him with more expense.其他办法只会使情况复杂化，而且可能给他带来更大花销。

VERB
意外到来；突然冒出
If something lands somewhere, it arrives there unexpectedly, often causing problems.
例：Two days later the book had already landed on his desk...两天后那本书竟然已经摆在了他的书桌上。
例：This was the weekend that the war finally landed on their doorstep.在这个周末，战火终于烧到了他们的家门口。

VERB
抓到，钓到（鱼）
If you land a fish, you succeed in catching it and getting it out of the water.
例：One angler landed fish of 10 lb and 9 lb on the same day.一名垂钓者在同一天分别钓到了10磅和9磅重的鱼。

VERB
搞到；弄到；捞到
If you land something that is difficult to get and that many people want, you are successful in getting it.
例：He landed a place on the graduate training scheme...他在毕业生培训计划中赢得了一个位置。
例：His flair with hair soon landed him a part-time job at his local barbers.他的美发天赋令他很快在当地一家发廊找到一份兼职工作。

V-ERG
击；打
If someone lands a blow or punch or if their blow or punch lands, they hit someone.
例：De Leon landed a punch on the Italian's mouth after the end of the eleventh round...11个回合过后，德莱昂一拳击在那个意大利人的嘴巴上。
例：I could hear the blows landing as he appealed for help.在他求救的同时我能听到拳头落下的声音。

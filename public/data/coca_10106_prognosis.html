prognosis 
英[prɒgˈnəʊsɪs] 美[prɑgˈnoʊsɪs]
 
n.[医]预后，判病结局；预测；
 "

N-COUNT
（尤指对病人病情的）预后，预断
A prognosis is an estimate of the future of someone or something, especially about whether a patient will recover from an illness.
例：If the cancer is caught early the prognosis is excellent.如果癌症在早期发现，预后效果还是非常好的。
例：...a gloomy prognosis of the Scots' championship prospects.对苏格兰队的夺冠前景并不看好

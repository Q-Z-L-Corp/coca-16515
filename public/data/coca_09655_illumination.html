illumination 
英[ɪˌlu:mɪˈneɪʃn] 美[ɪˌluməˈneʃən]
 
n.照明；阐明，解释清楚；<物>照度；彩饰，图案花饰；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
照明;照亮
Illumination is the lighting that a place has.
例：The only illumination came from a small window high in the opposite wall.唯一的亮光来自对面墙上高处的一扇小窗子。

N-PLURAL
彩灯;灯饰
Illuminations are coloured lights which are put up in towns, especially at Christmas, in order to make them look attractive, especially at night.
例：...the famous Blackpool illuminations.著名的布莱克浦彩灯节

N-UNCOUNT
觉悟;启发;精神启示
You can use illumination to describe an increased understanding of something, especially something of a religious or spiritual nature.
例：...a sense of illumination...顿悟
例：No further illumination can be had from this theory.这一理论不能给人以更深刻的启迪。

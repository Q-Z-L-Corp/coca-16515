delinquency 
英[dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi] 美[dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi, -ˈlɪn-]
 
n.不法行为；少年罪犯；过失； 罪过；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指青少年的)违法行为，犯罪行为
Delinquency is criminal behaviour, especially that of young people.
例：He had no history of delinquency.他没有违法记录。
例：...a whole range of crimes and delinquencies.各种违法犯罪行为

N-UNCOUNT
逾期未还债务（或未缴税款）
Delinquency is failure to pay a debt or tax.
例：Not all delinquencies lead to foreclosure.并不是所有未按时还债的情形都会导致丧失抵押品的赎回权。
例：...increases in mortgage delinquency rates.抵押贷款拖欠率的上升

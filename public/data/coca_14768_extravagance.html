extravagance 
英[ɪkˈstrævəgəns] 美[ɪkˈstrævəɡəns]
 
n.奢侈，挥霍；奢侈品；过分（的事情）；放纵的言行；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
奢侈;铺张;浪费
Extravagance is the spending of more money than is reasonable or than you can afford.
例：...gross mismanagement and financial extravagance...严重的管理不善和资金浪费
例：When the company went under, tales of his extravagance surged through the industry.公司破产后，有关他铺张浪费的种种传闻传遍了整个行业。

N-COUNT
奢侈品
An extravagance is something that you spend money on but cannot really afford.
例：Her only extravagance was horses...她唯一的奢侈品就是马。
例：Why waste money on such extravagances?为什么把钱浪费在这些奢侈品上呢？

pertain 
英[pəˈteɪn] 美[pərˈteɪn]
 
vi.关于，有关；适合；附属，从属；
 "

VERB
与…相关；属于；适用（于）
If one thing pertains to another, it relates, belongs, or applies to it.
例：The restrictions he imposed pertained to the type and height of buildings and the activities for which they could be used...他限制的是建筑物的种类和高度以及它们的用途。
例：I would much rather that you asked Mrs Zuckerman any questions pertaining to herself.我宁愿你问楚克尔曼夫人的是与她本人相关的问题。

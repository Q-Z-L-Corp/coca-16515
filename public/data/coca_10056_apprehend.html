apprehend 
英[ˌæprɪˈhend] 美[ˌæprɪˈhɛnd]
 
vt.理解；逮捕，拘押；忧虑；vi.理解；担心；
 "

VERB
逮捕；拘押
If the police apprehend someone, they catch them and arrest them.
例：Police have not apprehended her killer.警察还未抓获谋杀她的凶手。

VERB
了解;明白
If you apprehend something, you understand it fully.
例：Only now can I begin to apprehend the power of these forces.直到现在我才真正了解这些队伍的力量。

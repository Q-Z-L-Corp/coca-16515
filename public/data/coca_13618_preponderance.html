 "

N-SING
（数量上的）优势，大多数，主体
If there is a preponderance of one type of person or thing in a group, there is more of that type than of any other.
例：...a preponderance of bright, middle-class children in one group.一个小组中聪明伶俐的中产阶级家庭的孩子占大多数
例：...Bath, with its preponderance of small businesses.以小企业为主的巴斯地区

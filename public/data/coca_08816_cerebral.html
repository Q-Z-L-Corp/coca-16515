cerebral 
英[ˈserəbrəl] 美[səˈribrəl]
 
adj.大脑的；脑的；理智的；非感情方面的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
运用智力的;理智的;冷静的
If you describe someone or something as cerebral, you mean that they are intellectual rather than emotional.
例：Washington struck me as a precarious place from which to publish such a cerebral newspaper.我的感觉是，在华盛顿出版这样一份理性的报纸不是太靠谱。

ADJ
脑的;大脑的
Cerebral means relating to the brain.
例：...a cerebral haemorrhage.脑溢血

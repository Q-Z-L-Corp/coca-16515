engineer 
英[ˌendʒɪˈnɪə(r)] 美[ˌendʒɪˈnɪr]
 
n.工程师，技师；（轮船的）机师；〈美〉（火车的）司机，；（陆军的）工兵；vt.设计，策划；安排或处理；改变…的基因（或遗传）结构；
 "

N-COUNT
工程师；设计师
An engineer is a person who uses scientific knowledge to design, construct, and maintain engines and machines or structures such as roads, railways, and bridges.

N-COUNT
机械修理工;电气修理工；技师
An engineer is a person who repairs mechanical or electrical devices.
例：They send a service engineer to fix the disk drive.他们派出维修人员来修磁盘驱动器。

N-COUNT
（船上的）轮机手
An engineer is a person who is responsible for maintaining the engine of a ship while it is at sea.

VERB
设计制造（车辆、桥梁、建筑物等）
When a vehicle, bridge, or building is engineered, it is planned and constructed using scientific methods.
例：Many of Kuwait's spacious freeways were engineered by W S Atkins.科威特有很多宽阔的高速公路是阿特金斯集团设计建造的。
例：...the car's better designed and engineered rivals.比那款轿车的设计和制造更为精良的竞争车型

VERB
（巧妙或间接地）安排，筹划
If you engineer an event or situation, you arrange for it to happen, in a clever or indirect way.
例：He could stand no more and engineered an escape...他无法再忍受，便策划了出逃。
例：This boom has been engineered by the Chancellor for short-term political reasons.这片繁荣景象是总理为了短期政治利益而精心安排的。

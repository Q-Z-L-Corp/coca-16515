ADJ
分布的;分散的
If things are distributed throughout an area, object, or group, they exist throughout it.
例：These cells are widely distributed throughout the body...这些细胞在人体内分布广泛。
例：Galactic surveys show that distant galaxies are not as evenly distributed in space as theory predicts.对银河系的研究表明，遥远星系在太空中的分布并不像理论所预言的那样均匀。

essence 
英[ˈesns] 美[ˈɛsəns]
 
n.本质，实质；精华，精髓；香精；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
实质;本质;精髓
The essence of something is its basic and most important characteristic which gives it its individual identity.
例：The essence of consultation is to listen to, and take account of, the views of those consulted.当顾问的精髓就在于倾听并考虑咨询者的观点。
例：...the essence of life...生活的本质

N-MASS
精油;香精
Essence is a very concentrated liquid that is used for flavouring food or for its smell.
例：...a few drops of vanilla essence.几滴香草精油
例：...exotic bath essences.具有异国情调的沐浴香精

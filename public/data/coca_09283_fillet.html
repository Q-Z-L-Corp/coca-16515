fillet 
英[ˈfɪlɪt] 美[fɪˈleɪ]
 
n.肉片，鱼片；头带，带状物；[建]平缘，木摺；[纹章学] 筋鞘；vt.用带缚或装饰；把（鱼、肉）切成片；
 "

N-VAR
(尤指牛肉)无骨肉片
Fillet is a strip of meat, especially beef, that has no bones in it.
例：...fillet of beef with shallots.葱香无骨牛肉片
例：...chicken breast fillets.鸡脯肉片

N-COUNT
去骨鱼片
A fillet of fish is the side of a fish with the bones removed.
例：...anchovy fillets...去骨鳀鱼片
例：I ordered a fine fillet of salmon.我点了一份精品去骨鲑鱼片。

VERB
剔除(鱼或肉)的骨头
When you fillet fish or meat, you prepare it by taking the bones out.
例：Don't be afraid to ask your fishmonger to fillet flat fish.尽管叫鱼贩子把比目鱼的鱼刺剔掉。

reassurance 
英[ˌri:əˈʃʊərəns] 美[ˌriəˈʃʊrəns]
 
n.使安心；再保证；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
安慰；慰藉
If someone needs reassurance, they are very worried and need someone to help them stop worrying by saying kind or helpful things.
例：She needed reassurance that she belonged somewhere...她需要一种归属感让她安心。
例：'You really won't tell?' she asked, begging for reassurance.“你真的不说吗？”她问道，乞求得到安慰。

N-COUNT
安慰（的话语）；保证
Reassurances are things that you say to help people stop worrying about something.
例：...reassurances that pesticides are not harmful.杀虫剂没有危害的保证

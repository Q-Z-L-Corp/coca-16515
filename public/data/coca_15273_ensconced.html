ADJ
舒服地坐于…的;安坐的
If you are ensconced somewhere, you are settled there firmly or comfortably and have no intention of moving or leaving.
例：Brian was ensconced behind the bar...布赖恩安坐在柜台后。
例：She looked at Miss Melville, snugly ensconced among her new friends.她看了看梅尔维尔小姐，她正舒适地安坐在新朋友中间。

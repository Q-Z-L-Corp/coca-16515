N-COUNT
险境；危局；绝境
If you say that someone is on the edge of a precipice, you mean that they are in a dangerous situation in which they are extremely close to disaster or failure.
例：I was beginning to admit to myself that our marriage was rolling toward the edge of a precipice...我内心开始承认我们的婚姻正走向危险的边缘。
例：The King now stands on the brink of a political precipice.国王现在处于政治上岌岌可危的境地。

ADJ
身体的；肉体的
Your bodily needs and functions are the needs and functions of your body.
例：...descriptions of natural bodily functions...对人体自然生理功能的描述
例：There's more to eating than just bodily needs.饮食不仅仅是出于身体的需要。

ADV
整个身体地；全身地
You use bodily to indicate that an action involves the whole of someone's body.
例：I was hurled bodily to the deck.我整个人都被摔到了甲板上。

N-UNCOUNT
夜间；夜里
Night-time is the period of time between when it gets dark and when the sun rises.
例：They wanted someone responsible to look after the place at night-time...他们想要个有责任心的人夜间照管这个地方。
例：A twelve hour night time curfew is in force.正在执行12小时的宵禁。

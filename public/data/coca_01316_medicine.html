medicine 
英[ˈmedsn] 美[ˈmɛdɪsɪn]
 
n.医学；药物；有功效的东西，良药；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
医学;疗法;医疗
Medicine is the treatment of illness and injuries by doctors and nurses.
例：He pursued a career in medicine...他从事医学。
例：I was interested in alternative medicine and becoming an aromatherapist...我对替代疗法非常感兴趣，想做个芳香疗法治疗师。

N-MASS
（吞服的）药，药物，药剂
Medicine is a substance that you drink or swallow in order to cure an illness.
例：People in hospitals are dying because of shortage of medicine.由于药品匮乏，医院里的病人濒临死亡。
例：...herbal medicines.草药

N-VAR
（足球赛的）开赛
In football, the kick-off is the time at which a particular game starts.
例：The kick-off is at 1.30.开赛时间是1点30分。

N-COUNT
（橄榄球赛的）开球
In American football, a kickoff is the kick that begins a play, for example at the beginning of a half or after a touchdown or field goal.

N-SING
（事件或活动的）开始，开幕
The kick-off of an event or activity is its beginning.
例：People stood waiting for the kick-off of the parade.人们站着等候游行的开始。

ADJ
尚存的;现存的
If something is extant, it is still in existence, in spite of being very old.
例：Two fourteenth-century manuscripts of this text are still extant...这个文本仍有两份14世纪手抄本存世。
例：The oldest extant document is dated 1492.现存最古老的文献出自1492年。

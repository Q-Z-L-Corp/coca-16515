mite 
英[maɪt] 美[maɪt]
 
n.小虫；极小量；小孩子；微小的东西；
 "

PHRASE
一点点;极少量
A mite means to a small extent or degree. It is sometimes used to make a statement less extreme.
例：I can't help feeling just a mite uneasy about it...我对此不禁感到一丝不安。
例：I think if you were a woman you might feel a mite differently.我想如果你是女人的话，也许会感到些许不同。

N-COUNT
(生存在植物上或动物皮毛中的)螨
Mites are very tiny creatures that live on plants, for example, or in animals' fur.
例：...an itching skin disorder caused by parasitic mites.寄生螨虫引起的皮肤瘙痒

N-COUNT
（可怜的）小孩，小家伙
If you refer to a small child as a poor little mite, you mean that you feel sorry for them.
例：The poor mite was so ill.可怜的孩子病得这么重。

converted 
英[kən'vɜ:tɪd] 美[kən'vɜtɪd]
 
adj.更换信仰的，修改的；v.（使）转变( convert的过去式和过去分词 )；（使）转化；皈依；改变（信仰）；
 "

V-ERG
(使)改变;更改;(使)转变
If one thing is converted or converts into another, it is changed into a different form.
例：The signal will be converted into digital code.信号会被转变为数字代码。
例：...naturally occurring substances which the body can convert into vitamins.自然产生的能被身体转化成维生素的物质

VERB
改造，改建(房屋等)
If someone converts a room or building, they alter it in order to use it for a different purpose.
例：By converting the loft, they were able to have two extra bedrooms.把阁楼改造一下，他们就可以多出两间卧室。
例：...the entrepreneur who wants to convert County Hall into an hotel...想把市政厅改建成旅馆的企业家

VERB
改造，改装(车辆或设备)
If you convert a vehicle or piece of equipment, you change it so that it can use a different fuel.
例：Save money by converting your car to unleaded...将车改装一下，使用无铅汽油，这样可以省钱。
例：The programme to convert every gas burner in Britain took 10 years.完成对英国所有煤气灶的改造花了10年时间。

VERB
换算;折算
If you convert a quantity from one system of measurement to another, you calculate what the quantity is in the second system.
例：Converting metric measurements to U.S. equivalents is easy.将公制计量换算成美制计量非常简单。

V-ERG
(使)改变宗教（或政治）信仰;(使)皈依;(使)归附
If someone converts you, they persuade you to change your religious or political beliefs. You can also say that someone converts to a different religion.
例：If you try to convert him, you could find he just walks away...如果你试图改变他的信仰，他就会走开。
例：He was a major influence in converting Godwin to political radicalism...戈德温变成政治激进主义者主要是受他的影响。

N-COUNT
皈依者;归附者;改变信仰者
A convert is someone who has changed their religious or political beliefs.
例：She, too, was a convert to Roman Catholicism.她也皈依了天主教。
例：...a Muslim convert now known as Yusuf Islam.现以优素福·伊斯兰这个名字为大家熟知的伊斯兰教皈依者

VERB
使…迷上;使…热衷于
If someone converts you to something, they make you very enthusiastic about it.
例：He quickly converted me to the joys of cross-country skiing.他很快就使我迷上了越野滑雪。

N-COUNT
刚迷上…的人;最近开始热衷于…的人
If you describe someone as a convert to something, you mean that they have recently become very enthusiastic about it.
例：...recent converts to vegetarianism.最近开始热衷于素食主义的人

ADJ
不可避免的;无法规避的;必然(发生)的
If something is inevitable, it is certain to happen and cannot be prevented or avoided.
例：If the case succeeds, it is inevitable that other trials will follow...如果该案胜诉,其他审判必然随之而来。
例：The defeat had inevitable consequences for British policy.战败对英国政策不可避免地产生了影响。

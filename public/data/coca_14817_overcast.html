ADJ
多云的；阴沉的；阴天的
If it is overcast, or if the sky or the day is overcast, the sky is completely covered with cloud and there is not much light.
例：For three days it was overcast...连续3天都是阴天。
例：The weather forecast is for showers and overcast skies...天气预报上说多云并伴有阵雨。

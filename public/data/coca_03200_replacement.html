replacement 
英[rɪˈpleɪsmənt] 美[rɪˈplesmənt]
 
n.代替；归还，，复位；替代者；补充兵员；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
替代;替换;取代
If you refer to the replacement of one thing by another, you mean that the second thing takes the place of the first.
例：...the replacement of damaged or lost books.受损或丢失图书的更换或补充

N-COUNT
替补(成员或队员)；接替者
Someone who takes someone else's place in an organization, government, or team can be referred to as their replacement .
例：Taylor has nominated Adams as his replacement...泰勒提名亚当斯接替他。
例：The 18-year-old made his debut last week as a replacement for the injured Gordon Durie.这个18岁选手作为受伤的戈登·迪里的替补在上周首次出场。

planning 
英[ˈplænɪŋ] 美[]
 
n.计划；规划；v.“plan”的现在分词；计划；设计；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
计划；规划；设计
Planning is the process of deciding in detail how to do something before you actually start to do it.
例：The trip needs careful planning...这趟行程需要周密计划。
例：The new system is still in the planning stages.新体系仍处于规划阶段。

N-UNCOUNT
城镇规划
Planning is control by the local government of the way that land is used in an area and of what new buildings are built there.
例：...a masterpiece of 18th-century town planning...18世纪城镇规划的杰作
例：He supported the planning authority in refusing the application because of pressures on the Green Belt.他支持城镇规划部门拒绝该项申请，因为这会给绿化带带来损害。

N-UNCOUNT
怨恨;愤慨;憎恨

例：She expressed resentment at being interviewed by a social worker...她表达了对被一位社工采访的愤恨。
例：But the problems of inflation and unemployment still cause a lot of resentment.但是通货膨胀和失业问题仍然激起了很多人的怨恨。

solace 
英[ˈsɒləs] 美[ˈsɑləs]
 
n.安慰，安慰物；vt.安慰，慰藉；减轻；使快乐；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
安慰;慰藉
Solace is a feeling of comfort that makes you feel less sad.
例：I found solace in writing when my father died three years ago...我父亲 3 年前去世的时候，我从写作中找到了安慰。
例：Henry was inclined to seek solace in drink.亨利往往借酒消愁。

N-SING
给人慰藉的事物
If something is a solace to you, it makes you feel less sad.
例：She found the companionship of Marcia a solace.她觉得马西娅的陪伴是莫大的安慰。

enlarge 
英[ɪnˈlɑ:dʒ] 美[ɪnˈlɑrdʒ]
 
vt.& vi.扩大，放大；扩展，扩充；拉长说，详述；
 "

V-ERG
扩大;增大;放大
When you enlarge something or when it enlarges, it becomes bigger.
例：...the plan to enlarge Ewood Park into a 30,000 all-seater stadium...将伊伍德公园球场扩建成能容纳3万座席的体育场的计划
例：The glands in the neck may enlarge.颈部腺体可能增大。

VERB
放大（照片）
To enlarge a photograph means to develop a bigger print of it.
例：...newlyweds wishing to enlarge snaps of their big day.希望把他们大喜之日的照片放大的新婚夫妻

VERB
进一步补充;详述;详细说明
If you enlarge on something that has been mentioned, you give more details about it.
例：He didn't enlarge on the form that the interim government and assembly would take...他没有进一步说明过渡政府和立法机构将采取的形式。
例：I wish to enlarge upon a statement made by Gary Docking.我想就加里·多金的话再展开说一下。

fleshy 
英[ˈfleʃi] 美[ˈflɛʃi]
 
adj.（似）肉的；多肉的；肥胖的；肉质的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
多肉的;肥胖的
If you describe someone as fleshy, you mean that they are slightly too fat.
例：He was well-built, but too fleshy to be impressive.他肌肉发达，但是太胖了，没给人留下好印象。

ADJ-GRADED
肉厚的;肉质的;多果肉的
Fleshy parts of the body or fleshy plants are thick and soft.
例：...the fleshy part of the thigh.大腿肉多的部分
例：...fleshy fruits like apples, plums, pears, peaches.苹果、李子、梨和桃等果肉多的水果

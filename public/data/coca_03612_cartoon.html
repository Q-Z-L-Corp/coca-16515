cartoon 
英[kɑ:ˈtu:n] 美[kɑrˈtun]
 
n.漫画；讽刺画；动画片；草图；vt.为…画漫画；vi.画漫画；
 "

N-COUNT
（报刊中的）漫画，连环画，幽默画
A cartoon is a humorous drawing or series of drawings in a newspaper or magazine.
例：...one of Britain's best-loved cartoon characters, Rupert the Bear.英国最受欢迎的漫画形象之一——鲁珀特熊

N-COUNT
卡通片;动画片
A cartoon is a film in which all the characters and scenes are drawn rather than being real people or objects.
例：...the Saturday morning cartoons.星期六上午的动画片

N-COUNT
收购;接管;接收
A takeover is the act of gaining control of a company by buying more of its shares than anyone else.
例：...the government's takeover of the Bank of New England Corporation.政府对新英格兰银行的接管
例：...a hostile takeover bid for NCR, America's fifth-biggest computer-maker.针对美国第五大计算机制造商——NCR 公司的恶意收购投标

N-COUNT
(武装)占领，接管，控制
A takeover is the act of taking control of a country, political party, or movement by force.
例：There's been a military takeover of some kind.发生了类似军事接管的事件。

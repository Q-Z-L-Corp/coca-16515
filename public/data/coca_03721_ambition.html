ambition 
英[æmˈbɪʃn] 美[æmˈbɪʃən]
 
n.抱负；渴望得到的东西；追求的目标；夙愿；vt.追求；有…野心；
 "

N-COUNT
梦想；理想
If you have an ambition to do or achieve something, you want very much to do it or achieve it.
例：His ambition is to sail round the world...他的梦想是环球航行。
例：He harboured ambitions of becoming a Tory MP.他梦想成为托利党下院议员。

N-UNCOUNT
雄心；抱负；野心
Ambition is the desire to be successful, rich, or powerful.
例：Even when I was young I never had any ambition.即使是年轻时，我也从没有什么雄心壮志。
例：...a mixture of ambition and ruthlessness.既野心勃勃又冷酷无情

consolation 
英[ˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃn] 美[ˌkɑnsəˈleɪʃn]
 
n.安慰，慰问；起安慰作用的人（或事物）；
 "

VERB
安慰;慰藉;安抚
If you console someone who is unhappy about something, you try to make them feel more cheerful.
例：'Never mind, Ned,' he consoled me...“没关系，内德。”他安慰我说。
例：Often they cry, and I have to play the role of a mother, consoling them...经常他们一哭，我就要充当母亲的角色抚慰他们。

N-COUNT
(机器的)操纵台，控制台，仪表板
A console is a panel with a number of switches or knobs that is used to operate a machine.

tasteless 
英[ˈteɪstləs] 美[ˈtestlɪs]
 
adj.无味的；味道差的；乏味的；无判断力、鉴赏力等的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(家具、服装、房屋装修等)俗气的，难看的，没品位的
If you describe something such as furniture, clothing, or the way that a house is decorated as tasteless, you consider it to be vulgar and unattractive.
例：...a flat crammed with spectacularly tasteless objets d'art.摆满了俗不可耐的小艺术品的公寓

ADJ-GRADED
(话语、玩笑等)粗俗的，不雅的，不得体的
If you describe something such as a remark or joke as tasteless, you mean that it is offensive.
例：I think that is the most vulgar and tasteless remark I ever heard in my life.我觉得那是我平生听过的最粗俗不堪的话了。

ADJ-GRADED
(食物、饮料)淡而无味的，没滋味的
If you describe food or drink as tasteless, you mean that it has very little or no flavour.
例：The fish was mushy and tasteless.鱼做得太烂了，没滋没味的。

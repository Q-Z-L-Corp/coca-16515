watery 
英[ˈwɔ:təri] 美[ˈwɑtəri]
 
adj.含水的，水分多的；水一般的；无味的；有雨意的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
微弱的;淡薄的;无力的
Something that is watery is weak or pale.
例：A watery light began to show through the branches...一缕微弱的光线透过树枝。
例：Martha managed to produce a dim, watery smile.玛莎勉强挤出一丝笑容。

ADJ-GRADED
水分过多;(味)太淡的
If you describe food or drink as watery, you dislike it because it contains too much water, or has no flavour.
例：...a plateful of watery soup.一盘寡淡无味的汤
例：...watery beer.味道很淡的啤酒

ADJ-GRADED
含水的;似水的;由水组成的
Something that is watery contains, resembles, or consists of water.
例：There was a watery discharge from her ear...她耳朵里流出水样分泌物。
例：Emma's eyes went red and watery.埃玛双眼发红，泪水盈盈。

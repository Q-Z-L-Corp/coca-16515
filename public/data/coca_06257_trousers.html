裤子
Trousers are a piece of clothing that you wear over your body from the waist downwards, and that cover each leg separately.
例：He was smartly dressed in a shirt, dark trousers and boots...他穿着衬衫和黑裤子，脚蹬皮靴，看上去很帅气。
例：Alexander rolled up his trouser legs.亚历山大把裤腿卷起来。

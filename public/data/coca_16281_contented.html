contented 
英[kənˈtentɪd] 美[kənˈtɛntɪd]
 
adj.满意的，满足的；v.使满足(content的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
满足的;满意的
If you are contented, you are satisfied with your life or the situation you are in.
例：Whenever he returns to this place he is happy and contented...每次回到这个地方，他都十分快乐，心满意足。
例：She was gazing at him with a soft, contented smile on her face.她注视着他，脸上带着温柔而满足的微笑。

ADJ-GRADED
豪华的；奢华的；舒适的
If you describe something as plush, you mean that it is very smart, comfortable, or expensive.
例：...a plush, four-storey, Georgian house in Mayfair.坐落在梅费尔的乔治王朝时期风格的4层豪宅

N-UNCOUNT
（尤用于地毯和给家具覆面的）长毛绒
Plush is a thick soft material like velvet, used especially for carpets and to cover furniture.
例：All the seats were in red plush.所有座位都上了红色长毛绒面。

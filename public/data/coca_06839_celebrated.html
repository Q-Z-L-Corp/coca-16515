ADJ-GRADED
著名的;有名的；受人敬仰的
A celebrated person or thing is famous and much admired.
例：He was soon one of the most celebrated young painters in England...不久他就成了英格兰最负盛名的年轻画家之一。
例：Dean died three days before the opening of Rebel Without Cause, his most celebrated film.迪恩在他最知名的电影《无因的反叛》首映的3天之前去世。

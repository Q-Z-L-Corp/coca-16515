inclination 
英[ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃn] 美[ˌɪn.klɪˈneɪ.ʃən]
 
n.倾向；爱好；斜坡；
 "

N-VAR
意向;倾向;爱好
An inclination is a feeling that makes you want to act in a particular way.
例：He had neither the time nor the inclination to think of other things...他没有时间也不愿意去考虑其他事情。
例：She showed no inclination to go...她丝毫没有要走的意思。

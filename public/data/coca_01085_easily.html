ADV-GRADED
很可能;多半
You use easily to emphasize that something is very likely to happen, or is very likely to be true.
例：It could easily be another year before the economy starts to show some improvement.很可能还要再等一年经济才会开始出现好转。
例：...an ancient barn that is easily the length of two tennis courts.很可能有两个网球场那么长的一个古代畜棚

ADV-GRADED
迅速地；频繁地
You use easily to say that something happens more quickly or more often than is usual or normal.
例：He had always cried very easily...他总是动不动就哭鼻子。
例：They have nightmares, they startle easily.他们做噩梦，经常从梦中惊醒。

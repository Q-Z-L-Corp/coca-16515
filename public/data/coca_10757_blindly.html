ADV-GRADED
盲目地；未经思考地
If you say that someone does something blindly, you mean that they do it without having enough information, or without thinking about it.
例：Don't just blindly follow what the banker says...不要盲目听信银行家的话。
例：Without adequate information, many students choose a college almost blindly.因为缺少足够的信息，许多学生都是近乎盲目地选择大学。

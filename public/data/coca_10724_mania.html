N-COUNT
狂热;癖好
If you say that a person or group has a mania for something, you mean that they enjoy it very much or spend a lot of time on it.
例：It seemed to some observers that the English had a mania for travelling...在某些观察家看来，英国人对旅行似乎抱有一种狂热。
例：The media have a mania about rugby union.媒体格外青睐英式橄榄球联盟。

N-UNCOUNT
躁狂症
Mania is a mental illness which causes the sufferer to become very worried or concerned about something.
例：...the treatment of mania...对躁狂症的治疗
例：He almost wouldn't eat at all and had the oddest manias.他几乎颗粒未进，走火入魔。

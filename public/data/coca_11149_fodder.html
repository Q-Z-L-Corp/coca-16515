fodder 
英[ˈfɒdə(r)] 美[ˈfɑdə(r)]
 
n.草料；炮灰；<美>管自己的事；自己谋生；v.用饲料喂；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
饲料;草料;秣
Fodder is food that is given to cows, horses, and other animals.
例：...fodder for horses...马饲料
例：The alfalfa plant is widely used as animal fodder.苜蓿科植物广泛用作动物饲料。

N-UNCOUNT
素材;材料
If you say that something is fodder for a particular purpose, you mean that it is useful for that purpose and perhaps nothing else.
例：The press conference simply provided more fodder for another attack on his character...记者招待会只不过是提供了更多的再次击他人格的口实。
例：Old movies were the cheapest broadcast fodder.老电影是最廉价的播放素材。

ADJ-GRADED
苗条的;纤细的;修长的
A slender person is attractively thin and graceful.
例：She was slender, with delicate wrists and ankles.她身量苗条，手腕和脚踝曲线玲珑。
例：...a tall, slender figure in a straw hat...头顶草帽、高挑苗条的人影

ADJ-GRADED
微弱的;薄弱的;不足的;不充分的
You can use slender to describe a situation which exists but only to a very small degree.
例：The United States held a slender lead...美国以微弱优势领先。
例：He has won a vote of confidence but only by a slender majority.他以微弱多数赢得了信任票。

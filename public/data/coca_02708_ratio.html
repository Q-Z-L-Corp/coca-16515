n.比，比率；比例；系数；vt.求出比值，除，使…成比例；将（相片）按比例放大[缩小]；
 "

N-COUNT
比;比率;比例
A ratio is a relationship between two things when it is expressed in numbers or amounts. For example, if there are ten boys and thirty girls in a room, the ratio of boys to girls is 1:3, or one to three.
例：In 1978 there were 884 students at a lecturer/student ratio of 1:15...1978年有884名学生，师生比为1比15。
例：The bottom chart shows the ratio of personal debt to personal income...下面的图表显示了个人负债与个人收入之比。

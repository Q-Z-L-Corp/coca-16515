Victorian 
英[vɪkˈtɔ:riən] 美[vɪkˈtɔriən, -ˈtor-]
 
adj.维多利亚时代的；n.维多利亚时代的人；
 "

ADJ
（英国）维多利亚（女王）时代的
Victorian means belonging to, connected with, or typical of Britain in the middle and last parts of the 19th century, when Victoria was Queen.
例：We have a lovely old Victorian house.我们有一栋漂亮的维多利亚时代的老房子。
例：...a Victorian-style family portrait.一幅维多利亚时代风格的家庭画像

ADJ-GRADED
（尤指对良好行为的评判标准和道德观）有维多利亚时代特点的，传统的
You can use Victorian to describe people who have old-fashioned attitudes, especially about good behaviour and morals.
例：Victorian values are much misunderstood...维多利亚时代的价值观受到了严重误解。
例：My grandfather was very Victorian.我的祖父非常传统。

N-COUNT
（英国）维多利亚(女王）时代的人
The Victorians were the British people who lived in the time of Queen Victoria.

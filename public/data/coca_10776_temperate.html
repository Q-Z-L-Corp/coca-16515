temperate 
英[ˈtempərət] 美[ˈtɛmpərɪt, ˈtɛmprɪt]
 
adj.有节制的；（气候）温和的；适度的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(气候)温和的;(地区)温带的
Temperate is used to describe a climate or a place which is never extremely hot or extremely cold.
例：The Nile Valley keeps a temperate climate throughout the year.尼罗河流域一年四季气候温和。

ADJ-GRADED
(行为)温和的，心平气和的，平心静气的
If a person's behaviour is temperate, it is calm and reasonable, so that they do not get angry or lose their temper easily.
例：His final report to the President was far more temperate and balanced than the earlier memorandum.和之前的备忘录相比，他递交给总统的总结报告措辞要更为温和得体。

N-COUNT
（尤指犯罪后或为逃避某人的）逃跑，逃走，离开
If someone makes a getaway, they leave a place quickly, especially after committing a crime or when trying to avoid someone.
例：They made their getaway along a pavement on a stolen motorcycle.他们骑上偷来的摩托车沿着人行道逃走了。
例：...the burglar's getaway car.窃贼逃跑时所驾驶的汽车

N-COUNT
短假
A getaway is a short holiday somewhere.
例：Weekend tours are ideal for families who want a short getaway.周末旅行很适合那些想短期休假的家庭。

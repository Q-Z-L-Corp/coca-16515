ADV
（表示悲伤、怜悯等）哎呀，唉
You use alas to say that you think that the facts you are talking about are sad or unfortunate.
例：Such scandals have not, alas, been absent...这样的丑事，唉，也不是没有。
例：Alas, it's not that simple.哎呀，事情不是那么简单的。

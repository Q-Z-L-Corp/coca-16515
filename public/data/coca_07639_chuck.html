chuck 
英[tʃʌk] 美[tʃʌk]
 
n.（牛）颈部到肩部的肉；咕咕（呼鸡声），砸砸（呼马声）；抛出；亲爱的；vt.[机械工程]用卡盘夹紧；（口语）抛出，扔出；咕咕（咯咯、嘟嘟）地叫；（美国）辞职；
 "

VERB
(随意或胡乱地)丢，扔，抛
When you chuck something somewhere, you throw it there in a casual or careless way.
例：I took a great dislike to the clock, so I chucked it in the dustbin...我很讨厌那只钟，随手就把它扔进垃圾桶里了。
例：That second night, Sid Vicious chucked a bottle at the stage.第二天晚上，席德·维瑟斯朝舞台上扔了个瓶子。

VERB
辞去(工作);停止，终止(活动)
If you chuck your job or some other activity, you stop doing it.
例：Last summer, he chucked his 10-year career as a London stockbroker and headed for the mountains.去年夏天，他辞掉做了10年的伦敦股票经纪人的工作，去了山区。

VERB
(恋人)踹掉，甩掉
If your girlfriend or boyfriend chucks you, they end the relationship.
例：There wasn't a great fuss when I chucked her.我把她甩掉的时候，她没哭也没闹。

N-COUNT
(钻机等的)卡盘，夹盘
A chuck is a device for holding a tool in a machine such as a drill.

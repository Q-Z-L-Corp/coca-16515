VERB
发出长而尖的叫声
If someone or something squeals, they make a long, high-pitched sound.
例：Jennifer squealed with delight and hugged me...珍妮弗高兴地尖叫起来，然后拥抱了我。
例：The car's tires squealed again as it sped around the corner.汽车快速驶过拐角时，轮胎又一次发出嘎吱声。

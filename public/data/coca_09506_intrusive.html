ADJ-GRADED
打扰的;侵扰的
Something that is intrusive disturbs your mood or your life in a way you do not like.
例：The cameras were not an intrusive presence...那些摄像机的存在并不令人反感。
例：Staff are courteous but never intrusive.员工谦恭有礼却从不让人感到唐突。

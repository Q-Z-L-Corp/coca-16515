hospitable 
英[hɒˈspɪtəbl] 美[hɑˈspɪtəbl]
 
adj.好客的；热情友好的；（气候，环境）宜人的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
好客的;殷勤周到的;热情友好的
A hospitable person is friendly, generous, and welcoming to guests or people they have just met.
例：The locals are hospitable and welcoming...当地人热情好客。
例：He was very hospitable to me when I came to New York.我来到纽约时，他对我很热情周到。

ADJ-GRADED
（气候、环境等）有利的，适宜的
A hospitable climate or environment is one that encourages the existence or development of particular people or things.
例：Even in summer this place did not look exactly hospitable.即使在夏天，这个地方看起来也不太舒适宜人。
例：...hospitable political environments.有利的政治环境

translucent 
英[trænsˈlu:snt] 美[trænsˈlusənt, trænz-]
 
adj.半透明的；透亮的，有光泽的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
半透明的
If a material is translucent, some light can pass through it.
例：The building is roofed entirely with translucent corrugated plastic.这座建筑完全用半透明瓦楞塑料封顶。

ADJ-GRADED
透明般的
You use translucent to describe something that has a glowing appearance, as if light is passing through it.
例：She had fair hair, blue eyes and translucent skin...她金发碧眼，皮肤透亮。
例：The sky was changing from translucent blue to thicker grey.天空由通透的湛蓝色变成了暗灰色。

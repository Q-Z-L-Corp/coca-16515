N-COUNT
激变；剧变；动乱
An upheaval is a big change which causes a lot of trouble, confusion, and worry.
例：Wherever there is political upheaval, invariably there are refugees...哪里有政治动乱，哪里就一定有难民。
例：Having a baby will mean the greatest upheaval in your life.有了孩子意味着你的生活要发生重大的变化。

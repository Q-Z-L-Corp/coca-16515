N-COUNT
联系;关系
A nexus is a connection or series of connections within a particular situation or system.
例：The Prayer Book has provided a flexible enough nexus of beliefs to hold together the different church parties.祈祷书为各信仰之间建起了足够灵活的联系纽带，以期将各个不同教派凝聚在一起。
例：...the nexus between the dominant class and the State.主导阶层和国家之间的关系

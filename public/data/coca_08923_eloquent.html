eloquent 
英[ˈeləkwənt] 美[ˈɛləkwənt]
 
adj.雄辩的，有口才的；有说明力的；富于表情的，逼真的；动人的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（言辞、文字等）表达生动的，有说服力的
Speech or writing that is eloquent is well expressed and effective in persuading people.
例：I heard him make a very eloquent speech at that dinner.在那次晚宴上，我听到他作了一番非常有说服力的陈词。

ADJ-GRADED
雄辩的;口才流利的;能言善辩的
A person who is eloquent is good at speaking and able to persuade people.
例：He was eloquent about his love of books.他说起自己对书的喜爱来就会停不住嘴。
例：...one particularly eloquent German critic.非常能言善辩的德国评论家

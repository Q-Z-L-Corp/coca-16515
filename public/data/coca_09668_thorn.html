thorn 
英[θɔ:n] 美[θɔrn]
 
n.刺；棘刺，荆棘；带刺的植物；刺痛；
 "

N-COUNT
(某些植物、树木上的)刺，棘刺
Thorns are the sharp points on some plants and trees, for example on a rose bush.
例：Roses will always have thorns but with care they can be avoided.玫瑰花上都有刺，不过只要小心就碰不到。

N-VAR
荆棘;带刺的树;带刺小灌木
A thorn or a thorn bush or a thorn tree is a bush or tree which has a lot of thorns on it.
例：...the shade of a thorn bush.一丛荆棘下的阴凉
例：...the thorn and bramble thickets.荆棘密布的灌木丛

PHRASE
肉中刺;长期困扰的问题
If you describe someone or something as a thorn in your side or a thorn in your flesh, you mean that they are a continuous problem to you or annoy you.
例：She's a real thorn in his side...她真叫他头疼。
例：The Party was a thorn in the flesh of his coalition.该党是他所在联盟的眼中钉、肉中刺。

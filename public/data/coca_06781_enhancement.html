N-VAR
提高;增加;增强
The enhancement of something is the improvement of it in relation to its value, quality, or attractiveness.
例：Music is merely an enhancement to the power of her words...音乐只是增强了她话语的力度。
例：He was concerned with the enhancement of the human condition.他关心人类生存环境的改善。

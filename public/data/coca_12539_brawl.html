brawl 
英[brɔ:l] 美[brɔl]
 
n.吵架；打架；喧闹的舞会；乱哄哄的拳赛；vi.打架；争吵；互骂；（流水）哗哗地响；vt.大声喊出；为…争吵；
 "

N-COUNT
打架；斗殴
A brawl is a rough or violent fight.
例：He had been in a drunken street brawl.他参与了一场酒后街头斗殴。

V-RECIP
打架；斗殴
If someone brawls, they fight in a very rough or violent way.
例：He was suspended for a year from University after brawling with police over a speeding ticket...他为了一张超速罚单与警察厮打，被大学勒令停学1年。
例：Two gangs of youths brawled on the dance floor of the ferry.两伙青年在渡轮的舞池里打起来了。

forage 
英[ˈfɒrɪdʒ] 美[ˈfɔrɪdʒ]
 
n.牛马饲料；寻找粮草；vi.搜寻（食物），尤指动物觅（食）；（尤指用手）搜寻（东西）；
 "

VERB
搜查;搜寻
If someone forages for something, they search for it in a busy way.
例：They were forced to forage for clothing and fuel.他们不得不去寻找衣服和燃料。

VERB
(动物)觅食
When animals forage, they search for food.
例：We disturbed a wild boar that had been foraging by the roadside...我们惊动了一只一直在路边觅食的野猪。
例：The cat forages for food.猫在寻找食物。

N-UNCOUNT
饲料;草料
Forage is crops that are grown as food for cattle and horses.
例：...the amount of forage needed to feed one cow and its calf.喂养一头母牛和小牛犊所需要的饲料

clerical 
英[ˈklerɪkl] 美[ˈklɛrɪkəl]
 
adj.文书或办事员的；教士的，牧师的；教权主义的；n.牧师，教士；牧师服，教士服；

ADJ
文书工作的;文职人员的
Clerical jobs, skills, and workers are concerned with work that is done in an office.
例：...a strike by clerical staff in all government departments...所有政府部门的文员举行的罢工
例：The hospital blamed the mix-up on a clerical error.医院方面将这一混乱归咎于一处笔误。

ADJ
神职人员的;圣职人员的
Clerical means relating to the clergy.
例：...a bearded man in a dark suit and clerical collar.身穿黑西装，脖子上围着教士领的蓄着络腮胡子的男人
例：...Iran's clerical leadership.伊朗的教会领袖

conference 
英[ˈkɒnfərəns] 美[ˈkɑnfərəns]
 
n.会议；讨论；（正式）讨论会；[工会、工党用语]（每年的）大会；vi.举行或参加（系列）会议；
 "

N-COUNT
(常持续数天的)专题讨论会，研讨会
A conference is a meeting, often lasting a few days, which is organized on a particular subject or to bring together people who have a common interest.
例：The President summoned all the state governors to a conference on education.总统召集各州州长召开了一次教育研讨会。
例：...the Conservative Party conference...保守党大会

N-COUNT
会议;正式商谈
A conference is a meeting at which formal discussions take place.
例：They sat down at the dinner table, as they always did, before the meal, for a conference...他们在餐桌旁坐下，和平常一样，吃饭之前要开个会。
例：Her employer was in conference with two lawyers and did not want to be interrupted.她的老板在和两位律师谈事情，不想有人打扰。

N-COUNT; N-TITLE
(天主教的)红衣主教,枢机主教
A cardinal is a high-ranking priest in the Catholic church.
例：In 1448, Nicholas was appointed a cardinal...1448年,尼古拉斯被任命为红衣主教。
例：They were encouraged by a promise from Cardinal Winning.红衣主教温宁的许诺使他们受到鼓舞。

ADJ
最重要的;最主要的;基本的
A cardinal rule or quality is the one that is considered to be the most important.
例：As a salesman, your cardinal rule is to do everything you can to satisfy a customer...作为推销员,你的首要任务是竭尽全力让顾客满意。
例：Harmony, balance and order are cardinal virtues to the French.和谐、平衡、秩序是法国人最看重的三项基本美德。

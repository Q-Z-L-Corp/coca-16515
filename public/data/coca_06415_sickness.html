sickness 
英[ˈsɪknəs] 美[ˈsɪknɪs]
 
n.疾病；恶心；呕吐；弊病；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
患病;生病;不健康
Sickness is the state of being ill or unhealthy.
例：In fifty-two years of working he had one week of sickness...在52 年的工作中他仅病过一周。
例：There appears to be another outbreak of sickness among seals in the North Sea.看来北海的海豹又要爆发另一场疾病了。

N-UNCOUNT
恶心;呕吐感
Sickness is the uncomfortable feeling that you are going to vomit.
例：He felt a great rush of sickness...他忽然觉得非常想吐。
例：After a while, the sickness gradually passed and she struggled to the mirror.过了一会儿，她渐渐不那么恶心了，挣扎着到了镜子前。

N-VAR
病症;疾病
A sickness is a particular illness.
例：...radiation sickness.辐射病

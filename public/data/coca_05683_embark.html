embark 
英[ɪmˈbɑ:k] 美[ɪmˈbɑrk]
 
vi.上飞机，上船；着手，从事；vt.使…上船或飞机；使从事，使着手；投资于；
 "

VERB
着手；开始做
If you embark on something new, difficult, or exciting, you start doing it.
例：He's embarking on a new career as a writer...他即将开始新的职业生涯——当一名作家。
例：The government embarked on a programme of radical economic reform.政府开始实施一项彻底的经济改革计划。

VERB
登(船);上(船)
When someone embarks on a ship, they go on board before the start of a journey.
例：They travelled to Portsmouth, where they embarked on the battle cruiser HMS Renown...他们行至朴次茅斯，在那里登上了英国皇家海军“声望号”战列巡洋舰。
例：Bob ordered brigade HQ to embark.鲍勃命令旅指挥部登船。

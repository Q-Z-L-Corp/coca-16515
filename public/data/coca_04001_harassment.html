N-UNCOUNT
骚扰;烦扰
Harassment is behaviour which is intended to trouble or annoy someone, for example repeated attacks on them or attempts to cause them problems.
例：Another survey found that 51 per cent of women had experienced some form of sexual harassment in their working lives.另一个调查发现，51%的女性在工作中曾遭遇过某种形式的性骚扰。

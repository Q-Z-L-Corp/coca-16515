conjure 
英[ˈkʌndʒə(r)] 美[ˈkɑndʒɚ, kənˈdʒʊr]
 
vt.用魔术变出；祈求，恳求；vi.变戏法；施魔法；以咒文召唤；
 "

VERB
变魔术般地创造出
If you conjure something out of nothing, you make it appear as if by magic.
例：Thirteen years ago she found herself having to conjure a career from thin air...13年前，她认识到自己得白手起家创造出一番事业来。
例：They managed to conjure a victory.他们出人意料地取得了胜利。

PHRASE
重要人物(或组织)的名字;有影响力的名字
If you say that the name of a particular person or organization is a name to conjure with, you mean that that person or organization is very important and influential in the field you are discussing.
例：By 1920, Fox and Universal were already names to conjure with.到1920年，福克斯和环球已经声名显赫。

zone 
英[ zəʊn] 美[ zoʊn]
 
[地名] 地带、地区（德语）；
 "

N-COUNT
（有某些特色或特点的）地区，地带，区域
A zone is an area that has particular features or characteristics.
例：Many people have stayed behind in the potential war zone...许多人留在了可能会沦为交战区的地方。
例：The area has been declared a disaster zone.这个地区已经被宣布为灾难地带。

VERB
将…划为特殊区域;指定…为某项用途区域
If an area of land is zoned, it is formally set aside for a particular purpose.
例：The land was not zoned for commercial purposes...这块地未划作商业用地。
例：Most of the private land in the park wasn't zoned or protected in any way.公园中的大多数私人土地并未进行任何专门规划或保护。

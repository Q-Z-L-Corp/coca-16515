riser 
英[ˈraɪzə(r)] 美[ˈraɪzɚ]
 
n.起床者，起义者，叛乱者；
 "

N-COUNT
（习惯早或晚）起床的人
An early riser is someone who likes to get up early in the morning. A late riser is someone who likes to get up late.
例：He was an early riser and he would be at the breakfast table at seven.他习惯早起，7点钟就坐在桌前吃早餐。

N-COUNT
（台阶或楼梯的）竖板，立板
A riser is the flat vertical part of a step or a stair.

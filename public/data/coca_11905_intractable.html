ADJ-GRADED
难驾驭的;难对付的
Intractable people are very difficult to control or influence.
例：What may be done to reduce the influence of intractable opponents?能做些什么来减少那些难缠的反对者的影响呢？

ADJ-GRADED
(问题或情况)棘手的,难处理的
Intractable problems or situations are very difficult to deal with.
例：The economy still faces intractable problems.经济仍然面临着一些棘手的问题。

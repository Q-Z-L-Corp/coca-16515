VERB
（用力）拉，拖，提
If you lug a heavy or awkward object somewhere, you carry it there with difficulty.
例：Nobody wants to lug around huge suitcases full of clothes...谁都不想拖着个装满衣服的大箱子到处走。
例：I hastily packed the hamper and lugged it to the car.我匆忙装好食品篮，吃力地把它拎上车子。

counterproductive 
英[ˌkaʊntəprəˈdʌktɪv] 美[ˌkaʊntərprəˈdʌktɪv]
 
adj.反生产的，使达不到预期目标的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
产生相反效果的;事与愿违的;适得其反的
Something that is counter-productive achieves the opposite result from the one that you want to achieve.
例：In practice, however, such an attitude is counter-productive...然而，实际上这种态度只会适得其反。
例：It is counter-productive to address an interviewee in patronizing tones.以盛气凌人的语气同面试者讲话将会适得其反。

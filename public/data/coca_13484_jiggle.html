jiggle 
英[ˈdʒɪgl] 美[ˈdʒɪɡəl]
 
vt.& vi.轻摇，抖动；vt.坐立不安；n.轻摇；
 "

VERB
使上下急动；使左右晃动；抖动；拨动
If you jiggle something, you move it quickly up and down or from side to side.
例：He jiggled the doorknob noisily.他使劲地晃动门把手，响声很大。

VERB
晃动；急颤；抖动
To jiggle around means to move quickly up and down or from side to side.
例：He tapped his feet, hummed tunes and jiggled about...他脚下打着拍子，嘴里哼着调子，身子晃来晃去的。
例：A roll of fat jiggled on the underside of her arm.她胳膊下侧有一层肥肉一颤一颤的。

shower 
英[ˈʃaʊə(r)] 美[ˈʃaʊɚ]
 
n.阵雨；淋浴；淋浴器；一大批；vi.下阵雨；似阵雨般降落；洒落；纷纷降落；vt.大量地给予；抛洒，使纷纷降落；

N-COUNT
淋浴器;花洒;淋浴喷头
A shower is a device for washing yourself. It consists of a pipe which ends in a flat cover with a lot of holes in it so that water comes out in a spray.
例：She heard him turn on the shower.她听见他打开了淋浴喷头。

N-COUNT
淋浴间;淋浴房
A shower is a small enclosed area containing a shower.

N-COUNT
(运动中心等的)浴室
The showers or the shower in a place such as a sports centre is the area containing showers.
例：The showers are a mess...浴室里一片狼藉。
例：We all stood in the women's shower.我们都站在女浴室里。

N-COUNT
淋浴
If you have a shower, you wash yourself by standing under a spray of water from a shower.
例：I think I'll have a shower before dinner...我想晚餐前洗个淋浴。
例：She took two showers a day.她每天洗两次淋浴。

VERB
(洗)淋浴
If you shower, you wash yourself by standing under a spray of water from a shower.
例：There wasn't time to shower or change clothes.没时间淋浴换衣服了。

N-COUNT
阵雨;(尤指)小阵雨
A shower is a short period of rain, especially light rain.
例：There'll be bright or sunny spells and scattered showers this afternoon.今天下午天气晴好，间有零星阵雨。

N-COUNT
(落下的东西)一大批，一阵，一连串
You can refer to a lot of things that are falling as a shower of them.
例：Showers of sparks flew in all directions.一大串火星四处飞溅。
例：...a shower of meteorites.一阵流星雨

VERB
向…抛撒;撒落
If you are showered with a lot of small objects or pieces, they are scattered over you.
例：They were showered with rice in the traditional manner...人们按传统习俗朝他们抛撒大米。
例：Mr Reagan was showered with glass.里根先生被溅了一身的碎玻璃。

VERB
大量给予;慷慨给予
If you shower a person with presents or kisses, you give them a lot of presents or kisses in a very generous and extravagant way.
例：He showered her with emeralds and furs...他送给她很多绿宝石和裘皮大衣。
例：Her parents showered her with kisses.她父母不停地亲吻她。

N-COUNT
送礼聚会
A shower is a party or celebration at which the guests bring gifts.
例：...a bridal shower.为新娘举行的送礼聚会
例：...a baby shower.为新生儿举行的送礼聚会

N-SING
乌合之众
If you refer to a group of people as a particular kind of shower, you disapprove of them.
例：...a shower of wasters.一群饭桶

multimedia 
英[ˌmʌltiˈmi:diə] 美[ˌmʌltiˈmidiə, -taɪ-]
 
n.多媒体；adj.多媒体的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
多媒体
You use multimedia to refer to computer programs and products which involve sound, pictures, and film, as well as text.
例：...the case of an insurance company using multimedia to improve customer service in its branches.保险公司使用多媒体改进其分支机构的客户服务的例子
例：...the next generation of computers, which will be 'multimedia machines' that allow users to control and manipulate sound, video, text and graphics.将来的新一代电脑，即允许用户操控声音、录像、文本和制图的“多媒体机”

N-UNCOUNT
(教学中)多媒体的使用
In education, multimedia is the use of television and other different media in a lesson, as well as books.

N-UNCOUNT
(艺术中)多种材料的使用
In art, multimedia is the use of different kinds of material in a painting, sculpture or performance.

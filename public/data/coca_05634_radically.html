radically

ADJ-GRADED
重大的;根本的;基本的;彻底的
Radical changes and differences are very important and great in degree.
例：The country needs a period of calm without more surges of radical change...国家需要一段时间的稳定，其间不要再有重大的变革。
例：The Football League has announced its proposals for a radical reform of the way football is run in England.足联宣布了它关于对目前英格兰足球运作方式进行彻底改革的提议。

ADJ-GRADED
激进的；激进派的
Radical people believe that there should be great changes in society and try to bring about these changes.
例：...threats by left-wing radical groups to disrupt the proceedings.来自于左翼激进团体可能破坏会议进程的威胁

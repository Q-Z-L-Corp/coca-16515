revel

vi.陶醉；狂欢作乐；扬扬得意；n.狂欢，作乐；喧闹的宴会或庆典；
 "

VERB
陶醉于;沉湎于
If you revel in a situation or experience, you enjoy it very much.
例：Revelling in her freedom, she took a hotel room and stayed for several days...陶醉在自由中的她在宾馆开了一间房，住了几天。
例：Cats positively revel in heat, whether natural or man-made.猫确实喜暖，不管是自然的还是人工创造的温暖环境。

N-COUNT
狂欢;作乐;欢闹
Revels are noisy celebrations.
例：The revels often last until dawn.狂欢常常持续到黎明。

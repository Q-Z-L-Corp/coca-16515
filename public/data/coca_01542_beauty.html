beauty 
英[ˈbju:ti] 美[ˈbjuti]
 
n.美好；美人；完美；突出的范例；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
美丽；漂亮
Beauty is the state or quality of being beautiful.
例：...an area of outstanding natural beauty...自然美景美不胜收的景区
例：Everyone admired her elegance and her beauty.人人都羡慕她的优雅和美丽。

N-COUNT
美女；美人
A beauty is a beautiful woman.
例：She is known as a great beauty.她是出了名的大美女。

N-COUNT
极好的事物
You can say that something is a beauty when you think it is very good.
例：The pass was a real beauty, but the shot was poor.传得非常漂亮，但是临门一脚太臭了。

N-COUNT
魅力；美好；吸引人之处
The beauties of something are its attractive qualities or features.
例：He was beginning to enjoy the beauties of nature.他开始享受大自然之美。

ADJ
美容的
Beauty is used to describe people, products, and activities that are concerned with making women look beautiful.
例：Additional beauty treatments can be booked in advance.额外的美容服务可以提前预订。

N-COUNT
好处；妙处；优点
If you say that a particular feature is the beauty of something, you mean that this feature is what makes the thing so good.
例：There would be no effect on animals—that's the beauty of such water-based materials.对动物毫无影响——水基材料妙就妙在这儿。

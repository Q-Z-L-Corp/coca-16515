 "

ADJ
摄影的；照片的
Photographic means connected with photographs or photography.
例：...photographic equipment...摄影器材
例：The bank is able to provide photographic evidence of who used the machine.银行能提供用过该机器的顾客的影像证据。

ADJ
（记忆）精确的，能详细记录的
If you have a photographic memory, you are able to remember things in great detail after you have seen them.
例：He had a photographic memory for maps.他对地图过目不忘。

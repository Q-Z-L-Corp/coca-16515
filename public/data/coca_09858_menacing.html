ADJ-GRADED
威胁的;威吓的;危险的
If someone or something looks menacing, they give you a feeling that they are likely to cause you harm or put you in danger.
例：The strong dark eyebrows give his face an oddly menacing look...两道浓黑的眉毛使他的脸看起来异常凶狠。
例：He moved his menacing bulk closer to the table...他把自己骇人的庞大身躯向桌子挪近了些。

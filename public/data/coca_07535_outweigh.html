outweigh 
英[ˌaʊtˈweɪ] 美[aʊtˈwe]
 
vt.在重量上超过；比…重要；胜过，强过；比…有价值；
 "

VERB
（在重要性、益处、意义等上）重于，大于，超过
If one thing outweighs another, the first thing is of greater importance, benefit, or significance than the second thing.
例：The medical benefits of x-rays far outweigh the risk of having them...X光在医学上的益处远甚于它带来的风险。
例：The advantages of this deal largely outweigh the disadvantages.这笔交易的利远远大于弊。

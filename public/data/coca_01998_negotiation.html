negotiation 
英[nɪˌgəʊʃiˈeɪʃn] 美[nɪˌgoʊʃiˈeɪʃn]
 
n.协商，谈判；转让；通过；
 "

N-VAR
(尤指商业或政治方面的)谈判，协商，磋商
Negotiations are formal discussions between people who have different aims or intentions, especially in business or politics, during which they try to reach an agreement.
例：We have had meaningful negotiations and I believe we are very close to a deal...我们已进行了有益的磋商，我相信我们很快能成交。
例：After 10 years of negotiation, the Senate ratified the strategic arms reduction treaty.经过10年协商，参议院已经正式批准了《削减战略武器条约》。

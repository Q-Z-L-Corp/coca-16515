sunny 
英[ˈsʌni] 美[ˈsʌni]
 
adj.和煦的：照到阳光的；快活的，性情开朗亲切的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
阳光充足的;晴朗的
When it is sunny, the sun is shining brightly.
例：The weather was surprisingly warm and sunny...天气出奇地暖和，阳光充足。
例：There is a chance of sunny spells in the West.西部可能会有一段天气晴朗的日子。

ADJ-GRADED
阳光明媚的;阳光照耀的
Sunny places are brightly lit by the sun.
例：Most roses like a sunny position in a fairly fertile soil.多数玫瑰在土地肥沃、阳光充足的地方长得最好。
例：...a sunny terrace.光线充足的阳台

ADJ-GRADED
欢乐的;快乐的;乐天的
Someone who has a sunny disposition is usually cheerful and happy.
例：He was a nice lad — bright and with a sunny disposition...他是个不错的小伙子——聪明伶俐而且性格开朗。
例：The staff wear big sunny smiles.员工的脸上都带着灿烂的笑容。

PHRASE
(煎蛋)只煎一面的，单面煎的
If a fried egg is served sunny side up, it is cooked on one side only, with the yellow part on top.
例：I want my eggs sunny side up.我想要只煎一面的鸡蛋。

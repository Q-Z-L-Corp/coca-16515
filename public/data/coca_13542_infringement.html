infringement 
英[ɪn'frɪndʒmənt] 美[ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt]
 
n.侵权；违反；违背；
 "

N-VAR
(对他人权利等的)侵犯,侵害
An infringement is an action or situation that interferes with your rights and the freedom you are entitled to.
例：...infringement of privacy...对隐私的侵犯
例：They see it as an infringement on their own freedom of action.他们把它视为对他们行动自由的侵犯。

N-VAR
(对法律、规则等的)违反,违背
An infringement of a law or rule is the act of breaking it or disobeying it.
例：There might have been an infringement of the rules...可能存在违反规章制度的现象。
例：Infringement of the regulation is punishable by a fine.违反规则应处以罚款。

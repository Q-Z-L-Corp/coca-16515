provoke 
英[prəˈvəʊk] 美[prəˈvoʊk]
 
vt.激起，挑起；煽动；招致；触怒，使愤怒；
 "

VERB
挑衅；煽动；激怒；刺激
If you provoke someone, you deliberately annoy them and try to make them behave aggressively.
例：He started beating me when I was about fifteen but I didn't do anything to provoke him...在我快15岁时他开始打我，可我并没有任何招惹他的举动。
例：I provoked him into doing something really stupid.在我的刺激下他做了件非常愚蠢的事。

VERB
激起；引起
If something provokes a reaction, it causes it.
例：His election success has provoked a shocked reaction...他的当选引起一片震惊。
例：The destruction of the mosque has provoked anger throughout the Muslim world.毁坏清真寺的行为激怒了整个伊斯兰世界。

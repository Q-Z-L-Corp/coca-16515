英[ˈbrəʊkə(r)] 美[ˈbroʊkə(r)]
 
n.（股票、外币等）经纪人；中间人，代理人；旧货商人；〈口〉婚姻介绍人；vt.作为权力经纪人进行谈判；作为中间人来安排、设法；
 "

N-COUNT
(股票、外汇或商品）经纪人，代理商
A broker is a person whose job is to buy and sell shares, foreign money, or goods for other people.

VERB
（国家或政府作为中间人）协调，安排
If a country or government brokers an agreement, a ceasefire, or a round of talks, they try to negotiate or arrange it.
例：The United Nations brokered a peace in Mogadishu at the end of March.3月末，联合国出面在摩加迪沙进行了和平调解。

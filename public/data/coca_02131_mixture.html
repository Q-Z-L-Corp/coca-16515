mixture 
英[ˈmɪkstʃə(r)] 美[ˈmɪkstʃɚ]
 
n.混合，混杂；混杂的事物[人群]；混合（状态）；
 "

N-SING
混合；混合体
A mixture of things consists of several different things together.
例：They looked at him with a mixture of horror, envy, and awe.他们带着一种恐惧、嫉妒和敬畏交织在一起的复杂心情望着他。
例：...a mixture of spiced, grilled vegetables served cold.一道加香料烤制的蔬菜冷拼盘

N-COUNT
混合物；混合料
A mixture is a substance that consists of other substances which have been stirred or shaken together.
例：Prepare the gravy mixture.将肉汁混合料调好。
例：...a mixture of water and sugar and salt.水、糖和盐的混合液

mount 
英[maʊnt] 美[maʊnt]
 
vt.& vi.登上；骑上；vi.增加；上升；vt.安装，架置；镶嵌，嵌入；准备上演；成立（军队等）；n.山峰；攀，登；运载工具；底座；
 "

VERB
组织;发起;开展
If you mount a campaign or event, you organize it and make it take place.
例：The ANC announced it was mounting a major campaign of mass political protests.非洲人国民大会宣布他们正在开展一次大规模政治抗议活动。
例：...a security operation mounted by the army.军队开展的维护安全军事行动

VERB
增强;加剧
If something mounts, it increases in intensity.
例：For several hours, tension mounted...有几个小时，紧张局势有所加剧。
例：The decibel level was mounting...噪音在增强。

VERB
增多;增加
If something mounts, it increases in quantity.
例：The uncollected garbage mounts in city streets...未收的垃圾在市区街道上越积越多。
例：He ignored his mounting debts.他对日益增加的债务置之不理。

VERB
爬上，登上(台阶、梯级)
If you mount the stairs or a platform, you go up the stairs or go up onto the platform.
例：Llewelyn was mounting the stairs up into the keep...卢埃林登上台阶，走进城堡主楼。
例：The vehicle mounted the pavement.机动车开上了人行道。

VERB
骑上(马、自行车等)
If you mount a horse or cycle, you climb on to it so that you can ride it.
例：He mounted his horse and rode away...他骑上马走了。
例：A man in a crash helmet was mounting a motorbike...一位戴头盔的男子正跨上摩托车。

N-COUNT
坐骑;马
A mount is a horse.
例：...the number of owners who care for older mounts.愿意照料老马的马主的数量

VERB
安装;安置;裱
If you mount an object on something, you fix it there firmly.
例：Her husband mounts the work on velour paper and makes the frame...她丈夫把作品装裱在绒纸上，并制作了画框。
例：The support for the fence is mounted on an extension to the table.篱笆的支撑部分安装在桌子延长出的地方。

VERB
组织，举办(展览或展示)
If you mount an exhibition or display, you organize and present it.
例：The gallery has mounted an exhibition of art by Irish women painters.画廊已举办了一场爱尔兰女画家的作品展。

N-IN-NAMES
(用于山的名称中)山
Mount is used as part of the name of a mountain.
例：...Mount Kilimanjaro.乞力马扎罗山

sponsorship 
英[ˈspɒnsəʃɪp] 美[ˈspɑnsərʃɪp]
 
n.赞助；发起；倡议；保证人；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
资助(金);赞助(金);资金支持
Sponsorship is financial support given by a sponsor.
例：Campbell is one of an ever-growing number of skiers in need of sponsorship...坎贝尔是越来越多需要得到资助的滑雪运动员之一。
例：Private sponsorships only accounted for a third of all arts funding last year.个人赞助只占去年所有艺术基金的三分之一。

N-UNCOUNT
赞助行为;资助行为
Sponsorship of something is the act of sponsoring it.
例：When it is done properly, arts sponsorship can be more effective than advertising.如果方式得当的话，艺术赞助可能比做广告更有效。

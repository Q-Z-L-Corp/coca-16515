VERB
安抚；抚慰
If you placate someone, you do or say something to make them stop feeling angry.
例：He smiled, trying to placate me...他微笑着，想要安抚我。
例：'I didn't mean to upset you,' Agnew said in a placating voice.阿格纽用抚慰的语气说：“我不是有意让你不高兴。”

N-UNCOUNT
明星地位(或身份)
Stardom is the state of being very famous, usually as an actor, musician, or sports player.
例：In 1929 she shot to stardom on Broadway in a Noel Coward play.1929 年，她因出演诺埃尔·科沃德的一部剧而在百老汇一炮走红。

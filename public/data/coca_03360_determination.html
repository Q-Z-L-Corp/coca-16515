determination 
英[dɪˌtɜ:mɪˈneɪʃn] 美[dɪˌtɜrmɪˈneɪʃn]
 
n.决心；决定，确定；[物]测定，计算；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
决心;坚决;坚定;果断
Determination is the quality that you show when you have decided to do something and you will not let anything stop you.
例：Everyone concerned acted with great courage and determination...所有相关人员都表现出了极大的勇气和决心。
例：He reaffirmed their determination to tackle inflation...他重申了他们要解决通货膨胀问题的决心。

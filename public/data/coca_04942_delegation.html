N-COUNT
代表团
A delegation is a group of people who have been sent somewhere to have talks with other people on behalf of a larger group of people.
例：...a delegation from Somaliland...来自索马里兰的代表团
例：He was sent to New York as part of the Dutch delegation to the United Nations.他作为出席联合国会议的荷兰代表团成员被派往纽约。

n.表演场地，舞台；竞技场；活动或斗争的场所或场面；古罗马圆形剧场中央的竞技场；
 "

N-COUNT
运动场；竞技场；体育场
An arena is a place where sports, entertainments, and other public events take place. It has seats around it where people sit and watch.
例：...the largest indoor sports arena in the world.世界上最大的室内体育场

N-COUNT
活动领域;舞台
You can refer to a field of activity, especially one where there is a lot of conflict or action, as an arena of a particular kind.
例：He made it clear he had no intention of withdrawing from the political arena...他清楚地表明他无意退出政治舞台。
例：Oil speculation proved a natural arena for his skills.在石油业中进行投机活动对他来说游刃有余。

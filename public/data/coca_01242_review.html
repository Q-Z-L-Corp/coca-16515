review 
英[rɪˈvju:] 美[ rɪˈvju]
 
n.复习；回顾；（报刊的）评论；[法]复审；vt.检验；复查；复习；评论；vi.复习功课；写评论；
 "

N-COUNT
检查;审查;审核
A review of a situation or system is its formal examination by people in authority. This is usually done in order to see whether it can be improved or corrected.
例：The president ordered a review of US economic aid to Jordan...总统下令对美国向约旦提供的经济援助进行审查。
例：The White House quickly announced that the policy is under review.白宫很快宣布该项政策正在审核中。

VERB
仔细研究;审视;细查;详查
If you review a situation or system, you consider it carefully to see what is wrong with it or how it could be improved.
例：The Prime Minister reviewed the situation with his Cabinet yesterday...昨天，首相和他的内阁对局势进行了审度。
例：The next day we reviewed the previous day's work.第二天，我们仔细检查了头一天的工作。

N-COUNT
（对书、电影等的）评论文章;书评;影评
A review is a report in the media in which someone gives their opinion of something such as a new book or film.
例：Disney's 'Beauty And The Beast' has won rave reviews...迪斯尼出品的电影《美女与野兽》赢得了众多好评。
例：We've never had a good review in the music press.我们从未在音乐媒体获得过好评。

VERB
写（新书、新上映电影等）的评论
If someone reviews something such as a new book or film, they write a report or give a talk on television or radio in which they express their opinion of it.
例：Richard Coles reviews all of the latest video releases...理查德·科尔斯为所有最新发行的录影带都写了评论。
例：His book about Afghanistan is reviewed here by Anthony Hyman.安东尼·海曼在此就他那本关于阿富汗的书写了书评。

VERB
检阅(军队)；阅（兵）
When a military or political leader reviews troops, they examine them, or watch them marching.
例：When he reviewed the troops they cheered him as he smiled and raised his hat.他检阅军队时微笑着举起帽子，人们与此同时都向他欢呼。

VERB
（为备考）复习，温习
When you review for an examination, you read things again and make notes in order to be prepared for the examination.
例：Reviewing for exams gives you a chance to bring together all the individual parts of the course...考前复习让你有机会把一门课程所有零碎的知识都融会贯通。
例：Review all the notes you need to cover for each course.把每一门课程需要掌握的笔记都复习一下。

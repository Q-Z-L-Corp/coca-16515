resistant 
英[rɪˈzɪstənt] 美[rɪˈzɪstənt]
 
adj.有抵抗力的，抵抗的；顽固的；n.抵抗者；有抵抗力的东西；防染剂，防腐剂；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
反对的;抵制的;抗拒的
Someone who is resistant to something is opposed to it and wants to prevent it.
例：Some people are very resistant to the idea of exercise.有些人十分反对锻炼。

ADJ-GRADED
有抵抗力的;不受…损害的
If something is resistant to a particular thing, it is not harmed by it.
例：...how to improve plants to make them more resistant to disease...如何改良植物以增强它们对病害的抵抗力
例：The body may be less resistant if it is cold.天冷时，身体的抵抗力会下降。

stick 
英[stɪk] 美[stɪk]
 
vt.& vi.粘贴；张贴；插入；刺入；vt.容忍；产生作用；（尤指迅速或随手）放置；阻延或推迟；n.棍棒，棍枝；枝条；操纵杆；球棍；
 "

N-COUNT
枯枝;柴枝
A stick is a thin branch which has fallen off a tree.
例：...people carrying bundles of dried sticks to sell for firewood.背着成捆的干树枝去当薪柴卖的人们

N-COUNT
杖;棍;棒
A stick is a long thin piece of wood which is used for supporting someone's weight or for hitting people or animals.
例：He looks old and walks with a stick...他看起来很老，走路时拄着一根拐棍。
例：Crowds armed with sticks and stones took to the streets.人群手持棍棒和石块走上了街头。

N-COUNT
(用于某一特定用途的)木条，木棍
A stick is a long thin piece of wood which is used for a particular purpose.
例：...kebab sticks.烤肉扦
例：...lolly sticks.冰棍棍

N-COUNT
(体育运动中使用的)杖，棍，棒
Some long thin objects that are used in sports are called sticks .
例：...lacrosse sticks.兜网球棍
例：...hockey sticks.曲棍球棍

N-COUNT
条状物;棒状物;棍状物
A stick of something is a long thin piece of it.
例：...a stick of celery.一根芹菜
例：...cinnamon sticks.肉桂条

N-UNCOUNT
严厉的批评;大肆的取笑
If you give someone some stick, you criticize them or tease them roughly.
例：It's not motorists who give you the most stick, it's the general public...对你批评最严厉的并不是驾车人，而是普通民众。
例：I get some stick from the lads because of my faith but I don't mind.我由于自己的信仰遭到了伙伴们的取笑，但我不在乎。

N-PLURAL
偏远乡村;边远地区
If you say that someone lives in the sticks, you mean that they live a long way from any large cities.
例：He lived out in the sticks somewhere.他住在某个偏远的乡村。

PHRASE
批评别人的依据
If you say that something is a stick to beat someone with, you mean that it is used, or could be used, as a basis for criticism.
例：Unfortunately historic American fiction is constantly being used as a stick to beat contemporary British writers with.不幸的是，经典的美国小说经常被用来作为批评当代英国作家的依据。

PHRASE
(完全)误解;错误地理解
If someone gets the wrong end of the stick or gets hold of the wrong end of the stick, they do not understand something correctly and get the wrong idea about it.

PHRASE
很多
If you say that there are more things than you could or can shake a stick at, you are emphasizing in a humorous way that there are a lot of them.
例：...a man with more medals than you can shake a stick at.拥有很多奖章的人

ADJ-GRADED
软而黏的；黏糊糊的
If you describe a food or other substance as gooey, you mean that it is very soft and sticky.
例：These cakes are fudgy, gooey, and delicious.这些蛋糕绵软可口。
例：...a lovely, gooey, sticky mess.松软香糯的一团

ADJ-GRADED
过于煽情的；黏腻缠绵的
Gooey is sometimes used to describe very foolish, exaggerated ways of expressing love or affection.
例：I'm gonna write you a long, gooey letter...我要给你写一封情意绵绵的长信。
例：Women went gooey over him.女人们对他缠绵多情。

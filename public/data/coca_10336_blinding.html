blinding 
英[ˈblaɪndɪŋ] 美[]
 
adj.炫目的，耀眼的；使人眼花缭乱的；使人昏聩的；刺眼的；v.使失明，使眩晕(blind的现在分词)；n.碎石子，细沙；
 "

ADJ
（光线）刺目的，炫目的，耀眼的
A blinding light is extremely bright.
例：The doctor worked busily beneath the blinding lights of the delivery room.这位医生在产房刺目的灯光下忙碌着。

ADJ
显而易见的；非常明显的
You use blinding to emphasize that something is very obvious.
例：The miseries I went through made me suddenly realise with a blinding flash what life was all about.我所经历的痛苦让我突然一下子清楚地领悟到了生活的真谛。

ADJ
（痛苦）强烈的，巨大的
Blinding pain is very strong pain.
例：There was a pain then, a quick, blinding agony that jumped along Danlo's spine.先是一阵疼痛，接着一股瞬间的剧痛沿着丹洛的脊柱窜了上来。

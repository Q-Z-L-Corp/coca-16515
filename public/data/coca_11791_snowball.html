snowball 
英[ˈsnəʊbɔ:l] 美[ˈsnoʊbɔl]
 
n.雪球；[植]荚蒾属；vi.打雪仗；滚雪球般增大；vt.向…掷雪球；使滚雪球般增大；
 "

N-COUNT
雪球
A snowball is a ball of snow. Children often throw snowballs at each other.

VERB
(项目、运动等)迅速增长，滚雪球般增长
If something such as a project or campaign snowballs, it rapidly increases and grows.
例：From those early days the business has snowballed...这个企业从早期开始就一直在迅速地发展扩大。
例：The investigation snowballed from there and has since led to several arrests.从那里开始，调查范围迅速扩大，有几人已经被逮捕。

solitude 
英[ˈsɒlɪtju:d] 美[ˈsɑlətud]
 
n.单独，孤独；偏僻处，隐居处；幽静（的地方），荒野；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指平静愉快的)独居，独处
Solitude is the state of being alone, especially when this is peaceful and pleasant.
例：He enjoyed his moments of solitude before the pressures of the day began...他喜欢在白天的压力到来之前享受独处的时光。
例：Imagine long golden beaches where you can wander in solitude.想象一下那长长的金色海滩吧，在那里你可以独自徜徉。

self-serving 
英[ˈselfˈsə:viŋ] 美[ˈsɛlfˈsɚvɪŋ]
 
adj.自私的，自私自利的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
只为个人打算的;一心谋私利的
If you describe someone as self-serving, you are critical of them because they are only interested in what they can get for themselves.
例：They suggest that my motives for proposing reform are self-serving and mercenary.他们暗示说我之所以提议改革是贪图金钱，想谋取私利。
例：...corrupt, self-serving politicians.贪污腐败、一心谋私利的政治家们

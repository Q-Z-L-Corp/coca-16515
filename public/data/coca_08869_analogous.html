ADJ-GRADED
相似的；类似的；可比拟的
If one thing is analogous to another, the two things are similar in some way.
例：Marine construction technology like this is very complex, somewhat analogous to trying to build a bridge under water.像这样的海底建造技术很复杂，与尝试在水下建桥有些相似。

thankfully 
英[ˈθæŋkfəli] 美[ˈθæŋkfəli]
 
adv.（用以表示高兴或宽慰）幸亏；高兴地，感激地；
 "

ADV
幸运的是;幸亏;幸好
You use thankfully in order to express approval or happiness about a statement that you are making.
例：Thankfully, she was not injured...好在她没有受伤。
例：The next day dawned, thankfully with a drop in wind and waves.幸运的是，第二天天亮时，风浪平静了些。

placid 
英[ˈplæsɪd] 美[ˈplæsɪd]

 "

ADJ-GRADED
平和的；温和的
A placid person or animal is calm and does not easily become excited, angry, or upset.
例：She was a placid child who rarely cried...她是个性情温和的孩子，很少哭闹。
例：Marcus remained placid in the face of her outburst.马库斯面对她的发作保持平静。

ADJ-GRADED
宁静的；平静的；安宁的
A placid place, area of water, or life is calm and peaceful.
例：...the placid waters of Lake Erie...伊利湖宁静的湖水
例：He had been leading a placid life for the past eight years.他8年来一直过着平静的生活。

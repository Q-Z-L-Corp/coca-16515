adj.增加的，增强的；v.增加，增大，增多( increase的过去式和过去分词 )；增强，增进；[缝纫]放（针）；增强；

V-ERG
增加;增大;增长
If something increases or you increase it, it becomes greater in number, level, or amount.
例：The population continues to increase...人口继续增长。
例：Japan's industrial output increased by 2%...日本工业产量增长了2%。

N-COUNT
增加;增大;增长
If there is an increase in the number, level, or amount of something, it becomes greater.
例：...a sharp increase in productivity...生产率的大幅提高
例：He called for an increase of 1p on income tax.他呼吁所得税提高 1 便士。

PHRASE
正在增加;不断增长
If something is on the increase, it is happening more often or becoming greater in number or intensity.
例：Crime is on the increase.犯罪活动在不断增多。

ironically 
英[aɪ'rɒnɪklɪ] 美[aɪ'rɒnɪklɪ]
 
adv.嘲讽地，挖苦地；具有讽刺意味地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
具有讽刺意味的是;令人哭笑不得的是
You use ironically to draw attention to a situation which is odd or amusing because it involves a contrast.
例：Ironically, for a man who hated war, he would have made a superb war cameraman.具有讽刺意味的是,他这个痛恨战争的人曾有可能成为一个优秀的战地摄影师。

ADV-GRADED
讽刺挖苦地;说反话地
If you say something ironically, you do not mean it and are saying it as a joke.
例：Classmates at West Point had ironically dubbed him Beauty.西点军校的同学们曾挖苦地戏称他为“美人”。

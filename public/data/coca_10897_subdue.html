VERB
制服;征服;控制
If soldiers or the police subdue a group of people, they defeat them or bring them under control by using force.
例：Senior government officials admit they have not been able to subdue the rebels.政府高级官员承认他们还没能制服反叛者。

VERB
抑制，压制，克制(感情)
To subdue feelings means to make them less strong.
例：He forced himself to subdue and overcome his fears.他强迫自己克制并战胜恐惧心理。

N-COUNT
稀释物;冲淡物
A dilution is a liquid that has been diluted with water or another liquid, so that it becomes weaker.
例：'Aromatherapy oils' are not pure essential oils but dilutions.“香薰精油”并非纯精油，而是经过稀释的。

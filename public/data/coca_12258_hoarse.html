ADJ-GRADED
（嗓音）嘶哑的，粗哑的
If your voice is hoarse or if you are hoarse, your voice sounds rough and unclear, for example because your throat is sore.
例：'So what do you think?' she said in a hoarse whisper...“那么你认为如何？”她声音嘶哑地悄声问道。
例：Nick's voice was hoarse with screaming...尼克的声音因尖叫而变得嘶哑了。

wherever 
英[weərˈevə(r)] 美[werˈevə(r)]
 
adv.（究竟）在哪儿；conj.无论什么地方；各处，处处；
 "

CONJ-SUBORD
无论哪里;在任何地方
You use wherever to indicate that something happens or is true in any place or situation.
例：Some people enjoy themselves wherever they are...有些人能够随遇而安。
例：Jack believed in finding happiness wherever possible...杰克认为应该及时行乐。

CONJ-SUBORD
不管在哪里
You use wherever when you indicate that you do not know where a person or place is.
例：I'd like to leave as soon as possible and join my children, wherever they are...我想尽快离开，回到儿女们中间去，不管他们在哪儿。
例：'Till we meet again, wherever that is,' said the chairman.“再会了，日后再见。”主席说。

QUEST
(用在疑问句中作为where的强调形式，通常表示惊讶)究竟在哪里
You use wherever in questions as an emphatic form of 'where', usually when you are surprised about something.
例：Wherever did you get that idea?...你到底从哪儿得来的那个主意？
例：Wherever have you been?你究竟去哪里了？

PHRASE
或别的什么地方
You use or wherever to say that something might happen in a place other than the place you have mentioned, but that you are not able to say exactly where.
例：The next day she was gone to Lusaka, Kampala, or wherever.第二天她去了卢萨卡，坎帕拉或别的什么地方。
例：...language which will allow the students in class or wherever to express their opinions.学生可以用来在课堂上或其他什么地方表达自己想法的语言

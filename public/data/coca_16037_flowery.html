flowery 
英[ˈflaʊəri] 美[ˈflaʊəri]
 
adj.用花装饰的；<贬>（说话或作品）辞藻华丽的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(气味)似花的，芬芳的;花香味的
A flowery smell is strong and sweet, like flowers.
例：Amy thought she caught the faintest drift of Isabel's flowery perfume.埃米觉得她闻到了伊莎贝尔身上飘出的一丝极微弱的花香味香水的味道。

ADJ-GRADED
印花的;有花卉图案的
Flowery cloth, paper, or china has a lot of flowers printed or painted on it.
例：The baby, dressed in a flowery jumpsuit, waved her rattle.宝宝穿着印花的连身衣，挥动着她手中的拨浪鼓。

ADJ-GRADED
(演说或文章)词藻华丽的
Flowery speech or writing contains long or literary words and expressions.
例：They were using uncommonly flowery language.他们使用了极其华丽的词藻。

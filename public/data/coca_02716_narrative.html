narrative 
英[ˈnærətɪv] 美[ˈnærətɪv]
 
n.记叙文；故事；叙述，记事；叙述手法；adj.叙述的，叙事体的；善于叙述的；
 "

N-COUNT
故事;叙事
A narrative is a story or an account of a series of events.
例：...a fast-moving narrative...快节奏的叙事
例：Sloan began his narrative with the day of the murder.斯隆以发生谋杀案的那天作为故事的开端。

N-UNCOUNT
(通常指小说中一系列事件的)记叙，叙述，讲述
Narrative is the description of a series of events, usually in a novel.
例：Neither author was very strong on narrative.两个作者都不是很善于讲故事。
例：...Nye's simple narrative style.奈简约的叙事风格

drawl 
英[drɔ:l] 美[drɔl]
 
vt.& vi.拖长腔调慢吞吞地说；n.拖长腔调慢吞吞说的话；
 "

VERB
拉长调子说;慢吞吞地说
If someone drawls, they speak slowly and not very clearly, with long vowel sounds.
例：'I guess you guys don't mind if I smoke?' he drawled...“伙计们，我想你们不介意我抽烟吧？”他拉长音调说。
例：He has a deep voice and he drawls slightly.他嗓音低沉，说话略带拖腔。

N-COUNT
入侵者;侵略者
Invaders are soldiers who are invading a country.
例：The invaders were only finally crushed when troops overcame them at Glenshiel in June 1719.1719 年 6 月，部队在格兰舍一役击败了入侵者，直至此时他们才最终覆灭。

N-COUNT
侵略国;侵略军
You can refer to a country or army that has invaded or is about to invade another country as an invader .
例：...action against a foreign invader.抵抗外国侵略军的战斗

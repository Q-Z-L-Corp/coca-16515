headache 
英[ˈhedeɪk] 美[ˈhɛdˌek]
 
n.头痛；令人头痛的事；
 "

N-COUNT
头痛
If you have a headache, you have a pain in your head.
例：I have had a terrible headache for the last two days.两天来我一直头痛得厉害。

N-COUNT
使人头痛的事;棘手的事;难题
If you say that something is a headache, you mean that it causes you difficulty or worry.
例：The airline's biggest headache is the increase in the price of aviation fuel...令航空公司最头痛的事就是航空燃料价格的上涨。
例：This is a real headache for us.这对我们来说真是个难题。

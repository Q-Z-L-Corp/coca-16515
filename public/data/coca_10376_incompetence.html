N-UNCOUNT
无能力;不称职;不合格
If you refer to someone's incompetence, you are criticizing them because they are unable to do their job or a task properly.
例：The incompetence of government officials is appalling.政府官员的无能令人震惊。
例：...his incompetence in failing to conduct full inquiries.他未能实施全面调查

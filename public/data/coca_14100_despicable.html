ADJ-GRADED
可鄙的;卑鄙的
If you say that a person or action is despicable, you are emphasizing that they are extremely nasty, cruel, or evil.
例：The Minister said the bombing was a despicable crime.部长说这次爆炸是一起卑劣的罪行。

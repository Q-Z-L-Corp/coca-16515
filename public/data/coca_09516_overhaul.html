overhaul 
英[ˈəʊvəhɔ:l] 美[ˈoʊvərhɔl]
 
vt.彻底检查；翻修，检修；赶上（他船）；解松（船的）绳索；n.检查；彻底检修；详细检查；大修；
 "

VERB
大修；彻底检修；全面检查
If a piece of equipment is overhauled, it is cleaned, checked thoroughly, and repaired if necessary.
例：They had ensured the plumbing was overhauled a year ago...他们曾保证水管一年前彻底检修过。
例：Our car was towed away to have its suspension overhauled.我们的车被拖走了，要对悬架进行大修。

VERB
对…进行彻底革新；全面修订
If you overhaul a system or method, you examine it carefully and make many changes in it in order to improve it.
例：The government said it wanted to overhaul the employment training scheme to make it cost effective...政府表示希望彻底改革就业培训计划以实现低成本高效益。
例：The legal system needs to be overhauled.有必要对这套法律体系进行全面修正。

VERB
（尤指在体育比赛中）赶上，超过，超越
If someone overhauls a person who is ahead of them, especially in a sports competition, they pass them or become more successful than them.
例：Argentina need to beat Peru by at least four goals to overhaul Brazil and reach the final itself...阿根廷队至少要赢秘鲁队4球才能超越巴西队进入决赛。
例：Beattie led for several laps before he was overhauled by Itoh.比蒂在被伊藤赶超之前，曾有几圈一直处于领先。

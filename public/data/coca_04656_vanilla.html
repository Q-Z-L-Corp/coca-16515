vanilla 
英[vəˈnɪlə] 美[vəˈnɪlə]
 
n.香子兰，香草；香子兰荚；香子兰精，香草精；adj.香草的；香草味的；相对没有新意的，普通的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
香草精;香子兰精
Vanilla is a flavouring used in ice cream and other sweet food.
例：I added a dollop of vanilla ice-cream to the pie.我在馅饼里加了一块香草冰激凌。
例：...vanilla essence.香草精

ADJ
寻常的;无特色的
If you describe a person or thing as vanilla, you mean that they are ordinary, with no special or extra features.
例：...just plain vanilla couples like me and Tony...只是如我和托尼一样的平常夫妇
例：The tensions of the Nixon presidency were replaced by the plain vanilla administration of a friendly, middle-aged, middle-class man from the Middle West.尼克松时代的紧张局面被一届平淡无奇的政府所取代，继任总统温和友善，正值中年，出身中产阶级，来自中西部。

lovely 
英[ˈlʌvli] 美[ˈlʌvli]
 
adj.可爱的；令人愉快的；亲切友好的；慷慨大方的；n.美女，美人，佳人；漂亮的东西；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
可爱的；迷人的；悦耳的
If you describe someone or something as lovely, you mean that they are very beautiful and therefore pleasing to look at or listen to.
例：You look lovely, Marcia...你看上去真漂亮，马西娅。
例：He had a lovely voice...他的声音很动听。

ADJ-GRADED
令人愉快的；美好的
If you describe something as lovely, you mean that it gives you pleasure.
例：Mary! How lovely to see you!...玛丽，见到你真是太高兴了！
例：It's a lovely day...今天天气真好。

ADJ-GRADED
亲切和善的；心地善良的
If you describe someone as lovely, you mean that they are friendly, kind, or generous.
例：Laura is a lovely young woman...劳拉是个亲切友好的姑娘。
例：She's a lovely child.她是个心地善良的孩子。

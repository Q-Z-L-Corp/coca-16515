N-MASS
果汁
Juice is the liquid that can be obtained from a fruit.
例：...fresh orange juice...鲜榨橙汁
例：Soak the couscous overnight in the juice of about six lemons.将粗麦粉浸于大约6个柠檬榨出的果汁中，浸泡整夜。

N-PLURAL
肉汁
The juices of a piece of meat are the liquid that comes out of it when you cook it.
例：When cooked, drain off the juices and put the meat in a processor or mincer.肉煮熟后，沥干肉汁，将其放入食品加工机或绞肉机中。

N-PLURAL
胃液;消化液
The juices in your stomach are the fluids that help you to digest food.

midday 
英[ˌmɪdˈdeɪ] 美[ˈmɪdˌde]
 
n.中午；正午；adj.正午的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
中午;正午
Midday is twelve o'clock in the middle of the day.
例：At midday everyone would go down to Reg's Cafe...中午，大家都会下楼去雷吉餐馆。
例：It's eight minutes after midday.现在的时间是正午过8分钟。

N-UNCOUNT
中午时分(指中午12点钟前后的一段时间)
Midday is the middle part of the day, from late morning to early afternoon.
例：People were beginning to tire in the midday heat.在正午时分的热浪之中，人们开始感到疲倦。

awaken 
英[əˈweɪkən] 美[əˈwekən]
 
vt.& vi.唤醒，（使）觉醒；（使）意识到；vt.激起；
 "

VERB
唤起，激起（情感）
To awaken a feeling in a person means to cause them to start having this feeling.
例：The aim of the cruise was to awaken an interest in and an understanding of foreign cultures.此次巡游旨在唤起我们对外国文化的兴趣和理解。

V-ERG
（使）意识到；（使）认识到
When you awaken to a fact or when someone awakens you to it, you become aware of it.
例：The British never awaken to peril until it is almost too late.英国人从来不懂得防患于未然。
例：...the picture of the Earth, so blue and fragile, that awakened a generation to the Earth's mortality.照片上的地球，如此的忧郁而脆弱，唤起了一代人对地球生命的意识

V-ERG
醒来;唤醒;叫醒
When you awaken, or when something or someone awakens you, you wake up.
例：Unfortunately, Grandma always seems to awaken at awkward moments...不幸的是，奶奶好像总是醒来的不是时候。
例：He was snoring when Desmond awakened him.德斯蒙德叫醒他的时候他还在打呼噜呢。

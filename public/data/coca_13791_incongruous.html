incongruous 
英[ɪnˈkɒŋgruəs] 美[ɪnˈkɑŋgruəs]
 
adj.不协调的，不一致的；不和谐的；不相称的；异向；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不合适的;不和谐的;不相称的
Someone or something that is incongruous seems strange when considered together with other aspects of a situation.
例：She was small and fragile and looked incongruous in an army uniform...她又小又弱,穿一身军装看起来很不协调。
例：The Indian temple is an incongruous sight in the Welsh border country.坐落在威尔士边界乡村里的印度庙宇看上去相当突兀。

ADV
（不）再;再也（不）
If something does not happen or is not true anymore, it has stopped happening or is no longer true.
例：I don't ride my motorbike much anymore...我不怎么骑我的摩托车了。
例：I couldn't trust him anymore...我无法再相信他。

roasted 
英['rəʊstɪd] 美['roʊstɪd]
 
v.烤，烘，焙( roast的过去式和过去分词 )；嘲讽；（对某人）非常生气，严厉批评；
 "

VERB
烤;烘烤
When you roast meat or other food, you cook it by dry heat in an oven or over a fire.
例：I personally would rather roast a chicken whole.我个人更愿意烤整只鸡。

ADJ
烤熟的;烤制的
Roast meat has been cooked by roasting.
例：...roast beef.烤牛肉

N-COUNT
烤肉
A roast is a piece of meat that is cooked by roasting.
例：Come into the kitchen. I've got to put the roast in.到厨房来。我得把烤肉放进去。

N-UNCOUNT
担保金;抵押物
Collateral is money or property which is used as a guarantee that someone will repay a loan.
例：Many people use personal assets as collateral for small business loans...很多人把个人财产用作小额商业贷款的抵押品。
例：Most people here cannot borrow from banks because they lack collateral.由于拿不出东西作为抵押，这里大部分人无法从银行贷款。

ADV
据说；未必真实地
You use apparently to indicate that the information you are giving is something that you have heard, but you are not certain that it is true.
例：Apparently the girls are not at all amused by the whole business...据说女孩们觉得整件事情一点也不好笑。
例：Oil prices fell this week to their lowest level in fourteen months, apparently because of over-production.据说因为产量过剩，这个星期石油价格跌至14个月以来的最低点。

ADV
好像;似乎;看来
You use apparently to refer to something that seems to be true, although you are not sure whether it is or not.
例：The recent deterioration has been caused by an apparently endless recession...看似永无止境的经济衰退导致了最近的恶化。
例：Rudolph said no more. Apparently he was a man of few words.鲁道夫没再说话。看来他是个寡言少语的人。

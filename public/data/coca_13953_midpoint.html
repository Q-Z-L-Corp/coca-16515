N-SING
中点;中心位置
The midpoint between two things is the point that is the same distance from both things.
例：...the midpoint between Paris and Warsaw.巴黎和华沙之间的中点位置
例：...the midpoint of the front of your house.房子前院的中心位置

N-SING
（事件进程的）中间，一半
The midpoint of an event is the time halfway between the beginning and the end of it.
例：She has not yet reached the midpoint of her life.她的人生还没有走到一半。
例：...the midpoint in the current fiscal year.本财政年度的中期

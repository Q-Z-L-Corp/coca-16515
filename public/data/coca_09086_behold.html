behold 
英[bɪˈhəʊld] 美[bɪˈhoʊld]
 
vt.看到，注视；领悟；vi.瞧；用在祈使句中以引起人注意；int.瞧，看呀；
 "

VERB
看见；看到
If you behold someone or something, you see them.
例：She looked into his eyes and beheld madness...透过他的眼睛，她看到了疯狂。
例：He was a joy to behold.他看着让人赏心悦目。

CONVENTION
（用于引起注意）看，瞧
People used to say or write 'Behold' to draw people's attention to something.
例：Fear Not. Behold The Saviour...别怕。看救世主…
例：Behold a series of thrilling photographs of Felix.看费利克斯拍的一系列惊心动魄的照片。

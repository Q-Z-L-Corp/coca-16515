unwelcome 
英[ʌnˈwelkəm] 美[ʌnˈwelkəm]
 
adj.不受欢迎的，令人失望的，令人讨厌的；n.冷淡；vt.冷淡地对待；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不受欢迎的；不想要的；讨人嫌的
An unwelcome experience is one that you do not like and did not want.
例：The media has brought more unwelcome attention to the Royal Family...媒体对王室的过度关注令人反感。
例：A colleague made unwelcome sexual advances towards her.一个同事对她进行性骚扰。

ADJ-GRADED
（访客）不受欢迎的
If you say that a visitor is unwelcome, you mean that you did not want them to come.
例：...an unwelcome guest...不受欢迎的客人
例：She was, quite deliberately, making him feel unwelcome.她是有意让他觉得自己不受欢迎。

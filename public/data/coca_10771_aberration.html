N-VAR
偶发事件;非典型行为
An aberration is an incident or way of behaving that is not typical.
例：It became very clear that the incident was not just an aberration, it was not just a single incident.事实已经很清楚，这件事不是偶发的，它并不是一起孤立的事件。

N-VAR
不正常的怪人；异常行为
If someone considers a person or their behaviour to be an aberration, they think that they are strange and not socially acceptable.
例：Single people are treated as an aberration and made to pay a supplement.独身者被当成异类，而且要付一笔额外的费用。
例：...sexual aberration.性变态

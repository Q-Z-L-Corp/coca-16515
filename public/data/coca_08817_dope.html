N-UNCOUNT
麻醉剂;毒品
Dope is a drug, usually an illegal drug such as marijuana or cocaine.
例：A man asked them if they wanted to buy some dope...一名男子问他们想不想买些毒品。
例：He has failed a dope test for cocaine...他未能通过可卡因药检。

VERB
给…服麻醉剂（或毒品）；在…中掺麻醉剂（或毒品）
If someone dopes a person or animal or dopes their food, they put drugs into their food or force them to take drugs.
例：Anyone could have got in and doped the wine...任何人都可能会进来在酒中掺麻醉剂。
例：I'd been doped with Somnolin...我被人下了安眠药。

N-COUNT
笨蛋;呆子;蠢货
If someone calls a person a dope, they think that the person is stupid.
例：I'm more comfortable with them. I don't feel I'm such a dope.我和他们在一起更自在，也觉得自己没那么傻。

N-UNCOUNT
情报;密报
Dope is information which you have been given illegally or secretly.
例：The government had plenty of dope on him.政府掌握大量关于他的内部消息。

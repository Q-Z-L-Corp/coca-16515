kinda

（用于笔语中，表示非正式会话中 kind of 的发音）
Kinda is used in written English to represent the words 'kind of' when they are pronounced informally.
例：I'd kinda like to have a sheep farm in New Mexico...我有点儿想在新墨西哥州开一个牧羊场。
例：He looked kinda cool but kinda young.他看上去有点酷但也有点嫩。

trek 
英[trek] 美[trɛk]
 
v.长途跋涉；拖着腿走；徒步旅行；（牛）拉货车；n.长途跋涉；艰难的旅程；远距离行走；
 "

VERB
(通常指徒步)艰难旅行，跋涉，远足
If you trek somewhere, you go on a journey across difficult country, usually on foot.
例：...trekking through the jungles...徒步穿过丛林
例：This year we're going trekking in Nepal.今年我们准备去尼泊尔远足。

VERB
(通常因疲惫而缓慢勉强地)行进，拖着腿走
If you trek somewhere, you go there rather slowly and unwillingly, usually because you are tired.
例：They trekked from shop to shop in search of white knee-length socks.他们拖着步子从一家商店走到另一家，寻找齐膝的白袜子。

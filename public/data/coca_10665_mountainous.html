mountainous 
英[ˈmaʊntənəs] 美[ˈmaʊntənəs]
 
adj.多山的；巨大的；山一般的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
多山的
A mountainous place has a lot of mountains.
例：...the mountainous region of Campania.坎帕尼亚的多山地区

ADJ
波涛汹涌的；万丈波涛的
If you refer to mountainous seas, you are emphasizing that the sea is very rough.
例：The fishermen set off in mountainous seas.渔夫们冒着汹涌的海浪出海了。

ADJ
庞大的;巨大的;大量的
You use mountainous to emphasize that something is great in size, quantity, or degree.
例：In the lighted doorway stood the mountainous figure of a woman...一个身材高大的女人站在灯光明亮的门口。
例：The plan is designed to reduce some of the company's mountainous debt.该计划旨在减少公司堆积如山的债务。

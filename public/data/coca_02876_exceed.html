exceed 
英[ɪkˈsi:d] 美[ɪkˈsid]
 
vt.超过；超越；胜过；越过…的界限；vi.突出，领先；
 "

VERB
超过，超出（某数量、数字等）
If something exceeds a particular amount or number, it is greater or larger than that amount or number.
例：Its research budget exceeds $700 million a year...其研究预算每年超过7亿美元。
例：The demand for places at some schools exceeds the supply...某些学校的入学申请人数超过了招生。

VERB
超出，超越（限制、规定等）
If you exceed a limit or rule, you go beyond it, even though you are not supposed to or it is against the law.
例：He accepts he was exceeding the speed limit...他承认自己超速了。
例：I would be exceeding my powers if I ordered the march to be halted.如果我命令停止游行，我就越权了。

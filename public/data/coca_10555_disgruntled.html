ADJ-GRADED
恼火的;不满的;不高兴的
If you are disgruntled, you are cross and dissatisfied because things have not happened the way that you wanted them to happen.
例：Disgruntled employees recently called for his resignation...不满的雇员们最近要求他辞职。

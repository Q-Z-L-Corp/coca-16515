spew 
英[spju:] 美[spju]
 
vt.& vi.呕吐；（使某事物）喷出；涌出，射出；渗出；n.呕吐物；喷出物；渗出物；
 "

V-ERG
(使)喷出;(使)放出;(使)涌出
When something spews out a substance or when a substance spews from something, the substance flows out quickly in large quantities.
例：The volcano spewed out more scorching volcanic ashes, gases and rocks...火山喷出更多灼热的火山灰、气体和岩块。
例：Leaking oil spewed from the tanker...泄漏的油从油轮里涌出。

VERB
呕吐
If someone spews or spews up, they vomit.
例：He's pissed. Let's get out of his way before he starts spewing.他很恼火。趁他还没有发作，我们赶快离他远点儿。

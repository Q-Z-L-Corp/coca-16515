legion 
英[ˈli:dʒən] 美[ˈlidʒən]
 
n.大批部队；众多，大量；古罗马军团；adj.众多的；大量的；
 "

N-COUNT
军团；兵团
A legion is a large group of soldiers who form one section of an army.
例：...the Sudan-based troops of the Libyan Islamic Legion...利比亚伊斯兰军团驻扎在苏丹的部队
例：The last of the Roman legions left Britain in AD 410.罗马军团最后一支部队于公元410年离开英国。

N-COUNT
众多；大批；大量
A legion of people or things is a great number of them.
例：His delightful sense of humour won him a legion of friends.他的幽默风趣为他赢得了众多的朋友。
例：...a legion of stories about noisy neighbours.有关吵闹的邻居的众多故事

ADJ
比比皆是的；不胜枚举的；多如牛毛的
If you say that things of a particular kind are legion, you mean that there are a great number of them.
例：Ellie's problems are legion...埃莉的问题多如牛毛。
例：The number of women who become pregnant after adopting children is legion.领养小孩后怀孕的妇女比比皆是。

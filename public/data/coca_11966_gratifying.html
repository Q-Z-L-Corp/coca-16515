gratifying 
英[ˈgrætɪfaɪɪŋ] 美[]
 
adj.悦人的，令人满足的；v.使高兴，使满意( gratify的现在分词 )；
 "

VERB
使高兴；令人满意
If you are gratified by something, it gives you pleasure or satisfaction.
例：Mr. Dambar was gratified by his response.丹巴尔先生对他的回答感到满意。

VERB
满足（愿望等）
If you gratify your own or another person's desire, you do what is necessary to please yourself or them.
例：We gratified our friend's curiosity...我们满足了朋友的好奇心。
例：Every whim will be gratified.每一个突发的念头都会得到满足。

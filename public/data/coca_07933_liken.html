VERB
把…比作；把…比拟为
If you liken one thing or person to another thing or person, you say that they are similar.
例：She likens marriage to slavery...她把婚姻比作奴役。
例：The pain is often likened to being drilled through the side of the head.这种疼痛常被比作钻头钻入头部的感觉。

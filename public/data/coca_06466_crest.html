crest 
英[krest] 美[krɛst]
 
n.山顶；羽毛饰；鸡冠；（动物的）颈脊；vt.加上顶饰；到达…的顶部；形成冠毛状顶部；到达顶部；vi.到达绝顶；形成浪峰；达到顶点；
 "

N-COUNT
山顶;波峰;浪尖
The crest of a hill or a wave is the top of it.

VERB
到达山顶
When someone crests a hill, they reach the top of it.
例：The first wave of marchers crested the hill.第一批游行示威者登上了山顶。

N-COUNT
鸟冠;羽冠
A bird's crest is a group of upright feathers on the top of its head.
例：Both birds had a dark blue crest.两种鸟都长着深蓝色的羽冠。

N-COUNT
(贵族家庭、城镇或组织的)饰章，纹章
A crest is a design that is the symbol of a noble family, a town, or an organization.
例：On the wall is the family crest.墙上挂着那个家族的饰章。

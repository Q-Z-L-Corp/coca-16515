right-wing 
英[raɪt wɪŋ] 美[raɪt wɪŋ]
 
adj.右翼的，右派的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
右翼的;右派的
A right-wing person or group has conservative or capitalist views.
例：...a right-wing government...右翼政府
例：Liberals say the paper is too right-wing.自由主义者说这家报纸太保守了。

N-SING
右翼;右派
The right wing of a political party consists of the members who have the most conservative or the most capitalist views.
例：...the right wing of the Conservative Party...保守党右翼
例：Why did the right wing have so much power within the Kremlin?为什么右派在克里姆林宫有如此大的权力？

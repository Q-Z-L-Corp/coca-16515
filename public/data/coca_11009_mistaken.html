ADJ-GRADED
搞错的;弄错的；误解的
If you are mistaken about something, you are wrong about it.
例：I see I was mistaken about you...我明白我以前错怪你了。
例：You couldn't be more mistaken, Alex. You've utterly misread the situation.亚历克斯，你真是大错特错，你对局势的判断根本不对。

ADJ
(信仰、意见)错误的，不正确的
A mistaken belief or opinion is incorrect.
例：I had a mistaken view of what was happening.我对当时发生的事情曾有一种错误的认识。
例：...a limited understanding of addiction and mistaken beliefs about how it can be overcome.对上瘾的有限认识和关于如何将其戒除的错误观念

instruction 
英[ɪnˈstrʌkʃn] 美[ɪnˈstrʌkʃən]
 
n.授课；教诲；传授的或获得的知识，课程；[计算机科学]指令；
 "

N-COUNT
指示;命令
An instruction is something that someone tells you to do.
例：Two lawyers were told not to leave the building but no reason for this instruction was given.两名律师被告知不得离开大楼,但对这一命令并没有提供任何理由。

N-UNCOUNT
教学;讲授
If someone gives you instruction in a subject or skill, they teach it to you.
例：Each candidate is given instruction in safety...向每位申请人都讲授了安全知识。
例：All schoolchildren must now receive some religious instruction.现在所有的在校儿童都必须接受一定的宗教教育。

N-PLURAL
用法说明;操作指南
Instructions are clear and detailed information on how to do something.
例：Always read the instructions before you start taking the medicine.在服药之前一定要先阅读说明书。

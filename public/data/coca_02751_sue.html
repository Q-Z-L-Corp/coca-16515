VERB
(通常为索赔而)控告，对…提起诉讼
If you sue someone, you start a legal case against them, usually in order to claim money from them because they have harmed you in some way.
例：Mr Warren sued him for libel over the remarks...沃伦先生控告他散布诽谤性言论。
例：The company could be sued for damages...该公司可能会被起诉，要求其赔偿损失。

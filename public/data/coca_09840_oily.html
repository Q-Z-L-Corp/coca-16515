oily 
英[ˈɔɪli] 美[ˈɔɪli]
 
adj.油的，油质的；含油的，油多的；油腻的；油滑的，会奉承的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
涂油的；含油的；油腻的
Something that is oily is covered with oil or contains oil.
例：He was wiping his hands on an oily rag...他在用一块油乎乎的抹布擦手。
例：When she was younger, she had very oily skin...她年轻的时候是油性皮肤。

ADJ-GRADED
似油的；油状的；油味的
Oily means looking, feeling, tasting, or smelling like oil.
例：...traces of an oily substance.少许油状物质
例：...a medium dry wine with an oily, spicy flavour.滑腻而香辣的半干葡萄酒

ADJ-GRADED
油滑的；谄媚的；逢迎的
If you describe someone as oily, you dislike them because you think they are too polite or say exaggeratedly nice things, and are insincere.
例：He had behaved with undue and oily familiarity...他表现得过于亲热，媚态毕现。
例：The older man asked in his oily voice what he could do for them today.年长的那个男人声音谄媚地询问今天能为他们做些什么。

inaccessible 
英[ˌɪnækˈsesəbl] 美[ˌɪnækˈsɛsəbəl]
 
adj.达不到的；难见到的；不能接近的；无法理解的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
达不到的;难到达的;不可(或不易)进入的
An inaccessible place is very difficult or impossible to reach.
例：...the remote, inaccessible areas of the Andes rainforests.偏僻遥远、难以到达的安第斯雨林地区
例：The route took us through scenery quite inaccessible to the motorist.这条路线带我们穿越了驾车者难以抵达的风景区。

ADJ-GRADED
见不到的;不可使用的;买不到的
If something is inaccessible, you are unable to see, use, or buy it.
例：Ninety-five per cent of its magnificent collection will remain inaccessible to the public...其中 95% 的顶级收藏品仍不会向公众开放。
例：We gained a rich supply of data which would normally be inaccessible.我们得到了通常难以获取的大量数据。

ADJ-GRADED
难懂的;无法理解的
Someone or something that is inaccessible is difficult or impossible to understand or appreciate.
例：A lot of contemporary music is virtually inaccessible.许多当代音乐几乎难以理解。
例：...using language that is inaccessible to working people.使用劳动人民无法理解的语言

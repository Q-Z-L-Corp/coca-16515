fully 
英[ˈfʊli] 美[ˈfʊli]
 
adv.充分地；完全地；足足；彻底地；
 "

ADV
完全地;最大程度地
Fully means to the greatest degree or extent possible.
例：She was fully aware of my thoughts...她完全清楚我的想法。
例：I don't fully agree with that.对此我并不完全同意。

ADV
完全(结束)地
You use fully to say that a process is completely finished.
例：He had still not fully recovered.他还没有完全康复。

ADV-GRADED
全面地;充分地;彻底地
If you describe, answer, or deal with something fully, you leave out nothing that should be mentioned or dealt with.
例：Fiers promised to testify fully and truthfully...菲耶尔承诺所作证词将毫无保留且句句属实。
例：Major elements of these debates are discussed more fully later in this book.这些争论的主要内容将在本书后面的章节中更为充分地加以讨论。

ADV
(用于强调数量)整整，足足，至少
Fully is used to emphasize how great an amount is.
例：Fully 30% of the poor could not even afford access to illegal shanties.足足有 30%的贫困人口甚至连非法窝棚都住不起。

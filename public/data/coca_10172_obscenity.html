N-UNCOUNT
淫秽；色情；猥亵
Obscenity is behaviour, art, or language that is sexual and offends or shocks people.
例：He insisted these photographs were not art but obscenity...他坚持认为这些照片并非艺术，而是色情。
例：James Joyce justified his use of 'obscenity' on the grounds that it was a natural part of life.詹姆斯·乔伊斯以“淫秽”是生活的正常部分为由为自己运用“淫秽”进行辩解。

N-VAR
猥亵的话；下流话
An obscenity is a very offensive word or expression.
例：They shouted obscenities at us and smashed bottles on the floor.他们冲着我们大骂下流话，还把瓶子摔到地上。

N-COUNT
令人憎恶的行为（或事件);道德败坏的行为（或事件）
If you refer to an action or event as an obscenity, you disapprove of it very strongly and consider it to be offensive or immoral.
例：It is not the only place experiencing the obscenities of civil war.饱经内战蹂躏的不止这一个地方。

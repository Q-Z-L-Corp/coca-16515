impediment 
英[ɪmˈpedɪmənt] 美[ɪmˈpedɪmənt]
 
n.妨碍，阻止；口吃，结巴；障碍物；法定婚姻的障碍；
 "

N-COUNT
妨碍;阻碍;障碍物
Something that is an impediment to a person or thing makes their movement, development, or progress difficult.
例：He was satisfied there was no legal impediment to the marriage.这一婚姻没有任何司法上的障碍，他对此很满意。

N-COUNT
(言语)障碍
Someone who has a speech impediment has a disability which makes speaking difficult.
例：John's slight speech impediment made it difficult for his mother to understand him.约翰有轻微的言语障碍，这令母亲很难理解他的话。

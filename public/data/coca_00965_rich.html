rich 
英[rɪtʃ] 美[rɪtʃ]
 
adj.富有的；肥沃的；丰富多彩的；油腻的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
富裕的;有钱的;富有的
A rich person has a lot of money or valuable possessions.
例：You're going to be a very rich man...你将会成为一个非常有钱的人。
例：Their one aim in life is to get rich...他们人生的一个目标就是要变成有钱人。

N-PLURAL
财富;财产
Riches are valuable possessions or large amounts of money.
例：An Olympic gold medal can lead to untold riches for an athlete...一枚奥运金牌能给运动员带来无尽的财富。
例：Some people want fame or riches — I just wanted a baby.有人希望得到名望，有人希望获得财富——我只想要个孩子。

ADJ-GRADED
(国家)富有的，富饶的
A rich country has a strong economy and produces a lot of wealth, so many people who live there have a high standard of living.
例：There is hunger in many parts of the world, even in rich countries.世界上很多地方，甚至在富有的国家里，都有人忍饥挨饿。

N-PLURAL
资源
If you talk about the earth's riches, you are referring to things that exist naturally in large quantities and that are useful and valuable, for example minerals, wood, and oil.
例：...Russia's vast natural riches.俄罗斯丰富的自然资源
例：...the oil riches of the Middle East.中东的石油资源

ADJ-GRADED
富含…的;含有大量…的
If something is rich in a useful or valuable substance or is a rich source of it, it contains a lot of it.
例：Liver and kidney are particularly rich in vitamin A...肝脏和肾脏的维生素A含量尤其丰富。
例：Fish is a rich source of protein.鱼肉富含蛋白质。

ADJ-GRADED
(食物)油腻的
Rich food contains a lot of fat or oil.
例：...the hearty, rich foods of Gascony...加斯科涅丰盛而油腻的食物
例：Additional cream would make it too rich.再加奶油会让它过于油腻。

ADJ-GRADED
(土地)肥沃的，丰饶的，富饶的
Rich soil contains large amounts of substances that make it good for growing crops or flowers in.
例：Farmers grow rice in the rich soil.农民们在肥沃的土地上种植稻子。

ADJ-GRADED
(矿藏等)丰富的，大量的
A rich deposit of a mineral or other substance is a large amount of it.
例：...the country's rich deposits of the metal, lithium.这个国家丰富的锂金属矿藏

ADJ-GRADED
充满（幽默感、创意等）的;（信息）丰富的
If you say that something is a rich vein or source of something such as humour, ideas, or information, you mean that it can provide a lot of that thing.
例：The director discovered a rich vein of sentimentality...导演发现了一个丰富的情感源泉。
例：My collection of Victorian literature turned out to be a rich and often hilarious source of information.我收藏的维多利亚时代的文学作品竟成了一个资源丰富的信息宝库，而且内容常常令人捧腹。

ADJ-GRADED
(气味)浓郁的，香浓的;(颜色)浓艳的，强烈的，鲜艳的;（声音）低沉的，浑厚的
Rich smells are strong and very pleasant. Rich colours and sounds are deep and very pleasant.
例：...a rich and luxuriously perfumed bath essence.香味浓郁的沐浴香精
例：...an attractive, glossy rich red colour.迷人而富有光泽的艳红色

ADJ-GRADED
(生活或历史)丰富多彩的
A rich life or history is one that is interesting because it is full of different events and activities.
例：A rich and varied cultural life is essential for this couple.丰富多样的文化生活对这对夫妇而言至关重要。
例：...the rich history of the island...这个岛屿灿烂的历史

ADJ-GRADED
(收藏或混合物)丰富的，各种各样的
A rich collection or mixture contains a wide and interesting variety of different things.
例：Visitors can view a rich and colorful array of aquatic plants and animals...游客们可以看到一系列各种各样、色彩斑斓的水生动植物。
例：Australia has a rich cultural mix.澳大利亚拥有丰富多样的文化。

ADJ-GRADED
不合情理的;荒谬的;荒唐的
If you say that something a person says or does is rich, you are making fun of it because you think it is a surprising and inappropriate thing for them to say or do.
例：Gil says that women can't keep secrets. That's rich, coming from him, the professional sneak.吉尔说女人不能保守秘密。这话从他这样一个整天打小报告的人嘴里说出来真是太荒谬了。

PHRASE
富得流油;一身铜臭味
If you say that someone is filthy rich or stinking rich, you disapprove of them because they have a lot of money.
例：He's stinking rich, and with no more talent than he ever had before.他现在已经有几个臭钱了，但仍然和以前一样没什么才能。
例：...a handful of filthy rich young men who work eight months a year.少数几个每年只工作8个月的腰缠万贯的年轻人

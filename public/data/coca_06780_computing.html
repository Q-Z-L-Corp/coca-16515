N-UNCOUNT
计算机操作;计算机应用
Computing is the activity of using a computer and writing programs for it.
例：Courses range from cookery to computing.课程多种多样，从烹调到计算机操作应有尽有。

ADJ
计算机的;计算机应用的
Computing means relating to computers and their use.
例：Many graduates are employed in the electronics and computing industries.很多毕业生就职于电子和计算机行业。

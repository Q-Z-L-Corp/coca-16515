 "

N-UNCOUNT
放松;松弛;休息
Relaxation is a way of spending time in which you rest and feel comfortable.
例：You should be able to find the odd moment for relaxation...你应该能抽空休息一下。
例：Relaxation techniques are taught in hospitals in order to help all sorts of conditions.医院里教人学习放松技巧以应对各种各样的病情。

N-UNCOUNT
(规定或控制的)放松，放宽，削弱
If there is relaxation of a rule or control, it is made less firm or strong.
例：...the relaxation of travel restrictions.旅游限制的放宽

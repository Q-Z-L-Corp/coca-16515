well-defined 
英['weldɪ'faɪnd] 美[ˈwɛldɪˈfaɪnd]
 
adj.定义明确的；界限清楚的；已知的；已圈定的；

ADJ-GRADED
易于辨认理解的;明确的;定义清楚的
Something that is well-defined is clear and precise and therefore easy to recognize or understand.
例：Can you look back and see a well-defined path, or simply an aimless series of job moves with no coherent structure?...回顾过去时能看到一条清晰的轨迹，还是只能看到一连串毫无目的、前后不连贯的工作经历？
例：Today's pawnbrokers operate within well-defined financial regulations.当今的典当商按照明晰的金融规章运作。

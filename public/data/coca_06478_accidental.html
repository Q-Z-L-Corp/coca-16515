ADJ
意外的；偶然（发生）的
An accidental event happens by chance or as the result of an accident, and is not deliberately intended.
例：The jury returned a verdict of accidental death...陪审团作出意外死亡的正式裁决。
例：His hand brushed against hers; it could have been either accidental or deliberate.就在有意无意间，他的手轻轻拂过她的手。

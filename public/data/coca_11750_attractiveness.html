attractiveness 
英[ə'træktɪvnəs] 美[ə'træktɪvnəs]
 
吸引力；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（人）漂亮的，动人的，有魅力的
A person who is attractive is pleasant to look at.
例：She's a very attractive woman...她是个妩媚动人的女子。
例：I thought he was very attractive and obviously very intelligent...我觉得他魅力非凡，才智过人。

ADJ-GRADED
（外形）漂亮的；（声音）优美的
Something that is attractive has a pleasant appearance or sound.
例：The flat was small but attractive, if rather shabby...虽然有些老旧，这仍不失为一个漂亮的小公寓。
例：The creamy white flowers are attractive in the spring.在春天，这种乳白色的花朵分外迷人。

ADJ-GRADED
吸引人的;值得拥有（或做）的
You can describe something as attractive when it seems worth having or doing.
例：Co-operation was more than just an attractive option, it was an obligation...合作不仅仅是诱人的选择，它也是一种责任。
例：Smoking is still attractive to many young people who see it as glamorous.吸烟对很多年轻人还是很有诱惑力，他们觉得这让自己显得魅力十足。

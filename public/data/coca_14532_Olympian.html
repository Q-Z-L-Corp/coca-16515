ADJ
强有力的；巨大的；令人惊叹的
Olympian means very powerful, large, or impressive.
例：Getting his book into print has been an Olympian task in itself.将他的书付印本身就是一件浩大的工程。

N-COUNT
奥运会选手；奥运会运动员
An Olympian is a competitor in the Olympic Games.
例：The importance of being an Olympian will vary from athlete to athlete.参加奥运会对每个运动员而言意义不同。

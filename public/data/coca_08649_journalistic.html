ADJ
新闻业的；新闻工作（者）的；新闻记者创作的
Journalistic means relating to journalism, or produced by or typical of a journalist.
例：He began his journalistic career in the early eighties in Australia.他在80年代早期开始在澳大利亚从事新闻工作。
例：...journalistic descriptions of countries she visited.对她到访过的国家的新闻记述

subscriber 
英[səbˈskraɪbə(r)] 美[səbˈskraɪbər]
 
n.用户，订户；<英>（慈善机关等的）定期捐款者；预约者；签署者；
 "

N-COUNT
(杂志或报纸的)订阅人，订购者，订户
A magazine's or a newspaper's subscribers are the people who pay to receive copies of it regularly.
例：I have been a subscriber to Railway Magazine for many years.我订阅《铁路杂志》已有很多年了。

N-COUNT
消费者;用户
Subscribers to a service are the people who pay to receive the service.
例：China has almost 15 million subscribers to satellite and cable television.中国有将近 1,500 万卫星电视和有线电视用户。

N-COUNT
(慈善机构、活动的)定期捐款者，定期捐助者;(组织的)定期缴费者，会员
The subscribers to a charity, campaign, or organization are the people who support it by sending money regularly to it.

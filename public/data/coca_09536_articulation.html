articulation 
英[ɑ:ˌtɪkjuˈleɪʃn] 美[ɑrˌtɪkjuˈleɪʃn]
 
n.清晰度，咬合，关节；发音；接合；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
清楚的咬字（或发音）
Articulation is the action of producing a sound or word clearly, in speech or music.
例：...a singer able to sustain a full tone and clear articulation over extremely long periods.可以很长时间保持声音洪亮并且吐字清楚的歌手

N-UNCOUNT
（思想或感情的）表达
The articulation of an idea or feeling is the expression of it, especially in words.
例：This was seen as a way of restricting women's articulation of grievances.这被认为是一种限制妇女发牢骚的方式。

N-UNCOUNT
（不同部分、元素之间的）连接，联系，相互关系
The articulation of a structure or system is the way in which its different parts or elements are connected.
例：Capitalist social formations reflect the interaction, or articulation, of different modes of production.资本主义社会结构反映了不同形式的生产方式之间的互动，或者说相互联系。

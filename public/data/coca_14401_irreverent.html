irreverent 
英[ɪˈrevərənt] 美[ɪˈrɛvərənt]
 
adj.不敬的，无礼的；不虔诚的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不尊敬的;不恭的
If you describe someone as irreverent, you mean that they do not show respect for people or things that are generally respected.
例：She's irreverent, fun and hugely popular...她不惧权威，幽默风趣，很受欢迎。
例：Taylor combined great knowledge with an irreverent attitude to history.泰勒知识渊博，而且并不把历史太当回事。

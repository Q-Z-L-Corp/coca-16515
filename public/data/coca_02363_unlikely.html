adj.未必的，不一定有把握的；不太可能的；不可能发生的；不像是真的；

ADJ-GRADED
不太可能的；未必会发生的
If you say that something is unlikely to happen or unlikely to be true, you believe that it will not happen or that it is not true, although you are not completely sure.
例：A military coup seems unlikely...看起来不太可能会发生军事政变。
例：As with many technological revolutions, you are unlikely to be aware of it...就像诸多的技术革命一样，你可能不太会注意到它。

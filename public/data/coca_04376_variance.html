variance 
英[ˈveəriəns] 美[ˈveriəns]
 
n.变化，变动；不一致；分歧；<数>方差；
 "

PHRASE
（与…）不一致，相矛盾
If one thing is at variance with another, the two things seem to contradict each other.
例：Many of his statements were at variance with the facts...他的说法有很多和事实不符。
例：This idealistic concept is at variance with reality.这种理想主义的观念和现实相矛盾。

N-VAR
差异;不同
The variance between things is the difference between them.
例：...the variances in the stock price.股票价格的变化
例：...total revenue variance.总收入的差异

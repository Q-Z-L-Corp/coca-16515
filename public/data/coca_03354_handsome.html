handsome 
英[ˈhænsəm] 美[ˈhænsəm]
 
adj.英俊的，健美的；美观的；大方的；数量大的 ；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（男子）漂亮的，英俊的，帅气的
A handsome man has an attractive face with regular features.
例：...a tall, dark, handsome sheep farmer.高个子、黑皮肤的英俊养羊人

ADJ-GRADED
（女子）端庄健美的
A handsome woman has an attractive appearance with features that are large and regular rather than small and delicate.
例：...an extremely handsome woman with a beautiful voice.有着甜美嗓音的非常健美的女子

ADJ-GRADED
（金额）相当大的，可观的，优厚的
A handsome sum of money is a large or generous amount.
例：They will make a handsome profit on the property.他们会从这片地产中赚取丰厚的利润。

ADJ-GRADED
（建筑物、庭院等）宽敞而美观的，堂皇的，雄伟的
A place such as a building or garden that is handsome is large and well made with an attractive appearance.
例：...the ports of Dubrovnik and Zadar, with their handsome Renaissance buildings.拥有雄伟的文艺复兴时期建筑的杜布罗夫尼克港和扎达尔港

ADJ
（获胜）比分悬殊的，遥遥领先的
If someone has a handsome win or a handsome victory, they get many more points or votes than their opponent.
例：The opposition won a handsome victory in the election.反对党在竞选中以高票获胜。

aware 
英[əˈweə(r)] 美[əˈwer]
 
adj.意识到的；知道的；觉察到的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
知道的；明白的
If you are aware of something, you know about it.
例：Smokers are well aware of the dangers to their own health...吸烟的人都知道吸烟对自身健康的危害。
例：He should have been aware of what his junior officers were doing...他本应该清楚自己下级们的所作所为。

ADJ-GRADED
注意到的；察觉到的；意识到的
If you are aware of something, you realize that it is present or is happening because you hear it, see it, smell it, or feel it.
例：She was acutely aware of the noise of the city...她对城市噪音很敏感。
例：Jane was suddenly aware that she was digging her nails into her thigh.简突然间意识到她正在用指甲抠大腿。

ADJ-GRADED
警觉的；警惕的
Someone who is aware notices what is happening around them or happening in the place where they live.
例：They are politically very aware.他们政治上非常警觉。

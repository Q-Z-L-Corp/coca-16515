crystalline 
英[ˈkrɪstəlaɪn] 美[ˈkrɪstəlɪn, -ˌlaɪn, -ˌlin]
 
adj.水晶的；似水晶的；结晶质的；清澈的；n.结晶性，结晶度；
 "

ADJ
结晶的;晶状的;含水晶的
A crystalline substance is in the form of crystals or contains crystals.
例：Diamond is the crystalline form of the element carbon.钻石是碳元素的晶体结构。
例：...hard, crystalline rock.坚硬的水晶石

ADJ
清澈的;晶莹的
Crystalline means clear or bright.
例：...a huge plain dotted with crystalline lakes.辽阔的平原上遍布着清澈的湖泊

N-COUNT
（无线电或电视）广播；广播节目；电视节目
A broadcast is a programme, performance, or speech on the radio or on television.
例：In a broadcast on state radio the government also announced that it was willing to resume peace negotiations.在国家电台的广播中，政府也声明愿意重新开始和平谈判。

VERB
（电台或电视台）广播，播出（节目）
To broadcast a programme means to send it out by radio waves, so that it can be heard on the radio or seen on television.
例：The concert will be broadcast live on television and radio...音乐会将通过电视和广播现场直播。
例：CNN also broadcasts in Europe.美国有线新闻网也在欧洲播出节目。

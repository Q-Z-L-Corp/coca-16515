avant-garde 
英[ˌævɒ̃'ɡɑ:d] 美[ˌɑvɑntˈɡɑrd, ˌævɑnt-]
 
n.（艺术的）先锋派，前卫派；先驱，先锋；adj.先锋派的，前卫的；标新立异的；先锋的，先驱的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（艺术、音乐、戏剧、文学）先锋派的，前卫的，实验性的
Avant-garde art, music, theatre, and literature is very modern and experimental.
例：...avant-garde concert music.音乐会演奏的前卫音乐

N-SING
先锋派；前卫派
You can refer to the artists, writers, and musicians who introduce new and very modern ideas as the avant-garde .
例：In Paris he made friends among the avant-garde.他在巴黎结交先锋人士。

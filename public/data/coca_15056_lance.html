lance 
英[lɑ:ns] 美[læns]
 
n.长矛，标枪；长矛骑兵；[医]柳叶刀；矛状器具；vt.用柳叶刀切开；用矛刺穿；投，掷；vi.急速前进；

VERB
切开，割开（疖等）
If a boil on someone's body is lanced, a small cut is made in it so that the liquid inside comes out.
例：It is a painful experience having the boil lanced.把疖子切开是很痛的。

N-COUNT
（旧时骑兵用的）长矛
A lance is a long spear used in former times by soldiers on horseback.
例：...the clang of lances striking armour.长矛撞击盔甲发出的咣咣声

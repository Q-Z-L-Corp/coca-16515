relevance 
英['reləvəns] 美[ˈrɛləvəns]
 
n.相关性，关联；实用性；[计]资料检索能力；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(对…的)重要性，意义，相关性
Something's relevance to a situation or person is its importance or significance in that situation or to that person.
例：Politicians' private lives have no relevance to their public roles...政治家的私生活与他们的公众角色不相关。
例：There are additional publications of special relevance to new graduates.还有对于新毕业生来说特别重要的其他出版物。

glassy 
英[ˈglɑ:si] 美[ˈglæsi]
 
adj.似玻璃的；像玻璃一样光滑的；（眼神）呆滞的；无生气的；adv.（目光）呆滞地，无生气地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
像玻璃一样的；光滑的；光亮透明的
If you describe something as glassy, you mean that it is very smooth and shiny, like glass.
例：The water was glassy.水面清澈明亮。
例：...glassy green pebbles.光滑的绿卵石

ADJ-GRADED
（眼神或表情）呆滞的，无表情的
If you describe someone's eyes or expression as glassy, you mean that they are showing no feeling, emotion, or awareness.
例：He was very still, his eyes glassy...他纹丝不动，目光呆滞。
例：Henry gave Paul a glassy-eyed stare.亨利木然地盯着保罗。

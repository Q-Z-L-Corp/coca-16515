prep 
英[prep] 美[prɛp]
 
vi.进入预备学校；预备，准备；vt.使预备好；n.预备学校；准备；预备功课，家庭作业；大学预科生；adj.预备的；
 "

VERB
准备；预备
If you prep something, you prepare it.
例：After prepping the boat, they sailed it down to Carloforte.备好船后，他们就驶向了南边的卡洛福泰。

VERB
为（患者）做手术准备
If a doctor or nurse preps a patient, they get them ready for surgery or another procedure.
例：I was already prepped for surgery so I went straight into theatre.我已经做好了接受外科手术的准备，于是就直接进入了手术室。

N-UNCOUNT
（英国一些私立学校的）家庭作业
In some British private schools, prep is the name given to school work that children do in the evening after school has finished.
例：Anne struggled to help Peter and Zara with their prep.安妮努力帮助彼得和扎拉完成他们的家庭作业。

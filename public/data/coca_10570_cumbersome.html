cumbersome 
英[ˈkʌmbəsəm] 美[ˈkʌmbərsəm]
 
adj.笨重的；累赘的，难以携带的；缓慢复杂的，冗长的；麻烦的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
大而笨重的;难以携带的
Something that is cumbersome is large and heavy and therefore difficult to carry, wear, or handle.
例：Although the machine looks cumbersome, it is actually easy to use.尽管这台机器看上去很笨重，操作起来却很容易。
例：...muffled up in thick and cumbersome clothing.身上裹着厚重的衣服

ADJ-GRADED
复杂而低效的
A cumbersome system or process is very complicated and inefficient.
例：...an old and cumbersome computer system...过时而且低效的计算机系统
例：The proposed regulations are ill-defined and cumbersome and could be unnecessarily costly.提议的规章制度阐述不清、复杂低效，而且可能会带来不必要的过高花费。

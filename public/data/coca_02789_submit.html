submit 
英[səbˈmɪt] 美[səbˈmɪt]
 
vi.顺从，服从；甘受，忍受；vt.使服从，使顺从；提交，呈送；[法]主张，建议；
 "

VERB
顺从;屈服;投降;被迫接受
If you submit to something, you unwillingly allow something to be done to you, or you do what someone wants, for example because you are not powerful enough to resist.
例：In desperation, Mrs. Jones submitted to an operation on her right knee to relieve the pain...绝望之际，琼斯夫人只好同意在其右膝上做手术以减轻疼痛。
例：If I submitted to their demands, they would not press the allegations.如果那时我答应他们的要求，他们就不会坚持那些指控了。

VERB
提交，递呈(建议、报告或请求)
If you submit a proposal, report, or request to someone, you formally send it to them so that they can consider it or decide about it.
例：They submitted their reports to the Chancellor yesterday...他们昨天向财政大臣递交了报告。
例：Head teachers yesterday submitted a claim for a 9 per cent pay rise.校长们昨天提交了一份要求加薪 9%的声明。

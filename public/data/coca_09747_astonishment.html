astonishment 
英[əˈstɒnɪʃmənt] 美[əˈstɑnɪʃmənt]
 
n.惊讶，惊奇；令人惊讶的事物或人；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
惊讶;惊奇;惊愕
Astonishment is a feeling of great surprise.
例：I spotted a shooting star which, to my astonishment, was bright green in colour...我看见一颗流星，使我大为惊奇的是，它居然是鲜亮的绿色。
例：'What?' Meg asked in astonishment.‘什么？’梅格惊讶地问道。

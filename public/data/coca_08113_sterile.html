sterile

adj.不毛的，贫瘠的；不生育的；无菌的；无效果的；

ADJ
消过毒的;无菌的
Something that is sterile is completely clean and free from germs.
例：He always made sure that any cuts were protected by sterile dressings...他总是坚持要用无菌纱布包扎伤口。
例：Urine is sterile.尿液是无菌的。

ADJ
无生殖能力的;不育的
A person or animal that is sterile is unable to have or produce babies.
例：George was sterile.乔治没有生育能力。
例：...a sterile male.没有生育能力的男子

ADJ-GRADED
无生气的;缺乏新意的
A sterile situation is lacking in energy and new ideas.
例：Too much time has been wasted in sterile debate.在毫无新意的辩论上已经浪费了太多时间。

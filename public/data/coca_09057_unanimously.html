unanimously 
英[jʊ'nænɪməslɪ] 美[jʊˈnænəməslɪ]
 
adv.无异议地，全体一致地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（所有人）一致同意的，全体一致的，无异议的
When a group of people are unanimous, they all agree about something or all vote for the same thing.
例：Editors were unanimous in their condemnation of the proposals...编辑人员一致谴责这些提议。
例：They were unanimous that Chortlesby Manor must be preserved.他们一致认为必须对乔特勒斯比庄园加以保护。

ADJ
（投票、决定、协议等）全体同意的，无异议的，一致通过的
A unanimous vote, decision, or agreement is one in which all the people involved agree.
例：...the unanimous vote for Hungarian membership...投票一致同意匈牙利成为会员国
例：Their decision was unanimous.他们的决定是全体一致通过的。

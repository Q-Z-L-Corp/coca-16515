hypnotize 
英[ˈhɪpnətaɪz] 美[ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪz]
 
vt.对…施催眠术；使着迷，使精神恍惚；
 "

VERB
对…施催眠术;使进入催眠状态
If someone hypnotizes you, they put you into a state in which you seem to be asleep but can still see, hear, or respond to things said to you.
例：A hypnotherapist will hypnotize you and will stop you from smoking...一位催眠治疗师将对你实施催眠，从而帮你戒烟。
例：Surprisingly, the ability to hypnotize yourself can be learnt in a single session.出人意料的是，自我催眠术只要上一次课就可以学会。

VERB
使着迷;使沉醉
If you are hypnotized by someone or something, you are so fascinated by them that you cannot think of anything else.
例：He's hypnotized by that black hair and that white face...他为那乌黑的头发和白皙的面庞所倾倒。
例：Davey sat as if hypnotized by the sound of Nick's voice.戴维坐在那里，好像被尼克的声音迷住了似的。

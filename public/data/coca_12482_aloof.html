ADJ-GRADED
冷漠的；离群的
Someone who is aloof is not very friendly and does not like to spend time with other people.
例：He seemed aloof and detached.他显得冷漠离群。

ADJ-GRADED
置身事外的
If someone stays aloof from something, they do not become involved with it.
例：The Government is keeping aloof from the controversy...政府对争论保持置身事外的态度。
例：I will hold myself aloof from wrong and corruption.我会让自己远离错误和腐败。

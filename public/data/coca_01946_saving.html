saving 
英[ˈseɪvɪŋ] 美[ˈsevɪŋ]
 
n.节约；节俭；储蓄金；挽救；v.拯救( save的现在分词)；收集；保留；避免；prep.除…以外；adj.可取的，保存的；节省的，节约的；补偿的；救助的；
 "

N-COUNT
节省;节约
A saving is a reduction in the amount of time or money that is used or needed.
例：Fill in the form below and you will be making a saving of £6.60 on a one-year subscription.填写以下表格，全年订阅即可为您节省 6.60英镑。
例：...a program of household savings on energy use.家庭节能计划

N-PLURAL
(尤指在银行和购屋互助协会的)储蓄款，存款;积蓄
Your savings are the money that you have saved, especially in a bank or a building society.
例：Her savings were in the Post Office Savings Bank.她的钱存在邮政储蓄银行里。
例：...a savings account.储蓄账户

N-COUNT
监督员;管理员
A warden is a person who is responsible for a particular place or thing, and for making sure that the laws or regulations that relate to it are obeyed.
例：He was a warden at the local parish church...他是当地教堂的管理员。
例：Game wardens were appointed to enforce hunting laws in New Hampshire.狩猎监督官被派到新罕布什尔州去执行狩猎法令。

N-COUNT
监狱长;典狱长
The warden of a prison is the person in charge of it.
例：A new warden took over the prison.一位新的监狱长接管了这所监狱。

 "

N-COUNT
（加油站、停车场、更衣室等的）服务员，侍者
An attendant is someone whose job is to serve or help people in a place such as a petrol station, a car park, or a cloakroom.
例：Tony Williams was working as a car-park attendant in Los Angeles.托尼·威廉斯在洛杉矶的一个停车场当服务生。

ADJ
伴随的；附带的；相关的
You use attendant to describe something that results from a thing already mentioned or that is connected with it.
例：Mr Branson's victory, and all the attendant publicity, were well deserved.布兰森先生的胜利，以及随之而来的关注度，都是理所应当的。
例：...the risks attendant on the exploration of the unknown.探索未知所带来的危险

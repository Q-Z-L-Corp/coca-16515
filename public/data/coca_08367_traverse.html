VERB
横穿;横越;穿过
If someone or something traverses an area of land or water, they go across it.
例：I traversed the narrow pedestrian bridge.我走过狭窄的人行天桥。
例：…a steep-sided valley traversed by streams.溪流横贯、两侧山势陡峭的山谷

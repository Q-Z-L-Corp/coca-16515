obligate 
英['ɒblɪgeɪt] 美[ˈɑblɪˌɡet]
 
vt.使（在法律或道义上）负有责任或义务；对…施以恩惠；adj.有义务的，必要的；
 "

VERB
强迫;迫使
If something obligates you to do a particular thing, it creates a situation where you have to do it.
例：The ruling obligates airlines to release information about their flight delays...这一裁决规定航空公司有义务公布航班晚点信息。
例：Under a separation agreement, he was obligated to pay his ex-wife £50,000 a year for life.根据分居协议，他必须每年向前妻支付5万英镑。

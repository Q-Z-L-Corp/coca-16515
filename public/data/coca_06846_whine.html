whine 
英[waɪn] 美[hwaɪn, waɪn]
 
vi.发出哀叫声；哼哼唧唧地说；发呜呜声；vt.哀诉，诉怨；n.哀鸣；悲嗥声；牢骚，抱怨；
 "

VERB
尖叫;哀号;悲鸣
If something or someone whines, they make a long, high-pitched noise, especially one which sounds sad or unpleasant.
例：He could hear her dog barking and whining in the background...他能听见她的狗在暗处吠叫、哀嚎。
例：The engines whined.发动机呜呜作响。

VERB
（令人讨厌地）抱怨;唠叨
If you say that someone is whining, you mean that they are complaining in an annoying way about something unimportant.
例：They come to me to whine about their troubles.他们到我这儿来不停地唠叨他们的烦恼。
例：...children who whine that they are bored...抱怨说他们觉得很无聊的孩子们

humanity 
英[hju:ˈmænəti] 美[hjuˈmænɪti]
 
n.人类；人性；人道；人文学科；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
人类;（总称）人
All the people in the world can be referred to as humanity .
例：They face charges of committing crimes against humanity.他们被控犯有反人类罪。
例：...a young lawyer full of illusions and love of humanity.满脑子幻想并且博爱的年轻律师

N-UNCOUNT
人性;人的属性
A person's humanity is their state of being a human being, rather than an animal or an object.
例：...a man who's almost lost his humanity in his bitter hatred of his rivals.因痛恨竞争对手而几乎丧失人性的男子

N-UNCOUNT
人道;仁慈;博爱
Humanity is the quality of being kind, thoughtful, and sympathetic towards others.
例：Her speech showed great maturity and humanity.她的演讲思想高度成熟，并且洋溢着博爱精神。

N-PLURAL
（历史、哲学、文学等）人文学科
The humanities are the subjects such as history, philosophy, and literature which are concerned with human ideas and behaviour.
例：...students majoring in the humanities.主修人文学科的学生

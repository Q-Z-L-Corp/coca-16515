N-COUNT
(包裹、货物等上的)标签，单据
A docket is a certificate or ticket which shows the contents of something such as a parcel or cargo, and proves who the goods belong to.
例：The clerk asked me to sign the docket.店员让我在单据上签字。

N-COUNT
备审案件目录表
A docket is a list of cases waiting for trial in a law court.
例：The Court has about 1,400 appeals on its docket.法庭的备审案件约有 1,400 宗。

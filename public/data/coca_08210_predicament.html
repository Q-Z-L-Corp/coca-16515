N-COUNT
困境；艰难的处境；尴尬的境地
If you are in a predicament, you are in an unpleasant situation that is difficult to get out of.
例：Hank explained our predicament...汉克说明了我们的艰难处境。
例：The decision will leave her in a peculiar predicament.这个决定会让她处于尴尬的境地。

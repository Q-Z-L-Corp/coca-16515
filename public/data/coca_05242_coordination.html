coordination 
英[kəʊˌɔ:dɪˈneɪʃn] 美[koʊˌɔrdɪˈneɪʃn]
 
n.协调；和谐；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
协调
Co-ordination means organizing the activities of two or more groups so that they work together efficiently and know what the others are doing.
例：...the lack of co-ordination between the civilian and military authorities.老百姓和军事当局之间缺乏协调
例：...the co-ordination of economic policy.经济政策的协调

N-UNCOUNT
(身体部位的)协调能力
Co-ordination is the ability to use the different parts of your body together efficiently.
例：...clumsiness and lack of co-ordination...身体笨拙而且缺乏协调能力
例：To improve hand-eye co-ordination, practise throwing and catching balls.要想提高手眼协调能力，就练习投球和接球。

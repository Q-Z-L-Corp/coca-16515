adj.渴望的；贪婪的；热心的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
热衷的;着迷的;酷爱的
You use avid to describe someone who is very enthusiastic about something that they do.
例：He misses not having enough books because he's an avid reader.他是个酷爱阅读的人，总觉得书不够读。
例：...an avid collector of art and history.一个热衷收集艺术品和历史文物的收藏家

ADJ-GRADED
渴望的；急切的
If you say that someone is avid for something, you mean that they are very eager to get it.
例：He was intensely eager, indeed avid, for wealth.他嗜财如命。

equate 
英[iˈkweɪt] 美[ɪˈkwet]
 
vt.使相等；相当于；等同；
 "

V-ERG
把…相提并论;与…等同
If you equate one thing with another, or if you say that one thing equates with another, you believe that they are strongly connected.
例：I'm always wary of men wearing suits, as I equate this with power and authority...我向来提防穿西服的男人，因为我认为穿西服就等同于权力和权威。
例：The author doesn't equate liberalism and conservatism...该作者没有将自由主义和保守主义相提并论。

demeaning 
英[dɪˈmi:nɪŋ] 美[]
 
v.使降低身份，使卑下( demean的现在分词 )；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
降低身份的;贬低的;贬损的
Something that is demeaning makes people have less respect for the person who is treated in that way, or who does that thing.
例：...demeaning sexist comments...侮辱性的性别歧视言论
例：Aid, however it is obtained, is demeaning to the recipients.不管救助是如何取得的，对受益人来说都不是很光彩的事。

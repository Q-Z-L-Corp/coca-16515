literary 
英[ˈlɪtərəri] 美[ˈlɪtəreri]
 
adj.文学（上）的；精通文学的；爱好文学的；从事文学研究（或写作）的；
 "

ADJ
文学（上）的
Literary means concerned with or connected with the writing, study, or appreciation of literature.
例：Her literary criticism focuses on the way great literature suggests ideas...她的文学批评集中关注的是伟大的文学作品表达思想的方式。
例：She's the literary editor of the 'Sunday Review'.她是《星期天评论》的文学编辑。

ADJ-GRADED
（词语、表达方式等）书面的，书卷气的
Literary words and expressions are often unusual in some way and are used to create a special effect in a piece of writing such as a poem, speech, or novel.

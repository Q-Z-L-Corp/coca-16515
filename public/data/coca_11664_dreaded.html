dreaded 
英[ˈdredɪd] 美[]
 
adj.令人畏惧的；害怕的；v.害怕，恐惧，担心( dread的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
可怕的;令人畏惧的
Dreaded means terrible and greatly feared.
例：No one knew how to treat this dreaded disease.没人知道如何治疗这种可怕的疾病。

ADJ
令人恼火的;不方便的;不想要的
You can use the dreaded to describe something that you, or a particular group of people, find annoying, inconvenient, or undesirable.
例：She's a victim of the dreaded hay fever...她患上了花粉热这种讨厌的病症。
例：Team orders on the final day were to avoid the dreaded blank at all costs.最后一天，队里的指示就是不惜一切代价实现零的突破。

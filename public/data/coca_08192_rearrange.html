VERB
重整；重新安排；重新排列
If you rearrange things, you change the way in which they are organized or ordered.
例：When she returned, she found Malcolm had rearranged all her furniture...她回家时，发现马尔科姆把她所有的家具都重新摆放了。
例：A waiter was rapidly rearranging tables for the big group.一个服务生正在麻利地为这一大帮人重新布置餐桌。

VERB
重新安排（会议或约会）
If you rearrange a meeting or an appointment, you arrange for it to take place at a different time to that originally intended.
例：You may cancel or rearrange the appointment...你可以取消约会或是重新安排约会时间。
例：The meeting on 31 October is rearranged for 30 January.10月31日的会议重新安排到了1月30日。

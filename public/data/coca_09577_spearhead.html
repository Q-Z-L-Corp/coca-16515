spearhead 
英[ˈspɪəhed] 美[ˈspɪrhed]
 
n.先锋，前锋；先头部队；矛头；枪尖；vt.当…的先锋；带头；
 "

VERB
领导;倡导;当…的先锋
If someone spearheads a campaign or an attack, they lead it.
例：...Esther Rantzen, who is spearheading a national campaign against bullying...埃丝特·兰森，一场全国性的反抗暴力运动的领导者
例：Helicopters can to some extent take the place of tanks by spearheading the airborne attack.直升机可以担任空中进攻的前锋，从而在某种程度上取代坦克的地位。

N-COUNT
前锋;先头部队
The spearhead of a campaign is the person or group that leads it.
例：The marines went ashore as a spearhead this morning to capture key targets.今天早晨，海军陆战队作为先头部队率先登陆以占领主要目标。

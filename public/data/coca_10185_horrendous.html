horrendous 
英[hɒˈrendəs] 美[hɔˈrendəs]
 
adj.可怕的，令人吃惊的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
骇人的;令人震惊的
Something that is horrendous is very unpleasant or shocking.
例：He described it as the most horrendous experience of his life...他形容这是自己一生中最可怕的经历。
例：The violence used was horrendous.所使用的暴力骇人听闻。

ADJ-GRADED
（数量、程度等）大得惊人的，极其过分的
Some people use horrendous to describe something that is so big or great that they find it extremely unpleasant.
例：Nor do their debts looks so horrendous when related to the value of their fixed assets.如果和他们的固定资产值相比，他们的债务看起来也并不算惊人。
例：...the usually horrendous traffic jams.常常糟糕透顶的交通堵塞

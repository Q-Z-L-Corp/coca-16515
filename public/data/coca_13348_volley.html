volley 
英[ˈvɒli] 美[ˈvɑli]
 
n.（箭、子弹、抢、炮、等）群射，齐射；（质问，咒骂等的）迸发；vi.群射，齐射；迸发；截击；vt.进行群射；齐鸣；vt.& vi.（网球运动中）截击；
 "

VERB
截踢,截击(空中球)
In sport, if someone volleys the ball, they hit it before it touches the ground.
例：He volleyed the ball spectacularly into the far corner of the net...他一脚漂亮的凌空抽射，把球踢进了球门的远角。
例：McNeil volleyed more effectively in the second set.在第二局中，麦克尼尔对球的截击更加成功。

N-COUNT
（子弹的）群射，齐发
A volley of gunfire is a lot of bullets that travel through the air at the same time.
例：It's still not known how many died in the volleys of gunfire...现在还不知道有多少人在枪林弹雨中身亡。
例：Three mounted officers rode into the field after the volley.枪炮一起开火后，3名军官骑着马冲进战场。

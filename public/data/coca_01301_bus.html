N-COUNT
公共汽车；巴士；大客车
A bus is a large motor vehicle which carries passengers from one place to another. Buses drive along particular routes, and you have to pay to travel in them.
例：He missed his last bus home...他错过了回家的末班车。
例：They had to travel everywhere by bus.他们只得乘公共汽车出行。

V-ERG
用大客车运送；乘坐公共汽车
When someone is bussed to a particular place or when they bus there, they travel there on a bus.
例：On May Day hundreds of thousands used to be bussed in to parade through East Berlin...过去在五一劳动节这天，数十万人常常乘坐大巴到东柏林游行。
例：To get our Colombian visas we bussed back to Medellin...为了拿到前往哥伦比亚的签证，我们乘大巴返回麦德林。

VERB
用校车送
In some parts of the United States, when children are bused to school, they are transported by bus to a school in a different area so that children of different races can be educated together.
例：Many schools were in danger of closing because the children were bused out to other neighborhoods.很多学校面临倒闭的危险，因为学生被校车送到其他社区就读了。

disparate 
英[ˈdɪspərət] 美['dɪspərət]
 
adj.完全不同的；从根本上种类有区分或不同的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
全异的;完全不同的;异类的
Disparate things are clearly different from each other in quality or type.
例：Scientists are trying to pull together disparate ideas in astronomy...科学家们正试图将天文学中的各派观点整合在一起。
例：The nine republics are immensely disparate in size, culture and wealth.这9个共和国在面积、文化和财富上迥然不同。

ADJ-GRADED
由不同元素组成的
A disparate thing is made up of very different elements.
例：...a very disparate nation, with enormous regional differences.一个区域性差异巨大的多元化国度
例：...their disparate coalition of Southern conservatives and liberals.他们由南方保守党和自由党结成的多党派联盟

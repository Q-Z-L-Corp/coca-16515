N-UNCOUNT
傲慢;自大;自恃
If you accuse someone of hubris, you are accusing them of arrogant pride.
例：...a tale of how an honourable man pursuing honourable goals was afflicted with hubris and led his nation towards catastrophe.关于一个追求崇高目标的磊落君子是如何因自高自大而将自己的国家引向灾难的故事

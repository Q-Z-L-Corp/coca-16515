ADJ-GRADED
(表情、神态)调侃的，戏谑的，开玩笑的
A teasing expression or manner shows that the person is not completely serious about what they are saying or doing.
例：'But we're having such fun, aren't we?' he protested with a teasing smile...“可是我们现在玩得很开心，不是吗？”他戏谑地笑着抗议道。
例：Smith was at his most teasing.史密斯完全是在开玩笑。

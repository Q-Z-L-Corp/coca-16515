restricted 
英[rɪˈstrɪktɪd] 美[rɪˈstrɪktɪd]
 
adj.有受限制的；（土地）对公众不完全开放的；保密的；v.限制( restrict的过去式)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有限的;很少的
Something that is restricted is quite small or limited.
例：...the monotony of a heavily restricted diet...受到严格限制的饮食的单调
例：Plants, like animals, often have restricted habitats.植物和动物一样，生活环境常常有限。

ADJ
仅限…使用的;仅限…所有的
If something is restricted to a particular group, only members of that group have it. If it is restricted to a particular place, it exists only in that place.
例：Discipline problems are by no means restricted to children in families dependent on benefits...绝不只是依靠救济金生活的家庭的孩子才有纪律问题。
例：The problem is not restricted to the southeast.这个问题并不只存在于东南部。

ADJ-GRADED
(地区)不对外开放的
A restricted area is one that only people with special permission can enter.
例：...a highly restricted area close to the old Khodinka airfield.老科丁卡机场附近的一个禁区

ADJ
(文件等)保密的，仅限于内部的
A restricted document is one that only people with special permission can read.
例：The charge was that, in February 1975, he had leaked a restricted document to a journalist.对他的指控是他在1975年2月将一份保密文件泄露给了一位记者。

incidental 
英[ˌɪnsɪˈdentl] 美[ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntl]
 
adj.附属的；易发生的，偶然发生的；伴随发生的；n.附带事件；伴随事件，杂费；非主要的附带项或费用；
 "

ADJ
附带的;伴随的;次要的
If one thing is incidental to another, it is less important than the other thing or is not a major part of it.
例：The playing of music proved to be incidental to the main business of the evening...音乐演奏成了当晚唱主角的商业活动的陪衬。
例：At the bottom of the bill, you will notice various incidental expenses such as faxes.在账单底部,你会发现有传真等各项杂费。

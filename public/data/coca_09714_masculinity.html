masculinity 
英[ˌmæskjuˈlɪnəti] 美[ˌmæskjəˈlinɪti]
 
n.男性；男子气概；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
男子;男性
A man's masculinity is the fact that he is a man.
例：...a project on the link between masculinity and violence.关于男性与暴力之间关系的研究项目

N-UNCOUNT
男子气概;阳刚之气
Masculinity means the qualities, especially sexual qualities, which are considered to be typical of men.
例：The old ideas of masculinity do not work for most men.对于大多数男人来说，关于男子气概的老套观点现在已不再适用了。
例：...being unable to prove his masculinity to society by getting his partner pregnant.不能通过让伴侣怀上孩子的方式向世人证明他的男性气概

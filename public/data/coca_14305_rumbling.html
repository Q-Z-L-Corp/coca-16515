rumbling 
英[ˈrʌmblɪŋ] 美[]
 
n.隆隆声；辘辘声；v.隆隆响( rumble的现在分词)；低沉地说；（车辆）辘辘行驶；
 "

N-COUNT
持续而低沉的声音;隆隆声;辘辘声;咕噜声
A rumbling is a low continuous noise.
例：...the rumbling of an empty stomach...空空如也的肚子发出的咕噜声
例：Our peace was soon shattered by loud rumblings and explosions like cannon fire.我们的平静很快就被炮声之类的巨大轰鸣声和爆炸声给打破了。

N-COUNT
（情况恶化的）迹象，传闻;怨言
Rumblings are signs that a bad situation is developing or that people are becoming annoyed or unhappy.
例：There were rumblings of discontent within the ranks.士兵怨声载道。

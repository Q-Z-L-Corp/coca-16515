counteract 
英[ˌkaʊntərˈækt] 美[ˌkaʊntɚˈækt]

VERB
抵制;抵消;抵抗
To counteract something means to reduce its effect by doing something that produces an opposite effect.
例：My husband has to take several pills to counteract high blood pressure...我丈夫不得不吃几片药来降压。
例：This event will counteract such trends.这一事件将会削弱此类趋势。

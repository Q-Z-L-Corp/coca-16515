ADJ-GRADED
吵闹的;粗野的;惹是生非的
When people are rowdy, they are noisy, rough, and likely to cause trouble.
例：He has complained to the police about rowdy neighbours...他向警方投诉邻居们太吵闹。
例：There were rowdy scenes inside parliament during the debate.在争论的过程中，国会内部一片混乱。

N-PLURAL
粗野的人;吵闹的人

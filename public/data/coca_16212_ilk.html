ilk 
英[ɪlk] 美[ɪlk]
 
adj.相同的；n.家族，同类，种类；
 "

N-SING
…之辈;…之类
If you talk about people or things of the same ilk, you mean people or things of the same type as a person or thing that has been mentioned.
例：He currently terrorises politicians and their ilk on 'Newsnight'...他目前在《新闻之夜》节目中的讲话搞得政客们惶恐不安。
例：Where others of his ilk have battled against drugs, Gabriel's problems have centred on his marriage.加布里埃尔的同辈都在与毒品作斗争,而他的问题却集中在他的婚姻上。

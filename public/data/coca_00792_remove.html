remove 
英[rɪˈmu:v] 美[rɪˈmuv]
 
vt.开除；去除；脱掉，拿下；迁移；vi.迁移，移居；离开；n.距离，差距；移动；
 "

VERB
移动;搬动;取出
If you remove something from a place, you take it away.
例：As soon as the cake is done, remove it from the oven...蛋糕一烤好，立即把它从烤箱中取出。
例：At least three bullets were removed from his wounds...从他的伤口至少取出了3颗子弹。

VERB
脱下(衣服)
If you remove clothing, you take it off.
例：He removed his jacket.他脱下了夹克。

VERB
(用化学品)去除，洗掉(污渍)
If you remove a stain from something, you make the stain disappear by treating it with a chemical or by washing it.
例：This treatment removes the most stubborn stains...这种处理方式会清除最顽固的污渍。
例：Try using lemon juice to remove tobacco stains from your fingers.试试用柠檬汁去除手指上的烟渍。

VERB
免除(职务)
If people remove someone from power or from something such as a committee, they stop them being in power or being a member of the committee.
例：The student senate voted to remove Fuller from office...学生评议会投票免除了富勒的职位。
例：The president could only be removed from power once free elections were organised...总统只有在开始自由选举时才能被免职。

VERB
消除(障碍);取消(限制);解决(问题)
If you remove an obstacle, a restriction, or a problem, you get rid of it.
例：The agreement removes the last serious obstacle to the signing of the arms treaty...这个协议排除了签订武器条约的最后一个严重障碍。
例：Most of her fears had been removed.她的恐惧基本上消除了。

PHRASE
间接地
If you do or experience something at one remove, you do not do it or experience it yourself, but someone else does it or experiences it instead of you.
例：She enjoyed his company and the excitement of feeling herself linked at one remove to London society...她喜欢他的陪伴，也很享受离伦敦社交圈仅仅一步之遥的兴奋感觉。
例：She can know the world only at several removes.她只能间接地认知这个世界。

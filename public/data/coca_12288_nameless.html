nameless 
英[ˈneɪmləs] 美[ˈnemlɪs]
 
adj.不知名的；无名的；<文>不可名状的；（因听者知道你所指或不想令某人难堪）不会指名道姓；
 "

ADJ
不知其名的;无名的
You describe people or things as nameless when you do not know their name or when they do not have a name.
例：They can have their cases rejected, without reasons being given, by nameless officials.他们的诉求可能会被一些不知姓名的官员们莫名其妙地驳回。

ADJ
不宜说出名字（或名称）的
If you say that someone or something will remain nameless, you mean that you will not mention their name, often because you do not want to embarrass them.
例：A local friend who shall be nameless warned me that I was in for trouble soon.一个不便说出其名的当地朋友警告我，我不久肯定会有麻烦。

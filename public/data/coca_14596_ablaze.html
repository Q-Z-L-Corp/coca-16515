ablaze 
英[əˈbleɪz] 美[əˈblez]
 
adj.着火的；发光的；闪耀的；adv.着火；闪耀；
 "

ADJ
炽燃的；熊熊燃烧的
Something that is ablaze is burning very fiercely.
例：Shops, houses, and vehicles were set ablaze.店铺、房子和车辆全都陷于火海。

ADJ
闪耀的；闪亮的
If a place is ablaze with lights or colours, it is very bright because of them.
例：The chamber was ablaze with light...会议厅里灯火辉煌。
例：In spring, the valleys are ablaze with colour.春天，山谷里春色绚烂。

ADJ
（目光、神色等）激动的，兴奋的，愤怒的
If someone is ablaze, or if their eyes are ablaze, their expression shows that they are feeling a strong emotion, such as great excitement or anger.
例：He was ablaze with enthusiasm...他热情洋溢。
例：Her voice is passionate. Her eyes are ablaze.她声音激昂，目光如火。

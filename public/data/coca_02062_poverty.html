N-UNCOUNT
贫穷；贫困
Poverty is the state of being extremely poor.
例：According to World Bank figures, 41 per cent of Brazilians live in absolute poverty...根据世界银行的统计数字，41%的巴西人生活在赤贫中。
例：Garvey died in loneliness and poverty.加维在孤独和穷困中死去。

N-SING
贫乏；短缺；低劣
You can use poverty to refer to any situation in which there is not enough of something or its quality is poor.
例：Britain has suffered from a poverty of ambition.英国一直缺乏远大理想。
例：...a poverty of ideas.思想贫乏

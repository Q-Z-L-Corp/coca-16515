straighten 
英[ˈstreɪtn] 美[ˈstretn]
 
vt.& vi.变直，把…弄直；整理，整顿；使…改正；使…好转；vi.变好起来；
 "

VERB
把…整理好;使端正
If you straighten something, you make it tidy or put it in its proper position.
例：She sipped her coffee and straightened a picture on the wall.她抿了口咖啡，然后把墙上的一幅画扶正。
例：...tidying, straightening cushions and organising magazines.收拾、摆放靠垫和整理杂志

VERB
挺直，端正(身体)
If you are standing in a relaxed or slightly bent position and then you straighten, you make your back or body straight and upright.
例：The three men straightened and stood waiting.3 个男人挺直身体，站着等候。

V-ERG
(使)变直;(使)变正
If you straighten something, or it straightens, it becomes straight.
例：Straighten both legs until they are fully extended...挺直双腿直至完全伸展开。
例：The road straightened and we were on a plateau.路变直了，我们驶上了高原。

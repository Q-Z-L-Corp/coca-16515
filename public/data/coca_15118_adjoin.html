英[əˈdʒɔɪn] 美[əˈdʒɔɪn]
 
vt.& vi.邻近，毗连；接，贴连；附加，使结合；

VERB
紧挨；邻接；毗连
If one room, place, or object adjoins another, they are next to each other.
例：Fields adjoined the garden and there were no neighbours...田园相连，四下无邻。
例：We waited in an adjoining office.我们在旁边的办公室里等候。

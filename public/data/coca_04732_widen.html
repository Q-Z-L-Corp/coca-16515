widen 
英[ˈwaɪdn] 美[ˈwaɪdn]
 
vt.& vi.放宽，加宽，（使）变宽；
 "

V-ERG
（使）变宽;（把…）加宽
If you widen something or if it widens, it becomes greater in measurement from one side or edge to the other.
例：He had an operation last year to widen a heart artery...去年他接受了一个扩张心脏动脉的手术。
例：The river widens considerably as it begins to turn east.河流转向东流时河道大幅度变宽。

V-ERG
(使)扩大;(使)扩展;(使)增加
If you widen something or if it widens, it becomes greater in range or it affects a larger number of people or things.
例：U.S. prosecutors have widened a securities-fraud investigation...美国检察官扩大了对一起证券欺诈案的调查范围。
例：The search for my brother widened...对我弟弟的搜索范围扩大了。

VERB
(眼睛)睁大
If your eyes widen, they open more.
例：His eyes widened as he spoke the words.他说这番话时眼睛瞪大了。

V-ERG
(使)(差距)拉大
If a difference or gap widens or if something widens it, it becomes greater.
例：Wage differences in the two areas are widening...两个地区的工资差距正在拉大。
例：The US trade deficit widened to $7.59 billion in November.11月美国的贸易赤字扩大到75.9亿美元。

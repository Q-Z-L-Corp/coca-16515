zoom 
英[zu:m] 美[zum]
 
vi.嗡嗡作响；急速上升；n.嗡嗡声；隆隆声；（车辆等）疾驰的声音；变焦；vt.使急速上升；使猛增；
 "

VERB
疾行;快速移动
If you zoom somewhere, you go there very quickly.
例：We zoomed through the gallery...我们飞快地穿过画廊。
例：A police car zoomed by very close to them.一辆警车从他们身边擦身疾驰而过。

VERB
（价格或销量）飙升，急剧增长，猛涨
If prices or sales zoom, they increase greatly in a very short time.
例：The economy shrank and inflation zoomed...经济萎缩，通货膨胀加剧。
例：Profits zoomed from nil in 1981 to about 16 million last year.利润从1981年的零收益飙升至去年的 1,600万。

holding 
英[ˈhəʊldɪŋ] 美[ˈhoʊldɪŋ]
 
n.佃户租种的土地；所有物（如土地、债券、股份等）；私有财产；（因其热情、精力或决心等）阻拦不住某人；v.拿( hold的现在分词)；认为；包含；容纳；
 "

N-COUNT
股份
If you have a holding in a company, you own shares in it.
例：That would increase Olympia & York's holding to 35%...那样就会使奥林匹亚约克公司的股份上升到35%。
例：Sid's amazing wealth comes from holdings in oil, gas, land and property.锡德惊人的财富来自他在石油、天然气和房地产领域持有的股份。

N-COUNT
（自有或租用的）农田，耕地
A holding is an area of farm land which is owned or rented by the person who grows crops on it.

N-PLURAL
（博物馆、图书馆、美术馆等的）收藏品，馆藏
The holdings of a place such as a museum, library, or art gallery are the items such as books or paintings which are kept there.

ADJ
（活动或行动）暂时用作维持现状的
A holding operation or action is a temporary one that is intended to keep a situation under control and to prevent it from becoming worse.
例：A garden is, at best, a holding operation against nature.花园充其量不过是抗拒自然的一时之法。

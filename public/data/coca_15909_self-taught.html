ADJ
自学的;自修的
If you are self-taught, you have learned a skill by yourself rather than being taught it by someone else such as a teacher at school.
例：Paul is self-taught and became interested in photography just four years ago.保罗是自学的，他四年前才开始对摄影产生兴趣。
例：...a self-taught musician.自学成才的音乐家

congested 
英[kənˈdʒestɪd] 美[]
 
adj.拥挤的；堵塞的；v.拥挤(congest的过去式和过去分词)；堵塞；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（道路或区域）交通阻塞的，拥堵的
A congested road or area is extremely crowded and blocked with traffic or people.
例：He first promised two weeks ago to clear Britain's congested roads...他在两周前首次承诺解决英国道路拥堵的问题。
例：Some areas are congested with both cars and people.有些地方挤满了车和人。

ADJ-GRADED
(身体部位)堵塞的，充血的，淤血的
If a part of the body is congested, it is blocked.
例：The arteries in his neck had become fatally congested.他颈动脉栓塞，危在旦夕。

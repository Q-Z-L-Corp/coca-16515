disastrous 
英[dɪˈzɑ:strəs] 美[dɪˈzæstrəs]
 
adj.灾难性的；损失惨重的；极坏的；悲惨的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
极糟糕的;灾难性的
A disastrous event has extremely bad consequences and effects.
例：...the recent, disastrous earthquake...最近的灾难性地震
例：The effect on coffee prices has been disastrous for the producers.对咖啡价格的影响对生产商而言是灾难性的。

ADJ-GRADED
完全失败的;极糟的
If you describe something as disastrous, you mean that it was very unsuccessful.
例：England's cricketers have had another disastrous day.英格兰板球队又经历了灰色的一天。
例：...their disastrous performance in the general election of 1906.他们在1906大选中的糟糕表现

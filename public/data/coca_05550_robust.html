robust 
英[rəʊˈbʌst] 美[roʊˈbʌst]

ADJ-GRADED
强健的;健壮的;坚固的;结实的
Someone or something that is robust is very strong or healthy.
例：More women than men go to the doctor. Perhaps men are more robust or worry less?...看医生的女性多于男性。也许男性更强壮或者顾虑要少一些？
例：We've always specialised in making very robust, simply designed machinery.我们一直专注于制造极为结实耐用而且设计简单的机器。

ADJ-GRADED
(观点等)强烈的，坚定的，坚决的
Robust views or opinions are strongly held and forcefully expressed.
例：A British Foreign Office minister has made a robust defence of the agreement...一位英国外交大臣坚决维护这个协议。
例：He has the keen eye and robust approach needed.他具备了所需的敏锐的洞察力和坚定的行事态度。

chairman 
英[ˈtʃeəmən] 美[ˈtʃermən]
 
n.（主持会议的）主席；委员长；（委员会的）委员长；（公司等的）董事长；vt.担任…的主席；
 "

N-COUNT
(委员会、组织机构的)主席;(公司)总裁
The chairman of a committee, organization, or company is the head of it.
例：Glyn Ford is chairman of the Committee which produced the report...格林·福特是提交这份报告的委员会的主席。
例：I had done business with the company's chairman.我和那家公司的总裁有过生意往来。

N-COUNT; N-VOC
(会议、辩论的)主持人，主席
The chairman of a meeting or debate is the person in charge, who decides when each person is allowed to speak.
例：The chairman declared the meeting open...大会主席宣布会议开幕。
例：I hear you, Mr. Chairman.我明白，主席先生！

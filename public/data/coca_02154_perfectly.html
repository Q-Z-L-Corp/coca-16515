ADV
完全地，十足地（尤用于所说的话可能会受质疑时）
You can use perfectly to emphasize an adjective or adverb, especially when you think the person you are talking to might doubt what you are saying.
例：There's no reason why you can't have a perfectly normal child...你没有理由不能拥有一个完全正常的孩子。
例：They made it perfectly clear that it was pointless to go on...他们非常明确地表示继续下去毫无意义。

ADV-GRADED
完美地；十全十美地
If something is done perfectly, it is done so well that it could not possibly be done better.
例：This adaptation perfectly captures the spirit of Kurt Vonnegut's novel...这次改编非常好地抓住了库尔特·冯内古特小说的精髓。
例：The system worked perfectly.该系统运行完美。

ADV
绝对地；完全地
If you describe something as perfectly good or acceptable, you are emphasizing that there is no reason to use or get something else, although other people may disagree.
例：You can buy perfectly good instruments for a lot less.你可以少花一大笔钱买到顶好的仪器。

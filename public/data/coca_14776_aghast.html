adj.惊呆的；吓呆的；被…吓呆；被吓得目瞪口呆；

ADJ-GRADED
吃惊的;惊恐的
If you are aghast, you are filled with horror and surprise.
例：She watched aghast as his life flowed away...看着他的生命逐渐消逝，她惊骇万分。
例：His colleagues were aghast at the sackings...他的同事们对于这些解雇感到很吃惊。

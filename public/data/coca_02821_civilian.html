N-COUNT
平民;老百姓
In a military situation, a civilian is anyone who is not a member of the armed forces.
例：The safety of civilians caught up in the fighting must be guaranteed.必须保证陷入战斗中的平民的人身安全。

ADJ
民用的;平民的;非军用的
In a military situation, civilian is used to describe people or things that are not military.
例：...the country's civilian population.该国的平民人口
例：...civilian casualties.平民伤亡

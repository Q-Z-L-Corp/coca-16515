（哲学）博士学位
A PhD is a degree awarded to people who have done advanced research into a particular subject. PhD is an abbreviation for (缩略=) 'Doctor of Philosophy'.
例：He is more highly educated, with a PhD in Chemistry.他受过更高的教育，拥有化学博士学位。
例：...an unpublished PhD thesis.未发表的博士论文

（用于人名后）…博士
PhD is written after someone's name to indicate that they have a PhD.
例：...R.D. Combes, PhD.R.D.库姆斯博士

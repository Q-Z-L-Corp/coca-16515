idealistic 
英[ˌaɪdiəˈlɪstɪk] 美[aɪˌdiəˈlɪstɪk]
 
adj.理想主义的；空想家的；唯心主义的；唯心论的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
理想主义(者)的;空想的
If you describe someone as idealistic, you mean that they have ideals, and base their behaviour on these ideals, even though this may be impractical.
例：Idealistic young people died for the cause...理想主义青年为这项事业而献身。
例：Older mothers tend to be too idealistic about the pleasures of motherhood.较年长的母亲往往对身为人母的乐趣过于理想化。

dwell 
英[dwel] 美[dwɛl]
 
vi.居住；存在于；细想某事；
 "

VERB
沉湎于,唠叨,赘述(尤指令人不快的事)
If you dwell on something, especially something unpleasant, you think, speak, or write about it a lot or for quite a long time.
例：I'd rather not dwell on the past.我不想再沉湎于过去了。

VERB
居住;栖身
If you dwell somewhere, you live there.
例：They are concerned for the fate of the forest and the Indians who dwell in it...他们为这片森林及居住于其中的印第安人的命运而担心。
例：Shiva is a dark god; he dwells in the mountains and deserts.湿婆神匿影藏形于深山和沙漠之中。

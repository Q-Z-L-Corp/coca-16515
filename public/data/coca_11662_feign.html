feign 
英[feɪn] 美[fen]
 
vt.假装，伪装；捏造（借口、理由等）；装作；创造或虚构；vi.假装；装作；作假；佯作；
 "

VERB
装作;假装;冒充
If someone feigns a particular feeling, attitude, or physical condition, they try to make other people think that they have it or are experiencing it, although this is not true.
例：One morning, I didn't want to go to school, and decided to feign illness...有天早晨我不想上学，于是决定装病。
例：'Giles phoned this morning,' Mirella said with feigned indifference.“贾尔斯今早来电话了，”米雷拉装作若无其事地说。

blooming 
英[ˈblu:mɪŋ] 美[ˈblumɪŋ]
 
adj.盛开的；妙龄的；<俚>十足的、非常的；开坯；
 "

ADJ
（尤用于生气时表示强调）太，很，真是
Blooming is used by some people to emphasize what they are saying, especially when they are annoyed.
例：It's a blooming nuisance because it frightens my dog to death.它真让人讨厌，快把我的狗吓死了。

ADJ
容光焕发的；神采奕奕的；精力旺盛的
Someone who is blooming looks attractively healthy and full of energy.
例：If they were blooming with confidence they wouldn't need me...他们要是信心百倍的话，也就不会叫我了。
例：She's in blooming health.她身强体健。

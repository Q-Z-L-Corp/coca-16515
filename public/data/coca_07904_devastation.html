N-UNCOUNT
(大面积的严重)毁坏，破坏
Devastation is severe and widespread destruction or damage.
例：A huge bomb blast brought chaos and devastation to the centre of Belfast yesterday.昨天一颗威力巨大的炸弹在贝尔法斯特市中心爆炸，引起一片混乱并造成严重破坏。

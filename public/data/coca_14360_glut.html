 "

N-COUNT
供过于求；供应过剩
If there is a glut of something, there is so much of it that it cannot all be sold or used.
例：There's a glut of agricultural products in Western Europe.西欧的农产品供过于求。
例：...a world oil glut.全球石油供应过剩

VERB
充斥；使供应过剩
If a market is glutted with something, there is a glut of that thing.
例：The region is glutted with hospitals...该地区的医院数量已经饱和。
例：Soldiers returning from the war had glutted the job market.从战场返乡的士兵挤爆了劳动力市场。

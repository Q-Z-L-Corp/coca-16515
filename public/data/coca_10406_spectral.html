ADJ
鬼怪(似)的;幽灵(似)的
If you describe someone or something as spectral, you mean that they look like a ghost.
例：She is compelling, spectral, fascinating, an unforgettably unique performer.她极具吸引力，清幽如鬼魅，令人着迷，令人难忘，是个独具特色的演员。
例：...the spectral quality of the light.灯光迷离

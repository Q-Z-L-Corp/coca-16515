blessed 
英['blesɪd] 美['blesɪd]
 
adj.神圣的；有福的； 无忧无虑的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
拥有…的；享有…的
If someone is blessed with a particular good quality or skill, they have that good quality or skill.
例：Both are blessed with uncommon ability to fix things.两人在修理东西上都有一手绝活。
例：...the son of a doctor and well blessed with money.非常富有的医生家的公子

ADJ
令人快乐的；可喜的；美好的
You use blessed to describe something that you think is wonderful, and that you are grateful for or relieved about.
例：The birth of a live healthy baby is a truly blessed event...一个健康活泼的婴儿的出生真是一件可喜的事情。
例：Rainy weather brings blessed relief to hay fever victims.雨天会给枯草热患者带来欣喜，减轻他们的痛苦。

ADJ
（强调生气）该死的，倒霉的，讨厌的
Some people use blessed to emphasize that they are annoyed about something.
例：No-one knows a blessed thing.竟然没有一个人了解点儿情况。

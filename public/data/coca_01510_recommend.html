recommend 
英[ˌrekəˈmend] 美[ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd]
 
vt.推荐；劝告；使显得吸引人；托付；vi.推荐；建议；
 "

VERB
推荐;介绍
If someone recommends a person or thing to you, they suggest that you would find that person or thing good or useful.
例：I have just spent a holiday there and would recommend it to anyone...我刚刚在那里度过假，很想把它推荐给每一个人。
例：'You're a good worker, boy,' he told him. 'I'll recommend you for a promotion.'...“你是个好员工，孩子，”他告诉他，“我将推荐你升职。”

VERB
建议;劝说（做…)
If you recommend that something is done, you suggest that it should be done.
例：The judge recommended that he serve 20 years in prison...法官建议判处他20年监禁。
例：We strongly recommend reporting the incident to the police...我们强烈建议报警。

VERB
使受欢迎;使有优势
If something or someone has a particular quality to recommend them, that quality makes them attractive or gives them an advantage over similar things or people.
例：La Noblesse restaurant has much to recommend it...“贵族”餐馆有很多值得称道之处。
例：He had little but his enthusiasm to recommend him...他拥有的优势只有他的热情。

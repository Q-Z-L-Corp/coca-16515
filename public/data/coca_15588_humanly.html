humanly 
英[ˈhju:mənli] 美[ˈhjumənli]
 
adv.在人力所及的范围，依靠人力；
 "

ADV-GRADED
对人而言地;与人相关地
Humanly means relating to human beings.
例：A mother is not allowed to be humanly flawed; she has to be perfect.作为母亲不容许有常人的缺点，她必须十全十美。

PHRASE
人力能及的;尽最大努力的
If something is humanly possible, it is possible for people to do it.
例：She has gained a reputation for creating books as perfect as is humanly possible...她因在著书上追求极致之美而享有盛名。
例：They had done everything humanly possible for their son.他们已经为儿子竭尽了全力。

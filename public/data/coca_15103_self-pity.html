self-pity 
英[ˈselfˈpɪti:] 美[ˈsɛlfˈpɪti]
 
n.自怜；自哀；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指不必要或过度夸张的)自怜，自哀
Self-pity is a feeling of unhappiness that you have about yourself and your problems, especially when this is unnecessary or greatly exaggerated.
例：I was unable to shake off my self-pity...我无法摆脱自怜自哀的情绪。
例：Throughout, he showed no trace of self-pity.他从始至终都没有表现出一丝的自怜。

profess 
英[prəˈfes] 美[prəˈfɛs, pro-]
 
vt.声称；宣称；公开表明；信奉；
 "

VERB
自称；伪称；谎称；妄称
If you profess to do or have something, you claim that you do it or have it, often when you do not.
例：She professed to hate her nickname...她自称痛恨自己的外号。
例：Why do organisations profess that they care?...为什么机构都谎称自己对此很关心？

VERB
表达，表明（感情、观点、信仰等）
If you profess a feeling, opinion, or belief, you express it.
例：He professed to be content with the arrangement...他对这个安排表示满意。
例：Bacher professed himself pleased with the Indian tour.巴奇尔表示对印度之行很满意。

relapse 
英[rɪˈlæps] 美[rɪˈlæps]
 
vi.（病）再发，复发；故态复萌；再度堕落；再陷邪道；n.[医]再发，旧病复发；复旧，故态复萌；退步；堕落；
 "

VERB
故态复萌;重新陷入;倒退
If you say that someone relapses into a way of behaving that is undesirable, you mean that they start to behave in that way again.
例：'I wish I did,' said Phil Jordan, relapsing into his usual gloom...“我要是那样做了就好了，”菲尔·乔丹说道，又像往常一样消沉起来。
例：It is by no means clear that the financial markets will not relapse into panic.现在还不能确定金融市场不会重新陷入恐慌。

VERB
旧病复发;再次发病
If a sick person relapses, their health suddenly gets worse after it had been improving.
例：In 90 per cent of cases the patient will relapse within six months.90%的病人在6个月内会再次发病。

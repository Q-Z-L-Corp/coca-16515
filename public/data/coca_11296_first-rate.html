ADJ
一流的;极好的;高级的
If you say that something or someone is first-rate, you mean that they are extremely good and of the highest quality.
例：People who used his service knew they were dealing with a first-rate professional.雇请过他的人都知道，和他们打交道的是一流的专业人员。

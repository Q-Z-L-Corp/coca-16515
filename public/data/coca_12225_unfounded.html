ADJ
无事实根据的；没有理由的
If you describe a rumour, belief, or feeling as unfounded, you mean that it is wrong and is not based on facts or evidence.
例：There were unfounded rumours of alcohol abuse...一些毫无根据的酗酒谣言在流传。
例：The allegations were totally unfounded...这些指控完全没有事实根据。

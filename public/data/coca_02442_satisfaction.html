satisfaction 
英[ˌsætɪsˈfækʃn] 美[ˌsætɪsˈfækʃən]
 
n.满足，满意，舒服；妥善处理；（债务的）清偿；（伤害的）赔偿；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
满意;满足;称心
Satisfaction is the pleasure that you feel when you do something or get something that you wanted or needed to do or get.
例：She felt a small glow of satisfaction...她有了一丝满足感。
例：Both sides expressed satisfaction with the progress so far...双方对目前的进展都表示满意。

N-UNCOUNT
赔偿;补偿
If you get satisfaction from someone, you get money or an apology from them because you have been treated badly.
例：If you can't get any satisfaction, complain to the park owner.如果你得不到任何赔偿，向园区所有者投诉。

PHRASE
令…满意
If you do something to someone's satisfaction, they are happy with the way that you have done it.
例：She never could seem to do anything right or to his satisfaction...她似乎从没能做过一件正确或令他满意的事。
例：It is hard to see how the issue can be resolved to everyone's satisfaction.很难想象这个问题如何解决才能让每个人都满意。

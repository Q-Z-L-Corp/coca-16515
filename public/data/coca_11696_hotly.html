hotly 
英[ˈhɒtli] 美[ˈhɑtli]
 
adv.大发雷霆地；猛烈地；紧迫地；热心地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
激烈地;愤怒地;强烈地
If people discuss, argue, or say something hotly, they speak in a lively or angry way, because they feel strongly.
例：'You're a fool, then,' she said hotly...“那你就是个笨蛋，”她气冲冲地说。
例：The bank hotly denies any wrongdoing.银行断然否认有任何违规行为。

ADV-GRADED
紧追不放地;穷追不舍地
If you are being hotly pursued, someone is trying hard to catch you and is close behind you.
例：He'd snuck out of America hotly pursued by the CIA.在中央情报局的穷追不舍下，他已偷偷溜出了美国。

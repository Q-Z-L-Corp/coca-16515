VERB
适合；适宜
If something befits a person or thing, it is suitable or appropriate for them.
例：They offered him a post befitting his seniority and experience...他们给他提供了一个同他资历相当的职位。
例：He writes beautifully, as befits a poet.他文笔优美，适合当诗人。

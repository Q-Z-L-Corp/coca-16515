ADJ-GRADED
热情洋溢的;兴高采烈的;喜气洋洋的
If you are exuberant, you are full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness.
例：...an exuberant young girl who decided to become a screen actress.决定成为一名电影演员的热情洋溢的年轻女孩

revive 
英[rɪˈvaɪv] 美[rɪˈvaɪv]
 
vt.使复活，使恢复；使振奋，复原；使再生，使重新流行；唤醒，唤起；vi.复苏，恢复；振作，恢复；再生，重新流行；再生效力；
 "

V-ERG
(使)复苏;(使)复兴;(使)恢复;(使)再次流行
When something such as the economy, a business, a trend, or a feeling is revived or when it revives, it becomes active, popular, or successful again.
例：...an attempt to revive the British economy...试图复兴英国经济的努力
例：His trial revived memories of French suffering during the war...他的受审重新勾起了在战争中受苦受难的法国人民的回忆。

VERB
重新上演(戏剧、歌剧或芭蕾等)
When someone revives a play, opera, or ballet, they present a new production of it.
例：The Gaiety is reviving John B. Kean's comedy 'The Man from Clare'.盖伊提剧院正在重新上演约翰·B. 基恩的喜剧《来自克莱尔的人》。

V-ERG
(使)苏醒；(使)清醒
If you manage to revive someone who has fainted or if they revive, they become conscious again.
例：She and a neighbour tried in vain to revive him...她和邻居想尽一切办法都没能让他苏醒过来。
例：With a glazed stare she revived for one last instant.她最后清醒了一会儿，眼神呆滞。

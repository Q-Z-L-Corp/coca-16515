宝石
A gem is a jewel or stone that is used in jewellery.
例：...a gold mask inset with emeralds and other gems.一张镶有翡翠和其他宝石的金面具

N-COUNT
难能可贵的人（或物）；令人非常惬意的人（或事物）；精品
If you describe something or someone as a gem, you mean that they are especially pleasing, good, or helpful.
例：...a gem of a hotel, Castel Clara...卡斯特尔·克拉拉酒店，酒店中的明珠
例：Miss Famous, as she was called, was a gem.“知名小姐”人如其名，是个尤物。

V-ERG
（使）重新开始;（使）重新启动
If you restart something that has been interrupted or stopped, or if it restarts, it starts to happen or function again.
例：The commissioners agreed to restart talks as soon as possible...委员们同意尽快重新开始会谈。
例：The race was restarted after a break of around 20 minutes...在中断了大约20分钟后，比赛又重新开始了。

ADJ-GRADED
极好的;非常棒的;了不起的
If you describe something or someone as terrific, you are very pleased with them or very impressed by them.
例：What a terrific idea!...多么好的主意！
例：Everybody there was having a terrific time...在场的每个人都玩得开心极了。

ADJ-GRADED
很多的;很大的;很强的
Terrific means very great in amount, degree, or intensity.
例：He did a terrific amount of fundraising...他做了大量的筹款工作。
例：All of a sudden there was a terrific bang and a flash of smoke.突然传来惊天动地一声响，还冒出一股烟。

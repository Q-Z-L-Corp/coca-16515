incoming 
英[ˈɪnkʌmɪŋ] 美[ˈɪnˌkʌmɪŋ]
 
adj.进来的，回来的；即将就任的；增殖的；（居民等）移来的；n.进来，到来；收入；
 "

ADJ
(短信、电话)发过来的,打进来的
An incoming message or phone call is one that you receive.
例：We keep a tape of incoming calls.我们对来电进行录音。

ADJ
(飞机、乘客)即将到达的
An incoming plane or passenger is one that is arriving at a place.
例：The airport was closed for incoming flights.机场对进港航班关闭了。

ADJ
(官员、政府)新当选的，新上任的，新一任的
An incoming official or government is one that has just been appointed or elected.
例：...the problems confronting the incoming government.新一届政府面临的问题

ADJ
(潮汐、波浪)涌向岸边的
An incoming tide or wave is coming towards the shore.

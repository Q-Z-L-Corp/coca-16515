transfer 
英[trænsˈfɜ:(r)] 美[trænsˈfɚ]
 
vt.使转移；使调动；转让（权利等）；让与；vi.转让；转学；转乘；转会（尤指职业足球队）；n.转移；调动；换乘；（运动员）转会；
 "

V-ERG
(使)转移;(使)迁移
If you transfer something or someone from one place to another, or they transfer from one place to another, they go from the first place to the second.
例：Remove the wafers with a spoon and transfer them to a plate...用勺子抄起薄饼，把它们转盛到盘子里。
例：He was transferred from Weston Hospital to Frenchay…他从韦斯顿医院转院到了法兰查。

V-ERG
(使)转移，传播，移交
If something is transferred, or transfers ,from one person or group of people to another, the second person or group gets it instead of the first.
例：I realized she'd transferred all her love from me to you...我意识到她已把所有的爱从我身上转移到了你身上。
例：The chances of the disease being transferred to humans is extremely remote…该疾病传播到人类身上的几率微乎其微。

N-VAR
(技术)转让
Technology transfer is the process or act by which a country or organization which has developed new technology enables another country or organization to use the technology.
例：The Philippines needs capital and technology transfer...菲律宾需要资本与技术转让。
例：There will be a transfer of technology from this country to Mongolia.这个国家将会向蒙古国进行技术转让。

V-ERG
(使)(运动员)转会
In professional sports, if a player transfers or is transferred from one club to another, they leave one club and begin playing for another.
例：...Nick Barmby who transferred from Spurs to Middlesbrough...从热刺队转会到米德尔斯伯勒队的尼克·巴尔姆比
例：He was transferred from Crystal Palace to Arsenal for £2.5 million.他以 250 万英镑的身价从水晶宫队转入阿森纳队。

V-ERG
（使）调动;（使）换岗
If you are transferred, or if you transfer ,to a different job or place, you move to a different job or start working in a different place.
例：I was transferred to the book department...我被调到图书部。
例：I suspect that she is going to be transferred...我猜想她要被调走了。

VERB
转存;转录
When information is transferred onto a different medium, it is copied from one medium to another.
例：Such information is easily transferred onto microfilm.这种信息很容易转存到缩微胶卷上。
例：...systems to create film-quality computer effects and then transfer them to film.创造胶片级电脑画质并将之转录到胶片的系统

VERB
转让，让与(财产、土地)
When property or land is transferred, it stops being owned by one person or institution and becomes owned by another.
例：He has already transferred ownership of most of the works to a British foundation...他已把大部分作品的所有权转让给一家英国基金会。
例：Certain kinds of property are transferred automatically at death.死亡时某些财产会自动转让。

V-ERG
(使)转乘;(使)换乘
If you transfer or are transferred when you are on a journey, you change from one vehicle to another.
例：He likes to transfer from the bus to the Blue Line at 103rd Street in Watts...他喜欢在华兹区第 103 大街下公交车转乘地铁蓝线。
例：1,654 passengers were transferred at sea to a Norwegian cruise ship.1,654 名乘客在海上转搭了一艘挪威游轮。

N-COUNT
转印纸(纸上面印有图案，可通过加热或压力转印至其他材料上)
Transfers are pieces of paper with a design on one side. The design can be transferred by heat or pressure onto material, paper, or china for decoration.
例：...gold letter transfers.金字转印纸

N-COUNT
换乘票;转车票;通票
A transfer is a ticket that you get when you leave a bus or train that allows you to go on a different bus or train without paying again.

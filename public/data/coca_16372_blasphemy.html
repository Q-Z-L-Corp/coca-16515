英[ˈblæsfəmi] 美['blæsfəmɪ]
 
n.对上帝的亵渎，亵渎的言词[行为]；侮慢的言词（或行为）；

N-VAR
亵渎上帝（或宗教）的言行
You can describe something that shows disrespect for God or a religion as blasphemy .
例：He was found guilty of blasphemy and sentenced to three years in jail...他被判犯有亵渎神明罪，刑期3年。
例：The MP described the killings as a blasphemy before God.这位下院议员称这些谋杀是在上帝面前的公然亵渎。

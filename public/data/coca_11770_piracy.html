N-UNCOUNT
海盗行为；海上劫掠
Piracy is robbery at sea carried out by pirates.
例：Seven of the fishermen have been formally charged with piracy.其中7名渔夫因海盗行为遭正式起诉。

N-UNCOUNT
盗版行为；侵犯版权；非法翻印
You can refer to the illegal copying of things such as video tapes and computer programs as piracy .
例：...protection against piracy of books and films.对书籍和电影免遭盗版的保护

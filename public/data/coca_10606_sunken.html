sunken 
英[ˈsʌŋkən] 美[ˈsʌŋkən]
 
adj.沉没的；凹陷的，下陷的；低于周围平面的；水底的，埋着的；v.陷入，下沉；沉没；
 "

ADJ
沉没的;沉入水底的
Sunken ships have sunk to the bottom of a sea, ocean, or lake.
例：The sunken sailing-boat was a glimmer of white on the bottom...沉没的帆船在海底闪烁着白光。
例：Try diving for sunken treasure.试着潜入水下找寻沉没的财宝。

ADJ
(花园、公路等)低于周围地面的，低洼处的
Sunken gardens, roads, or other features are below the level of their surrounding area.
例：Steps lead down to the sunken garden...台阶通向低处的花园。
例：The room was dominated by a sunken bath.嵌入地面的浴缸占了房间的大半空间。

ADJ
(眼睛、面颊等)凹陷的，深陷的
Sunken eyes, cheeks, or other parts of the body curve inwards and make you look thin and unwell.
例：Her eyes were sunken and black-ringed.她眼圈发黑，眼窝深陷。
例：...an elderly man with sunken cheeks.脸颊凹陷的老人

ADJ-GRADED
不够格的;未达到应有水准的
If you find something or someone wanting, they are not of as high a standard as you think they should be.
例：He analysed his game and found it wanting...他分析了他的比赛，发现还有待改进。
例：Eleanor was scrutinized, too, and often found wanting...埃莉诺也被详细审查，结论经常是不够格。

woody 
英[ˈwʊdi] 美[ˈwʊdi]
 
adj.木质的；木本的；似木的；多木林的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(植物)木本的，木质的
Woody plants have very hard stems.
例：Care must be taken when trimming around woody plants like shrubs and trees...修剪灌木和乔木这样的木本植物时必须小心。
例：Trim any tough or woody stalks from the asparagus.剪掉芦笋上粗硬或老掉的梗子。

ADJ-GRADED
长满树木的;林木覆盖的
A woody area has a lot of trees in it.
例：...the wet and woody Vosges mountains.湿润葱郁的孚日山脉

ADJ
木头味的；木香的
Something that smells woody smells like wood.
例：This oil has a lovely woody fragrance.这种精油散发着怡人的木香。

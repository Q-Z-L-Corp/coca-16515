comic 
英[ˈkɒmɪk] 美[ˈkɑmɪk]
 
adj.喜剧的；滑稽的，好笑的；连环画的；n.连环漫画；喜剧演员；滑稽人物；滑稽新闻；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
滑稽的;好笑的
If you describe something as comic, you mean that it makes you laugh, and is often intended to make you laugh.
例：The novel is comic and tragic...这部小说令人笑中带泪。
例：Most of these trips had exciting or comic moments.大部分旅行途中都有激动人心或逗人开怀的时刻。

ADJ
喜剧的
Comic is used to describe comedy as a form of entertainment, and the actors and entertainers who perform it.
例：Grodin is a fine comic actor.格罗丁是一位优秀的喜剧演员。
例：...a comic opera.喜歌剧

N-COUNT
喜剧演员
A comic is an entertainer who tells jokes in order to make people laugh.

N-COUNT
连环画杂志;漫画书
A comic is a magazine that contains stories told in pictures.
例：Joe loved to read 'Superman' comics.乔喜欢看《超人》的漫画书。

N-PLURAL
(报纸上的)连环漫画栏
The comics is the part of a newspaper that contains the comic strips.

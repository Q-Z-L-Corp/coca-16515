musical 
英[ˈmju:zɪkl] 美[ˈmjuzɪkl]
 
adj.音乐的，用于音乐的；喜爱[精通]音乐的；音调优美的；悦耳的；n.（社交性的）音乐会；[影视]歌舞片；音乐（喜）剧 = musical comedy；
 "

ADJ
音乐的;与音乐有关的
You use musical to indicate that something is connected with playing or studying music.
例：We have a wealth of musical talent in this region...在这一地区有大量的音乐人才。
例：Stan Getz's musical career spanned five decades...斯坦·盖兹的音乐生涯跨越50个春秋。

N-COUNT
音乐剧;音乐歌舞片
A musical is a play or film that uses singing and dancing in the story.
例：...London's smash hit musical Miss Saigon.在伦敦轰动一时的音乐剧《西贡小姐》

ADJ-GRADED
有音乐天赋的;喜爱音乐的
Someone who is musical has a natural ability and interest in music.
例：I came from a musical family.我出身于音乐世家。

ADJ-GRADED
好听的;悦耳的
Sounds that are musical are light and pleasant to hear.
例：He had a soft, almost musical voice.他声音柔和，如音乐般动听。

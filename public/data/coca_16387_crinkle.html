crinkle

vt.& vi.（使）起皱；n.（布或纸上的）皱纹；
 "

V-ERG
(使)变皱;(使)起皱纹
If something crinkles or if you crinkle it, it becomes slightly creased or folded.
例：He shrugged whimsically, his eyes crinkling behind his glasses...他滑稽地耸了耸肩，眼镜片后面的双眼眯了起来。
例：When she laughs, she crinkles her perfectly-formed nose...她一笑，俊俏的鼻子就会皱起来。

N-COUNT
褶痕;皱纹
Crinkles are small creases or folds.

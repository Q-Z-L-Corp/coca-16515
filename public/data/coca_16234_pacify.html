pacify 
英[ˈpæsɪfaɪ] 美[ˈpæsəˌfaɪ]
 
vt.使（某人）安静；息怒；抚慰；在（有战争的地区、国家等）实现和平；
 "

VERB
安抚；抚慰；使平静
If you pacify someone who is angry, upset, or not pleased, you succeed in making them calm or pleased.
例：Is this a serious step, or is this just something to pacify the critics?...这一措施是来真的？还是仅仅为了安抚批评人士？
例：She shrieked again, refusing to be pacified.她又尖叫了，无法使她平静下来。

VERB
平定；平息；镇压
If the army or the police pacify a group of people, they use force to overcome their resistance or protests.
例：Government forces have found it difficult to pacify the rebels...政府军发现难以平定叛军。
例：They were eventually pacified by officers of the local police.当地警察最终把他们镇压了下去。

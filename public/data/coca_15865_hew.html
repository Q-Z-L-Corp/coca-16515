hew 
英[hju:] 美[hju]
 
vt.（用斧、刀等）砍、劈；砍成；劈出；开辟；vi.<美>坚持；遵守；
 "

VERB
砍;劈
If you hew stone or wood, you cut it, for example with an axe.
例：He felled, peeled and hewed his own timber.他自己砍树、剥皮、劈成木材。

VERB
（在岩石或木材上）砍成，劈出
If something is hewn from stone or wood, it is cut from stone or wood.
例：...the rock from which the lower chambers and subterranean passageways have been hewn.开凿出下闸室和地下通道的岩石
例：...medieval monasteries hewn out of the rockface.在岩壁上凿出的中世纪修道院

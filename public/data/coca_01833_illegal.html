illegal 
英[ɪˈli:gl] 美[ɪˈliɡəl]
 
adj.不合法的，违法的；违反规则的；n.非法移民，非法劳工；间谍；

ADJ
不合法的;非法的;违反规则的
If something is illegal, the law says that it is not allowed.
例：It is illegal to intercept radio messages...拦截无线电报是非法的。
例：Birth control was illegal there until 1978...1978 年以前那里节育是非法的。

ADJ
(移民)非法入境的;(劳工)非法务工的
Illegal immigrants or workers have travelled into a country or are working without official permission.

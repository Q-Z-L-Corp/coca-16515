gently 
英[ˈdʒentli] 美[ˈdʒentli]
 
adv.温柔地；轻轻地；有礼貌地；文雅地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
文静的；温和的；和蔼的
Someone who is gentle is kind, mild, and calm.
例：My son was a quiet and gentle man who liked sports and enjoyed life...我儿子是个安静温和的人，喜欢运动和享受生活。
例：Michael's voice was gentle and consoling.迈克尔的嗓音轻柔而且抚慰人心。

ADJ-GRADED
（动作）轻柔的，徐缓的
Gentle actions or movements are performed in a calm and controlled manner, with little force.
例：...a gentle game of tennis...不激烈的网球比赛
例：His movements were gentle and deliberate.他的动作舒缓而从容。

ADJ-GRADED
（天气）温和的； （尤指风）和煦的，微弱的
If you describe the weather, especially the wind, as gentle, you mean it is pleasant and calm and not harsh or violent.
例：The blustery winds of spring had dropped to a gentle breeze.呼啸的春风已经减弱，成了习习的微风。

ADJ-GRADED
（斜坡或弯道）平缓的，不陡的
A gentle slope or curve is not steep or severe.
例：...gentle, rolling meadows...缓缓起伏的牧场
例：There were two passes over 13,000 feet but the slopes were gentle.有两个超过13,000英尺的山口，但坡度平缓。

ADJ-GRADED
微热的
A gentle heat is a fairly low heat.
例：Cook for 30 minutes over a gentle heat.用文火煮30分钟。

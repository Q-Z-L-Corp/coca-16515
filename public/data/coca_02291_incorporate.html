incorporate 
英[ɪnˈkɔ:pəreɪt] 美[ɪnˈkɔrpəreɪt]
 
vt.组成公司；包含；使混合；使具体化；vi.包含；吸收；合并；混合；
 "

VERB
包含;吸收
If one thing incorporates another thing, it includes the other thing.
例：The new cars will incorporate a number of major improvements.新款汽车会有若干重大改进。

VERB
把…合并;使并入
If someone or something is incorporated into a large group, system, or area, they become a part of it.
例：The agreement would allow the rebels to be incorporated into a new national police force...协议将允许把叛军收编入一支新的国家警备部队。
例：The party vowed to incorporate environmental considerations into all its policies.该党宣誓要把环境因素纳入它所有的政策当中。

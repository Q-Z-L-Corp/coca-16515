skeletal 
英[ˈskelətl] 美[ˈskɛlɪtl]
 
adj.骨骼的，骸骨的；骨瘦如柴的；
 "

ADJ
骨骼的;骸骨的
Skeletal means relating to the bones in your body.
例：...the skeletal remains of seven adults.7 具成人遗骸
例：...the skeletal system.骨骼系统

ADJ-GRADED
骨瘦如柴的;皮包骨头的
A skeletal person is so thin that you can see their bones through their skin.
例：...a hospital filled with skeletal children.住满了骨瘦如柴的儿童的医院

ADJ-GRADED
框架的;最基本的
Something that is skeletal has been reduced to its basic structure.
例：Passenger services can best be described as skeletal.对乘客的服务已经少到不能再少。

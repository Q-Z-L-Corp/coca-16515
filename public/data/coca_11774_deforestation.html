deforestation 
英[ˌdi:ˌfɒrɪˈsteɪʃn] 美[ˌdiˌfɔrɪˈsteɪʃn]
 
n.采伐森林，森林开伐；
 "

VERB
毁掉…的森林;砍伐…的林木
If an area is deforested, all the trees there are cut down or destroyed.
例：400,000 square kilometres of the Amazon basin have already been deforested.亚马孙河流域已有40万平方公里的森林遭到滥伐。

N-COUNT
先决条件；前提；必要条件
If one thing is a prerequisite for another, it must happen or exist before the other thing is possible.
例：Good self-esteem is a prerequisite for a happy life...良好的自尊是幸福生活的前提。
例：Party membership was an essential prerequisite of a successful career.成为党员是事业有成必不可少的先决条件。

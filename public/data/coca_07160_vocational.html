ADJ
职业的;职业技术的
Vocational training and skills are the training and skills needed for a particular job or profession.
例：...a course designed to provide vocational training in engineering...工程学的职业培训课程
例：Vocational courses are often given more respect and funding than arts or philosophy.职业技术类课程通常能够获得比艺术或哲学类课程更多的重视和资助。

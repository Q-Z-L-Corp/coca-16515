V-RECIP
意见一致;同意
If one person concurs with another person, the two people agree. You can also say that two people concur .
例：Local feeling does not necessarily concur with the press...当地人的感受未必与媒体一致。
例：Daniels and Franklin concurred in an investigator's suggestion that the police be commended...丹尼尔斯和富兰克林一致同意一位调查人员的建议，即警方应该受到赞扬。

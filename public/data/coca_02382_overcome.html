overcome 
英[ˌəʊvəˈkʌm] 美[ˌoʊvərˈkʌm]
 
vt.& vi.战胜，克服；压倒，制服，…不堪；vt.被（烟、感情等）熏[压]倒，使受不了；vi.受到…的极大影响；
 "

VERB
克服；控制；战胜
If you overcome a problem or a feeling, you successfully deal with it and control it.
例：Molly had fought and overcome her fear of flying...莫莉已经努力克服了对飞行的恐惧。
例：Find a way to overcome your difficulties.找出办法战胜困难。

VERB
（感情上）受到极大影响，承受不起
If you are overcome by a feeling or event, it is so strong or has such a strong effect that you cannot think clearly.
例：The night before the test I was overcome by fear and despair...考试前的晚上，恐惧和绝望让我内心无比惊慌。
例：A dizziness overcame him, blurring his vision.他感到一阵眩晕，眼前发黑。

VERB
（烟、毒气等）熏倒，使受不了
If you are overcome by smoke or a poisonous gas, you become very ill or die from breathing it in.
例：The residents were trying to escape from the fire but were overcome by smoke.居民试图逃离火场，却被浓烟熏倒了。

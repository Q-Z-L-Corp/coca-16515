semblance 
英[ˈsembləns] 美[ˈsɛmbləns]
 
n.外表，外观；类似；假装；副本，拷贝；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
假象;表象;外观
If there is a semblance of a particular condition or quality, it appears to exist, even though this may be a false impression.
例：At least a semblance of normality has been restored to parts of the country...该国的部分地区至少已经恢复了表面的常态。
例：They had nursed Peter back to some semblance of health.在他们的照料下，彼得貌似恢复了健康。

inset 
英[ˈɪnset] 美[ˈɪnˌsɛt, ɪnˈsɛt]
 
n.插入物，插进；
 "

ADJ
镶嵌着…的
Something that is inset with a decoration or piece of material has the decoration or material set inside it.
例：...a gold pendant, inset with a diamond.镶嵌着一粒钻石的金制项链坠儿
例：...the glass bricks inset high along the corridor's opposite wall.高高地嵌在走廊另一面墙上的玻璃砖

N-COUNT
嵌图;套印小图;画中画
An inset is a small picture, diagram, or map that is inside a larger one.
例：I frequently paint between 10 and 20 insets for my murals.我经常会为我的壁画画上 10 到 20 个嵌套图画。

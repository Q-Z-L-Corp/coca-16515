circuit 
英[ˈsɜ:kɪt] 美[ˈsɜrkɪt]
 
n.电路，线路；巡回；唤醒，环形道；〔电〕电流；vt.巡回，周游；
 "

N-COUNT
电路;(电流的)回路
An electrical circuit is a complete route which an electric current can flow around.
例：Any attempts to cut through the cabling will break the electrical circuit.只要一割断电缆，电路就中断。

N-COUNT
（尤指工作时的）巡回线路，巡回，巡游
A circuit is a series of places that are visited regularly by a person or group, especially as a part of their job.
例：He joined the professional circuit...他参加了职业巡回赛。
例：It's a common problem, the one I'm asked about most when I'm on the lecture circuit.这问题很常见，我在巡回讲演时被问得最多的就是这个问题。

N-COUNT
赛车道
A racing circuit is a track on which cars, motorbikes, or cycles race.

N-COUNT
(绕着某地的)环行，环游
A circuit of a place or area is a journey all the way round it.
例：She made a slow circuit of the room.她绕着房间缓缓地走了一圈。

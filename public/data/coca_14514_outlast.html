outlast 
英[ˌaʊtˈlɑ:st] 美[ˌaʊtˈlæst]

 "

VERB
比…活得长；比…持久
If one thing outlasts another thing, the first thing lives or exists longer than the second.
例：These naturally dried flowers will outlast a bouquet of fresh blooms...这些自然风干的花会比一束鲜花更加持久。
例：People should listen to his music —it will outlast us all.人们应该听听他的音乐——它会是超越时间的经典。

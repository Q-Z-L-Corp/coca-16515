fearsome

ADJ-GRADED
可怕的;骇人的;令人生畏的
Fearsome is used to describe things that are frightening, for example because of their large size or extreme nature.
例：He had developed a fearsome reputation for intimidating people.他曾因恐吓他人而令人闻风丧胆。
例：...a fearsome array of weapons.一大批令人生畏的武器

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指建筑的)隔热层,隔热材料
Insulation is a thick layer of a substance that keeps something warm, especially a building.
例：High electricity bills point to a poor heating system or bad insulation...高额电费开支说明供暖系统不足或建筑隔热材料不良。
例：A wet suit provides excellent insulation.湿式潜水服有极佳的隔热性能。

blinking 
英[ˈblɪŋkɪŋ] 美['blɪŋkɪŋ]
 
adj.闪光的，一眨眼的；v.眨眼睛( blink的现在分词 )；闪亮，闪烁；
 "

VERB
眨（眼）
When you blink or when you blink your eyes, you shut your eyes and very quickly open them again.
例：Kathryn blinked and forced a smile...凯瑟琳眨了眨眼睛，挤出一丝微笑。
例：Suddenly, Momma's eyes blinked...突然，妈妈眼睛一闪。

VERB
（灯光）闪烁，闪动，忽明忽灭
When a light blinks, it flashes on and off.
例：Green and yellow lights blinked on the surface of the harbour...海港的水面上闪烁着绿色和黄色的灯光。
例：A warning light blinked on.警报灯闪亮了。

PHRASE
一眨眼的工夫；转眼之间；瞬间
If you say that something happens in the blink of an eye, you mean that it happens very quickly.
例：It was all over in the blink of an eye.转眼之间，一切都结束了。

PHRASE
（机器）不能正常工作，出故障，出毛病
If a machine goes on the blink, it stops working properly.
例：...an old TV that's on the blink.坏了的旧电视机

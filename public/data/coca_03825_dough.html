dough 
英[dəʊ] 美[doʊ]
 
n.生面团；〈美俚〉钱；
 "

N-MASS
(用于制作面包等的)生面团
Dough is a fairly firm mixture of flour, water, and sometimes also fat and sugar. It can be cooked to make bread, pastry, and biscuits.
例：Roll out the dough into one large circle...将面团擀成大的圆形面皮。
例：Work the flour and yeast mixture together until you have a sticky dough.将面粉和酵母混在一起和成黏糊糊的面团。

N-UNCOUNT
钱;现款
You can refer to money as dough .
例：He worked hard for his dough.他努力工作挣钱。

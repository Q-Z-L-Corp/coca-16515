nucleus 
英[ˈnju:kliəs] 美[ˈnukliəs]
 
n.中心，核心；（原子）核；起点，开始；[微]细胞核；
 "

N-COUNT
(原子)核
The nucleus of an atom or cell is the central part of it.
例：Neutrons and protons are bound together in the nucleus of an atom.在原子核里，中子和质子是结合在一起的。

N-COUNT
(组织的)核心，中心
The nucleus of a group of people or things is the small number of members which form the most important part of the group.
例：The Civic Movement could be the nucleus of a centrist party of the future.公民运动的成员可能会成为未来中间派政党的核心。

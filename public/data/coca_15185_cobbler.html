N-COUNT
鞋匠
A cobbler is a person whose job is to make or mend shoes.

N-UNCOUNT
胡说;废话
If you describe something that someone has just said as cobblers, you mean that you think it is nonsense.
例：The idea that greens are good for you is a load of cobblers...青菜对人体有益的说法是一派胡言。
例：These guys talk an awful load of old cobblers.这些人纯粹是胡说八道。

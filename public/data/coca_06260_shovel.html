shovel 
英[ˈʃʌvl] 美[ˈʃʌvəl]
 
n.铲子，铁锹；铲车；一铲；铲状物；vt.铲，铲出；用铲子挖；把…胡乱投入；铲除，挖清；vi.铲；
 "

N-COUNT
铲;铁锹
A shovel is a tool with a long handle that is used for lifting and moving earth, coal, or snow.
例：...a coal shovel...一把煤铲
例：She dug the foundation with a pick and shovel.她用镐和平锹挖地基。

VERB
铲;铲起;用铲挖
If you shovel earth, coal, or snow, you lift and move it with a shovel.
例：He has to get out and shovel snow...他得出去铲雪。
例：Pendergood had shovelled the sand out of the caravan.彭德古德把旅行拖车里的沙子铲了出来。

VERB
迅速把…大量送入
If you shovel something somewhere, you push a lot of it quickly into that place.
例：Randall was shoveling food into his mouth.兰德尔将食物大口大口地塞进嘴里。

ADJ
出乎意料的；未预见到的
If something that has happened was unforeseen, it was not expected to happen or known about beforehand.
例：Radiation may damage cells in a way that was previously unforeseen...辐射可能以一种我们过去未能预见到的方式破坏细胞。
例：Unfortunately, due to unforeseen circumstances, this year's show has been cancelled...遗憾的是，由于一些意外情况，今年的演出被取消了。

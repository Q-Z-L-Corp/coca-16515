ADJ
有限的;有尽的
Something that is finite has a definite fixed size or extent.
例：...a finite set of elements...有限元素集
例：Only a finite number of situations can arise...只有有限的几种情况可能会发生。

ADJ
(从句)限定的，定式的
A finite clause is a clause based on a verb group which indicates tense, such as 'went', 'is waiting', or 'will be found', rather than on an infinitive or a participle.

ADJ
完全的;彻底的;最大限度的
Full-scale means as complete, intense, or great in extent as possible.
例：...the possibility of a full-scale nuclear war.爆发全面核战争的可能性

ADJ
与实物一样大小的;原尺寸的
A full-scale drawing or model is the same size as the thing that it represents.
例：...working, full-scale prototypes.能够工作的原尺寸样机

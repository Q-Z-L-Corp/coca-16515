opaque 
英[əʊˈpeɪk] 美[oʊˈpeɪk]
 
adj.不透明的；无光泽的，晦暗的；不传导性的；含糊的，迟钝的；n.不透明，晦暗；[建]遮檐；遮光涂料；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不透明的;不透光的
If an object or substance is opaque, you cannot see through it.
例：You can always use opaque glass if you need to block a street view.如果不想从室内看到街景，可以使用不透明玻璃。

ADJ-GRADED
费解的；难懂的；晦涩的
If you say that something is opaque, you mean that it is difficult to understand.
例：...the opaque language of the inspector's reports.巡视员报告中晦涩难懂的语言

globally 
英['ɡləʊbəlɪ] 美['ɡloʊbəlɪ]
 
[计] 全局地；
 "

ADJ
全球的；全世界的
You can use global to describe something that happens in all parts of the world or affects all parts of the world.
例：...a global ban on nuclear testing...全球核试验禁令
例：On a global scale, AIDS may well become the leading cause of infant death.艾滋病很可能会成为造成全球婴儿死亡的首要原因。

ADJ-GRADED
全面的；整体的；总括的
A global view or vision of a situation is one in which all the different aspects of it are considered.
例：...the global view, the ability to make wider decisions based on a knowledge of all the facts, not just some of them.大局观——基于全部而非部分事实作出更加宏观的决定的能力
例：...a global vision of contemporary societies.对当代社会的总体看法

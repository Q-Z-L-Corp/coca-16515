over 
英[ˈəʊvə(r)] 美[ˈoʊvə(r)]
 
prep.（表示方向）越过；（部份或全部覆盖）在…上面；由于；（表示论及）关于；adv.结束；再；（倒）下；从一边至另一边；adj.过去的；外面的；在上的；上级的；n.额外；剩余；剩余（或多余）的量；剩余物；int.[电信学]报文完，请回复！；vt.走过，跳过；[美国方言]从…恢复过来；
 "

PREP
在…的上方；在…的上面；在…的上空
If one thing is over another thing or is moving over it, the first thing is directly above the second, either resting on it, or with a space between them.
例：He looked at himself in the mirror over the table.他对着桌子上方的镜子照了照。
例：...a bridge over the river Danube.多瑙河上的一座桥

PREP
搭挂在…的上面
If one thing is over another thing, it is supported by it and its ends are hanging down on each side of it.
例：A grey mackintosh was folded over her arm...她胳膊上搭着一件灰色雨衣。
例：Joe's clothing was flung over the back of a chair.乔的衣服扔在椅背上。

PREP
（部分或完全地）覆盖在…的上面
If one thing is over another thing, it covers part or all of it.
例：His hair fell over his brow instead of being brushed straight back...他的头发没有整齐地往后梳，而是搭在前额。
例：Mix the ingredients and pour over the mushrooms...将配料搅拌均匀浇在蘑菇上。

PREP
（身体上部）俯在…的上面
If you lean over an object, you bend your body so that the top part of it is above the object.
例：They stopped to lean over a gate...他们停下来，身子探过一扇栅栏门。
例：Everyone in the room was bent over her desk.房间里的每一个人都弯腰围在她的办公室桌前。

PREP
从…上面（看过去）；隔着…（交谈）
If you look over or talk over an object, you look or talk across the top of it.
例：I went and stood beside him, looking over his shoulder.我走过去站在他身边，从他的肩头望过去。
例：...conversing over the fence with your friend...与你的朋友隔着围栏交谈

PREP
(从…)可以看见;俯瞰
If a window has a view over an area of land or water, you can see the land or water through the window.
例：...a light and airy bar with a wonderful view over the River Amstel...可将阿姆斯特尔河美景尽收眼底的明亮通风的酒吧
例：His rooms looked out over a narrow lane behind the college.从他的房间可以看见学院后面一条狭窄的小巷。

PREP
（穿）过，（跨）过，（越）过（障碍、边界等）
If someone or something goes over a barrier, obstacle, or boundary, they get to the other side of it by going across it, or across the top of it.
例：Policemen jumped over the wall of the Spanish Embassy in pursuit...警察在追捕中跳过西班牙大使馆的围墙。
例：I stepped over a broken piece of wood...我跨过一块破木头。

PREP
从一边至…的另一边
If someone or something moves over an area or surface, they move across it, from one side to the other.
例：She ran swiftly over the lawn to the gate...她一溜烟穿过草地跑到了大门口。
例：Joe passed his hand over his face and looked puzzled.乔用手摸了一把脸，看上去疑惑不解。

PREP
在…的另一边；在…的对面
If something is on the opposite side of a road or river, you can say that it is over the road or river.
例：...Richard Garrick, who lived in the house over the road.住在马路对面那所房子里的理查德·加里克
例：...a fashionable neighbourhood, just over the river from Manhattan.与曼哈顿隔河相望的时尚街区

ADV
去，到（某处）
If you go over to a place, you go to that place.
例：I got out the car and drove over to Dervaig...我把车开出来，驶向德韦格。
例：I thought you might have invited her over.我以为你可能已经邀请她过来了。

ADV
在那边；在近旁
You can use over to indicate a particular position or place a short distance away from someone or something.
例：He noticed Rolfe standing silently over by the window...他注意到罗尔夫一声不吭地站在窗户旁边。
例：John reached over and took Joanna's hand...约翰伸手握住乔安娜的手。

ADV
（突然或猛力地）（倒）下，（掉）下
You use over to say that someone or something falls towards or onto the ground, often suddenly or violently.
例：If he drinks more than two glasses of wine he falls over...他喝酒超过两杯就会醉倒在地。
例：He was knocked over by a bus and broke his leg...他被一辆公共汽车撞倒，一条腿折了。

ADV
（翻）转过来
If something rolls over or is turned over, its position changes so that the part that was facing upwards is now facing downwards.
例：His car rolled over after a tyre was punctured...一只轮胎被扎破后，他的车翻了个底朝天。
例：The alarm did go off but all I did was yawn, turn over and go back to sleep.闹钟确实响了，但我只是打了个哈欠，翻个身又接着睡着了。

PREP-PHRASE
到处；遍及
All over a place means in every part of it.
例：...doctors who work all over the country.在全国各地工作的医生
例：...the letters she received from people all over the world.她收到的来自世界各地的信件

PHRASE
在这边；到这边；在身处国
Over here means near you, or in the country you are in.
例：Why don't you come over here tomorrow evening...你明天晚上过来吧。
例：My father was in the U.S. army over here.我父亲在这里的美国陆军服役。

PHRASE
在那边；到那边；在另一国
Over there means in a place a short distance away from you, or in another country.
例：The cafe is just across the road over there...咖啡馆就在那边马路对面。
例：She'd married some American and settled down over there.她嫁给了一位美国人，并在那边定居了。

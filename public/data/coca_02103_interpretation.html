interpretation 
英[ɪnˌtɜ:prɪˈteɪʃn] 美[ɪnˌtɜrprɪˈteɪʃn]
 
n.解释，说明；翻译；表演，演绎；理解；
 "

N-VAR
解释;说明;阐明
An interpretation of something is an opinion about what it means.
例：The opposition Conservative Party put a different interpretation on the figures...反对党保守党对这些数字提出一番不同的解释。
例：Analysis and interpretation is a very personal thing.分析与说明是个很主观的事情。

N-COUNT
表演;演绎;演奏
A performer's interpretation of something such as a piece of music or a role in a play is the particular way in which they choose to perform it.
例：...her full-bodied interpretation of the role of Micaela.她对米凯拉这个角色全身心的演绎

ADJ
认识过程的;认知的
Cognitive means relating to the mental process involved in knowing, learning, and understanding things.
例：As children grow older, their cognitive processes become sharper.孩子们越长越大，他们的认知过程变得更为敏锐。
例：...Vygotsky's theory of cognitive development.维果斯基的认知发展理论

spectrum 
英[ˈspektrəm] 美[ˈspɛktrəm]
 
n.光谱；波谱；范围；系列；
 "

N-SING
光谱
The spectrum is the range of different colours which is produced when light passes through a glass prism or through a drop of water. A rainbow shows the colours in the spectrum.

N-COUNT
系列;范围
A spectrum is a range of a particular type of thing.
例：She'd seen his moods range across the emotional spectrum...她见识过他的情绪波动会有多大。
例：Politicians across the political spectrum have denounced the act...各党派的政客们都对该行为表示谴责。

N-COUNT
波谱(如光波波谱、无线电波波谱等)
A spectrum is a range of light waves or radio waves within particular frequencies.
例：Vast amounts of energy, from X-rays right through the spectrum down to radio waves, are escaping into space...大量的能量，从 X 光直到无线电电波，都正被泄露到太空中去。
例：The individual colours within the light spectrum are believed to have an effect on health.光谱中不同的颜色被认为对健康有不同的影响。

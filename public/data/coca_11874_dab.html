dab 
英[dæb] 美[dæb]
 
n.少许；是…能手；做某事很在行；在某方面技术熟练；vt.（用某物）轻触；轻而快地擦掉（或抹掉）；快速擦拭；（用某物）轻而快地涂上（或点上）…；
 "

VERB
轻拍;轻触;轻拭;轻敷;轻涂
If you dab something, you touch it several times using quick, light movements. If you dab a substance onto a surface, you put it there using quick, light movements.
例：She arrived weeping, dabbing her eyes with a tissue...她来的时候一边哭着，一边用纸巾抹着眼泪。
例：She spread the icing over the cake, dabbing it with a knife...她把糖霜浇在蛋糕上面，并用刀子轻轻涂抹开。

N-COUNT
(表面的)少量，少许，一点儿
A dab of something is a small amount of it that is put onto a surface.
例：She wore no make-up, not even a dab of lipstick.她没有化妆，甚至连一点儿口红都没涂。
例：...a dab of glue.点滴胶水

孙鲽;黄盖鲽

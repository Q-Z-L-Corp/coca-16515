twirl 
英[twɜ:l] 美[twɜrl]
 
vt.（使）快速转动；（使）快速旋转；扭动；卷曲；n.旋转，转动；万能钥匙；捻弄；旋转的东西；vi.旋转，转动；
 "

V-ERG
(使)快速转动；（使）轻快地旋转
If you twirl something or if it twirls, it turns around and around with a smooth, fairly fast movement.
例：Bonnie twirled her empty glass in her fingers...邦妮用手指旋转着她的空杯子。
例：All around me leaves twirl to the ground.在我周围，树叶打着转儿纷纷飘落到地上。

VERB
(跳舞等时)旋转，转动
If you twirl, you turn around and around quickly, for example when you are dancing.
例：Several hundred people twirl around the ballroom dance floor.几百人在舞厅的舞池里旋转。

VERB
（用手指）缠绕，盘绕，卷曲
If you twirl something such as your hair, you twist it around your finger.
例：Sarah lifted her hand and started twirling a strand of hair.萨拉抬起手来开始用手指缠绕一缕头发。

ail 
英[eɪl] 美[el]
 
vi.生病；感到不舒服；处境困难；境况不佳；vt.使受病痛；使疼痛；使烦恼；折磨；n.病痛，苦恼，烦恼；
 "

VERB
使苦恼;困扰;使麻烦
If something ails a group or area of activity, it is a problem or source of trouble for that group or for people involved in that activity.
例：A full-scale debate is under way on what ails the industry.关于工业为何陷入困境正在展开一场全面的讨论。

VERB
使患病;使不适
If something ails someone, they are ill.
例：'What ails you?' he asked.“你哪里不舒服？”他问道。

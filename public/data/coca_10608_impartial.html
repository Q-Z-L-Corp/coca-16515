ADJ-GRADED
不偏不倚的;公正的;无偏见的
Someone who is impartial is not directly involved in a particular situation, and is therefore able to give a fair opinion or decision about it.
例：As an impartial observer my analysis is supposed to be objective...作为中立的观察员,我的分析应该客观。
例：Careers officers offer impartial advice to all pupils.就业指导员向所有学生提供公正无私的建议。

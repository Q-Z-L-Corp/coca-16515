ornament 
英[ˈɔ:nəmənt] 美[ˈɔrnəmənt]
 
n.装饰；装饰物；[乐]装饰音；v.装饰；美化；
 "

N-COUNT
（家中或花园里的）装饰物，装饰品，点缀品
An ornament is an attractive object that you display in your home or in your garden.
例：...a shelf containing a few photographs and ornaments.摆放着几张照片和些许装饰品的架子
例：...Christmas tree ornaments.圣诞树装饰物

N-COUNT
首饰；饰物
Pieces of jewellery are sometimes referred to as ornaments .
例：I guessed he was the chief because he wore more gold ornaments than the others.我估计他是酋长，因为他戴的金饰最多。

N-UNCOUNT
（建筑或家具的）装饰物，装饰图案
Decorations and patterns on a building or a piece of furniture can be referred to as ornament .
例：...walls of glass overlaid with ornament.贴着装饰物的玻璃墙

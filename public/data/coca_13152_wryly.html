wryly 
英[raɪlɪ] 美[raɪlɪ]
 
adv.（面部表情）做鬼脸地，苦笑地，嘲讽地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(表情)哭笑不得的
If someone has a wry expression, it shows that they find a bad situation or a change in a situation slightly amusing.
例：Matthew allowed himself a wry smile...马修不由得苦笑了一下。
例：She cast a wry glance in her grandmother's direction.她朝奶奶无可奈何地瞟了一眼。

ADJ-GRADED
（话语或文章）挖苦的，讽刺的，揶揄的
A wry remark or piece of writing refers to a bad situation or a change in a situation in an amusing way.
例：The play is a rueful, wry observation about the way we are all subject to the ravages of time...该剧凄然而讽刺地描述了我们大家是如何被岁月所摧残的。
例：There is a wry sense of humour in his work.他的作品带有一种讽刺意味的幽默。

ADJ
镶嵌的;嵌饰的;嵌花样的
An object that is inlaid has a design on it which is made by putting materials such as wood, gold, or silver into the surface of the object.
例：...a box delicately inlaid with little triangles.精巧地嵌有小三角的盒子

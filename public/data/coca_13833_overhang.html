overhang 
英[ˌəʊvəˈhæŋ] 美[ˌoʊvərˈhæŋ]
 
vt.伸突出，伸出；逼近，威胁；用悬挂物装饰；vi.悬垂；逼近；n.突出物；延伸量，伸出量；[航]突出船首（或船尾）；（无法兑换黄金的）美元过剩额；
 "

VERB
悬于…之上；凸出于…之上
If one thing overhangs another, it sticks out over and above it.
例：Part of the rock wall overhung the path at one point...石墙的一部分在某处悬于小路的上方。
例：These restored houses overhang a system of quiet canals.这些修葺一新的房屋俯视着静静流淌的运河河网。

N-COUNT
垂悬物；凸出物
An overhang is the part of something that sticks out over and above something else.
例：A sharp overhang of rock gave them cover...一块凸出来的尖利岩石成了他们的避身之所。
例：There is a wide veranda under the overhang of the roof.悬吊的屋顶下是一条宽阔的游廊。

VERB
搭挂在…之上
If one thing overhangs another, it is supported by it and hangs down its sides.
例：Let the pastry overhang the rim and brush between the layers with the melted butter.用油酥面皮盖住边缘，每层之间抹些熔化的黄油。

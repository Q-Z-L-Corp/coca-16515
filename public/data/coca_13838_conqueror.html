N-COUNT
征服者;占领者
The conquerors of a country or group of people are the people who have taken complete control of that country or group's land.
例：The people of an oppressed country obey their conquerors because they want to go on living.受压迫国的人民为了活下去只得听命于征服者。

N-COUNT
(比赛的)胜利者
The conqueror of a person or team is the person or team that beats them in a game or contest.
例：He easily overcame Garcia, Saturday's conqueror of Ernie Els...他轻松战胜了星期六击败厄尼·埃尔斯的加西亚。
例：Her conqueror, Senator Pete Wilson, is a diffident, moderate man.击败她的参议员皮特·威尔逊是一个谦卑的温和派人士。

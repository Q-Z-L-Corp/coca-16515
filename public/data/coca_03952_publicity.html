publicity 
英[pʌbˈlɪsəti] 美[pʌˈblɪsɪti]

N-UNCOUNT
宣传；报道
Publicity is information or actions that are intended to attract the public's attention to someone or something.
例：Much advance publicity was given to the talks...对于这次会谈有很多前期报道。
例：...government publicity campaigns...政府宣传活动

N-UNCOUNT
（媒体或公众的）关注
When the news media and the public show a lot of interest in something, you can say that it is receiving publicity.
例：The case has generated enormous publicity in Brazil.这个案件已经在巴西引起了极大的关注。
例：...the renewed publicity over the Casey affair.对凯西事件的再次关注

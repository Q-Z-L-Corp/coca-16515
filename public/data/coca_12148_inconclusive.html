inconclusive 
英[ˌɪnkənˈklu:sɪv] 美[ˌɪnkənˈklusɪv]
 
adj.不确定的，非决定性的，无结果的；不能使人信服的；无效的；模棱两可；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
无最后结果的;无结论的
If research or evidence is inconclusive, it has not proved anything.
例：Research has so far proved inconclusive...目前研究尚无定论。
例：I find the evidence inconclusive.我发现证据无说服力。

ADJ-GRADED
(竞赛、冲突)胜负不明的,无最后结果的
If a contest or conflict is inconclusive, it is not clear who has won or who is winning.
例：The past two elections were inconclusive.之前的两次选举都没有结果。
例：...eight years of bloody and inconclusive war.8年没完没了的血腥战争

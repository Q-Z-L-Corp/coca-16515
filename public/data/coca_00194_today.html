today 
英[təˈdeɪ] 美[təˈde]
 
adv.（在）今天；现在；现今；现代，当代；n.今天，今日；现代，当代；现今；
 "

ADV
在今天;在今日
You use today to refer to the day on which you are speaking or writing.
例：How are you feeling today?...你今天感觉怎么样？
例：I wanted him to come with us today, but he couldn't.我想让他今天和我们一起来，但他来不了。

ADV
当今;现今;在当代
You can refer to the present period of history as today .
例：The United States is in a serious recession today…当今美国正处于严重的衰退时期。
例：He thinks pop music today is as exciting as it's ever been.他认为现今的流行音乐与以往一样令人兴奋。

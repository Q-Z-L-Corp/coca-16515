ADJ-GRADED
以…为方向的；对…感兴趣的；重视…的
If someone is oriented towards or oriented to a particular thing or person, they are mainly concerned with that thing or person.
例：It seems almost inevitable that North African economies will still be primarily oriented towards Europe...看来北非经济体几乎注定还将以欧洲为主导。
例：Most students here are oriented to computers.这里的大部分学生都对计算机感兴趣。

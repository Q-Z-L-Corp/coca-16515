 "

VERB
吸气;吸入(烟等)
When you inhale, you breathe in. When you inhale something such as smoke, you take it into your lungs when you breathe in.
例：He took a long slow breath, inhaling deeply...他缓缓地深吸了一口气。
例：He was treated for the effects of inhaling smoke.他因吸入烟尘而接受治疗。

N-VAR
鼓励;激励
Encouragement is the activity of encouraging someone, or something that is said or done in order to encourage them.
例：I also had friends who gave me a great deal of encouragement...我还有些朋友给了我很多鼓励。
例：Thanks for your advice and encouragement.谢谢你的建议和鼓励。

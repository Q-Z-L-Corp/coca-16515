disoriented

adj.分不清方向或目标的，无判断力的；v.使（某人）迷失方向( disorient的过去式和过去分词 )；
 "

VERB
使迷失方向;使觉得迷惘
If something disorients you, you lose your sense of direction, or you generally feel lost and uncertain, for example because you are in an unfamiliar environment.
例：An overnight stay at a friend's house disorients me...在朋友家过夜让我觉得晕晕乎乎的。
例：They were disorientated by the smoke and were firing blindly into it.他们在烟雾中迷失了方向，向里面胡乱射击了一通。

agreeable 
英[əˈgri:əbl] 美[əˈɡriəbəl]
 
adj.令人愉快的，惬意的；有礼貌的；同意的，接受的；适合的，一致的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人愉快的；宜人的
If something is agreeable, it is pleasant and you enjoy it.
例：...workers in more agreeable and better paid occupations.在条件更惬意、报酬更丰厚的行业工作的人
例：...an agreeable surprise.一个惊喜

ADJ-GRADED
亲切的;友善的;讨人喜欢的
If someone is agreeable, they are pleasant and try to please people.
例：...sharing a bottle of wine with an agreeable companion...与友善的同伴共饮一瓶酒
例：I've gone out of my way to be agreeable to his friends.我已然特意地去讨好他的朋友们。

ADJ-GRADED
欣然同意的;令人赞同的
If you are agreeable to something or if it is agreeable to you, you are willing to do it or to allow it to happen.
例：She was agreeable to the project...她对这个项目表示欣然同意。
例：If you are agreeable, my husband's office will make all the necessary arrangements.如果你同意，我丈夫的事务所将作出所有必要的安排。

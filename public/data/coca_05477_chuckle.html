chuckle 
英[ˈtʃʌkl] 美[ˈtʃʌkəl]
 
vi.轻声地笑；咯咯笑；n.轻笑声；

VERB
低声轻笑;偷笑
When you chuckle, you laugh quietly.
例：The banker chuckled and said, 'Of course not.'...银行家暗自发笑并说道：“当然不。”
例：He chuckled at her forthrightness.她的直率让他窃笑。

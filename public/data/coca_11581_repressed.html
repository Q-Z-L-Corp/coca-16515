(感情、欲望，尤指性欲)被压抑的，受到抑制的
A repressed person is someone who does not allow themselves to have natural feelings and desires, especially sexual ones.
例：Some have charged that the Puritans were sexually repressed and inhibited.一些人谴责说清教徒的性欲受到压抑，并且个性拘谨。

sector 
英[ˈsektə(r)] 美[ˈsɛktɚ, -ˌtɔr]
 
n.部门；领域；防御地区；扇形；
 "

N-COUNT
(经济的)部门，行业，领域
A particular sector of a country's economy is the part connected with that specified type of industry.
例：...the nation's manufacturing sector.该国的制造业
例：...the service sector of the Hong Kong economy.香港经济中的服务行业

N-COUNT
(大型组织的)部分,小群体
A sector of a large group is a smaller group which is part of it.
例：Workers who went to the Gulf came from the poorest sectors of Pakistani society.前往海湾的工人来自巴基斯坦最贫穷的社会阶层。

N-COUNT
军事管制区;军事防御区
A sector is an area of a city or country which is controlled by a military force.
例：Officers were going to retake sectors of the city.军官们打算重新夺回该市的防御区。

N-COUNT
扇形
A sector is a part of a circle which is formed when you draw two straight lines from the centre of the circle to the edge.

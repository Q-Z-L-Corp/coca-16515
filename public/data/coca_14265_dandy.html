dandy 
英[ˈdændi] 美[ˈdændi]
 
adj.服装华丽的；最好的；n.花花公子；〈口〉最好的东西，上品；
 "

N-COUNT
时髦男子;注重打扮的男人
A dandy is a man who thinks a great deal about his appearance and always dresses in smart clothes.
例：He was handsome and a dandy.他英俊潇洒，还是个衣着讲究的时髦绅士。

ADJ
好的;棒的;正好的
If you say that something is dandy, you mean it is good or just right.
例：There's a zoo round here? That's dandy for my kids.这附近有个动物园？我的孩子们一定会很高兴的。

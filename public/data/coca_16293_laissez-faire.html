adj.[法]放任主义的，自由放任的；=“laisser-faire”；n.自由放任主义，无干涉主义；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（政府对工商业的）自由放任政策
Laissez-faire is the policy which is based on the idea that governments and the law should not interfere with business, finance, or the conditions of people's working lives.
例：...a policy of laissez faire.自由放任政策
例：...the doctrine of laissez-faire and unbridled individualism.不干预原则和放任不羁的自由主义

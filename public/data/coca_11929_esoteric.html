ADJ-GRADED
深奥的;难懂的;常人难以理解的
If you describe something as esoteric, you mean it is known, understood, or appreciated by only a small number of people.
例：...esoteric knowledge.深奥的知识
例：...a spoiled aristocrat with pretentious airs and esoteric tastes.自命不凡、品位刁钻且被惯坏了的贵族成员

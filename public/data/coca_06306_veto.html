veto 
英[ˈvi:təʊ] 美[ˈvitoʊ]
 
n.否决权，否认权；行使否决权；否决理由；vt.否决，不同意；不批准，禁止；vi.否决；禁止；
 "

VERB
否决;否定
If someone in authority vetoes something, they forbid it, or stop it being put into action.
例：De Gaulle vetoed Britain's application to join the EEC...戴高乐驳回了英国加入欧洲经济共同体的申请。
例：The President vetoed the economic package passed by Congress.总统否决了国会通过的一揽子经济计划。

N-UNCOUNT
否决权
Veto is the right that someone in authority has to forbid something.
例：...the President's power of veto.总统的否决权

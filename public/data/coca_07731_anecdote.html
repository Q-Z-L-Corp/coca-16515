 "

N-VAR
轶事；趣闻
An anecdote is a short, amusing account of something that has happened.
例：Pete was telling them an anecdote about their mother...皮特正在给他们讲一件他们母亲的趣闻。
例：He has a talent for recollection and anecdote.他在记忆力和讲述趣闻轶事方面颇具天赋。

N-VAR
（无根据的）传闻
Anecdotes are individual accounts of something that are not reliable evidence.
例：The image of the fox as a pest is grossly exaggerated in anecdote and folklore.狐狸的害人精形象在传闻和民间故事中遭到严重的夸大。

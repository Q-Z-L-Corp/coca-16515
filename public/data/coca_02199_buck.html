buck 
英[bʌk] 美[bʌk]
 
n.（美）钱，元；雄鹿；花花公子；vt.& vi.（马等）猛然弓背跃起；抵制；猛然震荡；
 "

N-COUNT
美元；澳元
A buck is a US or Australian dollar.
例：That would probably cost you about fifty bucks...那大概要花掉你50美元左右。
例：Why can't you spend a few bucks on a coat?...你为什么不能花点儿钱买件外套呢？

N-COUNT
（雄鹿、公羚羊、公兔、雄袋鼠等）雄性动物
A buck is the male of various animals, including the deer, antelope, rabbit and kangaroo.

N-COUNT
（美洲印第安裔或非洲裔）小伙子
A buck is a young Native American or African American man.
例：He'd been a real hell-raiser as a young buck.他年轻时是个十足的捣蛋鬼。

ADJ
龅牙的
If someone has buck teeth, their upper front teeth stick forward out of their mouth.

VERB
（马）猛地弓背跃起，尥起后蹄跳跃
If a horse bucks, it kicks both of its back legs wildly into the air, or jumps into the air wildly with all four feet off the ground.
例：The stallion bucked as he fought against the reins holding him tightly in.牡马四蹄腾空跳起，想挣脱紧紧缚住它的缰绳。
例：...cowboys riding bucking broncos.骑在弓背腾跃的野马上的牛仔

VERB
抵抗；抵制；反抗
If you buck the trend, you obtain different results from others in the same area. If you buck the system, you get what you want by breaking or ignoring the rules.
例：While other newspapers are losing circulation, we are bucking the trend...当其他报纸的发行量越来越少时，我们却逆势而进。
例：He wants to be the tough rebel who bucks the system...他想成为这种体制的坚定反抗者。

PHRASE
优厚的回报
If you get more bang for the buck, you spend your money wisely and get more for your money than if you were to spend it in a different way.
例：I think it's very important for those governments to do whatever they can to get a bigger bang for the buck.我认为，重要的是那些政府要采取一切措施来获取更大的资金回报。

PHRASE
轻易赚来的钱；（常指）不义之财
When someone makes a fast buck or makes a quick buck, they earn a lot of money quickly and easily, often by doing something which is considered to be dishonest.
例：His life isn't ruled by looking for a fast buck...他的生活没有被一味想发大财的心态左右。
例：They were just in it to make a quick buck.他们做这事就是为了迅速赚大钱。

PHRASE
赚钱；挣钱
If you are trying to make a buck, you are trying to earn some money.
例：The owners don't want to overlook any opportunity to make a buck.老板们不想错过任何可以赚钱的机会。

PHRASE
一丝不挂
Someone who is buck naked is not wearing any clothes at all.

PHRASE
推卸责任
If you pass the buck, you refuse to accept responsibility for something, and say that someone else is responsible.
例：David says the responsibility is Mr Smith's and it's no good trying to pass the buck.戴维说责任在史密斯先生，企图推脱责任是没有用的。

PHRASE
责任止于此/应由…承担责任
If you say 'The buck stops here' or 'The buck stops with me', you mean that you have to take responsibility for something and will not try to pass the responsibility on to someone else.
例：The buck stops with him. He is ultimately responsible for every aspect of the broadcast.责任在于他。他是该广播节目各方面的总负责人。

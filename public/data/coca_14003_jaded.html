ADJ-GRADED
精疲力竭的；厌倦的；腻烦的；无兴致的
If you are jaded, you feel bored, tired, and not enthusiastic, for example because you have had too much of the same thing.
例：We had both become jaded, disinterested, and disillusioned.我们两个都已经觉得疲惫不堪，毫无兴趣，失望透顶。
例：...his air of jaded cynicism.他那种不胜其烦、玩世不恭的神态

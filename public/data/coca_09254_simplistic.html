ADJ-GRADED
(观点、阐述)过分简单化的
A simplistic view or interpretation of something makes it seem much simpler than it really is.
例：He has a simplistic view of the treatment of eczema...他对湿疹治疗的看法过于简单化。
例：The whole process is flawed because the logic behind the questions is too simplistic.整个过程都有缺陷，因为问题背后的逻辑太过简单了。

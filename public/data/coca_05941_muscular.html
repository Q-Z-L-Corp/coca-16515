muscular 
英[ˈmʌskjələ(r)] 美[ˈmʌskjəlɚ]
 
adj.肌肉的；壮健的，肌肉发达的，强壮的；
 "

ADJ
肌肉的;影响肌肉的
Muscular means involving or affecting your muscles.
例：As a general rule, all muscular effort is enhanced by breathing in as the effort is made...通常，肌肉的力量能通过使劲儿吸气增强。
例：Early symptoms include anorexia, muscular weakness and fatigue.早期症状有厌食、肌无力和疲劳。

ADJ-GRADED
肌肉结实的;强壮的
If a person or their body is muscular, they are very fit and strong, and have firm muscles which are not covered with a lot of fat.
例：Like most female athletes, she was lean and muscular.像大多女运动员一样，她清瘦但肌肉发达。
例：...his tanned muscular legs.他那肌肉发达、肤色黝黑的双腿

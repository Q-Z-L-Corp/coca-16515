adj.晕眩的；茫然的；神魂颠倒的，失魂落魄的；vt.使眩晕，使惶惑(daze的过去式)；

ADJ-GRADED
恍惚的;头昏的;茫然的
If someone is dazed, they are confused and unable to think clearly, often because of shock or a blow to the head.
例：At the end of the interview I was dazed and exhausted.面试进行到最后，我感到头昏脑胀，筋疲力尽。

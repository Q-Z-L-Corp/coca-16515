adj.多沙的，含砂的；勇敢的，坚毅的，坚定的；

ADJ-GRADED
含沙砾的；覆有沙砾的；沙砾般的
Something that is gritty contains grit, is covered with grit, or has a texture like that of grit.
例：The sheets fell on the gritty floor, and she just let them lie.床单掉到满是沙砾的地板上，她都没去捡。

ADJ-GRADED
坚毅的；勇敢的
Someone who is gritty is brave and determined.
例：We have to prove how gritty we are.我们必须证明我们有多么勇敢无畏。
例：...a gritty determination to avoid humiliation.坚决避免蒙受羞辱

petrified 
英[ˈpetrɪfaɪd] 美[]
 
adj.惊呆的；目瞪口呆的；v.使吓呆，使惊呆；变僵硬；使石化(petrify的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
非常害怕的；吓呆的
If you are petrified, you are extremely frightened, perhaps so frightened that you cannot think or move.
例：I've always been petrified of being alone...我总是害怕孤单一人。
例：Most people seem to be petrified of snakes.好像很多人都怕蛇。

ADJ
（动植物）石化的
A petrified plant or animal has died and has gradually turned into stone.
例：...a block of petrified wood.一块木化石

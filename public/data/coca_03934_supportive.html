ADJ-GRADED
给予帮助的;热心援助的;支持的
If you are supportive, you are kind and helpful to someone at a difficult or unhappy time in their life.
例：They were always supportive of each other...他们一直互相帮助。
例：Her boss was very supportive and gave her time off work to see her mum.她的老板非常热心地帮助她，并准许她请假去看望母亲。

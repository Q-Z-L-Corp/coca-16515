flimsy 
英[ˈflɪmzi] 美[ˈflɪmzi]
 
adj.薄的，薄弱的；易损坏的；不足信的；没有价值的；n.薄纸；复写纸；打字纸；电报；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不结实的;劣质的;不牢固的
A flimsy object is weak because it is made of a weak material, or is badly made.
例：...a flimsy wooden door.很不结实的木门
例：...a pair of flimsy shoes.一双做工粗劣的鞋

ADJ-GRADED
(衣服或布料)薄的，不御寒的
Flimsy cloth or clothing is thin and does not give much protection.
例：...a very flimsy pink chiffon nightgown.薄如蝉翼的粉色雪纺绸睡衣

ADJ-GRADED
站不住脚的;不足信的
If you describe something such as evidence or an excuse as flimsy, you mean that it is not very good or convincing.
例：The charges were based on very flimsy evidence.这些指控建立在非常不具说服力的证据上。

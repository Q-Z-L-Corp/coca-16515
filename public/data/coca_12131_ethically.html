英['eθɪklɪ] 美['eθɪklɪ]
 
adv.伦理（学）上；
 "

ADJ
道德的;伦理的
Ethical means relating to beliefs about right and wrong.
例：...the moral and ethical standards in the school.学校的道德和伦理标准
例：...the medical, nursing and ethical issues surrounding terminally-ill people.围绕患不治之症的人们的医疗、护理和伦理方面的问题

ADJ-GRADED
合乎道德的;合乎伦理规范的
If you describe something as ethical, you mean that it is morally right or morally acceptable.
例：...ethical investment schemes...合乎道德规范的投资计划
例：Does the party think it is ethical to link tax policy with party fund-raising?该政党认为将税收政策和党内筹资挂钩是合乎道德的做法吗？

ADJ
相互冲突的;相互矛盾的
Competing ideas, requirements, or interests cannot all be right or satisfied at the same time.
例：They talked about the competing theories of the origin of life.他们讨论了几种相互矛盾的生命起源理论。
例：...the competing interests of beach development and sea turtle protection.开发海滩与保护海龟之间相互冲突的利益

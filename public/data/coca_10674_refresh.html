refresh 
英[rɪˈfreʃ] 美[rɪˈfrɛʃ]
 
vt.使恢复，使振作；使焕然一新，翻新；给（电池）充电；vi.恢复精神；补充给养；
 "

VERB
使精神抖擞;使精神振奋;使重新充满活力
If something refreshes you when you have become hot, tired, or thirsty, it makes you feel cooler or more energetic.
例：The lotion cools and refreshes the skin...这种润肤液凉爽清新，能够焕发肌肤的活力。
例：They had stopped by a spring to refresh themselves.他们曾在一处泉边停歇休息。

VERB
翻新;使变新
If you refresh something old or dull, you make it as good as it was when it was new.
例：Many view these meetings as an occasion to share ideas and refresh friendship...许多人把这些会面看成是交流想法和保持友谊长青的机会。
例：Lettie appeared, her make-up refreshed.莱蒂出现了，她重新上过妆。

VERB
使记起;提醒;提示
If someone refreshes your memory, they tell you something that you had forgotten.
例：Allow me to refresh your memory...让我帮你回忆吧。
例：He walked on the opposite side of the street to refresh his memory of the building.他走在街的对面，努力回忆这座建筑的原貌。

VERB
刷新（网页）
If you refresh a web page, you click a button in order to get the most recent version of the page.
例：Press the 'reload' button on your web browser to refresh the site and get the most current version.点击网页浏览器上的“刷新”按钮来刷新页面，显示最新的版本。

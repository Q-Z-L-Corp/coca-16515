ADJ-GRADED
富有经验的;老练的
You can use seasoned to describe a person who has a lot of experience of something. For example, a seasoned traveller is a person who has travelled a lot.
例：The author is a seasoned academic...作者是一位经验丰富的学者。
例：He began acting with the confidence of a seasoned performer.作为一名久经历练的表演者，他满怀自信地开始演出。

heavy 
英[ˈhevi] 美[ˈhɛvi]
 
adj.重的，沉重的；大量的，浓密的；激烈的；重型的；n.重物；庄重的角色；adv.密集地；大量地；笨重地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
重的;沉的
Something that is heavy weighs a lot.
例：These scissors are awfully heavy...这把剪刀太沉了。
例：Gosh, that was a heavy bag!...啊呀，那个袋子真重！

ADJ-GRADED
有…重的
You use heavy to ask or talk about how much someone or something weighs.
例：How heavy are you?...你有多重？
例：Protons are nearly 2000 times as heavy as electrons.质子的质量几乎是电子的2,000倍。

ADJ-GRADED
大量的;重大的;严重的;强烈的;剧烈的
Heavy means great in amount, degree, or intensity.
例：Heavy fighting has been going on...激烈的战斗一直持续不断。
例：The State fails to recognize the heavy responsibility that parents take on...政府没有认识到家长所承担的重大责任。

ADJ-GRADED
结实的;粗大的;厚重的
Someone or something that is heavy is solid in appearance or structure, or is made of a thick material.
例：We talk in her Belgrade flat, full of heavy old brown furniture...我们在她位于贝尔格莱德的公寓里进行了交谈，那屋里满是结实的老式棕色家具。
例：He was short and heavy...他矮小粗壮。

ADJ-GRADED
（质地）黏重的
A heavy substance is thick in texture.
例：It is advisable to mix coarse grit into heavy soil to improve drainage.建议将粗砂掺到黏重的土壤里，以提高排水性能。
例：...11 million gallons of heavy crude oil.1,100万加仑重质原油

ADJ-GRADED
（饭菜）量大的，难消化的
A heavy meal is large in amount and often difficult to digest.
例：He had been feeling drowsy, the effect of an unusually heavy meal.他一直感到昏昏沉沉的，这是吃了一顿超级大餐的结果。

ADJ-GRADED
充满…的;装满…的
Something that is heavy with things is full of them or loaded with them.
例：The air is heavy with moisture...空气的湿度很大。
例：She brought in a tray heavy with elegant sandwiches, scones and cakes.她端进来一只盘子，里面盛满了精美的三明治、烤饼和蛋糕。

ADJ-GRADED
（呼吸）粗重的，深沉的
If a person's breathing is heavy, it is very loud and deep.
例：Her breathing became slow and heavy.她的呼吸变得缓慢而粗重。

ADJ-GRADED
（动作）用力的，猛烈的
A heavy movement or action is done with a lot of force or pressure.
例：...a heavy blow on the back of the skull...后脑勺遭到的一记重击
例：The plane made a heavy landing.飞机粗猛着陆。

ADJ
（机器、武器等）重型的，大型的
A heavy machine or piece of military equipment is very large and very powerful.
例：...government militia backed by tanks and heavy artillery.以坦克和重型大炮为后盾的政府国民军
例：...armoured personnel carriers and other heavy vehicles.装甲兵员运输车和其他重型车辆

ADJ-GRADED
繁忙的;(日程)排满的
If you describe a period of time or a schedule as heavy, you mean it involves a lot of work.
例：It's been a heavy day and I'm tired...忙了一天，我累坏了。
例：Hopefully, Max would be able to spend a few days with them, depending on his heavy schedule.希望马克斯能从满档的日程中抽出时间和他们一起呆上几天。

ADJ-GRADED
费力的;繁重的
Heavy work requires a lot of strength or energy.
例：The business is thriving and Philippa employs two full-timers for the heavy work.业务蒸蒸日上，菲莉帕雇用了两个全职员工来应付繁重的工作。

ADJ-GRADED
耗费…多的;大量使用…的
If you say that something is heavy on another thing, you mean that it uses a lot of that thing or too much of that thing.
例：Tanks are heavy on fuel and destructive to roads.坦克要耗费大量的燃料，而且会破坏道路。
例：...Linda, a platinum blonde, heavy on the lipstick.琳达，一位口红涂得很厚的银发女郎

ADJ-GRADED
(空气、天气等）阴沉的，闷热的
Air or weather that is heavy is unpleasantly still, hot, and damp.
例：The outside air was heavy and moist and sultry.外面的空气沉滞、潮湿而闷热。

ADJ-GRADED
（表情）沉痛的，疲惫呆滞的，阴郁的
If you describe a person's face as heavy, you mean that it looks sad, tired, or unfriendly.
例：Many of them were policemen, with their heavy faces and cropped hair.他们中有很多人表情阴郁，留着平头，一看就是警察。

ADJ-GRADED
（心情）沉重的，悲伤的
If your heart is heavy, you are sad about something.
例：Mr Maddison handed over his resignation letter with a heavy heart.麦迪逊先生心情沉重地递上自己的辞呈。

ADJ-GRADED
（形势）棘手的，难应付的
A situation that is heavy is serious and difficult to cope with.
例：I don't want any more of that heavy stuff.我再也不想碰那种麻烦事了。

N-COUNT
（雇作保镖或打手的）彪形大汉
A heavy is a large strong man who is employed to protect a person or place, often by using violence.
例：They had employed heavies to evict shop squatters from neighbouring sites.他们雇了一些彪形大汉，将附近私自占点的商贩都赶走了。

selfishness 
英['selfɪʃnəs] 美[ ˈsɛlfɪʃnɪs]
 
n.自私；自我中心；利己主义；任性；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
自私的;自利的
If you say that someone is selfish, you mean that he or she cares only about himself or herself, and not about other people.
例：I think I've been very selfish. I've been mainly concerned with myself.我觉得我一直非常自私。总是只关心自己。
例：...the selfish interests of a few people.少数人的私利

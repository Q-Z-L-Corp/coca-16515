den 
英[den] 美[dɛn]
 
n.兽穴；贼窝；简陋污秽的小室；书斋；vi.穴居；藏到洞里；进窝（冬眠）；把…赶入洞中；
 "

N-COUNT
(狮子、狐狸等野生动物的)兽穴，兽窝
A den is the home of certain types of wild animals such as lions or foxes.

N-COUNT
(家里用来学习、工作或从事业余爱好的)书房，私室
Your den is a quiet room in your house where you can go to study, work, or carry on a hobby without being disturbed.

N-COUNT
(通常指为了从事非法活动的)秘密聚集处，窝点，巢穴
A den is a secret place where people meet, usually for a dishonest purpose.
例：I could provide you with the addresses of at least three illegal drinking dens.我可以为你提供至少 3 处非法饮酒窝点的地址。
例：...the crack dens of urban America.美国城市中的可卡因毒窟

N-COUNT
(某一不良行为或非法活动的猖獗)场所，渊薮
If you describe a place as a den of a particular type of bad or illegal behaviour, you mean that a lot of that type of behaviour goes on there.
例：...the one-bedroomed flat that was to become his den of savage debauchery.即将成为他野蛮放荡之所的一居室公寓
例：...a den of greed.贪欲横行之地

ADJ
军事的;军队的;军用的
Military means relating to the armed forces of a country.
例：Military action may become necessary...也许有必要采取军事行动。
例：The president is sending in almost 20,000 military personnel to help with the relief efforts.总统将派出近2万名军事人员协助救灾工作。

ADJ
陆军的
Military means relating to or belonging to the army, rather than to the navy or the air force.
例：The attack has caused severe damage to American naval and military forces.这次攻击给美国的海军和陆军都造成了重创。

N-COUNT-COLL
军队;军人;(尤指)高级军官
The military are the armed forces of a country, especially officers of high rank.
例：The bombing has been far more widespread than the military will admit...轰炸的范围远远大于军方愿意承认的范围。
例：Did you serve in the military?你当过兵吗？

ADJ-GRADED
军人般的;军人作风的
Military means well-organized, controlled, or neat, in a way that is typical of a soldier.
例：Your working day will need to be organized with military precision.需要对你的工作日进行精确有序的安排。
例：He has a military bearing, never failing to carry himself erect.他有一种军人的风姿，身板总是挺得笔直。

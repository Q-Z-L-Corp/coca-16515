languid 
英[ˈlæŋgwɪd] 美[ˈlæŋɡwɪd]
 
adj.疲倦的；没精打采的，呆滞的；萧条的；慢吞吞；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
无精打采的；懒洋洋的；倦怠的
If you describe someone as languid, you mean that they show little energy or interest and are very slow and casual in their movements.
例：To his delight a familiar, tall, languid figure lowered itself down the steps of a club.看到一个熟悉的高大身影沿着俱乐部的台阶无精打采地走下来，他高兴极了。
例：Time spent at Jumby Bay can be as energetic or as languid as you wish.来琼比湾消磨时光，动静皆宜，尽可随心所欲。

furry 
英[ˈfɜ:ri] 美[ˈfəri, ˈfʌri]
 
adj.毛皮的，毛皮制的；有皮毛般覆盖的；有舌苔的；<美俚>可怕的，毛骨悚然的；
 "

ADJ
(动物)毛皮覆盖的，毛茸茸的
A furry animal is covered with thick, soft hair.
例：...the coyote's furry tail.郊狼毛茸茸的尾巴

ADJ
毛皮般柔软的
If you describe something as furry, you mean that it has a soft rough texture like fur.
例：...his herringbone tweed coat with its furry lining.他那件带毛绒衬里的人字纹粗花呢外套

ADJ
(通常指因生病而)长(舌)苔的
If you have a furry tongue, your tongue is covered with a layer of a soft greyish-white substance, usually because you are unwell.

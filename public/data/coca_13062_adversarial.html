ADJ-GRADED

If you describe something as adversarial, you mean that it involves two or more people or organizations who are opposing each other.
例：In our country there is an adversarial relationship between government and business.在我国，官商之间存在一种对立关系。
例：...an adversarial legal system.两派对立的法律制度

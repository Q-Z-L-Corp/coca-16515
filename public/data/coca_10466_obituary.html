英[əˈbɪtʃuəri] 美[oʊˈbɪtʃueri]
 
n.讣告；讣闻；adj.讣告的；死亡的；有关死者的；
 "

N-COUNT
（报纸上或广播里的）讣告，讣闻
Someone's obituary is an account of their life and character which is printed in a newspaper or broadcast soon after they die.
例：I read your brother's obituary in the Times.我在《泰晤士报》上看到了你哥哥的讣告。

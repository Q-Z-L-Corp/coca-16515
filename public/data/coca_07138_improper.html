improper 
英[ɪmˈprɒpə(r)] 美[ɪmˈprɑpə(r)]
 
adj.不合适的，非正常的；不正确的；不正派的，不合礼仪的；不道德的；
 "

ADJ
不正当的;非法的
Improper activities are illegal or dishonest.
例：25 officers were investigated following allegations of improper conduct during the murder inquiry...25 名警察在被指控于调查谋杀案期间有非法行为之后受到调查。
例：Mr Matthews maintained that he had done nothing improper.马修斯先生坚称他没有做过非法的事情。

ADJ
不合适的;不适当的
Improper conditions or methods of treatment are not suitable or good enough for a particular purpose.
例：The improper use of medicine could lead to severe adverse reactions.用药不当会产生严重的不良反应。

ADJ-GRADED
不成体统的;不得体的
If you describe someone's behaviour as improper, you mean that it is rude or shocking.
例：He would never be improper, he is always the perfect gentleman.他总是举止得体,一直是个完美的绅士。

ADJ
死的；像死了一般的
If a person or animal is lifeless, they are dead, or are so still that they appear to be dead.
例：Their cold-blooded killers had then dragged their lifeless bodies upstairs to the bathroom...那些冷血杀手那时已经将他们的尸体拖到楼上浴室里。
例：There was no breathing or pulse and he was lifeless.没有呼吸，也没有脉搏，他已经死了。

ADJ
无生命的
If you describe an object or a machine as lifeless, you mean that they are not living things, even though they may resemble living things.
例：It was made of plaster, hard and white and lifeless, bearing no resemblance to human flesh.它由石膏制成，坚硬苍白，毫无生命，与人的肌体没有任何相似之处。

ADJ-GRADED
无生物的
A lifeless place or area does not have anything living or growing there at all.
例：Dry stone walls may appear stark and lifeless, but they provide a valuable habitat for plants and animals.干燥的石墙看起来也许荒凉且毫无生机，但它们为动植物提供了宝贵的栖息地。

ADJ-GRADED
无生气的；沉闷的；单调的
If you describe a person, or something such as an artistic performance or a town as lifeless, you mean they lack any lively or exciting qualities.
例：With one exception his novels are shallow and lifeless things.他的小说都是些肤浅沉闷的玩意儿，只有一本除外。
例：...a lifeless portrait of an elderly woman.一位老妇人的毫无生气的画像

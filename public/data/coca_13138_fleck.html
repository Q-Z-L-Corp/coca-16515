fleck 
英[flek] 美[flɛk]
 
n.斑纹，斑点；微粒；vt.使有斑点；使形成条纹；
 "

N-COUNT
斑点;(斑点状的)微粒
Flecks are small marks on a surface, or objects that look like small marks.
例：He went to the men's room to wash flecks of blood from his shirt...他去男洗手间清洗衬衫上的斑斑血迹。
例：His hair is dark grey with flecks of ginger.他深灰色的头发夹杂着几缕姜黄色。

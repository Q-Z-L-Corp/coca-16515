inquire 
英[ɪn'kwaɪə(r)] 美[ɪnˈkwaɪr]
 
vt.& vi.打听，询问；查究；
 "

VERB
询问;查询;打听
If you inquire about something, you ask for information about it.
例：'Is something wrong?' he enquired...“有什么不对劲吗?”他问道。
例：'Who are you?' he enquired of the first man...“你是谁?”他问第一个人。

VERB
调查;查究
If you inquire into something, you investigate it carefully.
例：Inspectors were appointed to inquire into the affairs of the company.督查员受委派调查该公司的事务。

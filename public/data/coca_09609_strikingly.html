strikingly

ADJ-GRADED
引人注目的;异乎寻常的;显著的
Something that is striking is very noticeable or unusual.
例：The most striking feature of those statistics is the high proportion of suicides...那些统计数据最引人注目之处是其极高的自杀率。
例：He bears a striking resemblance to Lenin.他酷似列宁。

ADJ-GRADED
妩媚动人的;标致的;俊秀的
Someone who is striking is very attractive, in a noticeable way.
例：She was a striking woman with long blonde hair.她是个留着金色长发的俏女郎。

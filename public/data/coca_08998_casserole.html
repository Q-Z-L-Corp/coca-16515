casserole 
英[ˈkæsərəʊl] 美[ˈkæsəroʊl]
 
n.焙盘；砂锅；焙盘菜；砂锅菜；
 "

N-COUNT
(以肉、蔬菜为原料做成的)炖菜，砂锅菜
A casserole is a dish made of meat and vegetables that have been cooked slowly in a liquid.
例：...a huge beef casserole, full of herbs, vegetables and wine.一大份加满了香料、蔬菜和葡萄酒的牛腩煲

N-COUNT
砂锅;炖锅
A casserole or a casserole dish is a large heavy container with a lid. You cook casseroles and other dishes in it.
例：Place all the chopped vegetables into a casserole dish.把所有切好的蔬菜放入砂锅。
例：...a flameproof casserole.耐火砂锅

VERB
用文火烹煮;炖
If you casserole meat and vegetables, you cook them slowly in a liquid.
例：If you casserole chicken pieces, take the skin off first.要是炖鸡块的话，先去掉鸡皮。
例：...casseroled chicken.鸡煲

manually 
英['mænjʊəlɪ] 美[ˈmænjʊəlɪ]
 
adv.用手地，手工地；
 "

ADJ
人力的;体力的；手工的
Manual work is work in which you use your hands or your physical strength rather than your mind.
例：...skilled manual workers...熟练的体力劳动者
例：They have no reservations about taking factory or manual jobs.他们对在工厂做工人或者干体力活都没有意见。

ADJ
用手的;手工的
Manual is used to talk about movements which are made by someone's hands.
例：...toys designed to help develop manual dexterity.用来培养手的灵活性的玩具

ADJ
手动的;用手操作的
Manual means operated by hand, rather than by electricity or a motor.
例：There is a manual pump to get rid of the water.有一只手摇水泵用来排水。

N-COUNT
使用手册;说明书
A manual is a book which tells you how to do something or how a piece of machinery works.
例：...the instruction manual.使用说明书

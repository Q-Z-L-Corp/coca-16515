swap 
英[swɒp] 美[swɑp]
 
n.交换；交换物，被掉换者；vi.交换（工作）；vt.用…替换，把…换成，掉换（过来）；
 "

V-RECIP
交换
If you swap something with someone, you give it to them and receive a different thing in exchange.
例：Next week they will swap places and will repeat the switch weekly...下周他们将交换座位，并且以后每周换一次。
例：I'd gladly swap places with mummy any day...什么时候让我和妈妈交换角色我都愿意。

VERB
用…替换;把…换成;停下(手头的事)改做
If you swap one thing for another, you remove the first thing and replace it with the second, or you stop doing the first thing and start doing the second.
例：Despite the heat, he'd swapped his overalls for a suit and tie...尽管很热，他还是脱掉工作服换上了西装和领带。
例：He has swapped his hectic rock star's lifestyle for that of a country gentleman...他一改摇滚歌星紧张忙碌的生活方式，开始过起了乡村绅士的生活。

VERB
交流，交换(意见);互相讲述(故事)
If you swap stories or opinions with someone, you tell each other stories or give each other your opinions.
例：They all sat together at table, laughing and swapping stories.他们都坐在桌子旁，有说有笑地讲述各自的故事。

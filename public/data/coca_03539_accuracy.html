精确（性）；准确（性）
The accuracy of information or measurements is their quality of being true or correct, even in small details.
例：We cannot guarantee the accuracy of these figures.我们不能保证这些数据的准确性。

N-UNCOUNT
准确无误
If someone or something performs a task, for example hitting a target, with accuracy, they do it in an exact way without making a mistake.
例：...weapons that could fire with accuracy at targets 3,000 yards away...能准确无误地击中3,000码以外目标的武器
例：Every bank pays close attention to the speed and accuracy of its staff.每家银行都非常关注雇员的工作速度和无差错率。

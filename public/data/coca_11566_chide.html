VERB
斥责;呵斥;指责
If you chide someone, you speak to them angrily because they have done something wicked or foolish.
例：Cross chided himself for worrying...克罗斯怨怪自己瞎操心。
例：He gently chided the two women.他温和地责备了那两名女子。

differential 
英[ˌdɪfəˈrenʃl] 美[ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl]
 
adj.差别的；特意的；[数]微分的；[机，物]差动的；n.[数]微分；差别；工资级差；[机]差动器；
 "

N-COUNT
(数学中的)微分;(经济学中的)差价，差额
In mathematics and economics, a differential is a difference between two values in a scale.
例：Germany and France pledged to maintain the differential between their two currencies.德国和法国承诺保持两国货币之间的差别汇率。

N-COUNT
差别;（尤指）工资级差
A differential is a difference between things, especially rates of pay.
例：During the Second World War, industrial wage differentials in Britain widened.第二次世界大战期间，英国的劳工工资级差进一步拉大了。

ADJ
差别的;级差的;依差别而定的
Differential means relating to or using a difference between groups or things.
例：...differential voting rights.差别投票

N-COUNT
信条;原则;纲领
A creed is a set of beliefs, principles, or opinions that strongly influence the way people live or work.
例：...their devotion to their creed of self-help.他们坚信自力更生的原则

N-COUNT
宗教
A creed is a religion.
例：The centre is open to all, no matter what race or creed.该中心向所有人开放，不论种族和宗教信仰如何。

N-COUNT
(长篇)小说
A novel is a long written story about imaginary people and events.
例：...a novel by Herman Hesse...一部赫尔曼·黑塞的小说
例：...historical novels set in the time of the Pharaohs.以法老时代为背景的历史小说

ADJ-GRADED
新的;新奇的;新颖的
Novel things are new and different from anything that has been done, experienced, or made before.
例：Protesters found a novel way of demonstrating against steeply rising oil prices...抗议者找到了抗议油价飞涨的新办法。
例：The very idea of a sixth form college was novel in 1962.第六学级学院在1962年还是新提法。

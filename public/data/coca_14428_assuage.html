assuage 
英[əˈsweɪdʒ] 美[əˈswedʒ]
 
vt.减轻；缓和；平息；使安静；n.缓和，减轻；减轻…的人；
 "

VERB
缓和，减轻（不快）
If you assuage an unpleasant feeling that someone has, you make them feel it less strongly.
例：To assuage his wife's grief, he took her on a tour of Europe...为了减轻妻子的悲痛，他带她去了欧洲旅游。
例：She was just trying to assuage her guilt by playing the devoted mother.她装出一副慈母的样子只是想要减轻自己的罪恶感。

VERB
满足（需要、欲望）
If you assuage a need or desire for something, you satisfy it.
例：The meat they'd managed to procure assuaged their hunger.他们把搞到手的肉拿来充饥。

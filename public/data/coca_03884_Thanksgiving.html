感恩节(美国的公共假日，在 11 月的第四个星期四)
In the United States, Thanksgiving or Thanksgiving Day is a public holiday on the fourth Thursday in November. It was originally a day when people celebrated the end of the harvest and thanked God for it.
例：No matter where his business took him, he always managed to be home for Thanksgiving...不管因生意关系去了什么地方，他总要赶回家过感恩节。
例：It's going to be the most magnificent Thanksgiving dinner we ever had.这将是我们参加过的最盛大的感恩节晚餐。

autonomy 
英[ɔ:ˈtɒnəmi] 美[ɔˈtɑnəmi]
 
n.自治，自治权；自主权；自治国，社区，或集团等；人身自由；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
自治
Autonomy is the control or government of a country, organization, or group by itself rather than by others.
例：Activists stepped up their demands for local autonomy last month.上个月激进分子对地方自治的呼声更高了。

N-UNCOUNT
独立自主
Autonomy is the ability to make your own decisions about what to do rather than being influenced by someone else or told what to do.
例：Each of the area managers enjoys considerable autonomy in the running of his own area.每个区域经理在他们各自负责的地区的运营上都享有高度的自主权。

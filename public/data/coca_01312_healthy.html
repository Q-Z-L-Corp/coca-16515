healthy 
英[ˈhelθi] 美[ˈhɛlθi]
 
adj.健康的；健全的；大量的；有益于健康的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
健康的;健壮的
Someone who is healthy is well and is not suffering from any illness.
例：Most of us need to lead more balanced lives to be healthy and happy...我们中大多数人需要过更为平衡的生活，以保持健康和快乐。
例：She had a normal pregnancy and delivered a healthy child.她正常妊娠，生了个健康的孩子。

ADJ-GRADED
显示健康的;反映健康的
If a feature or quality that you have is healthy, it makes you look well or shows that you are well.
例：...the glow of healthy skin.健康的肤色
例：...young adults with healthy appetites.食欲旺盛的年轻人

ADJ-GRADED
有益于健康的;促进健康的
Something that is healthy is good for your health.
例：...a great healthy outdoor pursuit.对健康大有裨益的室外活动
例：...a healthy diet.有益于健康的饮食

ADJ-GRADED
（机构或体系）兴旺的，运作良好的
A healthy organization or system is successful.
例：...an economically healthy socialist state.经济繁荣的社会主义国家

ADJ-GRADED
大量的;可观的
A healthy amount of something is a large amount that shows success.
例：He predicts a continuation of healthy profits in the current financial year.他预计本财年能继续保持可观的利润。
例：...a healthy bank account.巨额账户

ADJ-GRADED
明智的;聪明的;合理的
If you have a healthy attitude about something, you show good sense.
例：She has a refreshingly healthy attitude to work...她对工作持一种明智态度，令人耳目一新。
例：It's very healthy to be afraid when there's something to be afraid of.不逞匹夫之勇是很明智的。

 "

ADJ
运动的;体育的;运动员的
Athletic means relating to athletes and athletics.
例：They have been given college scholarships purely on athletic ability...他们完全是凭体育特长才获得了大学奖学金。
例：Most athletic activities are about individual effort.大多数体育项目是靠个人的拼搏。

ADJ-GRADED
运动型的；身体健壮的
An athletic person is fit, and able to perform energetic movements easily.
例：Xandra is an athletic 36-year-old with a 21-year-old's body...36岁的汉德拉身体健壮，有着21岁小伙子的体能。
例：He was tall, with an athletic build.他高大健壮。

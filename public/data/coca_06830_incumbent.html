adj.在职的；义不容辞的；靠在[压在]上面的；弯垂下来的；n.在职者；教会中的任职者；〈英〉教区牧师；
 "

N-COUNT
现任官员;在职官员
An incumbent is someone who holds an official post at a particular time.
例：In general, incumbents have a 94 per cent chance of being reelected.通常现任官员有 94% 的几率会再次当选。

ADJ
成为责任的;义不容辞的;必须履行的
If it is incumbent upon you to do something, it is your duty or responsibility to do it.
例：It is incumbent upon all of us to make an extra effort.我们所有人都必须加倍努力。

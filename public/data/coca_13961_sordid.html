If you describe someone's behaviour as sordid, you mean that it is immoral or dishonest.
例：He sat with his head buried in his hands as his sordid double life was revealed...自己卑鄙的双重生活被揭穿后，他双手抱头坐在那里。
例：I don't want to hear the sordid details of your relationship with Sandra...我不想听你和桑德拉之间的那些龌龊细节。

(地方)肮脏的，令人不快的，令人沮丧的

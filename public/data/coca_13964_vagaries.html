n.奇想( vagary的名词复数 )；异想天开；异常行为；难以预测的情况；

N-COUNT
出乎意料的变化;难以预测的转变
Vagaries are unexpected and unpredictable changes in a situation or in someone's behaviour which you have no control over.
例：I take an assortment of clothes on holiday, as a provision against the vagaries of the weather.我带了各种衣服去度假，以防天气突变。
例：...the perplexing vagaries of politics.复杂难解、变幻莫测的政坛风云

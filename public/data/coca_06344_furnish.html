furnish 
英[ˈfɜ:nɪʃ] 美[ˈfɜrnɪʃ]
 
vt.陈设，布置；提供，供应；装修（房屋）；
 "

VERB
布置;为(房间或建筑物)配备家具陈设
If you furnish a room or building, you put furniture and furnishings into it.
例：Many proprietors try to furnish their hotels with antiques.许多经营者都想用古董装饰他们的酒店。

VERB
提供;供应
If you furnish someone with something, you provide or supply it.
例：They'll be able to furnish you with the rest of the details.他们将可向你提供其余的细节。

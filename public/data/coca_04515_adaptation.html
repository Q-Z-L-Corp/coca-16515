例：...Branagh's screen adaptation of Shakespeare's Henry the Fifth.布拉纳改编自莎士比亚的《亨利五世》的影片

Adaptation is the act of changing something or changing your behaviour to make it suitable for a new purpose or situation.
例：Most living creatures are capable of adaptation when compelled to do so.大多数生物都能在迫于压力的情况下适应新环境。

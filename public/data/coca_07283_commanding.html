commanding 
英[kəˈmɑ:ndɪŋ] 美[kəˈmændɪŋ]
 
adj.指挥的；威严的；居高临下的；v.指挥，控制( command的现在分词)；命令；掌握；俯瞰；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
指挥的;统帅的
If you are in a commanding position or situation, you are in a strong or powerful position or situation.
例：Right now you're in a more commanding position than you have been for ages...现在你的权力超过了多年以来任何时候。
例：The French vessel has a commanding lead.法国舰艇遥遥领先。

ADJ-GRADED
威严的;权威的
If you describe someone as commanding, you mean that they are powerful and confident.
例：Lovett was a tall, commanding man with a waxed gray mustache...洛维特是个威严的高个男人，蓄着灰白的八字胡。
例：The voice at the other end of the line was serious and commanding.电话另一端传来庄重而威严的声音。

ADJ-GRADED
居高临下的;视野开阔的
If a building has a commanding position, it is high up and has good views of the surrounding area.
例：The size of the castle and its commanding position still impress the visitor today...城堡很大，加上其居高临下的位置，至今仍让参观者赞叹不已。
例：What other home offers such a commanding view of the capital?还有其他的房子能像这幢一样俯瞰首都吗？

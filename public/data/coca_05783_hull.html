hull 
英[hʌl] 美[hʌl]
 
n.果皮，外皮；[航海]船壳，船体；外壳；vt.去（谷物、豆等的）壳；
 "

N-COUNT
船体;（坦克的）车身
The hull of a boat or tank is the main body of it.
例：The hull had suffered extensive damage to the starboard side.船体右舷遭到重创。

N-COUNT
（草莓等浆果的）花萼
The hull of a soft fruit such as a strawberry is the stalk and ring of leaves at the base.

VERB
除去（草莓等浆果）的花萼
If you hull soft fruit such as strawberries, you remove the hulls.
例：Wash and hull the strawberries.洗一下草莓，并摘掉花萼。

N-COUNT
（尤指意大利城镇中的）广场
A piazza is a large open square in a town or city, especially in Italy.
例：They were seated at a table outside a pub in a pleasant piazza close by St Paul's...在圣保罗教堂旁一个怡人的广场上，他们坐在一家酒馆外的桌边。
例：Turn south at Mulinello to reach Piazza Armerina.在穆利内洛转向南，去往皮亚扎—阿尔梅里纳。

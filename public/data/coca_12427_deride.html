VERB
嘲弄;嘲笑;侮蔑
If you deride someone or something, you say that they are stupid or have no value.
例：Opposition MPs derided the Government's response to the crisis...反对党议员嘲弄政府针对危机作出的反应。
例：This theory is widely derided by conventional scientists.这一理论遭到了守旧派科学家的普遍嘲笑。

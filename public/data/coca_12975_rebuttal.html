N-COUNT
辩驳；反驳；回击
If you make a rebuttal of a charge or accusation that has been made against you, you make a statement which gives reasons why the accusation is untrue.
例：He is conducting a point-by-point rebuttal of charges from former colleagues...他正在对前同事的指责进行逐条反驳。
例：Pakistan has still not issued an official rebuttal to the latest Indian statements.巴基斯坦仍然没有对最近印度发表的声明进行官方驳斥。

borderline 
英[ˈbɔ:dəlaɪn] 美[ˈbɔrdərlaɪn]
 
n.分界线，国界线；adj.边界上的；边界附近的；暧昧的；不明确的；
 "

N-COUNT
（两种不同或相反事物之间的）分界线
The borderline between two different or opposite things is the division between them.
例：...a task which involves exploring the borderline between painting and photography.一项探究绘画与摄影之间界线的工作

ADJ-GRADED
勉强可以的；刚刚够格的
Something that is borderline is only just acceptable as a member of a class or group.
例：Some were obviously unsuitable and could be ruled out at once. Others were borderline cases.一些明显不合适，可以马上排除。另一些则勉强合格。

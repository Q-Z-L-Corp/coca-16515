VERB
大批杀死;大量毁灭
To decimate something such as a group of people or animals means to destroy a very large number of them.
例：The pollution could decimate the river's thriving population of kingfishers...污染可能会造成河边大量繁殖的翠鸟大批死亡。
例：British forces in the Caribbean were being decimated by disease.加勒比海地区的英国部队有大批的人病死。

VERB
严重削弱，大幅缩减(系统或机构)
To decimate a system or organization means to reduce its size and effectiveness greatly.
例：...a recession which decimated the nation's manufacturing industry.使该国制造业元气大伤的经济衰退

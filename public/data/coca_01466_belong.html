belong 
英[bɪˈlɒŋ] 美[bɪˈlɔŋ]
 
vi.属于；是…的成员；应被放在；适应；
 "

VERB
属于；归…所有
If something belongs to you, you own it.
例：The house had belonged to her family for three or four generations.这套房子归她们家有三四代人了。

VERB
与…有关；是…的一部分
You say that something belongs to a particular person when you are guessing, discovering, or explaining that it was produced by or is part of that person.
例：The handwriting belongs to a male...这是男性的笔迹。
例：They established that the body belonged to a 15-year-old girl.他们确定这是一具15岁女孩的尸体。

VERB
是…的成员；属于（某团体）
If someone belongs to a particular group, they are a member of that group.
例：I used to belong to a youth club.我过去是一个青年俱乐部的成员。

VERB
属于（某个类别、类型或群体）
If something or someone belongs in or to a particular category, type, or group, they are of that category, type, or group.
例：The judges could not decide which category it belonged in...评委们无法判定它属于哪一类。
例：I realized that he and I belonged to different worlds.我意识到我和他不是一路人。

VERB
源于，来自于（某个时代）
If something belongs to a particular time, it comes from that time.
例：The pictures belong to an era when there was a preoccupation with high society.这些画出自一个人人向往上流社会的时代。

VERB
（权利）属于（某人）
If you say that something belongs to someone, you mean that person has the right to it.
例：...but the last word belonged to Rosanne.但是最终决定权在罗莎娜手里

VERB
以…为特点；以…为特征
If you say that a time belongs to a particular system or way of doing something, you mean that that time is or will be characterized by it.
例：The future belongs to democracy.未来属于民主。

VERB
（孩子）是…的，为…所拥有（或监护）
If a baby or child belongs to a particular adult, that adult is his or her parent or the person who is looking after him or her.
例：He deduced that the two children belonged to the couple.他推断这俩小孩是这对夫妇的。

V-RECIP
属于彼此（恋人用来表示亲密关系）
When lovers say that they belong together, they are expressing their closeness or commitment to each other.
例：I really think that we belong together...我真的觉得我们属于彼此。
例：He belongs with me.他是我的。

VERB
归属于（某地或某一情形）
If a person or thing belongs in a particular place or situation, that is where they should be.
例：You don't belong here...你不属于这里。
例：This piece really belongs in the concert hall...这曲子很适合在音乐厅演奏。

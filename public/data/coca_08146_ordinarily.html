ADV
通常；正常情况下
If you say what is ordinarily the case, you are saying what is normally the case.
例：The streets would ordinarily have been full of people. There was no one...街上通常应该人满为患，当时却空无一人。
例：Similar arrangements apply to students who are ordinarily resident in Scotland.类似的安排适用于长期居住在苏格兰的学生。

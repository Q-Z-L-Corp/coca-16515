ADJ-GRADED
洪亮的;嘹亮的
A sound that is resonant is deep and strong.
例：His voice sounded oddly resonant in the empty room...他的声音在这空荡荡的房间里听起来异常嘹亮。
例：He responded with a resonant laugh.他报以洪亮的笑声。

ADJ
有特殊意义的;引起共鸣的
Something that is resonant has a special meaning or is particularly important to people.
例：It is a country resonant with cinematic potential, from its architecture to its landscape.从建筑到风景，这个国家到处都体现出其成为电影拍摄地的潜力。

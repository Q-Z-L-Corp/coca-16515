horrid 
英[ˈhɒrɪd] 美[ˈhɔrɪd]
 
adj.引起反感的；可怕的；恐怖的；极讨厌的；

ADJ-GRADED
极讨厌的;极糟的
If you describe something as horrid, you mean that it is very unpleasant indeed.
例：What a horrid smell!怎么这么臭！
例：The winter was horrid.冬天天气极糟。

ADJ-GRADED
（人）招人讨厌的，使人极不愉快的
If you describe someone as horrid, you mean that they behave in a very unpleasant way towards other people.
例：I must have been a horrid little girl...我以前一定是个招人讨厌的小女孩。
例：I love both my parents, but they're horrid to each other.我爱我的父母，但他们彼此之间极为不和。

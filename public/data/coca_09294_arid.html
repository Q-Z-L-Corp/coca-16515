英[ˈærɪd] 美[ˈærɪd]
 
adj.干旱的，干燥的；贫瘠的，荒芜的，不毛的；枯燥无味的，无趣的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
干旱的;不毛的
Arid land is so dry that very few plants can grow on it.
例：...new strains of crops that can withstand arid conditions.能抵抗干旱条件的新品种作物
例：...the arid zones of the country.该国的干旱区域

ADJ-GRADED
枯燥无味的；乏善可陈的
If you describe something such as a period of your life or an academic subject as arid, you mean that it has so little interest, excitement, or purpose that it makes you feel bored or unhappy.
例：She had given him the only joy his arid life had ever known.她为他枯燥无味的生活带来了从未有过的乐趣。
例：...the politically arid years of military dictatorship in the 1960s and '70s.在20世纪60和70年代军事独裁期间政事荒芜的岁月

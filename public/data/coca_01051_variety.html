variety 
英[vəˈraɪəti] 美[vəˈraɪɪti]
 
n.多样；种类；杂耍；变化，多样化；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
变化;多样化
If something has variety, it consists of things which are different from each other.
例：Susan's idea of freedom was to have variety in her life style...苏珊心目中的自由就是拥有丰富多彩的生活。
例：I know no store anywhere in the world that has such variety and display...我不知道世界上还有哪个商店的经营品种如此繁多，陈列如此美观。

N-SING
各式各样;多种多样
A variety of things is a number of different kinds or examples of the same thing.
例：West Hampstead has a variety of good shops and supermarkets...西汉普斯特德有各种各样不错的店铺和超级市场。
例：The island offers such a wide variety of scenery and wildlife...这个岛屿拥有丰富多样的景致及种类繁多的野生动植物。

N-COUNT
品种;种类
A variety of something is a type of it.
例：I'm always pleased to try out a new variety...我一直乐于尝试新产品。
例：She has 12 varieties of old-fashioned roses.她有12种双花玫瑰。

N-UNCOUNT
综艺表演;综艺节目
Variety is a type of entertainment which includes many different kinds of acts in the same show.
例：...a variety show of music, comedy, and magic.汇集了音乐、喜剧及魔术表演的一档综艺节目

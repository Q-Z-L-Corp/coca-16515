protector

N-COUNT
保护人
If you refer to someone as your protector, you mean that they protect you from being harmed.
例：Many mothers see their son as a potential protector and provider.许多母亲将儿子看成是未来的保护人和赡养人。

N-COUNT
保护装置
A protector is a device that protects someone or something from physical harm.
例：He was the only National League umpire to wear an outside chest protector.他是唯一在衣服外面穿护胸的全国联赛裁判。

crouch 
英[kraʊtʃ] 美[kraʊtʃ]
 
vi.屈膝，蹲伏；蜷伏；卑躬屈膝；vt.低头，屈膝；n.蹲着的姿势；
 "

VERB
蹲;蹲伏
If you are crouching, your legs are bent under you so that you are close to the ground and leaning forward slightly.
例：We were crouching in the bushes...我们蹲在树丛里。
例：I crouched on the ground...我蹲在地上。

VERB
俯身接近
If you crouch over something, you bend over it so that you are very near to it.
例：Meantime, here I crouch over a cup of tea in my unheated study.与此同时，在没有暖气的书房里，我只能就着一杯茶取暖。
例：...sitting crouched over the steering wheel.俯身坐在方向盘前

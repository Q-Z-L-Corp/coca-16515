ADJ
左手边的；左边的；左侧的
If something is on the left-hand side of something, it is positioned on the left of it.
例：They drive on the left-hand side of the road...他们靠公路左边行驶。
例：The keys are in the back left-hand corner of the drawer.钥匙放在抽屉里面左边一角。

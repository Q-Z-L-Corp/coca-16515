spectacular 
英[spekˈtækjələ(r)] 美[spɛkˈtækjəlɚ]
 
adj.场面富丽的，壮观的；引人注意的；惊动一时的；惊人的；n.壮观的场面，精彩的表演；〈美〉一小时半以上的大场面电视节目；特大霓虹灯广告；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
引人注目的;惊人的;壮观的
Something that is spectacular is very impressive or dramatic.
例：...spectacular views of the Sugar Loaf Mountain...甜面包山的壮观景色
例：They have revamped the business with spectacular success...他们成功地调整了业务，成绩斐然。

N-COUNT
壮观的表演;盛大的演出
A spectacular is a show or performance which is very grand and impressive.
例：...a television spectacular.一场电视盛宴
例：...one of the world's great sporting spectaculars.世界上最壮观的体育盛事之一

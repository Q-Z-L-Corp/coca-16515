英[ˈlɑ:dʒli] 美[ˈlɑrdʒli]

 "

ADV-GRADED
大致上；总体上；一般地说
You use largely to say that a statement is not completely true but is mostly true.
例：The fund is largely financed through government borrowing...该基金大致上由政府借款支持。
例：I largely work with people who already are motivated...我大多和积极主动的人一起工作。

ADV
（用于说明原因）主要地
Largely is used to introduce the main reason for a particular event or situation.
例：Retail sales dipped 6/10ths of a percent last month, largely because Americans were buying fewer cars...上月零售额下降了千分之六，主要是因为美国人买汽车少了。
例：The French empire had expanded largely through military conquest.法兰西帝国的扩张主要靠军事征服。

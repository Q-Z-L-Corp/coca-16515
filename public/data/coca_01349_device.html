device 
英[dɪˈvaɪs] 美[dɪˈvaɪs]
 
n.装置，设备；方法；策略；手段；
 "

N-COUNT
装置;设备;器具;仪器
A device is an object that has been invented for a particular purpose, for example for recording or measuring something.
例：...an electronic device that protects your vehicle 24 hours a day.24小时保护车辆的电子装置
例：...a device that could measure minute quantities of matter...能够测量微量物质的设备

N-COUNT
手段;策略;方法;技巧
A device is a method of achieving something.
例：They claim that military spending is used as a device for managing the economy.他们声称军费开支是用来作为调控经济的手段的。
例：...the literary device of the metaphor.隐喻的文学手法

PHRASE
听任…自便;让…自行发展;不去管
If you leave someone to their own devices, you leave them alone to do as they wish.
例：Left to his own devices, Osborn is a fluent — and often original — guitarist.任其自由发挥时，奥斯本是一个弹奏流畅、常常富有创意的吉他手。

management

N-UNCOUNT
（商务或机构的）管理，经营
Management is the control and organizing of a business or other organization.
例：The zoo needed better management rather than more money...这座动物园需要更好的管理，而不是更多的资金。
例：The dispute is about wages, working conditions and the management of the mining industry.这场争论围绕着工资、工作条件以及矿业管理展开。

N-VAR-COLL
资方;管理部门;经营者
You can refer to the people who control and organize a business or other organization as the management .
例：The management is doing its best to improve the situation...管理部门正在竭尽全力改善局面。
例：We need to get more women into top management...我们需要让更多的女性进入高层管理。

N-UNCOUNT
管理;安排;驾驭
Management is the way people control different parts of their lives.
例：...her management of her professional life.她对自己职业生涯的规划
例：...intelligent money management, for example paying big bills monthly where possible.聪明的理财手法，例如尽量按月支付大宗的账单

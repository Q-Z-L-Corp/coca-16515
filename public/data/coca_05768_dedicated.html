dedicated 
英[ˈdedɪkeɪtɪd] 美[ˈdɛdɪˌketɪd]
 
adj.专注的，投入的；献身的；专用的；v.奉献(dedicate的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
献身的;一心一意的;热忱的
You use dedicated to describe someone who enjoys a particular activity very much and spends a lot of time doing it.
例：Her great-grandfather had clearly been a dedicated and stoical traveller.她的曾祖父显然是一位非常投入而且坚忍不拔的旅行爱好者。
例：...dedicated followers of classical music.古典音乐的忠实追随者

ADJ
专用（于…）的;专门（为…而）设计制造的
You use dedicated to describe something that is made, built, or designed for one particular purpose or thing.
例：Such areas should also be served by dedicated cycle routes.这些区域也应该设专门的自行车道。
例：...the world's first museum dedicated to ecology.世界上第一个专门的生态博物馆

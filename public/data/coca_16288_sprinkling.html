N-SING
(尤指分布在较大面积上的)少量，少许
A sprinkling of something is a small quantity or amount of it, especially if it is spread over a large area.
例：...a light sprinkling of snow...薄薄的一层雪
例：Norway has a fair sprinkling of women ministers.挪威有好几位女部长。

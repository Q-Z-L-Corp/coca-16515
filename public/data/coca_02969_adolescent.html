ADJ
青少年的;青春期的
Adolescent is used to describe young people who are no longer children but who have not yet become adults. It also refers to their behaviour.
例：It is important that an adolescent boy should have an adult in whom he can confide...关键是，青春期的男孩身边应该有一个可以交心的成年人。
例：He spent his adolescent years playing guitar in the church band.他在教堂的乐队里弹吉他，度过了他的青少年时期。

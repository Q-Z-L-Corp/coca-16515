demanding 
英[dɪˈmɑ:ndɪŋ] 美[dɪˈmændɪŋ]
 
adj.要求高的；苛求的；强求的；费力的，吃力的；v.要求(demand的ing形式)；查问；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
费时的;费力的;劳神的;要求很高的
A demanding job or task requires a lot of your time, energy, or attention.
例：He found he could no longer cope with his demanding job...他发现自己已无力应对那份劳神费力的工作了。
例：It is a demanding role and she needs to work hard at it.那是一个要求很高的角色，她必须要加倍努力。

ADJ-GRADED
难伺候的;难取悦的;挑剔的;苛刻的
People who are demanding are not easily satisfied or pleased.
例：Ricky was a very demanding child...里基是个非常难伺候的孩子。
例：Her boss was very demanding but appreciative of Christina's talents.克里斯蒂娜的老板非常苛刻，但同时也很欣赏她的才华。

VERB
使用,挥,操（武器、工具等）
If you wield a weapon, tool, or piece of equipment, you carry and use it.
例：...a lone assailant wielding a kitchen knife.孤身一人、手持菜刀的袭击者

VERB
掌握，运用，行使(权利)
If someone wields power, they have it and are able to use it.
例：He remains chairman, but wields little power at the company.他还是主席，但在公司没有什么实权了。

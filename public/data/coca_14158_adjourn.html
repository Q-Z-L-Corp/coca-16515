英[əˈdʒɜ:n] 美[əˈdʒɜrn]
 
vt.& vi.（使）休会，（使）休庭；
 "

V-ERG
休（会）；休（庭）
If a meeting or trial is adjourned or if it adjourns, it is stopped for a short time.
例：The proceedings have now been adjourned until next week...会议休会至下周重开。
例：I am afraid the court may not adjourn until three or even later.我担心法庭要到3点或更晚时才会休庭。

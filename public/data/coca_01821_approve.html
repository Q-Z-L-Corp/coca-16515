approve 
英[əˈpru:v] 美[əˈpruv]
 
vt.& vi.赞成，同意；vt.批准；认可；核准；为…提供证据；vi.赞同；称许；称赞；满意、喜欢(of)；
 "

VERB
赞成；同意
If you approve of an action, event, or suggestion, you like it or are pleased about it.
例：Not everyone approves of the festival...不是所有人都赞成庆祝该节日。
例：I approved of the proposal.我赞成该提案。

VERB
赞许;嘉许;喜欢
If you approve of someone or something, you like and admire them.
例：You've never approved of Henry, have you?...你从来没有喜欢过亨利，对吗？
例：I didn't approve of his manner.我不喜欢他的态度。

VERB
批准；认可；通过
If someone in a position of authority approves a plan or idea, they formally agree to it and say that it can happen.
例：The Russian Parliament has approved a program of radical economic reforms...俄罗斯议会已经通过一项重大经济改革方案。
例：MPs approved the Bill by a majority of 97.议员们以97票的多数票通过了该议案。

VERB
批准；核准
If a product or person is approved by an official organization, they are declared to be of a good enough standard to be used or employed.
例：We have three suppliers in all who are approved by the Organic Farm Food Association.我们总共有3家获有机农产品协会认可的供货商。

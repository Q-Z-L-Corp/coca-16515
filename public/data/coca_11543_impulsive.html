ADJ-GRADED
易冲动的;草率的
If you describe someone as impulsive, you mean that they do things suddenly without thinking about them carefully first.
例：He is too impulsive to be a responsible prime minister...他容易冲动，很难成为值得依赖的首相。
例：Avoid making an impulsive decision.避免作出草率冲动的决定。

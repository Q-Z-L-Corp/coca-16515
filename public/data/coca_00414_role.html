role 
英[rəʊl] 美[roʊl]
 
n.作用；地位；角色；
 "

N-COUNT
作用;地位;职位;角色
If you have a role in a situation or in society, you have a particular position and function in it.
例：...the drug's role in preventing more serious effects of infection...这种药物在防止感染加重方面的作用
例：Both sides have roles to play.双方都各有自己的角色。

N-COUNT
(电影、戏剧或歌剧中的)角色
A role is one of the characters that an actor or singer can play in a film, play, or opera.
例：She has just landed the lead role in The Young Vic's latest production...她刚刚获得在小维克剧院上演的最新作品中出演女主角的机会。
例：Shakespearean women's roles were originally written to be played by men.莎士比亚笔下的女性角色在创作之初是打算由男性来扮演的。

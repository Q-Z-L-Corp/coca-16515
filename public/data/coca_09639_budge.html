budge 
英[bʌdʒ] 美[bʌdʒ]
 
vt.& vi.（使）稍微移动；（使）改变主意，（使）让步；（立场等）动摇；推动；n.（毛向外翻的）羔羊皮；革囊；〈美方〉酒；adj.用羔羊皮装饰的；<古>浮夸的；自负的；庄严的；
 "

V-ERG
（使）改变主意；（使）让步
If someone will not budge on a matter, or if nothing budges them, they refuse to change their mind or to come to an agreement.
例：Both sides say they will not budge...双方都声称决不让步。
例：The Americans are adamant that they will not budge on this point...美国人非常强硬，他们在这一点上是不会妥协的。

V-ERG
（使）移动；挪动
If someone or something will not budge, they will not move. If you cannot budge them, you cannot make them move.
例：Her mother refused to budge from London...她母亲不肯离开伦敦。
例：The window refused to budge...窗户推不开。

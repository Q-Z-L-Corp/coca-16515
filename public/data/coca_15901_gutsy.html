ADJ-GRADED
勇敢的；有胆量的；有决心的
If you describe someone as gutsy, you mean they show courage or determination.
例：I've always been drawn to tough, gutsy women...我总是为果敢坚毅的女性所吸引。
例：They admired his gutsy and emotional speech.他们很欣赏他信心十足、充满激情的演讲。

ADJ-GRADED
强劲的；热烈的；有趣的
If you describe something as gutsy, you mean that it is powerful and interesting.
例：...the rich, gutsy flavours of mature autumn vegetables...秋天成熟蔬菜的浓郁味道
例：'The Commitments' is the gutsy story of a Dublin rock band.《追梦者》这部影片讲述了都柏林一个摇滚乐队的激荡人心的故事。

proliferation 
英[prəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃn] 美[prəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃn]
 
n.增殖，分芽繁殖；再育；增生；
 "

VERB
激增；剧增
If things proliferate, they increase in number very quickly.
例：Computerized data bases are proliferating fast...计算机化的数据库正在激增。
例：In recent years commercial, cultural, travel and other contacts have proliferated between Europe and China.近年来，欧洲和中国之间的商业、文化、旅游及其他交往发展迅猛。

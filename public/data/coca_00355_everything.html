everything 
英[ˈevriθɪŋ] 美[ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ]
 
pron.每件事物；最重要的东西；（有关的）一切；万事；
 "

PRON-INDEF
所有事物;一切
You use everything to refer to all the objects, actions, activities, or facts in a particular situation.
例：He'd gone to Seattle long after everything else in his life had changed...他生活中的其他一切都改变了，很久之后他去了西雅图。
例：Early in the morning, hikers pack everything that they will need for the day's hike...一大早，徒步旅行者们把当天旅行所需的一切都装进包里。

PRON-INDEF
（可能的）一切事物，所有事物
You use everything to refer to all possible or likely actions, activities, or situations.
例：'This should have been decided long before now.' —'We can't think of everything.'...“这个在此之前早该决定了。”——“我们不可能考虑得面面俱到。”
例：Cathy thought that she had the answer to everything...凯茜觉得她能够处理一切。

PRON-INDEF
事态;情况;一切;生活
You use everything to refer to a whole situation or to life in general.
例：She says everything is going smoothly...她说一切进展顺利。
例：Is everything all right?...一切都好吗？

PRON-INDEF
（生命中）最重要的东西，最有价值的东西
If you say that someone or something is everything, you mean you consider them to be the most important thing in your life, or the most important thing that there is.
例：I love him. He is everything to me...我爱他，他是我的一切。
例：Crime cases were something that agents solved, and to him the case was everything...犯罪案件是执法人员要解决的事情，对于他而言，案件就是一切。

PRON-INDEF
（大多数人渴望的）一切，所有
If you say that someone or something has everything, you mean they have all the things or qualities that most people consider to be desirable.
例：She has everything: beauty, talent, children...她拥有一切：美貌、才干和子女。
例：It was a garden that had everything. It was rich and wild and beautiful, and exciting.那个花园里一切尽有，景物丰富多彩、自然、美丽，令人激动。

PHRASE
以及其他;等等
You say 'and everything' after mentioning a particular thing or list of things to indicate that they are only examples and that other things are also involved.
例：He had a bed and a fireplace and everything...他家里有一张床、一个壁炉及其他物品。
例：We become friends and everything, and we call each other on the phone all the time.我们成了非常要好的朋友，一直互通电话。

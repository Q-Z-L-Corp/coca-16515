VERB
削(木头等)
If you whittle something from a piece of wood, you carve it by cutting pieces off the wood with a knife.
例：He whittled a new handle for his ax...他给斧头削了一个新把手。
例：Chitty sat in his rocking-chair whittling wood.奇蒂坐在摇椅上削着木头。

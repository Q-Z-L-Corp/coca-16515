recitation 
英[ˌresɪˈteɪʃn] 美[ˌrɛsɪˈteʃən]
 
n.朗诵；背诵；习题课；
 "

N-VAR
（大声）朗诵，背诵
When someone does a recitation, they say aloud a piece of poetry or other writing that they have learned.
例：The transmission began with a recitation from the Koran.节目一开始是一段《古兰经》朗诵。

N-COUNT
声明；陈述
A recitation of something is a statement of it.
例：The letter was short — a simple recitation of their problem.信写得很短——只是简单地说了一下他们的问题。

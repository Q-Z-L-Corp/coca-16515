VERB
报复;反击
If you retaliate when someone harms or annoys you, you do something which harms or annoys them in return.
例：I was sorely tempted to retaliate...我非常想报复。
例：Christie retaliated by sending his friend a long letter detailing Carl's utter incompetence...为了报复，克里斯蒂给他朋友寄了一封长信，细述卡尔完全不称职。

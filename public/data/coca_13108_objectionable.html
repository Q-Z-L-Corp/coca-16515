objectionable 
英[əbˈdʒekʃənəbl] 美[əbˈdʒɛkʃənəbəl]
 
adj.<正>令人不快的，令人反感的，讨厌的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人不快的;令人反感的;讨厌的
If you describe someone or something as objectionable, you consider them to be extremely offensive and unacceptable.
例：I don't like your tone young woman, in fact I find it highly objectionable...小姑娘，我不喜欢你说话的口气，实际上我觉得这让人非常不快。
例：Such power is politically dangerous and morally objectionable.这样的权力在政治上极其危险，在道义上也令人反感。

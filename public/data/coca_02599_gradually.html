ADV-GRADED
逐渐地；逐步地
If something changes or is done gradually, it changes or is done in small stages over a long period of time, rather than suddenly.
例：Electricity lines to 30,000 homes were gradually being restored yesterday...昨天，3万户家庭逐渐恢复了供电。
例：Gradually we learned to cope.我们逐渐学会了应对。

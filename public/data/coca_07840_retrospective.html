retrospective 
英[ˌretrəˈspektɪv] 美[ˌrɛtrəˈspɛktɪv]
 
adj.回顾的；怀旧的；[法]有追溯力的；可追溯的；n.回顾展；
 "

N-COUNT
（艺术家作品的）回顾展
A retrospective is an exhibition or showing of work done by an artist over many years, rather than his or her most recent work.
例：...a retrospective of the films of Judy Garland...朱迪·加兰电影回顾展
例：They honoured him with a retrospective exhibition in 1987.他们在1987年为他举办了一次回顾展，以示敬意。

ADJ
回顾的;回想的
Retrospective feelings or opinions concern things that happened in the past.
例：Afterwards, retrospective fear of the responsibility would make her feel almost faint.后来，回想起要负的责任她几乎会吓晕过去。

ADJ
(法律、法律行为等)有追溯效力的
Retrospective laws or legal actions take effect from a date before the date when they are officially approved.
例：Bankers are quick to condemn retrospective tax legislation.银行家们立即声讨有追溯效力的税收立法。

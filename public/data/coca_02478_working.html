working 
英[ˈwɜ:kɪŋ] 美[ˈwɜrkɪŋ]
 
adj.（涉及）工作的；（人）有工作的；（时间）用于工作上的；（想法等）可作为基础的；v.（使）工作( work的现在分词)；（使）运作；运转；（使）产生效果；n.工作；活动；制作；操纵；
 "

ADJ
有工作的;有职业的
Working people have jobs which they are paid to do.
例：Like working women anywhere, Asian women are buying convenience foods.像其他地方的职业女性一样，亚洲女性也购买方便食品。

ADJ
从事普通工作的
Working people are ordinary people who do not have professional or very highly paid jobs.
例：The needs and opinions of ordinary working people were ignored...普通劳动者的需求和意见遭到忽视。
例：One or two, in blue suits, might have been bank officials. Others were clearly working men.有一两个穿蓝色西装的人可能是银行职员，其他的显然都是普通工人。

ADJ
上班时间的;工作时间的
A working day or week is the amount of time during a normal day or week which you spend doing your job.
例：For doctors the working day often has no end...医生常常没有下班的时候。
例：Automation would bring a shorter, more flexible working week.自动化会使工作周缩短，也更为灵活。

ADJ
工作日的;上班的
A working day is a day on which people go to work.
例：The full effect will not be apparent until Tuesday, the first working day after the three day holiday weekend.周二是周末3天假期后的第一个工作日，到那时效果才会完全显现出来。

ADJ
（人生阶段）工作的，处于就业年龄的
Your working life is the period of your life in which you have a job or are of a suitable age to have a job.
例：He started his working life as a truck driver.他的工作生涯是从当卡车司机开始的。

ADJ
(某一地区的人口)就业的，处于就业年龄的
The working population of an area consists of all the people in that area who have a job or who are of a suitable age to have a job.
例：Almost 13 per cent of the working population is already unemployed.就业人口中有将近13%已失业。

ADJ
（条件或常规做法）工作的，工作上的
Working conditions or practices are ones which you have in your job.
例：The strikers are demanding higher pay and better working conditions.罢工者要求加薪并改善工作环境。

ADJ
（衣服）工作时穿的
Working clothes are designed for doing work in, and are intended to be practical rather than attractive.

ADJ
（关系）工作上的，同事间的
A working relationship is the relationship you have with someone when you work with them.
例：A working relationship turned into a very close friendship...同事关系变成了非常亲密的友谊。
例：The vice-president seems to have a good working relationship with the president.副总统似乎与总统有着良好的工作关系。

ADJ
(农场或企业)经营性的，营利性的
A working farm or business exists to do normal work and make a profit, and not only for tourists or as someone's hobby.

ADJ
(机器零部件)能运转的，用于操作的
The working parts of a machine are the parts which move and operate the machine, in contrast to the outer case or container in which they are enclosed.

ADJ
(模型)活动的，可操作的
A working model is one that has parts that move.

ADJ
(知识)够用的;（多数票）有效的
A working knowledge or majority is not very great, but is enough to be useful.
例：This book was designed in order to provide a working knowledge of finance and accounts...本书旨在提供金融和会计方面的实用知识。
例：Neither candidate won a working majority.两位候选人都没有赢得有效的多数票。

ADJ
(题目或定义)暂用的，暂定的
A working title or definition is one which you use when starting to make or do something, but which you are likely to change or improve.
例：His working title for the script was 'Trust the People'.他给这个剧本暂定的标题是《相信人民》。

N-PLURAL
(设备、组织或系统的)工作方式，运行方式
The workings of a piece of equipment, an organization, or a system are the ways in which it operates and the processes which are involved in it.
例：Neural networks are computer systems which mimic the workings of the brain...神经网络是模拟大脑工作方式的计算机系统。
例：The bill would give people the right to much more information about the workings of government.该法案将赋予人们了解更多有关政府运作方面信息的权利。

N-PLURAL
矿坑;巷道
You can use workings to refer to deep tunnels or holes which have been dug in the ground in order to remove coal, metal, or stone.
例：...housing which was built above old mine workings.修建在老矿坑上的房屋

rented 
英[ˈrentɪd] 美[]
 
adj.租用的，租金的；v.租用( rent的过去式和过去分词 )；出租（房屋等）；租借（房屋、土地、机器等）；撕碎( rend的过去式和过去分词 )；
 "

VERB
租来;租借
If you rent something, you regularly pay its owner a sum of money in order to be able to have it and use it yourself.
例：She rents a house with three other girls...她和其他3个女孩合租一套房子。
例：He left his hotel in a rented car.他开着一辆租来的车离开了酒店。

VERB
出租;租给
If you rent something to someone, you let them have it and use it in exchange for a sum of money which they pay you regularly.
例：She rented rooms to university students.她把房间租给了大学生。

N-VAR
租金;租用费
Rent is the amount of money that you pay regularly to use a house, flat, or piece of land.
例：She worked to pay the rent while I went to college...在我读大学期间，她打工挣钱来付租金。
例：Traders in Marble Arch are facing huge rent increases.大理石拱门的经销商们面临租金的大幅上涨。

Rent is the past tense and past participle of rend .

ADJ-GRADED
（通常指坏事）普遍的，盛行的，常见的，充斥的
If you say that something, usually something bad, is rife in a place or that the place is rife with it, you mean that it is very common.
例：Speculation is rife that he will be sacked...人们纷纷猜测他会被解雇。
例：Bribery and corruption were rife in the industry...在这个行业中，贿赂和腐败现象猖獗。

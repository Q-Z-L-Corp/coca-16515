hurl 
英[hɜ:l] 美[hɜrl]
 
vt.丢下，用力投掷；大声叫骂，愤慨的说出；推到，推翻；vi.猛投，猛掷；
 "

VERB
猛投;用力掷
If you hurl something, you throw it violently and with a lot of force.
例：Groups of angry youths hurled stones at police...成群结伙愤怒的年轻人朝警察投掷石块。
例：One prisoner set fire to rags and hurled them into the courtyard...一名囚犯将破布点着，扔进院子。

VERB
气势汹汹地吐（恶言）
If you hurl abuse or insults at someone, you shout insults at them aggressively.
例：How would you handle being locked in the back of a cab while the driver hurled abuse at you?如果你被锁在出租车后座上而且还遭到司机的无礼谩骂，你会怎么办？

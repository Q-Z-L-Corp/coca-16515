N-UNCOUNT
黄昏;傍晚;薄暮
Dusk is the time just before night when the daylight has almost gone but when it is not completely dark.
例：We arrived home at dusk.我们傍晚到家。

N-UNCOUNT
暮色
The dusk is the dull, shadowy light there is at dusk.
例：She turned and disappeared into the dusk.她转身消失在暮色中。

behaviour 
英[bɪˈheɪvjə(r)] 美[bɪˈhevjɚ]
 
n.行为；举止；（人、动植物、化学药品等的）表现方式；态度；na.“behavior”的变体；
 "

N-VAR
行为；举止
People's or animals' behaviour is the way that they behave. You can refer to a typical and repeated way of behaving as a behaviour .
例：Make sure that good behaviour is rewarded.确保懿行得到嘉奖。
例：...human sexual behaviour...人类性行为

N-UNCOUNT
（科学领域中的）运动方式，活动方式，性能，特点
In science, the behaviour of something is the way that it behaves.
例：It will be many years before anyone can predict a hurricane's behavior with much accuracy.要能够相当准确地预测出飓风的特性尚需多年。

PHRASE
极力表现；尽量做到举止得体
If someone is on their best behaviour, they are trying very hard to behave well.

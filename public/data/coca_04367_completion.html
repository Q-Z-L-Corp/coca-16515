completion 
英[kəmˈpli:ʃn] 美[kəmˈpliʃən]
 
n.完成，结束；实现；[数]求全法；期满；
 "

ADJ
完全的;十足的
You use complete to emphasize that something is as great in extent, degree, or amount as it possibly can be.
例：The rebels had taken complete control...叛乱分子完全控制了局面。
例：It shows a complete lack of understanding by management...这表明管理层对此完全不了解。

ADJ
完整的;全部的;整个的
You can use complete to emphasize that you are referring to the whole of something and not just part of it.
例：A complete tenement block was burnt to the ground...整幢公寓大楼被付之一炬。
例：The job sheets eventually filled a complete book.工作指导卡最终装满了整整一个册子。

ADJ
齐全的;完整的
If something is complete, it contains all the parts that it should contain.
例：The list may not be complete.这个单子可能不全。
例：...a complete dinner service...一整套餐具

VERB
使齐全;使完整
To complete a set or group means to provide the last item that is needed to make it a full set or group.
例：Children don't complete their set of 20 baby teeth until they are two to three years old.小孩要到两三岁才会长齐20颗乳牙。
例：...the stickers needed to complete the collection.集全一整套所需的贴纸

ADJ
(作品)全部的，完整的
The complete works of a writer are all their books or poems published together in one book or as a set of books.
例：...the Complete Works of William Shakespeare.《威廉·莎士比亚全集》

PREP-PHRASE
配有…的;兼有…的
If one thing comes complete with another, it has that thing as an extra or additional part.
例：The diary comes complete with a gold-coloured ballpoint pen.这个日记本配有一支金色的圆珠笔。

ADJ
完成的;结束的
If something is complete, it has been finished.
例：The work of restoring the farmhouse is complete...修复农舍的工作已经完成。
例：It'll be two years before the process is complete.这个过程需要两年才能完成。

VERB
结束;完成
If you complete something, you finish doing, making, or producing it.
例：Peter Mayle has just completed his first novel.彼得·梅尔刚刚完成他的第一部小说。
例：...the rush to get the stadiums completed on time.为了让各个体育场按时完工进行的匆忙赶工

VERB
全部完成
If you complete something, you do all of it.
例：She completed her degree in two years...她用两年的时间修完了课程，拿到了学位。
例：This book took years to complete.这本书耗时多年才写完。

VERB
填写(表格或问卷)
If you complete a form or questionnaire, you write the answers or information asked for in it.
例：Simply complete the coupon below...填写下面的订货单即可。
例：We ask candidates to complete a psychometric questionnaire...我们让候选人填写一份心理测试问卷。

ADJ
全面的;全能的
You can use complete to emphasize that someone is skilled at all aspects of a particular activity and is therefore the best example of that kind of person.
例：He was the complete all-round journalist.他是一名全能的记者。

prologue 
英[ˈprəʊlɒg] 美[ˈproʊlɔg]
 
n.序言，开场白；vi.作序言，作开场白；
 "

N-COUNT
前言；序言；开场白
A prologue is a speech or section of text that introduces a play or book.
例：The prologue to the novel is written in the form of a newspaper account.这本小说的序言是以报纸报道的形式写的。

N-COUNT
序幕；开端
If one event is a prologue to another event, it leads to it.
例：This was a prologue to today's bloodless revolution.这拉开了当今不流血革命的序幕。

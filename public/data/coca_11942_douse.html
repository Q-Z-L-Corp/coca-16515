douse 
英[daʊs] 美[daʊs]
 
vt.浇水在…上；熄灯；vi.浸泡；n.泼洒；〈方〉一击；
 "

VERB
浇灭(火)
If you douse a fire, you stop it burning by pouring a lot of water over it.
例：The pumps were started and the crew began to douse the fire with water.水泵开动了，消防队员们开始用水灭火。

VERB
(把液体)浇（或洒、泼）在…上
If you douse someone or something with a liquid, you throw a lot of that liquid over them.
例：They hurled abuse at their victim as they doused him with petrol.他们一边大声辱骂这名受害者一边将汽油淋在他身上。

VERB
熄灭(灯)
If you douse a light, you turn it off.
例：I doused the headlights and got out.我灭了车前灯后下了车。

stance 
英[stæns] 美[stæns]
 
n.态度，立场；站姿，被放置的姿势；位置；（运动员的）始发姿势；
 "

N-COUNT
态度;立场
Your stance on a particular matter is your attitude to it.
例：The Congress had agreed to reconsider its stance on the armed struggle...国会已同意重新考虑其对武装斗争的态度。
例：They have maintained a consistently neutral stance...他们一直持中立态度。

N-COUNT
站姿
Your stance is the way that you are standing.
例：Take a comfortably wide stance and flex your knees a little...双腿适度分开站立，双膝稍稍弯曲。
例：The woman detective shifted her stance from one foot to another.女侦探改变了站姿，把重心从一只脚移到另一只脚。

N-COUNT
限制;束缚;约束
A constraint is something that limits or controls what you can do.
例：Their decision to abandon the trip was made because of financial constraints...他们决定放弃这次出游是因为财力有限。
例：Water shortages in the area will be the main constraint on development.水资源匮乏将是制约该地区发展的一个主要因素。

N-UNCOUNT
克制
Constraint is control over the way you behave which prevents you from doing what you want to do.

blazing 
英[ˈbleɪzɪŋ] 美[]
 
adj.酷热的；炽热的；极其愤怒的；感情强烈的；v.猛烈地燃烧( blaze的现在分词 )；发光，照耀；
 "

ADJ
炽热的；炎热的
Blazing sun or blazing hot weather is very hot.
例：Quite a few people were eating outside in the blazing sun.不少人在外面的炎炎烈日下吃饭。
例：...freezing cold winters and blazing hot summers.严冬酷暑

ADJ
（争吵）激烈的
When people have a blazing row, they quarrel in a very noisy and excited way.
例：My husband has just had a blazing row with his boss.我丈夫刚和他老板大吵了一架。

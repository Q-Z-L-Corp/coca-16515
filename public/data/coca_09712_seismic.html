seismic 
英[ˈsaɪzmɪk] 美[ˈsaɪzmɪk]

 "

ADJ
地震的;地震引起的
Seismic means caused by or relating to an earthquake.
例：Earthquakes produce two types of seismic waves...地震产生两种地震波。
例：The latest seismic activity was also felt in northern Kenya.肯尼亚北部也感觉到了最近的地震活动。

ADJ
(转变、变化)突如其来的，巨大的，急剧的
A seismic shift or change is a very sudden or dramatic change.
例：I have never seen such a seismic shift in public opinion in such a short period of time.我从未见过公众舆论在如此短的时间内发生如此巨大的转变。

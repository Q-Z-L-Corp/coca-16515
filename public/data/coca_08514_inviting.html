ADJ-GRADED
吸引人的;诱人的
If you say that something is inviting, you mean that it has good qualities that attract you or make you want to experience it.
例：The February air was soft, cool, and inviting...二月的空气柔和、凉爽而怡人。
例：There is an inviting restaurant with an outdoor terrace.有一家诱人的带露天阳台的饭馆。

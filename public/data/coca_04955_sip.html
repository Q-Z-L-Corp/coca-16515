sip 
英[sɪp] 美[sɪp]
 
n.呷的动作；一呷之量；vt.& vi.小口喝，呷；从…中呷吸；
 "

VERB
小口地喝;抿
If you sip a drink or sip at it, you drink by taking just a small amount at a time.
例：Jessica sipped her drink thoughtfully...杰茜卡小口地喝着饮料，若有所思。
例：He sipped at the glass and then put it down...他抿了一口杯中的酒，然后放了下来。

N-COUNT
(饮料)一小口;一啜的量
A sip is a small amount of drink that you take into your mouth.
例：Harry took a sip of bourbon...哈里抿了一口波旁威士忌。
例：Katherine took another sip from her glass to calm herself.凯瑟琳又抿了一口杯中的酒，竭力使自己镇静下来。

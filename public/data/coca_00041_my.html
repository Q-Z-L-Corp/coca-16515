my 
英[maɪ] 美[maɪ]
 
adj.我的(I的所有格形式)；int.啊呀；天啊；
 "

DET-POSS
我的
A speaker or writer uses my to indicate that something belongs or relates to himself or herself.
例：I invited him back to my flat for a coffee...我邀请他回我的公寓喝杯咖啡。
例：John's my best friend...约翰是我最好的朋友。

DET-POSS
(用于对话或书信中，表示亲昵)我的
In conversations or in letters, my is used in front of a word like 'dear' or 'darling' to show affection.
例：My sweet Freda...我亲爱的弗蕾达
例：Yes, of course, my darling.是，当然了，亲爱的。

DET-POSS
(用于表示惊讶或震惊)哎呀，天哪
My is used in phrases such as 'My God' and 'My goodness' to express surprise or shock.
例：My God, I've never seen you so nervous...天哪，我从没见你这么紧张过。
例：My goodness, Tim, you have changed!我的天，蒂姆，你变了！

suicide 
英[ˈsu:ɪsaɪd] 美[ˈsuɪˌsaɪd]
 
n.自杀；自杀者；自杀行为；vt.自杀；adj.自杀的；
 "

N-VAR
自杀
People who commit suicide deliberately kill themselves because they do not want to continue living.
例：She tried to commit suicide on several occasions.她自杀过好几次。
例：...a case of attempted suicide.自杀未遂事件

N-UNCOUNT
自杀性行为;自毁;自取灭亡的行为
You say that people commit suicide when they deliberately do something which ruins their career or position in society.
例：Quite a few have committed social suicide by writing their boring memoirs...有一些人因为写那些枯燥无味的回忆录而毁了自己的社会形象。
例：They say it would be political suicide for the party to abstain.他们说这个政党弃权无异于自毁政治前程。

ADJ
(袭击、任务、爆炸)自杀性的
The people involved in a suicide attack, mission, or bombing do not expect to survive.
例：According to the army, the teenager said he was on a 'suicide mission' for the movement.军方消息称，这个少年说他正在为这次运动执行“自杀性任务”。
例：...a suicide bomber.自杀性炸弹袭击者

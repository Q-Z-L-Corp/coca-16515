N-COUNT
国家
A nation is an individual country considered together with its social and political structures.
例：Such policies would require unprecedented cooperation between nations...这样的政策要求国家间进行空前的合作。
例：The Arab nations agreed to meet in Baghdad.阿拉伯各国同意在巴格达会晤。

N-SING
全体国民;国民
The nation is sometimes used to refer to all the people who live in a particular country.
例：It was a story that touched the nation's heart.这是一个感动了全体国民的故事。

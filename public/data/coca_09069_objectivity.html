objectivity 
英[ˌɒbdʒek'tɪvətɪ] 美[ˌɑbdʒɛkˈtɪvɪti]
 
n.客观性，客观现实；
 "

N-COUNT
目的；目标；宗旨
Your objective is what you are trying to achieve.
例：Our main objective was the recovery of the child safe and well...我们的主要目标是将孩子安然无恙地找回来。
例：His objective was to play golf and win.他的目标是参加高尔夫球比赛并赢得胜利。

ADJ
（信息）客观的，基于事实的
Objective information is based on facts.
例：He had no objective evidence that anything extraordinary was happening.他没有客观证据表明正在发生什么不同寻常之事。

ADJ-GRADED
（观点）客观的，不带个人感情的，没有个人偏见的
If someone is objective, they base their opinions on facts rather than on their personal feelings.
例：I believe that a journalist should be completely objective...我认为新闻记者不应当有丝毫偏见。
例：I would really like to have your objective opinion on this.我很想听听你对此事的客观见解。

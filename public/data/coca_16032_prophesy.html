VERB
预言；预告
If you prophesy that something will happen, you say that you strongly believe that it will happen.
例：He prophesied that within five years his opponent would either be dead or in prison...他预言5年内他的敌人要么会死去，要么会进监狱。
例：She prophesied a bad ending for the expedition.她预言这次探险结局不会好。

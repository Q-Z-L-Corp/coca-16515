ADJ-GRADED
受困的;受限制的
If you feel trapped, you are in an unpleasant situation in which you lack freedom, and you feel you cannot escape from it.
例：He follows me everywhere and it makes me feel so trapped.他走到哪里都跟着我，我觉得被困住了。
例：...people who think of themselves as trapped in mundane jobs.认为自己受缚于平凡工作的人们

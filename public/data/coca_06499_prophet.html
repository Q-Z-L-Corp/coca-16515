prophet 
英[ˈprɒfɪt] 美[ˈprɑfɪt]
 
n.预言家，先知；倡导者，主张者；
 "

N-COUNT
先知
A prophet is a person who is believed to be chosen by God to say the things that God wants to tell people.
例：...the sacred name of the Holy Prophet of Islam.伊斯兰先知的圣名

N-COUNT
预言者；预言家
A prophet is someone who predicts that something will happen in the future.
例：I promised myself I'd defy all the prophets of doom and battle back to fitness.我发誓要打破所有的死亡预言，力争恢复健康。

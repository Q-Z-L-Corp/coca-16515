ADJ-GRADED
不整洁的；未被照顾妥当的；未被收拾整齐的
If you describe something or someone as unkempt, you mean that they are untidy, and not looked after carefully or kept neat.
例：His hair was unkempt and filthy.他的头发蓬乱污秽。

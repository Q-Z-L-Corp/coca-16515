nowadays 
英[ˈnaʊədeɪz] 美[ˈnaʊəˌdez]

ADV
如今;现今
Nowadays means at the present time, in contrast with the past.
例：Nowadays it's acceptable for women to be ambitious. But it wasn't then...如今，女性胸怀大志也能被接受，但那时候不是这样。
例：I don't see much of Tony nowadays.我现在不经常见到托尼了。

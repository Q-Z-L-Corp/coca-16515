ADJ
蓄意谋杀的;杀人的
Someone who is murderous is likely to murder someone and may already have murdered someone.
例：This murderous lunatic could kill them both without a second thought.这个杀人狂会毫不犹豫地把他俩都杀了。

ADJ-GRADED
凶残的;残忍的
A murderous attack or other action is very violent and intended to result in someone's death.
例：He made a murderous attack on his wife that evening.他那天晚上想置妻子于死地。

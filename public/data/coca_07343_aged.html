aged 
英[eɪdʒd] 美[ˈedʒɪd]
 
adj.老年的，陈年的；（食品、酒等）陈年的；（动物）达到几岁龄的；老年人特有的；v.“age”的过去式和过去分词；使显老，使苍老；
 "

ADJ
…岁的
You use aged followed by a number to say how old someone is.
例：Alan has two children, aged eleven and nine.艾伦有两个孩子，分别为11岁和9岁。

ADJ
年迈的
Aged means very old.
例：She has an aged parent who's capable of being very difficult.她有一个年迈的父亲，可能会很不好相处。

N-PLURAL
老年人
You can refer to all people who are very old as the aged .
例：...people who work with the aged.为老年人服务的人们

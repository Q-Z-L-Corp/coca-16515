ADJ-GRADED
破坏性的;制造混乱的
To be disruptive means to prevent something from continuing or operating in a normal way.
例：Alcohol can produce violent, disruptive behavior...酒精会引发暴力和破坏性行为。
例：The process of implementing these changes can be very disruptive to a small company.对于小公司来说，实行这些变革可能会带来灭顶之灾。

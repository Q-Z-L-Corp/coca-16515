kettle 
英[ 'ket(ə)l] 美[ 'ket(ə)l]
 
[人名] [英格兰人姓氏] 凯特尔来源于古诺斯语人名，含义是u201c釜u201d(cauldron)；
 "

N-COUNT
水壶
A kettle is a covered container that you use for boiling water. It has a handle, and a spout for the water to come out of.
例：I'll put the kettle on and make us some tea.我去烧壶水给大家沏茶。

N-COUNT
锅
A kettle is a metal pot for boiling or cooking things in.
例：Put the meat into a small kettle.把肉放进一个小锅里。

PHRASE
完全不同的事物
If you say that something is a different kettle of fish, you mean that it is very different from another related thing that you are talking about.
例：Playing for the reserve team is a totally different kettle of fish.作为替补参加比赛完全是两码事。

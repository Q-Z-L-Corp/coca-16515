N-UNCOUNT
战斗
Combat is fighting that takes place in a war.
例：Over 16 million men had died in combat...1,600多万人在战斗中牺牲。
例：Yesterday saw hand-to-hand combat in the city.昨天该市发生了肉搏战。

N-COUNT
斗争;格斗
A combat is a battle, or a fight between two people.
例：It was the end of a long combat.这是一场长期斗争的结束。

VERB
与…作斗争;打击
If people in authority combat something, they try to stop it happening.
例：Congress has criticised new government measures to combat crime.国会对政府打击犯罪的新措施提出了批评。

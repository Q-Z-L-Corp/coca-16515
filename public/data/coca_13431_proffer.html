proffer 
英[ˈprɒfə(r)] 美[ˈprɑfə(r)]
 
vt.提供，贡献，提出；提出参见；n.提供；贡献；提供的行为；提议；
 "

VERB
提供；拿出；递给
If you proffer something to someone, you hold it towards them so that they can take it or touch it.
例：He rose and proffered a silver box full of cigarettes.他站起身，伸手递过一个装满香烟的银盒子。

VERB
提出，提供（建议等）
If you proffer something such as advice to someone, you offer it to them.
例：The army has not yet proffered an explanation of how and why the accident happened.军队还没有对这次事件发生的经过和原因给出解释。

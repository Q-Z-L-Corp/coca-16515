attend 
英[əˈtend] 美[əˈtɛnd]
 
vi.出席；致力于，献身于；侍候，照顾；关注；vt.出席，参加；[常用被动语态]（作为结果、情况）伴随；照顾；陪伴；
 "

VERB
出席；参加
If you attend a meeting or other event, you are present at it.
例：Thousands of people attended the funeral...数千人参加了葬礼。
例：The meeting will be attended by finance ministers from many countries...很多国家的财政部长将出席会议。

VERB
去（学校、教堂等）；上（学）
If you attend an institution such as a school, college, or church, you go there regularly.
例：They attended college together at the University of Pennsylvania.他们一起就读于宾夕法尼亚大学。

VERB
处理，料理（事情）；照顾，护理（受伤的人）
If you attend to something, you deal with it. If you attend to someone who is hurt or injured, you care for them.
例：The staff will helpfully attend to your needs...工作人员会帮忙满足您的需求。
例：There are more pressing matters to be attended to today...今天有更紧急的事情要处理。

envisage 
英[ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ] 美[ɛnˈvɪzɪdʒ]
 
vt.想像，设想；正视，面对；观察，展望；
 "

VERB
设想;想象;预想
If you envisage something, you imagine that it is true, real, or likely to happen.
例：He envisages the possibility of establishing direct diplomatic relations in the future...他设想在未来可以建立直接的外交关系。
例：He had never envisaged spending the whole of his working life in that particular job...他从没有想象过自己整个职业生涯都要从事那个工作。

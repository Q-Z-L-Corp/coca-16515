imperious 
英[ɪmˈpɪəriəs] 美[ɪmˈpɪriəs]
 
adj.专横的，飞扬跋扈的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
专横的;傲慢的;飞扬跋扈的
If you describe someone as imperious, you mean that they have a proud manner and expect to be obeyed.
例：Her attitude is imperious at times...她有时态度傲慢。
例：From across the desk she gave him a witheringly imperious look.她从桌子对面扫了他一眼，目光咄咄逼人，凌利而专横。

N-COUNT
(供一个人吃的)一份食物的量
A serving is an amount of food that is given to one person at a meal.
例：Quantities will vary according to how many servings of soup you want to prepare...量的多少根据你想准备的汤的份数而定。
例：Each serving contains 240 calories.每份食物含 240 卡热量。

ADJ
(勺子、盘子)分菜用的，上菜用的
A serving spoon or dish is used for giving out food at a meal.
例：Pile the potatoes into a warm serving dish.将土豆堆在一个保温菜盘里。

wispy 
英[ˈwɪspi] 美[ˈwɪspi]
 
adj.像小束状的，纤细的，脆弱的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(头发)稀疏的，稀稀拉拉的
If someone has wispy hair, their hair does not grow thickly on their head.
例：Grey wispy hair straggled down to her shoulders.稀疏的灰白头发披散在她肩头。

ADJ-GRADED
(云)一缕缕的，虚无缥缈的
A wispy cloud is thin or faint.
例：The half moon is hidden behind some wispy clouds.半轮月亮躲在淡淡的云彩之后。

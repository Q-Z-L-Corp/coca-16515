springboard 
英[ˈsprɪŋbɔ:d] 美[ˈsprɪŋbɔrd]
 
n.（有助于成功的）跳板；（跳水、体操等体育运动中的）跳板，踏板；
 "

N-COUNT
跳板;推动力
If something is a springboard for something else, it makes it possible for that thing to happen or start.
例：The 1981 budget was the springboard for an economic miracle...1981 年的预算促成了一场经济奇迹的出现。
例：It could provide a springboard to success.这可能是迈向成功的跳板。

N-COUNT
(跳水或体操运动用的)跳板
A springboard is a flexible board from which you jump into a swimming pool or onto a piece of gymnastic equipment.

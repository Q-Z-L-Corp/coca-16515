re-entry 
英[ri:ˈentrɪ] 美[riˈɛntrɪ]
 
n.再进入；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
再进入
Re-entry is the act of returning to a place, organization, or area of activity that you have left.
例：The house has been barred and bolted to prevent re-entry...房子已经被封了，门被闩上，防止有人入内。
例：The military men are contemplating a re-entry into politics.这些军人在考虑重返政界。

N-UNCOUNT
（航天器的）重返大气层，返回地球
Re-entry is used to refer to the moment when a spacecraft comes back into the earth's atmosphere after being in space.
例：The station would burn up on re-entry into the earth's atmosphere.空间站在重返地球大气层时会被烧毁。

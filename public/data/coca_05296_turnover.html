N-VAR
营业额
The turnover of a company is the value of the goods or services sold during a particular period of time.
例：Her annual turnover is around £45,000…她的年营业额大约是 4.5 万英镑。
例：The company had a turnover of £3.8 million.那家公司营业额为 380 万英镑。

N-VAR
(人员的)流动率
The turnover of people in an organization or place is the rate at which people leave and are replaced.
例：Short-term contracts increase staff turnover...短期合同使得员工流动率增高。
例：The industry has a high turnover of young people.该行业的年轻从业者流动率较高。

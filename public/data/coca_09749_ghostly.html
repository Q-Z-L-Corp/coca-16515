ghostly

ADJ
虚幻的；恐怖的；可怕的
Something that is ghostly seems unreal or unnatural and may be frightening because of this.
例：The moon shone, shedding a ghostly light on the fields.月亮闪耀，田野上洒下幽幽的亮光。
例：...Sonia's ghostly laughter.索尼娅瘆人的笑声

ADJ
鬼似的；幽灵般的；鬼魂萦绕的
A ghostly presence is the ghost or spirit of a dead person.
例：...the ghostly presences which haunt these islands...在这些岛屿上出没的鬼魂
例：Charles is haunted by ghostly children.孩子们的幽灵缠上了查尔斯。

ADJ-GRADED
误导性的;迷惑性的；欺骗性的
If you describe something as misleading, you mean that it gives you a wrong idea or impression.
例：It would be misleading to say that we were friends...说我们是朋友会让别人产生误解。
例：The article contains several misleading statements.这篇文章有几处误导性说法。

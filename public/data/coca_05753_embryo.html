embryo 
英[ˈembriəʊ] 美[ˈembrioʊ]
 
n.<生>胚，胚胎；胚芽；初期；
 "

N-COUNT
胚胎
An embryo is an unborn animal or human being in the very early stages of development.
例：The embryo lives in the amniotic cavity.胚胎在羊膜腔中发育。
例：...the remarkable resilience of very young embryos.早期胚胎非凡的适应力

ADJ
(想法、体系或机构)萌芽阶段的，初期的
An embryo idea, system, or organization is in the very early stages of development, but is expected to grow stronger.
例：They are an embryo party of government...他们这个政党有望组建政府，但目前还不成熟。
例：It was an embryo idea rather than a fully worked proposal.这只是初步的想法，并非成形的提议。

PHRASE
在萌芽期;在初期
Something that is in embryo is at a very early stage of its development.
例：These developments were foreseen in embryo more than a decade ago.这些发展早在十多年前的萌芽阶段就已预见到。

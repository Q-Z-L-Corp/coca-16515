cellar 
英[ˈselə(r)] 美[ˈsɛlɚ]
 
n.地下室，地窖；酒窖；<英>（都市住宅的）地下煤窖；[机]油盒；vt.把…藏入地窖（酒窖）；
 "

N-COUNT
地窖;地下室
A cellar is a room underneath a building, which is often used for storing things in.
例：The box of papers had been stored in a cellar at the family home.那箱文件一直藏在家中的地窖里。

N-COUNT
(私人或饭店的)酒窖
A person's or restaurant's cellar is the collection of different wines that they have.
例：...the restaurant's extensive wine cellar.饭店藏酒品种繁多的酒窖

negate 
英[nɪˈgeɪt] 美[nɪˈɡet]
 
vt.取消，使无效；否定，否认；使作废；vi.否定，否认；无效；n.反面，对立面；
 "

VERB
取消;使无效
If one thing negates another, it causes that other thing to lose the effect or value that it had.
例：These weaknesses negated his otherwise progressive attitude towards the staff.这些缺点抵消了他在其他方面对待员工还算开明的态度。

VERB
否认…之存在;否定
If someone negates something, they say that it does not exist.
例：He warned that to negate the results of elections would only make things worse.他警告说，否定选举的结果只会使事情更糟。

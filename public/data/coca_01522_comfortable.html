comfortable 
英[ˈkʌmftəbl] 美[ˈkʌmfətəbəl, ˈkʌmftəbəl]
 
adj.舒适的；安逸的，处于轻松的；充裕的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(家具、衣服等)让人舒服的，舒适的
If a piece of furniture or an item of clothing is comfortable, it makes you feel physically relaxed when you use it, for example because it is soft.
例：...a comfortable fireside chair...舒适的炉边座椅
例：Trainers are so comfortable to wear.便鞋穿着特别舒服。

ADJ-GRADED
(建筑物或房间)令人放松的，安逸的，舒适的
If a building or room is comfortable, it makes you feel physically relaxed when you spend time in it, for example because it is warm and has nice furniture.
例：A home should be comfortable and friendly.家应该让人觉得舒服、亲切。
例：...somewhere warm and comfortable.某个温暖舒服的地方

ADJ-GRADED
(人)愉快放松的，舒服的
If you are comfortable, you are physically relaxed because of the place or position you are sitting or lying in.
例：Lie down on your bed and make yourself comfortable...躺在床上，身体放松。
例：She tried to maneuver her body into a more comfortable position.她试着把身子挪了挪，换了一个较为舒服的姿势。

ADJ-GRADED
宽裕的;小康的
If you say that someone is comfortable, you mean that they have enough money to be able to live without financial problems.
例：'Is he rich?' — 'He's comfortable.'...“他很有钱吗？”“他生活挺宽裕的。”
例：She came from a stable, comfortable, middle-class family.她出身于一个收入稳定、生活宽裕的中产阶级家庭。

ADJ-GRADED
轻松领先的;易获胜的
In a race, competition, or election, if you have a comfortable lead, you are likely to win it easily. If you gain a comfortable victory or majority, you win easily.
例：By half distance we held a comfortable two-lap lead...到半程时，我们轻松领先两圈。
例：He appeared to be heading for a comfortable victory.看起来他会轻松获胜。

ADJ-GRADED
自信的;放心的;自在的
If you feel comfortable with a particular situation or person, you feel confident and relaxed with them.
例：Nervous politicians might well feel more comfortable with a step-by-step approach...神经脆弱的政治家们似乎更容易接受一种循序渐进的做法。
例：He liked me and I felt comfortable with him...他喜欢我，我跟他在一起觉得很自在。

ADJ
(病人或受伤者)情况稳定的，状况良好的
When a sick or injured person is said to be comfortable, they are in a stable physical condition.
例：He was described as comfortable in hospital last night.据说昨晚他在医院状况良好。

ADJ-GRADED
(生活、工作或所处的环境)轻松的，无忧无虑的
A comfortable life, job, or situation does not cause you any problems or worries.
例：...a comfortable teaching job at a university...在大学教书的轻松工作
例：Kohl's retirement looks far from comfortable.看来科尔的退休生活一点都不自在。

courtship 
英[ˈkɔ:tʃɪp] 美[ˈkɔrtʃɪp]
 
n.求爱期，追求期；求爱，求婚；[动]求偶（现象）；
 "

N-VAR
求爱;恋爱;求爱(或恋爱)期
Courtship is the activity of courting or the time during which a man and a woman are courting.
例：They were more interested in courtship and cars than in school...比起上学，他们对谈恋爱和汽车更感兴趣。
例：After a short courtship, she accepted his marriage proposal.恋爱没多长时间，她就接受了他的求婚。

N-UNCOUNT
(动物的)求偶
The courtship of male and female animals is their behaviour before they have sex.
例：Courtship is somewhat vocal with a lot of displaying by the male.雄性动物在求偶时会尽量展示自己，并发出一些声响。
例：...elaborate courtship dances.复杂的求偶舞

VERB
补偿;赔偿;向…偿付
If you reimburse someone for something, you pay them back the money that they have spent or lost because of it.
例：I'll be happy to reimburse you for any expenses you might have incurred...我将很乐意赔偿您所发生的任何费用。
例：The funds are supposed to reimburse policyholders in the event of insurer failure.这项基金将在保险公司不能偿付的情况下对投保人进行赔付。

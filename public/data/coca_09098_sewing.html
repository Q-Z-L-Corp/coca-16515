sewing 
英[ˈsəʊɪŋ] 美[ˈsoʊɪŋ]
 
n.缝纫；缝纫机；针线活；缝纫物；v.缝制；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
缝纫;缝补
Sewing is the activity of making or mending clothes or other things using a needle and thread.
例：Her mother had always done all the sewing.所有的针线活一直都是她母亲来做。

N-UNCOUNT
缝纫的衣物;针线活
Sewing is clothes or other things that are being sewn.
例：We all got out our own sewing and sat in front of the log fire.我们都把自己的针线活拿出来，坐到了火堆前。

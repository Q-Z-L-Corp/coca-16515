croak 
英[krəʊk] 美[kroʊk]
 
vi.呱呱地叫；vt.& vi.用粗的声音说；n.呱呱的叫声；
 "

VERB
(青蛙或鸟)发出低哑叫声，呱呱叫
When a frog or bird croaks, it makes a harsh, low sound.
例：Thousands of frogs croaked in the reeds by the riverbank.上千只青蛙在河岸边的芦苇荡里呱呱叫。

VERB
(人)用低哑的声音说
If someone croaks something, they say it in a low, rough voice.
例：Tiller moaned and managed to croak, 'Help me.'...蒂勒呻吟着，用沙哑的声音勉强喊出了一声“救救我”。
例：She croaked something unintelligible.她声音沙哑地说了些含糊不清的话。

N-COUNT
月;月份
A month is one of the twelve periods of time that a year is divided into, for example January or February.
例：The trial is due to begin next month.审判将于下个月开始。
例：...an exhibition which opens this month at London's Design Museum...这个月在伦敦设计博物馆开办的展览

N-COUNT
一个月（的时间）
A month is a period of about four weeks.
例：She was here for a month...她在这儿呆了一个月。
例：Over the next several months I met most of her family...在后来的几个月里，我见到了她的大部分家人。

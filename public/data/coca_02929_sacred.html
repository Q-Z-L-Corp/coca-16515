sacred 
英[ˈseɪkrɪd] 美[ˈsekrɪd]
 
adj.宗教的；神圣的；受崇敬的，值得崇敬的；有宗教性质的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
神圣的;上帝的;神的
Something that is sacred is believed to be holy and to have a special connection with God.
例：The owl is sacred for many Californian Indian people.对于很多加利福尼亚的印第安人而言，猫头鹰是圣物。
例：...shrines and sacred places.神龛和圣地

ADJ
宗教的;用于宗教仪式的
Something connected with religion or used in religious ceremonies is described as sacred .
例：...sacred art.宗教艺术
例：...sacred songs or music.宗教歌曲或音乐

ADJ-GRADED
不可侵犯的;不容干涉的;不可变更的
You can describe something as sacred when it is regarded as too important to be changed or interfered with.
例：My memories are sacred...我的记忆不容亵渎。
例：He said the unity of the country was sacred.他说国家的统一神圣不可侵犯。

silky 
英[ˈsɪlki] 美[ˈsɪlki]
 
adj.丝一样的，像绸缎的；柔软的，光滑的；优雅的；（酒）舐着舒服的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
丝绸一样的;柔软光洁的
If something has a silky texture, it is smooth, soft, and shiny, like silk.
例：...dresses in seductively silky fabrics...迷人的丝绸长裙
例：Claire smoothed Katy's silky hair.克莱尔捋了捋凯蒂如丝般的秀发。

ADJ-GRADED
(声音)轻柔的，柔和的，温和的
If you describe someone's voice as silky, you mean that it sounds confident but gentle and you find it attractive.
例：...a well-spoken man with a silky voice.说话温和而且彬彬有礼的男子

ADJ-GRADED
(行动)平稳的，流畅的
If you describe the way someone or something moves as silky, you mean that they move very smoothly.
例：Some people moved in silky, liquid movements, others were jerky, probably drunk...一些人动作平稳流畅，其他人则跌跌撞撞，大概是喝醉了。
例：I had the car for two years and much enjoyed its excellent handling, quietness and silky gearchange.我的车买了两年了，我非常喜欢它卓越的操控性，安静舒适的驾车环境和平稳流畅的换挡操作。

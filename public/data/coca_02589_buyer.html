N-COUNT
购买者；买主；买方
A buyer is a person who is buying something or who intends to buy it.
例：Car buyers are more interested in safety and reliability than speed.购车者们更感兴趣的是汽车的安全性和可靠性，而不是它的速度。

N-COUNT
（大商店的）采购员，进货员；买手
A buyer is a person who works for a large store deciding what goods will be bought from manufacturers to be sold in the store.
例：I was a buyer for the women's clothing department.我是女装部的进货员。

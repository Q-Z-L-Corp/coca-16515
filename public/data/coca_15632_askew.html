askew 
英[əˈskju:] 美[əˈskju]
 
adv.& adj.歪，斜；歪歪扭扭；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
斜的；歪的；不平的
Something that is askew is not straight or not level with what it should be level with.
例：She stood there, hat askew...她站在那里，歪戴着帽子。
例：There were no shutters at the windows, and some of the doors hung askew.窗户上没有百叶窗，有几扇门也歪歪扭扭的。

unwieldy 
英[ʌnˈwi:ldi] 美[ʌnˈwildi]
 
adj.笨拙的；笨重的；不便利的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
沉甸甸的；粗笨的；笨重的
If you describe an object as unwieldy, you mean that it is difficult to move or carry because it is so big or heavy.
例：They came panting up to his door with their unwieldy baggage.他们提着笨重的行李气喘吁吁地来到他门口。

ADJ-GRADED
臃肿的；庞大的
If you describe a system as unwieldy, you mean that it does not work very well as a result of it being too large or badly organized.
例：His firm must contend with the unwieldy Russian bureaucracy.他的公司必须与俄罗斯庞大的官僚机构周旋。
例：...an unwieldy legal system.臃肿的法律体系

ADV-GRADED
清楚地;明显地;肯定地;当然
You use definitely to emphasize that something is the case, or to emphasize the strength of your intention or opinion.
例：I'm definitely going to get in touch with these people...我肯定会跟这些人取得联系。
例：Something should definitely be done about that...对此一定要采取一些措施。

ADV
明确地;确定地
If something has been definitely decided, the decision will not be changed.
例：He told them that no venue had yet been definitely decided.他告诉他们还没有确定下场地。

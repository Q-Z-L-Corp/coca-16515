N-COUNT
警察
A cop is a policeman or policewoman.
例：Frank didn't like having the cops know where to find him.弗兰克不想让警察知道去哪里可以找到他。

PHRASE
受责骂;受罚
If you cop it, someone punishes you or speaks to you angrily because you have done something wrong.
例：Motel owners and restaurant managers copped it for neglecting their clients.汽车旅馆老板和饭店经理因为冷落顾客而受到责骂。

PHRASE
没有什么价值;不怎么样
If you say that something is not much cop, you mean that it is not very good, and is disappointing.
例：The Jane's 'Triple X Album' came out in 1986, and wasn't much cop actually.简在1986年推出了《XXX》专辑，但事实上并不怎么样。

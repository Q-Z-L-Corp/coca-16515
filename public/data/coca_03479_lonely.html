lonely 
英[ˈləʊnli] 美[ˈloʊnli]
 
adj.孤独的，孤单的；寂寞的；荒凉的，幽静的；在孤单中度过的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
孤独的；寂寞的；孤单的
Someone who is lonely is unhappy because they are alone or do not have anyone they can talk to.
例：...lonely people who just want to talk...只想找人倾诉的孤独的人
例：I feel lonelier in the middle of London than I do on my boat in the middle of nowhere.我身处伦敦市中心比驾船航行于茫茫大海更感到孤单。

ADJ-GRADED
（指状态或时期）孤独的，寂寞的，孤零零的
A lonely situation or period of time is one in which you feel unhappy because you are alone or do not have anyone to talk to.
例：I desperately needed something to occupy me during those long, lonely nights.在那一个个漫长、孤单的夜晚，我极需找点事做。
例：...her lonely childhood.她寂寞的童年

ADJ-GRADED
偏僻的；荒凉的；人迹罕至的
A lonely place is one where very few people come.
例：It felt like the loneliest place in the world.感觉这就像是天底下最荒凉的地方。
例：...dark, lonely streets.昏暗偏僻的街道

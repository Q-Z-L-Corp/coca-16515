ADJ-GRADED
可预言的；可预测的；可预料的
If you say that an event is predictable, you mean that it is obvious in advance that it will happen.
例：This was a predictable reaction, given the bitter hostility between the two countries...考虑到这两个国家之间激烈的敌对情绪，这种反应是可以预见的。
例：The result was entirely predictable.这个结果完全在预料之中。

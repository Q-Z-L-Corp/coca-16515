VERB
困扰；烦扰；使苦恼
If someone or something is beset by problems or fears, they have many problems or fears which affect them severely.
例：The country is beset by severe economic problems.这个国家被严重的经济问题所困扰。
例：The discussions were beset with difficulties.讨论困难重重。

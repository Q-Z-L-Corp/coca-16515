endemic 
英[enˈdemɪk] 美[ɛnˈdɛmɪk]
 
adj.某地特有的；（尤指疾病）地方性的；风土的；（动，植物）某些特产的；n.地方病；某地特产的植物[动物]；
 "

ADJ
（疾病）地方性的，流行的
If a disease or illness is endemic in a place, it is frequently found among the people who live there.
例：Polio was then endemic among children my age.那时小儿麻痹症在我这个年纪的孩子中很常见。

ADJ-GRADED
(情况、问题)常见的,流行的
If you say that a condition or problem is endemic, you mean that it is very common and strong, and cannot be dealt with easily.
例：Street crime is virtually endemic in large cities.街头犯罪近乎是大城市的通病。
例：...powerful radicals with an endemic hatred and fear of the West.普遍仇恨又惧怕西方世界的势力强大的激进分子

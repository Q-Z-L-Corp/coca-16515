tolerate 
英[ˈtɒləreɪt] 美[ˈtɑləreɪt]
 
vt.容许；承认；忍受；容忍（不同意或不喜欢的事物）；
 "

VERB
容忍;容许;宽容
If you tolerate a situation or person, you accept them although you do not particularly like them.
例：She can no longer tolerate the position that she's in...她再也受不了自己的处境了。
例：The cousins tolerated each other, but did not really get on well together.这对表亲互相容忍，但是相处得并不真正融洽。

VERB
忍受；忍耐
If you can tolerate something unpleasant or painful, you are able to bear it.
例：The ability to tolerate pain varies from person to person.对疼痛的忍受力因人而异。

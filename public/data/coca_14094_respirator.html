respirator 
英[ˈrespəreɪtə(r)] 美[ˈrɛspəˌretɚ]
 
n.口罩，防毒面具；（人工）呼吸机；
 "

N-COUNT
呼吸机;人工呼吸器
A respirator is a device that allows people to breathe when they cannot breathe naturally, for example because they are ill or have been injured.
例：She was so ill that she was put on a respirator.她病得很严重，被戴上了人工呼吸器。

N-COUNT
防毒面具
A respirator is a device you wear over your mouth and nose in order to breathe when you are surrounded by smoke or poisonous gas.

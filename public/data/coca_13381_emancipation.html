VERB
解放;使摆脱束缚
If people are emancipated, they are freed from unpleasant or unfair social, political, or legal restrictions.
例：Catholics were emancipated in 1792...天主教徒在1792年获得了解放。
例：That war preserved the Union and emancipated the slaves...那场战争保住了联邦政府，并解放了奴隶。

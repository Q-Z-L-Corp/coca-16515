ORD
第六
The sixth item in a series is the one that you count as number six.
例：...the sixth round of the World Cup.世界杯第六轮的比赛
例：...the sixth of December.12 月 6 日

FRACTION
六分之一
A sixth is one of six equal parts of something.
例：The company yesterday shed a sixth of its workforce.这家公司昨天裁掉了 1/6 的员工。
例：...five-sixths of a mile.5/6 英里

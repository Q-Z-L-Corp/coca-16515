ADJ
连续的;不间断的
Consecutive periods of time or events happen one after the other without interruption.
例：The Cup was won for the third consecutive year by the Toronto Maple Leafs...多伦多枫叶队连续第三年捧杯。
例：Photographs taken at the same time on two consecutive sunny days can be quite different from one another.在连续两个艳阳天的同一时间拍摄的照片也可能大不相同。

ADV
只;仅仅;完全
If something involves solely one thing, it involves only this thing and no others.
例：Too often we make decisions based solely upon what we see in the magazines...太多的时候，我们仅仅根据在杂志上面所看到的东西就作出了决定。
例：This program is a production of NPR, which is solely responsible for its content.这个节目由美国国家公共电台出品，并对其节目内容全权负责。

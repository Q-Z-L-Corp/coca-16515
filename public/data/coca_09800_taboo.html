N-COUNT
禁忌;忌讳
If there is a taboo on a subject or activity, it is a social custom to avoid doing that activity or talking about that subject, because people find them embarrassing or offensive.
例：The topic of addiction remains something of a taboo...毒瘾仍然是个有些忌讳的话题。
例：Religious dogmas often include strong sexual taboos and can create in children the impression that sex is sinful.宗教教义中经常包含严格的性禁忌，这会给儿童留下“性是罪恶”的印象。

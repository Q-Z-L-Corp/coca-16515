yard 
英[jɑ:d] 美[jɑrd]
 
n.院子；码（等于3英尺或36英寸或0。9144米）；场地；帆桁；
 "

N-COUNT
码（长度单位，等于36英寸或约91.4厘米）
A yard is a unit of length equal to thirty-six inches or approximately 91.4 centimetres.
例：The incident took place about 500 yards from where he was standing...事件发生在距他所站位置大约500码远的地方。
例：A few yards away, Jos Vargas stands beside his small home.几码之外，乔斯·瓦尔加斯站在他的小房子旁边。

N-COUNT
院子;庭院
A yard is a flat area of concrete or stone that is next to a building and often has a wall around it.
例：I saw him standing in the yard.我看到他站在院子里。

N-COUNT
（进行某项作业的）区域，场地，工场
You can refer to a large open area where a particular type of work is done as a yard.
例：...a railway yard.铁路调车场
例：...a ship repair yard.船舶修理场

N-COUNT
（住宅旁的）园圃，花园
A yard is a piece of land next to someone's house, with grass and plants growing in it.
例：He dug a hole in our yard on Edgerton Avenue to plant a maple tree when I was born.我出生的时候，他在埃杰顿大街我们家的园圃里挖了个坑，种了棵枫树。

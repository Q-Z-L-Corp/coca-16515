ADJ-GRADED
巨大的;庞大的;厚重的;强大的
Something that is massive is very large in size, quantity, or extent.
例：There was evidence of massive fraud.有证据表明是巨额欺诈。
例：...massive air attacks...大规模空袭

ADJ
(疾病)非常严重的，十分危急的
If you describe a medical condition as massive, you mean that it is extremely serious.
例：He died six weeks later of a massive heart attack.六周后他因严重的心脏病发作去世。

complexion 
英[kəmˈplekʃn] 美[kəmˈplɛkʃən]
 
n.肤色，面色，气色；形式，局面；
 "

N-COUNT
肤色;面色
When you refer to someone's complexion, you are referring to the natural colour or condition of the skin on their face.
例：She had short brown hair and a pale complexion.她留着褐色短发，皮肤白皙。
例：...those with acne or oily complexions.脸上有粉刺或油性肤质的人

N-COUNT
性质;特性
The complexion of something is its general nature or character.
例：Every time the political complexion of the government changed, so did the defence policy...每当政府的政治性质发生变化，国防政策也会随之变化。
例：But surely this puts a different complexion on things.但是这肯定让局势有了变化。

crowded 
英[ˈkraʊdɪd] 美[ˈkraʊdɪd]
 
adj.水泄不通的；拥挤的；肩摩踵接；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
人多的;拥挤的
If a place is crowded, it is full of people.
例：He peered slowly around the small crowded room...他慢慢地环视这个拥挤的小屋。
例：The street was crowded and noisy...这条街拥挤嘈杂。

ADJ-GRADED
拥挤的;住有很多人的
If a place is crowded, a lot of people live there.
例：...a crowded city of 2 million...有200万人口的拥挤的城市
例：The best housing they could afford was crowded and filthy.他们能住得起的最好的住所也非常拥挤、肮脏不堪。

ADJ-GRADED
排满的;占满的
If your timetable, your life, or your mind is crowded, it is full of events, activities, or thoughts.
例：Never before has a summit had such a crowded agenda.以前从来没有一次峰会的日程排得这么满。
例：...a long life crowded with incident...充满曲折的漫长人生

N-COUNT
女孩；姑娘
A girl is a female child.
例：...an eleven year old girl...11岁的女孩
例：I must have been a horrid little girl.我一定是个人见人烦的小女孩。

N-COUNT
女儿
You can refer to someone's daughter as a girl .
例：We had a little girl.我们有一个小女儿。

N-COUNT
年轻女子，小妞儿（可能具冒犯意味）
Young women are often referred to as girls. This use could cause offence.
例：...a pretty twenty-year-old girl.20岁的漂亮姑娘

N-COUNT
（男人的）女朋友

例：I've been with my girl for nine years.我同我女朋友交往9年了。

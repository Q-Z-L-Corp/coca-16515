awry 
英[əˈraɪ] 美[əˈraɪ]
 
adj.扭曲的；斜的；错误的；adv.歪斜地；错误地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
出岔子的;出错的
If something goes awry, it does not happen in the way it was planned.
例：She was in a fury over a plan that had gone awry.计划出了问题，她很愤怒。

ADJ-GRADED
歪的；斜的
If something is awry, it is not in its normal or proper position.
例：His dark hair was all awry.他的黑头发全乱了。

readiness 
英[ˈredinəs] 美[ˈrɛdɪnɪs]
 
n.准备就绪；愿意，乐意；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
愿意；乐意
If someone is very willing to do something, you can talk about their readiness to do it.
例：...their readiness to co-operate with the new US envoy.他们乐意和新任美国特使合作

N-UNCOUNT
准备就绪;以备
If you do something in readiness for a future event, you do it so that you are prepared for that event.
例：Security tightened in the capital in readiness for the president's arrival.首都加强了警备，准备迎接总统的到来。

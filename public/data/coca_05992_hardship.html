n.艰难；困苦；艰难情况；造成困苦与苦难的原因；
 "

N-VAR
艰难;艰苦;贫困
Hardship is a situation in which your life is difficult or unpleasant, often because you do not have enough money.
例：Many people are suffering economic hardship...很多人正处于经济困难之中。
例：One of the worst hardships is having so little time to spend with one's family.最大的痛苦之一是几乎没有时间和家人在一起。

mutter 
英[ˈmʌtə(r)] 美[ˈmʌtɚ]
 
vt.& vi.轻声低语，咕哝地抱怨；含糊地说；自言自语地说；n.咕哝；小声低语，小声抱怨；怨言；
 "

VERB
轻声说话;小声抱怨
If you mutter, you speak very quietly so that you cannot easily be heard, often because you are complaining about something.
例：'God knows what's happening in that madman's mind,' she muttered...她咕哝着：“天知道那个疯子在想什么。”
例：She can hear the old woman muttering about consideration...她能听到那个老妇人小声咕哝说考虑考虑。

poise 
英[pɔɪz] 美[pɔɪz]
 
n.泰然自若；自信；体态；姿态；vt.使平衡；保持（某种姿势）；抓紧；使稳定；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
镇定；沉着；泰然自若
If someone has poise, they are calm, dignified, and self-controlled.
例：What amazed him even more than her appearance was her poise...她的容貌让他惊叹，而更让他惊讶的是她的沉着。
例：It took a moment for Mark to recover his poise.马克过了一会儿才恢复镇静。

N-UNCOUNT
（优雅的）姿势，姿态
Poise is a graceful, very controlled way of standing and moving.
例：Ballet classes are important for poise and grace...芭蕾课对培养优雅的姿仪非常重要。
例：Even when he moved he did so without poise.他就连走起路来也姿势难看。

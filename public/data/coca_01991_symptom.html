symptom

 "

N-COUNT
症状
A symptom of an illness is something wrong with your body or mind that is a sign of the illness.
例：One of the most common symptoms of schizophrenia is hearing imaginary voices.精神分裂症的最常见的一个症状就是幻听。
例：...patients with flu symptoms...有流感症状的病人

N-COUNT
征候;征兆
A symptom of a bad situation is something that happens which is considered to be a sign of this situation.
例：Your problem with keeping boyfriends is just a symptom of a larger problem: making and keeping friends...你在维系同男友的关系时遭遇的困难暴露了一个更大的问题：如何结交朋友和维持友谊。
例：The contradictory statements are symptoms of disarray in the administration.这些互相矛盾的说法反映了管理的混乱。

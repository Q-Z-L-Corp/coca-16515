N-COUNT
犯罪者；违法者；罪犯
An offender is a person who has committed a crime.
例：The authorities often know that sex offenders will attack again when they are released.当局一般都知道性犯罪者在获释后往往会再次犯案。

N-COUNT
造成问题的人（或事物）
You can refer to someone or something which you think is causing a problem as an offender .
例：The contraceptive pill is the worst offender, but it is not the only drug to deplete the body's vitamin levels.避孕药的危害最大，但是它并不是唯一一种消耗体内维生素的药物。

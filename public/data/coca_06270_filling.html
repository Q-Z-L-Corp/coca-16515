filling 
英[ˈfɪlɪŋ] 美[ˈfɪlɪŋ]
 
n.装满，填满；填充物；（糕点内的）馅；（织品的）纬纱；v.填满(fill的ing形式)；
 "

N-COUNT
(补牙用的)填料
A filling is a small amount of metal or plastic that a dentist puts in a hole in a tooth to prevent further decay.
例：The longer your child can go without needing a filling, the better.孩子越晚需要补牙就越好。

N-MASS
(糕点的)馅
The filling in something such as a cake, pie, or sandwich is a substance or mixture that is put inside it.
例：Spread some of the filling over each pancake.在每个薄煎饼上摊上一些馅。

N-MASS
(软体家具或垫子的)芯，填充物
The filling in a piece of soft furniture or in a cushion is the soft substance inside it.
例：...second-hand sofas with old-style foam fillings.老式泡沫芯子的二手沙发

ADJ-GRADED
(食物)令人感到饱足的
Food that is filling makes you feel full when you have eaten it.
例：Although it is tasty, crab is very filling.螃蟹虽然好吃，却容易让人感到饱足。

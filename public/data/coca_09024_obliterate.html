obliterate 
英[əˈblɪtəreɪt] 美[əˈblɪtəˌret,oˈblɪt-]
 
vt.涂去，擦掉；使消失；使被忘却；忘掉；
 "

VERB
消灭;毁掉;清除
If something obliterates an object or place, it destroys it completely.
例：Their warheads are enough to obliterate the world several times over...他们拥有的弹头足以将世界毁灭好几次。
例：Whole villages were obliterated by fire.整座整座的村庄都被大火所吞噬。

VERB
抹除，忘却（回忆等）
If you obliterate something such as a memory, emotion, or thought, you remove it completely from your mind.
例：There was time enough to obliterate memories of how things once were for him.时间足以抹去他对过去经历的记忆。

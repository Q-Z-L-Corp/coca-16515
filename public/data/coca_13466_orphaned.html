N-COUNT
孤儿
An orphan is a child whose parents are dead.
例：I'm an orphan and pretty much grew up on my own.我是个孤儿，差不多是靠自己长大的。
例：...a young orphan girl brought up by peasants...由农民抚养长大的幼小孤女

V-PASSIVE
使成为孤儿
If a child is orphaned, their parents die, or their remaining parent dies.
例：Jones was orphaned at the age of ten, and taken in by next-door neighbours...琼斯10岁时成为孤儿，被隔壁的邻居收养。
例：By the end of the decade, some ten million children are expected to have been orphaned by the disease.到这个10年结束时，大约1,000万儿童将会因为这种疾病而失去双亲。

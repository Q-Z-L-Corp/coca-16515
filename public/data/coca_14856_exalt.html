exalt 
英[ɪgˈzɔ:lt] 美[ɪɡˈzɔlt]
 
vt.提高，提升；赞扬；使得意；加强；
 "

VERB
颂扬;赞扬
To exalt someone or something means to praise them very highly.
例：However difficult she might have been, this book exalts her as both mother and muse...尽管她可能很难相处，这本书还是对她给予了高度赞扬，称她既是母亲又是诗人。
例：His work exalts all those virtues that we, as Americans, are taught to hold dear.他的作品颂扬了所有那些身为美国人应该珍视的美德。

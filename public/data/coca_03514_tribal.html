ADJ
部落的;部族的
Tribal is used to describe things relating to or belonging to tribes and the way that they are organized.
例：They would go back to their tribal lands.他们会回到自己部族的地盘上。
例：…their rich heritage of ancient tribal customs.他们古老部落风俗的丰富遗产

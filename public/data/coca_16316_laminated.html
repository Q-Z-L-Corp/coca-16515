ADJ
由薄片叠成的；层压的；夹层的
Material such as wood or plastic that is laminated consists of several thin sheets or layers that are stuck together.
例：Modern windscreens are made from laminated glass.现在的挡风玻璃都由夹层玻璃制成。

ADJ
覆有薄片护层的；（尤指）覆有透明（或有色）塑料薄膜的
A product that is laminated is covered with a thin sheet of something, especially clear or coloured plastic, in order to protect it.
例：The photographs were mounted on laminated cards.照片镶在压膜卡片上。
例：...laminated work surfaces.贴了膜的工作台

ADJ-GRADED
(行为)致命的，后果极严重的，毁灭性的
A fatal action has very undesirable effects.
例：It would clearly be fatal for Europe to quarrel seriously with America...欧洲若与美国反目显然会有致命的后果。
例：He made the fatal mistake of compromising early...他犯了过早妥协的致命错误。

ADJ
(事故或疾病)致命的，致死的
A fatal accident or illness causes someone's death.
例：...the fatal stabbing of a police sergeant...对警官致命的一刺
例：A hospital spokesman said she had suffered a fatal heart attack.医院发言人说她死于心脏病发作。

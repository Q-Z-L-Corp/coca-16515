ADJ-GRADED
奇特的；古色古香的;古雅的
Something that is quaint is attractive because it is unusual and rather old-fashioned.
例：...a small, quaint town with narrow streets and traditional half-timbered houses...一座有着狭窄街道和传统的露木架房屋的古色古香的小镇
例：That's how concepts like general welfare start to sound quaint in this age.就这样，民众福利这一类概念在这个时代开始变得陈旧起来。

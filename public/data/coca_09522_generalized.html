generalized 
英[ˈdʒenrəlaɪzd] 美[ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzd]
 
adj.广泛的；普遍的；非具体的；整体的；v.概括，归纳；推论(generalize的过去式和过去分词)；推广，普及；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
普遍的；全面的
Generalized means involving many different things, rather than one or two specific things.
例：...a generalised discussion about admirable singers.对受追捧歌手方方面面的讨论
例：...generalised feelings of inadequacy.普遍的匮乏感

ADJ
（疾病）全身性的，非局部的
You use generalized to describe medical conditions or problems which affect the whole of someone's body, or the whole of a part of their body.
例：She experienced an increase in generalized aches and pains.她感到周身疼痛加剧。
例：...generalised muscle disorders.全身性肌肉疾病

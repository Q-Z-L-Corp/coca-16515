ADJ-GRADED
担心的;发愁的;焦虑的
When you are worried, you are unhappy because you keep thinking about problems that you have or about unpleasant things that might happen in the future.
例：He seemed very worried...他好像非常担心。
例：If you're at all worried about his progress, do discuss it with one of his teachers...如果你还有一点儿担心他的学业的话，一定要找一位教他的老师谈一谈。

sprinkle 
英[ˈsprɪŋkl] 美[ˈsprɪŋkəl]
 
v.洒，撒；用…点缀；下小雨；n.小雨；少量；
 "

VERB
洒(水);撒(粉末)
If you sprinkle a thing with something such as a liquid or powder, you scatter the liquid or powder over it.
例：Sprinkle the meat with salt and place in the pan...在肉上撒上盐，然后放进平底锅。
例：At the festival, candles are blessed and sprinkled with holy water...在节日里，要对蜡烛祝圣，并洒以圣水。

VERB
使…上零星分布(着…)
If something is sprinkled with particular things, it has a few of them throughout it and they are far apart from each other.
例：Unfortunately, the text is sprinkled with errors...遗憾的是，文本中零星地有一些错误。
例：Men in green army uniforms are sprinkled throughout the huge auditorium.穿着绿色军服的人星星点点地分布在整个大礼堂中。

VERB
下毛毛细雨
If it is sprinkling, it is raining very lightly.

fascinating 
英[ˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋ] 美[ˈfæsəˌnetɪŋ]
 
adj.迷人的，有极大吸引力的；使人神魂颠倒的；v.使…陶醉(fascinate的ing形式)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
迷人的;让人着迷的;极有吸引力的
If you describe something as fascinating, you find it very interesting and attractive, and your thoughts tend to concentrate on it.
例：Madagascar is the most fascinating place I have ever been to...马达加斯加是我去过的最迷人的地方。
例：Her perceptions and intuitions about human nature were fascinating.她对人性的理解和感知引人入胜。

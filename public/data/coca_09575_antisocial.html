antisocial 
英[ˌæntiˈsəʊʃl] 美[ˌæntiˈsoʊʃl]
 
adj.反社会的；危害社会安宁的；违反公益的；不喜欢社交的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不爱交际的；孤僻的；不合群的
Someone who is anti-social is unwilling to meet and be friendly with other people.
例：...teenagers who will become aggressive and anti-social.将会变得好斗、孤僻的青少年一代

ADJ-GRADED
（行为）反社会的，危害社会的，令人讨厌的
Anti-social behaviour is annoying or upsetting to other people.
例：Playing these games can lead to anti-social behaviour.玩这类游戏会引发反社会行为。

ADJ-GRADED
侮辱的;侮慢的;无礼的
Something that is insulting is rude or offensive.
例：The article was insulting to the families of British citizens...那篇文章侮辱了英国公民家庭。
例：One of the apprentices made an insulting remark to a passing officer.其中一名学徒对着一名路过的警官说了一句侮辱他的话。

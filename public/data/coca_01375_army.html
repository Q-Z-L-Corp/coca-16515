army 
英[ˈɑ:mi] 美[ˈɑrmi]
 
n.军队；陆军；团体；野战军；
 "

N-COUNT-COLL
陆军;陆军部队
An army is a large organized group of people who are armed and trained to fight on land in a war. Most armies are organized and controlled by governments.
例：After returning from France, he joined the army...从法国回来之后，他参了军。
例：The army is about to launch a major offensive.陆军部队即将发动一次大规模进攻。

N-COUNT-COLL
大批;大群
An army of people, animals, or things is a large number of them, especially when they are regarded as a force of some kind.
例：...data collected by an army of volunteers.由大批志愿者收集的资料
例：...armies of shoppers looking for bargains.成群的寻找便宜货的购物者

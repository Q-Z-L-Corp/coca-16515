ninth 
英[naɪnθ] 美[naɪnθ]
 
n.第九；九分之一；adj.第九的；九分之一的；
 "

ORD
第九
The ninth item in a series is the one that you count as number nine.
例：...January the ninth.1月9日
例：...students in the ninth grade.9年级学生

FRACTION
九分之一
A ninth is one of nine equal parts of something.
例：In Brussels the dollar rose by a ninth of a cent...在布鲁塞尔美元升值了1/9美分。
例：What you see is only the tip. Eight ninths of it is under the sea.你看到的只是一角，8/9的部分在海里。

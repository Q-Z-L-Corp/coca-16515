reprehensible 
英[ˌreprɪˈhensəbl] 美[ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsəbəl]
 
adj.应受谴责的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
应受斥责的;应该谴责的
If you think that a type of behaviour or an idea is very bad and morally wrong, you can say that it is reprehensible .
例：Mr Cramer said the violence by anti-government protestors was reprehensible.克拉默先生说反政府示威者的暴力活动应该受到谴责。
例：...behaving in the most reprehensible manner.最应该受到谴责的行为

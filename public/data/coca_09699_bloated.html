bloated 
英[ˈbləʊtɪd] 美[ˈbloʊtɪd]
 
adj.发胀的；浮肿的；傲慢的；v.使膨胀，使肿胀( bloat的过去式和过去分词)；用盐腌；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（身体或身体某部位）肿胀的，膨胀的，发胀的
If someone's body or a part of their body is bloated, it is much larger than normal, usually because it has a lot of liquid or gas inside it.
例：...the bloated body of a dead bullock...一头死的小阉牛肿胀的尸体
例：His face was bloated.他脸部浮肿。

ADJ-GRADED
吃撑了的；肚子胀的
If you feel bloated after eating a large meal, you feel very full and uncomfortable.
例：Diners do not want to leave the table feeling bloated.食客们不想吃到肚子胀鼓鼓的才离开饭桌。

ADJ-GRADED
（机构）臃肿的，人浮于事的
If you describe an organization as bloated, you mean that it is larger and less efficient than it should be.
例：...its massive state apparatus and bloated bureaucracy.它那庞大的国家机关和臃肿的官僚机构

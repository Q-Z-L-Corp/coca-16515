faculty 
英[ˈfæklti] 美[ˈfækəlti]
 
n.能力，才能；全体教职员；<英>（大学的）专科，系；特权，特许；
 "

N-COUNT
官能;能力
Your faculties are your physical and mental abilities.
例：He was drunk and not in control of his faculties...他喝醉了，浑身都不听使唤了。
例：It is also a myth that the faculty of hearing is greatly increased in blind people.盲人的听力会大幅增强也是一种谬谈。

N-VAR
(大学的)院系;(大学院系的)教职工
A faculty is a group of related departments in some universities, or the people who work in them.
例：...the Faculty of Social and Political Sciences.社会政治系

N-VAR
(大学、学院或院系的)全体教员
A faculty is all the teaching staff of a university or college, or of one department.
例：The faculty agreed on a change in the requirements...全体教师一致同意修改规定。
例：How can faculty improve their teaching so as to encourage creativity?教师们如何才能改进教学以激发创造性呢？

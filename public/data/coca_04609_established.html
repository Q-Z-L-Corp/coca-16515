ADJ-GRADED
已被认可的;已被接受的
If you use established to describe something such as an organization, you mean that it is officially recognized or generally approved of because it has existed for a long time.
例：Their religious adherence is not to the established church.他们信仰的不是国教。
例：...qualified lawyers with established and prestigious business addresses.在一些颇有声望的老牌事务所工作的资深律师们

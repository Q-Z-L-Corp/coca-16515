defiance 
英[dɪˈfaɪəns] 美[dɪˈfaɪəns]
 
n.蔑视；挑战；反抗；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
违抗;蔑视;挑战
Defiance is behaviour or an attitude which shows that you are not willing to obey someone.
例：...his courageous defiance of the government.他对政府的勇敢挑战

PHRASE
违抗（某人）；无视，不顾(规定、法律等)
If you do something in defiance of a person, rule, or law, you do it even though you know that you are not allowed to do it.
例：Thousands of people have taken to the streets in defiance of the curfew.成千上万的人不顾宵禁涌上街头。

ADV
特别；尤其
You use particularly to indicate that what you are saying applies especially to one thing or situation.
例：Keep your office space looking good, particularly your desk...保持办公环境的整洁，尤其是你的桌子。
例：More local employment will be created, particularly in service industries...当地会创造出更多的就业机会，尤其是服务行业。

ADV
格外；异乎寻常地
Particularly means more than usual or more than other things.
例：Progress has been particularly disap-pointing...进展情况格外令人失望。
例：I particularly liked the wooden chests and chairs.我格外喜欢木箱子和木椅子。

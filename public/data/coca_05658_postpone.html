VERB
推迟；使延期；延缓
If you postpone an event, you delay it or arrange for it to take place at a later time than was originally planned.
例：He decided to postpone the expedition until the following day...他决定把探险推迟到第二天。
例：The visit has now been postponed indefinitely.参观现在已经被无限期推迟了。

routine 
英[ru:ˈti:n] 美[ruˈtin]
 
n.常规；例行程序；生活乏味；（演出中的）一套动作；adj.例行的；常规的；日常的；普通的；
 "

N-VAR
惯例;常规;例行公事
A routine is the usual series of things that you do at a particular time. A routine is also the practice of regularly doing things in a fixed order.
例：The players had to change their daily routine and lifestyle...这些运动员不得不改变他们的日常生活习惯和方式。
例：They include the floor exercises as a regular part of their fitness routine...他们把自由体操做为日常健身运动的固定环节。

ADJ
例行的;常规的;惯常的
You use routine to describe activities that are done as a normal part of a job or process.
例：...a series of routine medical tests...一系列常规的医疗检查
例：The operator has to be able to carry out routine maintenance of the machine.操作员必须能对机器进行日常维护。

ADJ-GRADED
普通的;平常的;一般的;乏味的
A routine situation, action, or event is one which seems completely ordinary, rather than interesting, exciting, or different.
例：So many days are routine and uninteresting, especially in winter.许多日子都过得平淡乏味，尤其是在冬天。
例：...this routine thriller about a CIA man and a KGB operative.这部关于一个中情局特工和一个克格勃特务的平淡无奇的惊悚片

N-VAR
一成不变;平淡乏味
You use routine to refer to a way of life that is uninteresting and ordinary, or hardly ever changes.
例：...the mundane routine of her life...她平淡乏味的生活
例：Family holidays are meant to be a break from routine.家庭假日就是从平淡的日常生活中解脱一下。

N-COUNT
例行程序
A routine is a computer program, or part of a program, that performs a specific function.
例：... an installation routine.一个安装程序

N-COUNT
（演出中的）一组笑话（或谈话等），一套动作
A routine is a short sequence of jokes, remarks, actions, or movements that forms part of a longer performance.
例：... like a Marx Brothers routine.像马克思兄弟风格的搞笑套路
例：...an athletic dance routine.一套体育舞蹈动作

ecstasy 
英[ˈekstəsi] 美[ˈɛkstəsi]
 
n.狂喜；出神，忘形；无法自控的情绪；迷幻药；
 "

N-VAR
狂喜;极乐
Ecstasy is a feeling of very great happiness.
例：...a state of almost religious ecstasy.近乎宗教狂喜的状态
例：...the agony and ecstasy of holiday romance.假日恋情的大喜大悲

N-UNCOUNT
摇头丸;迷幻药
Ecstasy is an illegal drug which makes people feel happy and energetic.

PHRASE
欣喜若狂；处于狂热状态
If you are in ecstasy about something, you are very excited about it. If you go into ecstasies, you become very excited.
例：My father was in ecstasy when I won my scholarship...我获得奖学金时父亲欣喜若狂。
例：She went into ecstasies over actors.她对男演员们着了迷。

maroon 
英[məˈru:n] 美[məˈrun]
 
n.逃亡黑奴；孤立的人；酱紫色，褐红色；vt.把…放逐到孤岛；（因洪水等）使孤立；闲荡；adj.酱紫色的，褐红色的；
 "

COLOUR
紫褐色;栗色
Something that is maroon is dark reddish-purple in colour.
例：...maroon velvet curtains.绛紫色的天鹅绒窗帘

VERB
困住;使无法逃脱
If someone is marooned somewhere, they are left in a place that is difficult for them to escape from.
例：Five couples were marooned in their caravans when the River Avon broke its banks.埃文河决堤的时候，有5对夫妇被困在了他们的房车里。
例：...after years of being marooned on a desert island.在荒岛上被困数年之后

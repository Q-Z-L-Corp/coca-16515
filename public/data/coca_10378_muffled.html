ADJ
(穿许多厚衣服把自己的身体和脸)裹起来的，包住的
If you are muffled, you are wearing a lot of heavy clothes so that very little of your body or face is visible.
例：...children muffled in scarves and woolly hats.裹着围巾，戴着毛线帽的孩子们

enamel 
英[ɪˈnæml] 美[ɪˈnæməl]
 
n.搪瓷；珐琅；指甲油；vt.给…上珐琅；在…涂瓷漆；
 "

N-MASS
搪瓷;珐琅;釉
Enamel is a substance like glass which can be heated and put onto metal, glass, or pottery in order to decorate or protect it.
例：...a white enamel saucepan on the oil stove.煤油炉上的白色搪瓷炖锅
例：...enamel baths.搪瓷浴缸

N-MASS
（尤指用于金属和木材上漆的）瓷漆,瓷釉
Enamel is a hard, shiny paint that is used especially for painting metal and wood.
例：...enamel polymer paints...聚合瓷漆
例：He relied on translucent enamels to produce vivid, glowing pictures.他利用半透明瓷漆绘制出鲜艳生动的图画。

N-UNCOUNT
（牙齿的）珐琅质，釉质
Enamel is the hard white substance that forms the outer part of a tooth.

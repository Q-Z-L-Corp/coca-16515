reminiscent 
英[ˌremɪˈnɪsnt] 美[ˌrɛməˈnɪsənt]
 
adj.怀旧的；回忆往事的；使人联想…的；n.回忆录作者；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
使人想起的;使人忆起的
If you say that one thing is reminiscent of another, you mean that it reminds you of it.
例：The decor was reminiscent of a municipal arts-and-leisure centre...这种布置让人想起都市里的艺术休闲中心。
例：She bowed her head in a gesture somehow reminiscent of royalty.她低头的姿势让人想起皇室风范。

ADJ-GRADED
怀旧的;缅怀过去的
A reminiscent look or smile is a look or smile which shows that you are remembering something from the past, usually with pleasure.
例：A slow, reminiscent smile spread over her face.怀旧的微笑慢慢浮现在她脸上。

adj.有意的；预期的；n.已订婚者；v.打算(intend的过去式和过去分词)；意指；

ADJ
打算中的;预期的;预定的
You use intended to describe the thing you are trying to achieve or person you are trying to affect.
例：The intended target had been a military building...原定目标是一座军事大楼。
例：Keith hoped the obvious sarcasm would have its intended effect...基思希望这一明显的讽刺能达到预期的效果。

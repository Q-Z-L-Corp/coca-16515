N-UNCOUNT
不赞同;反对;不许可
If you feel or show disapproval of something or someone, you feel or show that you do not approve of them.
例：His action had been greeted with almost universal disapproval.他的行动几乎遭到了一致的反对。
例：...a society that registered its disapproval of alcohol by banning it.通过禁酒来反对饮酒

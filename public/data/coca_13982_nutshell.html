nutshell 
英[ˈnʌtʃel] 美[ˈnʌtˌʃɛl]
 
n.坚果的外壳；简言之，一言以蔽之；

PHRASE
概括地说;简言之
You can use in a nutshell to indicate that you are saying something in a very brief way, using few words.
例：In a nutshell, the owners thought they knew best...简而言之，业主们认为自己最了解情况。
例：This, in a nutshell, is what Richard Chaplin appears to have done.简言之，这好像就是理查德·查普林所做的一切。

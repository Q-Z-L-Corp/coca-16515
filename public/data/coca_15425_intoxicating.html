intoxicating 
英[ɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪtɪŋ] 美[ɪnˈtɑksɪkeɪtɪŋ]
 
adj.醉人的；令人陶醉的；使兴奋的；（指酒类）蒸馏的（非发酵的）；v.使喝醉( intoxicate的现在分词 )；使陶醉；使激动不已；使欣喜若狂；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(饮料)醉人的,致醉的
Intoxicating drink contains alcohol and can make you drunk.
例：...intoxicating liquor.喝了容易上头的烈酒

ADJ-GRADED
令人陶醉的;令人极其兴奋的
If you describe something as intoxicating, you mean that it makes you feel a strong sense of excitement or happiness.
例：...the intoxicating fragrance of lilies...百合花的沁人芳香
例：The music is pulsating and the atmosphere is intoxicating.音乐在有节奏地响着,气氛令人陶醉。

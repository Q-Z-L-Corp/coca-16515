ADJ
长距离的；长途的；远途的
Long-distance is used to describe travel between places that are far apart.
例：Trains are reliable, cheap and best for long-distance journeys.火车安全可靠、费用低廉，最适宜长途旅行。
例：...the first long-distance sea voyages made by Portuguese navigators at the end of the 15th century.15世纪末葡萄牙航海家进行的首次远航

ADJ
（通讯）长途的
Long-distance is used to describe communication that takes place between people who are far apart.
例：He received a long-distance phone call from his girlfriend in Colorado.他接到女朋友从科罗拉多打来的长途电话。

destroyed

VERB
毁坏;破坏;毁灭;摧毁
To destroy something means to cause so much damage to it that it is completely ruined or does not exist any more.
例：That's a sure recipe for destroying the economy and creating chaos...那样做肯定会破坏经济、制造混乱。
例：No one was injured in the explosion, but the building was completely destroyed...没有人在爆炸中受伤，但大楼遭到了彻底毁坏。

VERB
毁掉…的生活;使垮掉
To destroy someone means to ruin their life or to make their situation impossible to bear.
例：If I was younger or more naive, the criticism would have destroyed me.如果我再年轻一些，或是再单纯一些，那些批评可能就会把我给毁了。

VERB
杀死，消灭（生病或具危险性的动物）
If an animal is destroyed, it is killed, either because it is ill or because it is dangerous.
例：Lindsay was unhurt but the horse had to be destroyed.林赛没有受伤，但那匹马却不得不被人道毁灭。

profusion 
英[prəˈfju:ʒn] 美[prəˈfjuʒən,pro-]
 
n.丰富，充沛；慷慨，大方；浪费，挥霍；
 "

N-SING-COLL
大量；丰富
If there is a profusion of something or if it occurs in profusion, there is a very large quantity or variety of it.
例：The Dart is a delightful river with a profusion of wild flowers along its banks...达特河是一条令人心旷神怡的河，岸边到处开着野花。
例：Olive groves, grapes, and citrus fruits grow in profusion.橄榄、葡萄和柑橘类水果产量丰富。

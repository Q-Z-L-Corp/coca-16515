ADJ-GRADED
综合性的;全面的
Something that is comprehensive includes everything that is needed or relevant.
例：The Rough Guide to Nepal is a comprehensive guide to the region.《尼泊尔概况》是一本全面介绍该地区的旅游指南。

N-COUNT; N-IN-NAMES
(英国的公立性)综合中学
In Britain, a comprehensive is a state school in which children of all abilities are taught together.
例：...Birmingham's inner-city comprehensives...伯明翰旧城区的综合中学
例：She taught French at Cheam Comprehensive in South London.她在伦敦南部的奇姆综合中学教法语。

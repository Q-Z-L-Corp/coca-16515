英[dɪ'sendɪŋ] 美[dɪ'sendɪŋ]
 
n.递减；v.下来( descend的现在分词 )；下去；下降；下斜；

ADJ
（次序）下降的，递减的
When a group of things is listed or arranged in descending order, each thing is smaller or less important than the thing before it.
例：All the other ingredients, including water, have to be listed in descending order by weight.所有其他原料，包括水在内，都需要根据重量依次降序排列。

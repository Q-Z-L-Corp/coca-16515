N-UNCOUNT
代表；代替
If you do something by proxy, you arrange for someone else to do it for you.
例：Those not attending the meeting may vote by proxy.那些未出席会议的可以请他人代为投票。

N-COUNT
代理人；代替者；替代物
A proxy is a person or thing that is acting or being used in the place of someone or something else.
例：Price differences are used as a proxy for differences in quality.一分钱一分货。

n.甜食，糕点；
 "

N-COUNT
甜食;甜点
You can refer to a sweet food that someone has made as a confection .
例：...a confection made with honey and nuts.用蜂蜜和坚果制成的甜食

N-COUNT
制作精细的物品
A confection is something that has been made or built and is very complicated or decorated.
例：He found himself staring at an extraordinary architectural confection of old and new.他发现自己盯着一座新旧结合的建筑精品看出了神。

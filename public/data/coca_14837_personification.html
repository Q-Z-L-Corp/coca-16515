personification 
英[pəˌsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn] 美[pərˌsɑnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn]
 
n.人格化；拟人化；
 "

N-SING
体现某种品质（或特点）的人；典型；化身
If you say that someone is the personification of a particular thing or quality, you mean that they are a perfect example of that thing or that they have a lot of that quality.
例：Janis Joplin was the personification of the '60s female rock singer...贾尼斯·乔普林是20世纪60年代摇滚女歌手的代表。
例：He was usually the personification of kindness.他通常都很和善。

N-VAR
拟人化；象征
A personification of something abstract is its representation in the form of a person.
例：...personifications of the attributes of Justice, Prudence and Truth.正义、审慎和真理的化身

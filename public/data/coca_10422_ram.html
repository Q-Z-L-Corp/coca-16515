ram 
英[ræm] 美[ræm]
 
n.公羊；<天>(R-)白羊（星）座；<机>夯；攻城槌；vt.夯实（土等）；猛撞；猛压；反复灌输；vi.（使）猛烈撞击；迅速移动；迫使…明白；
 "

VERB
和…相撞;猛撞;撞击
If a vehicle rams something such as another vehicle, it crashes into it with a lot of force, usually deliberately.
例：The thieves fled, ramming the policeman's car...小偷撞了警车后逃走了。
例：They used a lorry to ram the main gate.他们用卡车猛撞大门。

VERB
猛推
If you ram something somewhere, you push it there with great force.
例：He rammed the key into the lock and kicked the front door open.他把钥匙猛力插进锁里，踢开了前门。

N-COUNT
成年公羊
A ram is an adult male sheep.

PHRASE
充分说明;使透彻理解
If something rams home a message or a point, it makes it clear in a way that is very forceful and that people are likely to listen to.
例：Railway lines are dangerous places and it is up to parents to ram home the dangers to their children...铁路是危险的地方，父母有责任让孩子们清楚地认识到这一点。
例：The report by Marks & Spencer's chairman will ram this point home...玛莎百货公司的主席作的报告会透彻地讲明这一点。

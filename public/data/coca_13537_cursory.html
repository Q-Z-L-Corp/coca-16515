ADJ-GRADED
仓促的;匆匆的;粗略的
A cursory glance or examination is a brief one in which you do not pay much attention to detail.
例：Burke cast a cursory glance at the menu, then flapped it shut...伯克匆匆地瞥了一眼菜单，然后啪地合上了。
例：I gave the letter a fairly cursory reading.我大略地读了一下那封信。

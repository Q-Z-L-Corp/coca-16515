commute 
英[kəˈmju:t] 美[kəˈmjut]
 
vi.通勤；代偿；vt.减刑；交换；用…交换；使…变成；n.通勤来往；通勤来往的路程；
 "

VERB
(经很远的路程)上下班往返;通勤
If you commute, you travel a long distance every day between your home and your place of work.
例：Mike commutes to London every day...迈克每天都去伦敦上班。
例：McLaren began commuting between Paris and London...麦克拉伦开始往返于巴黎与伦敦之间。

N-COUNT
上下班路程
A commute is the journey that you make when you commute.
例：The average Los Angeles commute is over 60 miles a day.洛杉矶的平均上下班路程为每天60多英里。

VERB
减轻(刑罚)
If a death sentence or prison sentence is commuted to a less serious punishment, it is changed to that punishment.
例：His death sentence was commuted to life imprisonment...他的死刑被减为无期徒刑。
例：Prison sentences have been commuted.服刑期得以减短。

ADJ-GRADED
(虽常事与愿违但)出于好心的，善意的，本意良好的
If you say that a person or their actions are well-meaning, you mean that they intend to be helpful or kind but they are unsuccessful or cause problems.
例：He is a well-meaning but ineffectual leader...他是个心有余而力不足的领导者。
例：Even well-meaning attempts at conservation can bring problems.即使是出于善意想开展自然保护也可能会引来麻烦。

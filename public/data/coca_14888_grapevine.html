N-SING
小道消息;传闻
If you hear or learn something on the grapevine, you hear it or learn it in casual conversation with other people.
例：He'd doubtless heard rumours on the grapevine...他无疑是听到了小道消息。
例：I had heard through the grapevine that he was quite critical of what we were doing.我听到有传言说他对我们在做的工作非常不满。

N-COUNT
葡萄藤
A grapevine is a climbing plant on which grapes grow.

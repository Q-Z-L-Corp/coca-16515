N-COUNT
(假想的)火星人
A Martian is an imaginary creature from the planet Mars.
例：Orson Welles managed to convince many Americans that they were being invaded by Martians.奥森·韦尔斯使得许多美国人相信他们正遭到火星人的入侵。

ADJ
火星的；与火星有关的
Something that is Martian exists on or relates to the planet Mars.
例：The Martian atmosphere contains only tiny amounts of water.火星的大气层中只含有极少量的水。

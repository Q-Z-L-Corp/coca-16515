tenth 
英[tenθ] 美[tɛnθ]
 
n.第十；十分之一；adj.第十的，第十个的；十分之一的；
 "

ORD
第十
The tenth item in a series is the one that you count as number ten.

FRACTION
十分之一
A tenth is one of ten equal parts of something.
例：We received only two tenths of an inch of rain during the entire month of June.整个 6 月，我们这里的降水量只有0.2英寸。

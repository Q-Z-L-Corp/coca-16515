N-COUNT
军官
In the armed forces, an officer is a person in a position of authority.
例：...a retired British army officer...退役的英国陆军军官
例：Her husband served during the Civil War as an officer in the White Army.她的丈夫在内战时期是一名白军的军官。

N-COUNT
（机构的）高级职员；（尤指政府机构的）官员，公务员
An officer is a person who has a responsible position in an organization, especially a government organization.
例：...a local authority education officer.当地政府的教育官员

警察；警官
Members of the police force can be referred to as officers .
例：...senior officers in the West Midlands police force...西米德兰兹郡警队的高级警官

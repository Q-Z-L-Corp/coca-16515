drinker 
英[ˈdrɪŋkə(r)] 美[ˈdrɪŋkɚ]
 
n.酒徒；喝的人；（给家禽喂水的）饮水器；
 "

N-COUNT
常饮…者;常喝…者
If someone is a tea drinker or a beer drinker, for example, they regularly drink tea or beer.
例：Are you a coffee drinker?你常喝咖啡吗？

N-COUNT
饮酒者;(尤指)酗酒者，酒徒
If you describe someone as a drinker, you mean that they drink alcohol, especially in large quantities.
例：You don't need to be a smoker or a drinker to risk heart disease...并不是只有抽烟喝酒的人才会犯心脏病。
例：I'm not a heavy drinker.我酒瘾不大。

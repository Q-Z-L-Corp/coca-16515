resignation

N-VAR
辞职书;辞呈
Your resignation is a formal statement of your intention to leave a job or position.
例：Mr Morgan has offered his resignation and it has been accepted.摩根先生己经递交了辞呈，并已获准。
例：...his letter of resignation.他的辞职书

N-UNCOUNT
屈从;顺从;听任;勉强接受
Resignation is the acceptance of an unpleasant situation or fact because you realize that you cannot change it.
例：There was no grief in his expression, only deep resignation...他的表情里没有悲痛，只有深深的无奈。
例：He sighed with profound resignation.他极其无奈地叹了一口气。

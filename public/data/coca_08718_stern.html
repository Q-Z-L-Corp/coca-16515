stern 
英[stɜ:n] 美[stɜrn]
 
adj.严厉的，严峻的；坚定的，不动摇的；严肃的；n.船尾，艉；末端；斯坦恩，英语男子名；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(话语或行动)严厉的，严正的
Stern words or actions are very severe.
例：Mr Straw issued a stern warning to those who persist in violence...斯特劳先生向那些坚持进行暴力活动的人发出了严正警告。
例：He said stern measures would be taken against the killers...他说将严惩凶手。

ADJ-GRADED
(人)严厉的，严格的，苛刻的
Someone who is stern is very serious and strict.
例：Her father was stern and hard to please.她父亲很苛刻，让他满意很难。

N-COUNT
船尾;艉部 from stem to stern
The stern of a boat is the back part of it.

PHRASE
(人)性格坚强的，坚忍的
If you say that someone is made of sterner stuff, you mean that they have a strong personality and are capable of overcoming difficulties and problems.
例：Whoever this woman is, she's made of sterner stuff than I am.不管这个女人是谁，她都比我坚强。

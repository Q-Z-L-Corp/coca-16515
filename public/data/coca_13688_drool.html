vi.流口水；对某人或某物流露出痴迷的神情；痴情地看；迷恋地看；

VERB
对…过分痴迷;对…垂涎欲滴
To drool over someone or something means to look at them with great pleasure, perhaps in an exaggerated or ridiculous way.
例：Fashion editors drooled over every item...时尚编辑们兴奋地注视着每一件东西。
例：Advertisers are already drooling at reports that this might bring 20 million dollars.广告商听闻这或许会带来2,000万美元的收益后已是垂涎欲滴了。

VERB
流口水;垂涎
If a person or animal drools, saliva drops slowly from their mouth.
例：My dog Jacques is drooling on my shoulder.我的狗雅克在我肩膀上流着口水。

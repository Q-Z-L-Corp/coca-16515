custody 
英[ˈkʌstədi] 美[ˈkʌstədi]
 
n.监管；拘留，羁押；抚养权，监护权；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指父母离异后孩子的)监护权
Custody is the legal right to keep and look after a child, especially the right given to a child's mother or father when they get divorced.
例：I'm going to go to court to get custody of the children...我将起诉，要求获得孩子们的监护权。
例：Child custody is normally granted to the mother.孩子的监护权通常判给母亲。

PHRASE
被拘留;在拘禁中
Someone who is in custody or has been taken into custody has been arrested and is being kept in prison until they can be tried in a court.
例：Three people appeared in court and two of them were remanded in custody...有3个人出庭，其中两个被还押候审。
例：She was taken into custody later that day.她那天后来被拘留了。

N-UNCOUNT
(类似监狱的)监禁，关押
If someone is being held in a particular type of custody, they are being kept in a place that is similar to a prison.
例：The youngster got nine months' youth custody...这个少年被处以9个月的管教。
例：Barrett was taken into protective custody.巴雷特被保护性地监禁起来。

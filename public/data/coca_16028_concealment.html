concealment 
英[kənˈsi:lmənt] 美[kənˈsilmənt]
 
n.隐藏；隐瞒；隐匿处；隐藏手段；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
掩盖;隐藏
Concealment is the state of being hidden or the act of hiding something.
例：The criminals vainly sought concealment from the searchlight.罪犯们徒劳地躲闪着探照灯光。
例：...the concealment of weapons.藏匿武器

N-UNCOUNT
隐瞒;掩饰
The concealment of information or a feeling involves keeping it secret.
例：His concealment of his true motives was masterly...他隐瞒自己真实动机的手段十分高明。
例：I think there was deliberate concealment of relevant documents.我认为有人故意隐瞒相关文件。

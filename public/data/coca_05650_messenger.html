n.信使，信差；先驱；[军]通信兵，传令兵；

N-COUNT
送信者;通信员;信使
A messenger takes a message to someone, or takes messages regularly as their job.
例：There will be a messenger at the Airport to collect the photographs from our courier...机场会有一名信使从我们的递送员手中取走照片。

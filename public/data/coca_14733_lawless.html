lawless 
英[ˈlɔ:ləs] 美[ˈlɔlɪs]
 
adj.违法的；法律不及的；无法无天的；无艺；
 "

ADJ
（尤指疯狂暴力的行为）违法的，非法的，不法的
Lawless actions break the law, especially in a wild and violent way.
例：The government recognised there were problems in urban areas but these could never be an excuse for lawless behaviour.政府承认市区存在问题，但决不可拿这些作为不法行为的借口。

ADJ-GRADED
（某一地方或时期）目无法纪的，法网不及的
A lawless place or time is one where people do not respect the law.
例：...lawless inner-city streets plagued by muggings, thefts, assaults and even murder.不断有抢劫、盗窃、伤人乃至谋杀案件发生的无法无天的市中心区街道
例：...people struggling to restore moral values in an increasingly lawless and godless age.在一个越来越罔顾法纪、缺失信仰的时代力图重树道德观的人们

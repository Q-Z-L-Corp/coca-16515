N-UNCOUNT
绝望;孤注一掷;铤而走险;拼命
Desperation is the feeling that you have when you are in such a bad situation that you will try anything to change it.
例：This feeling of desperation and helplessness was common to most of the refugees...大多数难民都有这种绝望和无助的感觉。
例：In desperation I joined a physical exercise class.在绝望中我参加了一个健身班。

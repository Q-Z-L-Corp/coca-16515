profile 
英[ˈprəʊfaɪl] 美[ˈproʊfaɪl]
 
n.侧面，半面；外形，轮廓；[航]翼型；人物简介；vt.描…的轮廓；给…画侧面图；为（某人）写传略；[机]铣出…的轮廓；
 "

N-COUNT
侧面像；侧影像
Your profile is the outline of your face as it is seen when someone is looking at you from the side.
例：His handsome profile was turned away from us.他英俊的侧影从我们眼前转开了。

N-UNCOUNT
（从）侧面（看）
If you see someone in profile, you see them from the side.
例：This picture shows the girl in profile.这张照片照的是女孩的侧面。

N-COUNT
人物简介；传略
A profile of someone is a short article or programme in which their life and character are described.
例：A newspaper published profiles of the candidates' wives.一家报纸刊登了几位候选人夫人的简介。

VERB
为…作传略
To profile someone means to give an account of that person's life and character.
例：Tamar Golan, a Paris-based journalist, profiles the rebel leader.驻巴黎记者塔玛·戈兰对反叛者的首领进行了介绍。

PHRASE
高调/低调；高姿态/低姿态
If someone has a high profile, people notice them and what they do. If you keep a low profile, you avoid doing things that will make people notice you.
例：...a move that would give Egypt a much higher profile in the upcoming peace talks...能使埃及在未来的和谈中更加引人关注的举动
例：Football is a high profile business...足球是一个引人注目的产业。

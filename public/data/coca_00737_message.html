message 
英[ˈmesɪdʒ] 美[ˈmɛsɪdʒ]
 
n.信息；消息；要旨；教训；v.给…发消息；给…留言；
 "

N-COUNT
音信;消息;留言
A message is a piece of information or a request that you send to someone or leave for them when you cannot speak to them directly.
例：I got a message you were trying to reach me...我收到一条留言，说你想跟我联系。
例：Would you like to leave a message?...您要留言吗？

N-COUNT
(书、戏剧等的)要旨，主题思想，寓意
The message that someone is trying to communicate, for example in a book or play, is the idea or point that they are trying to communicate.
例：The report's message was unequivocal...报告中传达的信息是明确的。
例：I no longer want to stay friendly with her but I don't know how to get the message across...我再也不想和她做朋友了，但是又不知如何告诉她。

VERB
(用电脑、手机等)给…发消息，给…留言
If you message someone, you send them a message electronically using a computer or another device such as a mobile phone.
例：People who message a lot feel unpopular if they don't get many back...如果人们发出大量短信但收到的回复却不多，就会觉得自己不受欢迎。
例：She messaged him saying she wished they were together.她给他留言说希望跟他在一起。

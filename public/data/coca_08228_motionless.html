ADJ-GRADED
不动的;静止的
Someone or something that is motionless is not moving at all.
例：He has this ability of being able to remain as motionless as a statue, for hours on end...他有本事连续几个小时一动不动，像雕塑一样。
例：Her hands were motionless...她双手静止不动。

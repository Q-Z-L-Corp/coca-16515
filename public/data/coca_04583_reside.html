reside 
英[rɪˈzaɪd] 美[rɪˈzaɪd]
 
vi.住，居住，（官吏）留驻，驻在；（性质）存在，具备，（权力，权利等）属于，归于；
 "

VERB
居住;定居
If someone resides somewhere, they live there or are staying there.
例：Margaret resides with her invalid mother in a London suburb...玛格丽特同她病弱的母亲住在伦敦郊区。
例：All single full-time students must reside in university residence halls.所有单身的全日制学生都必须住大学宿舍。

VERB
存在于;属于
If a quality resides in something, the thing has that quality.
例：Happiness does not reside in strength or money...幸福不是由实力或金钱决定的。
例：The military is based on the principle that the ultimate authority resides in the armed forces.军队建立的基本原则就是最高权力属于武装部队。

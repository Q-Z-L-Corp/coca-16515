ADJ
未经证实的；无根据的；不确凿的
A claim, accusation, or story that is unsubstantiated has not been proved to be valid or true.
例：I do object to their claim, which I find totally unsubstantiated.我强烈反对他们的说法，那是毫无根据的。
例：...unsubstantiated rumours about his private life.有关他私生活的捕风捉影的谣言

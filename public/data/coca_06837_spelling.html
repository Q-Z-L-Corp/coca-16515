spelling 
英[ˈspelɪŋ] 美[ˈspɛlɪŋ]
 
n.拼写；拼法；v.拼写( spell的现在分词)；意味着；
 "

N-COUNT
拼法;拼写;拼读
A spelling is the correct order of the letters in a word.
例：In most languages adjectives have slightly different spellings for masculine and feminine...在多数语言中，形容词的阳性和阴性的拼法略为不同。
例：If we got a spelling wrong we were forced to get a dictionary out.如果我们拼错了一个字，就会被要求去查字典。

N-UNCOUNT
正确拼写单词的能力;拼写
Spelling is the ability to spell words in the correct way. It is also an attempt to spell a word in the correct way.
例：His spelling is very bad...他的拼写能力很差。
例：...basic skills in reading, writing, grammar and spelling...阅读、写作、语法和拼写等基本技能

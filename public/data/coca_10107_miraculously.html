miraculously 
英[mɪ'rækjələslɪ] 美[məˈrækjələslɪ]
 
adv.奇迹般地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
神奇的;奇迹般的;不可思议的
If you describe a good event as miraculous, you mean that it is very surprising and unexpected.
例：The horse made a miraculous recovery to finish a close third.这匹马奇迹般地恢复了状态，以微弱的劣势位居第三。
例：...a miraculous escape...奇迹般的逃脱

ADJ
超自然的；神奇的
If someone describes a wonderful event as miraculous, they believe that the event was caused by God.
例：...miraculous healing.奇迹般的痊愈
例：...miraculous powers.神奇的力量

PHRASE
在远处；在远方
Further afield or farther afield means in places or areas other than the nearest or most obvious one.
例：They enjoy participating in a wide variety of activities, both locally and further afield...他们乐于参与本地及外地的各种活动。
例：You are not allowed to bring plants in from further afield without a licence.未经许可不得从外地带入植物。

PHRASE
从远处；从远方
If someone comes from far afield, they come from a long way away.
例：Many of those arrested came from far afield.那些被捕者有许多来自很远的地方。

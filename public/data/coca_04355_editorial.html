editorial 
英[ˌedɪˈtɔ:riəl] 美[ˌɛdɪˈtɔriəl, -ˈtor-]
 
adj.编辑的，主笔的；〈美〉社论的；n.社论（美）；
 "

ADJ
编辑的；编者的；主编的
Editorial means involved in preparing a newspaper, magazine, or book for publication.
例：He has been on the editorial staff of 'Private Eye' since 1963...自1963年以来，他一直是《第三只眼》杂志的编辑。
例：I went to the editorial board meetings when I had the time.我一有空就去参加编委会会议。

ADJ
（报纸、杂志、电视节目等）立场（或观点等）的;社论（式）的
Editorial means involving the attitudes, opinions, and contents of something such as a newspaper, magazine, or television programme.
例：We are not about to change our editorial policy.我们不会改变我们的社论方针。

N-COUNT
（报纸的）社论
An editorial is an article in a newspaper which gives the opinion of the editor or owner on a topic or item of news.
例：In an editorial, The Independent suggests the victory could turn nasty.《独立报》在一篇社论中暗示这场胜利可能会变味。

N-COUNT
（电视或电台）评论
An editorial on television or radio is an item which gives the opinion of the network or radio station.

beautifully 
英[ˈbju:tɪfli] 美[ˈbjutəfəlɪ]
 
adv.美丽地；漂亮地；<口>出色地；完美地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
美丽的；漂亮的
A beautiful person is very attractive to look at.
例：She was a very beautiful woman...她是个大美女。
例：To me he is the most beautiful child in the world.对我来说，他是世界上最美丽的孩子。

ADJ-GRADED
美好的；极好的；美妙的
If you describe something as beautiful, you mean that it is very attractive or pleasing.
例：New England is beautiful...新英格兰很美。
例：It was a beautiful morning...那是一个美妙的早晨。

ADJ-GRADED
巧妙的；娴熟的；漂亮的
You can describe something that someone does as beautiful when they do it very skilfully.
例：That's a beautiful shot!射得漂亮！

ADJ
朴素的；低调的；不夸张的；不惹眼的
If you describe a style, colour, or effect as understated, you mean that it is not obvious.
例：I have always liked understated clothes — simple shapes which take a lot of hard work to get right.我一直喜欢不太惹眼的衣服——简单的款式需要下大工夫才能弄得恰到好处。
例：...his typically understated humour.他那典型的低调幽默

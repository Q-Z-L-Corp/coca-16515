deference 
英[ˈdefərəns] 美[ˈdɛfərəns, ˈdɛfrəns]
 
n.顺从；依从；尊重；敬重；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（尤指对位重者的）敬重，尊敬，遵从
Deference is a polite and respectful attitude towards someone, especially because they have an important position.
例：The old sense of deference and restraint in royal reporting has vanished...在对皇室进行的新闻报道中，那种由来已久的尊重和收敛意识已经消失了。
例：Out of deference to him, I lowered my head as he prayed.出于对他的敬重，在他祈祷时我低下了头。

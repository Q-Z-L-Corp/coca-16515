N-UNCOUNT
（从高处俯视产生的）眩晕，头晕;恐高症
If you get vertigo when you look down from a high place, you feel unsteady and sick.
例：If you have vertigo it seems as if the whole room is spinning round you...如果你头晕，就会觉得整个房间都旋转起来。
例：He had a dreadful attack of vertigo.他忽然头晕得厉害。

N-COUNT
炸弹；爆炸装置
A bomb is a device which explodes and damages or destroys a large area.
例：Bombs went off at two London train stations...伦敦的两个地铁站发生了炸弹爆炸事件。
例：It's not known who planted the bomb...安放炸弹者身份不明。

N-SING
核武器；核弹
Nuclear weapons are sometimes referred to as the bomb .
例：They are generally thought to have the bomb.人们普遍认为他们拥有核武器。

VERB
轰炸；向…投炸弹
When people bomb a place, they attack it with bombs.
例：Airforce jets bombed the airport.空军的喷气式飞机轰炸了该机场。

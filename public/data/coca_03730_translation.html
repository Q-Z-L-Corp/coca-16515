translation 
英[trænsˈleɪʃn] 美[trænsˈleʃən, trænz-]
 
n.翻译；译本；转变；转化；
 "

N-COUNT
译文;译本
A translation is a piece of writing or speech that has been translated from a different language.
例：...MacNiece's excellent English translation of 'Faust'...麦克尼斯精彩的《浮士德》英文译本
例：I've only read Solzhenitsyn in translation.索尔仁尼琴的作品我只读过译本。

PHRASE
翻译中意义受损(或流失)
If you say that a quality of something has been lost in translation, you mean that it is not very good as a result of being translated into another language or told in a different way.
例：Much of the wit is lost in translation…很多诙谐的成分都在翻译中丢失了。
例：French filmmakers say American remakes are losing something in the translation.法国电影人说美国翻拍的版本丧失了原作的部分精髓。

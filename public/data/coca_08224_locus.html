N-COUNT
（发生）地点；核心；中心
The locus of something is the place where it happens or the most important area or point with which it is associated.
例：Barcelona is the locus of Spanish industry...巴塞罗那是西班牙工业中心。
例：Thereafter, the military remained the locus of real power.自此之后，军方一直掌握着实权。

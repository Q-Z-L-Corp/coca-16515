ADJ-GRADED
（空气、气候）潮湿的，湿热的
You use humid to describe an atmosphere or climate that is very damp, and usually very hot.
例：Visitors can expect hot and humid conditions...游客会遇到炎热潮湿的天气状况。
例：The day is overcast and humid.天气多云潮湿。

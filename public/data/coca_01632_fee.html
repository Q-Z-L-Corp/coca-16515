fee 
英[fi:] 美[fi]
 
n.（加入组织或做某事付的）费；专业服务费，业务报酬；小费，赏钱；[法] 继承的产业；vt.付给报酬，给小费；〈英〉雇用，聘请；
 "

N-COUNT
费;费用
A fee is a sum of money that you pay to be allowed to do something.
例：He hadn't paid his television licence fee.他尚未缴纳电视机使用许可费。

N-COUNT
服务费;报酬;酬金
A fee is the amount of money that a person or organization is paid for a particular job or service that they provide.
例：Find out how much your surveyor's and solicitor's fees will be.弄清你要向鉴定员和律师支付多少费用。

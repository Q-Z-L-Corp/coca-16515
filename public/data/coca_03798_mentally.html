mentally 
英[ˈmentəli] 美[ˈmɛntlɪ]

 "

ADJ
智力的;脑力的;思考的
Mental means relating to the process of thinking.
例：...the mental development of children.儿童的智力发展
例：...intensive mental effort.高强度的脑力劳动

ADJ
心理上的;精神上的
Mental means relating to the state or the health of a person's mind.
例：The mental state that had created her psychosis was no longer present.导致她精神失常的那种心理状态已经消失了。
例：...mental health problems.心理健康问题

ADJ
内心的;在头脑中进行的
A mental act is one that involves only thinking and not physical action.
例：Practise mental arithmetic when you go out shopping...外出购物的时候练习一下心算。
例：Graham made a quick mental calculation.格雷厄姆很快就心算出来了。

ADJ-GRADED
疯狂的;发疯的
If you say that someone is mental, you mean that you think they are mad.
例：I just said to him 'you must be mental'.我刚才对他说：“你一定是疯了。”

PHRASE
记在脑子里;把…牢记在心
If you make a mental note of something, you make an effort to store it in your memory so that you will not forget it.
例：She made a mental note to have his prescription refilled.她记着要拿他的处方再配一回药。
例：She made a mental note not to sit anywhere near him.她心里牢牢记着不坐在他的附近。

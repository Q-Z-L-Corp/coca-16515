chute 
英[ʃu:t] 美[ʃut]
 
n.斜槽，滑道；降落伞；vt.用斜槽或斜道运送；vi.顺斜道而下，在斜槽或滑道中滑行；
 "

N-COUNT
滑道;斜道;溜槽
A chute is a steep, narrow slope down which people or things can slide.
例：Passengers escaped from the plane's front four exits by sliding down emergency chutes.乘客们从飞机前部4个出口处的紧急滑道滑下逃生了。

N-COUNT
降落伞
A chute is a parachute.
例：You can release the chute with either hand, but it is easier to do it with the left.你随便哪只手都能打开降落伞，不过用左手容易些。

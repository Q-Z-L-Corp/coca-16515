ADJ-GRADED
心理扭曲的;变态的
If you describe a person as twisted, you dislike them because you think they are strange in an unpleasant way.
例：...a twisted man who shot at the president...向总统开枪的一个心理不正常的人
例：The letter showed horribly clearly the workings of a twisted mind.那封信非常清楚地表明了一个变态者的思维方式。

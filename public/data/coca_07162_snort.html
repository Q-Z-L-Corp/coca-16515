snort 
英[snɔ:t] 美[snɔrt]
 
v.用鼻子哼（以表示不耐烦，轻蔑等）；（马等）喷响鼻子；（轻蔑、愤怒时）高声大笑；汽锅)喷汽；n.喷鼻息，鼻息声；〈美俚〉一口酒；
 "

VERB
(人或牲畜)喷鼻息;(人表示不赞成、可笑等)哼鼻子，嗤之以鼻
When people or animals snort, they breathe air noisily out through their noses. People sometimes snort in order to express disapproval or amusement.
例：Harrell snorted with laughter...哈勒尔扑哧一声笑了。
例：He snorted loudly and shook his head.他响亮地哼了一声，摇了摇头。

VERB
(表示轻蔑)哼着鼻子说
If someone snorts something, they say it in a way that shows contempt.
例：'Reports,' he snorted. 'Anyone can write reports.'“报告，”他哼着鼻子说，“谁都会写。”

VERB
吸食(毒品)
To snort a drug such as cocaine means to breathe it in quickly through your nose.
例：He died of cardiac arrest after snorting cocaine.他死于吸食可卡因之后的心脏停搏。

goof 
英[gu:f] 美[ɡuf]
 
n.呆瓜；v.弄糟；混；打发时间；出大错；
 "

VERB
犯愚蠢的错误；出大差错
If you goof or goof up, you make a silly mistake.
例：We goofed last week at the end of our interview with singer Annie Ross.上周我们采访歌手安妮·罗斯，结果到快结束时犯了个愚蠢的错误。

N-COUNT
傻瓜；笨蛋
If you call someone a goof, you think they are silly.
例：I could write for TV as well as any of those goofs.我写的电视剧肯定一点儿不比那帮笨蛋写得差。

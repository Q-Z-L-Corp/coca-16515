hardy 
英[ˈhɑ:di] 美[ˈhɑrdi]
 
adj.强壮的；大胆，勇敢的；鲁莽的；[植]耐寒的；n.强壮的人；耐旱植物；方柄凿；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（植物）耐寒的
Plants that are hardy are able to survive cold weather.
例：The silver-leaved varieties of cyclamen are not quite as hardy.银叶仙客来品种不太耐寒。

ADJ-GRADED
（人或动物）强壮的，适应力强的
People and animals that are hardy are strong and able to cope with difficult conditions.
例：It should not surprise us that such an environment has produced a hardy and independent people...这样的环境造就了一个吃苦耐劳、独立自主的民族，对此我们不应该感到意外。
例：Hardy antelope wander in from the desert.适应力强的羚羊从沙漠游荡到了这里。

ADJ-GRADED
坚定不移的;非常忠诚的;顽强的
If you describe a group of people as hardy, you mean that they have been very patient or loyal, or have been trying hard to do something in difficult conditions.
例：...the ten hardy supporters who had made the trek to Dublin from Riga.从里加长途跋涉到都柏林的10名铁杆支持者

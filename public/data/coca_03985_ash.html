N-UNCOUNT
灰；灰烬
Ash is the grey or black powdery substance that is left after something is burnt. You can also refer to this substance as ashes .
例：A cloud of volcanic ash is spreading across wide areas of the Philippines...火山灰云层正在菲律宾大范围扩散。
例：He brushed the cigarette ash from his sleeve...他掸掉了袖子上的烟灰。

N-PLURAL
骨灰
A dead person's ashes are their remains after their body has been cremated.

VERB
纪念;使记住
If a person or event is memorialized, something is produced that will continue to exist and remind people of them.
例：He was praised in print and memorialized in stone throughout the South...在整个南方地区，他被人们著书称颂，刻碑纪念。
例：Our affair was memorialized by those children...那些孩子的存在时刻提醒着人们我俩的私情。

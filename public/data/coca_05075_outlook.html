outlook 
英[ˈaʊtlʊk] 美[ˈaʊtˌlʊk]
 
n.前景，展望；观点，见解；景色；眺望处；v.瞪着看，瞧；比…漂亮；用目光压倒；
 "

N-COUNT
世界观；人生观；（对生活的）观点，看法
Your outlook is your general attitude towards life.
例：The illness had a profound effect on his outlook...这场病对他的人生观产生了深刻的影响。
例：I adopted a positive outlook on life...我开始抱持积极向上的人生观。

N-SING
展望；前景；可能性
The outlook for something is what people think will happen in relation to it.
例：The economic outlook is one of rising unemployment...展望未来的经济，失业率将会上升。
例：Has motherhood changed your career outlook?当了母亲是否改变了你的职业前途？

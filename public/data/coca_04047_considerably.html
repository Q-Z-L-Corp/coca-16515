ADJ-GRADED
相当多的;相当大的
Considerable means great in amount or degree.
例：To be without Pearce would be a considerable blow...失去皮尔斯将是一个巨大的打击。
例：Doing it properly makes considerable demands on our time...把这事做好要耗费我们相当多的时间。

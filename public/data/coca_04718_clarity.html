clarity 
英[ˈklærəti] 美[ˈklærɪti]
 
n.清楚，明晰；透明；明确；清晰度；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(书、论点等的)清楚，清晰易懂，明晰
The clarity of something such as a book or argument is its quality of being well explained and easy to understand.
例：...the ease and clarity with which the author explains difficult technical and scientific subjects.作者解释复杂的科技主题时的游刃有余、清晰透彻

N-UNCOUNT
思路清晰;有条理
Clarity is the ability to think clearly.
例：In business circles he is noted for his flair and clarity of vision.在商界，他的天赋和远见卓识是出了名的。

N-UNCOUNT
(轮廓、声音的)清楚，清晰
Clarity is the quality of being clear in outline or sound.
例：This remarkable technology provides far greater clarity than conventional x-rays.这项卓越技术的清晰度比常规的X光高得多。

N-UNCOUNT
(液体、玻璃或空气的)清澈度，透明度
The clarity of a liquid, of glass, or of the air is the degree to which it is clear.
例：The first thing to strike me was the amazing clarity of the water.首先吸引我的是水的无比清澈。

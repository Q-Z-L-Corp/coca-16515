monarchy

n.君主政体；君主政治；君主国；
 "

N-VAR
君主政体;君主制
A monarchy is a system in which a country has a monarch.
例：In a few years we may no longer have a monarchy.几年后君主政体可能将不复存在。
例：...a serious debate on the future of the monarchy.关于君主政体前途的严肃辩论

N-COUNT
君主国;君主制国家
A monarchy is a country that has a monarch.

N-COUNT
皇室;王族
The monarchy is used to refer to the monarch and his or her family.
例：The monarchy has to create a balance between its public and private lives.皇室成员不得不在公众生活和私生活之间建立起一种平衡。

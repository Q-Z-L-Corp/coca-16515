massage

n.按摩，推拿；
 "

N-VAR
按摩;推拿
Massage is the action of squeezing and rubbing someone's body, as a way of making them relax or reducing their pain.
例：Alex asked me if I wanted a massage...亚力克斯问我是否想做按摩。
例：Massage isn't a long-term cure for stress.按摩对缓解压力并非长期有效。

VERB
为…按摩;给…推拿
If you massage someone or a part of their body, you squeeze and rub their body, in order to make them relax or reduce their pain.
例：She continued massaging her right foot, which was bruised and aching.她继续按摩又肿又疼的右脚。
例：...if you wish to massage your family and friends yourself.如果你希望亲自为家人及朋友按摩的话

VERB
修改，改动,窜改(数据或证据以掩人耳目)
If you say that someone massages statistics, figures, or evidence, you are criticizing them for changing or presenting the facts in a way that misleads people.
例：Their governments have no reason to 'massage' the statistics.他们的政府没有理由“窜改”这些数据。
例：...efforts to massage the unemployment figures for electoral purposes.为了选举的目的而试图窜改失业数字

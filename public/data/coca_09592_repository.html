N-COUNT
贮存处;存放处;仓库
A repository is a place where something is kept safely.
例：A church in Moscow became a repository for police files.莫斯科的一座教堂成为警方存放档案的地点。

N-COUNT
博学者;无所不知的人
A repository of information is a person or group of people who know a lot of information about a particular place or subject.
例：The repository of all important knowledge in a small town was the chief barman of the local pub.小镇上最无所不知的人就是当地酒吧里的服务员领班。

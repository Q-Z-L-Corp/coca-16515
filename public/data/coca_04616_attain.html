attain 
英[əˈteɪn] 美[əˈten]
 
vt.& vi.达到，获得；遂愿；经过努力到达某事物；vt.达到，实现；获得；到达；vi.达到；获得；到达；
 "

VERB
（通常指经过努力）获得，达到
If you attain something, you gain it or achieve it, often after a lot of effort.
例：Jim is halfway to attaining his pilot's licence.吉姆就快要拿到飞行员执照了。

VERB
达到，进入，实现（某种状态或情况）
If you attain a particular state or condition, you may reach it as a result of natural development or work hard to attain this state.
例：...attaining a state of calmness and confidence.达到镇定自若、信心十足的状态

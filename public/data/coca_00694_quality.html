quality 
英[ˈkwɒləti] 美[ˈkwɑləti]
 
n.质量，品质；美质，优点；才能，能力，技能，素养；品种；adj.优质的，高质量的；上流社会的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
质量；品质
The quality of something is how good or bad it is.
例：Everyone can greatly improve the quality of life...每个人都可以大大改善生活质量。
例：Other services vary dramatically in quality.其他服务在质量上差别很大。

N-UNCOUNT
优质;高级
Something of quality is of a high standard.
例：...a college of quality...优秀的学院
例：In our work, quality is paramount.我们的工作追求品质至上。

N-COUNT
优秀品质;品德
Someone's qualities are the good characteristics that they have which are part of their nature.
例：Sometimes you wonder where your kids get their good qualities...有时你会想，孩子们身上的优秀品质是从何而来呢？
例：He wanted to introduce mature people with leadership qualities...他想引进具有领导素质的成熟人才。

N-COUNT
特征；性质;特性
You can describe a particular characteristic of a person or thing as a quality .
例：...a childlike quality.孩子般的天真特质
例：...the pretentious quality of the poetry...这首诗浮夸造作的特点

ADJ
内容严肃的报纸
The quality papers or the quality press are the more serious newspapers which give detailed accounts of world events, as well as reports on business, culture, and society.
例：Even the quality papers agreed that it was a triumph.即使是内容严肃的报纸也认为这是一次胜利。

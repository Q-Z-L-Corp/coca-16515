mesh 
英[meʃ] 美[mɛʃ]
 
n.网孔，网眼，网状物；陷阱，困境；[机]（齿轮的）啮合；vt.& vi.（使）吻合；相配，匹配；用网捕，使缠住；紧密配合；vi.（机器零件）啮合；
 "

N-VAR
网眼织物;网状物
Mesh is material like a net made from wire, thread, or plastic.
例：The ground-floor windows are obscured by wire mesh.一楼的窗户装有防盗网。
例：...a mesh small enough to exclude tiny insects.网眼小得足以挡住小昆虫的网

V-RECIP-ERG
(使)相匹配;(使)相互协调;(使)吻合
If two things or ideas mesh or are meshed, they go together well or fit together closely.
例：Their senses of humor meshed perfectly...他们的幽默感配合得天衣无缝。
例：This of course meshes with the economic philosophy of those on the right...这自然与右派的经济哲学相吻合。

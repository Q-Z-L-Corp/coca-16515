coarse 
英[kɔ:s] 美[kɔrs]
 
adj.粗鄙的；粗糙的；粗野的；粗制滥造的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(质地)粗的，粗糙的;粗织的
Coarse things have a rough texture because they consist of thick threads or large pieces.
例：...a jacket made of very coarse cloth.粗布制成的夹克
例：...a beach of coarse sand.粗沙海滩

ADJ-GRADED
(言行)粗鲁的，粗俗的
If you describe someone as coarse, you mean that he or she talks and behaves in a rude and offensive way.
例：The soldiers did not bother to moderate their coarse humour in her presence.她在的时候，士兵们还是照样说粗俗的笑话，都懒得收敛。

resuscitate 
英[rɪˈsʌsɪteɪt] 美[rɪˈsʌsɪteɪt]
 
v.使复苏；使复兴；
 "

VERB
使复活;使苏醒;救活
If you resuscitate someone who has stopped breathing, you cause them to start breathing again.
例：A policeman and then a paramedic tried to resuscitate her.一名警察和一位护理人员先后试图救活她。

VERB
使复苏;使重新兴盛
If you resuscitate something, you cause it to become active or successful again.
例：He has submitted a bid to resuscitate the struggling magazine.他已经投标来拯救这个岌岌可危的周刊。

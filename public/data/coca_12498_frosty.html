frosty 
英[ˈfrɒsti] 美[ˈfrɔsti]
 
adj.严寒的；霜冻的；冷淡的；灰白的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
霜冻的;严寒的
If the weather is frosty, the temperature is below freezing.
例：...sharp, frosty nights.寒冷刺骨的夜晚

ADJ-GRADED
结霜的;被霜覆盖的
You describe the ground or an object as frosty when it is covered with frost.
例：The street was deserted except for a cat lifting its paws off the frosty stones.街上空无一人，只有一只猫在结霜的石砖上拾爪而行。

ADJ-GRADED
冷淡的;冷若冰霜的
If you describe someone's behaviour as frosty, you think it is unfriendly.
例：The president may get a frosty reception in New Hampshire...总统在新罕布什尔州可能会受到冷遇。
例：The relationship has obviously become frosty.很明显关系已经变冷了。

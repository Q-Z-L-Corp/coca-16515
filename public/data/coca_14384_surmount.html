surmount 
英[səˈmaʊnt] 美[sərˈmaʊnt]
 
vt.战胜，克服；登上，攀登；居于…之上；顶上覆盖着；
 "

VERB
克服;解决
If you surmount a problem or difficulty, you deal successfully with it.
例：I realized I had to surmount the language barrier.我意识到我不得不克服语言障碍。

VERB
处于…上面;置于…顶端
If something is surmounted by a particular thing, that thing is on top of it.
例：The island is surmounted by a huge black castle.岛的最高处耸立着一座巨大的黑色城堡。

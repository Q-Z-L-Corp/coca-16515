negation 
英[nɪˈgeɪʃn] 美[nɪˈgeɪʃn]
 
n.反面；对立面；否定；否认；
 "

N-SING
反面;对立面;否定
The negation of something is its complete opposite or something which destroys it or makes it lose its effect.
例：Unintelligible legislation is the negation of the rule of law and of parliamentary democracy.晦涩的法律法规有悖于法治和议会民主。

N-UNCOUNT
否定;否认;拒绝
Negation is disagreement, refusal, or denial.
例：The editor grimaced, gesturing in negation...编辑一脸苦相，做了个拒绝的手势。
例：Irena shook her head, but in bewilderment, not negation.伊连娜摇了摇头，是困惑，而非拒绝。

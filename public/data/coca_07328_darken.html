darken 
英[ˈdɑ:kən] 美[ˈdɑrkən]
 
vt.& vi.（使）变暗；（使）模糊；
 "

V-ERG
(使)变黑;(使)变深;(使)变暗
If something darkens or if a person or thing darkens it, it becomes darker.
例：The sky darkened abruptly...天突然暗了下来。
例：She had put on her make-up and darkened her eyelashes.她已化好妆，并涂黑了睫毛。

V-ERG
(使)变得阴郁;(使)心情沉重
If someone's mood darkens or if something darkens their mood, they suddenly become rather unhappy.
例：My sunny mood suddenly darkened...我的好心情突然变得阴郁起来。
例：Nothing was going to darken his mood today.今天什么都不会影响到他的心情。

VERB
(脸色)变阴沉;(面)有愠色
If someone's face darkens, they suddenly look angry.
例：Rawley's face darkened again.罗利的脸色又阴沉下来。

impoverished 
英[ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃt] 美[ɪmˈpɑvərɪʃt]
 
adj.穷困的，无力的，用尽了的；v.使（某人）贫穷( impoverish的过去式和过去分词 )；使（某物）贫瘠或恶化；
 "

VERB
使贫困;使赤贫
Something that impoverishes a person or a country makes them poor.
例：We need to reduce the burden of taxes that impoverish the economy.我们需要减轻导致经济困顿的税收负荷。
例：...a society impoverished by wartime inflation.因战时通货膨胀导致贫困的社会

VERB
使贫瘠;使枯竭
A person or thing that impoverishes something makes it worse in quality.
例：...plants that impoverish the soil quickly.使土壤迅速贫瘠的植物

ambient 
英[ˈæmbiənt] 美[ˈæmbiənt]
 
adj.周围的，包围着的；产生轻松氛围的；环境；
 "

ADJ
（气温）周围的
The ambient temperature is the temperature of the air above the ground in a particular place.

ADJ-GRADED
（声音、光线）四周的，环绕的
Ambient sound or light is the sound or light which is all around you.
例：...ambient sounds of children in the background.背景里环绕着孩子们的声音

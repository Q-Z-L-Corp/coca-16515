N-COUNT
(剧本、诗歌或音乐作品的)演绎，表演
A rendering of a play, poem, or piece of music is a performance of it.
例：...a rendering of Verdi's Requiem by the BBC Symphony Orchestra.由BBC交响乐团演奏的威尔第的《安魂曲》

N-COUNT
翻译
A rendering of an expression or piece of writing or speech is a translation of it.
例：This phrase may well have been a rendering of a popular Arabic expression.这个短语可能翻译自一个阿拉伯语常用表达。

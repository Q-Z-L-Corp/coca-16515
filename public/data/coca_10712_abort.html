abort 
英[əˈbɔ:t] 美[əˈbɔrt]
 
vt.使流产；使夭折；使中止；vi.流产；堕胎；发育不全；n.流产；中止计划；
 "

VERB
使流产;堕（胎）
If an unborn baby is aborted, the pregnancy is ended deliberately and the baby is not born alive.
例：Her lover walked out on her after she had aborted their child.她的情人在她打掉胎儿后甩了她。
例：...the latest date at which a foetus can be aborted.能够打胎的最迟日期

VERB
中止，中辍（计划、活动等）
If someone aborts a process, plan, or activity, they stop it before it has been completed.
例：The decision was made to abort the mission...决定中止这项任务。
例：The take-off was aborted...起飞取消了。

unused

ADJ
未用过的；未使用的
Something that is unused has not been used or is not being used at the moment.
例：...unused containers of food and drink...未用过的食物和饮料盛器
例：The insurance on his BMW has run out, and the car stands unused.他那部宝马车的保险已过期，而车还没用过。

ADJ-GRADED
不习惯的；不熟悉的
If you are unused to something, you have not often done it or experienced it before, so it feels unusual and unfamiliar to you.
例：Mother was entirely unused to such hard work.妈妈对这么艰苦的工作完全不习惯。

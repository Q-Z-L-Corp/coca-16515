N-COUNT
起源；来源；起因；由来
You can refer to the beginning, cause, or source of something as its origin or origins .
例：...theories about the origin of life...有关生命起源的各种理论
例：The disorder in military policy had its origins in Truman's first term...军事政策的混乱可追溯到杜鲁门的第一届任期。

N-COUNT
出身；身世；血统
When you talk about a person's origin or origins, you are referring to the country, race, or social class of their parents or ancestors.
例：Thomas has not forgotten his humble origins.托马斯没有忘记自己卑微的出身。
例：...people of Asian origin...亚裔民众

Torn is the past participle of tear .

ADJ-GRADED
犹豫的;举棋不定的
If you are torn between two or more things, you cannot decide which to choose, and so you feel anxious or troubled.
例：Robb is torn between becoming a doctor and a career in athletics...罗布在做医生还是当运动员之间痛苦抉择，左右为难。
例：I know the administration was very torn on this subject.我知道政府在这个问题上犹豫不决，不知该何去何从。

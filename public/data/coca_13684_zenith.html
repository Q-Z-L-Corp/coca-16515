zenith 
英[ˈzenɪθ] 美[ˈzinɪθ]
 
n.顶点， 极点；
 "

N-SING
全盛时期;鼎盛时期
The zenith of something is the time when it is most successful or powerful.
例：His career is now at its zenith...他的事业现在处于鼎盛时期。
例：The zenith of Perugia's influence came with the defeat of Siena in 1358.1358年击败锡耶纳之后佩鲁贾的影响力达到了顶峰。

N-SING
天顶（太阳或月亮在天空中的最高点）
The zenith is the point at which the sun or moon is directly above you and seems to be at its highest.
例：The sun rises, reaches its zenith and sets.太阳升起，达到最高点，然后落下。

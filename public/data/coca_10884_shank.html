N-COUNT
(物体)长而细直的部分，杆，柄
The shank of an object is the long, thin, straight part of the object.
例：These hooks are sharp with long shanks.这些钩身长的鱼钩很锋利。
例：...the shank of the club.球杆的长柄

N-COUNT
小腿;胫;(尤指)小腿肉
Shanks are the lower parts of the legs; used especially with reference to meat.
例：Turn the shanks and baste them once or twice as they cook.将小腿肉翻一下，烹调时在上面抹一两次油。

repetitive 
英[rɪˈpetətɪv] 美[rɪˈpɛtɪtɪv]
 
adj.重复的，啰嗦的；复唱的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
反复的;重复乏味的
Something that is repetitive involves actions or elements that are repeated many times and is therefore boring.
例：...factory workers who do repetitive jobs...从事重复性工作的工厂工人
例：Suddenly music that seemed dull and repetitive comes alive.突然间，似乎索然无味且反反复复的音乐生动了起来。

ADJ
重复多次的;反复性的
Repetitive movements or sounds are repeated many times.
例：...problems that occur as the result of repetitive movements.反复运动引起的问题

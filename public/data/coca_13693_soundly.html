soundly 
英[ˈsaʊndli] 美[ˈsaʊndlɪ]
 
adv.完好地，健全地；正确地；严厉地；酣畅地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
彻底地;完全地
If someone is soundly defeated or beaten, they are severely defeated or beaten.
例：Duke was soundly defeated in this month's Louisiana governor's race.杜克在本月的路易斯安那州长竞选中大败而归。

ADV-GRADED
(决定、观点或陈述等)正确地，明智地，可靠地
If a decision, opinion, or statement is soundly based, there are sensible or reliable reasons behind it.
例：Are today's hopes more soundly based than the false ones of 1990?...今天的希望是否比 1990 年那些虚幻的希望更靠谱呢？
例：Changes must be soundly based in economic reality.改革必须以经济现状作为切实的依据。

ADV-GRADED
酣睡地;熟睡地
If you sleep soundly, you sleep deeply and do not wake during your sleep.
例：How can he sleep soundly at night?...他晚上怎么能睡得着觉？
例：She was too soundly asleep to hear Stefano's return.她睡得太熟，没有听见斯蒂芬诺回来。

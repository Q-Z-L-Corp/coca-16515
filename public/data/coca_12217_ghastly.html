ghastly 
英[ˈgɑ:stli] 美[ˈgæstli]
 
adj.可怕的；（脸色）苍白的；极坏的；adv.可怕地；恐怖地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人反感的；令人不快的
If you describe someone or something as ghastly, you mean that you find them very unpleasant.
例：...a mother accompanied by her ghastly unruly child...身边带着一位极难管教的孩子的母亲
例：It was the worst week of my life. It was ghastly.这是我这辈子最倒霉的一周，别提有多烦了。

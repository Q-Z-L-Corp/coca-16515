hallucination 
英[həˌlu:sɪˈneɪʃn] 美[həˌlusəˈneʃən]
 
n.幻觉，幻想；错觉；
 "

N-VAR
（因生病或吸毒所致的）幻觉（体验）
A hallucination is the experience of seeing something that is not really there because you are ill or have taken a drug.
例：The drug induces hallucinations at high doses...大剂量地服用该药会致幻。
例：Hallucination is common in patients who have suffered damage to the brain.脑部受损的病人常会产生幻觉。

N-COUNT
（因生病或吸毒所见到的）幻象
A hallucination is something that is not real that someone sees because they are ill or have taken a drug.
例：Perhaps the footprint was a hallucination.脚印可能只是幻象。

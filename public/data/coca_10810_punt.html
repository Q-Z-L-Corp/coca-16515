punt 
英[pʌnt] 美[pʌnt]
 
n.踢悬空球；平底船；下赌注者；vi.用篙撑（方头平底船）；下赌注；弃踢；[美俚]撒手不干；vt.[足]踢悬空球；向庄家下赌注；乘方头或平底船旅行；
 "

N-COUNT
（用篙撑行的）平底长船
A punt is a long boat with a flat bottom. You move the boat along by standing at one end and pushing a long pole against the bottom of the river.

VERB
乘平底长船
When you punt, you travel along a river in a punt.
例：We punted up towards Grantchester and had a picnic in a meadow.我们乘坐平底长船溯河而上到格兰切斯特，在草地上举行野餐。

N-COUNT
爱尔兰镑（爱尔兰共和国使用欧元前的货币单位）
The punt was the unit of money used in the Republic of Ireland before it was replaced by the euro.
例：The round-trip fare to Havana is 550 Irish punts ($673).去哈瓦那的往返票价是550爱尔兰镑（673美元）。

N-COUNT
（橄榄球或美式足球中的）碰踢，弃踢，落踢
In rugby and American football, a punt is a kick where you drop the ball and then kick it before it reaches the ground, so that it goes a long way.
例：He caught a punt and scored the winning touchdown, with a minute left to play.他接住对方的弃踢球，成功达阵，取得了制胜得分，此时比赛只剩最后一分钟。
例：...a 66-yard punt return一个66码的凌空回踢

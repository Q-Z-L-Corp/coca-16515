palatable 
英[ˈpælətəbl] 美[ˈpælətəbəl]
 
adj.可口的；美味的；宜人的；可接受的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
美味的；可口的
If you describe food or drink as palatable, you mean that it tastes pleasant.
例：...flavourings and preservatives, designed to make the food look more palatable.用于使食物看起来更加可口的调味料和防腐剂

ADJ-GRADED
（想法、方法等）合意的，可接受的
If you describe something such as an idea or method as palatable, you mean that people are willing to accept it.
例：...a palatable way of sacking staff...可以接受的裁员方式
例：That option is not very palatable.那个选择不太可以接受。

CONJ-SUBORD
如果；假如；只要
If you say that something will happen providing or providing that something else happens, you mean that the first thing will happen only if the second thing also happens.
例：I do believe in people being able to do what they want to do, providing they're not hurting someone else...我真的认为人们只要不伤害别人，自己想做什么就可以做什么。
例：Providing they do not panic, I believe that their chances of survival will be beyond 95 per cent.如果他们不慌乱，我相信他们生还的概率将在95%以上。

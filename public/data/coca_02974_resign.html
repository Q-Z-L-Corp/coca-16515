resign 
英[rɪˈzaɪn] 美[rɪ'zaɪn]
 
v.辞职；放弃；屈从；勉强接受；
 "

VERB
辞职;辞去;放弃
If you resign from a job or position, you formally announce that you are leaving it.
例：A hospital administrator has resigned over claims he lied to get the job...一位医院的管理人员辞职了，因为有人指责他为了得到这份工作而撒了谎。
例：Mr Robb resigned his position last month.罗布先生上个月辞职了。

VERB
屈从;顺从;安于;勉强接受
If you resign yourself to an unpleasant situation or fact, you accept it because you realize that you cannot change it.
例：Pat and I resigned ourselves to yet another summer without a boat...我和帕特在没有船的情况下勉强又凑合了一个夏天。
例：He had resigned himself to watching the European Championships on television.他只好将就着在电视上看欧洲锦标赛。

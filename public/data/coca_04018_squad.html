N-COUNT
(专门处理某类犯罪的)警察分队
A squad is a section of a police force that is responsible for dealing with a particular type of crime.
例：The building was evacuated and the bomb squad called...大楼里进行了人员疏散，并叫来了拆弹小组。
例：The club is under investigation by the fraud squad.反诈骗调查组正在对该俱乐部进行调查。

N-COUNT
(将从中挑选运动员组队参赛的)运动队，大名单
A squad is a group of players from which a sports team will be chosen.
例：Sean O'Leary has been named in the England squad to tour Argentina.肖恩·奥利里已入选参加阿根廷巡回赛的英格兰队大名单。

glint 
英[glɪnt] 美[ɡlɪnt]
 
vt.& vi.闪烁，闪光；窥视；（箭一样）飞出；使发光，使闪光；n.闪光，闪耀；短暂微弱的显露；（雷达的）回波起伏；vi.闪烁；（光线）反射；闪闪发光；
 "

VERB
闪闪发光；闪亮
If something glints, it produces or reflects a quick flash of light.
例：The sea glinted in the sun...阳光下的海面波光粼粼。
例：Sunlight glinted on his spectacles.阳光照射下，他的眼镜片闪着光。

N-COUNT
闪光；闪烁
A glint is a quick flash of light.
例：...a glint of silver.银光闪闪
例：...glints of sunlight.阳光闪耀

VERB
（眼睛）闪光，闪动，闪亮
If someone's eyes glint, they shine and express a particular emotion.
例：Her eyes glinted wildly...她眼里流露出激动的神情。
例：A mischievous spark glinted in his eyes.他眼中闪现出一丝顽皮的神情。

N-SING
（眼神的）闪光，闪动，闪亮
A glint in someone's eyes is a brightness that expresses a particular emotion.
例：He came up to me with the glint of triumph in his eye.他向我走来，眼里闪现出一丝胜利的神情。

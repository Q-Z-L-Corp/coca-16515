superstitious 
英[ˌsu:pəˈstɪʃəs] 美[ˌsupərˈstɪʃəs]
 
adj.迷信的；有迷信观念的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
迷信的
People who are superstitious believe in things that are not real or possible, for example magic.
例：Jean was extremely superstitious and believed the colour green brought bad luck.琼非常迷信，认为绿色会带来厄运。

ADJ-GRADED
(恐惧、信念)非理性的，盲目的
Superstitious fears or beliefs are irrational and not based on fact.
例：A wave of superstitious fear spread among the townspeople...盲目的恐惧感在市民中蔓延。
例：In the countryside as a young doctor she encountered ancient superstitious beliefs and prejudices.作为一名年轻医生，她在乡下见识了古老的迷信思想和偏见。

halfway 
英[ˌhɑ:fˈweɪ] 美[ˌhæfˈweɪ]
 
adv.在中途；到一半；在中间；大致上；
 "

ADV
（空间上）在中间，在中途
Halfway means in the middle of a place or between two points, at an equal distance from each of them.
例：Half-way across the car-park, he noticed she was walking with her eyes closed...走到停车场中间时，他看到她在闭着眼走路。
例：He was halfway up the ladder...他正爬到梯子一半。

ADV
（时间）到一半;（事情）进行到中途
Halfway means in the middle of a period of time or of an event.
例：By then, it was October and we were more than halfway through our tour.那时已是10月，我们已经完成了行程的一大半了。

PHRASE
对…作部分让步;与…妥协
If you meet someone halfway, you accept some of the points they are making so that you can come to an agreement with them.
例：The Democrats are willing to meet the president halfway.民主党人愿意向总统作部分让步。

ADV
过得去地;大致上
Halfway means reasonably.
例：You need hard currency to get anything halfway decent...你得有硬通货才能买到还算不错的东西。
例：All I had to do was be halfway cool.我要做的只是保持适度的冷静。

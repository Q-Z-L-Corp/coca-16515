ADV-GRADED
（置于褒义词前表强调）非常，极其
You use eminently in front of an adjective describing a positive quality in order to emphasize the quality expressed by that adjective.
例：His books on diplomatic history were eminently readable...他写的有关外交史的书可读性非常强。
例：His family was eminently respectable.他的家族非常有名望。

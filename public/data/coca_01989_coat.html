coat 
英[kəʊt] 美[koʊt]
 
n.上衣，外套；vt.给…穿上上衣[外套]；盖上；
 "

N-COUNT
上衣;外衣;外套
A coat is a piece of clothing with long sleeves which you wear over your other clothes when you go outside.
例：He turned off the television, put on his coat and walked out.他关掉电视，穿上外套，出门了。

N-COUNT
(动物的)皮毛
An animal's coat is the fur or hair on its body.
例：Vitamin B6 is great for improving the condition of dogs' and horses' coats.维生素B6对于改善狗和马的皮毛有显著的作用。

VERB
给…涂上(或盖上、裹上)
If you coat something with a substance or in a substance, you cover it with a thin layer of the substance.
例：Coat the fish with seasoned flour.用拌有佐料的面粉将鱼裹上。

N-COUNT
(油漆、清漆等的)一层
A coat of paint or varnish is a thin layer of it on a surface.
例：The front door needs a new coat of paint...前门需要刷一层新漆了。
例：You will need to apply three coats of varnish.需要刷三层清漆。

well-known 
英['wel'nəʊn] 美[ˈwɛlˈnon]
 
adj.出名的，众所周知的，熟悉的；鼎鼎大名；闻；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
众所周知的;著名的;出名的
A well-known person or thing is known about by a lot of people and is therefore famous or familiar. If someone is well-known for a particular activity, a lot of people know about them because of their involvement with that activity.
例：He surrounds himself with attractive, intelligent, or well-known people...他混迹于有魅力、有学问或有名气的人当中。
例：He is well-known to the local police...当地警察都知道他。

ADJ-GRADED
为人所熟知的
A well-known fact is a fact that is known by people in general.
例：It may be a well-known fact, but I didn't know it...这可能是人所共知的事实，但我却不知道。
例：It is well known that bamboo shoots are a panda's staple diet.竹笋是熊猫的主要食物，这是人所共知的。

ADV
貌似，看似(但可能并非如此)
If something is seemingly the case, you mean that it appears to be the case, even though it may not really be so.
例：A seemingly endless line of trucks waits in vain to load up.一眼望不到头的一长列卡车眼巴巴地等着装货。
例：...bread made from a seemingly limitless variety of ingredients.貌似用了无数种原料制成的面包

ADV
似乎，好像(是事实)
You use seemingly when you want to say that something seems to be true.
例：He has moved to Spain, seemingly to enjoy a slower style of life...他已经移居西班牙，似乎是想过一种更为悠闲的生活。
例：He is a man with seemingly not an ounce of malice in him.他这个人好像没有丁点儿的坏心眼。

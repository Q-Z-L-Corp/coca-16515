dismantle 
英[dɪsˈmæntl] 美[dɪsˈmæntl]
 
vt.拆开；拆卸；废除；取消；
 "

VERB
拆卸;拆开;拆除
If you dismantle a machine or structure, you carefully separate it into its different parts.
例：He asked for immediate help from the United States to dismantle the warheads.他请求美国立即提供援助，拆除这批弹头。

VERB
（逐渐）废除，撤销，破坏
To dismantle an organization or system means to cause it to stop functioning by gradually reducing its power or purpose.
例：...opposition to the president's policy of dismantling apartheid...反对总统废除种族隔离的政策
例：Public services of all kinds are being dismantled.林林总总的公共设施正被逐渐废除。

ADJ-GRADED
贪婪的；贪心的；贪吃的
If you describe someone as greedy, you mean that they want to have more of something such as food or money than is necessary or fair.
例：He attacked greedy bosses for awarding themselves big rises...他抨击那些贪心的老板为他们自己大幅加薪。
例：She is greedy and selfish.她既贪婪又自私。

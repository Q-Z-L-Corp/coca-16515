collaborate 
英[kəˈlæbəreɪt] 美[kəˈlæbəˌret]
 
vi.合作，协作；（国家间的）协调，提携；勾结，通敌；
 "

V-RECIP
合作，协作(尤指著书或做研究)
When one person or group collaborates with another, they work together, especially on a book or on some research.
例：He collaborated with his son Michael on the English translation of a text on food production...他和儿子迈克尔合作，把一篇介绍食品生产的文章译成了英文。
例：The government is urging Japan's firms to collaborate with foreigners.政府正在督促日本的公司进行更多的海外合作。

VERB
通（敌）;勾结(敌人)
If someone collaborates with an enemy that is occupying their country during a war, they help them.
例：He was accused of having collaborated with the secret police.他被控与秘密警察勾结。

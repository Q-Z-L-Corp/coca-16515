N-UNCOUNT
(热通过气体或液体的)对流，运流
Convection is the process by which heat travels through air, water, and other gases and liquids.
例：...clouds which lift warm, moist air by convection high into the atmosphere.通过对流而使温暖潮湿的空气上升到大气中的云朵
例：...convection currents.对流气流

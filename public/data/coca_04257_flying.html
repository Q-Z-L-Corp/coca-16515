flying 
英[ˈflaɪɪŋ] 美[ˈflaɪɪŋ]
 
adj.飞行的；会飞的；迅速的；n.飞行；驾驶飞机；v.飞(fly的现在分词)；
 "

ADJ
(动物)会飞的，有翼的
A flying animal has wings and is able to fly.
例：...species of flying insects.飞行昆虫的种类

PHRASE
有良好的开端;顺利开始
If someone or something gets off to a flying start, or makes a flying start, they start very well, for example in a race or a new job.
例：Advertising revenue in the new financial year has got off to a flying start...广告收入在新财年取得了开门红。
例：Hendry made a flying start to the final.亨德利在决赛中取得梦幻开局。

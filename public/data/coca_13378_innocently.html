innocently 
英['ɪnəsntlɪ] 美[ˈɪnəsəntlɪ]
 
adv.无罪地；纯洁地；天真地；无知无识地；

ADV-GRADED
故作天真地;佯装无知地
If you say that someone does or says something innocently, you mean that they are pretending not to know something about a situation.
例：I caught Chrissie's eye, but she only smiled back at me innocently...我与克丽茜对视了一眼,但她只是一脸天真地对我微笑。
例：'Any chance you'd be heading out that way again, soon?' I asked innocently.“你很快会再往那边去吗?”我故作不知地问道。

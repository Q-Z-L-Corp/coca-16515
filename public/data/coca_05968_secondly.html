ADV
(用于列举观点或给出原因)第二，其次
You say secondly when you want to make a second point or give a second reason for something.
例：You need, firstly, a strong independent board of directors and secondly, an experienced and dedicated staff.你首先需要一个强大的独立董事会，其次需要一批富有经验并且爱岗敬业的员工。

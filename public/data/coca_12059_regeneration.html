regeneration 
英[rɪˌdʒenə'reɪʃn] 美[rɪˌdʒɛnəˈreʃən]
 
n.<生>再生；恢复；<宗>（灵魂的）重生；<电子>正反馈；
 "

VERB
革新;使复兴;重建
To regenerate something means to develop and improve it to make it more active, successful, or important, especially after a period when it has been getting worse.
例：The government will continue to try to regenerate inner city areas.政府将继续努力重建内城地区。

V-ERG
(使）(器官或肌体组织）再生；(使）再造
If organs or tissues regenerate or if something regenerates them, they heal and grow again after they have been damaged.
例：Nerve cells have limited ability to regenerate if destroyed...如果受到损伤，神经细胞再造的能力有限。
例：Newts can regenerate their limbs.蝾螈的肢体可以再生。

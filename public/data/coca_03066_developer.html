N-COUNT
（房地产）开发商，开发公司
A developer is a person or a company that buys land and builds houses, offices, shops, or factories on it, or buys existing buildings and makes them more modern.
例：...common land which would have a high commercial value if sold to developers.如果卖给开发商会有很高商业价值的公共用地
例：...a corrupt, self-made property developer.一个白手起家、营私舞弊的房地产开发商

N-COUNT
(想法、设计、产品等的)开发者
A developer is someone who develops something such as an idea, a design, or a product.
例：John Bardeen was also co-developer of the theory of superconductivity.约翰·巴丁也是超导电性理论的创立者之一。
例：...a software developer.软件开发者

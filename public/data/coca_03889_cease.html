cease 
英[si:s] 美[sis]
 
vt.停止，终止，结束；vi.终止，停止；永不停止的过程；停止行动；n.停止；
 "

VERB
停止;终了;消失
If something ceases, it stops happening or existing.
例：At one o'clock the rain had ceased.一点时，雨已停了。

VERB
停止;终止
If you cease to do something, you stop doing it.
例：He never ceases to amaze me...他总能给我惊喜。
例：The secrecy about the President's condition had ceased to matter...总统的健康状况已经没有保密的必要。

VERB
停止;终止
If you cease something, you stop it happening or working.
例：The Tundra Times, a weekly newspaper in Alaska, ceased publication this week.阿拉斯加的周报《苔原时报》本周停刊了。

powder 
英[ˈpaʊdə(r)] 美[ˈpaʊdɚ]
 
n.粉，粉末；粉状物质；vt.在…搽粉；vi.涂粉，变成粉末；
 "

N-MASS
粉末；细面
Powder consists of many tiny particles of a solid substance.
例：Put a small amount of the powder into a container and mix with water...把少量粉末放入容器，加水混合。
例：The wood turns to powder in his fingers.木头在他指间会变成粉末。

N-MASS
同 face powder
Powder is the same as face powder .

VERB
往…上搽粉；往…上抹粉
If a woman powders her face or some other part of her body, she puts face powder or talcum powder on it.
例：She powdered her face and applied her lipstick and rouge.她往脸上搽了粉，抹了口红和胭脂。
例：...the old woman's powdered face.老妇人那张搽了粉的脸

N-UNCOUNT
同 gunpowder
Powder is the same as gunpowder .
例：The smell of powder was in the air.空气里有股火药味儿。

N-UNCOUNT
粉状雪；干细雪
Powder is very fine snow.
例：...a day's powder skiing.一天的滑雪时光

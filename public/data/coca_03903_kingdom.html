N-COUNT
王国
A kingdom is a country or region that is ruled by a king or queen.
例：The kingdom's power declined.王国的势力衰弱了。
例：...the United Kingdom.联合王国

N-COUNT
管辖范围；领域
A kingdom is a place or area that is thought to be under the control of a person or organization.
例：It was infamous as a kingdom of brigands, scoundrels, and slave-traders.该地区因土匪、无赖和奴隶贩子猖獗而声名狼藉。

N-SING
（动物或植物）界
All the animals, birds, and insects in the world can be referred to together as the animal kingdom. All the plants can be referred to as the plant kingdom.

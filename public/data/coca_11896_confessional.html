confessional 
英[kənˈfeʃənl] 美[kənˈfɛʃənəl]
 
n.忏悔室，（教堂内的）告解室；“confession”的派生；adj.忏悔的；自白的；“confession”的派生；
 "

N-COUNT
(尤指天主教教堂的)告解室
A confessional is the small room in a church where Christians, especially Roman Catholics, go to confess their sins.

ADJ-GRADED
忏悔的;自白的
A confessional speech or writing contains confessions.
例：The convictions rest solely on disputed witness and confessional statements...仅仅根据有争议的目击者证言和供词就定了罪。
例：Their first album was painfully frank to the point of being confessional.他们的第一张专辑是那么的坦率，近乎自白。

N-COUNT
忏悔;自白;检讨会；自我批评会
A confessional is a statement or meeting in which people confess things.
例：In regular group confessionals, the worst performers have to explain themselves to their peers.在定期举行的集体检讨会上，表现最差的人必须向同事作出解释。

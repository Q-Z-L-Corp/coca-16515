fifth 
英[fɪfθ] 美[fɪfθ]
 
num.第五；n.五分之一；第五音，五度音程；<美>五分之一加仑（瓶）；
 "

ORD
第五
The fifth item in a series is the one that you count as number five.
例：Joe has recently returned from his fifth trip to Australia.乔最近结束了第五次澳大利亚之行归来。

FRACTION
五分之一
A fifth is one of five equal parts of something.
例：India spends over a fifth of its budget on defence.印度的国防开支占了其财政预算的 1/5 强。

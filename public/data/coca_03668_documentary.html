英[ˌdɒkjuˈmentri] 美[ˌdɑkjuˈmentri]

N-COUNT
（电视、广播等）纪实节目;纪录片
A documentary is a television or radio programme, or a film, which shows real events or provides information about a particular subject.
例：...a TV documentary on homelessness.探讨无家可归问题的电视纪录片

ADJ
（证据）有文件记录的，来源于文件的，书面的
Documentary evidence consists of things that are written down.
例：We have documentary evidence that they were planning military action.我们有书面证据证明他们正在策划军事行动。

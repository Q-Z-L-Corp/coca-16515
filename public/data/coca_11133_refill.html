VERB
再次填满；再次装满
If you refill something, you fill it again after it has been emptied.
例：I refilled our wine glasses.我再次斟满了我们的酒杯。

N-COUNT
补充装;替换装
A refill of a particular product, such as soap powder, is a quantity of that product sold in a cheaper container than the one it is usually sold in. You use a refill to fill the more permanent container when it is empty.
例：Refill packs are cheaper and lighter.补充包更便宜，也更轻。

VERB
说明，表示，证明（某事属实）
To attest something or attest to something means to say, show, or prove that it is true.
例：Police records attest to his long history of violence...警方记录证明他有长期从事暴力活动的前科。
例：I can personally attest that the cold and flu season is here...感冒和流感季节来了，对此我有切身体会。

VERB
使有趣;使生动；使更有活力
To enliven events, situations, or people means to make them more lively or cheerful.
例：I love the way a good flirtation can enliven the most mundane situation...恰到好处的打情骂俏能够使最平淡无奇的时刻变得生动有趣起来，我很喜欢这种感觉。
例：Even the most boring meeting was enlivened by Dan's presence.即使是最无聊的会议，只要丹一出现，也会变得有趣起来。

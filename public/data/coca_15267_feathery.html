feathery 
英[ˈfeðəri] 美[ˈfɛðəri]
 
adj.生有羽毛的，柔软如羽毛的；软绵绵；

ADJ-GRADED
羽毛似的;羽状的;轻薄的;绒绒的
If something is feathery, it has an edge divided into a lot of thin parts so that it looks soft.
例：The foliage was soft and feathery.叶子如羽毛般轻软柔和。

ADJ-GRADED
轻软的;轻柔的
Feathery is used to describe things that are soft and light.
例：...flurries of small, feathery flakes of snow.飘洒着轻柔小雪花的阵雪

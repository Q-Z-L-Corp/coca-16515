ADJ
自己动手做的;家里做的
Something that is home-made has been made in someone's home, rather than in a shop or factory.
例：The bread, pastry and mayonnaise are home-made...面包、油酥点心和蛋黄酱都是家里做的。
例：A home-made bomb exploded during the disturbances.一枚自制炸弹在骚乱中爆炸了。

enlighten 
英[ɪnˈlaɪtn] 美[ɛnˈlaɪtn]
 
vt.启发，启蒙；开导，教导；〈古〉照耀；
 "

VERB
启发;开导;教化
To enlighten someone means to give them more knowledge and greater understanding about something.
例：A few dedicated doctors have fought for years to enlighten the profession...一些有献身精神的医生多年来一直努力指导业内同仁。
例：If you know what is wrong with her, please enlighten me.如果你知道她出什么问题了，请告诉我。

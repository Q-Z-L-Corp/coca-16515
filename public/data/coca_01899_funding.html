N-UNCOUNT
(政府或机构提供的)资金，款项，拨款
Funding is money which a government or organization provides for a particular purpose.
例：They hope for government funding for the scheme...他们希望政府为这个计划提供资金。
例：Many colleges have seen their funding cut.许多学院的经费都遭到了削减。

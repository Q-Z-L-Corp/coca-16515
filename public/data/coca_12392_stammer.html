stammer 
英[ˈstæmə(r)] 美[ˈstæmɚ]
 
vt.& vi.结巴地说出；口吃；n.口吃，结巴；vi.口吃；结结巴巴地说；
 "

VERB
口吃;结结巴巴地说
If you stammer, you speak with difficulty, hesitating and repeating words or sounds.
例：Five per cent of children stammer at some point...5%的儿童在某个时期会口吃。
例：'Forgive me,' I stammered...“原谅我，”我期期艾艾地说。

N-SING
口吃;结巴
Someone who has a stammer tends to stammer when they speak.
例：A speech-therapist cured his stammer.一位言语矫治师治愈了他的口吃。

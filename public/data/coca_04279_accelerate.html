accelerate 
英[əkˈseləreɪt] 美[ækˈsɛləˌret]
 
vt.（使）加快，（使）增速；加速，催促；促进；速度增加；vi.加快，加速；
 "

V-ERG
（使）加快；（使）增速
If the process or rate of something accelerates or if something accelerates it, it gets faster and faster.
例：Growth will accelerate to 2.9 per cent next year...明年的增长会加快到2.9%。
例：The government is to accelerate its privatisation programme.政府将加快其私有化计划的进程。

VERB
（运动中的车辆）加速
When a moving vehicle accelerates, it goes faster and faster.
例：Suddenly the car accelerated...汽车突然加速。
例：She accelerated away from the kerb.她加速驶离了马路牙子。

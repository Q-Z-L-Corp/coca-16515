英[hɜ:z] 美[hɜrz]
 
pron.她的；属于她的；

PRON-POSS
她的(东西)
You use hers to indicate that something belongs or relates to a woman, girl, or female animal.
例：His hand as it shook hers was warm and firm...和她握手时他的手温暖而有力。
例：He'd never seen eyes as green as hers...他从未看到过像她那样澄绿的眼睛。

PRON-POSS
（书面英语中泛指人）他的，她的（有些人不喜欢这种用法，更倾向于使用 his or hers 或 theirs）
In written English, hers is sometimes used to refer to a person without saying whether that person is a man or a woman. Some people dislike this use and prefer to use 'his or hers' or 'theirs'.
例：The author can report other people's results which more or less agree with hers.作者可以提及其他人得出的和她自己基本吻合的结果。

PRON-POSS
（指国家或民族）她的，它的
Hers is sometimes used to refer to a country or nation.

PRON-POSS
（指汽车、机器或船）她的，它的
People sometimes use hers to refer to a car, machine, or ship.

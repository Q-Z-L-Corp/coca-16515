conceptualize 
英[kənˈseptʃuəlaɪz] 美[kənˈsɛptʃuəˌlaɪz]
 
vt.使有概念；vi.概念化；
 "

VERB
构思;使形成概念
If you conceptualize something, you form an idea of it in your mind.
例：How we conceptualize things has a lot to do with what we feel...我们对事物的概念在很大程度上取决于我们的感觉。
例：Tiffany conceptualized herself as a mother, whose primary task was to feed her baby.蒂法尼把自己定位为一位母亲，主要任务是哺育孩子。

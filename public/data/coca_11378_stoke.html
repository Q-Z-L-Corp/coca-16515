stoke 
英[stəʊk] 美[stoʊk]
 
vt.拨旺火；使饱吃一顿；给（机车等）烧火；vi.烧火；做司炉工；大吃大喝；n.斯托克；
 "

VERB
给…添煤(或柴火)
If you stoke a fire, you add coal or wood to it to keep it burning.
例：She was stoking the stove with sticks of maple.她正在往炉子里添枫树枝。

VERB
煽起;鼓动;激起
If you stoke something such as a feeling, you cause it to be felt more strongly.
例：These demands are helping to stoke fears of civil war.这些要求助长了人们对内战的恐惧。

N-COUNT
（广播或电视节目的）主持人
A radio or television presenter is a person who introduces the items in a particular programme.
例：Most people think being a television presenter is exciting...很多人认为当电视节目主持人是一件刺激的事情。
例：Marcel Berlins is the presenter of the BBC radio programme Law in Action.马塞尔·柏林斯是英国广播公司《法治进行时》节目的主持人。

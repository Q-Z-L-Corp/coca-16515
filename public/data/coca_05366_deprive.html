VERB
剥夺;使丧失;使不能享有
If you deprive someone of something that they want or need, you take it away from them, or you prevent them from having it.
例：The disintegration of the Soviet Union deprived western intelligence agencies of their main enemies...苏联解体使西方情报机构丧失了主敌。

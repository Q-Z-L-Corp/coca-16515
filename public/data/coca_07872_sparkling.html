sparkling 
英[ˈspɑ:klɪŋ] 美[ˈspɑrklɪŋ]
 
adj.发火花的，闪亮的；灿烂的，活泼的；闪闪发光的，闪烁的；起泡沫的；v.闪光，闪耀(sparkle的现在分词)；
 "

ADJ
(饮料)起泡的
Sparkling drinks are slightly fizzy.
例：...a glass of sparkling wine.一杯葡萄汽酒
例：...a new lightly sparkling drink.一杯刚刚斟上的微微冒着泡的饮料

ADJ
(公司业绩)斐然的，辉煌的
If a company is described as having sparkling figures or sparkling results, it has performed very well and made a lot of money.
例：Top retailer Marks & Spencer has romped in with another set of sparkling results.零售巨头玛莎百货再次凭借一系列耀眼的成绩轻松胜出。

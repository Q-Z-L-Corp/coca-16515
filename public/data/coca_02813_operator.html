operator 
英[ˈɒpəreɪtə(r)] 美[ˈɑpəreɪtə(r)]
 
n.经营者；操作员；电话接线员；运算符；
 "

N-COUNT
电话接线员
An operator is a person who connects telephone calls at a telephone exchange or in a place such as an office or hotel.
例：He dialled the operator and put in a call for Rome.他拨了接线员的号，让他接通一个到罗马的电话。

N-COUNT
（机器）操作员，技工
An operator is a person who is employed to operate or control a machine.
例：...computer operators.计算机操作员

N-COUNT
（企业的）经营者；运营公司
An operator is a person or a company that runs a business.
例：...'Tele-Communications', the nation's largest cable TV operator.全国最大的有线电视运营商“电视通信公司”

N-COUNT
精明圆滑的人；善于取巧的人
If you call someone a good operator, you mean that they are skilful at achieving what they want, often in a slightly dishonest way.
例：He was a smart operator. Don't underestimate him.他这个人精明圆滑，可别小瞧了他。
例：...one of the shrewdest political operators in the Arab World.阿拉伯世界最精明狡猾的政客之一

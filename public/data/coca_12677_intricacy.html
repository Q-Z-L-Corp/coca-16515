N-UNCOUNT
复杂精细;错综复杂
Intricacy is the state of being made up of many small parts or details.
例：The price depends on the intricacy of the work.价格取决于做工的复杂精细度。

N-PLURAL
错综复杂的细节;纷繁复杂之处
The intricacies of something are its complicated details.
例：Rose explained the intricacies of the job.罗斯解释了这项工作的繁琐复杂之处。

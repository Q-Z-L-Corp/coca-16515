delve 
英[delv] 美[dɛlv]
 
vi.探究；挖掘；vt.钻研；挖掘；n.穴；洞；
 "

VERB
探究;钻研
If you delve into something, you try to discover new information about it.
例：Tormented by her ignorance, Jenny delves into her mother's past...珍妮为自己的一无所知而苦恼，开始探究起母亲的过去。
例：If you're interested in a subject, use the Internet to delve deeper.如果对某一主题感兴趣，就上网进一步深入研究。

VERB
(在壁橱、袋子等中)搜寻，翻找
If you delve inside something such as a cupboard or a bag, you search inside it.
例：She delved into her rucksack and pulled out a folder.她在背包里翻找一番后拿出了一个文件夹。

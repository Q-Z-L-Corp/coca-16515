exhaustive 
英[ɪgˈzɔ:stɪv] 美[ɪɡˈzɔstɪv]
 
adj.彻底的，透彻的；消耗的，使枯竭的；详尽的，无遗的；详尽无遗；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
详尽无遗的;彻底全面的
If you describe a study, search, or list as exhaustive, you mean that it is very thorough and complete.
例：This is by no means an exhaustive list but it gives an indication of the many projects taking place...这绝称不上是详尽无遗的清单，但是列出了很多正在进行的项目。
例：The author's treatment of the subject is exhaustive.该作者对这个问题的分析很透彻。

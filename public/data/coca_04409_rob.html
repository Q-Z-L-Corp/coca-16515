rob 
英[rɒb] 美[rɑb]
 
vt.抢劫；抢夺，掠夺；非法剥夺；使丧失；vi.抢劫；掠夺；
 "

VERB
抢劫;抢夺;打劫
If someone is robbed, they have money or property stolen from them.
例：Mrs Yacoub was robbed of her £3,000 designer watch at her West London home...雅各布夫人那块价值3,000英镑的名牌手表在西伦敦的家中被人抢走了。
例：Police said Stefanovski had robbed a man just hours earlier.警察说斯泰凡诺夫斯基就在几个小时前抢劫了一名男子。

VERB
夺走;剥夺
If someone is robbed of something that they deserve, have, or need, it is taken away from them.
例：When Miles Davis died, jazz was robbed of its most distinctive voice...随着迈尔斯·戴维斯的去世，爵士乐界失去了其最与众不同的声音。
例：I can't forgive Lewis for robbing me of an Olympic gold.我不能原谅刘易斯夺走了我一块奥运会金牌。

therapy 
英[ˈθerəpi] 美[ˈθɛrəpi]
 
n.治疗，疗法，疗效；心理治疗；治疗力；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(非药物、非手术的)疗法，治疗
Therapy is the treatment of someone with mental or physical illness without the use of drugs or operations.
例：In therapy, she began to let go of her obsession with Mike...在接受治疗的过程中，她开始逐渐从对迈克的迷恋中解脱出来。
例：He is having therapy to conquer his phobia.他在接受治疗以克服自己的恐惧症。

N-VAR
(针对某人特定病情的)治疗方案，治疗方法
A therapy is a particular treatment of someone with a particular illness.
例：...hormonal therapies.激素疗法
例：...conventional drug therapy.常规药物疗法

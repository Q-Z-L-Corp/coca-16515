article 
英[ˈɑ:tɪkl] 美[ˈɑrtɪkl]
 
n.（报章杂志中的）文章，论文；条款；物品；[语] 冠词；vt.使受协议条款的约束；以协议（或契约）约束；订约将…收为学徒（或徒弟）；定约雇用；vi.进行控告，提出罪状（或指责）(against)；签订协议；
 "

N-COUNT
（报纸、杂志的）文章
An article is a piece of writing that is published in a newspaper or magazine.
例：...a newspaper article...一篇报载文章
例：...a travel article...一篇游记

N-COUNT
物品；物件
You can refer to objects as articles of some kind.
例：...articles of clothing...衣物
例：He had stripped the house of all articles of value.他把房间里值钱的东西洗劫一空。

PHRASE
正品;真货
If you describe something as the genuine article, you are emphasizing that it is genuine, and often that it is very good.
例：The vodka was the genuine article.这种伏特加酒是正品。

N-COUNT
（契约、文件的）条款，条文，项目
An article of a formal agreement or document is a section of it which deals with a particular point.
例：The country appears to be violating several articles of the convention.这个国家看起来违反了公约中的好几项条款。
例：...Article 50 of the UN charter.《联合国宪章》第50条

N-PLURAL
见习契约
Someone who is in articles is being trained as a lawyer or accountant by a firm with whom they have a written agreement.
例：In 1986, 44 per cent of those admitted to articles were women.1986年，44％的实习生为女性。

N-COUNT
冠词（a和an是不定冠词，the是定冠词）
In grammar, an article is a kind of determiner. In English, 'a' and 'an' are called the indefinite article, and 'the' is called the definite article .

N-VAR
伤害;损伤
An injury is damage done to a person's or an animal's body.
例：Four police officers sustained serious injuries in the explosion...4名警官在爆炸中受重伤。
例：The two other passengers escaped serious injury.另外两名乘客逃过一劫，未受重伤。

N-VAR
(感情、名誉上的)伤害,损害
If someone suffers injury to their feelings, they are badly upset by something. If they suffer injury to their reputation, their reputation is seriously harmed.
例：She was awarded £3,500 for injury to her feelings.她判得 3,500 英镑精神损失赔偿。

adj.使人不安的；（消息）混乱的；v.扰乱，使不安宁；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人担忧的；令人不安的
If you describe something as unsettling, you mean that it makes you feel rather worried or uncertain.
例：The prospect of change of this kind has an unsettling effect on any organisation...这种变化的前景对任何组织都有不确定的影响。
例：His sense of humour was really unsettling.他的幽默感确实令人不安。

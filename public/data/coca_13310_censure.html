VERB
严厉批评;指责
If you censure someone for something that they have done, you tell them that you strongly disapprove of it.
例：The ethics committee may take a decision to admonish him or to censure him...道德委员会也许会决定对他进行训诫或是严厉批评。
例：I would not presume to censure Osborne for hating his mother.我不会擅自批评奥斯本恨他妈妈这件事。

heartily 
英[ˈhɑ:tɪli] 美[ˈhɑrtɪli]
 
adv.尽情地；强烈地；坚定地；极为；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
大声嚷嚷的;兴高采烈的;精力充沛的
Hearty people or actions are loud, cheerful, and energetic.
例：Wade was a hearty, bluff, athletic sort of guy...韦德是个精力充沛、率直、运动型的人。
例：He gave a hearty laugh.他开怀大笑。

ADJ-GRADED
（情感、观点等）强烈的
Hearty feelings or opinions are strongly felt or strongly held.
例：With the last sentiment, Arnold was in hearty agreement.阿诺德强烈赞成最后一个观点。

ADJ-GRADED
（饭菜）丰盛的
A hearty meal is large and very satisfying.
例：The men ate a hearty breakfast.这些人吃了一顿丰盛的早餐。
例：...a hearty soup delicately flavoured with nutmeg.加了肉豆蔻调味的足料浓汤

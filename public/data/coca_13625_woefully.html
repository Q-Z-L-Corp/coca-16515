woefully 
英['wəʊfəlɪ] 美['woʊfəlɪ]
 
adv.忧愁地，可怜地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（人或事物）悲惨的，忧伤的，哀伤的
If someone or something is woeful, they are very sad.
例：...a woeful ballad.忧伤的情歌

ADJ-GRADED
恶劣的;糟糕的;不合意的
You can use woeful to emphasize that something is very bad or undesirable.
例：Such attitudes are the product of woeful ignorance.这样的态度是极度无知造成的。
例：...the woeful state of the economy.糟糕的经济状况

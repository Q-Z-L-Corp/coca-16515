ADJ
无与伦比的；无可匹敌的
If you describe something as unmatched, you are emphasizing that it is better or greater than all other things of the same kind.
例：...a landscape of unmatched beauty...无与伦比的美景
例：Brian's old-fashioned cuisine was unmatched for flavour.布赖恩的传统风味菜肴味道简直无与伦比。

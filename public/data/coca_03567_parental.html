ADJ
父母的；父的；母的；家长的
Parental is used to describe something that relates to parents in general, or to one or both of the parents of a particular child.
例：Medical treatment was sometimes given to children without parental consent...有时未征得家长同意就对孩子进行了药物治疗。
例：Parental attitudes vary widely.家长态度千差万别。

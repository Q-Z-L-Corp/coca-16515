ADJ-GRADED
极坏的;糟透的
If you describe a situation or the condition of something as abysmal, you think that it is very bad or poor in quality.
例：...our abysmal record at producing a scientifically trained workforce...我们在培育具有科学素养的专业人才方面惨不忍睹的记录
例：The general standard of racing was abysmal.竞赛总体水平极糟。

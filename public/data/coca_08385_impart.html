impart 
英[ɪmˈpɑ:t] 美[ɪmˈpɑrt]
 
vt.给予；告知，透露；传授；
 "

VERB
传授;告知
If you impart information to people, you tell it to them.
例：The ability to impart knowledge and command respect is the essential qualification for teachers...具备传授知识并赢得尊敬的能力是对教师的基本要求。
例：I am about to impart knowledge to you that you will never forget.我会向你传授让你终生牢记的知识。

VERB
给予(特定品质)
To impart a particular quality to something means to give it that quality.
例：She managed to impart great elegance to the unpretentious dress she was wearing...她使自己朴素的衣着看起来也十分优雅。
例：His production of Harold Pinter's play fails to impart a sense of excitement or danger.他所执导的哈罗德·品特的剧作没能表现出紧张、危险感。

vi.咆哮；粗野的下结论；大声责骂；vt.大声说出；n.大话；粗野的话；[英]狂欢；
 "

VERB
咆哮;大喊大叫;夸夸其谈
If you say that someone rants, you mean that they talk loudly or angrily, and exaggerate or say foolish things.
例：As the boss began to rant, I stood up and went out...老板开始咆哮的时候，我起身走了出去。
例：...the mentally ill patient we heard ranting about demons...我们听到正在大叫着魔鬼的精神病人

PHRASE
胡言乱语；大喊大叫
If you say that someone rants and raves, you mean that they talk loudly and angrily in an uncontrolled way.
例：I don't rant and rave or throw tea cups.我不会大喊大叫或摔茶杯。

rapprochement 
英[ræ'prɒʃmɒ̃] 美[ˌrɑprɔʃˈmənt]
 
n.友好，和睦；〈外〉和解；恢复邦交；
 "

N-SING
（国家、集体或个人的）重归于好，和解
A rapprochement is an increase in friendliness between two countries, groups, or people, especially after a period of unfriendliness.
例：There have been growing signs of a rapprochement with Vietnam.同越南恢复友好关系的迹象越来越明显了。
例：...the process of political rapprochement between the two former foes.两个宿敌之间的政治和解进程

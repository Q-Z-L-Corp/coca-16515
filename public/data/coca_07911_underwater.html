underwater 
英[ˌʌndəˈwɔ:tə(r)] 美[ˌʌndərˈwɔtə(r)]
 
adj.在水中的；水面下的；adv.在水下，在水中；
 "

ADV
在水下；在水中
Something that exists or happens underwater exists or happens below the surface of the sea, a river, or a lake.
例：...giant submarines able to travel at high speeds underwater...能够在水下高速行进的巨型潜艇
例：Some stretches of beach are completely underwater at high tide...海滩上有些地方在涨潮时完全淹没在水中。

ADJ
供水下用的
Underwater devices are specially made so that they can work in water.
例：...underwater camera equipment.水下摄影器材
例：...a pool of clear water lit by underwater lights.被水下灯光照亮的一池碧水

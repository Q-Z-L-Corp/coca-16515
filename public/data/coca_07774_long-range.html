ADJ-GRADED
（军事装备或运载工具）远程的
A long-range piece of military equipment or vehicle is able to hit or detect a target a long way away or to travel a long way in order to do something.
例：He is very keen to reach agreement with the US on reducing long-range nuclear missiles.他很想和美国达成协议，减少远程核导弹的数量。
例：...the growing use on the North Atlantic routes of long-range twin-engined aircraft.远程双引擎飞机在北大西洋航线上越来越多的使用

ADJ-GRADED
（计划、预测）长远的，长期的
A long-range plan or prediction relates to a period extending a long time into the future.
例：Eisenhower was intensely aware of the need for long-range planning.艾森豪威尔深谙长远规划的必要性。
例：...a bold, complex, and long-range strategy for improving US education.一项大胆、复杂的改善美国教育水平的长远策略

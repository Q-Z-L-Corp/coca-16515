unlock 
英[ˌʌnˈlɒk] 美[ˌʌnˈlɑk]
 
vt.开锁；开启；揭开；表露；vi.解开；被释放；
 "

VERB
（用钥匙）打开，开启(门、房间、箱匣等的锁)
If you unlock something such as a door, a room, or a container that has a lock, you open it using a key.
例：He unlocked the car and threw the coat on to the back seat...他用钥匙打开车门，把外套扔到了后座上。
例：She unlocked the case and carefully lifted out the vase.她用钥匙打开了箱子，小心翼翼地拿出那只花瓶。

VERB
释放（潜能）；揭开（秘密）
If you unlock the potential or the secrets of something or someone, you release them.
例：Education and training is the key that will unlock our nation's potential.教育和培训是发掘我们国家潜力的关键。
例：...the man who dedicated his life to unlocking the secrets of the universe.终生致力于揭开宇宙奥秘的人

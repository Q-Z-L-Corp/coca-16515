susceptibility 
英[səˌseptəˈbɪləti] 美[səˌsɛptəˈbɪlɪti]
 
n.易受影响或损害的状态，感受性；感情（脆弱之处）；磁化系数；
 "

N-VAR
易受影响(或损害)的状况;敏感性;过敏性
If you have a susceptibility to something unpleasant, you are likely to be affected by it.
例：...his increased susceptibility to infections.他越来越容易受感染的体质

N-PLURAL
感情脆弱之处;情感的要害
A person's susceptibilities are feelings which can be easily hurt.
例：I am well aware that in saying this I shall outrage a few susceptibilities.我十分清楚这样说会戳到一些人的痛处。

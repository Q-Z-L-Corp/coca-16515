strap

v.用带捆扎；用皮带抽打；拼命工作；n.带子；皮带；磨刀皮带；鞭打；
 "

N-COUNT
带子;皮带;布带
A strap is a narrow piece of leather, cloth, or other material. Straps are used to carry things, fasten things together, or to hold a piece of clothing in place.
例：Nancy gripped the strap of her beach bag...南希抓住了自己沙滩包的带子。
例：Brian pulled the straps through the buckles of his suitcase...布赖恩将带子穿过自己手提箱的带扣。

VERB
用带子系(或绑、捆、扎)
If you strap something somewhere, you fasten it there with a strap.
例：Strapping the skis on the roof, we boarded the hovercraft in Dover...用带子把滑水板绑在船顶后，我们就在多佛登上了气垫船。
例：She strapped the gun belt around her middle...她将枪带系在腰上。

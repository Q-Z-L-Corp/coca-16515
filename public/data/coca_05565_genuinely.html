genuinely 
英['dʒenjʊɪnlɪ] 美[ˈdʒɛnjʊɪnlɪ]
 
adv.真地；真正地；真诚地；诚实地；
 "

ADJ
真正的；非伪造的；名副其实的
Genuine is used to describe people and things that are exactly what they appear to be, and are not false or an imitation.
例：There was a risk of genuine refugees being returned to Vietnam.存在将真正的难民遣返回越南的风险。
例：...genuine leather...真皮

ADJ-GRADED
真挚的；真诚的；诚挚的
Genuine refers to things such as emotions that are real and not pretended.
例：There was genuine joy in this room...房间里洋溢着发自内心的快乐。
例：If this offer is genuine I will gladly accept it.如果这份帮助是诚心的，我欣然接受。

ADJ-GRADED
真诚的；诚实可靠的；诚恳的
If you describe a person as genuine, you approve of them because they are honest, truthful, and sincere in the way they live and in their relationships with other people.
例：She is very caring and very genuine.她非常体贴，非常诚实可靠。

V-RECIP-ERG
(使)发出叮当声;(使)叮当作响
If objects made of glass, pottery, or metal clink or if you clink them, they touch each other and make a short, light sound.
例：She clinked her glass against his...她和他碰了一下杯。
例：They clinked glasses...他们碰了杯。

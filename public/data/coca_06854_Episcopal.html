ADJ
（苏格兰和美国的）新教圣公会的
Episcopal means relating to a branch of the Anglican Church in Scotland and the USA.
例：...the Scottish Episcopal Church.苏格兰新教圣公会
例：...the Episcopal bishop of New York.纽约新教圣公会主教

ADJ
主教的
Episcopal means relating to bishops.
例：...episcopal conferences.主教会议
例：...a set of red episcopal vestments.一套红色的主教法衣

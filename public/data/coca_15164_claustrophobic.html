claustrophobic 
英[ˌklɔ:strəˈfəʊbɪk] 美[ˌklɔstrəˈfoʊbɪk]
 
adj.（患）幽闭恐怖症的，导致幽闭恐怖症的；令人不适地被关闭或被包围的；n.幽闭恐怖症患者；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(地方等)引起幽闭恐惧的
You describe a place or situation as claustrophobic when it makes you feel uncomfortable and unhappy because you are enclosed or restricted.
例：They lived in an unhealthily claustrophobic atmosphere...他们生活在一种令人感到幽闭恐惧的不健康氛围里。
例：The house felt too claustrophobic.这所房子太让人感到幽闭恐惧了。

ADJ-GRADED
感觉幽闭恐惧的
If you feel claustrophobic, you feel very uncomfortable or anxious when you are in a small, crowded, or enclosed place.
例：The churning, pressing crowds made her feel claustrophobic.挤来挤去的密集人群使她感到一种透不过气的恐惧。

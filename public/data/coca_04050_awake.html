awake 
英[əˈweɪk] 美[əˈwek]
 
adj.醒着的；警惕的；vt.& vi.唤醒；唤起；觉悟；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
醒着的；没睡着的
Someone who is awake is not sleeping.
例：I don't stay awake at night worrying about that...我并没有因为那件事而担心得彻夜不眠。
例：Nightmares kept me awake all night.噩梦扰得我整夜未眠。

PHRASE
毫无睡意的;失眠的
Someone who is wide awake is fully awake and unable to sleep.
例：I could not relax and still felt wide awake.我没法放松下来，还是觉得睡意全无。

V-ERG
醒来；唤醒；叫醒
When you awake or when something awakes you, you wake up.
例：At midnight he awoke and listened to the radio for a few minutes...他半夜醒来，听了一会儿收音机。
例：I awoke to the sound of the wind in the trees...外面风吹树响把我吵醒了。

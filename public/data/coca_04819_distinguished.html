distinguished 
英[dɪˈstɪŋgwɪʃt] 美[dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt]
 
adj.卓越的；著名的；受人尊敬的；显得重要的；v.辨别，区别( distinguish的过去式和过去分词)；突出；区别(distinguish的过去式)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
卓越的;杰出的;著名的
If you describe a person or their work as distinguished, you mean that they have been very successful in their career and have a good reputation.
例：...a distinguished academic family.杰出的学者之家
例：...a distinguished career.成就卓然的事业

ADJ-GRADED
高贵的;尊贵的;高尚的
If you describe someone as distinguished, you mean that they look very noble and respectable.
例：He looked very distinguished.他看上去非常高贵。
例：...a distinguished gentleman.一位尊贵的绅士

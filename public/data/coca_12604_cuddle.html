V-RECIP
拥抱;搂抱
If you cuddle someone, you put your arms round them and hold them close as a way of showing your affection.
例：He cuddled the newborn girl...他抱着刚出生的女婴。
例：They used to kiss and cuddle in front of everyone.他们过去经常在人前搂抱亲吻。

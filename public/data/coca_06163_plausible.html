plausible 
英[ˈplɔ:zəbl] 美[ˈplɔzəbəl]

ADJ-GRADED
（解释或说法）似乎真实的，貌似合理的
An explanation or statement that is plausible seems likely to be true or valid.
例：A more plausible explanation would seem to be that people are fed up with the Conservative government...貌似更加合理的解释似乎是，人们已经厌倦了保守党政府。
例：That explanation seems entirely plausible to me.那个解释在我看来完全说得通。

ADJ-GRADED
（人）貌似可信的，花言巧语的
If you say that someone is plausible, you mean that they seem to be telling the truth and to be sincere and honest.
例：He was so plausible that he conned everybody.他那么会花言巧语，以至于骗过了所有的人。

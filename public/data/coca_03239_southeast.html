southeast 
英[ˌsaʊθ'i:st] 美[saʊθˈist, saʊˈist]
 
n.东南，东南方，（美国）东南部；东南地区；adv.在东南；从东南；向东南；来自东南；adj.东南的；向东南的；位于东南的；东南部的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
东南;东南方
The south-east is the direction which is halfway between south and east.
例：The city of Chengdu lies some seven hundred miles to the South-East.成都市位于东南方大约 700 英里处。

N-SING
(地方、国家或地区的)东南部
The south-east of a place, country, or region is the part which is in the south-east.
例：...the regional electricity company serving the South-east of England...为英格兰东南部服务的区域电力公司
例：The heaviest snowfalls today are expected in the south east.今天最大的降雪预计将出现在东南部地区。

ADV
向东南方;朝东南
If you go south-east, you travel towards the south-east.
例：We turned south-east, making for Portoferraio.我们转向东南方向，朝费拉约港前进。

ADV
位于…的东南方;在…的东南
Something that is south-east of a place is positioned to the south-east of it.
例：...the potteries of Iznik, some 120km south-east of Istanbul.位于伊斯坦布尔东南大约120公里处的伊兹尼克出产的陶器

ADJ
东南部的;东南方的
The south-east part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the south-east.
例：...South-East Asia.东南亚
例：...the south-east quarter of Lincolnshire.林肯郡的东南区

ADJ
(风)来自东南方的
A south-east wind is a wind that blows from the south-east.

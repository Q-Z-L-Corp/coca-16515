unqualified 
英[ˌʌnˈkwɒlɪfaɪd] 美[ˌʌnˈkwɑlɪfaɪd]
 
adj.不合格的；无资格的；无限制的；不胜任的；
 "

ADJ
没资格的；资格不够的；不合格的
If you are unqualified, you do not have any qualifications, or you do not have the right qualifications for a particular job.
例：She was unqualified for the job...她没资格做这份工作。
例：Unqualified members of staff at the hospital were not sufficiently supervised.医院里一些还未取得资格的员工没有受到充分的督导。

ADJ
完全的；绝对的；无保留的；不受限制的
Unqualified means total or unlimited.
例：The event was an unqualified success...这一活动取得了极大成功。
例：Egypt has given almost unqualified backing to Washington.埃及几乎全力支持华盛顿。

cabin 
英[ˈkæbɪn] 美[ˈkæbɪn]
 
n.<美>小木屋；客舱；（轮船上工作或生活的）隔间；vt.把…关在小屋里；使受拘束；vi.住在小屋里；

N-COUNT
(船上的)小舱
A cabin is a small room in a ship or boat.
例：He showed her to a small cabin.他把她领到了一个小船舱。

N-COUNT
(飞机上的)机舱,客舱
A cabin is one of the areas inside a plane.
例：He sat quietly in the First Class cabin, looking tired.他一脸倦意,静静地坐在头等舱里。

N-COUNT
(尤指森林、群山中的)小木屋
A cabin is a small wooden house, especially one in an area of forests or mountains.
例：...a log cabin.原木小屋

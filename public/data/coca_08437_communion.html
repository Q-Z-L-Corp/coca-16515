communion 
英[kəˈmju:niən] 美[kəˈmjunjən]
 
n.交流，恳谈；共享，共有；宗教团体；圣餐仪式；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(思想或情感的)交流，交融
Communion with nature or with a person is the feeling that you are sharing thoughts or feelings with them.
例：...communion with nature.与大自然的交融
例：...a communion of souls.心灵之交

N-UNCOUNT
(基督教的)圣餐仪式
Communion is the Christian ceremony in which people eat bread and drink wine in memory of Christ's death.
例：Most villagers took communion only at Easter.大部分村民只在复活节的时候领受圣餐。
例：...the Communion service.圣餐仪式

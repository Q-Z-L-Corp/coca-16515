unbearable 
英[ʌnˈbeərəbl] 美[ʌnˈberəbl]
 
adj.难以忍受的，不能忍受的，经受不住的；承受不住的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不堪忍受的；无法容忍的；难以承受的
If you describe something as unbearable, you mean that it is so unpleasant, painful, or upsetting that you feel unable to accept it or deal with it.
例：War has made life almost unbearable for the civilians remaining in the capital...对留在首都的平民来说，战争已使生活变得几乎不堪忍受。
例：I was in terrible, unbearable pain.我当时疼痛难忍。

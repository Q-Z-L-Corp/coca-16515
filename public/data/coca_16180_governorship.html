governorship 
英['gʌvənəʃɪp] 美['gʌvənəˌʃɪp]
 
n.总督（或州长等地方行政长官）的职位（或职责、任期）；
 "

N-COUNT
州长职位；州长任期
The governorship of a particular country or state is the position of being its governor. Governorship is also used to refer to the period of time a particular person spends being the governor of a country or state.
例：The governorship went to a Democrat, Mrs Anne Richards...民主党人安妮·理查兹夫人当选为州长。
例：He had worked closely with the President during his governorship.他在任州长期间与总统紧密合作。

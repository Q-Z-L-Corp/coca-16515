N-UNCOUNT
（书、广告中的）插图，插画
Artwork is drawings and photographs that are prepared in order to be included in something such as a book or advertisement.
例：The artwork for the LP was done by Bill Hofstadter.林肯公园的广告插图出自比尔·霍夫斯塔特之手。

N-VAR
（高品质的）艺术作品
Artworks are paintings or sculptures which are of high quality.
例：The museum contains 6,000 contemporary and modern artworks.博物馆收藏了6,000件近现代艺术作品。
例：...a magnificent collection of priceless artwork.令人叹为观止的价值连城的艺术收藏品

ADJ-GRADED
可溶解的
A substance that is soluble will dissolve in a liquid.
例：Uranium is soluble in sea water.铀可溶解于海水。

COMB in ADJ
水溶性的/脂溶性的
If something is water-soluble or fat-soluble, it will dissolve in water or in fat.
例：The red dye on the leather is water-soluble.皮革上的这种红色染料可溶于水。
例：...fat-soluble vitamins.脂溶性的维生素

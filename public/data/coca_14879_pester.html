VERB
不断打扰；纠缠；烦扰
If you say that someone is pestering you, you mean that they keep asking you to do something, or keep talking to you, and you find this annoying.
例：I thought she'd stop pestering me, but it only seemed to make her worse...我原以为她会停止纠缠我，但结果她似乎是变本加厉了。
例：I know he gets fed up with people pestering him for money.我知道他烦透了那些缠着他要钱的人。

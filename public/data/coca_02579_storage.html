N-UNCOUNT
储藏;贮存
If you refer to the storage of something, you mean that it is kept in a special place until it is needed.
例：...the storage of toxic waste...有毒废弃物的存放
例：Some of the space will at first be used for storage...一部分空间将首先用于贮藏。

N-UNCOUNT
(计算机的)存储
Storage is the process of storing data in a computer.
例：His task is to ensure the fair use and storage of personal information held on computer.他的任务是确保计算机里的个人信息得到合理利用和妥善存储。
例：...data-storage devices.数据存储装置

patchwork 
英[ˈpætʃwɜ:k] 美[ˈpætʃwɜrk]
 
n.补缀品，拼缝物；混杂物；
 "

ADJ
拼缀的；拼缝的
A patchwork quilt, cushion, or piece of clothing is made by sewing together small pieces of material of different colours or patterns.
例：...beds covered in patchwork quilts.盖着百衲被的床

N-SING
拼凑物；混杂物
If you refer to something as a patchwork, you mean that it is made up of many different parts, pieces or colours.
例：The low mountains were a patchwork of green and brown.低矮的群山上，绿色和棕色混杂着。
例：...this complex republic, a patchwork of cultures, religions and nationalities.这个复杂的共和国，各种文化、宗教和民族的混合体

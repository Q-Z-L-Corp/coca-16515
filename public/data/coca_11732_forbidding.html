ADJ-GRADED
可怕的;令人生畏的
If you describe a person, place, or thing as forbidding, you mean they have a severe, unfriendly, or threatening appearance.
例：There was something a little severe and forbidding about her face.她脸上有种严肃、冷峻的表情。

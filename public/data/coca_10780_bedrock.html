N-SING
根本原则；基础；基石
The bedrock of something is the principles, ideas, or facts on which it is based.
例：Mutual trust is the bedrock of a relationship...互信是交往的基石。
例：Working-class voters form the bedrock of Labour's support.工薪阶层选民是支持工党的基本力量。

N-UNCOUNT
基岩
Bedrock is the solid rock in the ground which supports all the soil above it.
例：It took five years to drill down to bedrock.耗时五年才钻到了基岩。

ADJ-GRADED
足够的；合格的；可用的
If something is adequate, there is enough of it or it is good enough to be used or accepted.
例：One in four people worldwide are without adequate homes...全世界有1/4的人没有个像样的家。
例：The old methods weren't adequate to meet current needs...老一套方法已不足以满足当前的需要。

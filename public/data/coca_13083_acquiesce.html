acquiesce 
英[ˌækwiˈes] 美[ˌækwiˈɛs]
 
vi.默认，默许；adj.默认的；
 "

VERB
同意；默认；默许；顺从
If you acquiesce in something, you agree to do what someone wants or to accept what they do.
例：Steve seemed to acquiesce in the decision...史蒂夫好像默认了这个决定。
例：He has gradually acquiesced to the demands of the opposition...他渐渐顺从了反对派的要求。

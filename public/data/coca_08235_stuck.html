stuck 
英[stʌk] 美[stʌk]
 
v.刺(stick 的过去式及过去分词)；adj.动不了的；被卡住的；被…缠住的；被…难住的，不知所措；
 "

Stuck is the past tense and past participle of stick .

ADJ
卡住的;动不了的;陷入…的
If something is stuck in a particular position, it is fixed tightly in this position and is unable to move.
例：He said his car had got stuck in the snow...他说他的汽车陷在了雪地里。
例：She had got something stuck between her teeth.她的牙缝里塞进了东西。

ADJ
困住的;无法离开的
If you are stuck in a place, you want to get away from it, but are unable to.
例：I was stuck at home with flu...感冒把我困在了家中。
例：Certainly, I wouldn't have wanted to be stuck on a desert island with her for any length of time.当然，我根本一刻也不想和她一起被困在荒岛上。

ADJ
陷入(无聊或不愉快局面)的;受困的;无法脱身的
If you are stuck in a boring or unpleasant situation, you are unable to change it or get away from it.
例：I don't want to get stuck in another job like that...我不想陷入另一份那样的工作中。
例：I am stuck in a relationship which I don't enjoy.我被一段自己并不喜欢的恋情困住了。

ADJ
停滞不前的;无所变化的
If something is stuck at a particular level or stage, it is not progressing or changing.
例：The negotiations have got stuck on a number of key issues...谈判在一些重大问题上陷入了僵局。
例：US unemployment figures for March showed the jobless rate stuck at 7 per cent...3 月份的美国失业数据显示失业率一直徘徊在 7%上下。

ADJ
无法摆脱的;甩不掉的
If you are stuck with something that you do not want, you cannot get rid of it.
例：Many people are now stuck with expensive fixed-rate mortgages...许多人目前都有高额的定息按揭缠身。
例：I know you think I'm stupid, but we're stuck with each other, aren't we?我知道你认为我愚蠢，但我们俩谁都摆脱不了谁，不是吗？

ADJ
难以继续的;被难倒的
If you get stuck when you are trying to do something, you are unable to continue doing it because it is too difficult.
例：They will be there to help if you get stuck...如果你遇到难题的话他们会帮助你。
例：If he gets stuck on a word, he can make the computer prompt him.如果他被某个单词绊住，他可以从电脑上获得提示。

PHRASE
起劲地干;下定决心做
If you get stuck in, you do something with enthusiasm and determination.
例：We're bottom of the league and we have to get stuck in.我们是联赛的最后一名，所以我们必须拼命赶超。

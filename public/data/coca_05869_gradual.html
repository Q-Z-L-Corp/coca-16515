ADJ-GRADED
逐渐的；逐步的
A gradual change or process occurs in small stages over a long period of time, rather than suddenly.
例：Losing weight is a slow, gradual process...减肥是一个缓慢而渐进的过程。
例：You can expect her progress at school to be gradual rather than brilliant.你可以指望她在学习上循序渐进，但别指望她突飞猛进。

例：He took out a sheaf of papers and leafed through them.他拿出一沓报纸，匆匆翻阅了一下。

N-COUNT
一束，一捆(收割的谷物)
A sheaf of corn or wheat is a number of corn or wheat plants that have been cut down and tied together.

ADJ-GRADED
（产品或服务）高档的,质优价高的
Upscale is used to describe products or services that are expensive, of good quality, and intended to appeal to people in a high social class.
例：Vodka has acquired an upscale image in the US.伏特加在美国已树立起高档商品的形象。
例：...upscale department-store chains such as Bloomingdale's and Saks Fifth Avenue.诸如布鲁明黛和萨克斯第五大街等高级百货连锁店

ADJ
赤脚的；光着脚的
Someone who is barefoot or barefooted is not wearing anything on their feet.
例：I wore a white dress and was barefoot...我穿着一件白色连衣裙，赤着脚。
例：Alan came running barefoot through the house.艾伦赤脚跑过房子。

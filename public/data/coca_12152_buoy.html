buoy 
英[bɔɪ] 美[ˈbui, bɔɪ]
 
n.浮标；救生圈；航标；vt.使浮起；支持，鼓励；
 "

N-COUNT
浮标；航标
A buoy is a floating object that is used to show ships and boats where they can go and to warn them of danger.

VERB
鼓舞；鼓励；使振奋
If someone in a difficult situation is buoyed by something, it makes them feel more cheerful and optimistic.
例：In May they danced in the streets, buoyed by their victory...5月里，他们在大街上载歌载舞，欢庆胜利。
例：German domestic consumption buoyed the German economy.德国的国内消费刺激了德国经济。

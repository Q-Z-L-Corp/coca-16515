n.教学；教义；所教的东西；学说；adj.教学的；教导的；v.教( teach的现在分词)；教书；训练；教育；

N-UNCOUNT
教学;教书
Teaching is the work that a teacher does in helping students to learn.
例：The Government funds university teaching.政府为高校教学提供资金。
例：...the teaching of English in schools.学校的英语教学

N-COUNT
教义;教导;学说
The teachings of a particular person, group of people, or religion are all the ideas and principles that they teach.
例：...the teachings of Jesus.耶稣的教义
例：...their teachings on sexuality and marriage.他们关于性和婚姻的教义

VERB
使随机化;任意排列
If you randomize the events or people in scientific experiments or academic research, you use a method that gives them all an equal chance of happening or being chosen.
例：The wheel is designed with obstacles in the ball's path to randomise its movement...设计轮盘时在小球的行进路线上设置了障碍，以使其运动随机化。
例：Properly randomized studies are only now being completed.真正的随机研究现在才接近完成。

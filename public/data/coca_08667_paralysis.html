paralysis 
英[pəˈræləsɪs] 美[pəˈrælɪsɪs]
 
n.麻痹，瘫痪；中风；无能，无气力；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
麻痹；瘫痪
Paralysis is the loss of the ability to move and feel in all or part of your body.
例：...paralysis of the leg.腿部麻痹

N-UNCOUNT
瘫痪状态
Paralysis is the state of being unable to act or function properly.
例：The paralysis of the leadership leaves the army without its supreme command.领导层瘫痪导致军队没有最高指挥。
例：...a kind of mental paralysis.一种精神瘫痪状态

ADV
遍及全世界地;世界各地地
If something exists or happens worldwide, it exists or happens throughout the world.
例：His books have sold more than 20 million copies worldwide...他的书在全世界已经售出两千多万本。
例：Every day the newspapers tell stories of children worldwide who are abused...每天报纸都在讲述世界各地受虐儿童的故事。

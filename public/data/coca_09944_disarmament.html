N-UNCOUNT
裁军;裁减军备(尤指核武器)
Disarmament is the act of reducing the number of weapons, especially nuclear weapons, that a country has.
例：...the pace of nuclear disarmament.裁减核武器的步伐
例：...unilateral disarmament.单边裁军

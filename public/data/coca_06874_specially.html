specially 
英[ˈspeʃəli] 美[ˈspeʃəli]
 
adv.特别地；专门地；特意地；格外地；
 "

ADV
专门地;特别地;特地
If something has been done specially for a particular person or purpose, it has been done only for that person or purpose.
例：...a soap specially designed for those with sensitive skins...特别为皮肤敏感者设计的香皂
例：Patrick needs to use specially adapted computer equipment...帕特里克需要使用专门改装过的计算机设备。

ADV
特别地;超常地
Specially is used to mean more than usually or more than other things.
例：Stay in bed extra late or get up specially early...起床过晚或过早。
例：What was specially enjoyable about that job?那份工作有什么特别有趣之处？

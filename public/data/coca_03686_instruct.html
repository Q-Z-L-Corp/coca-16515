instruct 
英[ɪnˈstrʌkt] 美[ɪnˈstrʌkt]
 
vt.教，讲授；教导，指导；通知；命令；
 "

VERB
指示;吩咐;命令
If you instruct someone to do something, you formally tell them to do it.
例：The family has instructed solicitors to sue Thomson for compensation...那家人已经指示律师起诉汤姆森,要求赔偿。
例：'Go and have a word with her, Ken,' Webb instructed...“肯,去和她谈谈吧,”韦布吩咐道。

VERB
教授;讲授;指导
Someone who instructs people in a subject or skill teaches it to them.
例：He instructed family members in nursing techniques.他教给家人护理技巧。

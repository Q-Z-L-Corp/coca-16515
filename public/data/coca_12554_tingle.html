tingle 
英[ˈtɪŋgl] 美[ˈtɪŋɡəl]
 
vi.感到刺痛；引起刺痛；（耳朵等）鸣响；vt.使…感到刺痛；n.刺痛；激动；鸣响；
 "

VERB
感到刺痛
When a part of your body tingles, you have a slight stinging feeling there.
例：The backs of his thighs tingled.他大腿后侧感到刺痛。
例：…a taste which is first sweet, then bitter, leaving a tingling sensation on the tongue.先甜后苦，在舌头上留下一丝刺痛感觉的味道

VERB
强烈地感到;激动
If you tingle with a feeling such as excitement, you feel it very strongly.
例：She tingled with excitement...她激动不已。
例：When I look over and see Terry I tingle all over.当我放眼望去见到特里时，我整个人兴奋不已。

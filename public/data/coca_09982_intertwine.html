intertwine 
英[ˌɪntəˈtwaɪn] 美[ˌɪntərˈtwaɪn]
 
vt.缠结在一起；使缠结；vi.纠缠；编结；
 "

V-RECIP-ERG
(使)密切关联;(使)密切联系
If two or more things are intertwined or intertwine, they are closely connected with each other in many ways.
例：Their destinies are intertwined...他们的命运交织在一起。
例：Three major narratives intertwine within Foucault's text, 'Madness and Civilisation'...3个主要故事在福柯的《疯癫与文明》一书当中密切相联。

V-RECIP
缠结;缠绕在一起
If two things intertwine, they are twisted together or go over and under each other.
例：Trees, undergrowth and creepers intertwined, blocking our way...树木、灌木丛与藤蔓植物盘根错节,挡住了我们的去路。
例：The towels were embroidered with their intertwined initials.毛巾上绣着他们姓名首字母的连写组合。

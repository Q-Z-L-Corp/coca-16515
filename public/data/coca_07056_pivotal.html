ADJ
中枢的；关键的
A pivotal role, point or figure in something is one that is very important and affects the success of that thing.
例：The Court of Appeal has a pivotal role in the English legal system...上诉法院在英格兰司法体系中起着中心作用。
例：The elections may prove to be pivotal in Colombia's political history.这一选举可能成为哥伦比亚政治史上关键的一次。

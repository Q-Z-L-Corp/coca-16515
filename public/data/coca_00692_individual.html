individual 
英[ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl] 美[ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəl]
 
adj.个人的；独特的；个别的；n.个人；个体；
 "

ADJ
个别的;单独的
Individual means relating to one person or thing, rather than to a large group.
例：They wait for the group to decide rather than making individual decisions...他们等待团体作决定而不是各自作出决定。
例：Aid to individual countries would be linked to progress towards democracy...对个别国家的援助会与其民主进程相挂钩。

N-COUNT
个体;个人
An individual is a person.
例：...anonymous individuals who are doing good things within our community.一些在我们社区内做好事却不留名的个人
例：...the rights and responsibilities of the individual...个人的权利和责任

ADJ-GRADED
独特的;有个性的
If you describe someone or something as individual, you mean that you admire them because they are very unusual and do not try to imitate other people or things.
例：It was really all part of her very individual personality...那其实都是她独特个性的表现。
例：The language is highly individual.语言非常独特。

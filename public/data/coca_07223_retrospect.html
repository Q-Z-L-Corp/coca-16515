retrospect 
英[ˈretrəspekt] 美[ˈrɛtrəˌspɛkt]
 
n.回顾，回想；vt.&vi.回顾；追溯；
 "

PHRASE
回顾;回想
When you consider something in retrospect, you think about it afterwards, and often have a different opinion about it from the one that you had at the time.
例：In retrospect, I wish that I had thought about alternative courses of action...回想起来，我真希望自己当时考虑过其他做法。
例：It was a very strange feeling in retrospect — I was frightened, but excited at the same time.回想起来那是一种非常奇怪的感觉——我当时既害怕又兴奋。

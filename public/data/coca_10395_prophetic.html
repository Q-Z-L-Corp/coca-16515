prophetic

 "

ADJ-GRADED
预言（性）的；预示的
If something was prophetic, it described or suggested something that did actually happen later.
例：This ominous warning soon proved prophetic...这个不祥的警告很快证明是有预见性的。
例：Friends recalled Elisabeth's prophetic words of several years ago.朋友们回想起几年前伊丽莎白预言性的一番话。

ADJ
预言的；先知的；预言者的
Prophetic means related to a prophecy or a prophet.
例：...a charming romance intermingled with scientific fact and prophetic vision.融科学事实和先知远见于其中的一个动人浪漫故事

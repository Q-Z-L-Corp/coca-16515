forbid 
英[fəˈbɪd] 美[fərˈbɪd]
 
vt.禁止；阻止；阻碍；妨碍；
 "

VERB
禁止;不准
If you forbid someone to do something, or if you forbid an activity, you order that it must not be done.
例：They'll forbid you to marry...他们不会准许你结婚。
例：She was shut away and forbidden to read...她被关了起来，并且不允许看书。

VERB
阻止;妨碍;阻碍
If something forbids a particular course of action or state of affairs, it makes it impossible for the course of action or state of affairs to happen.
例：His own pride forbids him to ask Arthur's help...他的自尊心不允许他开口向阿瑟求助。
例：Custom forbids any modernisation.传统习惯阻碍着现代化的进程。

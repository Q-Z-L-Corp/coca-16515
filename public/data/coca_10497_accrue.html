accrue 
英[əˈkru:] 美[əˈkru]
 
vi.增加；（通过自然增长）产生；获得；（使钱款、债务）积累；vt.（利息等）自然增值；[法]（诉讼）发生；
 "

V-ERG
（使）（钱或利息）逐渐增加
If money or interest accrues or if you accrue it, it gradually increases in amount over a period of time.
例：I owed £5,000 — part of this was accrued interest...我欠款5,000英镑，其中一部分是累计的利息。
例：If you do not pay within 28 days, interest will accrue...如果28天之内不付款，就会自然生息。

V-ERG
（利润、好处等）累积，积聚，增加
If things such as profits or benefits accrue to someone, they are added to over a period of time.
例：...the expectation that profits will accrue.希望利润会增加的期待
例：...a project from which considerable benefit will accrue to the community...使社区持续受益的项目

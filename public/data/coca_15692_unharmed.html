ADJ
无恙的；完好无损的
If someone or something is unharmed after an accident or violent incident, they are not hurt or damaged in any way.
例：The car was a write-off, but everyone escaped unharmed...汽车报废了，但是大家都安然脱险。
例：His eleven-year-old daughter was unharmed in the attack...他11岁的女儿在袭击中毫发未损。

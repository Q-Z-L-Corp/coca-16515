nectar 
英[ˈnektə(r)] 美[ˈnɛktɚ]
 
n.花蜜；（古希腊、罗马神话中的）众神饮的酒；琼浆玉液；甘美的饮料；adj.<古>充满美酒的，芳香甜蜜的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
花蜜
Nectar is a sweet liquid produced by flowers, which bees and other insects collect.

N-UNCOUNT
甘露;(尤指酒类)琼浆玉液
If you refer to a drink, especially an alcoholic drink, as nectar, you think it tastes very nice.
例：Tony sipped from his glass. 'Mmm. Ambrosia. Nectar of the gods. Divine. Wonderful.'托尼从杯中抿了一口感叹道：“哇，人间美味啊，神灵的琼浆玉液呀，天赐之物，太棒了。”

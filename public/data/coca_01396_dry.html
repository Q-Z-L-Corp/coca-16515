dry 
英[draɪ] 美[draɪ]
 
adj.干的干燥的，干旱的；干旱的，口渴的；干咳的；无趣味的，枯燥的；vt.从…去掉水分，使…变干；脱水保存（如肉或其它食物）；vi.变干；n.干涸；[复数] drys [非正式用语]主张禁酒的人；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
干的;干燥的
If something is dry, there is no water or moisture on it or in it.
例：Clean the metal with a soft dry cloth...用柔软的干布擦拭金属。
例：Pat it dry with a soft towel...用柔软的毛巾将它拍干。

V-ERG
(使)变干;弄干;擦干
When something dries or when you dry it, it becomes dry.
例：The washing might dry outside today, the sun's shining...洗过的衣物可能今天晾在外面就能干,阳光正足。
例：Leave your hair to dry naturally whenever possible...尽可能让头发自然晾干。

VERB
(洗后）擦干(盘、杯、刀、锅等)
When you dry the dishes after a meal, you wipe the water off the plates, cups, knives, pans, and other things when they have been washed, using a cloth.
例：Mrs. Madrigal began drying dishes.马德里加尔夫人开始擦干碟子。

ADJ-GRADED
(皮肤或头发)发干的,无水分的
If you say that your skin or hair is dry, you mean that it is less oily than, or not as soft as, normal.
例：Nothing looks worse than dry, cracked lips...没有什么比干燥开裂的嘴唇更难看的了。
例：Dry hair can be damaged by washing it too frequently...干性头发洗得太频繁容易受损。

ADJ-GRADED
干旱的;雨量少的;无雨的
If the weather or a period of time is dry, there is no rain or there is much less rain than average.
例：Exceptionally dry weather over the past year had cut agricultural production...去年罕见的干旱天气已导致农业减产。
例：The spring had been unusually dry and we received only two tenths of an inch of rain during the entire month of June.春天异常干旱，我们在整个 6 月只有 0.2 英寸的降雨量。

ADJ-GRADED
(地方或气候)少雨的,干燥的
A dry place or climate is one that gets very little rainfall.
例：It was one of the driest and dustiest places in Africa.那是非洲最干旱、尘土最多的地方之一。
例：...a hot, dry climate where the sun is shining all the time.终年阳光明媚的炎热干旱气候

N-SING
干燥;少雨
In the dry means in a place or at a time that is not damp, wet, or rainy.
例：Such cars, however, do grip the road well, even in the dry.不过，这种车即使在干燥路面上也表现出了良好的抓地性能。

ADJ-GRADED
(通常指河流、湖泊或水井由于天热少雨而)干涸的
If a river, lake, or well is dry, it is empty of water, usually because of hot weather and lack of rain.
例：The aquifer which had once fed the wells was pronounced dry...曾为水井供水的蓄水层正式宣告干涸了。
例：The single-engine plane landed at a dry lake in western Arizona...单引擎飞机在亚利桑那州西部的一个干涸的湖底着陆了。

ADJ
(油井)枯竭的,不再产油的
If an oil well is dry, it is no longer producing any oil.
例：To harvest oil and gas profitably from the North Sea, it must focus on the exploitation of small reserves as the big wells run dry.随着大型油井的枯竭，要想从北海的石油和天然气开采中盈利,必须着力于开采小储量的油井。

ADJ-GRADED
口渴的;干渴的
If you are dry, you need to drink something.
例：She was suddenly thirsty and dry.她突然口渴了。

ADJ-GRADED
(因紧张、生病等)口干的,喉咙干的
If your mouth or throat is dry, it has little or no saliva in it, and so feels very unpleasant, perhaps because you are tense or ill.
例：His mouth was dry, he needed a drink...他口很干,需要喝上一杯。
例：My throat was dry. I was at a loss for words.我喉咙很干,不知说什么才好。

ADJ
(咳嗽)无痰的,干咳的
A dry cough is one that does not produce any mucus.

ADJ
(眼睛)无泪的
If someone has dry eyes, there are no tears in their eyes; often used with negatives or in contexts where you are expressing surprise that they are not crying.
例：There were few dry eyes in the house when I finished...我说完后房间里没几个人不掉泪的。
例：She didn't wince and her eyes were dry. Talk about brave. She was unbelievable.她连眉头也未皱一下,也没哭。说到勇敢,她令人难以置信。

ADJ
禁酒的
If a country, state, or city is dry, it has laws or rules which forbid anyone to drink, sell, or buy alcoholic drink.
例：Gujurat has been a totally dry state for the past thirty years.在过去的 30 年里古吉拉特一直是完全禁酒的一个州。

ADJ
（被）吸干的;（被）榨干的
If you say that someone is sucking something dry or milking it dry, you are criticizing them for taking all the good things from it until there is nothing left.
例：...a shady rip-off industry that sucks its talent dry then discards it...见不得人的山寨行业吸干了它的才华后又将它抛弃
例：He's just milking the company dry.他是要把公司榨干。

ADJ-GRADED
(幽默)不露声色的,含蓄不露的,机敏的
Dry humour is very amusing, but in a subtle and clever way.
例：Fulton has retained his dry humour...富尔顿保持着他含而不露的幽默。
例：Mr Brooke is renowned for his dry wit.布鲁克先生因他不露声色的机智而闻名。

ADJ-GRADED
冷淡的;淡然的;不动感情的;冷冰冰的
If you describe a voice as dry, you mean that it is cold or dull, and does not express any emotions.
例：When he crept back to his desk, he heard the dry voice of Father Laurence.当他轻手轻脚地回到读经台时,他听到了劳伦斯神父冷冰冰的声音。

ADJ-GRADED
枯燥乏味的;干巴巴的;无趣的
If you describe something such as a book, play, or activity as dry, you mean that it is dull and uninteresting.
例：...dry, academic phrases.枯燥无味的学术词汇
例：A lot of the work was very dry and boring in Westminster.威斯敏斯特的许多工作非常枯燥无味令人厌烦。

ADJ
(面包或吐司)不涂黄油和果酱的
Dry bread or toast is plain and not covered with butter or jam.
例：For breakfast, they had dry bread and tea.他们早餐吃了无黄油的面包，还喝了茶。

ADJ
(酒)无甜味的,干的
Dry sherry or wine does not have a sweet taste.
例：...a glass of chilled, dry white wine.一杯冰镇干白葡萄酒

PHRASAL VERB
(河流、湖泊或水井)干涸
If a river, lake, or well dries up, it becomes empty of water, usually because of hot weather and a lack of rain.
例：Reservoirs are drying up and farmers have begun to leave their land...水库逐渐干涸,农民开始背井离乡。
例：The fountain is reputed never to dry up.这口泉号称从不干涸。

PHRASAL VERB
（供应）枯竭，停止，中断
If a supply of something dries up, it stops.
例：Investment could dry up and that could cause the economy to falter...投资可能会中断,而这会引起经济衰退。
例：Credit from foreign banks is drying up...外资银行的放贷金额持续萎缩，面临枯竭。

PHRASAL VERB
无话可说;突然住口
If you dry up when you are speaking, you stop in the middle of what you were saying, because you cannot think what to say next.
例：If you ask her what she's good at she will dry up after two minutes.如果你问她擅长什么，两分钟后她就没话说了。

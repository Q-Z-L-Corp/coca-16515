ADJ-GRADED
形形色色的;各式各样的
If a group or range of things is diverse, it is made up of a wide variety of things.
例：...shops selling a diverse range of gifts...出售各种礼品的商店
例：Society is now much more diverse than ever before.当今社会较之以往任何时候都要丰富多彩得多。

ADJ-GRADED
不同的;迥异的
Diverse people or things are very different from each other.
例：Jones has a much more diverse and perhaps younger audience.琼斯的观众群更为多样化而且可能更年轻。

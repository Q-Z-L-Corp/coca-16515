unseen 
英[ˌʌnˈsi:n] 美[ʌnˈsin]
 
adj.看不见的；未看见的；未事先过目的；即席的；n.即席翻译；即席翻译的文章；
 "

ADJ
未被看到的；被忽略的
If you describe something as unseen, you mean that it has not been seen for a long time.
例：...a spectacular ballroom, unseen by the public for over 30 years...公众30多年来未曾见过的豪华舞厅
例：We print a selection of previously unseen photos from the Spanish rider's early years.我们选了这位西班牙骑手早年一些不为人知的照片刊登出来。

ADJ
看不见的；无形的
You can use unseen to describe things which people cannot see.
例：For me, a performance is in front of a microphone, over the radio, to an unseen audience...对我来说，表演就是在麦克风前通过无线电波面对看不见的听众进行的。
例：There was barely time for the two boys to escape unseen.两个男孩想趁人不备偷偷溜走几乎是来不及的。

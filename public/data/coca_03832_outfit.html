outfit 
英[ˈaʊtfɪt] 美[ˈaʊtˌfɪt]
 
n.全套装备；一套服装；集体；组织；vt.装备；配置设备；供给服装；vi.得到装备；
 "

N-COUNT
全套服装
An outfit is a set of clothes.
例：She was wearing an outfit she'd bought the previous day...她穿着前一天买的那套衣服。
例：I spent lots of money on smart new outfits for work.我花大笔钱购置了上班时穿的漂亮新套装。

N-COUNT
机构;组织
You can refer to an organization as an outfit .
例：He works for a private security outfit...他在一家私人保安公司工作。
例：We are a professional outfit and we do require payment for our services.我们是一家专业机构，对所提供服务确实要收取费用。

VERB
装备；配备；供给
To outfit someone or something means to provide them with equipment for a particular purpose.
例：They outfitted him with artificial legs...他们为他安了假腿。
例：I outfitted an attic bedroom as a studio.我把阁楼卧室改装成了工作室。

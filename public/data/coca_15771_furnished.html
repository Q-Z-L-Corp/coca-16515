furnished 
英[ˈfɜ:nɪʃt] 美[ˈfɜrnɪʃt]
 
adj.家具，有家具的；v.陈设，布置( furnish的过去式和过去分词)；提供，装备；
 "

ADJ
带家具的;配有家具的
A furnished room or house is available to be rented together with the furniture in it.
例：Eleanor moved into a small furnished apartment.埃莉诺搬进了一间配有家具的小公寓里。

ADJ
(房间或房屋)配有…家具的
When you say that a room or house is furnished in a particular way, you are describing the kind or amount of furniture that it has in it.
例：We took tea by lamplight in his sparsely furnished house...在他那所少有陈设的房子里，我们坐在灯下喝着茶。
例：All 300 elegantly furnished rooms have private bath.300 个布置讲究的房间全都配有独立浴室。

steward 
英[ˈstju:əd] 美[ˈstuərd]
 
n.管家；乘务员；干事，理事；[部门，工厂]的工会代表；vi.当服务员；当管事；vt.管理；
 "

N-COUNT
(船舶、飞机或火车上的)男服务员，男乘务员
A steward is a man who works on a ship, plane, or train, looking after passengers and serving meals to them.

N-COUNT
（财产）管理员;管家
A steward is someone who has the responsibility for looking after property.
例：The Earl didn't have the money or good judgement to employ a steward to manage the place for him.这位伯爵既没钱也没有良好的判断力去雇用一名管家替自己管理这所房子。

N-COUNT
(协助组织赛马、游行及其他公共活动的)负责人，管事
A steward is a man or woman who helps to organize a race, march, or other public event.
例：The steward at the march stood his ground while the rest of the marchers decided to run.当其他的游行者决定逃跑时，游行活动的负责人坚守着阵地。

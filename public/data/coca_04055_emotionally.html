emotionally 
英[ɪ'məʊʃənəlɪ] 美[ɪˈmoʃənlɪ]
 
adv.感情上，情绪上，冲动地；
 "

ADJ
情感上的;情绪上的
Emotional means concerned with emotions and feelings.
例：I needed this man's love, and the emotional support he was giving me...我需要这个男人的爱，以及他在情感上给我的支持。
例：Victims are left with emotional problems that can last for life.受害者心中留下了可能会持续一生的情感创伤。

ADJ-GRADED
激起强烈感情的;激动人心的
An emotional situation or issue is one that causes people to have strong feelings.
例：It's a very emotional issue. How can you advocate selling the ivory from elephants?这是个很容易激起公愤的话题。你怎么能够鼓吹贩卖象牙？

ADJ-GRADED
情绪激动的;（尤指）愤怒的
If someone is or becomes emotional, they show their feelings very openly, especially because they are upset.
例：He is a very emotional man...他是个很情绪化的人。
例：I don't get as emotional as I once did.我不像以前那么冲动了。

ADV-GRADED
令人满意的;合意的
If something is happening or working nicely, it is happening or working in a satisfactory way or in the way that you want it to.
例：She has a bit of private money, so they manage quite nicely...她有一点私房钱，所以他们过得很舒心。
例：The crowds had been soaked and were now nicely drying out.这群人都浑身湿透，现在谢天谢地快晾干了。

PHRASE
成功;进展顺利
If someone or something is doing nicely, they are being successful.
例：...another hotel owner who is doing very nicely.又一个成功的酒店老板

PHRASE
很不错;很适合
If you say that something will do nicely, you mean that it is good enough for the situation.
例：A shirt and jersey and an ordinary pair of trousers will do nicely, thank you...一件衬衫和毛衣，再加一条普通的裤子就很不错，谢谢你。
例：A quick nod of approval would have done nicely.要是当时立即点头同意就好了。

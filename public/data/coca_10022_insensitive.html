insensitive 
英[ɪnˈsensətɪv] 美[ɪnˈsɛnsɪtɪv]
 
adj.感觉迟钝的；（对某事物）无感觉的；（对变化）懵然不知的；不敏感的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不敏感的;感觉迟钝的;麻木不仁的
If you describe someone as insensitive, you are criticizing them for being unaware of or unsympathetic to other people's feelings.
例：I feel my husband is very insensitive about my problem.我感觉丈夫对我的问题麻木不仁。

ADJ-GRADED
漠视的;不在乎的;毫不在意的
Someone who is insensitive to a situation or to a need does not think or care about it.
例：Women's and Latino organizations that say he is insensitive to civil rights.指责他漠视人权的女性组织与拉美裔组织

ADJ
(对体感)没有感觉的,麻木的
Someone who is insensitive to a physical sensation is unable to feel it.
例：He had become insensitive to cold.他对寒冷已经没有感觉了。

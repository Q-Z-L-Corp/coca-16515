N-UNCOUNT
（总称）色情（或淫秽）作品
Pornography refers to books, magazines, and films that are designed to cause sexual excitement by showing naked people or referring to sexual acts.
例：A nationwide campaign against pornography began in the summer.一场全国性打击淫秽作品的行动在夏季打响了。

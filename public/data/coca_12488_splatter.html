V-ERG
溅泼；洒落
If a thick wet substance splatters on something or is splattered on it, it drops or is thrown over it.
例：The rain splattered against the french windows...雨溅落在落地窗上。
例：'Sorry Edward,' I said, splattering the cloth with jam.“对不起，爱德华，”我说道，因为我把果酱洒在了桌布上。

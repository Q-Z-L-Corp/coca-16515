conceal 
英[kənˈsi:l] 美[kənˈsil]
 
vt.隐藏，隐瞒，遮住；
 "

VERB
掩盖;隐藏
If you conceal something, you cover it or hide it carefully.
例：Frances decided to conceal the machine behind a hinged panel...弗朗西斯决定把机器藏在带铰链的嵌板的后面。
例：Five people were arrested for carrying concealed weapons.5人因身上藏有武器被捕。

VERB
隐瞒（信息);掩饰(感情)
If you conceal a piece of information or a feeling, you do not let other people know about it.
例：Robert could not conceal his relief...罗伯特无法掩饰自己如释重负的心情。
例：She knew at once that he was concealing something from her.她马上知道了他有事瞒着她。

VERB
掩盖;遮住
If something conceals something else, it covers it and prevents it from being seen.
例：...a pair of carved Indian doors which conceal a built-in cupboard...嵌入式壁橱上的一对印度雕花门
例：The hat concealed her hair.帽子遮住了她的头发。

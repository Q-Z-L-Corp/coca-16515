wipe 
英[waɪp] 美[waɪp]
 
vt.擦，拭；擦去，消除；涂上；刷卡；n.擦，拭；抹布，毛巾；用力打；vi.擦，抹；打，揍；
 "

VERB
拭;擦;揩
If you wipe something, you rub its surface to remove dirt or liquid from it.
例：I'll just wipe the table...我就擦擦桌子。
例：When he had finished washing he began to wipe the basin clean...他清洗完后开始把洗脸池擦拭干净。

VERB
擦去;擦掉
If you wipe dirt or liquid from something, you remove it, for example by using a cloth or your hand.
例：Gleb wiped the sweat from his face...格莱布擦去脸上的汗水。
例：He shook his head and wiped his tears with a tissue.他摇摇头，用纸巾擦掉眼泪。

N-COUNT
（一次性）湿巾
A wipe is a small moist cloth for cleaning things and is designed to be used only once.
例：...antiseptic wipes.消毒湿巾

PHRASE
让…笑容顿失;使…兴致全无;使…丧失优势
If you say that something wipes the smile off someone's face, you mean that it suddenly spoils their enjoyment or removes an advantage that they had and that you are pleased about it.
例：Tony Holmes wiped the smile off the faces of his rivals with a solo 30-second win.托尼·福尔摩斯以30秒的领先优势获得了这一赛段的个人胜利，使对手再也笑不起来。

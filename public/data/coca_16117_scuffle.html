N-COUNT
(短暂的)扭打,混战
A scuffle is a short, disorganized fight or struggle.
例：Violent scuffles broke out between rival groups demonstrating for and against independence.支持和反对独立的两支游行队伍间爆发了激烈的冲突。

V-RECIP
扭打;混战
If people scuffle, they fight for a short time in a disorganized way.
例：Police scuffled with some of the protesters...警察与一些抗议者发生了冲突。
例：He and Hannah had been scuffling in the yard outside his house.他和汉娜一直在他房外的院子里扭打。

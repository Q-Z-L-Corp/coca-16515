insanity 
英[ɪnˈsænəti] 美[ɪnˈsænətɪ]
 
n.疯狂；精神失常；精神病；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
精神错乱;精神失常;疯狂
Insanity is the state of being insane.
例：The film is a powerful study of a woman's descent into insanity...那部电影深入剖析了一个女人堕入疯狂的过程。
例：The defence pleaded insanity, but the defendant was found guilty and sentenced.辩方以精神错乱为由进行申辩,但被告被判有罪并获刑。

N-UNCOUNT
极端的愚蠢(行为);荒唐(事)
If you describe a decision or an action as insanity, you think it is very foolish.
例：It was a period of collective insanity, of legalized murder and mayhem.那是个集体丧失理智、谋杀与暴力合法化的时期。
例：...the final financial insanity of the 1980s.20 世纪 80 年代最后的财政疯狂

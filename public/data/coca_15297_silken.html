ADJ
光洁柔软的;丝绸般的
Silken is used to describe things that are very pleasantly smooth and soft.
例：...her long silken hair.她那丝滑的长发
例：...her long, silken legs.她那光洁修长的双腿

ADJ
丝质的;丝绸(般)的
A silken garment, fabric, or rope is made of silk or a material that looks like silk.
例：...a silken nightshirt.丝绸睡衣
例：...silken cushions.丝绸垫子

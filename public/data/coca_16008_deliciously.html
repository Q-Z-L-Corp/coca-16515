deliciously 
英[dɪ'lɪʃəslɪ] 美[dɪ'lɪʃəslɪ]
 
adv.美味地，芬香地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
美味的;可口的
Food that is delicious has a very pleasant taste.
例：There's always a wide selection of delicious meals to choose from...总是有很多美味可口的饭菜可供选择。
例：Pecan nuts are delicious both raw and cooked.美洲山核桃不论生的还是熟的都很好吃。

ADJ-GRADED
美妙的;怡人的;有趣的
If you describe something as delicious, you mean that it is very pleasant.
例：There is a delicious irony in all this.这一切中包含了一种绝妙的讽刺。
例：..that delicious feeling of surprise.那种惊喜之情

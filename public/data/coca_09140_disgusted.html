ADJ-GRADED
厌恶的;憎恶的
If you are disgusted, you feel a strong sense of dislike and disapproval at something.
例：I'm disgusted with the way that he was treated...他受到如此对待让我气愤。
例：He was disgusted that a British minister could have behaved so disgracefully.他对一位英国大臣竟有如此丢人的举止感到厌恶。

stroll 
英[strəʊl] 美[stroʊl]
 
n.闲逛；漫步；vi.散步；奔波；vt.溜达；
 "

VERB
散步;溜达;闲逛
If you stroll somewhere, you walk there in a slow, relaxed way.
例：He collected some orange juice from the refrigerator and, glass in hand, strolled to the kitchen window...他从冰箱里拿了点橘子汁，然后手拿杯子溜达到厨房的窗口。
例：Afterwards, we strolled back, put the kettle on and settled down with the newspapers.然后，我们溜达回来，烧上水，又坐下看报纸。

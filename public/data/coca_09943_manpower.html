N-UNCOUNT
人力;劳动力
Workers are sometimes referred to as manpower when they are being considered as a part of the process of producing goods or providing services.
例：...the shortage of skilled manpower in the industry...该行业熟练劳动力的短缺
例：These people do not have the equipment or the manpower to cut down the trees.这些人没有设备或者人手砍伐这些树。

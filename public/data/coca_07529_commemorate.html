VERB
纪念
To commemorate an important event or person means to remember them by means of a special action, ceremony, or specially created object.
例：One room contained a gallery of paintings commemorating great moments in baseball history...有一个房间里陈列着很多纪念棒球史上伟大时刻的画作。
例：The 200th anniversary of Mozart's death is being commemorated around the world with concerts featuring his work.世界各地将举办以莫扎特的作品为主题的音乐会，以纪念他逝世200周年。

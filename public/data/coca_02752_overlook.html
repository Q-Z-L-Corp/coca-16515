overlook 
英[ˌəʊvəˈlʊk] 美[ˌoʊvərˈlʊk]
 
vt.忽视，忽略；瞭望；检查；远眺；n.从高处眺望到的景色；忽视，忽略；可眺望四周的高地；
 "

VERB
俯瞰；俯视；眺望
If a building or window overlooks a place, you can see the place clearly from the building or window.
例：Pretty and comfortable rooms overlook a flower-filled garden...漂亮舒适的房间俯视着花团锦簇的花园。
例：Jack Aldwych lived in a huge, old two-storeyed house overlooking Harbord.杰克·阿尔德维奇住在一幢可以看见哈博德的巨大的两层旧房子里。

VERB
忽略；忽视；未注意到
If you overlook a fact or problem, you do not notice it, or do not realize how important it is.
例：We overlook all sorts of warning signals about our own health.我们忽视有关自身健康的种种预警信号。
例：...a fact that we all tend to overlook.我们所有人往往视而不见的事实

VERB
宽容；宽恕
If you overlook someone's faults or bad behaviour, you forgive them and take no action.
例：...satisfying relationships that enable them to overlook each other's faults.能使他们互相包容对方缺点的舒心的关系

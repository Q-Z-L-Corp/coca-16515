culminate 
英[ˈkʌlmɪneɪt] 美[ˈkʌlməˌnet]
 
vt.& vi.达到极点；
 "

VERB
(以…)结束，告终
If you say that an activity, process, or series of events culminates in or with a particular event, you mean that event happens at the end of it.
例：They had an argument, which culminated in Tom getting drunk...他们争论了一番，最后汤姆喝醉了。
例：The celebration of the centenary will culminate with a dinner on November 20.百年庆典活动将在11月20日的宴会中宣告结束。

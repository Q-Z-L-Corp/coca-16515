ADJ
泡制的；腌制的；腌渍的
Pickled food, such as vegetables, fruit, and fish, has been kept in vinegar or salt water to preserve it.
例：...a jar of pickled fruit.一罐腌渍的水果
例：...little tins of pickled herring.小听腌鲱鱼

ADJ-GRADED
醉的；喝醉的
If you say that someone is pickled, you mean that they are drunk.

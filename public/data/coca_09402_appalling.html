ADJ-GRADED
令人震惊的；使人惊骇的；极为恶劣的
Something that is appalling is so bad or unpleasant that it shocks you.
例：They have been living under the most appalling conditions for two months.他们已经在恶劣不堪的条件下生活两个月了。

ADJ-GRADED
非常的;极其严重的
You can use appalling to emphasize that something is very great or severe.
例：I developed an appalling headache.我头痛欲裂。

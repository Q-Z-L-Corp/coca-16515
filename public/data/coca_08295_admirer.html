admirer 
英[ədˈmaɪərə(r)] 美[ədˈmaɪərər]
 
n.赞赏者；羡慕者；崇拜者；（女子的）爱慕者；
 "

N-COUNT
钦佩者；欣赏者
If you are an admirer of someone, you like and respect them or their work very much.
例：He was an admirer of her grandfather's paintings.他非常欣赏她祖父的画作。

N-COUNT
（女人的）追求者,爱慕者
A woman's admirers are the men who are attracted to her.
例：Johnny was the most persistent and most harmless of her admirers.约翰尼是追她的人中最痴心、最没有心机的一个。

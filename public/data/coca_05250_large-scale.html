ADJ-GRADED
大规模的；大范围的；大型的
A large-scale action or event happens over a very wide area or involves a lot of people or things.
例：...a large scale military operation.大规模的军事行动

ADJ-GRADED
（地图或图表）大比例尺的
A large-scale map or diagram represents a small area of land or a building or machine on a scale that is large enough for small details to be shown.
例：...a large-scale map of the county.一张大比例尺的郡地图

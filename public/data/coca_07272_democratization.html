democratization 
英[dɪˌmɒkrətaɪ'zeɪʃn] 美[dɪˌmɒkrətaɪ'zeɪʃn]
 
n.民主化；
 "

VERB
(使）(国家或制度)民主化
If a country or a system is democratized, it is made democratic.
例：...a further need to democratize the life of society as a whole...进一步从整体上加强社会生活民主化的需要
例：When he left his native country, he said he would not return until it had been fully democratised.离开祖国时，他说直到国家完全实现民主化后他才会回来。

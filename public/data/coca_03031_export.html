export 
英[ˈekspɔ:t] 美[ˈekspɔrt]
 
vt.& vi.出口，输出；vt.传播，输出（思想或活动）；
 "

VERB
出口；输出
To export products or raw materials means to sell them to another country.
例：The nation also exports beef...该国也出口牛肉。
例：They expect the antibiotic products to be exported to Southeast Asia and Africa...他们希望向东南亚和非洲出口抗生素产品。

N-COUNT
输出品;出口商品
Exports are goods which are sold to another country and sent there.
例：He did this to promote American exports...他这么做是为了推广美国出口产品。
例：Ghana's main export is cocoa.加纳的主要出口商品是可可豆。

VERB
输出;传播
To export something means to introduce it into another country or make it happen there.
例：It has exported inflation at times.它有时会输出通货膨胀。
例：...hecklers who said the deal would export jobs to Mexico.声称这项协议会把就业机会转往墨西哥的质疑者

VERB
（在计算机中）导出，输出
In computing, if you export files or information from one type of software into another type, you change their format so that they can be used in the new software.
例：Files can be exported in ASCII or PCX formats.文件可以以ASCII或者PCX格式导出。

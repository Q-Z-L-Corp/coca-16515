ADJ
不受拘束的；没有限制的
If an activity is unrestricted, you are free to do it in the way that you want, without being limited by any rules.
例：Freedom to pursue extracurricular activities is totally unrestricted...参加课外活动的自由完全不受限制。
例：The Commissioner has absolutely unrestricted access to all the files.特派员可以随意查阅所有文件。

ADJ
完整的；清晰的
If you have an unrestricted view of something, you can see it fully and clearly, because there is nothing in the way.
例：Nearly all seats have an unrestricted view.几乎在所有的座位上都可以看到完整清晰的画面。

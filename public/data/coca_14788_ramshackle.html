ramshackle 
英[ˈræmʃækl] 美[ˈræmˌʃækəl]
 
adj.要倒似的，要塌似的；摇晃的；没主意的；放荡的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（建筑）破烂不堪的，摇摇欲坠的
A ramshackle building is badly made or in bad condition, and looks as if it is likely to fall down.
例：They entered the shop, which was a curious ramshackle building.他们进了商店，那是幢年久失修的奇特建筑。

ADJ-GRADED
（系统、联盟、收集物等）草率组合成的，拼凑的
A ramshackle system, union, or collection of things has been put together without much thought and is not likely to work very well.
例：They joined with a ramshackle alliance of other rebels.他们和一个由其他反叛者草草拼凑成的同盟联起手来。
例：...the present tax system, which the opposition says is ramshackle and complicated.被反对派批评为草率且复杂的现行税收体系

hue 
英[hju:] 美[hju]
 
n.色彩，色调；外表，样子；喊叫声；信仰；
 "

N-COUNT
颜色;色调
A hue is a colour.
例：The same hue will look different in different light.同一颜色在不同的光线下看起来会有所不同。
例：...a selection of tops in natural hues and fibres.一批自然色彩和纤维的上衣

PHRASE
大声抗议;强烈抗议
If people raise a hue and cry about something, they protest angrily about it.
例：Just as the show ended, he heard a huge hue and cry outside.演出刚结束，他就听到外面愤怒的高声抗议。

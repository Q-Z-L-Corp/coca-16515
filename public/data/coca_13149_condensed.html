condensed 
英[kən'denst] 美[kənˈdɛnst]
 
v.（使）变稠或变浓，浓缩( condense的过去式和过去分词 )；（使）凝结；简说，摘要，简述；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(书、解释或信息)压缩的，精简的
A condensed book, explanation, or piece of information has been made shorter, usually by including only the most important parts.
例：The Council was merely given a condensed version of what had already been disclosed in Washington.地方议会只得到了华盛顿已披露内容的压缩版。

ADJ-GRADED
(液体)浓缩的
Condensed liquids have been made thicker by removing some of the water in them.
例：...condensed mushroom soup.蘑菇浓汤

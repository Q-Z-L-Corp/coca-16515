ADJ-GRADED
(数量或尺寸)不成比例的，不相称的
Something that is disproportionate is surprising or unreasonable in amount or size, compared with something else.
例：A disproportionate amount of time was devoted to one topic...纠缠在同一个话题上的时间太长了。
例：This sentence is totally disproportionate to the alleged offence.从所犯罪行来看，这一量刑完全不合理。

gripe 
英[graɪp] 美[ɡraɪp]
 
n.紧握，抓住；肠绞痛；抱怨；vt.绞痛；握紧；惹恼；vi.抱怨，发牢骚；肠绞痛；
 "

VERB
抱怨；发牢骚
If you say that someone is griping, you mean they are annoying you because they keep on complaining about something.
例：Why are football players griping when the average salary is half a million dollars?...足球运动员的平均薪水已达50万美金，他们为什么还要抱怨呢？
例：They were always griping about high prices.他们总是抱怨物价太高。

N-COUNT
抱怨；牢骚
A gripe is a complaint about something.
例：My only gripe is that one main course and one dessert were unavailable.我唯一不满的就是连一道主菜和一份甜点都没有。

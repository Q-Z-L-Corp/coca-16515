mushy 
英[ˈmʌʃi] 美[ˈmʌʃi, ˈmʊʃi]
 
adj.糊状的；感情脆弱的，感伤的；adv.感伤地；n.糊状物；脆弱的感情，感伤；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(蔬菜和水果)软塌塌的
Vegetables and fruit that are mushy are soft and have lost most of their shape.
例：When the fruit is mushy and cooked, remove from the heat.当水果变软煮好后，就从火上端下来。

ADJ-GRADED
感伤的;多愁善感的;多情的
If you describe someone or something as mushy, you mean that they are very sentimental.
例：Don't go getting all mushy and sentimental.不要变得过于多愁善感。

celebration 
英[ˌselɪˈbreɪʃn] 美[ˌselɪˈbreɪʃn]
 
n.庆祝；庆祝会（仪式）；颂扬；
 "

N-COUNT
庆祝活动;庆典
A celebration is a special enjoyable event that people organize because something pleasant has happened or because it is someone's birthday or anniversary.
例：I can tell you, there was a celebration in our house that night.告诉你，那天晚上我家开了个庆祝会。
例：...his eightieth birthday celebrations.他80大寿的庆祝活动

N-SING
赞扬;赞美;颂扬
The celebration of something is praise and appreciation which is given to it.
例：This was not a memorial service but a celebration of his life...这不是在开追悼会，而是在颂扬他的一生。
例：He sees the poem as a celebration of human love.他认为，这首诗是对人世间爱的赞美。

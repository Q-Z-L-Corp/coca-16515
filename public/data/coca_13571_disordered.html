disordered 
英[dɪsˈɔ:dəd] 美[dɪsˈɔrdərd]
 
adj.混乱的；杂乱的；错乱的；失调的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
混乱的;杂乱的
If you describe something as disordered, you mean it is untidy and is not neatly arranged.
例：Moretti ran a hand through his disordered red hair.莫雷蒂用手理了理他那一头凌乱的红发。
例：...a disordered heap of mossy branches.一堆杂乱的长满苔藓的树枝

ADJ-GRADED
(精神)失调的，错乱的，不正常的，有病的
Someone who is mentally disordered or who has a disordered mind is mentally ill.
例：...agencies working with mentally disordered offenders.帮助患有心理障碍的罪犯的社会机构
例：...events that crystallized into a conspiracy in one disordered mind.在精神不正常的人看来是一个阴谋的事件

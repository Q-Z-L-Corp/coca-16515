thoughtfully 
英['θɔ:tfəlɪ] 美[ˈθɔtfəlɪ]
 
adv.沉思地；深虑地；亲切地；体贴地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
沉思的;默想的;认真思考的
If you are thoughtful, you are quiet and serious because you are thinking about something.
例：Nancy, who had been thoughtful for some time, suddenly spoke...南希沉思了一会儿，突然开口说话了。
例：He was looking very thoughtful...他似乎在静静思考着什么。

ADJ-GRADED
体贴入微的;考虑周到的
If you describe someone as thoughtful, you approve of them because they remember what other people want, need, or feel, and try not to upset them.
例：...a thoughtful and caring man...体贴入微、关心他人的男人
例：Thank you. That's very thoughtful of you...谢谢你，你想的真周到。

ADJ-GRADED
(书籍、电影、讲话等)严肃的，思想性强的
If you describe something such as a book, film, or speech as thoughtful, you mean that it is serious and well thought out.
例：...a thoughtful and scholarly book.一本兼具思想性和学术性的书

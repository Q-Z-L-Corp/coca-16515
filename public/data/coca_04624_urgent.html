ADJ-GRADED
紧迫的；紧急的；紧要的
If something is urgent, it needs to be dealt with as soon as possible.
例：There is an urgent need for food and water...现在亟需食物和水。
例：He had urgent business in New York.他在纽约有急事。

ADJ-GRADED
急切的；极力主张的
If you speak in an urgent way, you show that you are anxious for people to notice something or to do something.
例：His voice was low and urgent...他的嗓音低沉而急切。
例：His mother leaned forward and spoke to him in urgent undertones.他母亲身子前倾，小声而急切地跟他说话。

twelfth 
英[twelfθ] 美[twɛlfθ]
 
n.第十二；十二分之一；adj.第十二的；第十二个的；十二分之一的；
 "

ORD
第十二
The twelfth item in a series is the one that you count as number twelve.
例：...the twelfth anniversary of the April revolution.四月革命 12 周年纪念
例：...a twelfth-century church.12 世纪的教堂

FRACTION
十二分之一
A twelfth is one of twelve equal parts of something.
例：She is entitled to a twelfth of the cash.她有权获得这笔钱的 1/12。

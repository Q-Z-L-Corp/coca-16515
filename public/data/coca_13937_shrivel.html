shrivel 
英[ˈʃrɪvl] 美[ˈʃrɪvəl]
 
vt.& vi.（因热力，严寒、干燥、年老等而）枯萎，皱缩；
 "

V-ERG
(使)枯萎;(使)干枯;(使)皱缩
When something shrivels or when something shrivels it, it becomes dryer and smaller, often with lines in its surface, as a result of losing the water it contains.
例：The plant shrivels and dies.植物干枯而死。
例：...dry weather that shrivelled this summer's crops.造成今夏作物枯萎的干旱天气

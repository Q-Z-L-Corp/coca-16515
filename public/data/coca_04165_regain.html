regain 
英[rɪˈgeɪn] 美[riˈɡen]
 
vt.复得，重回；回收；恢复（失物、失地、健康等）；n.收复，取回；
 "

VERB
恢复；失而复得；重新得到
If you regain something that you have lost, you get it back again.
例：Troops have regained control of the city...军队重新控制了该市。
例：It took him a while to regain his composure.他过了一会儿才恢复镇定。

VERB
重返
If you regain a place that you have left, you succeed in getting back there.
例：Davis went to regain his carriage.戴维斯回到他的马车上。

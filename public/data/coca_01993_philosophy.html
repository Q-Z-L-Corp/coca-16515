philosophy 
英[fəˈlɒsəfi] 美[fəˈlɑsəfi]
 
n.哲学；哲学体系，哲学思想；生活信条；哲理；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
哲学
Philosophy is the study or creation of theories about basic things such as the nature of existence, knowledge, and thought, or about how people should live.
例：He studied philosophy and psychology at Cambridge.他在剑桥大学学习哲学和心理学。
例：...traditional Chinese philosophy.中国传统哲学

N-COUNT
哲学思想；哲学体系；哲学理论
A philosophy is a particular set of ideas that a philosopher has.
例：...the philosophies of Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle.苏格拉底、柏拉图和亚里士多德的哲学思想
例：...a whole spectrum of political philosophies.完整的政治哲学体系

N-COUNT
人生哲学；生活信条
A philosophy is a particular theory that someone has about how to live or how to deal with a particular situation.
例：The best philosophy is to change your food habits to a low-sugar, high-fibre diet...最好的生活方式是改变饮食习惯，食用低糖、高纤维食品。
例：When I interviewed Shakira I felt in tune with her philosophy of life...采访莎基拉时，我觉得自己和她的人生哲学很合拍。

PRON-SING
她;（雌性动物）它
You use her to refer to a woman, girl, or female animal.
例：I went in the room and told her I had something to say to her...我走进房间，告诉她我有话要和她说。
例：Catherine could not give her the advice she most needed...凯瑟琳无法给她最亟需的建议。

PRON-SING
（书面英语中不区分性别地泛指人）他或她（一些人不喜欢这种用法，更倾向于使用 him or her 或 them）
In written English, her is sometimes used to refer to a person without saying whether that person is a man or a woman. Some people dislike this use and prefer to use 'him or her' or 'them'.
例：Talk to your baby, play games, and show her how much you enjoy her company.和你的宝宝说话，做游戏，让他（她）知道你很喜欢和他（她）在一起。

PRON-SING
（有时指国家或民族）她，它
Her is sometimes used to refer to a country or nation.

PRON-SING
（有时指汽车、机器或船）它
People sometimes use her to refer to a car, machine, or ship.
例：Kemp got out of his car. 'Just fill her up, thanks.'肯普下了车。“给它加满油，谢谢。”

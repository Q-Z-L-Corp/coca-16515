nocturnal 
英[nɒkˈtɜ:nl] 美[nɑkˈtɜrnl]
 
adj.夜的，夜间的；（动物）夜间活动的；（植物）夜间开花的；[乐]夜曲的；
 "

ADJ
夜间发生的;夜间的
Nocturnal means occurring at night.
例：...long nocturnal walks.远途夜路
例：...the immensity of the nocturnal sky.夜空的浩瀚

ADJ
(动物)夜间活动的，夜行性的
Nocturnal creatures are active mainly at night.
例：When there is a full moon, this nocturnal rodent is careful to stay in its burrow.月圆之夜，这种夜间活动的啮齿类动物会小心地呆在地洞里不出来。

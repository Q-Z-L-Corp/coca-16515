inhalation 
英[ˌɪnhə'leɪʃn] 美[ˌɪnhəˈleʃən]
 
n.吸入；吸入剂，吸入物；
 "

N-VAR
(空气等的)吸入
Inhalation is the process or act of breathing in, taking air and sometimes other substances into your lungs.
例：...a complete cycle of inhalation and exhalation...吸入与呼出的完整循环
例：They were taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation...他们因吸入大量烟尘被送往医院。

N-COUNT
吸入药;吸入剂;吸入物
An inhalation is a treatment for colds and other illnesses in which you dissolve substances in hot water and breathe in the vapour.
例：Inhalations can soothe and control the cough...吸入药剂可以缓解并抑制咳嗽。
例：Inhalations of menthol may do the trick.吸入薄荷醇或许可以起到作用。

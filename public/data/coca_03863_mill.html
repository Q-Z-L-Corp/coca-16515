mill 
英[mɪl] 美[mɪl]
 
n.磨坊；磨粉机；榨汁机；工厂；vt.研磨，粉碎；搅拌；磨，铣；轧花边（在纸币上）；vi.惊跑，乱闯；经研磨；
 "

N-COUNT
磨坊;磨粉厂
A mill is a building in which grain is crushed to make flour.

N-COUNT
磨粉机;碾磨器
A mill is a small device used for grinding something such as coffee beans or pepper into powder.
例：...a pepper mill.胡椒碾磨器

N-COUNT
(制造加工钢材、羊毛、棉花等的)工厂
A mill is a factory used for making and processing materials such as steel, wool, or cotton.
例：...a steel mill.钢铁厂
例：...a textile mill.纺织厂

VERB
(用磨粉机)碾碎，磨碎(麦子、胡椒等)
To mill something such as wheat or pepper means to grind it in a mill.
例：They mill 1,000 tonnes of flour a day in every Australian state.澳大利亚各州每天都要磨1,000吨面粉。
例：...freshly milled black pepper.刚刚磨好的黑胡椒粉

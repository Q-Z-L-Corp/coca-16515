artillery 
英[ɑ:ˈtɪləri] 美[ɑrˈtɪləri]
 
n.炮，大炮；炮兵部队；炮术；〈美俚〉防身武器；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
火炮；大炮
Artillery consists of large, powerful guns which are transported on wheels and used by an army.
例：Using tanks and heavy artillery, they seized the town.他们倚仗坦克和重型火炮占领了这个镇。
例：...the sound of artillery fire.炮火声

N-SING-COLL
炮兵（部队）
The artillery is the section of an army which is trained to use large, powerful guns.
例：At some stage he left the Artillery to command a radar unit.有段时间他离开了炮兵部队去指挥一支雷达小分队。

VERB
(尤指靠近另一人)依偎，舒适地蜷卧
If you snuggle somewhere, you settle yourself into a warm, comfortable position, especially by moving closer to another person.
例：Jane snuggled up against his shoulder...简依偎着他的肩膀。
例：I snuggled down in the big, comfortable seat.我蜷缩在舒适的大座位里。

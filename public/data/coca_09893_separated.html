separated 
英[ˈsepəreɪtɪd] 美['sepəreɪtɪd]

 "

ADJ
分居的
Someone who is separated from their wife or husband lives apart from them, but is not divorced.
例：Most single parents are either divorced or separated...大多数单身父母要么离婚了要么分居了。
例：Tristan had been separated from his wife for two years.特里斯坦和妻子已经分居两年了。

ADJ
(与家人等)分离的，不在一起的
If you are separated from someone, for example your family, you are not able to be with them.
例：The idea of being separated from him, even for a few hours, was torture...一想到要和他分开，即便是短短几小时，都感到备受煎熬。
例：They're trying their best to bring together those separated families.他们正尽最大的努力让离散的家人重聚。

ADJ-GRADED
提供有用消息(或资料)的;增长知识的;增长见闻的
Something that is informative gives you useful information.
例：The adverts are not very informative...这些广告并没有包含太多有用信息。
例：'Holidays That Don't Cost the Earth' is a lively, informative read.《绿色环保的假期》是本生动有趣、增长见闻的读物。

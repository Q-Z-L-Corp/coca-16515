item 
英[ˈaɪtəm] 美[ˈaɪtəm]
 
n.条，条款；项目；一则；一件商品（或物品）；adv.又，同上；
 "

N-COUNT
(系列物品或清单中的)一件
An item is one of a collection or list of objects.
例：The most valuable item on show will be a Picasso drawing...展览会上最贵重的展品是毕加索的一幅素描。
例：The menu includes the occasional off-beat item.菜单中包括了偶尔会有的非传统菜肴。

N-COUNT
项目;条目;条款
An item is one of a list of things for someone to do, deal with, or talk about.
例：The other item on the agenda is the tour.日程中的另一项是旅游。

N-COUNT
一篇报道(或文章);(新闻等的)一条,一则
An item is a report or article in a newspaper or magazine, or on television or radio.
例：There was an item in the paper about him.报纸上有一条关于他的报道。

N-SING
(关系亲密的)一对
If you say that two people are an item, you mean that they are having a romantic or sexual relationship.
例：She and Gino were an item.她和吉诺曾是一对。

attraction 
英[əˈtrækʃn] 美[əˈtrækʃən]
 
n.吸引；[物]引力；魅力；引人注意的东西，有趣的东西；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
爱慕;喜欢;性吸引
Attraction is a feeling of liking someone, and often of being sexually interested in them.
例：Our level of attraction to the opposite sex has more to do with our inner confidence than how we look...我们对异性的吸引程度更多的来自内在的自信，而不是长相。
例：His love for her was not just physical attraction.他对她的爱慕不只是肉体的吸引。

N-COUNT
吸引力；诱惑力
An attraction is a feature which makes something interesting or desirable.
例：...the attractions of living on the waterfront.令人神往的滨水生活

N-COUNT
有吸引力的地方（或事物）
An attraction is something that people can go to for interest or enjoyment, for example a famous building.
例：The walled city is an important tourist attraction.这座由城墙围绕的城市是个重要的旅游胜地。

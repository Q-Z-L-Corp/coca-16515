ADJ
异常大的;庞大的
If you describe an amount as whopping, you are emphasizing that it is large.
例：The Russian leader won a whopping 89.9 percent yes vote...这位俄罗斯领导人赢得了89.9%的高票支持。
例：Planned spending amounts to a whopping $31.4 billion.预计的开支高达314亿美元。

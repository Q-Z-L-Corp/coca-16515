dietary 
英['daɪətərɪ] 美[ˈdaɪɪˌtɛri]
 
adj.饮食的，规定食物的；n.规定的食物；食谱；
 "

ADJ
饮食的;有关饮食的
You can use dietary to describe anything that concerns a person's diet.
例：Dr Susan Hankinson has studied the dietary habits of more than 50,000 women...苏珊·汉金森博士对5万多名女性的饮食习惯进行了研究。
例：As with all dietary changes, reducing salt should be done gradually.像所有改变饮食结构的情况一样，减少盐的摄入量也应当采取渐进的方式。

ADJ
饮食中的;膳食中的
You can use the word dietary to describe substances such as fibre and fat that are found in food.
例：...a source of dietary fibre.膳食纤维的一种来源

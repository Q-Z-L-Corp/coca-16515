suffice 
英[səˈfaɪs] 美[səˈfaɪs]
 
vi.足够；有能力；vt.满足…的需要；使满足；
 "

VERB
足够;充足
If you say that something will suffice, you mean it will be enough to achieve a purpose or to fulfil a need.
例：A cover letter should never exceed one page; often a far shorter letter will suffice.附信不应超过一页，通常来说，一封相对非常简短的信就足够了。

PHRASE
(用于句首)无须多说，只需说…就够了
Suffice it to say or suffice to say is used at the beginning of a statement to indicate that what you are saying is obvious, or that you will only give a short explanation.
例：Suffice it to say that afterwards we never met again...简单地说，此后我们再也没有见过面。
例：Suffice to say, it was more than a couple of years ago!我只想说，那是几年前的事了！

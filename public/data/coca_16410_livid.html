ADJ-GRADED
盛怒的；暴怒的
Someone who is livid is extremely angry.
例：I am absolutely livid about it...我对此极为愤怒。
例：She is livid that I have invited Dick.她因为我请了迪克而大发雷霆。

ADJ-GRADED
青紫色的；黑红色的
Something that is livid is an unpleasant dark purple or red colour.
例：The scarred side of his face was a livid red.他结了伤疤的那侧脸青紫发红。

stomp 
英[stɒmp] 美[stɑmp]
 
n.跺脚，重踩；顿足爵士舞；v.跺脚，重踩；践踏；
 "

VERB
(常指因生气而)跺着脚走，迈着重重的步伐
If you stomp somewhere, you walk there with very heavy steps, often because you are angry.
例：He turned his back on them and stomped off up the hill...他转身不理他们，噔噔噔地爬上了山。
例：He stomped out of the room.他跺着脚走出了房间。

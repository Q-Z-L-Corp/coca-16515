sensitivity 
英[ˌsensəˈtɪvəti] 美[ˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti]
 
n.敏感，感受性；仪器等的)灵敏性；[摄]感光度；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
体恤的;体贴的;善解人意的
If you are sensitive to other people's needs, problems, or feelings, you show understanding and awareness of them.
例：The classroom teacher must be sensitive to a child's needs...班主任必须对小学生的需求体察入微。
例：He was always so sensitive and caring.他总是如此理解和关心别人。

ADJ-GRADED
易生气的;易担忧的;敏感的
If you are sensitive about something, you are easily worried and offended when people talk about it.
例：Young people are very sensitive about their appearance...年轻人对外表很在意。
例：Take it easy. Don't be so sensitive.放松点，别这么敏感。

ADJ-GRADED
(问题、话题)敏感的，棘手的，易引起纷争的，须谨慎对待的
A sensitive subject or issue needs to be dealt with carefully because it is likely to cause disagreement or make people angry or upset.
例：Employment is a very sensitive issue.就业是一个非常棘手的问题。
例：...politically sensitive matters.政治敏感事件

ADJ-GRADED
(文件、报告)机密的，须小心对待的，须妥善保管的
Sensitive documents or reports contain information that needs to be kept secret and dealt with carefully.
例：He instructed staff to shred sensitive documents.他吩咐员工将机密文件用碎纸机销毁。
例：...sensitive information which, in the wrong hands, could jeopardise the safety of British troops.落入敌人手中会危及英军安全的机要情报

ADJ-GRADED
过敏的;易受影响的;敏感的
Something that is sensitive to a physical force, substance, or treatment is easily affected by it and often harmed by it.
例：...a chemical which is sensitive to light.对光敏感的化学品
例：...gentle cosmetics for sensitive skin.适用于敏感性皮肤的温和化妆品

ADJ-GRADED
(科学仪器)灵敏的，灵敏度高的
A sensitive piece of scientific equipment is capable of measuring or recording very small changes.
例：...an extremely sensitive microscope.高灵敏度的显微镜

ADJ-GRADED
奇怪的；离奇的；费解的
If you describe something as uncanny, you mean that it is strange and difficult to explain.
例：The hero, Danny, bears an uncanny resemblance to Kirk Douglas...主人公丹尼与柯克·道格拉斯出奇地相像。
例：I had this uncanny feeling that Alice was warning me.我有一种奇怪的感觉：艾丽斯是在警告我。

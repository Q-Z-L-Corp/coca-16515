injunction 
英[ɪnˈdʒʌŋkʃn] 美[ɪnˈdʒʌŋkʃən]
 
n.命令，禁令，强制令；
 "

N-COUNT
(法院下的)强制令，禁制令
An injunction is a court order, usually one telling someone not to do something.
例：He took out a court injunction against the newspaper demanding the return of the document.他取得了法院强制令，要求报社归还那份文件。

N-COUNT
命令;指令;训谕
An injunction to do something is an order or strong request to do it.
例：We hear endless injunctions to build a sense of community among staff.我们听到上面没完没了地讲要在员工中培养一种集体归属感。

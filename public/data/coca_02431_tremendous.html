tremendous 
英[trəˈmendəs] 美[trɪˈmɛndəs]
 
adj.极大的，巨大的；可怕的，惊人的；极好的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
巨大的;极大的;大量的
You use tremendous to emphasize how strong a feeling or quality is, or how large an amount is.
例：I felt a tremendous pressure on my chest...我感到胸口堵得难受。
例：There's tremendous tension between the local population and the refugees…当地居民与难民之间关系非常紧张。

ADJ-GRADED
精彩的;出色的;极好的
You can describe someone or something as tremendous when you think they are very good or very impressive.
例：He was a tremendous person…他是个非常了不起的人。
例：I thought it was absolutely tremendous.我认为它精彩极了。

religiosity 
英[rɪˌlɪdʒiˈɒsəti] 美[rɪˌlɪdʒiˈɑsəti]
 
n.笃信宗教，虔诚；

N-UNCOUNT
(过度)虔诚;笃信宗教
If you refer to a person's religiosity, you are referring to the fact that they are religious in a way which seems exaggerated and insincere.
例：...their hypocritical religiosity.他们对宗教虚伪的虔诚

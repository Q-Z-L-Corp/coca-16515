n.（船舶、飞机的）右舷，右侧；v.向右转舵；adj.右舷的；

ADJ
(船舶的)右舷的，右侧的
In sailing, the starboard side of a ship is the right side when you are on it and facing towards the front.
例：He detected a ship moving down the starboard side of the submarine.他发现有一艘船正在潜艇的右侧航行。

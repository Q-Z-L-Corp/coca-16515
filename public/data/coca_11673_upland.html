ADJ
高地的；高原的
Upland places are situated on high land.
例：...San Marino, the tiny upland republic...圣马力诺，面积狭小的高地共和国
例：It's important that these upland farms continue to survive.这些高原农场能继续存在下去非常重要。

N-PLURAL
高地；高原；山地
Uplands are areas of high land.
例：Flooding was caused by water gushing into rivers from uplands.从山地奔流而下的水涌入河中引发了洪水。
例：...a deep valley ringed about by green uplands.绿色高地环抱的深谷

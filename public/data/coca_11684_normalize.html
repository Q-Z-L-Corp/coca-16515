V-ERG
(使)正常化;(使)变得正常
When you normalize a situation or when it normalizes, it becomes normal.
例：Meditation tends to lower or normalize blood pressure...冥想有助于降低血压或使血压恢复正常。
例：There may be some deep-seated emotional reason which has to be dealt with before your eating habits normalize.要使饮食习惯正常，你也许要先处理好一些根深蒂固的情感问题。

V-RECIP-ERG
(使)(关系)正常化
If people, groups, or governments normalize relations, or when relations normalize, they become normal or return to normal.
例：The two governments were close to normalizing relations...两国政府即将恢复正常邦交。
例：The United States says they are not prepared to join in normalizing ties with their former enemy...美国表示他们不准备加入到和宿敌恢复正常关系的行动中。

devotion 
英[dɪˈvəʊʃn] 美[dɪˈvoʊʃn]
 
n.献身，奉献；忠诚；信仰，祈祷；热爱；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
深爱;挚爱;仰慕
Devotion is great love, affection, or admiration for someone.
例：At first she was flattered by his devotion.刚开始他的爱慕让她感到荣幸。

N-UNCOUNT
献身;奉献
Devotion is commitment to a particular activity.
例：...devotion to the cause of the people and to socialism...献身于人民的事业，献身于社会主义
例：I don't mean to keep criticising his devotion to his job.我并不是要一再批评他对工作的专注。

N-UNCOUNT
（对宗教的）虔诚
Devotion is religious worship or strong religious feeling.
例：He was kneeling by his bed in an attitude of devotion.他虔诚地跪在床边。

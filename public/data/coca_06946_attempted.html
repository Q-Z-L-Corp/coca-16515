ADJ
（犯罪、不法行为）未遂的，未得手的
An attempted crime or unlawful action is an unsuccessful effort to commit the crime or action.
例：...a case of attempted murder...谋杀未遂案
例：The attempted coup took place in January.那场未遂政变发生在1月份。

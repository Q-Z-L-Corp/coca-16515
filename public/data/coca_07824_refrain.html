refrain 
英[rɪˈfreɪn] 美[rɪˈfren]
 
vt.抑制，克制；n.（诗歌的）叠句，副歌；经常重复的评价（或抱怨）；vi.忍耐，节制；
 "

VERB
克制;忍住
If you refrain from doing something, you deliberately do not do it.
例：Mrs Hardie refrained from making any comment...哈迪夫人忍住不作任何评论。
例：He appealed to all factions to refrain from violence.他呼吁所有派系避免使用暴力。

N-COUNT
（歌曲的）叠句，副歌部分
A refrain is a short, simple part of a song, which is repeated many times.
例：...a refrain from an old song.一首老歌的副歌部分

N-COUNT
反复说的话;老调
A refrain is a comment or saying that people often repeat.
例：Rosa's constant refrain is that she doesn't have a life.罗莎总是重复的一句话就是她没有像样的生活。

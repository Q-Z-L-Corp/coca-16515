opposing 
英[əˈpəʊzɪŋ] 美[əˈpoʊzɪŋ]
 
adj.反作用的；反向的；相反的；对立的；v.反对；
 "

ADJ
（观点、倾向等）对立的，截然相反的
Opposing ideas or tendencies are totally different from each other.
例：I have a friend who has the opposing view and felt that the war was immoral...我有一位朋友的看法截然不同，他认为这场战争是不道德的。
例：Water is the opposing force to fire.水火不相容。

ADJ
（群体等）对立的，对抗的
Opposing groups of people disagree about something or are in competition with one another.
例：The Georgian leader said in a radio address that he still favoured dialogue between the opposing sides...这位格鲁吉亚领导人在广播讲话中称他依然赞成对立双方进行对话。
例：The opposing team must in turn try to keep the ball in the air before hitting it back over the net.对方球员必须轮流击球，在球落地前将球击过网。

itch 
英[ɪtʃ] 美[ɪtʃ]
 
v.痒，发痒；渴望，极想；n.渴望，热望；痒；疥疮；
 "

VERB
发痒
When a part of your body itches, you have an unpleasant feeling on your skin that makes you want to scratch.
例：When someone has hayfever, the eyes and nose will stream and itch...花粉热临床表现为流泪、流涕，眼睛、鼻子发痒。
例：My skin was hard and scaly and I itched.我的皮肤又硬又粗糙,而且还很痒。

VERB
渴望;迫切想要
If you are itching to do something, you are very eager or impatient to do it.
例：I was itching to get involved and to bring my own theories into practice...我渴望参与其中，把我的理论付诸实践。
例：The general was itching for a fight.将军盼着打一仗。

cope 
英[kəʊp] 美[koʊp]
 
vi.成功地应付，对付；对抗；n.长袍；斗篷状覆盖物；墙帽；vt.笼罩；给…盖墙压顶；
 "

VERB
(成功地)处理，应付，对付
If you cope with a problem or task, you deal with it successfully.
例：It was amazing how my mother coped with bringing up three children on less than three pounds a week...我妈妈用每周不到3英镑的钱抚养大了3个孩子，简直太不可思议了。
例：The problems were an annoyance, but we managed to cope.这些问题很讨厌，但是我们都设法解决了。

VERB
应付，忍受(不愉快的局面)
If you have to cope with an unpleasant situation, you have to accept it or bear it.
例：Never before has the industry had to cope with war and recession at the same time...这个行业以前从未有过必须同时应付战争和萧条的经历。
例：She has had to cope with losing all her previous status and money.她不得不忍受彻底失去以前所拥有的地位和金钱的痛苦。

VERB
(机器、系统等)能够处理，应付
If a machine or a system can cope with something, it is large enough or complex enough to deal with it satisfactorily.
例：New blades have been designed to cope with the effects of dead insects...新的桨片设计使其可以应付昆虫尸体带来的影响。
例：The country's prisons are filled with drug-takers, and cannot cope with the numbers...这个国家的监狱里挤满了瘾君子，都容纳不下了。

N-COUNT
(基督教牧师在特殊仪式中所穿的)长袍，法衣
A cope is a long sleeveless piece of clothing worn by some Christian priests on special occasions.

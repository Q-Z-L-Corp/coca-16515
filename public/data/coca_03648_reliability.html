reliability 
英[rɪˌlaɪə'bɪlətɪ] 美[rɪˌlaɪəˈbɪlətɪ]
 
n.可靠，可信赖；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
可靠的;可信赖的
People or things that are reliable can be trusted to work well or to behave in the way that you want them to.
例：She was efficient and reliable...她办事很有效率，也很可靠。
例：Japanese cars are so reliable.日本产的汽车质量非常可靠。

ADJ-GRADED
(信息)可靠的，可信的,确凿的
Information that is reliable or that is from a reliable source is very likely to be correct.
例：There is no reliable information about civilian casualties...关于平民的伤亡还没有确凿的信息。
例：It's very difficult to give a reliable estimate...要给出可靠的估计非常困难。

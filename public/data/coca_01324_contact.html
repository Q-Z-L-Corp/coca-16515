contact 
英[ˈkɒntækt] 美[ˈkɑntækt]
 
n.接触；触点；[医]（传染病）接触人；门路；vt.使接触；与…联系；与…通讯（或通话）；vi.联系，接触；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指经常性的)联系，联络
Contact involves meeting or communicating with someone, especially regularly.
例：Opposition leaders are denying any contact with the government in Kabul...反对派领袖否认与喀布尔政府有任何联系。
例：He forbade contacts between directors and executives outside his presence.他禁止董事与管理层绕开他私下进行任何联系。

PHRASE
保持联系;有联系
If you are in contact with someone, you regularly meet them or communicate with them.
例：He was in direct contact with the kidnappers...他与绑架者有直接联系。
例：We do keep in contact.我们确实保持着联系。

VERB
联系;联络
If you contact someone, you telephone them, write to them, or go to see them in order to tell or ask them something.
例：Contact the Tourist Information Bureau for further details...详情请洽旅游信息处。
例：When she first contacted me Frances was upset.弗朗西丝第一次与我联系时很心烦意乱。

ADJ
(信息、电话号码等)供联系的，供联络的
Your contact details or number are information such as a telephone number where you can be contacted.
例：You must leave your full name and contact details when you phone.打电话时须留下全名与联络信息。

N-UNCOUNT
接触;遇见
If you come into contact with someone or something, you meet that person or thing in the course of your work or other activities.
例：Doctors I came into contact with voiced their concern...我接触到的医生都表示了他们的担心。
例：The college has brought me into contact with western ideas.上大学使我接触了西方的思想。

PHR-RECIP
与…联络;和…取得联系
If you make contact with someone, you find out where they are and talk or write to them.
例：Then, after she had become famous, he tried to make contact with her.后来，她出名之后，他曾试图与她联系。

PHR-RECIP
与…失去联系;与…断了联系
If you lose contact with someone who you have been friendly with, you no longer see them, speak to them, or write to them.
例：Though they all live nearby, I lost contact with them really quickly...虽然他们都住在附近，但我很快与他们失去了联系。
例：Mother and son lost contact when Nicholas was in his early twenties.尼古拉斯20岁出头的时候母子就失去了联系。

N-UNCOUNT
触碰;接触
When people or things are in contact, they are touching each other.
例：They compared how these organisms behaved when left in contact with different materials...他们比较了与不同材料接触时这些生物体的反应。
例：The cry occurs when air is brought into contact with the baby's larynx...婴儿的喉咙接触到空气时，就会哭出来。

N-UNCOUNT
(无线电)联络，通信
Radio contact is communication by means of radio.
例：He failed to make radio contact.他未能进行无线电联络。
例：...a technical problem reported by the pilot moments before he lost contact with the control tower.飞行员与控制塔失去联络前不久报告的技术故障

N-COUNT
熟人;有用的社会关系
A contact is someone you know in an organization or profession who helps you or gives you information.
例：Their contact in the United States Embassy was called Phil.他们在美国大使馆的熟人叫菲尔。

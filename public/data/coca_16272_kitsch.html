N-UNCOUNT
庸俗的艺术作品；迎合低级趣味的拙劣作品
You can refer to a work of art or an object as kitsch if it is showy and thought by some people to be in bad taste.
例：...a hideous ballgown verging on the kitsch...近乎俗丽的难看的舞会礼服
例：Collectors of Fifties kitsch should pop over to Brussels for the flea market.50年代庸俗艺术作品的收藏者应该到布鲁塞尔的跳蚤市场去看看。

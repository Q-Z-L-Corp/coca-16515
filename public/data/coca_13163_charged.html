ADJ-GRADED
(形势)紧张的，引起强烈感情的
If a situation is charged, it is filled with emotion and therefore very tense or exciting.
例：There was a highly charged atmosphere...气氛高度紧张。
例：A wedding is an emotionally charged situation.婚礼是个让人动情的场合。

develop 
英[dɪˈveləp] 美[dɪˈvɛləp]
 
vi.发展；生长；形成；发达 ；vt.发展；开发；研制；冲洗（胶片）；
 "

VERB
发展;成长;发育;生长
When something develops, it grows or changes over a period of time and usually becomes more advanced, complete, or severe.
例：As children develop, some of the most important things they learn have to do with their sense of self...在成长过程中，孩子学到的一些最重要的东西与他们的自我意识有关。
例：It's hard to say at this stage how the market will develop...在现阶段，很难说市场将会如何发展。

VERB
(问题或困难)出现，产生
If a problem or difficulty develops, it begins to occur.
例：A huge row has developed about the pollution emanating from a chemical plant.一家化工厂造成的污染已经引发了激烈争论。
例：...blood clots in his lungs, a problem which developed from a leg injury.由腿伤引起的他肺部的血块

VERB
(国家从贫穷的农业国向富有的工业国)发展
If you say that a country develops, you mean that it changes from being a poor agricultural country to being a rich industrial country.
例：All countries, it was predicted, would develop and develop fast.据预测，所有国家都会实现发展而且发展速度会很快。

V-ERG
（使）（企业或行业）发展；（使）扩展；（使）壮大
If you develop a business or industry, or if it develops, it becomes bigger and more successful.
例：She won a grant to develop her own business...她赢得了一笔拨款，用以扩展自己的企业。
例：Over the last few years tourism here has developed considerably.在过去几年里，这里的旅游业获得了相当大的发展。

VERB
开发(土地或地产)
To develop land or property means to make it more profitable, by building houses or factories or by improving the existing buildings.
例：Entrepreneurs developed fashionable restaurants and bars in the area.实业家在这一地区建起了许多时尚的餐馆和酒吧。
例：...the cost of acquiring or developing property.获得或开发地产的成本

VERB
养成(习惯);获得(名声);形成(信仰)
If you develop a habit, reputation, or belief, you start to have it and it then becomes stronger or more noticeable.
例：She later developed a taste for expensive nightclubs...她后来逐渐变得喜欢去消费高昂的夜总会。
例：Mr Robinson has developed the reputation of a ruthless cost-cutter.大家都知道鲁宾逊先生在降低成本上毫不手软。

V-ERG
（使）提高;（使）加强
If you develop a skill, quality, or relationship, or if it develops, it becomes better or stronger.
例：Now you have an opportunity to develop a greater understanding of each other...现在你们有一个好机会，可以大大增进对彼此的了解。
例：...weekly workshops that are designed to develop acting and theatre skills...为了提高表演和舞台技巧而设的每周一次的培训班

V-ERG
患(病);得(病)
If you develop an illness, or if it develops, you become affected by it.
例：The test should identify which smokers are most prone to develop lung cancer...这项检查应该可以确认哪些吸烟者更容易得肺癌。
例：A sharp ache developed in her back muscles.她后背的肌肉疼得厉害。

VERB
发生(故障等)
If a piece of equipment develops a fault, it starts to have the fault.
例：The aircraft made an unscheduled landing after developing an electrical fault.飞机由于发生电力故障而作了临时降落。

VERB
研制(新产品)
If someone develops a new product, they design it and produce it.
例：He claims that several countries have developed nuclear weapons secretly.他声称有几个国家已经秘密研制出了核武器。
例：...a computer system specially developed for the Coastguard service.专为海岸警卫队研发的计算机系统

V-ERG
阐释(想法、理论、故事或主题)；（使）展开；（使）完善
If you develop an idea, theory, story, or theme, or if it develops, it gradually becomes more detailed, advanced, or complex.
例：I would like to thank them for allowing me to develop their original idea...我想感谢他们允许我深入完善他们最初的想法。
例：This point is developed further at the end of this chapter...这一点在本章结尾处有进一步阐释。

VERB
冲洗(胶片);使显影
To develop photographs means to make negatives or prints from a photographic film.
例：...after developing one roll of film.冲洗了一卷胶卷后

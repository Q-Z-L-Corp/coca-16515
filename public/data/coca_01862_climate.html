 "

N-VAR
气候
The climate of a place is the general weather conditions that are typical of it.
例：...the hot and humid climate of Cyprus.塞浦路斯炎热潮湿的气候

N-COUNT
氛围；形势;局势;状况
You can use climate to refer to the general atmosphere or situation somewhere.
例：The economic climate remains uncertain.经济形势依然不确定。
例：...the existing climate of violence and intimidation...目前暴力和恐吓的氛围

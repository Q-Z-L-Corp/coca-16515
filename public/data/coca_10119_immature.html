immature

adj.不成熟的；未完成的；粗糙的；幼小；
 "

ADJ
发育未全的;不成熟的
Something or someone that is immature is not yet completely grown or fully developed.
例：The birds were in immature plumage...这些鸟儿羽翅未全。
例：She is emotionally immature.她情感上不成熟。

ADJ-GRADED
冒失的;鲁莽的;冲动的
If you describe someone as immature, you are being critical of them because they do not behave in a sensible or responsible way.
例：...grossly immature drivers who flout the rules of the road...无视交通法规、极端鲁莽的司机
例：She's just being childish and immature.她在耍小孩子脾气，真是任性。

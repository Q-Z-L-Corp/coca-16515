nakedness 
英['neɪkɪdnəs] 美['neɪkɪdnəs]

ADJ
裸露的;赤身裸体的
Someone who is naked is not wearing any clothes.
例：Her naked body was found wrapped in a sheet in a field...在田里发现了她的尸体，全身赤裸，裹在一条床单里。
例：The hot paving stones scorched my naked feet...烫人的铺路石灼烤着我的一双赤脚。

ADJ
(动物)没有皮毛的，没有羽毛的
If an animal or part of an animal is naked, it has no fur or feathers on it.
例：The nest contained eight little mice that were naked and blind.窝里有8只还没长毛、眼睛尚未睁开的小老鼠。

ADJ
赤手空拳的;无防备的
If you say that someone is naked or feels naked, you mean they are powerless or have no way of protecting themselves.
例：If the reports are accurate, the deal leaves the authorities and the President virtually naked.如果报道属实，此交易的确使当局和总统猝不及防。
例：...Bevan's appeal against going 'naked into the conference chamber'.贝文“不要毫无准备地走进会议室”的呼吁

ADJ
无遮蔽的;无覆盖的
You can describe an object as naked when it does not have its normal covering.
例：...a naked bulb dangling in a bare room...空荡荡的房间里吊着的一只没有灯罩的灯泡
例：The water was heated by a naked gas flame.水是用燃气明火加热的。

ADJ
(情感)明显的，直率的
Naked emotions are easy to recognize, because they are very strongly felt.
例：The naked hatred in the woman's face shocked me...那位女士一脸毫不掩饰的憎恨让我震惊。
例：There had been naked misery in her voice when she'd spoken about the letter.她说起那封信时，声音里流露出明显的痛苦。

ADJ
公然的;赤裸裸的
You can use naked to describe unpleasant or violent actions and behaviour which are not disguised or hidden in any way.
例：Naked aggression and an attempt to change frontiers by force could not go unchallenged.公然的侵略和企图用武力改变边界的行径是不能置之不理的。
例：...violence and the naked pursuit of power.暴力以及对权力赤裸裸的追求

PHRASE
(观察时)用肉眼，用裸眼
If you say that something cannot be seen by the naked eye, you mean that it cannot be seen without the help of equipment such as a telescope or microscope.
例：The worms cannot be seen by the naked eye...这些虫子用肉眼看不见。
例：The planet Mars will be visible to the naked eye all week...整周都可以用肉眼观察到火星。

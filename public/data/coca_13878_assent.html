assent 
英[əˈsent] 美[əˈsɛnt]
 
vi.赞成：赞成，如某建议；赞同；n.同意，赞同：；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
同意；准许；批准
If someone gives their assent to something that has been suggested, they formally agree to it.
例：He gave his assent to the proposed legislation...他批准了提请审议的法案。
例：Without their assent a political settlement cannot be reached.没有他们的同意，不可能达成政治上的和解。

VERB
同意；赞成
If you assent to something, you agree to it or agree with it.
例：I assented to the request of the American publishers to write this book...我同意了美国出版商的要求，答应写这本书。
例：'But it's good grub,' he added. 'You're right,' Pantieri assented.“但是伙食不错，”他补充道。“没错，”潘蒂埃里表示同意。

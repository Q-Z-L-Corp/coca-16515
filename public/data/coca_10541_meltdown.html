n.彻底垮台；
 "

N-VAR
(核反应堆的)堆芯熔毁
If there is meltdown in a nuclear reactor, the fuel rods start melting because of a failure in the system, and radiation starts to escape.
例：Emergency cooling systems could fail and a reactor meltdown could occur.紧急冷却系统有可能失灵，核反应堆有可能发生堆芯熔毁。

N-UNCOUNT
(公司、机构或系统的)崩溃，彻底失败
The meltdown of a company, organization, or system is its sudden and complete failure.
例：Urgent talks are going on to prevent the market going into financial meltdown during the summer.正在进行紧急会谈，以防止市场在夏季出现金融危机。

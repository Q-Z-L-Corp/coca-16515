loving 
英[ˈlʌvɪŋ] 美[ˈlʌvɪŋ]
 
adj.爱的，表示爱意的，亲爱的；adv.亲爱地；n.爱；慈爱；v.爱，热爱(love的现在分词)；喜爱；喜好；喜欢；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
充满爱的；钟爱的；关爱的
Someone who is loving feels or shows love to other people.
例：Jim was a most loving husband and father...吉姆是个非常体贴的丈夫和慈爱的父亲。
例：The children there were very loving to me.那里的孩子都很爱我。

ADJ-GRADED
（行为）爱护有加的，细心周到的
Loving actions are done with great enjoyment and care.
例：The house has been restored with loving care.此屋已被悉心修复。

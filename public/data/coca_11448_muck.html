muck 
英[mʌk] 美[mʌk]
 
n.污物，污泥；废石；湿粪；秽物；vt.施粪肥于…；弄脏；清除；

N-UNCOUNT
污物;污秽
Muck is dirt or some other unpleasant substance.
例：This congealed muck was interfering with the filter.这团凝固的污物堵塞了过滤器。

N-UNCOUNT
(动物的)粪便，排泄物
Muck is animal faeces.
例：He could smell muck and clean fresh hay.他能闻到粪臭和清新的干草味。

N-UNCOUNT
无用的东西;质量低劣的东西
If you refer to something as muck, you are emphasizing that you think it is of very bad quality.
例：He hasn't eaten anything for days. And are you surprised, when this muck is all I have to give him?...他已有好几天没吃东西了，可我只能给他这种垃圾一样的东西，你奇怪吗？
例：The script is utterly banal. It is incredible that human minds can put such muck on to paper.这个剧本是十足的平庸之作，难以想象有人会写出这样的垃圾。

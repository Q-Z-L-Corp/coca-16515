emphatically 
英[ɪm'fætɪklɪ] 美[ɪmˈfætɪklɪ]
 
adv.强调地，断然地，明显地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
强调地;加强语气地;断然地
If you say something emphatically, you say it in a forceful way which shows that you feel very strongly about what you are saying.
例：'No fast food', she said emphatically...“不能吃快餐，”她坚决地说。
例：Mr Davies has emphatically denied the charges.戴维斯先生断然否认了那些控诉。

ADV-GRADED
肯定地;显然地
You use emphatically to emphasize the statement you are making.
例：Making people feel foolish is emphatically not my strategy...让人出丑显然不是我的策略。
例：Politics is most emphatically back on the agenda.政治问题显然重新提上了日程。

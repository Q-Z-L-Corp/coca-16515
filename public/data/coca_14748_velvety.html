ADJ-GRADED
天鹅绒般的;柔软光滑的
If you describe something as velvety, you mean that it is pleasantly soft to touch and has the appearance or quality of velvet.
例：The grass grew thick and velvety...草长得又茂密又柔软。
例：I rubbed the velvety grooves inside the calf's ears.我摩挲着小牛耳朵里面柔软的凹槽。

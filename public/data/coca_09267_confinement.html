confinement 
英[kənˈfaɪnmənt] 美[kənˈfaɪnmənt]
 
n.监禁，关押；分娩，生产；限制，约束；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
监禁;关押;禁闭
Confinement is the state of being forced to stay in a prison or another place which you cannot leave.
例：She had been held in solitary confinement for four months...她被单独关押了4个月。
例：He'd obviously kept himself fit despite his years of confinement.显然，尽管监禁多年，他依旧保持着身体健康。

N-VAR
分娩;生产
A woman's confinement is the period of time just before and during which she gives birth to a child.
例：There has been a movement to support women seeking home confinement.一场支持女性在家分娩的运动一直在进行。

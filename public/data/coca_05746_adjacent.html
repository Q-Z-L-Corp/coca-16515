ADJ
邻近的；毗连的
If one thing is adjacent to another, the two things are next to each other.
例：He sat in an adjacent room and waited...他坐在隔壁房间里等候。
例：The schools were adjacent but there were separate doors.这些学校紧挨着，但各自独门独户。

furniture 
英[ˈfɜ:nɪtʃə(r)] 美[ˈfɜrnɪtʃə(r)]
 
n.家具；设备；附属品；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
家具
Furniture consists of large objects such as tables, chairs, or beds that are used in a room for sitting or lying on or for putting things on or in.
例：Each piece of furniture in their home suited the style of the house.他们家里的每件家具都和房子的风格相得益彰。

PHRASE
被认作当然的人(或事物);习以为常的人(或事物)
If you describe someone or something as part of the furniture, you are suggesting that they have been somewhere such as their place of work for such a long time that it is hard to imagine that place without them.

N-UNCOUNT
温和;中庸
If you say that someone's behaviour shows moderation, you approve of them because they act in a way that you think is reasonable and not extreme.
例：The United Nations Secretary General called on all parties to show moderation.联合国秘书长呼吁各方要表现出克制。
例：...the moderation and deep sense of responsibility which characterized their thinking.他们的思维方式中所特有的中庸之道和强烈的责任感

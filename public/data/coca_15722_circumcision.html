circumcision 
英[ˌsɜ:kəmˈsɪʒn] 美[ˌsɜrkəmˈsɪʒn]
 
n.割礼，包皮环切（术），（身心）净化；
 "

VERB
对(男孩或男子)行割礼;环割…的包皮
If a boy or man is circumcised, the loose skin at the end of his penis is cut off.
例：He had been circumcised within eight days of birth as required by Jewish law.根据犹太法律的规定，他在出生后8天内接受了割礼。

VERB
对(女孩或妇女)行割礼;为…切除部分(或全部)阴蒂
In some cultures, if a girl or woman is circumcised, her clitoris is cut or cut off.
例：An estimated number of 90 to 100 million women around the world living today have been circumcised.当今世界上，约有9,000万到1亿的女性接受过割礼。

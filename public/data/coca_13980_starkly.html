 "

ADJ-GRADED
(选择、陈述等)严峻的，令人不快的
Stark choices or statements are harsh and unpleasant.
例：UK companies face a stark choice if they want to stay competitive...英国公司如果想要保持竞争力就不得不面对严峻的选择。
例：In his celebration speech, he issued a stark warning to Washington and other Western capitals.在他的庆祝演说中，他向华盛顿和其他西方国家提出了严正警告。

ADJ-GRADED
(对比)鲜明的
If two things are in stark contrast to one another, they are very different from each other in a way that is very obvious.
例：...secret cooperation between London and Washington that was in stark contrast to official policy.与官方的政策形成鲜明对比的伦敦和华盛顿之间的秘密合作

ADJ-GRADED
光秃的;简易的;荒凉的
Something that is stark is very plain in appearance.
例：...the stark white, characterless fireplace in the drawing room.客厅里简易的、毫无特色的白色壁炉

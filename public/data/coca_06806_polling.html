N-UNCOUNT
（选举中的）投票
Polling is the act of voting in an election.
例：There has been a busy start to polling in today's local elections...今天地方选举的投票一开始就人头攒动。
例：Elections were postponed the day before polling was due to take place.在预定进行投票的前一天选举被延期。

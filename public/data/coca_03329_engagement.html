engagement 
英[ɪnˈgeɪdʒmənt] 美[ɛnˈɡedʒmənt]
 
n.订婚，婚约；约会；约定，契约；雇用；
 "

N-COUNT
约会;约定
An engagement is an arrangement that you have made to do something at a particular time.
例：He had an engagement at a restaurant in Greek Street at eight.他8点在希腊街的一个餐厅有个约会。
例：...business-related social engagements.与公事有关的社交约会

N-COUNT
婚约
An engagement is an agreement that two people have made with each other to get married.
例：I've broken off my engagement to Arthur...我已经解除了与阿瑟的婚约。
例：Announcing our engagement was a relief.宣布婚约让我们松了一口气。

N-COUNT
订婚期
You can refer to the period of time during which two people are engaged as their engagement .
例：I felt our engagement was quite an unhappy time.我觉得我们订婚期间非常不愉快。

N-VAR
交战;战斗
A military engagement is an armed conflict between two enemies.
例：The constitution prohibits them from military engagement on foreign soil.宪法禁止他们在外国采取军事行动。

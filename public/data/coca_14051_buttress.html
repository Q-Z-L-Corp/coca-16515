buttress 
英[ˈbʌtrəs] 美[ˈbʌtrɪs]
 
n.扶壁；支撑物；vt.支持，鼓励；用扶壁支撑，加固；
 "

N-COUNT
扶壁；撑墙；扶垛
Buttresses are supports, usually made of stone or brick, that support a wall.

VERB
加强（论点或制度）；支持（某人）
To buttress an argument, system, or person means to give them support and strength.
例：He sought to buttress some of his arguments with quotations from Mein Kampf...他引用《我的奋斗》中的语录来论证自己的观点。
例：The president's tough line is, however, buttressed by a democratic mandate.然而，总统推行的强硬路线受到了民主党发布的训令的支持。

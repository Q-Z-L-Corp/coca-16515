ADJ
男女同校的
A co-ed school or college is the same as a co-educational school or college.
例：He was educated at a co-ed comprehensive school.他就读于一所男女生同校的综合性学校。

N-COUNT
(男女同校的大学里的)女生
A co-ed is a female student at a co-educational college or university.
例：...two University of Florida coeds.佛罗里达大学的两名女学生

ADJ
(体育设施或体育活动)对男女都开放的，男女都可参加的
A co-ed sports facility or sporting activity is one that both males and females use or take part in at the same time.
例：You have a choice of co-ed or single-sex swimming exercise classes.可以选择男女兼收或是分班的游泳课。

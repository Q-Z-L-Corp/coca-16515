sole 
英[səʊl] 美[soʊl]
 
adj.单独的；惟一的；专有的，独占的；n.鞋底；有…底的 -soled；脚底；鳎（可食用比目鱼）；vt.给（鞋）换底；
 "

ADJ
唯一的;仅有的
The sole thing or person of a particular type is the only one of that type.
例：Their sole aim is to destabilize the Indian government.他们唯一的目的就是要动摇印度政府的统治。

ADJ
(控制权或所有权)唯一的，独占的
If you have sole charge or ownership of something, you are the only person in charge of it or who owns it.
例：Many women are left as the sole providers in families after their husband has died...许多女人在丈夫去世之后只好独自供养一家人。
例：Chief Hart had sole control over that fund.哈特主任独自掌控那笔资金。

N-COUNT
脚掌;鞋底;袜底
The sole of your foot or of a shoe or sock is the underneath surface of it.
例：...shoes with rubber soles...橡胶底的鞋子
例：He had burned the sole of his foot.他的一只脚脚底被灼伤了。

N-COUNT
鲽鱼;板鱼
A sole is a kind of flat fish that you can eat.

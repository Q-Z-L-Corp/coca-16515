glide 
英[glaɪd] 美[ɡlaɪd]
 
n.滑行，滑翔；[乐]滑音，延音；流逝，消逝；静悄悄的流水；vt.（鸟或飞机）滑翔，滑动；掠过；悄悄地走；消逝；vi.使滑行；使滑动；
 "

VERB
滑动；滑行；悄悄地走
If you glide somewhere, you move silently and in a smooth and effortless way.
例：Waiters glide between tightly packed tables bearing trays of pasta.侍者们端着一盘盘意大利面在拥挤的餐桌之间自如穿行。

VERB
滑翔
When birds or aeroplanes glide, they float on air currents.
例：Our only companion is the wandering albatross, which glides effortlessly and gracefully behind the yacht.陪伴我们的仅有那只盘旋的信天翁，它在游艇后面优雅轻盈地翱翔。

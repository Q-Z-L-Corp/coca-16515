ADJ-GRADED
滑稽的;令人发笑的
If you describe something as comical, you mean that it makes you want to laugh because it seems funny or silly.
例：Her expression is almost comical...她的表情近乎滑稽。
例：Events took a comical turn.事态出现了有趣的转折。

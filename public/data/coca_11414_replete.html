replete 
英[rɪˈpli:t] 美[rɪˈplit]
 
adj.饱食的；充满的，供应充足的；n.[昆] 贮蜜蚁；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
充满的;装满的
To be replete with something means to be full of it.
例：The Harbor was replete with boats...港口里满是船只。
例：History is replete with examples of populations out of control.历史上人口失控的例子比比皆是。

ADJ
饱的;吃饱喝足的
If you are replete, you are pleasantly full of food and drink.
例：Replete, guests can then retire to the modern conservatory for coffee.吃饱喝足后，宾客们可以到现代化的暖房里享受一杯咖啡。

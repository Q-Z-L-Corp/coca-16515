tomorrow 
英[təˈmɒrəʊ] 美[təˈmɑroʊ]
 
adv.在明天，在明日；n.明天；最近，不远的将来；
 "

ADV
在明天;在明日
You use tomorrow to refer to the day after today.
例：Bye, see you tomorrow...再见，明天见。
例：The first official results will be announced tomorrow.第一批官方结果将于明天宣布。

N-UNCOUNT
Tomorrow is also a noun.
例：What's on your agenda for tomorrow?…你明天的日程是怎么安排的？
例：Davies plays for the Barbarians in tomorrow's match against England...戴维斯将在明天对阵英格兰队的比赛中为野人队效力。

ADV
在未来;(尤指)在不久的将来
You can refer to the future, especially the near future, as tomorrow .
例：What is education going to look like tomorrow?未来的教育会是什么样子？

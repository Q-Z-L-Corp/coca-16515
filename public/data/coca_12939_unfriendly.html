ADJ-GRADED
不友好的；不友善的；有敌意的
If you describe a person, organization, or their behaviour as unfriendly, you mean that they behave towards you in an unkind or rather hostile way.
例：It is not fair for him to be permanently unfriendly to someone who has hurt him...他对伤害过自己的人永远存有敌意，这样是不对的。
例：People always complain that the big banks and big companies are unfriendly and unhelpful...人们总是抱怨大银行和大公司不友善、不愿提供帮助。

ADJ
（体制、活动、角色）参与式的
A participatory system, activity, or role involves a particular person or group of people taking part in it.
例：Fishing is said to be the most popular participatory sport in the U.K...钓鱼据说是英国最受欢迎的参与式体育活动。
例：Studies have shown that women tend to be more participatory in their management style.研究表明，女性的管理风格往往具有更强的参与式特点。

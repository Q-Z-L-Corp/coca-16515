specification 
英[ˌspesɪfɪˈkeɪʃn] 美[ˌspɛsəfɪˈkeʃən]
 
n.规格；详述；说明书；
 "

N-COUNT
规格;具体要求
A specification is a requirement which is clearly stated, for example about the necessary features in the design of something.
例：Troll's exclusive, personalized luggage is made to our own exacting specifications in heavy-duty PVC/nylon...特罗尔专用的、个性化的行李箱是按照我们自己严格的规格要求用耐用聚氯乙烯/尼龙材料定制的。
例：Legislation will require UK petrol companies to meet an EU specification for petrol.法规将要求英国的汽油公司必须达到欧盟对汽油的规格要求。

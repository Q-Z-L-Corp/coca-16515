ADJ-GRADED
依赖的;依靠的
A person or thing that is reliant on something needs it and often cannot live or work without it.
例：These people are not wholly reliant on Western charity...这些人并非完全依赖西方慈善团体。
例：Lithuania is heavily reliant on Moscow for almost all its oil.立陶宛的石油供应几乎完全依赖莫斯科。

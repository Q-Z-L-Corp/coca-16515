ADV
在别处;去别处
Elsewhere means in other places or to another place.
例：Almost 80 percent of the state's residents were born elsewhere...该州居民中几乎有80%出生在异地。
例：They were living rather well, in comparison with people elsewhere in the world...和世界上其他地方的人相比，他们生活得相当好。

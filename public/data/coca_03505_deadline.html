N-COUNT
最后期限;截止时间
A deadline is a time or date before which a particular task must be finished or a particular thing must be done.
例：We were not able to meet the deadline because of manufacturing delays...因为制造方面的延误，我们没能赶上最后期限。
例：The deadline for submissions to the competition will be Easter 1994...递交参赛申请的截止时间为 1994 年的复活节。

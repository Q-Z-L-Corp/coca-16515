N-UNCOUNT
（作为文学形式的）诗，诗歌，韵文
Poems, considered as a form of literature, are referred to as poetry .
例：...Russian poetry...俄罗斯诗歌
例：Lawrence Durrell wrote a great deal of poetry...劳伦斯·达雷尔创作了大量的诗歌。

N-UNCOUNT
诗的意境；诗一般美的事物
You can describe something very beautiful as poetry .
例：His music is purer poetry than a poem in words.他的音乐比真正的诗歌更具诗意。

whenever

 "

CONJ-SUBORD
无论什么时候;每当
You use whenever to refer to any time or every time that something happens or is true.
例：She always called at the vicarage whenever she was in the area...只要她在那一带，她总会去拜访教区牧师。
例：You can have my cottage whenever you like...只要你愿意，随时都可以去我的乡间小屋住。

CONJ-SUBORD
不知什么时候
You use whenever to refer to a time that you do not know or are not sure about.
例：He married Miss Vancouver in 1963, or whenever it was.他好像在1963年还是什么时候娶了当时的温哥华小姐。

N-UNCOUNT
打猎;狩猎;（野兽的）猎食
Hunting is the chasing and killing of wild animals by people or other animals, for food or as a sport.
例：Hunting is one of Italy's most popular sports...狩猎是意大利最受欢迎的体育运动之一。
例：Deer hunting was banned in Scotland in 1959.猎鹿于1959年在苏格兰被禁止。

N-UNCOUNT
搜寻;寻找
Hunting is the activity of searching for a particular thing.
例：Jobclub can help you with job hunting.失业救助所可以为您在求职方面提供帮助。

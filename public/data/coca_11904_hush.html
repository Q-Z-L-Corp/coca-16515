hush 
英[hʌʃ] 美[hʌʃ]
 
vt.& vi.（使）安静下来；n.安静，寂静；vi.安静，别说话，别叫喊；
 "

CONVENTION
（用于要求或命令别人安静）嘘
You say 'Hush!' to someone when you are asking or telling them to be quiet.
例：Hush, my love, it's all right.嘘，宝贝，没事了。

V-ERG
（使）安静下来;（使）停止说话
If you hush someone or if they hush, they stop speaking or making a noise.
例：She tried to hush her noisy father...她试图让吵吵嚷嚷的父亲安静下来。
例：I had to box Max's ears to get him to hush.我不得不打了马克斯一耳光让他闭嘴。

N-SING
寂静;宁静;（突然出现的）安静
You say there is a hush in a place when everything is quiet and peaceful, or suddenly becomes quiet.
例：A hush fell over the crowd and I knew something terrible had happened.人群突然一片肃静，我知道一定发生了什么可怕的事。

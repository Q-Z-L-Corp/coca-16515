polemic 
英[pəˈlemɪk] 美[pəˈlɛmɪk]
 
n.争论，辩论；辩论者，争论者；adj.辩论的，好争论的；
 "

N-VAR
（书面或口头的）争辩，辩论，论战
A polemic is a very strong written or spoken attack on, or defence of, a particular belief or opinion.
例：...a polemic against the danger of secret societies...抨击秘密社团危险性的论辩
例：The book is both a history and a passionate polemic for tolerance.这本书既是一段历史，也是一篇主张宽容的激情论辩。

N-UNCOUNT
争辩术；辩论法
Polemics is the skill or practice of arguing very strongly for or against a belief or opinion.
例：He enjoys polemics, persuasion, and controversy.他喜欢辩论、说服和争议。

adj.不能想像的，不可思议的，难以置信的；〈口〉难于相信的，惊人的；

ADJ-GRADED
不能想象的;不可思议的
If you describe something as inconceivable, you think it is very unlikely to happen or be true.
例：It was inconceivable to me that Toby could have been my attacker...真想不到托比竟会是袭击我的人。
例：Until now, a devaluation of the dollar seemed inconceivable.直到现在,美元的贬值似乎都无法想象。

valuable 
英[ˈvæljuəbl] 美[ˈvæljuəbəl, ˈvæljə-]
 
adj.贵重的，宝贵的；有价值的；可评估的；n.贵重物品，财宝；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有价值的;有益的;有用的
If you describe something or someone as valuable, you mean that they are very useful and helpful
例：Many of our teachers also have valuable academic links with Heidelberg University...我们的很多老师也和海德堡大学保持着有益的学术联系。
例：If you decide to do you own make-up, here are a few valuable tips that will help you look your best...如果你决定自己化妆，这里有一些有用的小窍门，可以帮助你呈现最美的一面。

ADJ-GRADED
贵重的;值钱的
Valuable objects are objects which are worth a lot of money.
例：Just because a camera is old does not mean it is valuable.古旧的相机不一定就值钱。
例：...valuable books.贵重书籍

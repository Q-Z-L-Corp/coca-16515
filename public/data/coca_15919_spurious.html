spurious 
英[ˈspjʊəriəs] 美[ˈspjʊriəs]
 
adj.假的，伪造的；欺骗性的，谬误的；私生的；赝；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
形似而实非的;虚假的;伪造的
Something that is spurious seems to be genuine, but is false.
例：He was arrested in 1979 on spurious corruption charges...他于1979年因被诬告贪污而被捕。
例：Quite a lot of allegations of misjustice are spurious.有关不公正的指控有不少是虚假的。

ADJ-GRADED
(论点、推理)不正确的，站不住脚的
A spurious argument or way of reasoning is incorrect, and so the conclusion is probably incorrect.
例：...a spurious framework for analysis...不正确的分析框架
例：The justification of this chart is entirely spurious.这张图表的依据完全站不住脚。

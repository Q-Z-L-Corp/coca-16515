disposable 
英[dɪˈspəʊzəbl] 美[dɪˈspoʊzəbl]
 
adj.一次性的，可任意处理的；用后就抛弃的；免洗的；可供使用的；n.〈美口〉使用后随即抛掉的东西（尤指容器等）；
 "

ADJ
用后即可丢弃的;一次性的
A disposable product is designed to be thrown away after it has been used.
例：...disposable nappies suitable for babies up to 8lb...适合8磅以下婴儿使用的一次性尿布
例：He shaved himself with a disposable razor.他用一次性刀片刮脸。

ADJ
(收入)可自由支配的、扣除税款后余下的
Your disposable income is the amount of income you have left after you have paid income tax and social security charges.
例：Gerald had little disposable income...杰拉尔德的可支配收入很少。
例：The nation's disposable earnings in 1987 amounted to £86 billion.1987 年国民可自由支配收入达到了860亿英镑。

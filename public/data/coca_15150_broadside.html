broadside

n.<船>（吃水线以上的）舷侧；（书面或口头的）猛烈抨击；全部舷侧炮；宽阔面；adv.侧对着；侧向地；漫无目标地；胡乱地；vt.<美>撞上（车）的一侧；
 "

N-COUNT
（书面或口头的）猛烈抨击
A broadside is a strong written or spoken attack on a person or institution.
例：The Social Democratic leader launched a broadside against both monetary and political union.这位社会民主党领袖对金钱和政治联盟进行了猛烈抨击。

ADV
（船只）以舷侧对着
If a ship is broadside to something, it has its longest side facing in the direction of that thing.
例：The ship was moored broadside to the pier.这艘船横泊在码头旁。

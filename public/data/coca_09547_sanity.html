sanity 
英[ˈsænəti] 美[ˈsænɪti]
 
n.神志正常；心智健康；头脑清楚；通情达理；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
心智健全;精神正常
A person's sanity is their ability to think and behave normally and reasonably.
例：He and his wife finally had to move from their apartment just to preserve their sanity.为了不致发疯，他和妻子最终只得从公寓中搬出。

N-UNCOUNT
清醒;明智;通情达理
If there is sanity in a situation or activity, there is a purpose and a regular pattern, rather than confusion and worry.
例：Rafsanjani has been considering various ways of introducing some sanity into the currency market.拉夫桑贾尼一直考虑通过各种方式使货币市场趋于理性。

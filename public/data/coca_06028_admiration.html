n.钦佩，赞美；引人赞美的人或物；怀着对…的赞美；

N-UNCOUNT
钦佩；羡慕；赞赏
Admiration is a feeling of great liking and respect for a person or thing.
例：I have always had the greatest admiration for him...我始终对他最为钦佩。
例：Meg's eyes widened in admiration.梅格羡慕地睁大了双眼。

drooping 
英['dru:pɪŋ] 美['drupɪŋ]
 
adj.下垂的，无力的；v.弯曲或下垂，发蔫( droop的现在分词 )；
 "

VERB
低垂;下垂;垂落
If something droops, it hangs or leans downwards with no strength or firmness.
例：Crook's eyelids drooped and he yawned...克鲁克的眼皮耷拉下来,打了个哈欠。
例：Pale wilting roses drooped from a blue vase.泛白凋零的玫瑰从蓝色的花瓶里耷拉下来。

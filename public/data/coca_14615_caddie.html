caddie 
英['kædɪ] 美[ˈkædi]
 
n.球童；<英>杂役；（运物用的）有轮手推车；v.当球童，=caddy；
 "

N-COUNT
(为高尔夫球手背球棒等器具的)球童
In golf, a caddie is a person who carries golf clubs and other equipment for a player.

VERB
(为高尔夫球手)当球童
If you caddie for a golfer, you act as their caddie.
例：Lili caddied for her son.莉莉给儿子当球童。

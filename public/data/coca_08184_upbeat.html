ADJ-GRADED
达观的；乐观的；充满希望的
If people or their opinions are upbeat, they are cheerful and hopeful about a situation.
例：The Defense Secretary gave an upbeat assessment of the war so far...国防部长对目前的战事给予了乐观的评价。
例：Neil's colleagues say he was actually in a joking, upbeat mood...内尔的同事们说他实际上在说笑逗乐，心情不错。

parking 
英[ˈpɑ:kɪŋ] 美[ˈpɑrkɪŋ]
 
n.（车辆等的）停放；停车场所，停车位；v.停车(park的ing形式)；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
停车；泊车
Parking is the action of moving a vehicle into a place in a car park or by the side of the road where it can be left.
例：In many towns parking is allowed only on one side of the street...很多小镇只允许将车停在街道的一侧。
例：I knew I'd never find a parking space in the Square.我知道在广场上肯定找不到停车位。

N-UNCOUNT
停车位
Parking is space for parking a vehicle in.
例：Cars allowed, but parking is limited.车辆准许进入，但是车位有限。

orientation 
英[ˌɔ:riənˈteɪʃn] 美[ˌɔriɛnˈteʃən, -ən-, ˌor-]
 
n.方向，定位，取向，排列方向；任职培训；（外交等的）方针[态度]的确定；环境判定；
 "

N-VAR
（组织、国家等的）方向，目标，定位
If you talk about the orientation of an organization or country, you are talking about the kinds of aims and interests it has.
例：...a marketing orientation...营销定位
例：To a society which has lost its orientation he has much to offer...对于一个迷失了方向的社会，他能做的有很多。

N-VAR
（某人在信仰或偏好上基本的）态度，取向
Someone's orientation is their basic beliefs or preferences.
例：...legislation that would have made discrimination on the basis of sexual orientation illegal.本来会将性取向歧视定为非法的法规

N-UNCOUNT
（岗前、学前、课前等的）情况介绍，培训，训练
Orientation is basic information or training that is given to people starting a new job, school, or course.
例：They give their new employees a day or two of perfunctory orientation.他们对新员工进行了一两天例行公事的培训。
例：...a one-day orientation session.为期一天的迎新会

N-COUNT
（建筑物等的）朝向
The orientation of a structure or object is the direction it faces.
例：Farnese had the orientation of the church changed so that the front would face a square.法尔内塞改变了教堂的朝向，这样它的正面就可以对着广场。

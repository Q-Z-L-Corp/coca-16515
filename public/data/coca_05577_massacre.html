massacre 
英[ˈmæsəkə(r)] 美[ˈmæsəkɚ]
 
n.大屠杀，残杀；（牲畜的）成批屠宰；vt.屠杀，残杀；损害，毁坏；<口>彻底击败；<口>惨败；
 "

N-VAR
大屠杀;大残杀
A massacre is the killing of a large number of people at the same time in a violent and cruel way.
例：Maria lost her 62-year-old mother in the massacre.在大屠杀中玛丽亚失去了62岁的母亲。
例：...reports of massacre, torture and starvation.关于屠杀、拷打与挨饿的报道

VERB
大规模屠杀;大规模残杀
If people are massacred, a large number of them are attacked and killed in a violent and cruel way.
例：300 civilians are believed to have been massacred by the rebels...据信有300位平民被叛乱分子残杀。
例：Troops indiscriminately massacred the defenceless population.军队肆无忌惮地屠杀手无寸铁的民众。

协定；契约；盟约；条约
A pact is a formal agreement between two or more people, organizations, or governments to do a particular thing or to help each other.
例：Last month he signed a new non-aggression pact with Germany...上个月，他与德国签订了新的互不侵犯条约。
例：The other two opposition parties cannot agree on an electoral pact between themselves.另外两个反对党之间无法就选举达成协议。

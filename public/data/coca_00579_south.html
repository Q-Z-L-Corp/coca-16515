south 
英[saʊθ] 美[saʊθ]
 
n.南方；南部；美国南方各州；（南半球的）发展中国家；adj.南的；（风）来自南方的；南风的；南方吹来的；adv.向南方，在南方；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
南;南方
The south is the direction which is on your right when you are looking towards the direction where the sun rises.
例：The town lies ten miles to the south of here...那个小镇位于这里以南 10 英里处。
例：All around him, from east to west, north to south, the stars glittered in the heavens.在他的四周，从东到西，从北向南，苍穹里到处繁星闪闪。

N-SING
(某个地方、国家、区域的)南部
The south of a place, country, or region is the part which is in the south.
例：...holidays in the south of France.在法国南部度过的假日

ADV
向南方;朝南
If you go south, you travel towards the south.
例：We did an extremely fast U-turn and shot south up the Boulevard St. Michel...我们飞快地来了个 180 度的转弯，然后沿着圣米歇尔大道向南疾驰而去。
例：He went south to climb Taishan, a mountain sacred to the Chinese.他南下去爬泰山了，那是中国人心目中一座神圣的山。

ADV
位于…以南;在…南边
Something that is south of a place is positioned to the south of it.
例：They now own and operate a farm 50 miles south of Rochester...他们现在拥有并经营着一家位于罗切斯特以南 50 英里处的农场。
例：I was living in a house just south of Market Street.我就住在市场街南边的一座房子里。

ADJ
南面的;南部的;南方的
The south edge, corner, or part of a place or country is the part which is towards the south.
例：...the south coast of Alderney.奥尔德尼岛的南部海岸

ADJ
(用于国家、州或地区等的名称中)南部的
'South' is used in the names of some countries, states, and regions in the south of a larger area.
例：Next week the President will visit five South American countries in six days.总统将在下周的 6 天时间内访问南美洲的 5 个国家。
例：...the states of Mississippi and South Carolina.密西西比州与南卡罗来纳州

ADJ
(风)来自南方的
A south wind is a wind that blows from the south.

N-SING
南方国家(指世界上较贫穷、欠发达的国家)
The South is used to refer to the poorer, less developed countries of the world.
例：The debate will pit the industrial North against developing countries in the South.这次辩论会将形成北方工业国家与南方发展中国家针锋相对的局面。

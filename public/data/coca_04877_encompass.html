encompass 
英[ɪnˈkʌmpəs] 美[ɛnˈkʌmpəs]
 
vt.围绕，包围；包含或包括某事物；完成；
 "

VERB
包括;包含
If something encompasses particular things, it includes them.
例：...the extra services, which start next September and encompass a wide range of special interests...将从明年9月开始并涵盖各种特殊需求的额外服务
例：His repertoire encompassed everything from Bach to Schoenberg.他能演奏从巴赫到勋伯格的所有作品。

VERB
包围;围绕;覆盖
To encompass a place means to completely surround or cover it.
例：Encompassing over a million square miles, this remote and mountainous domain presides over the rest of Asia...这片偏远多山的疆土位于亚洲大陆，占地超过100万平方英里。
例：The map shows the rest of the western region, encompassing nine states.地图上显示了西部地区的其他部分，包括9个州。

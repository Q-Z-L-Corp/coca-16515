ADJ
无止境的；无限的
If you describe something as boundless, you mean that there seems to be no end or limit to it.
例：The work demanded boundless energy and theatrical imagination...这种工作需要具备无限的精力和夸张的想象力。
例：His reforming zeal was boundless.他对于改革热情无限。

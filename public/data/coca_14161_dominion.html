dominion 
英[dəˈmɪniən] 美[dəˈmɪnjən]
 
n.统治权；领土，疆土；版图；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
控制;统治(权);支配(权)
Dominion is control or authority.
例：They truly believe they have dominion over us.他们真的以为他们对我们有统治权。

N-COUNT
领土;版图;领地
A dominion is an area of land that is controlled by a ruler.
例：The Republic is a dominion of the Brazilian people.这个共和国是巴西人民的领土。

N-COUNT
(旧时的)英联邦自治领
The Dominions were the nations which in the past were part of the British Empire but which had their own government.

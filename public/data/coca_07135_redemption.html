redemption 
英[rɪˈdempʃn] 美[rɪˈdɛmpʃən]
 
n.赎回；偿还；补救；
 "

N-VAR
赎回;偿还;拯救;救赎
Redemption is the act of redeeming something or of being redeemed by something.
例：He craves redemption for his sins.他渴望赎清自己的罪孽。
例：...redemption of the loan.偿付贷款

PHRASE
无可挽救的；不可救药的
If you say that someone or something is beyond redemption, you mean that they are so bad it is unlikely that anything can be done to improve them.
例：No man is beyond redemption...没有人是不可救药的。
例：I have no doubt that we are polluting the environment beyond redemption.我丝毫都不怀疑我们对环境的污染是不可挽回的。

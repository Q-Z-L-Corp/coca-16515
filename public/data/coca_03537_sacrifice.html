VERB
牺牲;舍弃;献出
If you sacrifice something that is valuable or important, you give it up, usually to obtain something else for yourself or for other people.
例：She sacrificed family life to her career...她为事业舍弃了家庭。
例：Her husband's pride was a small thing to sacrifice for their children's security...为了孩子们的安全，舍弃她丈夫的尊严不算什么。

VERB
献祭;以…为祭品
To sacrifice an animal or person means to kill them in a special religious ceremony as an offering to a god.
例：The priest sacrificed a chicken...祭司用一只鸡作祭品。
例：Two white bulls were sacrificed and a feast was held.献祭了两头白牛，并举行了盛宴。

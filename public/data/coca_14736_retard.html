retard 
英[rɪˈtɑ:d] 美[rɪˈtɑrd]
 
vt.使减速，妨碍，阻止，推迟；vi.减慢，受到阻滞；n.减速，阻滞，延迟；
 "

VERB
延迟;减缓;妨碍
If something retards a process, or the development of something, it makes it happen more slowly.
例：Continuing violence will retard negotiations over the country's future.持续不断的暴力活动会阻碍关系到国家未来的谈判的进行。

N-COUNT
笨蛋;傻子
If you describe someone as a retard, you mean that they have not developed normally, either mentally or socially.
例：What the hell do I want with an emotional retard?和一个感情用事的傻瓜在一起，我究竟是图什么？

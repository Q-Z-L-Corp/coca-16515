horrifying

adj.令人恐惧的，使人惊骇的；v.使震惊，使感到恐怖( horrify的现在分词 )；

VERB
使感到震惊;使毛骨悚然;使产生反感
If someone is horrified, they feel shocked or disgusted, usually because of something that they have seen or heard.
例：His family were horrified by the change.他的家人对这一变化感到震惊。
例：...a crime trend that will horrify all parents.会让所有父母感到毛骨悚然的犯罪趋势

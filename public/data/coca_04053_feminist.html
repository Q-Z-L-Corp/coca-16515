N-COUNT
女权主义者;男女平等主义者
A feminist is a person who believes in and supports feminism.
例：Only 16 per cent of young women in a 1990 survey considered themselves feminists.在 1990 年的一项调查中，只有 16％ 的年轻女性认为自己是女权主义者。
例：...a daunting feminist academic.一位令人望而生畏的女性主义研究者

ADJ
女权主义的;与女权主义相关的
Feminist groups, ideas, and activities are involved in feminism.
例：...the concerns addressed by the feminist movement.女权运动致力解决的问题

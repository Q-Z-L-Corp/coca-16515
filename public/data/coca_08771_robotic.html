robotic 
英[rəʊˈbɒtɪk] 美[roʊˈbɑtɪk]
 
adj.机器人的；自动的；像机器人似的；呆板的；
 "

ADJ
自动操作的;机械的
Robotic equipment can perform certain tasks automatically.
例：Astronaut Pierre Thuot tried to latch the 15-foot robotic arm onto the satellite.宇航员皮埃尔·索特想要把这个长15英尺的机械手安到人造卫星上。

ADJ-GRADED
像机器人的;机械呆板的
If you describe someone as robotic, you mean that they speak or move in a stiff and mechanical way like a robot.
例：There is something a little robotic about him.他有点呆板。

ADJ
为公众所接受的；公认的
Accepted ideas are agreed by most people to be correct or reasonable.
例：There is no generally accepted definition of life...关于生活的定义不一而足。
例：It is accepted wisdom that science has been partly responsible for the decline of religion.人们普遍认为，科学的兴起是宗教衰落的部分原因。

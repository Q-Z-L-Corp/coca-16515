ADJ
帝国的;皇帝的
Imperial is used to refer to things or people that are or were connected with an empire.
例：...the Imperial Palace in Tokyo...东京的皇宫
例：They executed Russia's imperial family in 1918.他们 1918 年处死了俄国沙皇皇族。

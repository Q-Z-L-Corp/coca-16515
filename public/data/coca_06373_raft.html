raft 
英[rɑ:ft] 美[ræft]
 
n.木筏；橡皮艇；[动]（昆虫的）卵筏；〈美口〉大量；vt.筏运；制成筏；使用筏子；vi.乘筏；
 "

N-COUNT
木排；木筏；筏子
A raft is a floating platform made from large pieces of wood or other materials tied together.
例：...a river trip on bamboo rafts through dense rainforest.坐竹筏穿过茂密雨林的河上之旅

N-COUNT
充气浮垫；救生筏
A raft is a small rubber or plastic boat that you blow air into to make it float.
例：The crew spent two days and nights in their raft.船员在救生筏上呆了两天两夜。

N-COUNT
大堆；大量
A raft of people or things is a lot of them.
例：He has surrounded himself with a raft of advisers who are very radical...他周围聚集了一大群偏激的顾问。
例：This is likely to revive consumer spending and a whole raft of consumer industries.这可能会带动消费性支出和一大批消费工业的复苏。

pathetic 
英[pəˈθetɪk] 美[pəˈθɛtɪk]
 
adj.令人同情的，可怜的；又笨又可怜的，无价值的；荡气回肠；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
可怜的；令人怜悯的；悲惨的
If you describe a person or animal as pathetic, you mean that they are sad and weak or helpless, and they make you feel very sorry for them.
例：...a pathetic little dog with a curly tail...可怜的卷尾巴小狗
例：The small group of onlookers presented a pathetic sight...这一小群旁观者看起来真是让人悲哀。

ADJ-GRADED
差劲的；软弱的；令人生厌的
If you describe someone or something as pathetic, you mean that they make you feel impatient or angry, often because they are weak or not very good.
例：What pathetic excuses...多差劲的借口啊！
例：It's a pound for a small glass of wine, which is pathetic.一小杯葡萄酒要1英镑，真差劲。

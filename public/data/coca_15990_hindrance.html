hindrance 
英[ˈhɪndrəns] 美[ˈhɪndrəns]
 
n.妨害，障碍；障碍物；起妨碍作用的人；
 "

N-COUNT
妨碍者;障碍物
A hindrance is a person or thing that makes it more difficult for you to do something.
例：The higher rates have been a hindrance to economic recovery...高利率已经成为经济复苏的障碍。
例：You would be more of a hindrance than a help.你帮不上忙，反倒会碍事。

N-UNCOUNT
阻碍;妨碍
Hindrance is the act of hindering someone or something.
例：They boarded their flight to Paris without hindrance.他们顺利地登上了飞往巴黎的航班。

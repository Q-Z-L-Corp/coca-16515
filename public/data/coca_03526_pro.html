pro 
英[prəʊ] 美[proʊ]
 
n.赞成者；赞成的意见；赞成票，投赞成票者；adv.赞成地；肯定地；
 "

N-COUNT
职业人员；专业人员
A pro is a professional.
例：In the professional theater, there is a tremendous need to prove that you're a pro...在专业戏剧界，非常有必要证明你是专业演员。
例：I have enjoyed playing with some of the top pros from Europe and America.我很高兴能和来自欧美的一些顶级职业运动员打比赛。

ADJ
（运动员、体育运动）职业的，专业的
A pro player is a professional sportsman or woman. You can also use pro to refer to sports that are played by professional sportsmen or women.
例：...a former college and pro basketball player...以前打过大学赛和职业赛的篮球运动员
例：Corsin played pro football for nine years.科尔辛踢了9年的职业足球赛。

PREP
赞成；支持
If you are pro a particular course of action or belief, you agree with it or support it.
例：I'm one of the few that's very pro performance-related pay.我是少数极力赞成绩效工资的人之一。

PHRASE
利弊；优缺点
The pros and cons of something are its advantages and disadvantages, which you consider carefully so that you can make a sensible decision.
例：They sat for hours debating the pros and cons of setting up their own firm...他们在一起坐了数小时，讨论自己创办公司的利弊。
例：Motherhood has both its pros and cons.当妈妈既有好的一面，也有不好的一面。

N-VAR
（某人所掌握的）词汇,词汇量
Your vocabulary is the total number of words you know in a particular language.
例：His speech is immature, his vocabulary limited...他说话很幼稚，使用的词汇有限。
例：We read to improve our vocabularies.我们阅读以便扩大词汇量。

N-SING
(某一语言的）词汇，词汇量
The vocabulary of a language is all the words in it.
例：...a new word in the German vocabulary.德语词汇中的一个新词

N-VAR
专业词汇
The vocabulary of a subject is the group of words that are typically used when discussing it.
例：...the vocabulary of natural science.自然科学词汇

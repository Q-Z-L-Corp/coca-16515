ADJ
(尤指为获取新信息)观察的，观测的
Observational means relating to the watching of people or things, especially in order to learn something new.
例：...observational humour...依靠观察发掘的幽默
例：The observational work is carried out on a range of telescopes.观测工作借助于各种望远镜。

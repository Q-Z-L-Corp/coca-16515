 "

N-VAR
装饰物
An adornment is something that is used to make a person or thing more beautiful.
例：It was a building without any adornment or decoration.这是一幢没有任何装潢或粉饰的大楼。

N-UNCOUNT
装饰；装扮
Adornment is the process of making something more beautiful by adding something to it.
例：Cosmetics are used for adornment.化妆品是用来打扮的。

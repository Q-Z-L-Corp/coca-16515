N-UNCOUNT
同谋;串通
Complicity is involvement with other people in an illegal activity or plan.
例：Recently a number of policemen were sentenced to death for their complicity in the murder...最近，几名警察因串谋杀人被判处死刑。
例：He is accused of complicity with the leader of the coup, former Colonel Gregorio Honasan.他被控与政变首领——前上校格雷戈里奥·霍纳桑——串通一气。

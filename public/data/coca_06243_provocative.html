provocative 
英[prəˈvɒkətɪv] 美[prəˈvɑkətɪv]
 
adj.挑衅的；刺激性的；n.刺激物；兴奋剂；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
挑衅的；煽动的；激怒的；刺激的
If you describe something as provocative, you mean that it is intended to make people react angrily or argue against it.
例：He has made a string of outspoken and sometimes provocative speeches in recent years...他近些年发表了一连串直言不讳、有时带煽动性的演说。
例：His behavior was called provocative and antisocial.他的行为被认为是煽动性的和反社会的。

ADJ-GRADED
激发性欲的；挑逗的
If you describe someone's clothing or behaviour as provocative, you mean that it is intended to make someone feel sexual desire.
例：Some adolescents might be more sexually mature and provocative than others.一些青少年可能相比同龄人在性方面更成熟、更具挑逗性。

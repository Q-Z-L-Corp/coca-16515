boon 
英[bu:n] 美[bun]
 
n.恩惠；福利；adj.快乐的；<古>慷慨的；
 "

N-COUNT
给生活带来方便的事物
You can describe something as a boon when it makes life better or easier for someone.
例：It is for this reason that television proves such a boon to so many people...就是因为这个原因，电视才会给这么多人带来如此大的便利。
例：This battery booster is a boon for photographers.这种电池充电器是摄影工作者的好帮手。

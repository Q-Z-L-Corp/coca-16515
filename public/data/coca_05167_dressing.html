A salad dressing is a mixture of oil, vinegar, and herbs or flavourings, which you pour over salad.
例：Mix the ingredients for the dressing in a bowl.在碗里将各种原料混成调料。

例：Miss Finkelstein will put a dressing on your thumb.芬克尔斯坦小姐会为你包扎拇指的。

woolly 
英[ˈwʊli] 美[ˈwʊli]
 
adj.羊毛的；像羊毛的；朦胧的；n.羊毛制品；羊；
 "

ADJ
毛料的；羊毛制的;像羊毛的
Something that is woolly is made of wool or looks like wool.
例：She wore this woolly hat with pompoms.她戴着这顶有小绒球的毛绒帽子。

N-COUNT
毛料衣服；(尤指)毛衣，羊毛衫
A woolly is a woollen piece of clothing, especially a sweater.

N-PLURAL
内衣;(尤指)长袖毛线衣裤
You can refer to underwear as woollies, especially when it is knitted from wool, and has long legs or long sleeves.

ADJ-GRADED
（人或观点）混乱的，糊涂的，不明确的
If you describe a person or their ideas as woolly, you are criticizing them for being confused or vague.
例：...a weak and woolly Government...软弱、糊涂的政府
例：It is no good setting vague and woolly goals — you will not know whether or not you have really achieved them.树立模糊而不明确的目标是没有用处的——你无法知道自己是否已经达到了目标。

 "

N-UNCOUNT
(因种族、性别、宗教不同而采取的)隔离措施，隔离政策
Segregation is the official practice of keeping people apart, usually people of different sexes, races, or religions.
例：The Supreme Court unanimously ruled that racial segregation in schools was unconstitutional...联邦最高法院一致裁定学校实行的种族隔离措施违反宪法。
例：Connecticut agreed to end its segregation of prison inmates suffering from AIDS.康涅狄格州同意不再隔离患艾滋病的囚犯。

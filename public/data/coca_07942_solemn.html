ADJ-GRADED
严肃的;庄严的
Someone or something that is solemn is very serious rather than cheerful or humorous.
例：His solemn little face broke into smiles...他严肃的小脸蛋上突然绽放出笑容。
例：He looked solemn.他看上去很严肃。

ADJ-GRADED
(承诺、协定等)庄严的，郑重的
A solemn promise or agreement is one that you make in a very formal, sincere way.
例：...a solemn pledge that he would never remarry.他永远不再结婚的郑重承诺

 "

VERB
详细检查;仔细察看
If you scrutinize something, you examine it very carefully, often to find out some information from it or about it.
例：Her purpose was to scrutinize his features to see if he was an honest man...她的目的是通过仔细观察他的相貌以判断他是否诚实。
例：Lloyds' results were carefully scrutinised as a guide to what to expect from the other banks.对劳埃德银行的业绩进行了仔细分析，以确定对其他银行的表现可以作何种期待。

crop 
英[krɒp] 美[krɑp]
 
n.农作物；产量；庄稼；平头；vt.种植；收割；修剪；剪短；vi.收成；收获；
 "

N-COUNT
庄稼;作物
Crops are plants such as wheat and potatoes that are grown in large quantities for food.
例：Rice farmers here still plant and harvest their crops by hand...这里种植水稻的农场主仍是人工种植和收割庄稼。
例：The main crop is wheat and this is grown even on the very steep slopes.主要作物是小麦，就连很陡的坡地都种上了小麦。

N-COUNT
(谷物或水果的)收成，产量
The plants or fruits that are collected at harvest time are referred to as a crop .
例：Each year it produces a fine crop of fruit...这里每年水果都大丰收。
例：The US government says that this year's corn crop should be about 8 percent more than last year...美国政府称今年的玉米产量应该会比去年提高8%左右。

N-SING
(在一起的)一群（人）;(同时发生的)一些（事情）
You can refer to a group of people or things that have appeared together as a crop of people or things.
例：The present crop of books and documentaries about Marilyn Monroe exploit the thirtieth anniversary of her death...时下出现的这些有关玛丽莲·梦露的书和纪录片是借她逝世30周年之际推出的。
例：Next year's crop of undergraduates opting to take physics represents just 2 per cent of all undergraduates.明年选修物理的大学生仅占大学生总数的2%。

VERB
结果实;有收成
When a plant crops, it produces fruits or parts which people want.
例：Although these vegetables adapt well to our temperate climate, they tend to crop poorly.虽然这些蔬菜作物很适应我们这里温和的气候，它们的收成却很差。

VERB
收割;收获
When you crop something that you have planted, you collect the fruits or parts that you want from it.
例：I started cropping my beans in July.我7月份开始收割豆类作物。

VERB
(牛、马等)啃吃(树叶或植物)
When an animal such as a cow or horse crops leaves or plants, it eats them.
例：I let the horse drop his head to crop the spring grass.我让马低下头啃吃春天的青草。

VERB
剪短(头发)
To crop someone's hair means to cut it short.
例：She cropped her hair and dyed it blonde.她把头发剪短并染成了金黄色。

N-COUNT
短发
A crop is a short hairstyle.
例：She had her long hair cut into a boyish crop.她把长发剪成了男孩子般的短发。

VERB
剪裁，裁切(照片)
If you crop a photograph, you cut part of it off, in order to get rid of part of the picture or to be able to frame it.
例：I decided to crop the picture just above the water line...我决定把这张照片水印以下的部分裁掉。
例：Her husband was cropped from the photograph.她丈夫被从照片上剪下去了。

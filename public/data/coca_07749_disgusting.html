disgusting 
英[dɪsˈgʌstɪŋ] 美[dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋ]
 
adj.令人作呕的；令人厌恶的；；不堪入目；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人厌恶的;令人作呕的
If you say that something is disgusting, you are criticizing it because it is extremely unpleasant.
例：It tasted disgusting...这味道令人作呕。
例：Smoking is a disgusting habit.吸烟是一个不良嗜好。

ADJ-GRADED
完全不能接受的;可怕的
If you say that something is disgusting, you mean that you find it completely unacceptable.
例：It's disgusting that the taxpayer is subsidising this project.由纳税人来资助这一项目实在是说不过去。

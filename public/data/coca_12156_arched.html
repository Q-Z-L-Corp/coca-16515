arched 
英[ɑ:tʃt] 美[ɑrtʃt]
 
adj.拱形的；弓形结构的；v.成弓形弯曲“arch”的过去式和过去分词；
 "

ADJ
（房顶、窗户或门）拱形的
An arched roof, window, or doorway is curved at the top.

ADJ
（桥）拱形的
An arched bridge has arches as part of its structure.
例：...a fortified arched bridge spanning the River Severn.横跨塞文河的加固拱桥

ADJ
弓形的;弧形的
You use arched to describe something that is curved like an arch.
例：A frown formed between her arched brows.她皱起两道弯眉。

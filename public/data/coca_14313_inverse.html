inverse 
英[ˌɪnˈvɜ:s] 美[ˌɪnˈvɜrs]

ADJ
相反的;成反比的;相逆的
If there is an inverse relationship between two things, one of them becomes larger as the other becomes smaller.
例：The tension grew in inverse proportion to the distance from their final destination.拉力的大小与他们离终点的距离成反比。

N-SING
相反的事物;对立面
The inverse of something is its exact opposite.
例：There is no sign that you bothered to consider the inverse of your logic.你根本就不想把你的逻辑反过来考虑。

fussy 
英[ˈfʌsi] 美[ˈfʌsi]
 
adj.大惊小怪的；难取悦的；易烦恼的；（衣着，字句等上）充分讲究的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
过分注重细节的;爱挑剔的;难以取悦的
Someone who is fussy is very concerned with unimportant details and is difficult to please.
例：She is not fussy about her food...她不挑食。
例：Her aunt was small, with a rather fussy manner.她的姨妈个子矮小，为人相当挑剔。

ADJ-GRADED
过分装饰的;过于繁琐的
If you describe things such as clothes and furniture as fussy, you are criticizing them because they are too elaborate or detailed.
例：We are not very keen on floral patterns and fussy designs.我们不是很喜欢花卉图案和过于繁琐的设计。

lair 
英[leə(r)] 美[ler]
 
n.（野兽的）巢穴，窝；（人的）藏身处；
 "

N-COUNT
兽穴；兽窝；巢穴
A lair is a place where a wild animal lives, usually a place which is underground or well-hidden.
例：...a fox's lair.狐狸窝

N-COUNT
藏匿之所；隐避之处；（尤指）独居处
Someone's lair is the particular room or hiding place that they go to, especially when they want to get away from other people.
例：Green recounts how he once went to see Bremner in his lair...格林讲述他有一次去布雷姆纳隐居之处看他的经历。
例：The village was once a pirates' lair.这个村子曾一度是海盗藏匿之处。

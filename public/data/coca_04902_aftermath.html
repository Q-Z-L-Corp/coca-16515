N-SING
后果；余波
The aftermath of an important event, especially a harmful one, is the situation that results from it.
例：In the aftermath of the coup, the troops opened fire on the demonstrators...政变结果是军队向游行示威者开火了。
例：During the Soviet era and its immediate aftermath, the region was officially known as 'Middle Asia and Kazakhstan'.在苏联时代和紧随其后的一段时期，该地区的官方名称为“中亚和哈萨克斯坦”。

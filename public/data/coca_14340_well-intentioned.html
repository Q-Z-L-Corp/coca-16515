well-intentioned 
英['welɪn'tenʃənd] 美[ˈwɛlɪnˈtɛnʃənd]
 
adj.好意的，出于好意的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(虽常事与愿违但)出于好心的,善意的,本意良好的
If you say that a person or their actions are well-intentioned, you mean that they intend to be helpful or kind but they are unsuccessful or cause problems.
例：He is well-intentioned but a poor administrator.他是个空有一番好意但能力很差的主管。
例：...rules that, however well-intentioned, are often hopelessly impractical.尽管出发点很好却往往完全不切实际的规定

seriousness 
英[ˈsɪəriəsnəs] 美[ˈsɪriəsnəs]
 
n.认真；严肃地；严重地；非常严肃地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
严重的;危急的;令人担忧的
Serious problems or situations are very bad and cause people to be worried or afraid.
例：Crime is an increasingly serious problem in Russian society...犯罪猖獗是俄罗斯社会面临的一个日益严重的问题。
例：The government still face very serious difficulties...政府仍然面临着非常严峻的困难。

ADJ-GRADED
重要的;需要认真考虑的;严肃的
Serious matters are important and deserve careful and thoughtful consideration.
例：I regard this as a serious matter...我认为这件事很重要。
例：Don't laugh boy. This is serious.小伙子，别笑，这很严肃。

ADJ-GRADED
认真的;郑重的;严肃的
When important matters are dealt with in a serious way, they are given careful and thoughtful consideration.
例：My parents never really faced up to my drug use in any serious way...我父母从未认真地正视过我的吸毒问题。
例：It was a question which deserved serious consideration.这是一个值得认真考虑的问题。

ADJ
(音乐、文学作品)严肃的，非供消遣(或娱乐)的
Serious music or literature requires concentration to understand or appreciate it.
例：...serious classical music...严肃的古典音乐
例：There is no point reviewing a blockbuster as you might review a serious novel.为一部大片写影评时，没必要像为一部严肃小说写书评那样认真谨慎。

ADJ-GRADED
认真的;当真的;真心的;郑重其事的
If someone is serious about something, they are sincere about what they are saying, doing, or intending to do.
例：You really are serious about this, aren't you?...你这话的确是发自内心的，是不是？
例：I hope you're not serious.我希望你不是认真的。

ADJ-GRADED
(人)若有所思的，庄重的，严肃的
Serious people are thoughtful and quiet, and do not laugh very often.
例：He's quite a serious person...他是一个非常一本正经的人。
例：She looked at me with big, serious eyes.她睁大眼睛若有所思地看着我。

ADJ
(钱等)数目巨大的，大量的
Serious money is a very large amount of money.
例：He started earning serious money only in the sixties.他在 60 年代才开始挣大钱。

radiance 
英[ˈreɪdiəns] 美[ˈrediəns]
 
n.发光；光辉；辐射；欣喜的神色；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
容光焕发；喜气洋洋
Radiance is great happiness which shows in someone's face and makes them look very attractive.
例：She has the vigour and radiance of someone young enough to be her granddaughter...她拥有与孙女辈的年轻人一样的活力和容光。
例：There was about her a new radiance.她焕发出新的神采。

N-UNCOUNT
光辉；发光；闪烁
Radiance is a glowing light shining from something.
例：The dim bulb of the bedside lamp cast a soft radiance over his face.床头灯朦胧的光柔和地投射在他脸上。

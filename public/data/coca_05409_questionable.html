adj.可疑的，有疑问的；adv.可疑地，不真实地；

ADJ-GRADED
可疑的;不可靠的;有问题的;不确定的
If you say that something is questionable, you mean that it is not completely honest, reasonable, or acceptable.
例：He has been dogged by allegations of questionable business practices...他一直被那些指控他有不正当商业行为的说法所困扰。
例：It is questionable whether the expenditure on this project is really justified...该项目的开支是否真的合理还未可知。

fixture

n.（房屋等的）固定装置；[商]定期放款，定期存款；固定在某位置的人或物；（定期定点举行的）体育活动；

N-COUNT
(房间等建筑物内的)固定装置，固定设施
Fixtures are pieces of furniture or equipment, for example baths and sinks, which are fixed inside a house or other building and which stay there if you move.
例：...a detailed list of what fixtures and fittings are included in the purchase price.房屋购买价中包括的固定装置及设备的详细清单

N-COUNT
(定期举行的)体育活动，体育赛事
A fixture is a sports event which takes place on a particular date.
例：City won this fixture 3-0 last season.上个赛季曼城队在该项赛事中以3比0获胜。

N-COUNT
(…的)常客;(…的)固定事物
If you describe someone or something as a fixture in a particular place or occasion, you mean that they always seem to be there.
例：She was a fixture in New York's nightclubs...她是纽约夜总会的常客。
例：The cordless kettle may now be a fixture in most kitchens.无绳电热水壶现在可能是多数厨房的固定设备。

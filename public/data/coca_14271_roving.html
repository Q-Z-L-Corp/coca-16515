roving 
英[ˈrəʊvɪŋ] 美[ˈroʊvɪŋ]
 
adj.流浪的；流动的；不固定的；n.粗纱；v.流浪( rove的现在分词)；
 "

ADJ
（人）流动的，漂泊的,不固定的
You use roving to describe a person who travels around, rather than staying in a fixed place.
例：...a roving reporter...流动记者
例：Left to raise themselves on the streets, these children form roving bands of delinquents.这些孩子被迫流浪街头，结成了一些流窜的犯罪团伙。

PHRASE
(男人)好色，眼神不安分
If you say that a man has a roving eye, you are criticizing him for continually paying attention to different women.

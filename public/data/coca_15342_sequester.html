sequester 
英[sɪˈkwestə(r)] 美[sɪˈkwɛstɚ]
 
vt.使隔绝，使隔离；<律>扣押；
 "

VERB
同 sequestrate
Sequester means the same as sequestrate .
例：Everything he owned was sequestered.他的所有财产都被扣押了。

VERB
使隔离;使与他人隔绝
If someone is sequestered somewhere, they are isolated from other people.
例：This jury is expected to be sequestered for at least two months.预计这个陪审团将至少被隔离两个月。

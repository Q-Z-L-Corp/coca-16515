simplification 
英[ˌsɪmplɪfɪˈkeɪʃn] 美[ˌsɪmpləfəˈkeʃən]
 
n.单纯化，简单化；
 "

N-COUNT
简化的事物
You can use simplification to refer to the thing that is produced when you make something simpler or when you reduce it to its basic elements.
例：Like any such diagram, it is a simplification.像其他这种表格一样，这是简化了的。

N-UNCOUNT
简化
Simplification is the act or process of making something simpler.
例：Everyone favours the simplification of court procedures.每个人都希望法庭程序能够简化。

ADJ-GRADED
自视高人一等的；屈尊俯就的
If someone is patronizing, they speak or behave towards you in a way that seems friendly, but which shows that they think they are superior to you.
例：The tone of the interview was unnecessarily patronizing.面试官的口气让人觉得受了他多大恩惠似的，实在没有必要。
例：...his patronising attitude to the homeless.他对无家可归者屈尊俯就的态度

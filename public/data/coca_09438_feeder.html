feeder 
英[ˈfi:də(r)] 美[ˈfidɚ]
 
n.支流，支线；进料器；供给装置；饲养员；
 "

ADJ
汇入主干道的;汇入干流的;(铁路)支线的
A feeder road, railway, or river is a smaller one that leads to a more important one.
例：...the feeder road leading to the airport.通往机场的岔路

N-COUNT
(航空或铁路)支线
Feeder airline and railway services connect major routes and local destinations.
例：...a feeder to British Airways's transatlantic destinations.英国航空公司飞往大西洋彼岸的航班的支线航线

N-COUNT
(为重点学校或球队输送学生或球员的)生源学校，直属学校，直属球队
A feeder school or team provides students or players for a larger or more important one.
例：...children coming up from two infant feeder schools.从两个直属幼儿学校来的孩子

N-COUNT
鸟食罐;喂食器
A feeder is a container that you fill with food for birds or animals.

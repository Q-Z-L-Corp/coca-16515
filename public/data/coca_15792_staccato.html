staccato 
英[stəˈkɑ:təʊ] 美[stəˈkɑtoʊ]
 
adv.& adj.<音>以断音（的），以断奏方式（的）；n.断奏的曲乐段，断唱；（品牌）思加图；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(噪音)断断续续的，短促不连贯的
A staccato noise consists of a series of short, sharp, separate sounds.
例：He spoke in Arabic, a short staccato burst.他突然用阿拉伯语叽里呱啦说了一串话。
例：...the staccato chattering of several machine-guns.几架机关枪嗒嗒嗒的射击声

ADV-GRADED
(乐谱上)以断音，以断奏方式
The instruction staccato on a piece of music means that the notes should be played or sung very briefly with gaps between them.

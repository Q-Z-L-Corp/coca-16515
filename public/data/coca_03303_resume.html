resume 
英[rɪ'zju:m] 美[rɪ'zjum]
 
v.重新开始；继续；恢复职位；
 "

V-ERG
（使）重新开始;（使）继续进行
If you resume an activity or if it resumes, it begins again.
例：After the war he resumed his duties at Emmanuel College...战争过后，他重新开始了在伊曼纽尔学院的工作。
例：The search is expected to resume early today.预计搜寻工作会于今天早些时候重新开始。

VERB
恢复(职位);回到（座位）
If you resume your seat or position, you return to the seat or position you were in before you moved.
例：'I changed my mind,' Blanche said, resuming her seat.“我改变主意了，”布兰奇说着回到她的座位上。

VERB
继续说;接着说
If someone resumes, they begin speaking again after they have stopped for a short time.
例：'Hey, Judith,' he resumed, 'tell me all about yourself.'“嘿，朱迪思，”他接着说，“跟我谈谈你自己吧。”

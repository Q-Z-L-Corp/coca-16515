magnetism 
英[ˈmægnətɪzəm] 美[ˈmæɡnɪˌtɪzəm]
 
n.磁性，磁力；磁学；吸引力；催眠术；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
魅力;吸引力
Someone or something that has magnetism has unusual, powerful, and exciting qualities which attract people to them.
例：There was no doubting the animal magnetism of the man...毫无疑问，这个男人身上透着一种野性魅力。
例：Later, she would describe his magnetism as irresistible.她过后说起时会形容他的魅力难以抵挡。

N-UNCOUNT
磁性;磁力
Magnetism is the natural power of some objects and substances, especially iron, to attract other objects towards them.

VERB
使政治化；使具有政治性；使关心（或参与）政治
If you politicize someone or something, you make them more interested in politics or more involved with politics.
例：...ideas which might politicize the labouring classes and cause them to question the status quo...可能唤醒工人阶级的政治意识、并促使他们质疑现状的思想
例：Some feminists had attempted to politicize personal life.一些女权主义者尝试过将个人生活政治化。

scribble 
英[ˈskrɪbl] 美[ˈskrɪbəl]
 
vt.& vi.潦草地书写；乱写，胡写；滥写（文学作品等）；[印，纺]粗梳；n.潦草书写；乱写；拙劣文字；粗制滥造的文章；vi.乱写，乱涂；
 "

VERB
潦草地写;匆匆地写
If you scribble something, you write it quickly and roughly.
例：She scribbled a note to tell Mum she'd gone out...她匆匆写了个便条告诉妈妈她已外出。
例：As I scribbled in my diary the light went out.我正草草地写着日记，灯灭了。

VERB
乱涂;乱画
To scribble means to make meaningless marks or rough drawings using a pencil or pen.
例：When Caroline was five she scribbled on a wall.卡罗琳 5 岁时在墙上乱涂乱画。

N-VAR
潦草的字迹;涂鸦
Scribble is something that has been written or drawn quickly and roughly.
例：I'm sorry what I wrote was such a scribble.很抱歉我写得如此潦草。

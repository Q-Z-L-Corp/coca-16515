petitioner 
英[pəˈtɪʃənə(r)] 美[ pəˈtɪʃənɚ]

N-COUNT
请愿者
A petitioner is a person who presents or signs a petition.

N-COUNT
诉愿人；上诉人
A petitioner is a person who brings a legal case to a court of law.
例：The judge awarded the costs of the case to the petitioners.法官判定由这起案件的上诉人支付诉讼费用。

N-COUNT
上诉请求离婚者
A petitioner is someone who goes to a court of law to ask for a divorce.
例：Both the petitioner and respondent provide for the upkeep of the children.离婚上诉方和被告方共同抚养子女。

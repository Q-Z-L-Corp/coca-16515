milestone 
英[ˈmaɪlstəʊn] 美[ˈmaɪlstoʊn]
 
n.里程碑；划时代事件；
 "

N-COUNT
里程碑;重大事件;重要阶段
A milestone is an important event in the history or development of something or someone.
例：He said the launch of the party represented a milestone in Zambian history...他说该党的创立是赞比亚历史上的一个里程碑。
例：Starting school is a milestone for both children and parents.对于孩子和家长而言，开始上学是一件大事。

N-COUNT
（路边显示到某地距离的）里程碑，里程标志
A milestone is a stone by the side of a road showing the distances to particular places.

starch 
英[stɑ:tʃ] 美[stɑrtʃ]
 
n.淀粉，含淀粉的食物；形式主义；古板，僵硬；元气；vt.把（衣服）上浆；给…上浆；浆硬；使僵硬；
 "

N-MASS
淀粉
Starch is a substance that is found in foods such as bread, potatoes, pasta, and rice and gives you energy.
例：She reorganised her eating so that she was taking more fruit and vegetables and less starch, salt, and fat.她重新调整了自己的饮食，多吃水果、蔬菜，少吃淀粉、盐和脂肪。

N-UNCOUNT
(给布上浆用的)淀粉浆
Starch is a substance that is used for making cloth stiffer, especially cotton and linen.

VERB
给(布)上浆
If you starch cloth, you make it stiffer using starch.
例：I took down the curtains, washed, and starched them.我摘下窗帘，洗了洗，然后给它们上了浆。

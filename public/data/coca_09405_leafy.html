leafy 
英[ˈli:fi] 美[ˈlifi]
 
adj.多叶的，叶茂的，多树木的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（植物）叶茂的，多叶的
Leafy trees and plants have lots of leaves on them.
例：His two-story brick home was graced with a patio and surrounded by tall, leafy trees.他那两层高的砖房有一个漂亮的露台，四周环拥绿叶成阴的大树。

ADJ-GRADED
（地方）草木葱茏的，树木繁茂的
You say that a place is leafy when there are lots of trees and plants there.
例：...semi-detached homes with gardens in leafy suburban areas.位于草木葱茏的市郊住宅区、带花园的半独立房子

broaden 
英[ˈbrɔ:dn] 美[ˈbrɔdn]
 
vt.& vi.使。。变宽，扩展；放宽；扩大；加宽；
 "

VERB
变宽
When something broadens, it becomes wider.
例：The trails broadened into roads...羊肠小道变成了宽阔的公路。
例：The smile broadened to a grin.笑容变得更加灿烂。

V-ERG
扩大…的范围；增长，增加（经验、受欢迎程度）
When you broaden something such as your experience or popularity or when it broadens, the number of things or people that it includes becomes greater.
例：We must broaden our appeal...我们必须进行更广泛的呼吁。
例：I thought you wanted to broaden your horizons...我想你是想拓宽自己的视野。

PHRASE
开阔（心胸）
If an experience broadens your mind, it makes you more willing to accept other people's beliefs and customs.
例：They say that travel broadens the mind.他们说旅行能够开阔心胸。

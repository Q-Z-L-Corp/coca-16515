ADJ
不可逆转的;不可挽回的;不可撤销的
If a change is irreversible, things cannot be changed back to the way they were before.
例：She could suffer irreversible brain damage if she is not treated within seven days...如果她在 7 天之内没有得到治疗,她的大脑有可能遭受不可逆转的损伤。
例：The reforms are irreversible.改革没有回头路。

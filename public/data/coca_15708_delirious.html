delirious 
英[dɪˈlɪriəs] 美[dɪˈlɪriəs]
 
adj.精神混乱的；非常激动的；极兴奋的，发狂的；神不守舍；
 "

ADJ
(通常指因生病发烧而)谵妄的，神志不清的，说胡话的
Someone who is delirious is unable to think or speak in a sensible and reasonable way, usually because they are very ill and have a fever.
例：I was delirious and blacked out several times.我神志不清，昏迷了好几次。

ADJ-GRADED
欣喜若狂的;极度兴奋的
Someone who is delirious is extremely excited and happy.
例：His tax-cutting pledge brought a delirious crowd to their feet...他减税的承诺让亢奋的人群欢呼雀跃起来。
例：I was delirious with joy.我欣喜若狂。

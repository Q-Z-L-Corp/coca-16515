N-COUNT
竞争者;对手
A company's competitors are companies who are trying to sell similar goods or services to the same people.
例：The bank isn't performing as well as some of its competitors.这家银行表现得不如几家竞争对手出色。

N-COUNT
选手;参赛者
A competitor is a person who takes part in a competition or contest.
例：Herbert Blocker of Germany, one of the oldest competitors, won the individual silver medal.德国的赫伯特·布洛克尔是年纪最大的选手之一，赢得了个人项目的银牌。

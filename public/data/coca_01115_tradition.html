tradition 
英[trəˈdɪʃn] 美[trəˈdɪʃən]
 
n.传统；惯例；[宗]经外传说；[法]移交，引渡；
 "

N-VAR
传统;传说
A tradition is a custom or belief that has existed for a long time.
例：...the rich traditions of Afro-Cuban music, and dance...非裔古巴人丰富的音乐、舞蹈传统
例：Mary has carried on the family tradition of giving away plants...玛丽承袭了向别人赠送植物的家族传统。

PREP-PHRASE
承袭…风格的;具有…特点的
If you say that something or someone is in the tradition of a person or thing from the past, you mean that they have many features that remind you of that person or thing.
例：They're marvellous pictures in the tradition of Gainsborough.这些是具有庚斯博罗风格的绝妙画作。
例：…a Catholic novelist in the tradition of Graham Greene.与格雷厄姆·格林一脉相承的天主教小说家

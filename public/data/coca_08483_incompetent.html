ADJ-GRADED
无能力的;不称职的;不合格的
If you describe someone as incompetent, you are criticizing them because they are unable to do their job or a task properly.
例：The court declared him incompetent to manage his financial affairs...法庭宣布他不具备管理自己财务的能力。
例：I was incompetent at playing the piano...我钢琴弹得不好。

infusion 
英[ɪnˈfju:ʒn] 美[ɪnˈfjuʒən]
 
n.注入；灌输；泡制，浸渍；注入物；
 "

N-VAR
输入;灌输
If there is an infusion of one thing into another, the first thing is added to the other thing and makes it stronger or better.
例：He brought a tremendous infusion of hope to the people.他给人们带来巨大希望。
例：...a series of capital infusions.一系列的资本输入

N-COUNT
浸剂;浸液
An infusion is a liquid made by leaving herbs in hot water until the flavour is strong.
例：...a herbal infusion...草药浸液
例：This remedy contains infusions of five flowers.这个药方中包含了 5 种花的浸剂。

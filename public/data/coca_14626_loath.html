loath 
英[ləʊθ, ləʊð] 美[loθ, loð]
 
adj.不愿意的，不喜欢的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不乐意的；不情愿的；勉强的
If you are loath to do something, you do not want to do it.
例：She is loath to give up her hard-earned liberty...她不愿放弃好不容易得来的自由。
例：The new finance minister seems loth to cut income tax.新任财政部长似乎不愿削减所得税。

ADJ
关上百叶窗的
A shuttered window, room, or building has its shutters closed.
例：I opened a shuttered window...我打开了一扇百叶窗已合上的窗户。
例：Schools and government offices have been closed, and many shops remain shuttered.学校和政府办公楼都关闭了，许多商店的百叶窗也一直关着。

ADJ
装有百叶窗的
A shuttered window, room, or building has shutters fitted to it.
例：The stone-built property is in good condition, with a new tiled roof, shuttered windows and electric heating.这栋石砌房屋的条件很不错，有新铺的瓦片屋顶，装有百叶窗的窗户还有电力供暖系统。

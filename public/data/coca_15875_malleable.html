ADJ-GRADED
（人）温顺的，易受影响的，顺从的
If you say that someone is malleable, you mean that they are easily influenced or controlled by other people.
例：The malleable mayor of New York was under his control...这位听话的纽约市市长听命于他。
例：She was young enough to be malleable.她还年轻，还很乖。

ADJ-GRADED
（材料）柔软的，可锻的，可塑的，有延展性的
A substance that is malleable is soft and can easily be made into different shapes.
例：Silver is the most malleable of all metals.银是延展性最好的金属。
例：...using clay, plasticine or another malleable material.用黏土、橡皮泥或其他延展性强的材料

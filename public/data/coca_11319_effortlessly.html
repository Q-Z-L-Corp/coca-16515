ADJ-GRADED
不费力的;轻松的
Something that is effortless is done easily and well.
例：In a single effortless motion, he scooped Frannie into his arms.他一下子就轻松地把弗兰尼拥入怀里。
例：...effortless and elegant Italian cooking.简单精致的意大利烹饪

ADJ-GRADED
(品质)自然的，天生具有的
You use effortless to describe a quality that someone has naturally and does not have to learn.
例：She liked him above all for his effortless charm.她最喜欢他身上那种与生俱来的魅力。

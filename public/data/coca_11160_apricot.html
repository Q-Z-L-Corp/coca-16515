N-VAR
杏
An apricot is a small, soft, round fruit with yellowish-orange flesh and a stone inside.
例：...12 oz apricots, halved and stoned.12盎司切成两半并去核的杏子
例：...apricot tart.杏肉馅饼

COLOUR
杏黄色
Apricot is used to describe things that are yellowish-orange in colour.
例：The bridesmaids wore apricot and white organza...伴娘身着杏黄色和白色相间的透明硬纱礼服。
例：The heavy curtains were a warm apricot.这些厚重的窗帘是温暖的杏黄色。

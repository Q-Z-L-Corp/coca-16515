immersion 
英[ɪˈmɜ:ʃn] 美[ɪˈmɜrʃn]
 
n.沉浸；洗礼；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
潜心钻研;专心
Someone's immersion in a subject is their complete involvement in it.
例：...long-term assignments that allowed them total immersion in their subjects.可以让他们潜心研究课题的长期任务

N-UNCOUNT
浸没;浸泡
Immersion of something in a liquid means putting it into the liquid so that it is completely covered.
例：The wood had become swollen from prolonged immersion.因长时间浸泡，木头发胀了。

anecdotal 
英[ˌænɪkˈdəʊtl] 美[ˌænɪkˈdoʊtl]
 
adj.轶事的；趣闻的；多轶事的；含轶事的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
传闻的；轶事的
Anecdotal evidence is based on individual accounts, rather than on reliable research or statistics, and so may not be valid.
例：Anecdotal evidence suggests that sales in Europe have slipped.传闻有证据表明欧洲的销售量已经下滑。
例：...countless anecdotal reports.无数的八卦消息

ADJ-GRADED
（讲演或写作）含轶事的，多轶闻的
Anecdotal speech or writing is full of anecdotes or is based on anecdotes.
例：Gray's book is anecdotal and entertaining.格雷的书中尽是些八卦消息，颇具娱乐性。

instil 
英[ɪnˈstɪl] 美[ɪnˈstɪl]
 
vt.逐步灌输；使渗透；滴注；
 "

VERB
逐渐灌输
If you instil an idea or feeling in someone, especially over a period of time, you make them think it or feel it.
例：They hope that their work will instil a sense of responsibility in children...他们希望自己的工作能够逐步培养起孩子们的一种责任感。
例：The motive of the executions would be to instil fear.执行死刑的动机是要灌输恐惧。

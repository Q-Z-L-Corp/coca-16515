pare 
英[peə(r)] 美[per]
 
vt.削皮；削减；修剪（指甲等）；
 "

VERB
削去…的皮；削去；剥去
When you pare something, or pare part of it off or away, you cut off its skin or its outer layer.
例：Pare the brown skin from the meat with a very sharp knife...用一把非常锋利的刀将褐色的皮从肉上剥去。
例：He took out a slab of cheese, pared off a slice and ate it hastily.他拿出一大块奶酪，切下一片，匆忙吃了。

VERB
削减；减少
If you pare something down or back, or if you pare it, you reduce it.
例：The number of Ministries has been pared down by a third...部委的数量已经减少了1/3。
例：The luxury tax won't really do much to pare down the budget deficit...奢侈品税其实对削减预算赤字并不会有多少帮助。

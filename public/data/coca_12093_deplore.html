deplore 
英[dɪˈplɔ:(r)] 美[dɪˈplɔr, -ˈplor]
 
vt.悲悼，哀叹；谴责，强烈反对；

VERB
谴责;强烈反对
If you say that you deplore something, you think it is very wrong or immoral.
例：He's a judo black belt but he says he deplores violence...他是一名柔道黑带选手，但他说自己强烈反对暴力。
例：He deplored the fact that the Foreign Secretary was driven into resignation...他对外交大臣被迫辞职一事表示强烈谴责。

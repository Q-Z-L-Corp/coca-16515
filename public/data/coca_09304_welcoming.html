welcoming 
英[ˈwelkəmɪŋ] 美[]
 
v.欢迎( welcome的现在分词 )；迎接；乐于接受；希望有；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
热情的;友好的;好客的
If someone is welcoming or if they behave in a welcoming way, they are friendly to you when you arrive somewhere, so that you feel happy and accepted.
例：When we arrived at her house Susan was very welcoming...当我们到达苏珊家时，她非常热情。
例：Her face spread in a welcoming smile.她脸上展露出热情的笑容。

ADJ-GRADED
（建筑物或房间）舒适的，惬意的
A welcoming building or room looks pleasant and you feel as though you would be happy and comfortable spending time there.
例：The restaurant is small and green and very welcoming.这家餐厅小巧精致，绿意盎然，令人感到非常惬意。

repulsive 
英[rɪˈpʌlsɪv] 美[rɪˈpʌlsɪv]
 
adj.令人厌恶的，可憎的；丑恶；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人憎恶的;让人厌恶的;使人反感的
If you describe something or someone as repulsive, you mean that they are horrible and disgusting and you want to avoid them.
例：...repulsive fat white slugs.让人恶心的白白胖胖的鼻涕虫
例：...the most repulsive mass murderer America has known.美国历史上最令人憎恶的杀人恶魔

ADJ
相斥的;排斥的;斥力的
A repulsive force is a force which pushes away what is around it.
例：The repulsive force within the nucleus is enormous.核子内部的斥力是巨大的。

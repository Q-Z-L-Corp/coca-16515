immerse 
英[ɪˈmɜ:s] 美[ɪˈmɜrs]
 
vt.浸没；施浸礼；沉迷…中，陷入；
 "

VERB
使沉浸(于);使专心(于);使深陷(于)
If you immerse yourself in something that you are doing, you become completely involved in it.
例：Since then I've lived alone and immersed myself in my career.自那以后,我一直一个人生活,全身心投入我的事业当中。

VERB
使浸没(于液体中)
If something is immersed in a liquid, someone puts it into the liquid so that it is completely covered.
例：The electrodes are immersed in liquid.电极浸没在液体中。

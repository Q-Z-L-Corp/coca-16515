ADJ
不复杂的；简单的
If you describe someone or something as uncomplicated, you approve of them because they are easy to deal with or understand.
例：She is a beautiful, uncomplicated girl.她是一个美丽而率真的女孩。
例：...good, fresh British cooking with its uncomplicated, direct flavours.新鲜可口的英式菜肴，简单而又原汁原味

application 
英[ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃn] 美[ˌæplɪˈkeʃən]
 
n.适用，应用，运用；申请，请求，申请表格；勤勉，用功；敷用，敷用药；
 "

N-COUNT
申请；请求；申请书；申请表
An application for something such as a job or membership of an organization is a formal written request for it.
例：His application for membership of the organisation was rejected...他想要加入该组织的申请遭到了拒绝。
例：...Turkey's application to join the European Community...土耳其要求加入欧共体的申请

N-VAR
应用；运用
The application of a rule or piece of knowledge is the use of it in a particular situation.
例：Students learned the practical application of the theory they had learned in the classroom...学生们学会了将课堂上学到的理论付诸实际应用。
例：Simon's book provides a succinct outline of artificial intelligence and its application to robotics.西蒙的书简明扼要地概括了人工智能及其在机器人技术方面的应用。

N-COUNT
（计算机的）应用软件
In computing, an application is a piece of software designed to carry out a particular task.

N-UNCOUNT
勤奋;专心;努力
Application is hard work and concentration on what you are doing over a period of time.
例：...his immense talent, boundless energy and unremitting application.他横溢的才华、充沛的精力和不懈的努力

N-VAR
涂抹；敷用；施用
The application of something to a surface is the act or process of putting it on or rubbing it into the surface.
例：With repeated applications of weedkiller, the weeds were overcome.经过反复施用除草剂，杂草得以彻底根除。

N-COUNT
（食物的）一份，一客
A helping of food is the amount of it that you get in a single serving.
例：She gave them extra helpings of ice-cream.她又给了他们几份冰激凌。

N-COUNT
（尤指某种特性的）一些，一定量
You can refer to an amount of something, especially a quality, as a helping of that thing.
例：It took a generous helping of entrepreneurial confidence to persevere during this incident.在这一事件中坚持下来是有了充足的创业信心的。

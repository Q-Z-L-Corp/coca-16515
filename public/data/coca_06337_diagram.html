diagram 
英[ˈdaɪəgræm] 美[ˈdaɪəˌɡræm]
 
n.图表；图解；示意图；[数]线图；vt.用图表示；图解；
 "

N-COUNT
图表;简图;示意图
A diagram is a simple drawing which consists mainly of lines and is used, for example, to explain how a machine works.
例：...a circuit diagram...电路图
例：You can reduce long explanations to simple charts or diagrams.你可以把冗长的解释简化成一目了然的图表。

VERB
绘制…的示意图;用图表表示（或说明）
To diagram something means to draw a diagram of it or to explain it using a diagram.
例：The sound waves of the voice could be diagramed as in B.这个声音的声波如图B所示。
例：...diagramming the movement of a system's variables.用图表表示系统各个变量的变化情况

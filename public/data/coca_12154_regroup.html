V-ERG
(将…)重新组合;（尤指将士兵）重新整编
When people, especially soldiers, regroup, or when someone regroups them, they form an organized group again, in order to continue fighting.
例：Now the rebel army has regrouped and reorganised...现在叛军已经进行了重新整编。
例：The rebels may simply be using the truce to regroup their forces.叛军可能只想利用停火重新组织他们的军事力量。

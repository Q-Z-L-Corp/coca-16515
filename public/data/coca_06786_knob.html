knob 
英[nɒb] 美[nɑb]
 
n.球形把手；（收音机等的）旋钮；小块；疙瘩；v.鼓起；使有球形突出物；给（门等）装球形把手；
 "

N-COUNT
（门或抽屉的）球形把手
A knob is a round handle on a door or drawer which you use in order to open or close it.
例：He turned the knob and pushed against the door.他扭动把手推了推门。

N-COUNT
（柱子或棍子顶部的）疙瘩，节，球状物
A knob is a rounded lump or ball on top of a post or stick.
例：A loose brass knob on the bedstead rattled.床架上一个松动的球形铜扣发出嘎啦声。

N-COUNT
（机器或设备的）旋钮
A knob is a round switch on a piece of machinery or equipment.
例：...the volume knob.音量旋钮

N-COUNT
小块（黄油）
A knob of butter is a small amount of it.
例：Top the steaming hot potatoes with a knob of butter.在冒热气的马铃薯上放一小块黄油。

ADJ
（蛋）煮得老的，煮硬的
A hard-boiled egg has been boiled in its shell until the whole of the inside is solid.

ADJ-GRADED
（人）不动感情的，强硬的
You use hard-boiled to describe someone who is tough and does not show much emotion.
例：She's hard-boiled, tough and funny.她冷峻、坚韧而风趣。

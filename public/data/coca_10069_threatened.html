ADJ-GRADED
受到威胁的;感到危险的
If you feel threatened, you feel as if someone is trying to harm you.
例：Anger is the natural reaction we experience when we feel threatened or frustrated...我们感觉受到威胁或遭遇挫折时，生气是正常反应。
例：The survey found that 4 per cent of people felt threatened by the sight of beggars.调查发现，4% 的人看见乞丐时会觉得危险。

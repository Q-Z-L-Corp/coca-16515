VERB
用力嚼;出声地嚼
If you munch food, you eat it by chewing it slowly, thoroughly, and rather noisily.
例：Luke munched the chicken sandwiches...卢克津津有味地吃着鸡肉三明治。
例：Across the table, his son Benjie munched appreciatively...桌子对面，他儿子本吉有滋有味地吃着。

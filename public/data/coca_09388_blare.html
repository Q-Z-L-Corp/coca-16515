V-ERG
高声鸣响；（使）发出刺耳的声音
If something such as a siren or radio blares or if you blare it, it makes a loud, unpleasant noise.
例：The fire engines were just pulling up, sirens blaring...消防车正要停下来，警报器发出刺耳的声音。
例：Music blared from the flat behind me...刺耳的音乐从我身后的公寓传出来。

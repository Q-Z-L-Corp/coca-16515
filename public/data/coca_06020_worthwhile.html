ADJ-GRADED
有价值的;令人愉快的；值得花费时间（或金钱、精力）的
If something is worthwhile, it is enjoyable or useful, and worth the time, money, or effort that is spent on it.
例：The President's trip to Washington this week seems to have been worthwhile...总统本周的华盛顿之行看来是有价值的。
例：...a worthwhile movie that was compelling enough to watch again...非常吸引人、值得再看一遍的精彩影片

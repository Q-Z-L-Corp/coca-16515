characterize 
英[ˈkærəktəraɪz] 美[ˈkærəktəˌraɪz]
 
vt.描述（人或物）的特性；具有…的特征；vi.塑造人物；
 "

VERB
使具有特点
If something is characterized by a particular feature or quality, that feature or quality is an obvious part of it.
例：This election campaign has been characterized by violence...暴力行为已成为这次竞选活动的一大特点。
例：A bold use of colour characterizes the bedroom.这间卧室的特点是用色大胆。

VERB
描绘;描述
If you characterize someone or something as a particular thing, you describe them as that thing.
例：Both companies have characterized the relationship as friendly...两家公司都认为彼此之间关系友好。
例：This play is characterized as a comedy.这出戏被认为是喜剧。

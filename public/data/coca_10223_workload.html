N-COUNT
(人或组织的)工作量，工作负荷
The workload of a person or organization is the amount of work that has to be done by them.
例：The sudden cancellation of Mr Blair's trip was due to his heavy workload...布莱尔先生因为工作繁重而突然取消行程。
例：This office's resources and staff would have to be increased to cope with the extra workload.要应付额外的工作，必须增加这个办公室的资源和人手。

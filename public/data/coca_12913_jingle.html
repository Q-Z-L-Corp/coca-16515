jingle 
英[ˈdʒɪŋgl] 美[ˈdʒɪŋɡəl]
 
n.叮当声；节拍十分规则的简单诗歌；vt.& vi.（使）叮当作响；具有简单而又引人注意的韵律；
 "

V-ERG
（使）叮当响；（使)发出叮当声
When something jingles or when you jingle it, it makes a gentle ringing noise, like small bells.
例：Brian put his hands in his pockets and jingled some change...布赖恩把手插进口袋里，把几枚硬币拨弄得叮当作响。
例：Her bracelets jingled like bells.她的手镯叮当作响，如铃声般悦耳。

N-COUNT
（广播或电视中宣传产品或节目的）短歌，短曲，广告歌
A jingle is a short, simple tune, often with words, which is used to advertise a product or programme on radio or television.
例：...advertising jingles.广告短歌

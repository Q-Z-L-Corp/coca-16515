polar 
英[ˈpəʊlə(r)] 美[ˈpoʊlə(r)]
 
adj.极地的，两极的；正好相反的；[化，物]磁极的，有磁性的；n.[几]极线，极面；
 "

ADJ
近地极的；极地的
Polar means near the North and South Poles.
例：...the rigours of life in the polar regions...在极地地区生活的艰苦
例：Warmth melted some of the polar ice.温暖的天气融化了一些极地的冰雪。

ADJ
（在性质、类型等方面）完全相反的，截然对立的
Polar is used to describe things which are completely opposite in character, quality, or type.
例：In many ways, Brett and Bernard are polar opposites.在许多方面，布雷特和伯纳德截然不同。
例：...economists at polar ends of the politico-economic spectrum.政治经济观点完全对立的经济学家

richly 
英[ˈrɪtʃli] 美[ˈrɪtʃli]
 
adv.丰富地；富裕地；完全地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
（色彩）艳丽地，强烈地;（味道、香味）浓郁地
If something is richly coloured, flavoured, or perfumed, it has a pleasantly strong colour, flavour, or perfume.
例：...an opulent display of richly coloured fabrics.各种色彩艳丽的织物的华美展示
例：...the richly flavoured, hearty foods of the region.该地区风味浓郁、品种丰盛的食物

ADV-GRADED
（装饰）精美地，华美地，华丽地
If something is richly decorated, patterned, or furnished, it has a lot of elaborate and beautiful decoration, patterns, or furniture.
例：Coffee steamed in the richly decorated silver pot.咖啡在装饰精美的银壶中冒着热气。
例：...a richly illustrated introduction to the art of the Islamic world.对伊斯兰国家艺术的配有精美插图的介绍

ADV-GRADED
非常;充分;完全
If you say that someone richly deserves an award, success, or victory, you approve of what they have done and feel very strongly that they deserve it.
例：He achieved the success he so richly deserved.他取得了自己完全应得的成功。
例：...a deeply affecting performance that won him a richly deserved Oscar.让他理所当然地获得了奥斯卡奖的那感人至深的表演

ADV-GRADED
充分地;丰富地
If a person or place is richly endowed or supplied with something, they have a lot of it.
例：...a boy richly endowed with courage...一个充满勇气的男孩
例：The hands are richly supplied with nerve endings.手上布满了神经末梢。

ADV-GRADED
（回报）丰厚地，令人惬意地
If you are richly rewarded for doing something, you get something very valuable or pleasant in return for doing it.
例：It is a difficult book to read, but it richly rewards the effort...这本书很难读懂，但是非常值得一读。
例：Their caution has been richly rewarded.他们的谨慎得到了很好的回报。

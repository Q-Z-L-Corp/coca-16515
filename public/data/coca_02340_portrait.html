portrait 
英[ˈpɔ:treɪt] 美[ˈpɔrtrət]
 
n.肖像，肖像画；模型，标本；半身雕塑像；人物描写；
 "

N-COUNT
肖像；画像
A portrait is a painting, drawing, or photograph of a particular person.
例：Lucian Freud has been asked to paint a portrait of the Queen.卢西恩·弗洛伊德已受邀为女王画肖像画。
例：...the English portrait painter Augustus John.英格兰肖像画家奥古斯塔斯·约翰

N-COUNT
描绘；描写；描述
A portrait of a person, place, or thing is a verbal description of them.
例：...this gripping, funny portrait of Jewish life in 1950s London.这段扣人心弦、令人捧腹的对伦敦20世纪50年代犹太人生活的描写

waft 
英[wɒft] 美[wɑft]
 
vt.& vi.吹送，飘送；使飘荡；漂浮；n.一股；一阵微风；飘荡；风信旗；
 "

V-ERG
(使)飘荡;吹拂
If sounds or smells waft through the air, or if something such as a light wind wafts them, they move gently through the air.
例：The scent of climbing roses wafts through the window...藤本月季的芳香飘进了窗户。
例：The music from the party wafts out to the terrace...晚会的音乐飘到了外面的露台上。

N-COUNT
(尤指正式服装或官员服装上的)腰带，彩带,肩带
A sash is a long piece of cloth which people wear round their waist or over one shoulder, especially with formal or official clothes.
例：She wore a white dress with a thin blue sash...她身着一件系有蓝色细腰带的白色连衣裙。
例：She arrived to receive the sash of office from the outgoing president.她走上前去从即将离职的总统手中接过了就职腰带。

N-COUNT
(垂直推拉窗的)窗框
A sash is one of the sliding frames in a sash window.

N-UNCOUNT
消息;资料;情报
Information about someone or something consists of facts about them.
例：Pat refused to give her any information about Sarah...帕特拒绝向她提供任何关于萨拉的消息。
例：Each centre would provide information on technology and training...每个中心都会提供技术与培训方面的信息。

N-UNCOUNT
(计算机程序储存和使用的)数据,资料
Information consists of the facts and figures that are stored and used by a computer program.
例：Pictures are scanned into a form of digital information that computers can recognize.照片被扫描成某种电脑可以识别的数字资料。

N-UNCOUNT
问讯处;信息台
Information is a service which you can telephone to find out someone's telephone number.

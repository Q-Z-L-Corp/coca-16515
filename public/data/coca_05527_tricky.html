ADJ-GRADED
难办的;难对付的;棘手的
If you describe a task or problem as tricky, you mean that it is difficult to do or deal with.
例：Parking can be tricky in the town centre...在市中心泊车可能很困难。
例：It's a very tricky problem, but I think there are a number of things you can do.那是个非常棘手的问题，但我想有几样事你是可以做到的。

ADJ-GRADED
诡计多端的;奸诈的;狡猾的
If you describe a person as tricky, you mean that they are likely to deceive you or cheat you.

VERB
使权力中央化;对…集权控制
To centralize a country, state, or organization means to create a system in which one central group of people gives instructions to regional groups.
例：In the mass production era multinational firms tended to centralize their operations...在大规模生产的时代，跨国公司往往实行集权化经营。
例：The economy of the times made it difficult to support centralized rule.当时的经济状况不利于实行集权统治。

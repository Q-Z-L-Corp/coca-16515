N-UNCOUNT
联合（状态）；统一（状态）
Unity is the state of different areas or groups being joined together to form a single country or organization.
例：Senior politicians met today to discuss the future of European economic unity.高级政要今天会晤，探讨欧洲经济一体化的未来。
例：...German unity.德国的统一

N-UNCOUNT
团结；一致
When there is unity, people are in agreement and act together for a particular purpose.
例：...a renewed unity of purpose...目标的重新确认一致
例：Speakers at the rally mouthed sentiments of unity...集会上的发言人空泛地呼吁大家要团结一致。

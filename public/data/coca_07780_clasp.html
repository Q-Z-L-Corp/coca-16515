clasp 
英[klɑ:sp] 美[klæsp]
 
n.扣钩，扣环；握紧；拥抱；vt.& vi.扣住；紧拥；紧握；
 "

VERB
紧紧抓住;攥紧；抱紧
If you clasp someone or something, you hold them tightly in your hands or arms.
例：She clasped the children to her...她把孩子们紧紧地搂进怀里。
例：He paced the corridor, hands clasped behind his back.他双手紧扣，背在身后，在走廊里踱来踱去。

N-COUNT
扣环;搭扣
A clasp is a small device that fastens something.
例：...the clasp of her handbag.她手提包的搭扣

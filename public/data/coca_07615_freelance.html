freelance 
英[ˈfri:lɑ:ns] 美[ˈfrilæns]
 
n.自由作家；自由记者；adj.特约的；自由职业的；vi.当自由职业者；
 "

ADJ
自由职业的;独立的
Someone who does freelance work or who is, for example, a freelance journalist or photographer is not employed by one organization, but is paid for each piece of work they do by the organization they do it for.
例：Michael Cross is a freelance journalist...迈克尔·克罗斯是一名自由新闻记者。
例：Jill was starting to get some freelance writing jobs from trade magazines...吉尔开始从一些行业杂志接点自由撰稿的活儿。

N-COUNT
同freelancer
A freelance is the same as a freelancer .

VERB
从事自由职业;独立工作
If you freelance, you do freelance work.
例：She has freelanced as a writer and researcher.她从事自由职业，一边写作，一边搞研究。

ADV
(强调令人不快或负面的事或行为)彻头彻尾地，十足地，完全地
You use downright to emphasize unpleasant or bad qualities or behaviour.
例：...ideas that would have been downright dangerous if put into practice...如果付诸实践就会极度危险的想法
例：She was often downright rude to him.她常对他十分粗鲁。

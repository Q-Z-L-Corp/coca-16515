ADJ
(美国)全州范围的，遍及全州的
Statewide means across or throughout the whole of one of the states of the United States.
例：Each year they compete in a prominent statewide bicycle race...每年他们都要参加著名的全州自行车大赛。
例：These voters often determine the outcome of statewide elections.这些选民们往往决定着全州大选的结果。

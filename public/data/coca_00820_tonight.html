ADV
在今晚;在今夜
Tonight is used to refer to the evening of today or the night that follows today.
例：I'm at home tonight...我今晚在家。
例：Tonight, I think he proved to everybody what a great player he was...我认为今晚他向所有人证明了他是个多么出色的选手。

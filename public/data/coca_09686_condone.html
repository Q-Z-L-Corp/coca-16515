VERB
容忍，纵容（不道德行为）
If someone condones behaviour that is morally wrong, they accept it and allow it to happen.
例：I have never encouraged nor condoned violence...我从不煽动也不纵容暴力行为。
例：I couldn't condone what she was doing.我无法容忍她在做的事。

ADV
在（或往）后台
In a theatre, backstage refers to the areas behind the stage.
例：He went backstage and asked for her autograph...他跑到后台向她索要签名。
例：He met Jackson backstage at his concert in Leeds.他在利兹音乐会的后台见到了杰克逊。

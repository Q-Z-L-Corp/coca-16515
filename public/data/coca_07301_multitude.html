multitude 
英[ˈmʌltɪtju:d] 美[ˈmʌltɪtud]
 
n.大量，许多；大众，人群；
 "

QUANT
许多;大量;大批;大群
A multitude of things or people is a very large number of them.
例：There are a multitude of small quiet roads to cycle along...有很多可以骑车的宁静小路。
例：Addiction to drugs can bring a multitude of other problems.毒瘾会带来许多其他问题。

N-COUNT
一大群人;人群
You can refer to a very large number of people as a multitude .
例：...surrounded by a noisy multitude.被喧闹的人群围着
例：...the multitudes that throng around the Pope.围在主教周围的人群

N-COUNT-COLL
民众；大众；群众
You can refer to the great majority of people in a particular country or situation as the multitude or the multitudes .
例：The hideous truth was hidden from the multitude...向大众隐瞒了骇人听闻的真相。
例：It is our task to convince the multitudes that we are pursuing a lawful hobby in a lawful way.我们的任务是要让民众相信，我们是在用合法的方式来从事一种合法的爱好。

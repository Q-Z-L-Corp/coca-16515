VERB
笑话;嘲笑;奚落;羞辱
If someone taunts you, they say unkind or insulting things to you, especially about your weaknesses or failures.
例：A gang taunted a disabled man...一伙小混混嘲笑一位残疾人。
例：Other youths taunted him about his clothes.其他年轻人笑话他的衣服。

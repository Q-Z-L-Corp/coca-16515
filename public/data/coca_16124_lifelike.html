ADJ-GRADED
像有生命的
Something that is lifelike has the appearance of being alive.
例：...a lifelike doll.像真人的玩偶

ADJ-GRADED
（艺术品）逼真的，栩栩如生的，生动真切的
A lifelike work of art is so skilfully done that it appears very like the person or thing that it is supposed to represent.
例：The painting depicted a hall so lifelike that a casual observer might believe himself to be looking through an open door.这幅画描绘的厅堂如此逼真，若不细看，还真以为是从一扇开着的门往里看呢。

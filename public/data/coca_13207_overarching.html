ADJ
包罗万象的；影响一切的
You use overarching to indicate that you are talking about something that includes or affects everything or everyone.
例：The overarching question seems to be what happens when the US pulls out?...首要的问题看来在于美国撤军后事态将会如何。
例：Home ownership has been an overarching and innate desire of the British.拥有私宅是所有英国人心中压倒一切和根深蒂固的强烈愿望。

contagious 
英[kənˈteɪdʒəs] 美[kənˈtedʒəs]
 
adj.有传染性的；传染病的；有感染力的；会蔓延的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（病）接触传染的
A disease that is contagious can be caught by touching people or things that are infected with it.
例：...a highly contagious disease of the lungs.极具传染性的肺病

ADJ-GRADED
(感情、态度等)感染性的，会迅速蔓延开的
A feeling or attitude that is contagious spreads quickly among a group of people.
例：Laughing is contagious...笑是有感染力的。
例：Antonio has a contagious enthusiasm for the beautiful aspect of food.安东尼奥对食品赏心悦目这一方面的热情非常有感染性。

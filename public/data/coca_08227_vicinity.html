N-SING
邻近地区;附近
If something is in the vicinity of a particular place, it is near it.
例：There were a hundred or so hotels in the vicinity of the railway station...在火车站附近大约有100家旅馆。
例：The police roadblocks are still in place and the immediate vicinity of the house remains cordoned off.警察设置的路障还在，房子四周仍然用警戒线隔离着。

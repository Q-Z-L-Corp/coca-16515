N-COUNT
(探测光、热等的)传感器,探测设备,敏感元件
A sensor is an instrument which reacts to certain physical conditions or impressions such as heat or light, and which is used to provide information.
例：The latest Japanese vacuum cleaners contain sensors that detect the amount of dust and type of floor.日本的最新真空吸尘器配有可探测灰尘多少和地板类型的传感器。
例：...a light sensor.光传感器

lope 
英[ləʊp] 美[loʊp]
 
vi.（尤指动物）轻跳着奔跑；n.大步慢跑；
 "

VERB
（轻松而大步地）慢跑，缓跑
If a person or animal lopes somewhere, they run in an easy and relaxed way, taking long steps.
例：He was loping across the sand toward Nancy...他迈着大步穿过沙滩缓缓向南希跑去。
例：Matty saw him go loping off, running low.马蒂看着他大步慢慢跑远。

ADJ-GRADED
恢复健康（或体力、精神）的
Something that is restorative makes you feel healthier, stronger, or more cheerful after you have been feeling tired, weak, or miserable.
例：She opened the door to her bedroom, thinking how restorative a hot bath would feel tonight.她打开了卧室的门，想着今晚洗个热水澡该多么令人惬意。

VERB
使最优化；使最有效率；使尽可能完善
To optimize a plan, system, or machine means to arrange or design it so that it operates as smoothly and efficiently as possible.
例：Doctors are concentrating on understanding the disease better, and on optimizing the treatment...医生正致力于更好地了解这种疾病，并使治疗方法尽可能完善。
例：The new systems have been optimised for running Microsoft Windows.已经对这些新系统进行了优化设置，以便运行微软视窗。

VERB
充分利用
To optimize a situation or opportunity means to get as much advantage or benefit from it as you can.
例：What can you do to optimize your family situation?你如何能充分利用自己的家庭条件？

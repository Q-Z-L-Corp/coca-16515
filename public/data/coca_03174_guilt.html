guilt 
英[gɪlt] 美[ɡɪlt]
 
n.有罪，犯罪行为，罪恶；[法律] 轻罪，过失；内疚，知罪；自责；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
内疚；悔恨；负罪感
Guilt is an unhappy feeling that you have because you have done something wrong or think that you have done something wrong.
例：Her emotions had ranged from anger to guilt in the space of a few seconds...几秒钟的时间里，她经历了从愤怒到内疚的情感变化。
例：Some cancer patients experience strong feelings of guilt.一些癌症病人懊悔不已。

N-UNCOUNT
犯罪；罪行；有罪
Guilt is the fact that you have done something wrong or illegal.
例：The trial is concerned only with the determination of guilt according to criminal law...依照刑法规定，审判仅涉及定罪。
例：You weren't convinced of Mr Matthews' guilt.你不相信马修斯先生有罪。

(社会地位、财富、机会等)不平等，不均等
Inequality is the difference in social status, wealth, or opportunity between people or groups.
例：People are concerned about social inequality...人们很关注社会不平等问题。
例：Blacks have been hurt by racial inequalities in housing and education.住房与教育方面的种族不平等现象让黑人深受伤害。

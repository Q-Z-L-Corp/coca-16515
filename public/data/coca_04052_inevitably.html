ADV
不可避免地;必然(发生)地
If something will inevitably happen, it is certain to happen and cannot be prevented or avoided.
例：Technological changes will inevitably lead to unemployment...技术变革必然会导致失业。
例：Inevitably, the proposal is running into difficulties.提案不可避免地遭遇了麻烦。

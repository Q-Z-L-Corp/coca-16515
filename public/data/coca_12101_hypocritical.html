hypocritical 
英[ˌhɪpə'krɪtɪkl] 美[ˌhɪpəˈkrɪtɪkəl]
 
adj.伪善的；虚伪的；伪善者的；言不由衷的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
虚伪的;伪善的
If you accuse someone of being hypocritical, you mean that they pretend to have qualities, beliefs, or feelings that they do not really have.
例：It would be hypocritical to say I travel at 70mph simply because that is the law...如果因为法律规定的时速是70英里我就说自己的车速是这么高，那就是虚伪了。
例：If someone is being hypocritical then it is fair to expose that.如果有人虚伪矫饰，那就应该暴露他的真面目。

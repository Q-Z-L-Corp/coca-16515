roomy 
英[ˈru:mi] 美[ˈrumi, ˈrʊmi]
 
adj.宽敞的，宽大的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
宽敞的;空间大的
If you describe a place as roomy, you mean that you like it because it is large inside and you can move around freely and comfortably.
例：The car is roomy and a good choice for anyone who needs to carry equipment.这种车非常宽敞，对于需要携带装备的人来说是不错的选择。

ADJ-GRADED
(衣服)宽松的
If you describe a piece of clothing as roomy, you mean that you like it because it is large and fits loosely.
例：...roomy jackets.宽松的夹克

desk 
英[desk] 美[dɛsk]
 
n.书桌，办公桌；服务台；部门；（美）讲道台；adj.书桌的，书桌上用的；
 "

N-COUNT
书桌;写字台;办公桌
A desk is a table, often with drawers, which you sit at to write or work.

N-SING
(宾馆、医院、机场等的)服务台，问询台
The place in a hotel, hospital, airport, or other building where you check in or obtain information is referred to as a particular desk .
例：I spoke to the girl on the reception desk...我跟服务台的女孩说了一下。
例：A map and a bird-watchers' field checklist are available at the front desk.前台有地图和观鸟地点一览表可供拿取。

N-SING
(广播公司、报社、杂志社等的)部，室，组
A particular department of a broadcasting company, or of a newspaper or magazine company, can be referred to as a particular desk .
例：Let our news desk know as quickly as possible on 414 3926...请尽快拨打电话 4143926 通知我们的新闻部。
例：Over now to Simon Ingram at the sports desk.现在交给体育组的西蒙·英格拉姆。

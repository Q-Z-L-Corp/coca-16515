hero 
英[ˈhɪərəʊ] 美[ˈhɪroʊ]
 
n.英雄，勇士；男主角；（古代神话中的）神人，半神的勇士；
 "

N-COUNT
（书、戏剧、电影或故事中品行端正的）男主角，男主人公
The hero of a book, play, film, or story is the main male character, who usually has good qualities.
例：The hero of Doctor Zhivago dies in 1929.《日瓦戈医生》的男主角于1929年去世。
例：...the author's decision to make his hero a photographer.作者将男主角设计成一名摄影师的决定

N-COUNT
（尤指男性）英雄，英雄人物，豪杰，勇士
A hero is someone, especially a man, who has done something brave, new, or good, and who is therefore greatly admired by a lot of people.
例：He called Mr Mandela a hero who had inspired millions.他称曼德拉先生为鼓舞了数百万民众的英雄。
例：...the goalscoring hero of the British hockey team...英国曲棍球队的得分英雄

N-COUNT
偶像;崇拜对象;心目中的英雄
If you describe someone as your hero, you mean that you admire them a great deal, usually because of a particular quality or skill that they have.
例：My boyhood hero was Bobby Charlton...我童年时代的偶像是博比·查尔顿。
例：No matter, he remained the hero of the crowds.不要紧，他仍是众人心目中的英雄。

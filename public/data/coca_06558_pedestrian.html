pedestrian 
英[pəˈdestriən] 美[pəˈdɛstriən]
 
n.步行者；行人；adj.徒步的；平淡无奇的；一般的；
 "

N-COUNT
（尤指城镇的）步行者，行人
A pedestrian is a person who is walking, especially in a town or city, rather than travelling in a vehicle.
例：In Los Angeles a pedestrian is a rare spectacle...在洛杉矶很少见到步行者。
例：More than a third of all pedestrian injuries are to children.所有受伤的行人中有1/3以上是儿童。

ADJ-GRADED
平庸的；乏味的；平淡无奇的
If you describe something as pedestrian, you mean that it is ordinary and not at all interesting.
例：His style is so pedestrian that the book becomes a real bore...他的文风十分平庸，那本书乏味至极。
例：I drove home contemplating my own more pedestrian lifestyle.我开车回家，一路上思考着自己更为乏味的生活方式。

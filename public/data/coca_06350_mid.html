PREFIX
(用于构成名词或形容词)中间，中部，中期
Mid- is used to form nouns or adjectives that refer to the middle part of a particular period of time, or the middle point of a particular place.
例：...the mid-eighteenth century...18世纪中叶
例：Davis is in her mid-thirties.戴维丝35岁左右。

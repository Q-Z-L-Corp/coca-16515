 "

N-COUNT
复制品;仿制品
A replica of something such as a statue, building, or weapon is an accurate copy of it.
例：...a human-sized replica of the Statue of Liberty...真人大小的自由女神像复制品
例：Royce Hall was an exact replica of the basilica of Sant Ambrogio in Milan...罗伊斯大厅是位于米兰的圣安布洛乔大教堂的原样复制建筑。

N-COUNT
翻版;酷似的人
If you say that one person is a replica of another, you mean that the first person looks very like the second.
例：Tina as a child was a replica of her mother.蒂娜小的时候和她妈妈简直一模一样。

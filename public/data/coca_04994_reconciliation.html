reconciliation 
英[ˌrekənsɪliˈeɪʃn] 美[ˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeʃən]
 
n.和解，调停；一致；服从，顺从；和谐；
 "

N-VAR
和解;调和;调解
Reconciliation between two people or countries who have quarrelled is the process of their becoming friends again. A reconciliation is an instance of this.
例：...an appeal for reconciliation between Catholics and Protestants...天主教徒和新教徒之间达成和解的诉求
例：The couple have separated but he wants a reconciliation...夫妇俩已经分居了，但是他想要和解。

N-SING
（两种貌似对立的信仰、事实或要求的）和谐，调和，一致
The reconciliation of two beliefs, facts, or demands that seem to be opposed is the process of finding a way in which they can both be true or both be successful.
例：...the ideal of democracy based upon a reconciliation of the values of equality and liberty.建立在平等和自由两种价值观和谐统一基础上的民主理想

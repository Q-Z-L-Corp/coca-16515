feel-good 
英[fi:l ɡud] 美[fil ɡʊd]
 
phr.愉悦感，对未来的乐观态度（新闻用语）；adj.（电影）令人愉悦的；
 "

ADJ
(电影)令人愉悦的
A feel-good film is a film which presents people and life in a way which makes the people who watch it feel happy and optimistic.
例：This could be the feel-good movie of the autumn.这可以称得上是今秋令人赏心悦目的影片了。
例：...a bright and enjoyable feelgood romance.基调明快、让人乐观向上的爱情故事

PHRASE
愉悦感，对未来的乐观态度（新闻用语)
When journalists refer to the feel-good factor, they mean that people are feeling hopeful and optimistic about the future.
例：There were obvious signs of the feel-good factor in the last survey taken in the wake of the election result.选举结果公布后的最近一次调查显示，人们普遍对未来持乐观态度。

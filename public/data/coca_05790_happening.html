happening 
英[ˈhæpənɪŋ] 美[ ˈhæpənɪŋ]
 
n.事件；意外发生的事；v.发生(happen的现在分词)；碰巧；
 "

N-COUNT
发生的事情;意外事件;难以解释的事
Happenings are things that happen, often in a way that is unexpected or hard to explain.
例：The Budapest office plans to hire freelance reporters to cover the latest happenings.布达佩斯办事处计划聘用自由撰稿记者来报道最近发生的一些事件。

ADJ-GRADED
热闹的;活跃的;新潮的
If you describe something or someone as happening, you mean that they are exciting or lively, and involved in the newest fashions or trends.
例：...a definitive area-by-area guide to the hip and happening bands.介绍各个地区新潮流行乐队的权威指南
例：...the most happening place at the moment, the Que Club.时下最热门的去处——Que俱乐部

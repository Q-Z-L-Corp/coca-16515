ADJ
即将发生的;逼近的
An impending event is one that is going to happen very soon.
例：On the morning of the expedition I awoke with a feeling of impending disaster...出发远征的那个早上,我醒来就有一种灾难即将来临的感觉。
例：He'd spoken to Simon that morning of his impending marriage.那个早上他已经对西蒙讲过他即将结婚的事了。

N-COUNT
厚块;大块
Chunks of something are thick solid pieces of it.
例：They had to be careful of floating chunks of ice.他们必须当心大块浮冰。
例：...a chunk of meat...一大块肉

N-COUNT
大量;大部分
A chunk of something is a large amount or large part of it.
例：The company owns a chunk of farmland near Gatwick Airport.该公司拥有盖特威克机场周边的大片农田。

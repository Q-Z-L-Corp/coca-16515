scholarship

N-COUNT
奖学金
If you get a scholarship to a school or university, your studies are paid for by the school or university or by some other organization.
例：He got a scholarship to the Pratt Institute of Art.他获得了普拉特艺术学院的奖学金。
例：...scholarships for women over 30.为 30 岁以上女性设立的奖学金

N-UNCOUNT
学术;学问;学术研究
Scholarship is serious academic study and the knowledge that is obtained from it.
例：I want to take advantage of your lifetime of scholarship.我希望能够用到您一生的学识。

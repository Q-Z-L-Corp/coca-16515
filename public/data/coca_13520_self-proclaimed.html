self-proclaimed 
英[ˈselfprəˈkleimd] 美[ˌsɛlfproˈklemd, -prə-]
 
adj.自称的；
 "

ADJ
自称的;自命的;自封的
Self-proclaimed is used to show that someone has given themselves a particular title or status rather than being given it by other people.
例：...a self-proclaimed expert...自封的专家
例：He is President of his own self-proclaimed republic.他是其自己宣布成立的共和国的总统。

ADJ
不吝承认的;无耻自称的
Self-proclaimed is used to show that someone says themselves that they are a type of person which most people would be embarrassed or ashamed to be.
例：One of the prisoners is a self-proclaimed racist who opened fire on a crowd four years ago.其中一名囚犯恬不知耻地自称是种族主义者，他曾在四年前向一群人开枪射击。
例：...a self-proclaimed Anglophile.不吝承认的崇英者

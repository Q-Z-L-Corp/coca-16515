ADJ
湿透的;浸透的
If someone or something gets soaked or soaked through, water or some other liquid makes them extremely wet.
例：I have to check my tent — it got soaked last night in the storm...我得检查一下帐篷——昨天夜里暴风雨把它淋透了。
例：My goodness, you're soaked through. Where's your car?...天哪，你都成落汤鸡了。你的车呢？

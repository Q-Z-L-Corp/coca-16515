dotted 
英[ˈdɒtɪd] 美[ˈdɑtɪd]
 
adj.有点的，星罗棋布的；v.点缀(dot的过去式和过去分词)；布满；（口）打，击（某人）；以小圆点标出（某物）；
 "

ADJ
由点组成的
A dotted line is a line which is made of a row of dots.
例：Cut along the dotted line.沿虚线裁开。

ADJ
遍布的;布满的
If a place or object is dotted with things, it has many of those things scattered over its surface.
例：The maps were dotted with the names of small towns.地图上散布着小城镇的名字。
例：...a pond that's dotted with water lilies.开满睡莲的池塘

ADJ
散布的;遍布的
If things are dotted around a place, they can be found in many different parts of that place.
例：Many pieces of sculpture are dotted around the house.多件雕塑作品散落在房子各处。

ADJ
遍布大圆点的
You use dotted to describe something that is covered with large dots.
例：...a dotted bow tie.大圆点蝶形领结

torrent 
英[ˈtɒrənt] 美[ˈtɔrənt]
 
n.奔流，急流；爆发，迸发；连续不断；（质问等的）连发；
 "

N-COUNT
急流;激流;洪流
A torrent is a lot of water falling or flowing rapidly or violently.
例：Torrents of water gushed into the reservoir...急流涌入了水库。
例：The rain came down in torrents, and we could see nothing…大雨倾盆而下，我们看不见任何东西。

N-COUNT
(谩骂、问题等的)连发，迸发
A torrent of abuse or questions is a lot of abuse or questions directed continuously at someone.
例：He turned round and directed a torrent of abuse at me.他转过身来冲我劈头盖脸地骂了一通。
例：…a £45,000 offer which prompted a torrent of criticism in the media.激起媒体一片指责的 4.5 万英镑的出价

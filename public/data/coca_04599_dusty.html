dusty 
英[ˈdʌsti] 美[ˈdʌsti]
 
adj.布满灰尘的；尘状的；浅灰色的；枯燥无味的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(通常指因为少雨而)布满灰尘的,满是尘土的
If places, roads, or other things outside are dusty, they are covered with tiny bits of earth or sand, usually because it has not rained for a long time.
例：They started strolling down the dusty road in the moonlight.在月光下他们开始沿着满是尘土的路漫步。
例：...a dusty old car.落满灰尘的老爷车

ADJ-GRADED
(房子、物件等)覆有灰尘的,尘封的
If a room, house, or object is dusty, it is covered with very small pieces of dirt.
例：...a dusty attic...满是灰尘的阁楼
例：The books looked faded, dusty and unused.那些书看上去褪了色,尘封已久。

cube 
英[kju:b] 美[kjub]
 
n.立方形，立方体；立方，三次幂；小房间；vt.求…的立方；把（食物）切成小方块；测定…的体积；
 "

N-COUNT
立方体;立方形的东西
A cube is a solid object with six square surfaces which are all the same size.
例：...cold water with ice cubes in it...放了冰块的冷水
例：...a box of sugar cubes...一盒方糖

VERB
把(食物)切成小方块
When you cube food, you cut it into cube-shaped pieces.
例：Remove the seeds and stones and cube the flesh...把籽和果核弄掉，将果肉切成小方块。
例：Serve with cubed bread.和小面包块一起食用。

N-COUNT
立方;三次幂
The cube of a number is another number that is produced by multiplying the first number by itself twice. For example, the cube of 2 is 8.

roam 
英[rəʊm] 美[roʊm]
 
vt.& vi.漫游；漫步；n.漫步，漫游；
 "

VERB
漫步;漫游;游荡;闲逛
If you roam an area or roam around it, you wander or travel around it without having a particular purpose.
例：Barefoot children roamed the streets...光脚丫的孩子们在街上游荡。
例：They're roaming around the country shooting at anything that moves...他们在乡下到处转悠，看到活动的东西就开枪。

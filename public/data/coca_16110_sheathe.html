sheathe 
英[ʃi:ð] 美[ʃið]
 
vt.将（刀、剑等）插入鞘；包，覆盖；
 "

VERB
用…盖住;将…包起来
If something is sheathed in a material or other covering, it is closely covered with it.
例：The television was sheathed in a snug coverlet.电视机用一个床罩严严实实地盖住了。
例：...her long legs, sheathed in sheer black tights.她那裹在一条纯黑的紧身裤下的修长的双腿

VERB
将(刀)插入鞘中
When someone sheathes a knife, they put it in its sheath.
例：He sheathed the knife and strapped it to his shin.他将刀插入鞘中,绑在小腿上。

rural 
英[ˈrʊərəl] 美[ˈrʊrəl]
 
adj.乡下的，农村的；田园的；地方的；农业的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
乡下的;乡村的;农村的
Rural places are far away from large towns or cities.
例：These plants have a tendency to grow in the more rural areas.这些植物大多生长在偏远的乡村。
例：...the closure of rural schools.农村学校的关闭

ADJ-GRADED
有乡村特色的;田园的;有乡土气息的
Rural means having features which are typical of areas that are far away from large towns or cities.
例：...the old rural way of life...昔日的田园生活
例：He spoke with a heavy rural accent.他说话带着浓重的农村口音。

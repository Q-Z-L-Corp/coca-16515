VERB
理解;领会
If something is construed in a particular way, its nature or meaning is interpreted in that way.
例：What may seem helpful behaviour to you can be construed as interference by others...在你看来似乎是施助的行为在别人眼中可能就会被解读成一种干涉。
例：He may construe the approach as a hostile act...他也许会将这种做法理解为敌视行为。

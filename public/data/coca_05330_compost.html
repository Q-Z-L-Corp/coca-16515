compost 
英[ˈkɒmpɒst] 美[ˈkɑmpoʊst]
 
n.堆肥；混合，合成；vt.施堆肥；把（蔬菜等）制成堆肥；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
堆肥;混合肥料
Compost is a mixture of decayed plants and vegetable waste which is added to the soil to help plants grow.
例：...a small compost heap.一小堆堆肥

N-MASS
(在花盆中栽培用的)特制土
Compost is specially treated soil that you buy and use to grow seeds and plants in pots.

VERB
把…制成堆肥
To compost things such as unwanted bits of plants means to make them into compost.
例：Cut down and compost spent cucumbers, tomatoes and other crops...将已收获过的黄瓜、西红柿和其他作物的秧蔓割下，制成堆肥。
例：All garden waste should be composted and returned to the garden.应该把花园里所有枯萎的花草制成堆肥，然后再用它们给花园施肥。

baritone 
英[ˈbærɪtəʊn] 美[ˈbærɪtoʊn]
 
n.男中音，男中音歌手；上低音部；上低音乐器；
 "

N-COUNT
男中音歌手；男中音歌唱家
In music, a baritone is a man with a fairly deep singing voice that is lower than that of a tenor but higher than that of a bass.
例：...the young American baritone Monte Pederson.年轻的美国男中音歌唱家蒙特·佩德森

N-SING
低沉悦耳的声音
If a man has a baritone speaking voice, his voice is low and pleasant to listen to.
例：...a baritone voice...低沉悦耳的嗓音
例：He speaks in a melodious baritone.他的声音浑厚而有磁性。

ADJ
与…成比例的
If one amount is proportional to another, the two amounts increase and decrease at the same rate so there is always the same relationship between them.
例：Loss of weight is directly proportional to the rate at which the disease is progressing.体重的下降与病情发展的速度成正比。
例：...a proportional fee based on the final sale price.与最后销售价格成比例的费用

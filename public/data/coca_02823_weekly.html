ADJ
一周一次的;每周的
A weekly event or publication happens or appears once a week or every week.
例：Each course comprises 10-12 informal weekly meetings...每门课程都包括10至12次每周一次的非正式讨论会。
例：We go and do the weekly shopping every Thursday.我们每周四去采购一次。

ADJ
(数量或费用)按周计算的
Weekly quantities or rates relate to a period of one week.
例：In addition to my weekly wage, I got a lot of tips.除了每周的薪水外，我还能得到不少小费。

N-COUNT
周报;周刊
A weekly is a newspaper or magazine that is published once a week.
例：Two of the four national daily papers are to become weeklies.4份全国性日报中有两份将改为周报。

ADV
独自地;单独地;无人帮助地;单枪匹马地
If you do something single-handed, you do it on your own, without help from anyone else.
例：I brought up my seven children single-handed...我独自一人抚养了 7 个孩子。
例：She changed the face of British politics almost single-handed.她几乎是一手改变了英国政治的面貌。

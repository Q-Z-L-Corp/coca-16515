trickle 
英[ˈtrɪkl] 美[ˈtrɪkəl]
 
v.滴；细细地流；使淌下；慢慢地移动；n.滴；涓流；细流；稀稀疏疏缓慢来往的东西；
 "

V-ERG
(使)滴;(使)淌
When a liquid trickles, or when you trickle it, it flows slowly in very small amounts.
例：A tear trickled down the old man's cheek...一滴眼泪沿着老人的脸颊淌了下来。
例：Trickle water gently over the back of your baby's head...往婴儿的脑后部轻撩些水。

VERB
(小批地)慢慢移动
When people or things trickle in a particular direction, they move there slowly in small groups or amounts, rather than all together.
例：Some donations are already trickling in.一些捐赠已经陆续到来。

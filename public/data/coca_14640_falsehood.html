falsehood 
英[ˈfɔ:lshʊd] 美[ˈfɔlsˌhʊd]
 
n.<正> 谎言，假话，虚假；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
虚伪性;虚假性;谬误
Falsehood is the quality or fact of being untrue or of being a lie.
例：She called the verdict a victory of truth over falsehood.她将这一判决称为真理对谬误的胜利。

N-COUNT
谎言
A falsehood is a lie.
例：He accused them of knowingly spreading falsehoods about him.他指责他们故意散布有关他的谎言。

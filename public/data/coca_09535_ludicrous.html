ADJ-GRADED
愚蠢的；荒唐的；可笑的
If you describe something as ludicrous, you are emphasizing that you think it is foolish, unreasonable, or unsuitable.
例：It was ludicrous to suggest that the visit could be kept secret...认为此次访问能够保密是可笑的。
例：It's a completely ludicrous idea.这主意真是荒唐透顶。

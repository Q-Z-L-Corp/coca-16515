 "

N-VAR
总数；总额
The amount of something is how much there is, or how much you have, need, or get.
例：He needs that amount of money to survive...他需要那样一笔钱以生存下去。
例：I still do a certain amount of work for them...我仍然为他们做一些工作。

VERB
合计；共计
If something amounts to a particular total, all the parts of it add up to that total.
例：Consumer spending on sports-related items amounted to £9.75 billion.消费者在体育相关产品上的花费共计97.5亿英镑。

PHRASE
大量
If you say that there is any amount of something, there is a lot of it. If you say that there are any amount of people or things, there are many people or things.
例：I'm able to lay my hands on any amount of cash at a minute's notice...多少钱我都可以马上弄到。
例：There are any amount of clubs you could join.有无数个俱乐部你可以加入。

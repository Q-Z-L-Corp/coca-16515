N-UNCOUNT
正常状态;常态
Normalcy is a situation in which everything is normal.
例：He said that the government was committed to restore normalcy and hold elections in the Punjab...他说政府会致力于恢复正常秩序，并在旁遮普省进行选举。
例：Underneath this image of normalcy, addiction threatened to rip this family apart.在这一切正常的表象下潜伏的是将使这个家庭四分五裂的毒瘾。

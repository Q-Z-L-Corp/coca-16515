spotty 
英[ˈspɒti] 美[ˈspɑti]
 
adj.多粉刺的（尤指脸上）；带斑点的，有污迹的；adv.多斑点地；n.有污迹，带斑点；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
脸上有斑点的;多粉刺的
Someone who is spotty has spots on their face.
例：She was rather fat, and her complexion was muddy and spotty.她比较胖，脸色暗淡、多斑。

ADJ-GRADED
时好时坏的
Something that is spotty does not stay the same but is sometimes good and sometimes bad.
例：He quit in 1981—had a spotty political career...他在1981年辞职了——其政治生涯几经沉浮。
例：His attendance record was spotty.他的出勤记录时好时差。

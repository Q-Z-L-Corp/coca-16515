chatter 
英[ˈtʃætə(r)] 美[ˈtʃætɚ]
 
vi.唠叨，喋喋不休；（鸟等）鸣，啁啾；（牙齿，机器等）振动，打颤；运动时摇摆或嘎嘎作响；vt.不加思索地说出；n.闲聊；咔哒声；（动物的）啁啾声；潺潺流水声；
 "

VERB
唠叨;喋喋不休
If you chatter, you talk quickly and continuously, usually about things which are not important.
例：Everyone's chattering away in different languages...大家操着不同的语言喋喋不休地说着。
例：Erica was friendly and chattered about Andrew's children...埃丽卡很和气，不停地念叨着安德鲁的孩子们。

VERB
(牙齿)打战，咯咯响
If your teeth chatter, they keep knocking together because you are very cold or very nervous.
例：She was so cold her teeth chattered.她冻得牙齿咯咯响。

VERB
发出咯咯声(或嗒嗒声)
If objects chatter, they make short repeated sounds.
例：The telex chattered all day and night with news bulletins.电传机没日没夜“嗒嗒嗒”地接收新闻稿。

VERB
(鸟)鸣叫，啁啾;(动物)唧唧叫，吱吱叫
When birds or animals chatter, they make high-pitched noises.
例：Birds were chattering somewhere.鸟儿在某处唧唧喳喳地叫着。

N-UNCOUNT
囚禁;关押;拘禁
Captivity is the state of being kept imprisoned or enclosed.
例：The great majority of barn owls are reared in captivity...大多数仓鸮都是笼养的。
例：An American missionary was released today after more than two months of captivity...一位美国传教士在被关押两个多月后于今天获释。

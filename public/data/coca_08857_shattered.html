ADJ-GRADED
无比惊愕难过的;遭受极大打击的
If you are shattered by something, you are extremely shocked and upset about it.
例：It is desperately sad news and I am absolutely shattered to hear it.这是个令人悲痛欲绝的消息，我听后彻底崩溃了。

ADJ-GRADED
筋疲力尽的
If you say you are shattered, you mean you are extremely tired and have no energy left.
例：He was shattered and too tired to concentrate on schoolwork.他筋疲力尽，累得都无法集中注意力做作业。

photograph 
英[ˈfəʊtəgrɑ:f] 美[ˈfoʊtəgræf]
 
n.照片，相片；vt.& vi.为…拍照；拍照，摄影；成为拍照对象，在照片上显得；
 "

N-COUNT
照片；相片
A photograph is a picture that is made using a camera.
例：He wants to take some photographs of the house...他想给这幢房子拍一些照片。
例：Her photograph appeared on the front page of The New York Times.她的照片登在了《纽约时报》的头版上。

VERB
给…拍照；为…拍照
When you photograph someone or something, you use a camera to obtain a picture of them.
例：She photographed the children...她给孩子们拍了照。
例：I hate being photographed...我讨厌照相。

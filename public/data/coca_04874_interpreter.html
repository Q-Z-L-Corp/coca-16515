interpreter 
英[ɪnˈtɜ:prɪtə(r)] 美[ɪnˈtɜrprɪtə(r)]
 
n.解释者；口译译员；[军事]判读员；[自]翻译器；
 "

N-COUNT
口译者;口译员
An interpreter is a person whose job is to translate what someone is saying into another language.
例：Aristide spoke to the press through an interpreter.阿里斯蒂德通过口译员向媒体发表讲话。

N-COUNT
演奏者;表演者
The interpreter of something such as a piece of music is the person who performs it.
例：Over the years Freni has been one of the supreme interpreters of Puccini's heroines.多年来弗雷尼一直是普契尼歌剧女主角的最佳表演者之一。

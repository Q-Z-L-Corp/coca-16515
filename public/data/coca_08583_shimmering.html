英['ʃɪmərɪŋ] 美['ʃɪmərɪŋ]

VERB
闪着微弱的光;闪烁
If something shimmers, it shines with a faint, unsteady light or has an unclear, unsteady appearance.
例：The lights shimmered on the water...水面上波光粼粼。
例：The trees shimmered in the afternoon heat.午后的热浪中,树木闪着微光。

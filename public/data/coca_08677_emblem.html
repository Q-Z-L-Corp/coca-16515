N-COUNT
（国家或组织的）徽章，纹章
An emblem is a design representing a country or organization.
例：...the emblem of the Soviet Union.苏联国徽
例：...the Red Cross emblem.红十字会徽章

N-COUNT
象征;标志
An emblem is something that represents a quality or idea.
例：The eagle was an emblem of strength and courage.鹰是力量和勇气的象征。

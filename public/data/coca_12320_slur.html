N-COUNT
毁谤;诋毁;侮辱性的言辞
A slur is an insulting remark which could damage someone's reputation.
例：This is yet another slur on the integrity of the Metropolitan Police.这是对伦敦市警察局刚正廉明作风的又一次诽谤。
例：...racial slurs.种族毁谤的言论

V-ERG
(由于醉酒、生病或困倦等)含糊不清地说，大着舌头说
If someone slurs their speech or if their speech slurs, they do not pronounce each word clearly, because they are drunk, ill, or sleepy.
例：He repeated himself and slurred his words more than usual...他重复了一遍，说得更含糊了。
例：The newscaster's speech began to slur...新闻广播员的话音变得含糊不清起来。

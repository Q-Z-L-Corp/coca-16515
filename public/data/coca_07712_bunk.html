bunk 
英[bʌŋk] 美[bʌŋk]
 
n.（车、船等的）铺位；睡觉处，卧处；牲口食槽；空话，废话；vi.睡在铺上；合铺，合住；vt.为…提供铺位；
 "

N-COUNT
（尤指船上或车上的）铺位，卧铺
A bunk is a bed that is fixed to a wall, especially in a ship or caravan.
例：He left his bunk and went up on deck again.他离开自己的铺位再次走到甲板上。

N-UNCOUNT
胡说；谎言
If you describe something as bunk, you think that it is foolish or untrue.
例：...Henry Ford's opinion that 'history is bunk'.亨利·福特认为“历史都是谎言”的观点

PHRASE
不辞而别；溜走
If you do a bunk, you suddenly leave a place without telling anyone.

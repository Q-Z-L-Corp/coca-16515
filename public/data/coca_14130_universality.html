universality 
英[ˌju:nɪvɜ:'sælətɪ] 美[ˌjunɪvəˈsælətɪ]
 
n.普遍性；通用性；普适性；一般性；
 "

ADJ
普遍的；全体的
Something that is universal relates to everyone in the world or everyone in a particular group or society.
例：The insurance industry has produced its own proposals for universal health care...保险业提出了自己的全民医疗保健方案。
例：The desire to look attractive is universal.爱美之心，人皆有之。

ADJ
宇宙的;全世界的
Something that is universal affects or relates to every part of the world or the universe.
例：...universal diseases.全球性的疾病

N-COUNT
普遍原则；通用原理
A universal is a principle that applies in all cases or a characteristic that is present in all members of a particular class.
例：There are no economic universals.没有普遍适用的经济学原理。

ADJ-GRADED

You use lengthy to describe an event or process which lasts for a long time.
例：...a lengthy meeting.冗长的会议
例：...the lengthy process of filling out passport application forms.填写护照申请表的漫长过程

（报告、文章等）长篇大论的，冗长的
A lengthy report, article, book, or document contains a lot of speech, writing, or other material.
例：...a lengthy report from the Council of Ministers...部长会议发布的冗长报告

contemporary 
英[kənˈtemprəri] 美[kənˈtempəreri]
 
adj.当代的，现代的；同时代的，同属一个时期的；n.同代人；同辈人；同龄人；当代人；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
当代的;现代的
Contemporary things are modern and relate to the present time.
例：She writes a lot of contemporary music for people like Whitney Houston...她给像惠特尼·休斯敦这样的人写了很多当代音乐作品。
例：Perhaps he should have a more updated look, a more contemporary style...也许他应该有一个更新的形象，更具时代性的风格。

ADJ
同时发生的;同时期的;同时代的
Contemporary people or things were alive or happened at the same time as something else you are talking about.
例：...drawing upon official records and the reports of contemporary witnesses.利用官方的记录与当时的见证者的报告

N-COUNT
同时期的人;同时代的人
Someone's contemporary is a person who is or was alive at the same time as them.
例：Like most of my contemporaries, I grew up in a vastly different world.和大多数同辈人一样，我生长在一个截然不同的世界。
例：...a glossary of musical terms found in Shakespeare and his contemporaries.莎士比亚及其同时代人音乐术语汇编

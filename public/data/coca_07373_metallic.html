metallic 
英[məˈtælɪk] 美[məˈtælɪk]
 
adj.金属的；金属性的；金属制的；含金属的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
金属撞击般的；叮当声的
A metallic sound is like the sound of one piece of metal hitting another.
例：There was a metallic click and the gates swung open...随着一声金属碰击的咔嗒声，大门打开了。
例：It gave a metallic clang, like a cracked bell.它发出了金属碰击般的哐啷声，跟破钟似的。

ADJ-GRADED
尖厉刺耳的;难听的
A metallic voice has a harsh unpleasant sound.
例：...that creaking metallic voice of hers.她那尖厉刺耳的嗓音

ADJ
(涂料、颜色)有金属光泽的，金属般闪亮的
Metallic paint or colours shine like metal.
例：He had painted all the wood with metallic silver paint...他给所有的木材都涂上了一层闪耀着金属光泽的银漆。
例：Metallic finishes are seen as upmarket.金属般闪亮的罩面漆被认为是高档漆。

ADJ-GRADED
金属味的
Something that tastes metallic has a bitter unpleasant taste.
例：There was a metallic taste at the back of his throat.他喉咙深处有一股金属味儿。

ADJ
含金属的；金属制的
Metallic means consisting entirely or partly of metal.
例：Even the smallest metallic object, whether a nail file or cigarette lighter, is immediately confiscated...即便是最小的金属物品，不管是指甲锉刀还是打火机，都会马上被没收。
例：Place the salmon in a nonstick metallic dish.将鲑鱼放在不粘底的金属盘内。

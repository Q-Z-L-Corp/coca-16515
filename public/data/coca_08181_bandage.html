bandage 
英[ˈbændɪdʒ] 美[ˈbændɪdʒ]

 "

N-COUNT
绷带
A bandage is a long strip of cloth which is wrapped around a wounded part of someone's body to protect or support it.
例：We put some ointment and a bandage on his knee...我们在他的膝盖上涂了些药膏并打上了绷带。
例：His chest was swathed in bandages.他的胸部缠着绷带。

VERB
用绷带包扎
If you bandage a wound or part of someone's body, you tie a bandage around it.
例：Apply a dressing to the wound and bandage it.给伤口盖上敷料并用绷带包扎起来。
例：...a bandaged hand.缠着绷带的手

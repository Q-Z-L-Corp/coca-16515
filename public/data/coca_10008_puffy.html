ADJ-GRADED
（尤指脸部）肿胀的，浮肿的
If a part of someone's body, especially their face, is puffy, it has a round, swollen appearance.
例：Her cheeks were puffy with crying.她哭得面颊浮肿。
例：...dark-ringed puffy eyes.眼圈发黑、眼泡肿起

ADJ-GRADED
（云朵）白色棉团状的
If you describe a cloud as puffy, you mean that it looks like a large ball of white cotton wool. Puffy clouds appear during fine weather.
例：The sea was blue, and white puffy clouds sailed by.大海一片蔚蓝，朵朵白云飘过。

choke 
英[tʃəʊk] 美[tʃoʊk]
 
vt.& vi.阻止，扼止；填塞；使窒息；哽咽；n.窒息，哽咽；拥塞；（车辆发动机的）阻风门；
 "

V-ERG
(使)噎住;(使)哽住;(使)卡住
When you choke or when something chokes you, you cannot breathe properly or get enough air into your lungs.
例：The coffee was almost too hot to swallow and made him choke for a moment...咖啡烫得几乎咽不下去，呛得他好一会儿喘不过气来。
例：A small child could choke on the doll's hair...洋娃娃的头发可能会让小孩窒息。

VERB
掐死;扼死;勒死
To choke someone means to squeeze their neck until they are dead.
例：The men pushed him into the entrance of a nearby building where they choked him with his tie.那些人把他推进附近大楼的入口处，用他的领带勒死了他。

VERB
挤满;塞满
If a place is choked with things or people, it is full of them and they prevent movement in it.
例：The village's roads are choked with traffic...村里的路上挤满了车辆行人。
例：His pond has been choked by the fast-growing weed.他的池塘里长满了恣意蔓生的杂草。

N-COUNT
(汽车、卡车等的)阻气门
The choke in a car, truck, or other vehicle is a device that reduces the amount of air going into the engine and makes it easier to start.

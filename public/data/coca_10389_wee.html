wee 
英[wi:] 美[wi]
 
adj.很小的，极小的；很早的；n.一点点；一会儿；
 "

ADJ
很小的;极小的;一丁点儿的
Wee means small in size or extent.
例：I've got a wee kitten in the flat...我在公寓里养了一只小猫。
例：He just needs to calm down a wee bit.他只是需要稍微冷静一下。

VERB
尿尿，嘘嘘（尤用于儿语）
To wee means to urinate. Wee is an informal word used especially by children.
例：He said he wanted to wee.他说他要撒尿。

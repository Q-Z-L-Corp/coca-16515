viability 
英[ˌvaɪə'bɪlətɪ] 美[ˌvaɪə'bɪlətɪ]
 
n.生存能力，发育能力；生活力；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
切实可行的;能办到的
Something that is viable is capable of doing what it is intended to do.
例：Cash alone will not make Eastern Europe's banks viable...仅靠现金不能使东欧的银行维持下去。
例：The goal has been to establish and sustain a nation of viable family farms.目标是要建立一个能够使家庭农场得以存续的国家，并使其维持下去。

ADJ
能存活的;能生长发育的
Foetuses, seeds, or eggs are described as viable if they are capable of developing into living beings without outside help.
例：Five viable pregnancies were established.已确定5个胎儿能够成活。

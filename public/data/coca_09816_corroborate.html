VERB
证实;确证
To corroborate something that has been said or reported means to provide evidence or information that supports it.
例：I had access to a wide range of documents which corroborated the story...我能够查阅证实这一说法的大量文件。
例：Alice corroborated what Blair had said.艾丽斯证实了布莱尔所说的。

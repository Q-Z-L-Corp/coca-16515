well-being

n.幸福；生活安宁；福利；康；

N-UNCOUNT
健康;安乐;康乐
Someone's well-being is their health and happiness.
例：Singing can create a sense of wellbeing...唱歌能够给人一种心旷神怡的感觉。
例：His work emphasised the emotional as well as the physical well-being of children.他的工作不仅重视孩子们的身体健康，同时也关注他们的情感健康。

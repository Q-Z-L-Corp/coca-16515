ignore 
英[ɪgˈnɔ:(r)] 美[ɪɡˈnɔr, -ˈnor]
 
vt.忽视，不顾；[法律]驳回（诉讼）；
 "

VERB
不顾;不理;忽视
If you ignore someone or something, you pay no attention to them.
例：She said her husband ignored her...她说丈夫不关心她。
例：The government had ignored his views on the subject...政府没有理睬他对这一问题的看法。

VERB
忽略;忽视
If you say that an argument or theory ignores an important aspect of a situation, you are criticizing it because it fails to consider that aspect or to take it into account.
例：Such arguments ignore the question of where ultimate responsibility lay.这些争论忽略了最终责任由谁来负的问题。

necessitate 
英[nəˈsesɪteɪt] 美[nəˈsɛsɪˌtet]
 
vt.使…成为必要，需要；强迫，迫使；
 "

VERB
使成为必需;需要
If something necessitates an event, action, or situation, it makes it necessary.
例：A prolonged drought had necessitated the introduction of water rationing...由于持续干旱，用水需要实行配给了。
例：Frank was carrying out fuel-system tests which necessitated turning the booster pumps off.弗兰克在做燃料系统测试，这一测试需要关掉升压泵。

N-UNCOUNT
排水系统;排水;放水
Drainage is the system or process by which water or other liquids are drained from a place.
例：Line the pots with pebbles to ensure good drainage...在罐子里铺一层鹅卵石，以确保排水良好。
例：The drainage system has collapsed because of too much rain.由于降雨过多，排水系统已经彻底瘫痪了。

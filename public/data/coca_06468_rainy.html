rainy 
英[ˈreɪni] 美[ˈreni]
 
adj.下雨的，多雨的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（时期、季节等）多雨的
During a rainy day, season, or period it rains a lot.
例：They walked along the promenade on a rainy night...他们在一个雨夜沿着海滨道散步。
例：The rainy season in the Andes normally starts in December.安第斯山区的雨季通常在12月份来临。

PHRASE
以备不时之需;未雨绸缪
If you say that you are saving something, especially money, for a rainy day, you mean that you are saving it until a time in the future when you might need it.
例：I'll put the rest in the bank for a rainy day.我要把余款存入银行以备不时之需。

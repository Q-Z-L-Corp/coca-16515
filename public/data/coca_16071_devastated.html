devastated

ADJ-GRADED
极为震惊难过的;非常伤心的
If you are devastated by something, you are very shocked and upset by it.
例：Bishop Daly said he was devastated by news of the Cardinal's death...戴利主教说他听到红衣主教去世的消息后感到极为震惊和难过。
例：Teresa was devastated, her dreams shattered.特雷莎伤心欲绝——她的梦想彻底破灭了。

N-COUNT
蜘蛛网
A web is the thin net made by a spider from a sticky substance which it produces in its body.
例：...the spider's web in the window.窗户上的蜘蛛网

N-COUNT
错综复杂的联系(或关系网)
A web is a complicated pattern of connections or relationships, sometimes considered as an obstacle or a danger.
例：He's forced to untangle a complex web of financial dealings...他被迫去理清一堆错综复杂的金融交易。
例：They accused him of weaving a web of lies and deceit.他们指控他编织了一个满是谎言和欺诈的骗局。

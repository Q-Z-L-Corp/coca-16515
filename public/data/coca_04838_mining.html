采矿;采矿业
Mining is the industry and activities connected with getting valuable or useful minerals from the ground, for example coal, diamonds, or gold.
例：...traditional industries such as coal mining and steel making...诸如采煤与钢铁制造之类的传统工业
例：He was born at Aberdare in South Wales, the son of a mining engineer.他出生在南威尔士的阿伯德尔，是一位采矿工程师的儿子。

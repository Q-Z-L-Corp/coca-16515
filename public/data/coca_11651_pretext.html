N-COUNT
借口；托词
A pretext is a reason which you pretend has caused you to do something.
例：They wanted a pretext for subduing the region by force...他们想找借口用武力征服这个地区。
例：He excused himself on the pretext of a stomach upset...他借口自己胃不舒服离开了。

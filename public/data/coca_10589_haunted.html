ADJ
（房屋等）有鬼魂出没的，闹鬼的
A haunted building or other place is one where a ghost regularly appears.
例：Tracy said the cabin was haunted.特蕾西说小木屋闹鬼。
例：...a haunted house.闹鬼的房子

ADJ-GRADED
（表情）焦虑的，忧心忡忡的
Someone who has a haunted expression looks very worried or troubled.
例：She looked so haunted, I almost didn't recognize her.她愁容满面，我差点儿没认出她来。

N-UNCOUNT
（尤指不同寻常的）服装，制服，装束
Someone's garb is the clothes they are wearing, especially when these are unusual.
例：...a familiar figure in civilian garb...一位身着平民装束、为大家所熟悉的人物
例：He wore the garb of a scout, not a general.他身着童子军制服，而不是将军的制服。

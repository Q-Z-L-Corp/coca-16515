ADJ-GRADED
（政变或胜利）不流血的，无伤亡的
A bloodless coup or victory is one in which nobody is killed.
例：Reports from the area indicate that it was a bloodless coup...发自该地区的报道表明这是一次不流血的政变。
例：The campaign would be short and relatively bloodless.这场战役应该是速战速决，基本没什么伤亡。

ADJ
（人的面容或肤色）苍白的，无血色的
If you describe someone's face or skin as bloodless, you mean that it is very pale.
例：...her face grey and bloodless.她的脸色苍白，没有血色

amateur 
英[ˈæmətə(r)] 美[ˈæmətər]
 
n.业余爱好者；外行，生手；adj.业余的，非职业的；外行的；
 "

N-COUNT
业余爱好者
An amateur is someone who does something as a hobby and not as a job.
例：Jerry is an amateur who dances because he feels like it...杰里是个业余跳舞爱好者，他跳舞是因为自己喜欢。
例：Taylor began his playing career as an amateur goalkeeper.泰勒的运动生涯是从一名业余守门员开始的。

ADJ
（体育运动或活动）业余爱好的
Amateur sports or activities are done by people as a hobby and not as a job.
例：...the local amateur dramatics society...地方业余戏剧社团
例：At college he studied English and did amateur boxing.大学时，他学习英语，同时还参加业余拳击。

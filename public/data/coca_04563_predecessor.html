predecessor 
英[ˈpri:dɪsesə(r)] 美[ˈpredəsesə(r)]
 
n.前任，前辈；原有事物，前身；<古>祖先；
 "

N-COUNT
前任；前辈
Your predecessor is the person who had your job before you.
例：He maintained that he learned everything he knew from his predecessor Kenneth Sisam.他坚称他所知道的一切都获自前任肯尼思·赛瑟姆。

N-COUNT
（物件、机器的）前身
The predecessor of an object or machine is the object or machine that came before it in a sequence or process of development.
例：The car is some 40mm shorter than its predecessor.这辆轿车比原先的车型短了约40毫米。

within 
英[wɪˈðɪn] 美[wɪðˈɪn, wɪθ-]
 
adv.在内，在里面；在屋内；在心中，心里是；prep.不超过，在…的范围内；在…能达到的地方；在…内，在…里面；n.内部，里面；

PREP
在…里;在…内部
If something is within a place, area, or object, it is inside it or surrounded by it.
例：Clients are entertained within private dining rooms...在私人餐室内款待客户。
例：An olive-coloured tent stood within a thicket of trees.一顶橄榄色帐篷扎在树丛当中。

PREP
在(社会、组织或体系)中(发生或存在)
Something that happens or exists within a society, organization, or system, happens or exists inside it.
例：The motives that attract people to work within a social service are variable.吸引人们到社会福利部门工作的动机各不相同。
例：...the spirit of self-sacrifice within an army...军队中的自我牺牲精神

PREP
在…心中(有某种情感)
If you experience a particular feeling, you can say that it is within you.
例：He's coping much better within himself...他心里好受多了。
例：You've got to identify these inadequacies within yourself.你必须认识到自身的这些不足。

PREP
在(某种限度或规则)之内
If something is within a particular limit or set of rules, it does not go beyond it or is not more than what is allowed.
例：Troops have agreed to stay within specific boundaries to avoid confrontations...军队同意驻扎在特定地界内，以避免冲突。
例：Exercise within your comfortable limit...要进行适度锻炼。

PREP
在(某一距离)之内;不超出(某一距离)
If you are within a particular distance of a place, you are less than that distance from it.
例：The man was within a few feet of him...这个男子离他只有几英尺。
例：It was within easy walking distance of the hotel...它离旅馆仅几步之遥。

PREP
在(某段时间)内;不超出(某段时间)
Within a particular length of time means before that length of time has passed.
例：About 40% of all students entering as freshmen graduate within 4 years...所有入学新生中约有40%在4年内毕业。
例：Within 24 hours the deal was completed.交易在24小时内完成。

PREP
看得见／听得见／够得着
If something is within sight ,within earshot, or within reach, you can see it, hear it, or reach it.
例：His twenty-five-foot boat was moored within sight of West Church.他那条25英尺长的小船停泊在看得见西教堂的地方。
例：...her heels clicking on the tiled floor, probably an irritating noise to other people within earshot...她的鞋跟踩在瓷砖地板上咯噔咯噔响，这声音可能会让听见的人觉得心烦

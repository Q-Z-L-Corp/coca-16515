resonate 
英[ˈrezəneɪt] 美[ˈrɛzəˌnet]
 
vi.共鸣或共振；回响；
 "

VERB
发出嘹亮的声音;回响;回荡
If something resonates, it vibrates and produces a deep, strong sound.
例：The bass guitar began to thump so loudly that it resonated in my head.低音电吉他开始砰砰作响，巨大的声音一直在我的脑海里回响。

VERB
有特殊意义;引起共鸣
You say that something resonates when it has a special meaning or when it is particularly important to someone.
例：London is confident and alive, resonating with all the qualities of a civilised city.伦敦洋溢着自信和活力，呈现出一个文明城市的所有特性。

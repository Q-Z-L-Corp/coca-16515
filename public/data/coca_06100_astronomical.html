ADJ
（数量，尤指费用）极其巨大的,天文数字的
If you describe an amount, especially the cost of something as astronomical, you are emphasizing that it is very large indeed.
例：Houses in the village are selling for astronomical prices...乡村的房价正在飙升。
例：The cost will be astronomical.费用将是一笔天文数字。

liberalism 
英[ˈlɪbərəlɪzəm] 美[ˈlɪbərəˌlɪzəm, ˈlɪbrə-]
 
n.自由主义；开明的思想或见解；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（主张通过修改法律逐步实现社会进步的）自由主义
Liberalism is a belief in gradual social progress by changing laws, rather than by revolution.
例：...a democrat who has decided that economic liberalism is the best way to secure change.认定经济自由主义是促进变革的最好办法的民主党党员
例：...the tradition of nineteenth-century liberalism.19世纪的自由主义传统

N-UNCOUNT
（主张个体拥有广泛的政治和个人自由的）自由主义
Liberalism is the belief that people should have a lot of political and individual freedom.
例：He was concerned over growing liberalism in the Church.他对教会中日益增强的自由主义倾向心存忧虑。

N-COUNT
红利;股息;股利
A dividend is the part of a company's profits which is paid to people who have shares in the company.
例：The first quarter dividend has been increased by nearly 4 per cent.第一季度的股息增长了近 4%。

PHRASE
有回报;产生效益
If something pays dividends, it brings advantages at a later date.
例：Steps taken now to maximise your health will pay dividends later on.现在采取措施增强体质会使你日后受益。

milky 
英[ˈmɪlki] 美[ˈmɪlki]
 
adj.像牛奶的，乳白色的；浑浊的，柔弱的；（食品）加了牛奶的；（植物）分泌乳汁的；
 "

ADJ
乳白色的;苍白的;淡白色的
If you describe something as milky, you mean that it is pale white in colour. You can describe other colours as milky when they are very pale.
例：A milky mist filled the valley.白蒙蒙的薄雾弥漫山谷。
例：...the distant view of mountain ranges, white against a milky blue sky.映衬在淡蓝色天空背景下的白茫茫的山脉远景

ADJ-GRADED
含奶多的；掺奶的
Drinks or food that are milky contain a lot of milk.
例：...a large bowl of milky coffee.一大杯拿铁咖啡

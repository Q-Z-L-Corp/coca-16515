N-SING
基础训练；基础知识；基础
If you have a grounding in a subject, you know the basic facts or principles of that subject, especially as a result of a particular course of training or instruction.
例：The degree provides a thorough grounding in both mathematics and statistics.该学位课程将为数学和统计学打下扎实的基础。

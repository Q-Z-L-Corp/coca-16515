ADJ
（动物）捕食性的
Predatory animals live by killing other animals for food.
例：...predatory birds like the eagle.像鹰一样的捕食性动物

ADJ-GRADED
掠夺性的；弱肉强食的；损人利己的
Predatory people or organizations are eager to gain something out of someone else's weakness or suffering.
例：People who run small businesses are frightened by the predatory behaviour of the banks.小公司经营者被银行的掠夺行为吓坏了。

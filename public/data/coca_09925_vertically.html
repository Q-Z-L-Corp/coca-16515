vertically 
英['vɜ:tɪklɪ] 美[ˈvətɪklɪ]
 
adv.垂直地；直立地；陡峭地；在顶点；
 "

ADJ
垂直的;直立的
Something that is vertical stands or points straight up.
例：The climber inched up a vertical wall of rock...攀登者慢慢爬上了陡直的石壁。
例：The gadget can be attached to any vertical or near vertical surface.该装置可以装在任何垂直或近似垂直的平面上。

N-SING
垂直方向
The vertical is the direction that points straight up, at an angle of 90 degrees to a flat surface.
例：Pluto seems to have suffered a major collision that tipped it 122 degrees from the vertical.冥王星似乎受到过严重撞击，倾斜了122度。

N-COUNT
垂直线;垂直面
A vertical is a line or structure that is vertical.
例：As long as the verticals align, the design will look regular.只要所有垂直面在一条线上，图案看起来就会很整齐。

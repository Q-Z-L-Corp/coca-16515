meditative

adj.沉思的，冥想的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
沉思的;深思的
Meditative describes things that are related to the act of meditating or the act of thinking very deeply about something.
例：Music can induce a meditative state in the listener...音乐能够引导倾听者沉思。
例：Yogis trained in Eastern meditative techniques can change their heart rate.接受过东方冥想训练的瑜伽信徒能够改变他们的心率。

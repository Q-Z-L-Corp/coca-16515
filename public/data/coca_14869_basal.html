ADJ
基础的；基本的；基部的
Basal means relating to or forming the base of something.
例：Side shoots should be cut back to one leaf above the basal cluster.应将旁枝剪至主串上一叶高处
例：...the basal layer of the skin.皮肤的基底层

ADJ
基本的；处于休息状态的
Your basal metabolic rate is the rate at which your body uses energy when it is at rest.
例：Regular exercise can increase your basal metabolic rate...定期运动能够提高你的基础代谢率。
例：Basal metabolism is much lower for creatures in cold water.冷水中生物的基础代谢速度要低很多。

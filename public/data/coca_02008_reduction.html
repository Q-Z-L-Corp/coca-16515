reduction

N-COUNT
减少；降低
When there is a reduction in something, it is made smaller.
例：...a future reduction in UK interest rates...未来英国利率的下调
例：Many companies have announced dramatic reductions in staff.许多公司已经宣布大幅裁员。

N-UNCOUNT
减少；降低；缩小
Reduction is the act of making something smaller in size or amount, or less in degree.
例：...a new strategic arms reduction agreement.新的战略武器削减协议

ignorance

N-UNCOUNT
无知;愚昧
Ignorance of something is lack of knowledge about it.
例：I am embarrassed by my complete ignorance of history...我对历史一无所知,这让我很难堪。
例：There is so much ignorance about mental illness...人们对精神疾病很不了解。

V-ERG
表现出;散发;流露
If a quality emanates from you, or if you emanate a quality, you give people a strong sense that you have that quality.
例：Intelligence and cunning emanated from him...他透着一种智慧和狡黠。
例：He emanates sympathy.他流露出同情。

VERB
来自(于);(从…)散发出
If something emanates from somewhere, it comes from there.
例：The heady aroma of wood fires emanated from the stove.炉子里散发出木柴燃烧时的浓烈香味。
例：...reports emanating from America.来自美国的报道

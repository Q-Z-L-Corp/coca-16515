categorization 
英[ˌkætəɡəraɪ'zeɪʃn] 美[ˌkætəɡəraɪ'zeɪʃn]
 
编目方法，分门别类；
 "

VERB
对…进行分类;把…归类
If you categorize people or things, you divide them into sets or you say which set they belong to.
例：Lindsay, like his films, is hard to categorise...林赛这个人和他的电影一样，很难归类。
例：Make a list of your child's toys and then categorise them as sociable or antisocial.把孩子的玩具列个单子，然后将其分为交际型和非交际型两类。

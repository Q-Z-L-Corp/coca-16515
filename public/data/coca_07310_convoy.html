convoy 
英[ˈkɒnvɔɪ] 美[ˈkɑnvɔɪ]
 
n.护航队；护送；被护送者；vt.护航；护送；
 "

N-COUNT
车队;舰队
A convoy is a group of vehicles or ships travelling together.
例：...a U.N. convoy carrying food and medical supplies...运送食品和医疗用品的联合国车队
例：...humanitarian relief convoys...人道主义援助车队

VERB
护送;护卫;为…护航
To convoy goods or people somewhere means to move them there in a convoy.
例：He ordered the combined fleet to convoy troops to Naples.他命令联合舰队将军队护送到那不勒斯。

adv.不在乎地，鲁莽地，不顾一切地；混；愣；大肆；

ADJ-GRADED
轻率的;不计后果的;鲁莽的;无所顾忌的
If you say that someone is reckless, you mean that they act in a way which shows that they do not care about danger or the effect their behaviour will have on other people.
例：She loved to ride; on horseback, she was reckless and utterly without fear...她喜欢骑马；在马背上，她纵横驰骋，毫无畏惧。
例：He is charged with causing death by reckless driving.他被控危险驾驶致人死亡。

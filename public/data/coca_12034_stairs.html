stairs 
英[steəz] 美[sterz]
 
n.楼梯；楼梯（ stair的名词复数 ）；（楼梯的）一级；阶；
 "

N-PLURAL
楼梯
Stairs are a set of steps inside a building which go from one floor to another.
例：Nancy began to climb the stairs...南希开始爬楼梯。
例：We walked up a flight of stairs...我们上了一段楼梯。

N-SING
一层楼梯;一段楼梯
A stair is a flight of stairs.
例：I followed her down the stair.我跟着她下了楼梯。

N-COUNT
一级楼梯
A stair is one of the steps in a flight of stairs.
例：Terry was sitting on the bottom stair.特里正坐在最下面的一级楼梯上。

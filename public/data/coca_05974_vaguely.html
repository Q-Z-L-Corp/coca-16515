vaguely 
英[ˈveɪgli] 美[ˈveɪɡli]
 
adv.含糊地；茫然地；暧昧的；
 "

ADV-GRADED
轻微地;有点儿
Vaguely means to some degree but not to a very large degree.
例：The voice on the line was vaguely familiar, but Crook couldn't place it at first...电话里的声音有点儿熟悉，但是克鲁克一开始想不起来到底是谁。
例：Arnold felt vaguely embarrassed...阿诺德感到有点儿尴尬。

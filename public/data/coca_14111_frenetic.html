adj.发狂的；狂乱的；狂热的；非常激动的；n.疯子；狂人；

ADJ-GRADED
发狂似的;狂热的
If you describe an activity as frenetic, you mean that it is fast and energetic, but rather uncontrolled.
例：...the frenetic pace of life in New York...纽约疯狂的生活节奏
例：This frenetic activity is the sign of a worried man.这种发了疯似的行为活动是一个人极度焦虑的表现。

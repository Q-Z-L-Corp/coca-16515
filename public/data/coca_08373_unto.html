unto 
英[ˈʌntə] 美[ˈʌntu]
 
prep.对，向；直到；在…旁边；
 "

PREP
于;对;向
Unto was used to indicate that something was done or given to someone.
例：And he said unto him, 'Who is my neighbor?'...他于是对他说道：“谁是我邻居？”
例：I will do unto others what they did to me.我会以其人之道还治其人之身。

PREP
迄至；直至；直到
Unto was used to indicate that something continued until a particular time.
例：Be ye faithful unto the end.汝等应至死不渝。

N-COUNT
激愤的长篇演说;长篇的批评性发言
A tirade is a long angry speech in which someone criticizes a person or thing.
例：She launched into a tirade against the policies that ruined her business...她就造成自己企业破产的政策发表了激愤的长篇演说。
例：He too has met a tirade of abuse.他也遭到了长篇累牍的谩骂攻击。

N-COUNT
包装盒；包装袋
A packet is a small container in which a quantity of something is sold. Packets are either small boxes made of thin cardboard, or bags or envelopes made of paper or plastic.
例：Cook the rice according to instructions on the packet.按照包装袋上的说明煮饭。
例：...a cigarette packet.香烟盒

N-COUNT
（扁平的）小包裹
A packet is a small flat parcel.
例：...to send letters and packets abroad.将信和包裹寄往国外
例：...a packet of photographs.一包照片

N-SING
一大笔钱
You can refer to a lot of money as a packet .
例：It'll cost you a packet...那会花你一大笔钱。
例：You could save yourself a packet...你能为自己省一大笔钱。

atmospheric 
英[ˌætməsˈferɪk] 美[ˌætməˈsfɛrɪk, -ˈsfɪr-]
 
adj.大气的；大气引起的；有…气氛的；
 "

ADJ
大气的;大气层的;空气的
Atmospheric is used to describe something which relates to the earth's atmosphere.
例：...atmospheric gases.大气中的气体
例：...atmospheric pressure.大气压

ADJ-GRADED
（地方、音乐等）有情调的，有魅力的，有感染力的
If you describe a place or a piece of music as atmospheric, you like it because it has a particular quality which is interesting or exciting and makes you feel a particular emotion.
例：...beautiful, atmospheric music...优美的、有情调的音乐
例：One of the most atmospheric corners of Prague is the old Jewish ghetto.古老的犹太人社区是布拉格最具特色的角落之一。

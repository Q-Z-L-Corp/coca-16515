N-COUNT
大陆;洲
A continent is a very large area of land, such as Africa or Asia, that consists of several countries.
例：She loved the African continent...她热爱非洲大陆。
例：Dinosaurs evolved when most continents were joined in a single land mass.恐龙进化的时候，多数大陆连为一体。

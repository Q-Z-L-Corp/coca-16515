adj.发黏的；俗气的；褴褛的；缺乏教养或风度的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
做工粗糙的;劣质的;俗气的
If you describe something as tacky, you dislike it because it is cheap and badly made or vulgar.
例：...a woman in a fake leopard-skin coat and tacky red sunglasses...身穿假豹皮大衣、戴着俗气的大红色太阳镜的女人
例：The whole thing is dreadfully tacky.整件东西糟糕透顶。

ADJ-GRADED
(油漆、胶水)发黏的，未干的
If something such as paint or glue is tacky, it is slightly sticky and not yet dry.
例：Test to see if the finish is tacky, and if it is, leave it to harden.看看最后一道漆干了没有，要是没干，就先让它干透。

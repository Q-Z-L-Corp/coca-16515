vessel 
英[ˈvesl] 美[ˈvɛsəl]
 
n.容器；船，飞船；血管，管束；…的化身；
 "

N-COUNT
舰;轮船;大船
A vessel is a ship or large boat.
例：A Moroccan fishing vessel and a South Korean cargo ship collided in rough seas.一艘摩洛哥渔船与一艘韩国货船在波涛汹涌的大海上相撞了。
例：...a New Zealand navy vessel.一艘新西兰海军军舰

N-COUNT
（盛液体用的）容器，器皿
A vessel is a bowl or other container in which liquid is kept.
例：He makes decorative vessels in copper, stainless steel and silver.他用铜、不锈钢和银制造装饰性器皿。
例：...storage vessels.储物容器

experimental 
英[ɪkˌsperɪˈmentl] 美[ɪkˌspɛrəˈmɛntl]
 
adj.实验的；根据实验的；试验性的；n.试验性的东西；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
试验性的
Something that is experimental is new or uses new ideas or methods, and might be modified later if it is unsuccessful.
例：...an experimental air conditioning system...试验性的空调系统
例：He tends to write bizarre and highly experimental pieces of music...他倾向于创作高度试验性而且风格奇异的音乐作品。

ADJ
实验的;用于实验的;根据实验的
Experimental means using, used in, or resulting from scientific experiments.
例：...the main techniques of experimental science.实验科学的主要技术
例：...the use of experimental animals...实验动物的使用

ADJ
（行动）试验性的，试探性的
An experimental action is done in order to see what it is like, or what effects it has.
例：The British Sports Minister is reported to be ready to argue for an experimental lifting of the ban.据报道，英国体育大臣准备争取试行取消那条禁令。

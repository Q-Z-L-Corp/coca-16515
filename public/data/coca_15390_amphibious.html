amphibious 
英[æmˈfɪbiəs] 美[æmˈfɪbiəs]
 
adj.两栖的；水陆两用的；两栖作战的；具有双重性的；
 "

ADJ
（军事行动）两栖作战的
In an amphibious military operation, army and navy forces attack a place from the sea.
例：A third brigade is at sea, ready for an amphibious assault.还有一个旅已在海上待命，准备两栖进攻。

ADJ
（车辆)水陆两用的
An amphibious vehicle is able to move on both land and water.
例：...an amphibious landing craft which can carry helicopters and up to 2,000 troops.能运送直升机和多达两千人的部队的水陆两用登陆艇

ADJ
（动物）水陆两栖的
Amphibious animals are animals such as frogs and toads that can live both on land and in water.

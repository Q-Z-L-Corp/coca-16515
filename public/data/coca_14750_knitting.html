N-UNCOUNT
编织物；针织品
Knitting is something, such as an article of clothing, that is being knitted.
例：She had been sitting with her knitting at her fourth-floor window.她那时正坐在5楼的窗前织东西。

N-UNCOUNT
编织；针织
Knitting is the action or process of knitting.
例：Take up a relaxing hobby, such as knitting.培养一种能放松身心的爱好，比如编织。
例：...knitting patterns.针织图案

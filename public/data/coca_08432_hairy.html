hairy 
英[ˈheəri] 美[ˈheri]
 
adj.多毛的；毛制的，仿毛的；[俚] 险象环生的，危险的；

ADJ-GRADED
多毛的;毛茸茸的
Someone or something that is hairy is covered with hairs.
例：He was wearing shorts which showed his long, muscular, hairy legs...他穿着短裤，露出自己强壮多毛的长腿。
例：The plant has serrated and hairy leaves.这棵植物长着锯齿状的毛茸茸的叶子。

ADJ-GRADED
（情况）惊险刺激的，令人担忧的，令人害怕的
If you describe a situation as hairy, you mean that it is exciting, worrying, and rather frightening.
例：His driving was a bit hairy.他的车技让人不放心。

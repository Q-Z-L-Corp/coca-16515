N-VAR
丁香（树）
A lilac or a lilac tree is a small tree which has sweet-smelling purple, pink, or white flowers in large, cone-shaped groups.
例：Lilacs grew against the side wall.丁香树倚着边墙生长。
例：...a twig of lilac.丁香细枝

COLOUR
丁香紫；淡紫色；淡雪青
Something that is lilac is pale pinkish-purple in colour.
例：All shades of mauve, lilac, lavender and purple were fashionable.深深浅浅的各种木槿色、丁香色、熏衣草色和紫色都是流行色。

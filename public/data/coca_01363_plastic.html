plastic 
英[ˈplæstɪk] 美[ˈplæstɪk]
 
n.塑料制品；〈口〉（使用）信用卡；整形；可塑体；adj.可塑的，塑性的，受范的，柔软的；造型的，塑造的，给与形态的；塑料[塑胶]的，塑料[塑胶]制的，合成树脂做的；有形成力的，有创造力的；
 "

N-MASS
塑料
Plastic is a material which is produced from oil by a chemical process and which is used to make many objects. It is light in weight and does not break easily.
例：...a wooden crate, sheltered from wetness by sheets of plastic...用塑料薄膜防潮的木板箱
例：A lot of the plastics that carmakers are using cannot be recycled.汽车制造商使用的许多塑料制品是无法回收利用的。

ADJ-GRADED
不自然的；不真实的
If you describe something as plastic, you mean that you think it looks or tastes unnatural or not real.
例：...plastic airline food...航班上味同嚼蜡的食品
例：When girls put on too much eye-shadow, they look plastic.女孩子如果把眼影涂得过重，看起来就不自然了。

N-UNCOUNT
（塑料制）信用卡
If you use plastic or plastic money to pay for something, you pay for it with a credit card instead of using cash.
例：Using plastic to pay for an order is simplicity itself.用信用卡支付订单是很简单的事。
例：...shopping with their plastic money.用他们的信用卡购物

ADJ
可塑的；塑性的
Something that is plastic is soft and can easily be made into different shapes.
例：The mud is smooth, gray, soft, and plastic as butter.这种泥浆呈灰色，它光滑、柔软，像黄油一样易塑。

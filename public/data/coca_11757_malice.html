N-UNCOUNT
恶意;怨恨
Malice is behaviour that is intended to harm people or their reputations, or cause them embarrassment and upset.
例：There was a strong current of malice in many of his portraits...他的许多肖像画中都透着一股强烈的怨恨。
例：There was no malice on his part.他并无恶意。

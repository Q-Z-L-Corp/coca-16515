stirring 
英[ˈstɜ:rɪŋ] 美[ˈstərɪŋ]
 
adj.激动人心的；鼓舞人心的；令人激情澎湃的；v.激起（stir的ing形式）；n.萌动；出现；酝酿 ；开始；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(活动、演出或描述)激动人心的，鼓舞人心的
A stirring event, performance, or account of something makes people very excited or enthusiastic.
例：The Prime Minister made a stirring speech...首相发表了一篇鼓舞人心的演讲。
例：Stowe gives a stirring performance as a strong spirited female.斯托扮演了一位坚强而且充满活力的女性，她的表演震撼人心。

N-COUNT
(感情、想法的)萌生，出现，酝酿
A stirring of a feeling or thought is the beginning of one.
例：I feel a stirring of curiosity.我开始产生了好奇心。
例：...the first stirrings of a sense of guilt.负罪感的最初萌生

midway 
英[ˌmɪdˈweɪ] 美[ˈmɪdˌwe]
 
n.中途，半途；娱乐场，游乐场；折中办法，中间道路；adv.中途地，中间地；中途，半路；adj.居中的，中途的；
 "

ADV
中途;半路;中间
If something is midway between two places, it is between them and the same distance from each of them.
例：The studio is midway between his aunt's old home and his cottage.工作室就在从他姑姑的老宅到他的小屋的中途。

ADV
(时间)中间地，一半地
If something happens midway through a period of time, it happens during the middle part of it.
例：He crashed midway through the race...他在比赛中途撞车了。
例：He returned midway through the afternoon.他下午三四点时返回了。

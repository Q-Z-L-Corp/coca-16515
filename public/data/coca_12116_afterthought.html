afterthought 
英[ˈɑ:ftəθɔ:t] 美[ˈæftərθɔt]
 
n.事后的考虑或想法；事后聪明；事后再想起；后来添加的东西；

N-COUNT
事后的想法;追加的事物
If you do or say something as an afterthought, you do or say it after something else as an addition, perhaps without careful thought.
例：Almost as an afterthought he added that he missed her.他又几乎不假思索地补充说他想念她。

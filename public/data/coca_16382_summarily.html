summarily 
英['sʌmərəlɪ] 美[ˈsʌmərəlɪ]
 
adv.概括地；即刻地；简单（化）地；草率地；
 "

N-COUNT
总结;摘要;概括
A summary of something is a short account of it, which gives the main points but not the details.
例：What follows is a brief summary of the process...接下来是对此流程的一个简短概括。
例：Here's a summary of the day's news...下面是今日新闻简报。

ADJ
从速的;即决的;草草的
Summary actions are done without delay, often when something else should have been done first or done instead.
例：It says torture and summary execution are common...那上面称酷刑和草草处决很常见。
例：There is no doubt that some considered that a beating was no more than summary justice...毫无疑问，有人认为拷打比草菅人命好不到哪去。

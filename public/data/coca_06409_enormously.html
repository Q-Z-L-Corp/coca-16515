enormously 
英[ɪˈnɔ:məsli] 美[ɪˈnɔrməsli]
 
adv.巨大地，庞大地；大大；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
巨大的;极大的;很多的
Something that is enormous is extremely large in size or amount.
例：The main bedroom is enormous...主卧室非常大。
例：There is, of course, an enormous amount to see.当然有很多可看的。

ADJ
（范围、程度）极大的
You can use enormous to emphasize the great degree or extent of something.
例：It was an enormous disappointment.真是让人万分失望。
例：...his enormous capacity for brutality.他的穷凶极恶

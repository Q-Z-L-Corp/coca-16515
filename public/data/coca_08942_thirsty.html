thirsty 
英[ˈθɜ:sti] 美[ˈθɜrsti]
 
adj.口渴的；渴望（求）…的；干旱的，缺水的；耗油的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
渴的;干渴的
If you are thirsty, you feel a need to drink something.
例：If a baby is thirsty, it feeds more often...婴儿要是渴了，就会吃得更频繁。
例：Drink whenever you feel thirsty during exercise.运动过程中渴了就喝点水。

ADJ-GRADED
渴望的;渴求的
If you are thirsty for something, you have a strong desire for it.
例：People should understand how thirsty for revenge they are.人们应该理解他们复仇的愿望是多么强烈。

lover 
英[ˈlʌvə(r)] 美[ˈlʌvɚ]
 
n.爱好者，嗜好者；情人；情妇，情夫；情侣；
 "

N-COUNT
（非婚的）爱人，情人
Someone's lover is someone who they are having a sexual relationship with but are not married to.
例：Every Thursday she would meet her lover Leon...每周四她都会和情人利昂幽会。
例：He and Liz became lovers soon after they first met.他和莉兹初次见面后不久就成了情侣。

N-COUNT
热爱者；爱好者；发烧友
If you are a lover of something such as animals or the arts, you enjoy them very much and take great pleasure in them.
例：She is a great lover of horses and horse racing...她热爱马匹和赛马。
例：Are you an opera lover?你是歌剧发烧友吗？

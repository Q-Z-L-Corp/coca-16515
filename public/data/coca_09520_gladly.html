gladly 
英[ˈglædli] 美[ˈɡlædlɪ]
 
adv.高兴地，乐意地，欣然；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
高兴的；愉快的
If you are glad about something, you are happy and pleased about it.
例：I'm glad I relented in the end...我很高兴自己的态度最终缓和了下来。
例：The people seem genuinely glad to see you...这些人见到你好像发自内心地感到喜悦。

ADJ-GRADED
乐意的；非常愿意的
If you say that you will be glad to do something, usually for someone else, you mean that you are willing and eager to do it.
例：I'll be glad to show you everything...我将很乐意向你们展示一切。
例：We should be glad to answer any questions.我们愿意回答任何问题。

PHRASE
好消息
Glad tidings means good news.
例：...the bringer of glad tidings.报喜讯的人

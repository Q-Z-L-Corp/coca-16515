N-COUNT
(尤指词典中给出的)定义，释义
A definition is a statement giving the meaning of a word or expression, especially in a dictionary.
例：There is no general agreement on a standard definition of intelligence...“智力”一词目前还没有一个普遍认可的标准释义。
例：My definition of a good hospice is one where some of the patients start feeling too good to die.我对“好的晚期病人安养院”的定义是一个能让一些病人开始感到生有所恋的地方。

N-UNCOUNT
清晰;分明;明确
Definition is the quality of being clear and distinct.
例：Give your brows extra definition with Outdoor Girl's Eyebrow Pencil in Brown...用“户外女孩”品牌的棕色眉笔更加清晰地勾勒出眉形。
例：The speakers criticised his new programme for lack of definition.几位发言者批评他的新方案不够清楚明确。

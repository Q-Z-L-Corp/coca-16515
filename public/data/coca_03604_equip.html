equip 
英[ɪˈkwɪp] 美[ɪˈkwɪp]
 
vt.装备，配备；使具备，使有准备；
 "

VERB
装备;配备
If you equip a person or thing with something, you give them the tools or equipment that are needed.
例：They become obsessed with trying to equip their vehicles with gadgets to deal with every possible contingency...他们变得痴迷于给自己的车辆装备小配件，以应付可能出现的各种紧急情况。
例：Owners of restaurants would have to equip them to admit disabled people...餐厅老板将必须在餐厅里配备能够接纳残疾人的设施。

VERB
（尤指通过教育）使做好准备，使有能力，使有资格
If something equips you for a particular task or experience, it gives you the skills and attitudes you need for it, especially by educating you in a particular way.
例：Relative poverty, however, did not prevent Martin from equipping himself with an excellent education...然而，较为贫困的状况并没有阻止马丁让自己接受一流的教育。
例：A basic two-hour first aid course would equip you to deal with any of these incidents.两小时的基础急救课程能够让你有能力处理这类事件。

utility 
英[ju:ˈtɪləti] 美[juˈtɪləti]
 
n.功用，效用；有用的物体或器械；公用事业公司；公用事业；adj.有多种用途的；各种工作都会做的；能在数个位置作替补的；为获得经济价值而饲养；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
功用;效用;实用
The utility of something is its usefulness.
例：Belief in the utility of higher education is shared by students nationwide...全国的学生都相信高等教育是有用的。
例：He inwardly questioned the utility of his work.他内心质疑自己的工作是否有用。

N-COUNT
公用事业
A utility is an important service such as water, electricity, or gas that is provided for everyone, and that everyone pays for.
例：...public utilities such as gas, electricity and phones.煤气、电和电话等公用事业

ADJ-GRADED

Fuzzy hair sticks up in a soft, curly mass.

ADJ-GRADED
覆有细绒毛的;绒毛状的;毛茸茸的

例：...fuzzy material.绒毛料子

ADJ-GRADED
(图片、声音等)不清楚的，模糊的
A fuzzy picture, image, or sound is unclear and hard to see or hear.
例：A couple of fuzzy pictures have been published.有几张模糊的照片刊登了出来。
例：...fuzzy bass lines.含糊的低音乐段

ADJ-GRADED
(人或头脑)稀里糊涂的，迷迷糊糊的
If you or your thoughts are fuzzy, you are confused and cannot think clearly.
例：He had little patience for fuzzy ideas.他没什么耐心听那些糊里糊涂的想法。

ADJ-GRADED
不明确的;含混的
You describe something as fuzzy when it is vague and not clearly defined.
例：The border between science fact and science fiction gets a bit fuzzy.科学事实和科幻小说之间的界限变得有点儿模糊了。

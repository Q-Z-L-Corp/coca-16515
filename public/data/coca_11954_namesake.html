N-COUNT
同名的人;同名物
Someone's or something's namesake has the same name as they do.
例：He is putting together a four-man team, including his son and namesake Tony o'Reilly Jnr...他正在组建一个4人小队，包括和自己同名的儿子小托尼·奥赖利。
例：Notre-Dame Cathedral in Senlis is less famous than its namesake in Paris.桑利斯的圣母院不如在巴黎与其同名的圣母院有名。

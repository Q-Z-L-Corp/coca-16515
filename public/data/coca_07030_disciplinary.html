ADJ
惩戒性的;执行纪律的
Disciplinary bodies or actions are concerned with making sure that people obey rules or regulations and that they are punished if they do not.
例：He will now face a disciplinary hearing for having an affair...他因有外遇而要面临纪律听证会的裁决。
例：He was unhappy that no disciplinary action was being taken.他对没有任何纪律惩罚而心生不满。

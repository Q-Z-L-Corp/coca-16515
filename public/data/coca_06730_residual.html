ADJ
残留的;剩余的;遗留的
Residual is used to describe what remains of something when most of it has gone.
例：...residual radiation from nuclear weapons testing...核武器实验后残留的辐射
例：Turn the hotplate off and allow the residual heat to keep the mixture simmering.把烤盘关掉，让余热把混合物再煨一下。

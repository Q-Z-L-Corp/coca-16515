mighty 
英[ˈmaɪti] 美[ˈmaɪti]
 
adj.趾高气扬；巨大的；强有力的；浩瀚的；adv.非常，很；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
强大的;强有力的;巨大的
Mighty is used to describe something that is very large or powerful.
例：There was a flash and a mighty bang.一道闪光和一声巨响接踵而至。
例：...a land marked with vast lakes and mighty rivers.一片拥有大片湖泊和宽广河流的土地

ADV-GRADED
(置于形容词和副词之前表示强调)很，非常，十分
Mighty is used in front of adjectives and adverbs to emphasize the quality that they are describing.
例：It's something you'll be mighty proud of...这是你们将会非常引以为豪的事情。
例：Being paper money, it rotted away mighty fast.由于是纸币，所以烂得很快。

N-COUNT
混战
A melee is a noisy confusing fight between the people in a crowd.
例：A policeman was killed and scores of people were injured in the melee.混战中一名警察被打死，许多人受伤。

N-SING
混乱的情况;无序的局面
A melee of things is a large, confusing, disorganized group of them.
例：...the melee of streets around the waterfront.滨水区混乱交错的街道

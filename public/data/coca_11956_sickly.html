sickly 
英[ˈsɪkli] 美[ˈsɪkli]
 
adj.有病的；苍白的；疾病流行的；令人作呕的；v.使现病容；使苍白；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
常生病的;虚弱的;健康欠佳的
A sickly person or animal is weak, unhealthy, and often ill.
例：He had been a sickly child.他曾是个体弱多病的孩子。

ADJ-GRADED
(通常指因为太甜而)让人恶心的，令人作呕的
A sickly smell or taste is unpleasant and makes you feel slightly sick, often because it is extremely sweet.
例：...the sickly smell of rum.朗姆酒甜得发腻的气味

ADJ-GRADED
(颜色、灯光等)暗淡的，苍白的，微弱的
A sickly colour or light is unpleasantly pale or weak.
例：Wallpapers for children too often come only in sickly pastel shades.给孩子们用的墙纸往往颜色苍白暗淡。

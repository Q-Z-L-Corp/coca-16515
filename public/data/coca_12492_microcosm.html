microcosm 
英[ˈmaɪkrəʊkɒzəm] 美[ˈmaɪkroʊkɑzəm]
 
n.微观世界；小宇宙；缩影；小规模地；
 "

N-COUNT
微观世界;缩影
A microcosm is a small society, place, or activity which has all the typical features of a much larger one and so seems like a smaller version of it.
例：Kitchell says the city was a microcosm of all American culture during the '60s...基切尔说这个城市曾经是20世纪60年代整个美国文化的缩影。
例：In many respects, Mahan's story is that of the Asian community in microcosm.从许多方面来说，马汉的经历就是亚裔团体的经历的缩影。

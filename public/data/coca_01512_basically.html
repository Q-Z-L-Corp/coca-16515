ADV
主要地；基本上
You use basically for emphasis when you are stating an opinion, or when you are making an important statement about something.
例：This gun is designed for one purpose—it's basically to kill people...这种枪只有一个用途——就是用来杀人。
例：Basically I think he would be someone who complemented me in terms of character.我基本上认为他是在性格上和我互补的人。

ADV
大致说来；从根本上来讲
You use basically to show that you are describing a situation in a simple, general way, and that you are not concerned with less important details.
例：Basically you've got two choices...大致说来，你有两个选择。
例：It's basically a vegan diet...从根本上来讲，这是严格的素食主义饮食。

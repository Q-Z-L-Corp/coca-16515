deny 
英[dɪˈnaɪ] 美[dɪˈnaɪ]
 
vt.拒绝；拒绝承认；拒绝…占有；否认知情；
 "

VERB
否认
When you deny something, you state that it is not true.
例：She denied both accusations...她对两项指控均予以否认。
例：The government has denied that there was a plot to assassinate the president...政府否认曾有人密谋暗杀总统。

VERB
否认与…有关系;否认…是自己的;不承认;抛弃
If you deny someone or something, you say that they have no connection with you or do not belong to you.
例：I denied my father because I wanted to become someone else.我和父亲断绝了关系，因为我想成为一个不一样的自己。

VERB
拒绝给予;拒绝…的要求
If you deny someone something that they need or want, you refuse to let them have it.
例：If he is unlucky, he may find that his ex-partner denies him access to his children...如果不走运的话，他可能会发现前妻拒绝让他接近自己的孩子。
例：Don't deny yourself pleasure...不要有乐不享。

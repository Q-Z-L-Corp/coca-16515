apathy 
英[ˈæpəθi] 美[ˈæpəθi]
 
n.漠然；冷淡；无兴趣；无动于衷；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
冷淡;漠然;漠不关心
You can use apathy to talk about someone's state of mind if you are criticizing them because they do not seem to be interested in or enthusiastic about anything.
例：They told me about isolation and public apathy.他们向我谈到了孤立无助和公众的冷漠。
例：...the political apathy and emotional uncertainty of young Americans.美国年轻人对政治的漠不关心和情感上的多变

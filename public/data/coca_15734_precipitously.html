precipitously 
英[prɪ'sɪpɪtəslɪ] 美[prɪˈsɪpɪtəslɪ]

ADJ-GRADED
险峻的；陡峭的
A precipitous slope or drop is very steep and often dangerous.
例：The town is perched on the edge of a steep, precipitous cliff.该镇坐落在陡峭险峻的悬崖边上。

ADJ-GRADED
突然的；急剧的
A precipitous change is sudden and unpleasant.
例：The stock market's precipitous drop frightened foreign investors...股票市场的猛跌吓坏了国外投资者。
例：Meryl's health started a precipitous decline.梅里尔的健康状况开始急剧下降。

ADJ-GRADED
急促的；轻率的；仓猝的
A precipitous action happens very quickly and often without being planned.
例：...a precipitous decision.轻率的决定

collector 
英[kəˈlektə(r)] 美[kəˈlɛktɚ]
 
n.收藏家，采集者；收集器；收税员；[电子学] 集流器；
 "

N-COUNT
收集者;收藏家
A collector is a person who collects things of a particular type as a hobby.
例：...a stamp-collector.集邮爱好者
例：...a respected collector of Indian art...受人尊敬的印第安艺术品收藏家

N-COUNT
(…的)收取人;讨债人;收税员;收票员;收垃圾者
You can use collector to refer to someone whose job is to take something such as money, tickets, or rubbish from people. For example, a rent collector collects rent from people.
例：He earned his living as a tax collector.他以收税为业。
例：...a garbage collector.垃圾清运工

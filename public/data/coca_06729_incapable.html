incapable 
英[ɪnˈkeɪpəbl] 美[ɪnˈkepəbəl]
 
adj.无能力的，不会的；不能的；[法]没有资格的；不舞之鹤；
 "

ADJ
无能力的;不能的
Someone who is incapable of doing something is unable to do it.
例：She seemed incapable of taking decisions...她似乎无法作出决定。
例：He was a man incapable of violence.他不是个会施暴的人。

ADJ
软弱无能的;蠢笨的
An incapable person is weak or stupid.
例：He lost his job for allegedly being incapable.据说他由于软弱无能而丢掉了工作。

presentation 
英[ˌpreznˈteɪʃn] 美[ˌprizenˈteɪʃn]

N-UNCOUNT
外观；外貌；表象
Presentation is the appearance of something, which someone has worked to create.
例：We serve traditional French food cooked in a lighter way, keeping the presentation simple...我们供应制作清淡、样式简洁的传统法国菜肴。
例：Check the presentation. Get it properly laid out with a title page.检查一下外观，保证有扉页和合适的版式。

N-COUNT
颁奖仪式；颁奖典礼
A presentation is a formal event at which someone is given a prize or award.
例：He received his award at a presentation in London yesterday.昨天，他在伦敦的颁奖典礼上领了奖。
例：...at the presentation ceremony.在颁奖典礼上

N-COUNT
介绍；陈述；报告；说明
When someone gives a presentation, they give a formal talk, often in order to sell something or get support for a proposal.
例：James Watson, Philip Mayo and I gave a slide and video presentation...詹姆斯·沃森、菲利普·梅奥和我利用幻灯片和视频进行了介绍。
例：I always ask how much time I have to make my presentation.我总是问我的报告需要做多长时间。

N-COUNT
表演；演出
A presentation is something that is performed in front of an audience, for example a play or a ballet.
例：...Blackpool Opera House's presentation of Buddy, the musical.布莱克浦歌剧院上演的音乐剧《朋友》

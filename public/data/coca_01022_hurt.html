hurt 
英[hɜ:t] 美[hɜrt]
 
vt.损害；使受伤；使伤心；使受皮肉之苦；vi.疼痛；感到疼痛；受痛苦；有坏处，有害；n.伤害；痛苦；adj.受伤的；痛苦的；（受）损坏的；
 "

VERB
使（自己或身体某部位）受伤
If you hurt yourself or hurt a part of your body, you feel pain because you have injured yourself.
例：Yasin had seriously hurt himself while trying to escape from the police...亚辛试图从警察手中逃脱时受了重伤。
例：He had hurt his back in an accident.他在一次事故中伤了后背。

VERB
（身体某部位）感到疼痛
If a part of your body hurts, you feel pain there.
例：His collar bone only hurt when he lifted his arm.他的锁骨只有在抬臂时才感觉到痛。

ADJ-GRADED
受伤的
If you are hurt, you have been injured.
例：His comrades asked him if he was hurt...战友问他是否受伤了。
例：They were dazed but did not seem to be badly hurt.他们有些神志不清，但看起来没有受重伤。

VERB
弄伤，弄痛（他人）
If you hurt someone, you cause them to feel pain.
例：I didn't mean to hurt her, only to keep her still...我不是故意要弄痛她，只是想让她别乱动。
例：You're hurting my arm...你弄痛我的胳膊了。

VERB
伤害（感情）;使伤心
If someone hurts you, they say or do something that makes you unhappy.
例：He is afraid of hurting Bessy's feelings...他怕伤害了贝茜的感情。
例：She's afraid she's going to be hurt and that she'll never fall in love again...她害怕自己会受到伤害，从而永远不会再恋爱了。

ADJ-GRADED
（感情上）受到伤害的，伤心的
If you are hurt, you are upset because of something that someone has said or done.
例：Yes, I was hurt, jealous...是的，我感到伤心、嫉妒。
例：He gave me a slightly hurt look.他有点受伤地看了我一眼。

VERB
伤心;感到痛苦
If you say that you are hurting, you mean that you are experiencing emotional pain.
例：I am lonely and I am hurting.我很孤单，很难过。

VERB
危害;损害;妨碍;对…产生不良影响
To hurt someone or something means to have a bad effect on them or prevent them from succeeding.
例：The combination of hot weather and decreased water supplies is hurting many industries...炎热的天气和供水的减少正危及很多行业。
例：They may fear hurting their husbands' careers.她们可能担心对丈夫的事业造成损害。

N-VAR
伤心;痛苦;委屈
A feeling of hurt is a feeling that you have when you think that you have been treated badly or judged unfairly.
例：...feelings of hurt and anger, fear and despair...伤心、愤怒、恐惧、绝望的复杂情感
例：I was full of jealousy and hurt...我满怀嫉妒，伤心不已。

PHRASE
（做…）不会有坏处
If you say 'It won't hurt to do something' or 'It never hurts to do something', you are recommending an action which you think is helpful or useful.
例：It never hurts to ask...多问无妨。
例：It wouldn't hurt you to be a bit more serious.认真一点对你没有坏处。

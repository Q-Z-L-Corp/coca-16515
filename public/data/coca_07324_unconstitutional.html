ADJ
违反宪法的
If something is unconstitutional, it breaks the rules of a political system.
例：The Moldavian parliament has declared the elections unconstitutional...摩尔达维亚议会已宣布选举违宪。
例：Banning cigarette advertising would be unconstitutional, since selling cigarettes is legal.既然出售香烟是合法的，那么禁止做香烟广告就是违宪的。

habitual 
英[həˈbɪtʃuəl] 美[həˈbɪtʃuəl]
 
adj.习惯的；惯常的；习以为常的；
 "

ADJ
（动作、状态或行为方式）习惯性的，惯常的，典型的
A habitual action, state, or way of behaving is one that someone usually does or has, especially one that is considered to be typical or characteristic of them.
例：He soon recovered his habitual geniality...他不久就恢复了自己一贯的和蔼可亲的态度。
例：If bad posture becomes habitual, you risk long-term effects.不良姿势一旦定型，日后可就有你受的了。

ADJ
（人）积习难改的，习以为常的
You use habitual to describe someone who usually or often does a particular thing.
例：She was a habitual daydreamer.她总是耽于幻想。
例：...the home secretary's plans for minimum sentences for habitual criminals.内政大臣关于设置惯犯起刑点的打算

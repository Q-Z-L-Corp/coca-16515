N-VAR
（尤指政治、经济或商业方面的）政策，方针，策略
A policy is a set of ideas or plans that is used as a basis for making decisions, especially in politics, economics, or business.
例：...plans which include changes in foreign policy and economic reforms.包括转变外交政策和改革经济的计划
例：...the UN's policy-making body.联合国决策机构

N-COUNT
（针对某一问题或某国的）政策，态度，行动
An official organization's policy on a particular issue or towards a country is their attitude and actions regarding that issue or country.
例：...the organisation's future policy towards South Africa.该组织未来对南非的政策
例：...the government's policy on repatriation.有关遣返的政府态度

N-COUNT
保险单；保险契约
An insurance policy is a document which shows the agreement that you have made with an insurance company.
例：You are advised to read the small print of household and motor insurance policies.建议阅读家庭财产和汽车保险单中的附属细则。

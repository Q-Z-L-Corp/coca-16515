ADJ
连续的;接连的;相继的
Successive means happening or existing one after another without a break.
例：Jackson was the winner for a second successive year...杰克逊已经是连续第二年获胜了。
例：Britain was suffering from the failure of successive governments to co-ordinate a national transport policy.接连几届政府都没能协调出一个全国性的交通运输政策，英国因此而深受其苦。

reluctance 
英[rɪ'lʌktəns] 美[rɪˈlʌktəns]
 
n.不愿意，勉强；[电]磁阻；反应式；

ADJ-GRADED
勉强的;不情愿的
If you are reluctant to do something, you are unwilling to do it and hesitate before doing it, or do it slowly and without enthusiasm.
例：Mr Spero was reluctant to ask for help...斯佩罗先生不愿意寻求帮助。
例：The police are very reluctant to get involved in this sort of thing.警方很不愿意卷入这类事情。

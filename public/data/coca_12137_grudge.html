N-COUNT
不满；积怨；怨恨
If you have or bear a grudge against someone, you have unfriendly feelings towards them because of something they did in the past.
例：He appears to have a grudge against certain players...他看来对某些选手怀恨在心。
例：There is no doubt it was an accident and I bear no grudges.这无疑是个意外，我没有怨言。

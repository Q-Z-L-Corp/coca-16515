educate 
英[ˈedʒukeɪt] 美[ˈɛdʒəˌket]
 
vt.& vi.教育，教导；训练，培养；vt.使理解或接受；
 "

VERB
教育，教(尤指儿童)
When someone, especially a child, is educated, he or she is taught at a school or college.
例：He was educated at Haslingden Grammar School.他在哈斯灵登文法学校读书。

VERB
教导;培养
To educate people means to teach them better ways of doing something or a better way of living.
例：Drinkwise Day is mainly designed to educate people about the destructive effects of alcohol abuse.设置“理智饮酒日”的主要目的是让人们了解酗酒的恶果。

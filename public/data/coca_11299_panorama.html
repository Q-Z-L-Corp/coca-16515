panorama 
英[ˌpænəˈrɑ:mə] 美[ˌpænəˈræmə]
 
n.全景画；全景照片；一连串景象或事；概论；
 "

N-COUNT
全景；远景
A panorama is a view in which you can see a long way over a wide area of land, usually because you are on high ground.
例：Horton looked out over a panorama of fertile valleys and gentle hills.霍顿向外望去，肥沃的谷地和平缓的丘陵尽收眼底。

N-COUNT
概述；概论
A panorama is a broad view of a state of affairs or of a constantly changing series of events.
例：...a gripping panorama of medieval history.引人入胜的中世纪史概论

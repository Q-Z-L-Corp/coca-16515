scenic 
英[ˈsi:nɪk] 美[ˈsinɪk]
 
adj.风景优美的；舞台的；戏剧性的；布景的，背景的；n.风景照片；风景胜地；实景电影；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(地方)景色优美的，风景秀丽的
A scenic place has attractive scenery.
例：This is an extremely scenic part of America.这是美国风景非常秀丽的一个地区。
例：...a 2-hour drive through scenic country.穿越景色优美的乡间的两个小时车程

ADJ-GRADED
(路线)景色优美的，观光的
A scenic route goes through attractive scenery and has nice views.
例：It was even marked on the map as a scenic route...它甚至还作为观光路线标在了地图上。
例：I went some miles out of my way to take the scenic road into Macon.我绕行了几英里，想要经由那条风景秀丽的公路进入梅肯。

ADJ
景色迷人的
If a place has scenic beauty, its scenery is attractive.
例：...a land of unparalleled scenic beauty.风光无比秀丽的土地

ADJ-GRADED
（结构、家具）摇摇晃晃的，摇摇欲坠的，不牢固的
A rickety structure or piece of furniture is not very strong or well made, and seems likely to collapse or break.
例：Mona climbed the rickety wooden stairway.莫娜爬上了摇摇晃晃的木楼梯。

fleet 
英[fli:t] 美[flit]
 
n.舰队；船队；车队；港湾，小河；adj.快速的，敏捷的；转瞬即逝的；vi.疾驰；飞逝；vt.使（时间）飞逝；消磨；
 "

N-COUNT
船队;舰队
A fleet is a group of ships organized to do something together, for example to fight battles or to catch fish.
例：The damage inflicted upon the British fleet was devastating.英国舰队遭到了毁灭性的重创。
例：...restaurants supplied by local fishing fleets.由当地捕鱼船队供货的饭馆

N-COUNT
(尤指同一机构或统一行动的)车队
A fleet of vehicles is a group of them, especially when they all belong to a particular organization or business, or when they are all going somewhere together.
例：With its own fleet of trucks, the company delivers most orders overnight...这个公司拥有自己的货车车队，大多数订购货品可以隔日送到。
例：A fleet of ambulances took the injured to hospital.一队救护车把伤者送往医院。

ski 
英[ski:] 美[ski]
 
n.滑雪；滑雪板；滑水橇；滑行装置；v.滑雪，滑冰；
 "

N-COUNT
滑雪板;滑水板
Skis are long, flat, narrow pieces of wood, metal, or plastic that are fastened to boots so that you can move easily on snow or water.
例：...a pair of skis.一副滑雪板

VERB
滑雪;滑水
When people ski, they move over snow or water on skis.
例：They surf, ski and ride...他们冲浪，滑雪，还骑马。
例：The whole party then skied off.然后整群人都滑着雪离去。

ADJ
滑雪用的;滑雪的;滑水的
You use ski to refer to things that are concerned with skiing.
例：...the Swiss ski resort of Klosters.瑞士克洛斯特斯滑雪胜地
例：...a private ski instructor.私人滑雪教练

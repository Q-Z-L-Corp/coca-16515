ADV
狂热地；疯狂地
You can use madly to indicate that one person loves another a great deal.
例：She has fallen madly in love with him...她疯狂地爱上了他。
例：She was devoted to him, but she no longer loved him madly.她一心一意地爱他，但是这份爱不再疯狂。

ADV
非常;很;十分
You can use madly in front of an adjective in order to emphasize the quality expressed by the adjective.
例：Inside it is madly busy...里面异常忙碌。
例：This seemed madly dangerous.这看上去岌岌可危。

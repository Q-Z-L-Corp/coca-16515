非常容易的问题;容易作出的决定
If you describe a question or decision as a no-brainer, you mean that it is a very easy one to answer or make.
例：If it's illegal for someone under 21 to drive, it should be illegal for them to drink and drive. That's a no-brainer.如果不满21岁开车是违法的，那他们酒后驾车也应是违法的，这非常容易理解。

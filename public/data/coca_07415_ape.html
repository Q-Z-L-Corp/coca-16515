ape 
英[eɪp] 美[ep]
 
n.猿；狂热；模仿者；vt.模仿；
 "

N-COUNT
无尾猿；类人猿；猿
Apes are chimpanzees, gorillas, and other animals in the same family.
例：...the theory that man is descended from the apes.认为人类是从类人猿演变而来的理论

VERB
模仿；学…的样子
If you ape someone's speech or behaviour, you imitate it.
例：Modelling yourself on someone you admire is not the same as aping all they say or do.以偶像为榜样并不是说要模仿他们的一言一行。
例：...French films which merely aped Hollywood.只是照搬好莱坞模式的法国电影

PHRASE
怒不可遏;暴跳如雷
If someone goes ape, they become very angry and upset.
例：When Colonel Harper found out, he would go ape.哈珀上校发现了一定会勃然大怒。

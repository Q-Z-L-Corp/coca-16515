n.支柱，柱石；骨干，中流砥柱；主要的依靠；主桅支索；

N-COUNT
支柱;中坚
If you describe something as the mainstay of a particular thing, you mean that it is the most basic part of it.
例：Fish and rice were the mainstays of the country's diet...鱼和米是该国的主要食品。
例：This principle of collective bargaining has been a mainstay in labor relations in this country.这一集体谈判原则已经成为该国劳动关系的重要基础。

entail 
英[ɪnˈteɪl] 美[ɛnˈtel, ɪn-]
 
vt.牵涉；需要；使必要；限定继承；
 "

VERB
使…必然发生;势必造成；使…成为必需
If one thing entails another, it involves it or causes it.
例：Such a decision would entail a huge political risk...这样的决定势必带来巨大的政治风险。
例：The changed outlook entails higher economic growth than was previously assumed...观念的改变势必会使得经济增长高于之前预计的水平。

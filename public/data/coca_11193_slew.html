V-ERG
(使)(车辆失控)滑行,(使)侧滑
If a vehicle slews or is slewed across a road, it slides across it.
例：The bus slewed sideways...公共汽车滑到了一边。
例：A seven-ton lorry slewed across their path...一辆 7 吨的货车侧滑到了一侧。

N-COUNT
许多;大量
A slew of things is a large number of them.
例：There have been a whole slew of shooting incidents...发生过大量枪击案。
例：They dealt with a slew of other issues.他们处理了大量的其他问题。

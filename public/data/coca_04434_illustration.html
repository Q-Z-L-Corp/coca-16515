illustration 
英[ˌɪləˈstreɪʃn] 美[ˌɪləˈstreʃən]
 
n.说明；例证；图解；插图；
 "

N-COUNT
例证;实例
An illustration is an example or a story which is used to make a point clear.
例：...a perfect illustration of the way Britain absorbs and adapts external influences.英国吸收借鉴外来影响的绝好例证

N-COUNT
插图;图案;图表
An illustration in a book is a picture, design, or diagram.
例：She looked like a princess in a nineteenth-century illustration.她看起来像19世纪插图读物中的公主。

criminally 
英[ˈkrɪmɪnəli] 美[ˈkrɪmɪnəli]
 
adv.刑法上，犯了罪地；
 "

N-COUNT
罪犯
A criminal is a person who regularly commits crimes.
例：A group of gunmen attacked a prison and set free nine criminals in Moroto.一伙持枪歹徒袭击了莫罗托的一所监狱，放走了9名罪犯。

ADJ
犯罪的;犯法的
Criminal means connected with crime.
例：Her husband faces various criminal charges...她丈夫面临多项犯罪指控。
例：At 17, he had a criminal record for petty theft...他17岁就有了小偷小摸的犯罪前科。

ADJ
罪过的;严重错误的
If you describe an action as criminal, you think it is very wrong or a serious mistake.
例：He said a full-scale dispute involving strikes would be criminal.他说如果出现罢工等大规模纠纷那将是极不应该的。

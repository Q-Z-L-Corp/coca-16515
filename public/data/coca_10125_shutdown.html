N-COUNT
(工厂、商店等的)停业，歇业，关闭，倒闭
A shutdown is the closing of a factory, shop, or other business, either for a short time or for ever.
例：The shutdown is the latest in a series of painful budget measures...此次关闭是一系列艰难预算措施中最新实施的一项。
例：All seven plants have had temporary shutdowns this year.今年所有 7 家工厂都暂时歇业了。

curly 
英[ˈkɜ:li] 美[ˈkɜrli]
 
adj.有卷发的，长卷毛的；（木材）有皱状纹理的；蜷缩的；（植物）有卷叶病的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(头发)卷曲的，鬈曲的
Curly hair is full of curls.
例：I've got naturally curly hair...我的头发是自来卷儿。
例：Her hair was dark and curly.她的头发乌黑卷曲。

ADJ-GRADED
弧形的;螺旋形的
Curly is sometimes used to describe things that are curved or spiral in shape.
例：...cauliflowers with extra long curly leaves.叶子极长而卷曲的花椰菜
例：...dragons with curly tails.卷尾龙

along 
英[əˈlɒŋ] 美[əˈlɔŋ]
 
adv.一起；向前；进展；到某处；prep.沿着；顺着；靠着…边；
 "

PREP
沿着；顺着
If you move or look along something such as a road, you move or look towards one end of it.
例：Newman walked along the street alone...纽曼独自走在街上。
例：The young man led Mark Ryle along a corridor...那个年轻人领着马克·赖尔沿走廊而去。

PREP
在…边上；在…里
If something is situated along a road, river, or corridor, it is situated in it or beside it.
例：...enormous traffic jams all along the roads.马路上严重的交通阻塞
例：...houses built on piles along the river...沿河打桩盖的房子

ADV
朝某个方向而去;向前
When someone or something moves along, they keep moving in a particular direction.
例：She skipped and danced along...她蹦蹦跳跳地往前走着。
例：He raised his voice a little, talking into the wind as they walked along...他们往前走时，他略微提高了嗓音，迎着风说话。

ADV
进展;进行
If you say that something is going along in a particular way, you mean that it is progressing in that way.
例：...the negotiations which have been dragging along interminably...一直在无休止拖延的谈判
例：Everything was coming along fine after all...一切居然进行得很顺利。

ADV
随身;一起;一道
If you take someone or something along when you go somewhere, you take them with you.
例：This is open to women of all ages, so bring along your friends and colleagues...这对所有年龄段的女性开放，所以请带上你的朋友和同事一起光临。
例：Wives will have to bring along their marriage certificate.已婚妇女必须携带结婚证。

ADV
（前）来;到某处
If someone or something is coming along or is sent along, they are coming or being sent to a particular place.
例：She invited everyone she knew to come along...她邀请了每一个她认识的人前来。
例：He had the material tested and sent along the results.他对材料做了检验并送出了检验结果。

PREP-PHRASE
与…一起
You use along with to mention someone or something else that is also involved in an action or situation.
例：The baby's mother escaped from the fire along with two other children...婴儿的母亲和另外两个孩子一起逃离了火海。
例：There are 32 different kinds of chocolate on sale along with the bread and cakes.有32种不同的巧克力与面包和蛋糕一起出售。

PHRASE
一直
If something has been true or been present all along, it has been true or been present throughout a period of time.
例：I've been fooling myself all along...我一直在欺骗自己。
例：I think she had been planning all along to leave Hungary.我想她一直在计划离开匈牙利。

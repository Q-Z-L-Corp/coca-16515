sterilization 
英[ˌsterəlaɪ'zeɪʃn] 美[ˌstɛrəlɪˈzeʃən]
 
n.杀菌，绝育；灭菌；
 "

VERB
对…作灭菌(或消毒)处理
If you sterilize a thing or a place, you make it completely clean and free from germs.
例：Sulphur is also used to sterilize equipment...硫也可用来给器材消毒。
例：The milk was sterilized and sealed in bottles.牛奶经过灭菌处理后被封装在瓶子里。

VERB
使绝育
If a person or an animal is sterilized, they have a medical operation that makes it impossible for them to have or produce babies.
例：My wife was sterilized after the birth of her fourth child.我妻子生完第4个孩子后做了绝育手术。

N-UNCOUNT
行李
Your baggage consists of the bags that you take with you when you travel.
例：The passengers went through immigration control and collected their baggage.旅客通过入境检查后领取了自己的行李。
例：...excess baggage.超重行李

N-UNCOUNT
（思想）包袱；成见；偏见
You can use baggage to refer to someone's emotional problems, fixed ideas, or prejudices.
例：How much emotional baggage is he bringing with him into the relationship?他背负着多重的感情包袱开始这段恋情？
例：...the ideological baggage of Fascism.法西斯主义在意识形态方面的偏执

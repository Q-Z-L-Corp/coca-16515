forced 
英[fɔ:st] 美[fɔrst]
 
adj.被迫的；强迫的；v.强制(force的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ
被迫的;强迫的;非自愿的
A forced action is something that you do because someone else makes you do it.
例：A system of forced labour was used on the cocoa plantations.可可种植园中曾实行强迫劳役制。

ADJ
不得已的;不得不做的
A forced action is something that you do because circumstances make it necessary.
例：He made a forced landing on a highway.他迫降在了公路上。

ADJ-GRADED
不自然的;勉强的
If you describe something as forced, you mean it does not happen naturally and easily.
例：...a forced smile...强挤出来的微笑
例：She called him darling. It sounded so forced.她称他为亲爱的。听起来很不自然。

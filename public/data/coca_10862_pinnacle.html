pinnacle 
英[ˈpɪnəkl] 美[ˈpɪnəkəl]
 
n.顶峰；顶点；尖顶；[建]（哥德式建筑的）小尖塔；vt.为…加尖塔；把…放在高处；
 "

N-COUNT
尖锥形石；尖岩
A pinnacle is a pointed piece of stone or rock that is high above the ground.
例：A walker fell 80ft from a rocky pinnacle.一位徒步者从一块80英尺高的尖锥形岩石上摔了下来。

N-COUNT
顶峰；极点；顶点
If someone reaches the pinnacle of their career or the pinnacle of a particular area of life, they are at the highest point of it.
例：John Major has reached the pinnacle of British politics...约翰·梅杰已攀上了英国政界的顶峰。
例：She was still a screen goddess at the pinnacle of her career.她仍然是一位处于事业巅峰期的银幕女神。

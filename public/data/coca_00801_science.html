N-UNCOUNT
科学;自然科学;自然科学知识
Science is the study of the nature and behaviour of natural things and the knowledge that we obtain about them.
例：The best discoveries in science are very simple.最伟大的科学发现都非常简单。
例：...science and technology.科学技术

N-COUNT
(自然科学的)学科;理科
A science is a particular branch of science such as physics, chemistry, or biology.
例：Physics is the best example of a science which has developed strong, abstract theories.物理学是自然科学中发展出了强有力的抽象理论的最好例子。
例：...the science of microbiology.微生物学

N-COUNT
(研究人类行为的)学科，社会科学
A science is the study of some aspect of human behaviour, for example sociology or anthropology.
例：...the modern science of psychology.现代心理学

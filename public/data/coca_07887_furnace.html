furnace 
英[ˈfɜ:nɪs] 美[ˈfɜrnɪs]
 
n.熔炉，火炉；极热的地方；严峻的考验或磨难；
 "

N-COUNT
火炉;熔炉
A furnace is a container or enclosed space in which a very hot fire is made, for example to melt metal, burn rubbish, or produce steam.

N-SING
火炉般的地方;极闷热的地方
If you say that a place is a furnace, you mean that it is very hot there.
例：How can we walk? It's a furnace out there.我们怎么能走路呢？外面跟火炉似的。

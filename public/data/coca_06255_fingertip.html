fingertip 
英[ˈfɪŋgətɪp] 美[ˈfɪŋgərtɪp]
 
n.指尖；指套；
 "

N-COUNT
指尖
Your fingertips are the ends of your fingers.
例：The fat and flour are rubbed together with the fingertips as for pastry.像做油酥面团那样用指尖把脂油和面粉揉搓到一起。

PHRASE
近在手边;随时可用
If you say that something is at your fingertips, you approve of the fact that you can reach it easily or that it is easily available to you.
例：I had the information at my fingertips and hadn't used it.资料就在我手边，但一直都没有用。

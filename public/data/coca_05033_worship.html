worship 
英[ˈwɜ:ʃɪp] 美[ˈwɜrʃɪp]
 
vt.崇拜，尊崇；爱慕；vi.做礼拜；热爱；爱慕；崇拜（尤指达到看不到缺点的地步）；n.（对治安官或市长的尊称）阁下；尊敬；崇拜；礼拜；
 "

VERB
敬奉(神)
If you worship a god, you show your respect to the god, for example by saying prayers.
例：I enjoy going to church and worshipping God.我喜欢去教堂做礼拜。
例：...Jews worshipping at the Wailing Wall.在哭墙前敬拜的犹太人

VERB
崇拜;景仰;热爱
If you worship someone or something, you love them or admire them very much.
例：She had worshipped him for years...她仰慕他已有多年。
例：They worship James Brown, Bob Marley and Jimi Hendrix.他们崇拜詹姆斯·布朗、鲍勃·马利和吉米·亨德里克斯。

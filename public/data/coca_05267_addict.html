addict 
英[ˈædɪkt] 美[əˈdɪkt]
 
n.有瘾的人；入迷的人；（吸毒）成瘾者；vt.使沉溺；使上瘾；使自己沾染（某些恶习）；
 "

N-COUNT
吸毒成瘾者；瘾君子
An addict is someone who takes harmful drugs and cannot stop taking them.
例：He's only 24 years old and a drug addict.他只有24岁，却是个瘾君子。

N-COUNT
对…入迷的人;…迷
If you say that someone is an addict, you mean that they like a particular activity very much and spend as much time doing it as they can.
例：She is a TV addict and watches as much as she can.她是个电视迷，看起电视来没完没了。

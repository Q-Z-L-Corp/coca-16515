tour 
英[tʊə(r)] 美[tʊr]
 
n.旅行，观光；巡回演出；任职期；轮班；vt.到…旅游；在…巡回演出；vi.观光；巡回；
 "

N-COUNT
巡回访问;巡回演出；巡回比赛
A tour is an organized trip that people such as musicians, politicians, or theatre companies go on to several different places, stopping to meet people or perform.
例：The band are currently on a two-month tour of Europe...乐队目前正在欧洲进行为期两个月的巡回演出。
例：It will be the first official cricket tour of South Africa for 22 years.这将是 22 年来南非首次正式的板球巡回赛。

VERB
作巡回活动(或演出、比赛)
When people such as musicians, politicians, or theatre companies tour, they go on a tour, for example in order to perform or to meet people.
例：A few years ago they toured the country in a roadshow...几年前他们在全国作了巡回演出。
例：He toured for nearly two years and played 500 sell-out shows...他巡回演出了近两年，500场演出场场爆满。

N-COUNT
旅行;游历;观光
A tour is a journey during which you visit several places that interest you.
例：It was week five of my tour of the major cities of Europe.那是我在欧洲主要城市游历的第 5 周。

N-COUNT
（短时间的）游览，参观,观光
A tour is a short trip that you make round a place, for example round a historical building, so that you can look at it.
例：...a guided tour of a ruined Scottish castle.由导游带领游览苏格兰荒废城堡

VERB
在…旅行;游历;游览;参观
If you tour a place, you go on a journey or trip round it.
例：You can also tour the site on modern coaches equipped with videos...您也可以乘坐配有视频设备的现代化长途汽车游览那处遗址。
例：We toured the streets of Milan.我们游览了米兰的街道。

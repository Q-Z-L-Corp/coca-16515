N-UNCOUNT
垃圾；（尤指）厨房垃圾
Garbage is rubbish, especially waste from a kitchen.
例：...a garbage bag.垃圾袋
例：...rotting piles of garbage.成堆腐烂的垃圾

N-UNCOUNT
废话；无聊话
If someone says that an idea or opinion is garbage, they are emphasizing that they believe it is untrue or unimportant.
例：I personally think this is complete garbage...我个人认为这纯粹是废话。
例：Furious government officials branded her story 'garbage'.大发雷霆的政府官员称她的报道为“胡说八道”。

N-UNCOUNT
谨慎；慎重；精明
Prudence is care and good sense that someone shows when making a decision or taking action.
例：Western businessmen are showing remarkable prudence in investing in the region...西方商人在向该地区投资时表现出非同一般的深谋远虑。
例：A lack of prudence may lead to financial problems.不够谨慎可能会导致财政上出现问题。

N-UNCOUNT
放大；扩大
Magnification is the act or process of magnifying something.
例：Pores are visible without magnification...毛孔无须放大就可以看见。
例：I find England strange and unique and beautiful. And for me, London is the magnification of all that.我觉得英格兰可谓奇、绝、美。在我看来，伦敦在这些方面更为明显。

N-VAR
放大率;放大倍数
Magnification is the degree to which a lens, mirror, or other device can magnify an object, or the degree to which the object is magnified.
例：The electron microscope uses a beam of electrons to produce images at high magnifications...电子显微镜利用电子束形成高倍影像。
例：The magnification is 833,333 times the original size.放大倍数为实物大小的833,333倍。

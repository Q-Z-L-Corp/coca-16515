piece 
英[pi:s] 美[pis]
 
n.块，片，段；部分，部件；文章，音乐作品；vt.修补；连接，接上；
 "

N-COUNT
（断下、撕下或切下的）块，片，段，截
A piece of something is an amount of it that has been broken off, torn off, or cut off.
例：...a piece of cake.一块蛋糕
例：...a piece of wood.一截木头

N-COUNT
（尤指可拆卸的）部件，部分
A piece of an object is one of the individual parts or sections which it is made of, especially a part that can be removed.
例：...assembling objects out of standard pieces...用标准部件组装物品
例：The equipment was taken down the shaft in pieces.该设备被拆卸成几部分送下了井。

N-COUNT
（土地的）一块，一片
A piece of land is an area of land.
例：People struggle to get the best piece of land.人们竞相争夺最好的土地。

N-COUNT
（与不可数名词连用）桩，项，条，件
You can use piece of with many uncount nouns to refer to an individual thing of a particular kind. For example, you can refer to some advice as a piece of advice.
例：When I produced this piece of work, my lecturers were very critical...当我拿出这件作品时，我的讲师们提出了诸多批评意见。
例：It is a highly complex piece of legislation.它是一项非常复杂的法规。

N-COUNT
（报纸或杂志上的一篇）文章；（一首或一部）音乐作品；（一段）广播节目；（一出）戏剧
You can refer to an article in a newspaper or magazine, some music written by someone, a broadcast, or a play as a piece .
例：I disagree with Andrew Russell over his piece on British Rail.我不同意安德鲁·拉塞尔那篇关于英国铁路公司的文章。
例：...a vaguely familiar orchestral piece...隐约有点熟悉的一首管弦乐

N-COUNT
（一件）艺术品
You can refer to a work of art as a piece .
例：Each piece is unique, an exquisite painting of a real person, done on ivory...每一件艺术品都是绘在象牙上的精美绝伦的真人肖像，都是独一无二的。
例：None of the pieces is insured.没有一件艺术品上过保险。

N-COUNT
硬币
You can refer to specific coins as pieces. For example, a 10p piece is a coin that is worth 10p.

N-COUNT
棋子
The pieces which you use when you play a board game such as chess are the specially made objects which you move around on the board.

QUANT
（某物的）一部分，一份
A piece of something is part of it or a share of it.
例：They got a small piece of the net profits and a screen credit.他们得到了一小部分纯利润以及电影演职人员字幕中的署名权。
例：...the disclosure that Texas Air might sell a piece or all of Continental.得克萨斯航空公司可能会卖掉部分或整个大陆航空公司这一消息的披露

PHRASE
向…表明不满；对…直言
If you give someone a piece of your mind, you tell them very clearly that you think they have behaved badly.
例：How very thoughtless. I'll give him a piece of my mind.真是太没头脑了，我要告诉他我很生气。

PHRASE
（与…）一致
If something with several different parts is all of a piece, each part is consistent with the others. If one thing is of a piece with another, it is consistent with it.
例：At its peak in the Thirties, Underground design and architecture was all of a piece...在30年代的顶峰时期，地铁的设计和建筑风格都一模一样。
例：The essays that Parsons completed in the latter part of his life are of a piece with his earlier work.帕森斯后半生完成的文章和他的早期著作一脉相承。

PHRASE
安然无恙的；完好无损的
If someone or something is still in one piece after a dangerous journey or experience, they are safe and not damaged or hurt.
例：...providing that my brother gets back alive and in one piece from his mission.如果我哥哥执行任务能够平安归来

PHRASE
说自己想说的话；（毫不保留且不受打断地）发表意见
If you say your piece, you say everything you want to say about a particular matter without being interrupted, although people may be wanting to express opposing views.
例：I'll answer your questions when I've said my piece.说完我想说的，我会回答你的问题。

PHRASE
成碎片地；散了架地；成组件地
You use to pieces in expressions such as 'smash to pieces', and mainly in British English 'fall to pieces' or 'take something to pieces', when you are describing how something is broken or comes apart so that it is in separate pieces.
例：If the shell had hit the boat, it would have blown it to pieces...如果炮弹击中了小船，它早就被炸成碎片了。
例：He took it all to pieces, cleaned it inside and out and put it together again...他把它全拆了，里外清洁了一下，然后又装了起来。

PHRASE
（难过或紧张得）崩溃，垮掉
If you go to pieces, you are so upset or nervous that you lose control of yourself and cannot do what you should do.
例：She's a strong woman, but she nearly went to pieces when Arnie died.她是个坚强的女性，但阿尼去世时，她几乎崩溃了。

PHRASE
把…说得一钱不值；把…批得体无完肤
If someone tears you to pieces ,pulls your work to pieces, or picks your work to pieces, they criticize you or your work very severely.
例：He made numerous errors of fact and was torn to pieces during the subsequent question time...他犯了数不清的事实错误，并在随后的答疑时间中被批得体无完肤。
例：Every error is captured, every decision picked to pieces.每个错误都会被抓住，每个决定都会被骂得一无是处。

PHRASE
下流坯；坏东西；恶棍
If you say that someone is a nasty piece of work, you mean that they are very unkind or unpleasant.

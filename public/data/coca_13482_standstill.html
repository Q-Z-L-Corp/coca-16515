n.停顿；停止，停滞；adj.停顿的，停滞的；引起停顿的；造成停滞的；
 "

N-SING
停止;停顿;停滞
If movement or activity comes to or is brought to a standstill, it stops completely.
例：Abruptly the group ahead of us came to a standstill...我们前面的小组突然停了下来。
例：Production is more or less at a standstill.生产几乎处于停顿状态。

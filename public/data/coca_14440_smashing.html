ADJ
很棒的;极好的
If you describe something or someone as smashing, you mean that you like them very much.
例：It was smashing. I really enjoyed it...真的很棒，我由衷地喜欢。
例：She's a smashing girl.她是一个很棒的女孩。

ADJ
轰动的;极好的;不一般的
A smashing success or victory is one that is impressive and definite.
例：We gave a party and everybody came and I think it was a smashing success...我们举办了一个派对，所有人都来参加了。我认为派对非常成功。
例：Johnson's smashing victory in 1964 changed the political horizon substantially.1964 年约翰逊的大获成功给政界带来了翻天覆地的变化。

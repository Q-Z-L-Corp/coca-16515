PHRASE
吃惊，震惊（以至于无法立刻作出反应）
If you are taken aback by something, you are surprised or shocked by it and you cannot respond at once.
例：Roland was taken aback by our strength of feeling...我们反应如此强烈，令罗兰大吃一惊。

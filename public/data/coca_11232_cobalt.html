cobalt 
英[ˈkəʊbɔ:lt] 美[ˈkoʊbɔlt]
 
n.[化]钴（符号为Co）；钴类颜料；深蓝色；

N-UNCOUNT
钴
Cobalt is a hard silvery-white metal which is used to harden steel and for producing a blue dye.
例：...a country rich in copper, cobalt and diamonds.铜、钴和钻石资源丰富的国家

COLOUR
钴蓝色;深蓝色
Cobalt or cobalt blue is a deep blue colour.
例：They walked past stalls selling huge sprays of crimson, saffron and cobalt flowers.他们经过很多卖花的摊位，摊位上摆着大把大把的鲜花，有深红色的，有金黄色的，还有深蓝色的。
例：...a woman in a soft cobalt blue dress.穿着质地柔软的钴蓝色连衣裙的女人

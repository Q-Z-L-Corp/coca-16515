thick 
英[θɪk] 美[θɪk]
 
adj.浓的，茂密的；厚的，粗大的；稠密的；不透明的；adv.密集地；不清晰地；〈口〉太过分；频频地，时常；n.最厚的部分；最活跃或最激烈的部分；〈口〉笨蛋；〈俚〉可可粉；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
厚的;粗的
Something that is thick has a large distance between its two opposite sides.
例：For breakfast I had a thick slice of bread and syrup...早餐我吃了一片厚厚的涂了糖浆的面包。
例：He wore glasses with thick rims...他戴了一副厚框眼镜。

ADJ
宽的;深的
You can use thick to talk or ask about how wide or deep something is.
例：The folder was two inches thick...那个文件夹有两英寸厚。
例：How thick are these walls?这些墙有多厚？

ADJ-GRADED
浓的;密的;密集的;茂密的
If something that consists of several things is thick, it has a large number of them very close together.
例：She inherited our father's thick, wavy hair...她继承了我们父亲那头浓密的卷发。
例：They walked through thick forest.他们徒步穿越了茂密的森林。

ADJ-GRADED
充满的;密布的
If something is thick with another thing, the first thing is full of or covered with the second.
例：The air is thick with acrid smoke from the fires...空气中弥漫着火灾产生的刺鼻浓烟。
例：She ate scones thick with butter.她吃了一些涂满了黄油的烤饼。

ADJ-GRADED
(衣服)厚的，厚重的，暖和的
Thick clothes are made from heavy cloth, so that they will keep you warm in cold weather.
例：In the winter she wears thick socks, Wellington boots and gloves...冬天，她穿着厚袜子和橡胶雨靴，戴着手套。
例：She wore a thick tartan skirt and a red cashmere sweater.她穿了一条厚厚的格子呢裙和一件红色羊绒衫。

ADJ-GRADED
(烟、雾、云)浓的，混浊的，能见度低的
Thick smoke, fog, or cloud is difficult to see through.
例：The smoke was bluish-black and thick...那烟黑中带蓝，而且很浓。
例：It wasn't very thick fog.雾不算很浓。

ADJ-GRADED
(液体)浓的，稠的
Thick liquids are fairly stiff and solid and do not flow easily.
例：They had to battle through thick mud to reach construction workers...他们只好挣扎着穿过厚厚的泥巴去找建筑工人。
例：The sauce is thick and rich so don't bother trying to diet.这个酱汁又浓又香，别费劲节食了。

ADJ-GRADED
(声音)不清楚的，沙哑的，低沉的
If someone's voice is thick, they are not speaking clearly, for example because they are ill, upset, or drunk.
例：When he spoke his voice was thick with bitterness.他说话时，声音低沉，充满痛苦。

ADJ-GRADED
(口音)重的，浓的
A thick accent is very obvious and easy to identify.
例：He answered our questions in English but with a thick accent...他用英语回答了我们的问题，不过口音很重。
例：'What do you want?' a teenage girl demanded in a thick German accent.“你想要什么？”一个十几岁少女操着一口浓重的德国口音问道。

ADJ-GRADED
笨的;蠢的
If you describe someone as thick, you think they are stupid.
例：How could she have been so thick?她怎么能蠢到那个地步？

PHRASE
大量而迅速地;接二连三地
If things happen thick and fast, they happen very quickly and in large numbers.
例：The rumours have been coming thick and fast...谣言接二连三地传来。
例：Distress calls were pouring in thick and fast from all over the area.求救电话从该地区各地蜂拥而至。

PHRASE
积极从事;深度参与
If you are in the thick of an activity or situation, you are very involved in it.
例：I enjoy being in the thick of things...我喜欢全身心投入到事情当中。
例：Peterson suddenly found himself in the thick of desperate fighting.彼得森突然发现自己身陷一场殊死搏斗中。

PHRASE
不顾艰难险阻;历尽千辛万苦
If you do something through thick and thin, you do it although the conditions or circumstances are very bad.
例：She'd stuck by Bob through thick and thin...她对鲍勃不离不弃，甘苦与共。
例：I will go on loving James through thick and thin no matter what happens.不管发生什么，不管经历多少艰辛，我都会一直爱着詹姆斯。

PRON-SING
我(第一人称单数宾格代词)
A speaker or writer uses me to refer to himself or herself. Me is a first person singular pronoun. Me is used as the object of a verb or a preposition.
例：I had to make important decisions that would affect me for the rest of my life...我不得不作出将影响我后半生的重大决定。
例：He asked me to go to Cambridge with him...他要我和他一起去剑桥。

holder 
英[ˈhəʊldə(r)] 美[ˈhoʊldə(r)]
 
n.支持物；持有者；（支票等）持有人；支托（或握持）…之物；
 "

N-COUNT
拥有人;持有人
A holder is someone who owns or has something.
例：This season the club has had 73,500 season-ticket holders.本赛季该俱乐部已经有73,500名持有季票的观众。
例：...Peter Koech, the record holder in the 3,000 metres steeplechase.3,000米障碍赛跑的纪录保持者彼得·克奇

N-COUNT
盛放器;支托物
A holder is a container in which you put an object, usually in order to protect it or to keep it in place.
例：...a toothbrush holder.牙刷托
例：...a cigarette holder.烟嘴

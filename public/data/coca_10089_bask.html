bask 
英[bɑ:sk] 美[bæsk]
 
vi.晒太阳，取暖；享受乐趣，得到满足；
 "

VERB
（舒适地）晒太阳（取暖）
If you bask in the sunshine, you lie somewhere sunny and enjoy the heat.
例：All through the hot, still days of their holiday Amy basked in the sun...在整个炎热、安静的假日，埃米一直在晒太阳。
例：Crocodiles bask on the small sandy beaches.鳄鱼在小沙滩上晒太阳。

VERB
沉浸于，尽情享受（赞美、宠爱或崇拜）
If you bask in someone's approval, favour, or admiration, you greatly enjoy their positive reaction towards you.
例：He has spent a month basking in the adulation of the fans back in Jamaica...他整整一个月都沉浸在牙买加影迷们的追捧中。
例：Livy smiled and basked in Rachel's approval.莉薇微笑着，雷切尔的赞许令她如沐春风。

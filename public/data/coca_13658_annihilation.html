annihilation 
英[əˌnaɪə'leɪʃn] 美[əˌnaɪəˈleʃən]
 
n.歼灭；灭绝；<物>（粒子与反粒子的）湮灭；湮没；
 "

VERB
歼灭；消灭；毁灭；彻底击溃
To annihilate something means to destroy it completely.
例：There are lots of ways of annihilating the planet...毁灭地球有很多方法。
例：The Army was annihilated.陆军被彻底击溃了。

VERB
（在竞赛或争论中）彻底击败，大胜
If you annihilate someone in a contest or argument, you totally defeat them.
例：The Dutch annihilated the Olympic champions 5-0.荷兰队以5比0完胜奥运冠军队。

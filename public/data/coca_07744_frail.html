frail 
英[freɪl] 美[frel]
 
adj.脆弱的，虚弱的；意志薄弱的，不坚定的；易损的，易碎的；n.<美俚>少女，少妇；灯心草篓；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
虚弱的;体弱的
Someone who is frail is not very strong or healthy.
例：She lay in bed looking particularly frail.她躺在床上，看上去特别虚弱。

ADJ-GRADED
易碎的;易坏的
Something that is frail is easily broken or damaged.
例：The frail craft rocked as he clambered in.他爬进来的时候，那件易碎的工艺品摇晃起来。

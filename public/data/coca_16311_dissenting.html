N-UNCOUNT
(尤指与多数人或权威人士的)意见不一致,意见分歧
Dissent is strong disagreement or dissatisfaction with a decision or opinion, especially one that is supported by most people or by people in authority.
例：He is the toughest military ruler yet and has responded harshly to any dissent...他是迄今最强硬的军事统治者，对任何异议都一律进行打压。
例：Political dissent would no longer be tolerated...政见不和将不再被容忍。

VERB
不同意，持异议(尤指与多数人或权威人士支持的观点、决定相左)
If you dissent, you express disagreement with a decision or opinion, especially one that is supported by most people or by people in authority.
例：Just one of the 10 members dissented...只有1/10的成员不同意。
例：No one dissents from the decision to unify...没有人反对统一的决定。

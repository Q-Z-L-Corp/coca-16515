prep.（表示位置）在…中间；（表示环境）处于…环境中；由于存在…情况；（表示让步）尽管有…的情况；

PREP
在…过程中；在…中
If something happens amid noises or events of some kind, it happens while the other things are happening.
例：A senior leader cancelled a trip to Britain yesterday amid growing signs of a possible political crisis...爆发政治危机的迹象日趋明显，一位高级领导人昨天取消了预定的英国之行。
例：Children were changing classrooms amid laughter and shouts.孩子们在欢笑声和喊叫声中换教室。

PREP
在…中间；在…之中
If something is amid other things, it is surrounded by them.
例：...a tiny bungalow amid clusters of trees.在树丛中的一间小平房

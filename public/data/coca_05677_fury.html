fury 
英[ˈfjʊəri] 美[ˈfjʊri]
 
n.狂怒；[罗神]复仇三女神之一；暴怒；激怒者；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
狂怒;暴怒
Fury is violent or very strong anger.
例：She screamed, her face distorted with fury and pain.她尖叫着，脸部因狂怒和痛苦而扭曲。

PHRASE
异常愤怒;怒不可遏
If you are in a fury, you are very angry.
例：I had reacted in a fury of grief...我的反应是悲愤交加。
例：He rose to his feet in a fury to leave no doubt about where he stood on the issue.他怒不可遏地站起身来，这就清楚地表明了他在这个问题上的态度。

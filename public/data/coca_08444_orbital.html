ADJ
（道路）环城的，绕城的，外环的
An orbital road goes all the way round a large city.
例：...a new orbital road round Paris.一条新修的巴黎外环道路

轨道的
Orbital describes things relating to the orbit of an object in space.
例：The newly discovered world followed an orbital path unlike that of any other planet.新发现的这个天体具有不同于任何其他行星的运行轨道。

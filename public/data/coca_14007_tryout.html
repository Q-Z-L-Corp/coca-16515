N-COUNT
试用;试验
If you give something a tryout, you try it or test it to see how useful it is.
例：The recycling scheme gets its first try-out in rural Dorset.这些再循环方案在多塞特郡的农村地区首先试用。

N-COUNT
(争取入队资格的)角逐赛，选拔赛;(争取角色的)试演

例：…tryouts for the U.S. junior national athletics team.入选美国国家少年体育队的选拔赛

benevolent 
英[bəˈnevələnt] 美[bəˈnɛvələnt]
 
adj.好心肠的；与人为善的；乐善好施的；慈善的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（当权者）仁慈的，慈爱的，乐善好施的
If you describe a person in authority as benevolent, you mean that they are kind and fair.
例：The company has proved to be a most benevolent employer...事实证明，这家公司是非常好的雇主。
例：They believe that the country needs a benevolent dictator.他们认为国家需要一位仁爱的独裁者。

ADJ
（用于慈善机构名称）慈善的，救济的
Benevolent is used in the names of some organizations that give money and help to people who need it.
例：...the Army Benevolent Fund.陆军慈善基金

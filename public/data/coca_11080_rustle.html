vt.& vi.发出沙沙的声音；n.沙沙声；急忙；飒飒声；

V-ERG
(使)发出沙沙声;(使)沙沙作响
When something thin and dry rustles or when you rustle it, it makes soft sounds as it moves.
例：The leaves rustled in the wind...树叶在风中沙沙作响。
例：She rustled her papers impatiently...她不耐烦地把文件翻得沙沙作响。

ADJ-GRADED
关怀体贴的;关心照顾的；有同情心的
If someone is caring, they are affectionate, helpful, and sympathetic.
例：He is a lovely boy, very gentle and caring.他是个可爱的男孩,非常温柔体贴。
例：...a loving, caring husband.爱意绵绵、知冷知热的丈夫

ADJ
(职业)护理的，关怀的，照顾他人的
The caring professions are those such as nursing and social work that are involved with looking after people who are ill or who need help in coping with their lives.
例：The course is also suitable for those in the caring professions.本门课程同样适合护理业从业人士学习。
例：...the caring services.护理服务

architecture 
英[ˈɑ:kɪtektʃə(r)] 美[ˈɑrkɪtektʃə(r)]
 
n.建筑学；建筑风格；体系结构；（总体、层次）结构；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
建筑学;建筑术
Architecture is the art of planning, designing, and constructing buildings.
例：He studied classical architecture and design in Rome.他在罗马学习了古典建筑学和设计。

N-UNCOUNT
建筑式样；建筑风格
The architecture of a building is the style in which it is designed and constructed.
例：...modern architecture.现代建筑式样
例：...a fine example of Moroccan architecture.摩洛哥建筑风格的典范

N-UNCOUNT
结构;架构
The architecture of something is its structure.
例：...the crumbling intellectual architecture of modern society.现代社会处于崩溃边缘的知识结构
例：...the architecture of muscle fibres.肌肉纤维的结构

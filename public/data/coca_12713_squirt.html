squirt 
英[skwɜ:t] 美[skwɜrt]
 
vt.喷射，喷湿；注射；vi.喷出；n.喷，细的喷流；注射器；傲慢无礼的年轻人；喷气式飞机；
 "

V-ERG
(使)(液体)喷射
If you squirt a liquid somewhere or if it squirts somewhere, the liquid comes out of a narrow opening in a thin fast stream.
例：Norman cut open his pie and squirted tomato sauce into it...诺尔曼切开自己的派，往里面挤了点番茄酱。
例：The water squirted from its throat...水从它的喉部喷出来。

VERB
朝…喷…
If you squirt something with a liquid, you squirt the liquid at it.
例：I squirted him with water...我朝他喷水。
例：Its linings were simply squirted with oil.它的内衬只是简单地喷了些油。

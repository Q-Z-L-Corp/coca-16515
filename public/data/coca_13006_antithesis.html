antithesis 
英[ænˈtɪθəsɪs] 美[ænˈtɪθɪsɪs]
 
n.对立；对立面；对照；对偶；

N-COUNT
对立面；形成对立（或对照）的事物
The antithesis of something is its exact opposite.
例：The little black dress is the anithesis of fussy dressing.与花哨的打扮形成鲜明对比的是这件小黑裙子。

N-COUNT
（二者间的）对比，对照
If there is an antithesis between two things, there is a contrast between them.
例：...the antithesis between instinct and reason.本能和理智的对立

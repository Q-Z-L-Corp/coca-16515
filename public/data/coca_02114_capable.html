capable 
英[ˈkeɪpəbl] 美[ˈkepəbəl]
 
adj.能干的；有才能的；有才华的；能胜任的；n.有才能的，能干的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有…能力的;能够…的
If a person or thing is capable of doing something, they have the ability to do it.
例：He appeared hardly capable of conducting a coherent conversation...他好像连话都说不清楚。
例：The kitchen is capable of catering for several hundred people...厨房能同时容纳数百人进餐。

ADJ-GRADED
熟练的;胜任的;有能力的
Someone who is capable has the skill or qualities necessary to do a particular thing well, or is able to do most things well.
例：She's a very capable speaker...她能说会道。
例：Her husband was such a fine, capable man.她丈夫又体贴又能干。

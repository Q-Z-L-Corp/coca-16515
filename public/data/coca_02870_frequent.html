frequent 
英[ˈfri:kwənt] 美[ˈfrikwənt]
 
adj.频繁的，时常发生的，常见的；（脉搏等）急促的，快的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
经常性的;频繁的
If something is frequent, it happens often.
例：Bordeaux is on the main Paris-Madrid line so there are frequent trains...波尔多位于巴黎到马德里的铁路主干线上，来往列车频繁。
例：He is a frequent visitor to the house.他是这家的常客。

VERB
常到;常去;常出入于
If someone frequents a particular place, they regularly go there.
例：I hear he frequents the Cajun restaurant in Hampstead.我听说他经常光顾汉普斯特德的卡津餐厅。

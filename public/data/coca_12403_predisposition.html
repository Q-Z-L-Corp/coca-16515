predisposition 
英[ˌpri:dɪspəˈzɪʃn] 美[ˌpridɪspəˈzɪʃən]
 
n.倾向，素质；易染病体质；
 "

N-COUNT
倾向；禀性
If you have a predisposition to behave in a particular way, you tend to behave like that because of the kind of person that you are or the attitudes that you have.
例：...a woman's predisposition to use the right side of her brain...女性使用右脑的倾向
例：There is a thin dividing line between educating the public and creating a predisposition to panic.对公众进行教育和制造恐慌倾向之间没有明显的分界。

N-COUNT
（易患某种疾病的）倾向，体质
If you have a predisposition to a disease or illness, it is likely that you will suffer from that disease or illness.
例：...a genetic predisposition to lung cancer.易患肺癌的遗传倾向

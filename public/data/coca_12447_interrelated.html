V-RECIP
相互关联;相互影响
If two or more things interrelate, there is a connection between them and they have an effect on each other.
例：The body and the mind interrelate...身体与头脑相互影响。
例：Each of these cells have their specific jobs to do, but they also interrelate with each other.这些细胞都有各自的具体分工,但它们也相互联系。

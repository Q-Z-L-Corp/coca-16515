conquer 
英[ˈkɒŋkə(r)] 美[ˈkɑŋkə(r)]
 
vt.征服；攻克；打败（敌人）；克服；vi.得胜，胜利；
 "

VERB
占领;征服
If one country or group of people conquers another, they take complete control of their land.
例：During 1936, Mussolini conquered Abyssinia...1936年，墨索里尼攻占了阿比西尼亚。
例：Early in the eleventh century the whole of England was again conquered by the Vikings.11世纪早期，整个英格兰再次被北欧海盗征服。

VERB
克服;攻克
If you conquer something such as a problem, you succeed in ending it or dealing with it successfully.
例：I was certain that love was quite enough to conquer our differences...我相信爱足以克服我们的种种差异。
例：He has never conquered his addiction to smoking.他一直未能戒除烟瘾。

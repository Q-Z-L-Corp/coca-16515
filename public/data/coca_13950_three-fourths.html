QUANT
四分之三(美国英语)
In American English, people sometimes use three-fourths to mean three-quarters .
例：Three-fourths of the apartments in the ghetto had no heat...贫民区有 3/4 的公寓没有暖气。
例：Government expenditures absorbed nearly three-fourths of the national income.政府开支耗费了将近 3/4 的国民收入。

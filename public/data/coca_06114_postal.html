ADJ
邮政的；邮政业务的
Postal is used to describe things or people connected with the public service of carrying letters and packages from one place to another.
例：Compensation for lost or damaged mail will be handled by the postal service...对丢失或毁坏邮件的赔偿将由邮政部门处理。
例：Include your full postal address.写上完整的邮政地址。

ADJ
邮寄的；邮递的
Postal is used to describe activities that involve sending things by post.
例：Unions would elect their leadership by secret postal ballot.工会将通过秘密邮寄选票的方式选举领导人。
例：...a postal course in freelance photography.自由摄影的函授课程

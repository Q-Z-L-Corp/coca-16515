N-UNCOUNT
先见之明;预见;深谋远虑
Someone's foresight is their ability to see what is likely to happen in the future and to take appropriate action.
例：He was later criticised for his lack of foresight...他后来被指责缺少先见之明。
例：They had the foresight to invest in new technology.他们有投资新技术的远见。

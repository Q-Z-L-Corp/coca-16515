convey 
英[kənˈveɪ] 美[kənˈve]
 
vt.传达，传递；运送，输送；[法]让与，转让（财产等）；表达；
 "

VERB
传达;表达;传递
To convey information or feelings means to cause them to be known or understood by someone.
例：When I returned home, I tried to convey the wonder of this machine to my husband...回到家后，我竭力地想把这台机器的精妙之处描述给丈夫听。
例：In every one of her pictures she conveys a sense of immediacy...她拍的每一张照片中都传递出一种临场感，使人有如亲临现场。

VERB
运输;运送;输送
To convey someone or something to a place means to carry or transport them there.
例：The railway company extended a branch line to Brightlingsea to convey fish direct to Billingsgate.铁路公司修建了一条通往布赖特灵西的支线，以便直接将鱼运送到比林斯门。

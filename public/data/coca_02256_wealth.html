wealth 
英[welθ] 美[wɛlθ]
 
n.财产，财富；富豪（阶级）；丰富，大量；繁荣；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
财富;财产
Wealth is the possession of a large amount of money, property, or other valuable things. You can also refer to a particular person's money or property as their wealth .
例：Economic reform has brought relative wealth to peasant farmers...经济改革给农民带来了相对的财富。
例：His own wealth grew.他的个人财富在增长。

N-SING
大量;丰富
If you say that someone or something has a wealth of good qualities or things, you are emphasizing that they have a very large number or amount of them.
例：...such a wealth of creative expertise...如此丰富的创造性专门技能
例：The city boasts a wealth of beautiful churches.这座城市拥有众多美丽的教堂。

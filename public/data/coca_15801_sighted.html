ADJ
有视力的;看得见的;不盲的
Sighted people have the ability to see. This word is usually used to contrast people who can see with people who are blind.
例：Blind children tend to be more passive in this area of motor development than sighted children.失明的孩子在动作发展的这一方面往往比不失明的孩子更加被动。

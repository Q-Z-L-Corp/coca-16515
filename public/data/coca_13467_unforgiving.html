unforgiving 
英[ˌʌnfəˈgɪvɪŋ] 美[ˌʌnfərˈgɪvɪŋ]
 
adj.不可宽恕的，不能原谅的；
 "

ADJ
不宽容的；不原谅人的
If you describe someone as unforgiving, you mean that they are unwilling to forgive other people.
例：He was an unforgiving man who never forgot a slight...他斤斤计较，很不大度。
例：He finds human foibles endearing, but is unforgiving of pretension.他觉得人性的弱点很可爱，但不能容忍惺惺作态。

ADJ-GRADED
（形势或活动）无情的，难以应付的，不利的
If you describe a situation or activity as unforgiving, you mean that it causes a lot of people to experience great difficulty or failure, even people who deserve to succeed.
例：Business is a competitive activity. It is very fierce and very unforgiving.商场如战场，竞争激烈，不近人情。

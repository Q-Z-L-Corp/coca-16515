ADJ-GRADED
凄惨的;糟透的;蹩脚的
You use abject to emphasize that a situation or quality is extremely bad.
例：Both of them died in abject poverty...他们两人都于穷困潦倒中死去。
例：This scheme was an abject failure.这项计划彻底失败了。

ADJ-GRADED
卑躬屈节的；自卑的；怯懦的
If you describe someone as abject, you think they have no courage or respect for themselves.
例：He sounded abject and eager to please...他低声下气，急于讨好别人。
例：He looked back at the abject, silent girl and repeated his question.他转过头看着这个噤若寒蝉的女孩，又重复了一遍他的问题。

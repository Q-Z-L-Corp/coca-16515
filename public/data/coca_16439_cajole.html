cajole 
英[kəˈdʒəʊl] 美[kəˈdʒoʊl]
 
vt.劝诱，以甜言蜜语哄骗；vt.& vi.勾引；
 "

VERB
哄骗;劝诱;说服
If you cajole someone into doing something, you get them to do it after persuading them for some time.
例：It was he who had cajoled Garland into doing the film...是他劝服嘉兰拍这部片子的。
例：He cajoled Mr Dobson to stand for mayor.他说服多布森先生竞选市长。

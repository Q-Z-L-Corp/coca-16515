episodic 
英[ˌepɪˈsɒdɪk] 美[ˌepɪˈsɑdɪk]
 
adj.（小说、剧本等）由松散片段组成的，插曲般的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不定期发生的;偶尔发生的
Something that is episodic occurs at irregular and infrequent intervals.
例：...episodic attacks of fever.不定期的发烧

ADJ-GRADED
（写作或电影）有许多片段的，不连贯的
An episodic piece of writing or film consists of a series of events, which often seem not to be connected.
例：...an episodic narrative of unrelated characters connected only by time and place.仅靠时间和地点把那些不相关的角色联系在一起的片段式叙述

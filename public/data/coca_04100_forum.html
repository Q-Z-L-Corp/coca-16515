N-COUNT
(尤指讨论重大公共问题的)论坛，会议场所，讨论会
A forum is a place, situation, or group in which people exchange ideas and discuss issues, especially important public issues.
例：Members of the council agreed that it still had an important role as a forum for discussion...理事会成员一致认为它仍然发挥着论坛的重要作用。
例：The organisation would provide a forum where problems could be discussed.这个组织将提供一个可以讨论问题的平台。

N-COUNT
(古罗马城镇中讨论商业和政治问题的)广场
In ancient Roman towns, the forum was a square where people met to discuss business and political matters.

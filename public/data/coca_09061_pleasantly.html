ADJ-GRADED
令人愉快的；舒适的；合意的
Something that is pleasant is nice, enjoyable, or attractive.
例：I've got a pleasant little apartment...我有一套舒适的小公寓。
例：It's always pleasant to do what you're good at doing.做自己擅长的事总是令人愉快的。

ADJ-GRADED
友好的；可亲的；讨人喜欢的
Someone who is pleasant is friendly and likeable.
例：The woman had a pleasant face...这位女士面相和善。

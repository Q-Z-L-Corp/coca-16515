ADV-GRADED
立即；马上；及时
If you do something promptly, you do it immediately.
例：Sister Francesca entered the chapel, took her seat, and promptly fell asleep.弗朗西丝卡修女走进小教堂，坐下来，很快就睡着了。

ADV
准时地
If you do something promptly at a particular time, you do it at exactly that time.
例：Promptly at a quarter past seven, we left the hotel.我们于7点一刻准时离开了宾馆。

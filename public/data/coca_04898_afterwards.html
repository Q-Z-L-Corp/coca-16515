ADV
（某个事件或已提及的时间）之后
If you do something or if something happens afterwards, you do it or it happens after a particular event or time that has already been mentioned.
例：Shortly afterwards, police arrested four suspects...之后不久，警方逮捕了4名嫌犯。
例：James was taken to hospital but died soon afterwards...詹姆斯被送往医院，但随后不久就死了。

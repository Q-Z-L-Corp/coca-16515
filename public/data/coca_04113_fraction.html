fraction 
英[ˈfrækʃn] 美[ˈfrækʃən]
 
n.[数]分数；一小部分，些微；不相连的一块，片段；[化]分馏；
 "

N-COUNT
少量;小份;一点儿
A fraction of something is a tiny amount or proportion of it.
例：She hesitated for a fraction of a second before responding...她在回答之前犹豫了一下。
例：Here's how to eat like the stars, at a fraction of the cost...这就是如何花少量钱却能像明星一样吃喝的方法。

N-COUNT
分数
A fraction is a number that can be expressed as a proportion of two whole numbers. For example, ½ and ⅓ are both fractions.
例：The students had a grasp of decimals, percentages and fractions.学生们掌握了小数、百分数和分数。

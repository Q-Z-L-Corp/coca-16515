N-COUNT
放慢;放缓
A slowdown is a reduction in speed or activity.
例：There has been a sharp slowdown in economic growth.经济增长的速度明显放慢。

N-COUNT
怠工
A slowdown is a protest in which workers deliberately work slowly and cause problems for their employers.
例：It's impossible to assess how many officers are participating in the slowdown.要估算出究竟多少高级职员参与了怠工是不可能的。

dissatisfied 
英[dɪsˈsætɪsfaɪd] 美[dɪsˈsætɪsˌfaɪd]
 
adj.感到不满的，不满意的，不高兴的；显出不满的；v.使不满意；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不满的;不高兴的
If you are dissatisfied with something, you are not contented or pleased with it.
例：82% of voters are dissatisfied with the way their country is being governed...82% 的选民对本国的治理方式不满意。
例：Dissatisfied customers can return the product for a full refund...顾客如不满意可以全额退货。

ADJ-GRADED
分散的;散布的
Things that are dispersed are situated in many different places, a long way apart from each other.
例：...his widely dispersed businesses...他的布局广泛的企业网点
例：They live high in the Andes, in small and dispersed groups.他们零星散居在安第斯山脉的高处。

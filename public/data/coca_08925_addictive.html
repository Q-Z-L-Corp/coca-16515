addictive 
英[əˈdɪktɪv] 美[əˈdɪktɪv]
 
adj.使成瘾的；上瘾的；易令人沉溺的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
使人成瘾的
If a drug is addictive, people who take it cannot stop taking it.
例：Cigarettes are highly addictive...香烟很容易使人上瘾。
例：Crack is the most addictive drug on the market.强效纯可卡因是市面上最容易让人上瘾的毒品。

ADJ-GRADED
使人着迷的
Something that is addictive is so enjoyable that it makes you want to do it or have it a lot.
例：Video movie-making can quickly become addictive.制作视频电影能很快让人入迷。

ADJ-GRADED
易着迷的；易上瘾的
If someone has an addictive personality, they easily become addicted to something.
例：I have an eating disorder and an addictive personality.我饮食没有规律，而且性格上容易沉迷某事。

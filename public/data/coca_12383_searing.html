searing 
英[ˈsɪərɪŋ] 美[ˈsɪrɪŋ]
 
adj.烧灼的；灼热的；炽热的；引起或描写强烈情感的；v.烧焦( sear的现在分词)；
 "

ADJ
灼热的;强烈的
Searing is used to indicate that something such as pain or heat is very intense.
例：She woke to feel a searing pain in her feet.她醒来感到脚上火辣辣地痛。
例：...the searing heat of the Saudi Arabian desert.沙特阿拉伯沙漠的灼热

ADJ-GRADED
(言谈、文章)吹毛求疵的，尖刻的,苛责的
A searing speech or piece of writing is very critical.
例：...searing criticism.尖刻的批评

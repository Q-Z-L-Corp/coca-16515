nationality 
英[ˌnæʃəˈnæləti] 美[ˌnæʃə'næləti]
 
n.国籍；国家；民族性；部落；
 "

N-VAR
国籍
If you have the nationality of a particular country, you were born there or have the legal right to be a citizen.
例：Asked his nationality, he said British...被问及国籍时，他说是英国。
例：The crew are of different nationalities and have no common language.船员来自不同国家，语言不通。

N-COUNT
(尤指未建立独立国家的)民族
You can refer to people who have the same racial origins as a nationality, especially when they do not have their own independent country.
例：...the many nationalities that comprise Ethiopia.组成埃塞俄比亚的众多民族

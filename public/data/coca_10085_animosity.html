N-UNCOUNT
敌意；怨恨；憎恶
Animosity is a strong feeling of dislike and anger. Animosities are feelings of this kind.
例：There's a long history of animosity between the two nations...两国之间宿怨已久。
例：Sir Geoffrey had no personal animosity towards the Prime Minister.杰弗里爵士对首相并无私人恩怨。

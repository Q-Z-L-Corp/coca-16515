N-VAR
不连贯;间断性;不连续性
Discontinuity in a process is a lack of smooth or continuous development.
例：The text is good in parts, but suffers from discontinuity...文章分开看还好，但是问题在于不连贯。
例：There may appear to be discontinuities between broadcasts.广播节目中可能会出现断音。

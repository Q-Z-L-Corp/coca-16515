flak 
英[flæk] 美[flæk]
 
n.高射炮；过分的或抨击性的批评；争吵，敌对；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
严厉批评;抨击;责难
If you get a lot of flak from someone, they criticize you severely. If you take the flak, you get the blame for something.
例：The President is getting a lot of flak for that...总统正因为那件事备受指责。
例：In recent years they have attracted more than their fair share of flak from the press.最近几年他们受到了新闻界过多的责难。

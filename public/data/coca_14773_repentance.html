repentance 
英[rɪˈpentəns] 美[rɪˈpɛntəns]
 
n.悔悟；悔改；后悔；忏悔；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
后悔;悔改;悔意;忏悔
If you show repentance for something wrong that you have done, you make it clear that you are sorry for doing it.
例：They showed no repentance during their trial.在他们受审的过程中没有表现出一丝悔意。

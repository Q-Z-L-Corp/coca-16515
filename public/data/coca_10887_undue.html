ADJ
过分的；过多的；过度的
If you describe something bad as undue, you mean that it is greater or more extreme than you think is reasonable or appropriate.
例：This would help the families to survive the drought without undue suffering...这会帮助那些家庭度过干旱期，不至于遭受太多的苦。
例：It might give the Commission undue influence over the coming negotiations...这可能会在即将开启的谈判问题上给委员会带来过多的影响。

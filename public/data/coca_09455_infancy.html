N-UNCOUNT
婴儿期;幼年
Infancy is the period of your life when you are a very young child.
例：...minute details of Deborah's infancy.德博拉幼年生活的详尽细节
例：...the development of the mind from infancy onwards.从婴儿期开始的心智发展

N-UNCOUNT
初期;初创阶段
If something is in its infancy, it is new and has not developed very much.
例：Computing science was still in its infancy.计算科学仍然处于初期阶段。

ADV
在（或到）舞台上
When someone such as an actor or musician goes onstage, they go onto the stage in a theatre to give a performance.
例：When she walked onstage she was given a standing ovation...当她走上台时，观众起立鼓掌欢迎。
例：You have to be onstage at eight o'clock...你务必8点钟上台演出。

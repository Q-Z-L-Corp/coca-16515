unhealthy 
英[ʌnˈhelθi] 美[ʌnˈhɛlθi]
 
adj.不健康的；对健康有害的；危险的；反常的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
可能致病的；不健康的
Something that is unhealthy is likely to cause illness or poor health.
例：Avoid unhealthy foods such as hamburger and chips...不要吃不健康食品，如汉堡和薯条。
例：He worked in the notoriously unhealthy environment of a coal mine.他在一个环境出了名恶劣的煤矿里工作。

ADJ-GRADED
不舒服的；身体不适的
If you are unhealthy, you are not very fit or well.
例：I'm quite unhealthy really.我真的很不舒服。
例：...a poorly dressed, unhealthy looking fellow with a poor complexion.衣衫褴褛、脸色憔悴、一副病容的人

ADJ-GRADED
（经济体或公司）状况不佳的，面临财务危机的
An unhealthy economy or company is financially weak and unsuccessful.
例：The redundancy of skilled and experienced workers is a terrible waste and a clear sign of an unhealthy economy.技术熟练、经验丰富的工人过剩是可怕的浪费，也是经济运行不良的明显征兆。

ADJ-GRADED
（行为或爱好）不正常的，病态的
If you describe someone's behaviour or interests as unhealthy, you do not consider them to be normal and think they may involve mental problems.
例：Frank has developed what I would term an unhealthy relationship with these people...弗兰克与这些人的关系在我看来不正常。
例：This government has developed an unhealthy obsession with secrecy...本届政府在保密方面做过了头。

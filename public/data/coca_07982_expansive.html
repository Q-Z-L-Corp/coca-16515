expansive 
英[ɪkˈspænsɪv] 美[ɪkˈspænsɪv]
 
adj.易扩张的，膨胀性的；胸襟开阔的，豁达的；辽阔的；健谈的；
 "

ADJ
广袤的;辽阔的;全面的
If something is expansive, it covers or includes a large area or many things.
例：...an expansive grassy play area...广阔的游乐草地
例：They have played an expansive style of rugby.他们采用向外展开打法打了一场橄榄球。

ADJ-GRADED
豪爽的;开朗的;健谈的
If you are expansive, you talk a lot, or are friendly or generous, because you are feeling happy and relaxed.
例：He was becoming more expansive as he relaxed...他放松的时候变得更加开朗。
例：The premier was in expansive mood.总理心情开朗。

ADJ-GRADED
扩张的;发展的
If you describe something such as a period of time or an economy as expansive, you mean that it is associated with growth or expansion.
例：An active and expansive market economy is a necessary condition for progress.积极扩张的市场经济是发展的必要条件。

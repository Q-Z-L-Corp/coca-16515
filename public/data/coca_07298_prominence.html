N-UNCOUNT

If someone or something is in a position of prominence, they are well-known and important.
例：He came to prominence during the World Cup in Italy...他在意大利的世界杯赛中声名鹊起。
例：Crime prevention had to be given more prominence.防止犯罪方面应当受到更多的重视。

ADV
正常地;通常;平常
If you say that something normally happens or that you normally do a particular thing, you mean that it is what usually happens or what you usually do.
例：All airports in the country are working normally today...今天国内所有机场都运转正常。
例：Social progress is normally a matter of struggles and conflicts...社会进步通常是斗争和冲突的结果。

ADV
常规地;按惯例
If you do something normally, you do it in the usual or conventional way.
例：She would apparently eat normally and then make herself sick.她显然还会照吃不误，然后把自己吃到恶心。
例：...failure of the blood to clot normally.血液未能正常凝结

amuse 
英[əˈmju:z] 美[əˈmjuz]
 
vt.使人发笑；逗乐；使消遣；娱乐；
 "

VERB
逗乐；逗笑
If something amuses you, it makes you want to laugh or smile.
例：The thought seemed to amuse him...这种想法好像把他逗乐了。
例：Their antics never fail to amuse.他们滑稽的举止总是让人发笑。

VERB
给…提供娱乐（或消遣）
If you amuse yourself, you do something in order to pass the time and not become bored.
例：I need distractions. I need to amuse myself so I won't keep thinking about things...我需要分散我的注意力。需要消遣一下，让自己不去想这样那样的事。
例：Put a selection of baby toys in his cot to amuse him if he wakes early.要在婴儿床里放一些玩具，以便他醒得早时可以自己玩。

deal 
英[di:l] 美[dil]
 
vt.[牌戏]分；分配；经营；施予；n.（一笔）交易；许多；待遇；发牌；vi.论述；（有效地或成功地）处理；惩处；交易；adj.冷杉木制的，松木制的；
 "

QUANT
许多;大量
If you say that you need or have a great deal of or a good deal of a particular thing, you are emphasizing that you need or have a lot of it.
例：...a great deal of money...大量的钱
例：I am in a position to save you a good deal of time.我能够为你节省许多时间。

QUANT
许多;很多
A deal of something is a lot of it.
例：He had a deal of work to do.他有许多工作要做。

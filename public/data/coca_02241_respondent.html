N-COUNT
(调查和问题的)回答者;答卷人
A respondent is a person who replies to something such as a survey or set of questions.
例：60 percent of the respondents said they disapproved of the president's performance.60％的调查对象表示不认可总统的表现。

N-COUNT
被告
A respondent is someone who has to defend a case in a law court.
例：In addition, the respondent disclosed professional confidences to one of the patients.另外，被告还向其中一位病人透露了行业机密。

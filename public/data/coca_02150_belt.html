belt 
英[belt] 美[bɛlt]
 
n.腰带；区域；传送带；带状物；vt.环绕，包围；用带做记号；用力打；绕着系上带子；
 "

N-COUNT
腰带；裤带；皮带
A belt is a strip of leather or cloth that you fasten round your waist.
例：He wore a belt with a large brass buckle.他系着一条带大铜扣的腰带。

N-COUNT
（机器的）皮带，输送带
A belt in a machine is a circular strip of rubber that is used to drive moving parts or to move objects along.
例：The turning disc is connected by a drive belt to an electric motor.转盘由一根传动带与电机相连。

N-COUNT
地带；带状区域
A belt of land or sea is a long, narrow area of it that has some special feature.
例：Miners in Zambia's northern copper belt have gone on strike...赞比亚北部铜矿带的矿工已经罢工了。
例：Behind him was a belt of trees, and behind the trees hills and fields.在他身后是一片树林，树林后面是小山和田地。

VERB
抽打；痛打
If someone belts you, they hit you very hard.
例：'Is it right she belted old George in the gut?' she asked.“她真的把老乔治痛打了一顿？”她问。

VERB
飞奔；疾驰
If you belt somewhere, you move or travel there very fast.
例：We belted down Iveagh Parade to where the motor was.我们沿着艾维格大街一路飞奔到汽车那儿。

N-COUNT
（表示柔道或空手道级别的）段，带
If someone is or has a belt of a particular colour in judo or karate, they have reached the standard which that colour represents.
例：He is a black belt in karate.他是空手道黑带选手。

PHRASE
残酷而不公正的
Something that is below the belt is cruel and unfair.
例：Do you think it's a bit below the belt what they're doing?你觉得他们的做法是不是有点儿不太光明正大？
例：...this kind of below-the-belt discrimination.这种不公正的歧视

PHRASE
勒紧裤腰带；紧缩开支；节衣缩食
If you have to tighten your belt, you have to spend less money and manage without things because you have less money than you used to have.
例：Clearly, if you are spending more than your income, you'll need to tighten your belt.明摆着，要是你入不敷出，你就得省着点了。

PHRASE
已经取得（或完成）
If you have something under your belt, you have already achieved it or done it.
例：Clare is now a full-time author with six books, including four novels, under her belt.克莱尔现在是全职作家，著有6本书，其中有4部小说。

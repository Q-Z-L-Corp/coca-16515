bouquet 
英[buˈkeɪ] 美[boˈke, bu-]
 
n.花束；酒香；
 "

N-COUNT
花束
A bouquet is a bunch of flowers which is attractively arranged.
例：The woman carried a bouquet of dried violets.这个女人拿着一束干枯的紫罗兰。

N-VAR
（尤指葡萄酒散发的）香味，芬芳
The bouquet of something, especially wine, is the pleasant smell that it has.
例：...a Sicilian wine with a light red colour and a bouquet of cloves.颜色淡红、散发着丁香香味的西西里葡萄酒

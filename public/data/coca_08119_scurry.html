scurry 
英[ˈskʌri] 美[ˈskɜri]
 
vi.急匆匆地走；使急赶；催促；n.快步急跑，疾走；仓皇奔跑声；骤雨，骤雪；短距离赛马；
 "

VERB
(尤指受惊吓时)急奔，乱窜
When people or small animals scurry somewhere, they move there quickly and hurriedly, especially because they are frightened.
例：The attack began, sending residents scurrying for cover...袭击发动后，居民们纷纷奔逃躲避。
例：The rats scurry around, searching for scraps of food in the rubbish.老鼠乱窜，在垃圾中寻找残羹剩饭。

VERB
急匆匆;赶忙
If people scurry to do something, they do it as soon as they can.
例：Pictures of starving children have sent many people scurrying to donate money.看到一张张忍饥挨饿的孩子们的照片，许多人赶忙捐钱。

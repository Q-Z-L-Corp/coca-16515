hamper 
英[ˈhæmpə(r)] 美[ˈhæmpɚ]
 
vt.妨碍，束缚，限制；使困累；n.（有盖的）大篮子；阻碍物，足械；[航]船具；
 "

VERB
妨碍;阻碍;牵制;束缚
If someone or something hampers you, they make it difficult for you to do what you are trying to do.
例：The bad weather hampered rescue operations...恶劣的天气阻碍了救援工作的进行。
例：I was hampered by a lack of information.我受到了信息不足的牵累。

N-COUNT
（装有各种食品的）礼物篮
A hamper is a basket containing food of various kinds that is given to people as a present.
例：...a luxury food hamper.豪华食品礼物篮

N-COUNT
（尤指装食品用的）带盖大篮
A hamper is a large basket with a lid, used especially for carrying food in.
例：...a picnic hamper.野餐篮子

ADJ-GRADED
容易管理的;容易对付的
Something that is manageable is of a size, quantity, or level of difficulty that people are able to deal with.
例：He will now try to cut down the task to a manageable size...他现在要努力把任务的规模降至可控的范围。
例：The present flow of refugees was manageable...目前难民的流动尚可控制。

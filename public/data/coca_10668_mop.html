mop 
英[mɒp] 美[mɑp]
 
vt.用拖把拖；擦拭；vi.用拖把拖；擦拭；搭拉着脸；n.拖把；蓬松的头发；鬼脸；
 "

N-COUNT
拖把
A mop is a piece of equipment for washing floors. It consists of a sponge or many pieces of string attached to a long handle.

VERB
用拖把擦(地板)
If you mop a surface such as a floor, you clean it with a mop.
例：There was a woman mopping the stairs.一个女人在拖楼梯。

VERB
(用布)擦去，揩干(汗水)
If you mop sweat from your forehead or mop your forehead, you wipe it with a piece of cloth.
例：He mopped perspiration from his forehead...他擦去了前额的汗水。
例：The Inspector took out a handkerchief and mopped his brow.检察员掏出手帕，擦了擦眉头。

N-COUNT
乱蓬蓬的头发;蓬乱不洁的头发
If someone has a mop of hair, they have a lot of hair and it looks rather untidy.
例：He was long-limbed and dark-eyed, with a mop of tight, dark curls.他四肢修长、眼睛乌黑发亮，留着一头蓬乱浓密的黑色卷发。

vt.吐露（秘密、心事等）；委托，托付；vi.吐露秘密；信任，信赖；
 "

VERB
吐露（或倾诉）秘密
If you confide in someone, you tell them a secret.
例：I knew she had some fundamental problems in her marriage because she had confided in me a year earlier...我知道她的婚姻存在一些根本性的问题，早在一年前她就向我倾诉过。
例：He confided to me that he felt like he was being punished...他向我诉说他觉得自己像是在受罚。

N-UNCOUNT
（派士兵或飞机）侦察；（用卫星）搜索，勘测
Reconnaissance is the activity of obtaining military information about a place by sending soldiers or planes there, or by the use of satellites.
例：The helicopter was returning from a reconnaissance mission...直升机完成侦察任务后正在返航。
例：The aircraft will be used for reconnaissance rather than combat.这架飞机将用于执行侦察任务而不是战斗任务。

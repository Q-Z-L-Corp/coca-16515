ADJ-GRADED
大量的；丰富的
A bountiful supply or amount of something pleasant is a large one.
例：State aid is less bountiful than it was before.国家提供的援助比以往减少了。
例：...a bountiful harvest of fruits and vegetables.水果和蔬菜的大丰收

ADJ-GRADED
（地区或时期）物产富饶的，（尤指）食物充足的
A bountiful area or period of time produces or provides large amounts of something, especially food.
例：The land is bountiful and no one starves.这片土地物产丰富，没有人挨饿。

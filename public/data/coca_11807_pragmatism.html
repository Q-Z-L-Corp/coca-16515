N-UNCOUNT
实用主义；务实主义
Pragmatism means thinking of or dealing with problems in a practical way, rather than by using theory or abstract principles.
例：She had a reputation for clear thinking and pragmatism.她曾以思路清晰、观点务实而闻名。

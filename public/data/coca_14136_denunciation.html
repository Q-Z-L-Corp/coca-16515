denunciation 
英[dɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃn] 美[dɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən, -ʃi-]
 
n.指责，弹劾，谴责；控诉，告发，揭发；
 "

N-VAR
谴责;痛斥
Denunciation of someone or something is severe public criticism of them.
例：On September 24, he wrote a stinging denunciation of his critics...9月24号，他写了一篇痛斥那些批评者的措词强烈的文章。
例：He has been scathing in his denunciation of corrupt and incompetent politicians.他抨击起那些腐败无能的政客来一直都很尖刻犀利。

N-VAR
告发;检举
Denunciation is the act of reporting someone who has broken a rule or law to the authorities.
例：...the denunciation of Jews to the Nazis during the Second World War.第二次世界大战期间向纳粹分子告发犹太人

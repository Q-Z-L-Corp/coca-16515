VERB
释放；放纵…而出
If you say that someone or something unleashes a powerful force, feeling, activity, or group, you mean that they suddenly start it or send it somewhere.
例：Then he unleashed his own, unstoppable, attack...接着他开始了自己不可阻挡的攻击。
例：The officers were still reluctant to unleash their troops in pursuit of a defeated enemy.军官们仍不愿发兵乘胜追击溃败的敌人。

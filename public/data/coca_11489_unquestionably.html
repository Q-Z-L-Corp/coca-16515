ADJ

If you describe something as unquestionable, you are emphasizing that it is so obviously true or real that nobody can doubt it.
例：He inspires affection and respect as a man of unquestionable integrity...他无可争议的忠诚正直赢得了人们的爱戴和尊敬。
例：There is an unquestionable link between job losses and deteriorating services.人员的裁减和服务质量的下降之间显然是有联系的。

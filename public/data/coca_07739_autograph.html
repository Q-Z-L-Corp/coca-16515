autograph

n.亲笔签名；亲笔稿；手稿；真迹石版复制品；vt.在…上亲笔签名；亲笔书写；亲笔签字；用石版术复制；adj.亲笔的；自署的；

N-COUNT
（名人的）亲笔签名
An autograph is the signature of someone famous which is specially written for a fan to keep.
例：He went backstage and asked for her autograph...他去后台跟她要了签名。
例：Young autograph hunters clustered around the players' entrance.年轻人都挤在演员入口处索要签名。

VERB
（名人）签名于
If someone famous autographs something, they put their signature on it.
例：I autographed a copy of one of my books.我在自己的一本书上签了名。
例：...an autographed photo of Clark Gable.克拉克·盖博的签名照片

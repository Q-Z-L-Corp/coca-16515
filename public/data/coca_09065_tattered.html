ADJ-GRADED
(衣服、书等)破烂的，破旧的
If something such as clothing or a book is tattered, it is damaged or torn, especially because it has been used a lot over a long period of time.
例：He fled wearing only a sarong and a tattered shirt.他逃走时只穿着莎笼和破旧的衬衫。
例：...a very old, tattered, phone directory.破旧的电话号码簿

ADJ-GRADED
损坏严重的;一败涂地的
If you describe something as tattered, you mean that it has been badly damaged or has failed completely.
例：But, two-and-a-half years later, things haven't quite gone to plan and Stanley's dreams of fame and fortune lie tattered and torn.但是，两年半之后，事情并未如愿发展，斯坦利追求名利的梦想完全破灭了。
例：...the last tattered remnants of a defeated army.最后的残兵败将

causation 
英[kɔ:ˈzeɪʃn] 美[kɔˈzeʃən]
 
n.原因，（某种结果的）出现，发生因果关系；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
诱因;起因;祸端
The causation of something, usually something bad, is the factors that have caused it.
例：The gene is only part of the causation of illness.基因只是致病的部分原因。

N-UNCOUNT
因果关系；因果律
Causation is a study of the factors involved in causing something.
例：...assumptions concerning social causation.有关社会因果律的假设

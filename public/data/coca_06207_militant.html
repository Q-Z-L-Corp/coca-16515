militant 
英[ˈmɪlɪtənt] 美[ˈmɪlɪtənt]
 
adj.斗志昂扬的，富于战斗性的；激进的，好战的；战斗中的，交战中的；好斗者；n.激进分子，好斗分子；富有战斗精神的人，斗士；

ADJ-GRADED
激进的;好战的
You use militant to describe people who believe in something very strongly and are active in trying to bring about political or social change, often in extreme ways that other people find unacceptable.
例：Militant mineworkers in the Ukraine have voted for a one-day stoppage next month.乌克兰激进的矿工们已经投票决定下个月罢工一天。
例：...one of the most active militant groups.最为活跃的激进团体之一

mindful 
英[ˈmaɪndfl] 美[ˈmaɪndfəl]
 
adj.留心的，注意的；记住的，不忘的；警觉的；经意；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
留心的;注意的
If you are mindful of something, you think about it and consider it when taking action.
例：We must be mindful of the consequences of selfishness...我们必须考虑自私自利的后果。
例：Mindful of the needs of its students, Cardiff has invested heavily in providing new and improved residences.考虑到学生的需要，加的夫大学投入了巨资兴建和改善学生宿舍。

pander 
英[ˈpændə(r)] 美[ˈpændɚ]
 
vi.勾引，（为…）拉皮条；怂恿，帮助（…）做（坏事）；煽动；迎合；n.拉皮条者；勾引者；帮人做坏事的人；
 "

VERB
（常指为谋私利而）迎合
If you pander to someone or to their wishes, you do everything that they want, often to get some advantage for yourself.
例：He has offended the party's traditional base by pandering to the rich and the middle classes.他逢迎富人和中产阶级，得罪了该党的传统支持者。
例：...books which don't pander to popular taste.不迎合大众口味的书

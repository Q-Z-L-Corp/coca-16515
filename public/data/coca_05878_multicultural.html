ADJ-GRADED
融合(或包含)多种文化的;多元文化的
Multicultural means consisting of or relating to people of many different nationalities and cultures.
例：...children growing up in a multicultural society...在多元文化社会中长大的孩子们
例：The school has been attempting to bring a multicultural perspective to its curriculum.这所学校已经在尝试将一种多元文化视角引入其课程。

anatomy 
英[əˈnætəmi] 美[əˈnætəmi]
 
n.解剖，分解，分析；（详细的）剖析；（生物体的）解剖结构；骨骼；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
解剖学
Anatomy is the study of the structure of the bodies of people or animals.

N-COUNT
（人的）身体
You can refer to your body as your anatomy.
例：The ball hit him in the most sensitive part of his anatomy.球击中了他身上最敏感的部位。

N-COUNT
（动物体的）结构
An animal's anatomy is the structure of its body.
例：He had worked extensively on the anatomy of living animals.他在活体解剖方面经验丰富。

N-SING
剖析；分析
The anatomy of a subject or an idea is an examination or investigation of it.
例：This was a troubling essay on the anatomy of nationhood.这是一篇剖析民族主义的让人不安的文章。

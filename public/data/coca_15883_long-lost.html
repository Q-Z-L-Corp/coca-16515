ADJ
失去已久的；久违的；久别的
You use long-lost to describe someone or something that you have not seen for a long time.
例：For me it was like meeting a long-lost sister. We talked, and talked, and talked...对我来说就像久别的姐妹重逢一样。我们聊呀聊呀，话总是说不完。
例：...finding a long-lost sixth century manuscript.发现一份失传很久的6世纪手稿

amenable 
英[əˈmi:nəbl] 美[əˈminəbəl, əˈmɛnə-]
 
adj.（对法律等）负责的；易控制的；经得起检验（或考查）的；可用某种方式处理的；

ADJ-GRADED
顺从的；服从的
If you are amenable to something, you are willing to do it or accept it.
例：The Jordanian leader seemed amenable to attending a conference...约旦领导人似乎愿意参加会议。
例：I never had a long-term relationship as I wasn't good enough, or interesting or amenable enough.因为我不够优秀，不够有趣或者不够亲切，所以恋爱关系从来都维持不长。

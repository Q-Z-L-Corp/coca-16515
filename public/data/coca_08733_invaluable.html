ADJ
非常宝贵的;极为有用的
If you describe something as invaluable, you mean that it is extremely useful.
例：I was able to gain invaluable experience over that year...在那一年里我有幸获得了非常宝贵的经验。
例：The research should prove invaluable in the study of linguistics...该调查研究在语言学研究领域的重要性应该会得到证实。

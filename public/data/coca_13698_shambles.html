N-SING
(地方、事件或局势)混乱无序，凌乱不堪，一片狼藉
If a place, event, or situation is a shambles or is in a shambles, everything is in disorder.
例：The ship's interior was an utter shambles...船里面一片狼藉。
例：The economy is in a shambles.经济混乱无序。

VERB
替代;取代
If a person or thing is supplanted, another person or thing takes their place.
例：He may be supplanted by a younger man...他可能会被一个年龄比他小的人取代。
例：By the 1930s the wristwatch had almost completely supplanted the pocket watch.到了 20 世纪 30 年代，手表几乎完全取代了怀表。

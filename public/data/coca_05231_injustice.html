injustice 
英[ɪnˈdʒʌstɪs] 美[ɪnˈdʒʌstɪs]
 
n.不公平；非正义；待…不公正；冤枉；
 "

N-VAR
非正义;不公平;不公正
Injustice is a lack of fairness in a situation.
例：They'll continue to fight injustice...他们将继续与不公平现象作斗争。
例：They resented the injustices of the system.他们怨恨制度的种种不公。

PHRASE
不公正地评判;不公正地对待
If you say that someone has done you an injustice, you mean that they have been unfair in the way that they have judged you or treated you.
例：The article does them both an injustice.那篇文章对他们俩都不公。

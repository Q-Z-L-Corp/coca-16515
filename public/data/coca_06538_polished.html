polished 
英[ˈpɒlɪʃt] 美[ˈpɑlɪʃt]
 
adj.磨光的，擦亮的；完美的，精良的；优雅的；v.使光滑，擦亮(polish的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
优雅的；有教养的
Someone who is polished shows confidence and knows how to behave socially.
例：He is polished, charming, articulate and an excellent negotiator.他文雅、迷人、能言善辩，是一名优秀的谈判人。

ADJ-GRADED
完美无缺的；炉火纯青的
If you describe a performance, ability, or skill as polished, you mean that it is of a very high standard.
例：It was simply a very polished performance.这简直就是一场完美的演出。
例：...polished promotional skills.高明的促销技巧

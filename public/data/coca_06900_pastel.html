pastel 
英[ˈpæstl] 美[pæˈstel]
 
n.彩色粉笔，粉蜡笔；彩色粉笔画，蜡笔画；轻淡柔和的色彩；adj.彩色粉笔画的；蜡笔画的；（色彩）淡的，柔和的；
 "

ADJ
（色彩）淡的，柔和的
Pastel colours are pale rather than dark or bright.
例：...delicate pastel shades.淡雅的色调
例：...pastel pink, blue, peach and green.淡粉色、蓝色、桃红色和绿色

N-COUNT
彩色粉笔
Pastels are also small sticks of different coloured chalks that are used for drawing pictures.
例：...pastels and charcoal.彩色粉笔和木炭笔
例：...the portrait in pastels.用彩色粉笔画的肖像

N-COUNT
彩色粉笔画；彩粉画
A pastel is a picture that has been done using pastels.
例：...Degas's paintings, pastels, and prints.德加的油画、彩粉画和版画
例：...a pastel by Toulouse-Lautrec.土鲁斯－劳特累克的彩粉画

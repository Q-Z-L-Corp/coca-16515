ADJ
死后的；身后的
Posthumous is used to describe something that happens after a person's death but relates to something they did before they died.
例：His family and supporters have campaigned for many years for a posthumous pardon.他的家人和支持者为争取其获得身后赦免而开展了多年的运动。
例：...the posthumous publication of his first novel.他的第一部小说在他死后的出版

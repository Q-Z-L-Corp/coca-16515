newsprint 
英[ˈnju:zprɪnt] 美[ˈnuzprɪnt]
 
n.新闻纸；白报纸；（印刷报纸的）油墨；报刊文章；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
新闻纸;白报纸
Newsprint is the cheap, fairly rough paper on which newspapers are printed.

N-UNCOUNT
报纸上的文字
Newsprint is the text that is printed in newspapers.
例：...the acres of newsprint devoted to celebrities' personal lives.报纸对名流私人生活的大篇幅报道

N-UNCOUNT
印刷油墨
Newsprint is the ink which is used to print newspapers and magazines.
例：They get their hands covered in newsprint.他们手上满是油墨。

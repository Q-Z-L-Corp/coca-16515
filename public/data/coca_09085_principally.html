ADV
主要地；首要地；最重要地
Principally means more than anything else.
例：This is principally because the major export markets are slowing...这主要是因为主要出口市场发展缓慢。
例：Embryonic development seems to be controlled principally by a very small number of master genes.胚胎发育似乎主要受制于少数主导基因。

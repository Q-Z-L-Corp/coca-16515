dismal 
英[ˈdɪzməl] 美[ˈdɪzməl]
 
adj.阴沉的，惨淡的，凄凉的；忧郁的；n.低落的情绪；<美>沼泽；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
忧郁的;凄凉的;令人沮丧的
Something that is dismal is bad in a sad or depressing way.
例：...Israel's dismal record in the Olympics...以色列在奥运会上让人沮丧的记录
例：My prospects of returning to a suitable job are dismal...我重新找到一个合适的工作岗位的希望很渺茫。

ADJ-GRADED
(尤指外表)阴暗的，阴沉的，沉闷的
Something that is dismal is sad and depressing, especially in appearance.
例：The main part of the hospital is pretty dismal but the children's ward is really lively.医院的主体部分死气沉沉，但是儿科病房却充满生气。
例：...a dark dismal day with rain falling steadily...天空昏暗、阴雨绵绵的一天

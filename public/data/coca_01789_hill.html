hill 
英[hɪl] 美[hɪl]
 
n.小山，山冈；斜坡；v.把…堆成小山；培土于…周围；
 "

N-COUNT; N-IN-NAMES
小山;山冈;山丘
A hill is an area of land that is higher than the land that surrounds it.
例：We trudged up the hill to the stadium.我们费力地爬到山上的体育场。
例：...Maple Hill.梅普尔山

PHRASE
人老珠黄;在走下坡路;在衰退中;过气
If you say that someone is over the hill, you are saying rudely that they are old and no longer fit, attractive, or capable of doing useful work.
例：He doesn't take kindly to suggestions that he is over the hill.他不爱听别人说他风光不再。

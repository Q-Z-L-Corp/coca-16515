ADJ
想象中的;假想的;虚构的
An imaginary person, place, or thing exists only in your mind or in a story, and not in real life.
例：Lots of children have imaginary friends.许多孩子都会凭空想象一些朋友。
例：...creating an imaginary world.创造一个虚构的世界

debilitating 
英[dɪ'bɪlɪteɪtɪŋ] 美[dɪ'bɪləˌteɪtɪŋ]
 
v.使（人或人的身体）非常虚弱( debilitate的现在分词 )；
 "

VERB
使虚弱;使衰弱
If you are debilitated by something such as an illness, it causes your body or mind to become gradually weaker.
例：Stewart took over yesterday when Russell was debilitated by a stomach virus.昨天拉塞尔因肠胃病毒感染身体不适，由斯图尔特暂时接手。

VERB
削弱（组织、社团或政府）的力量
To debilitate an organization, society, or government means to gradually make it weaker.
例：...their efforts to debilitate the political will of the Western alliance.他们试图削弱西方联盟的政治决心的努力

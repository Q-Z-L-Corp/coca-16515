wiggle 
英[ˈwɪgl] 美[ˈwɪɡəl]
 
vt.& vi.摆动；扭动；n.扭动；
 "

V-ERG
（使）扭动;（使）摆动
If you wiggle something or if it wiggles, it moves up and down or from side to side in small quick movements.
例：She wiggled her finger...她扭了扭手指。
例：His ears wiggled if you scratched his chin...如果你挠他的下巴，他的耳朵就会摆动。

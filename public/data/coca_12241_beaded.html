ADJ
饰以小珠的；带珠子的
A beaded dress, cushion, or other object is decorated with beads.

ADJ
布满（某种液体的）液滴的
If something is beaded with a liquid, it is covered in small drops of that liquid.
例：The man's bald head was beaded with sweat...这个男人的秃头上满是汗珠。
例：The cave walls were beaded with moisture.这个洞穴壁上布满了水珠。

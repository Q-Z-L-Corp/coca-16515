silk 
英[sɪlk] 美[sɪlk]
 
n.丝，蚕丝；丝绸，丝织物；（用于缝纫的）丝线；王室法律顾问；vi.（玉米）处于长须的阶段中；adj.丝的；丝绸的；丝制的；
 "

N-MASS
蚕丝;丝绸;丝线
Silk is a substance which is made into smooth fine cloth and sewing thread. You can also refer to this cloth or thread as silk .
例：They continued to get their silks from China...他们继续从中国购买丝绸。
例：Pauline wore a silk dress with a strand of pearls.保利娜穿了件丝绸长裙，戴了串珍珠。

N-UNCOUNT
(蜘蛛等所分泌的)丝
You can refer to the substance produced by some creatures such as spiders as silk .
例：...the silk threads of a spider's web.蜘蛛网的丝线

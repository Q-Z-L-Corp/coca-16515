misrepresentation 
英[ˌmɪsˌreprɪzen'teɪʃn] 美[ˌmɪsˌreprɪzen'teɪʃn]
 
n.误传；
 "

VERB
误述;对…作不准确的叙述;歪曲
If someone misrepresents a person or situation, they give a wrong or inaccurate account of what the person or situation is like.
例：He said that the press had misrepresented him as arrogant and bullying...他说新闻媒体把他歪曲成了一个傲慢而又霸道的人。
例：Hollywood films misrepresented us as drunks, maniacs and murderers...好莱坞电影把我们歪曲成酒鬼、疯子和杀人凶手。

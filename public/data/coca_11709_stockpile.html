stockpile 
英[ˈstɒkpaɪl] 美[ˈstɑkpaɪl]
 
n.（原料，食品等的）储备，准备急用的备用原料或物资，贮存；资源，富源，矿藏量；（为战争准备的）核武器；vt.大量贮备；
 "

VERB
大量贮备，囤积(食品或武器)
If people stockpile things such as food or weapons, they store large quantities of them for future use.
例：People are stockpiling food for the coming winter.人们正在为即将到来的冬天贮备大量食物。

N-COUNT
大量的储备;囤积
A stockpile of things is a large quantity of them that have been stored for future use.
例：The two leaders also approved treaties to cut stockpiles of chemical weapons.两位领导人还同意签署削减化学武器储备的几项条约。

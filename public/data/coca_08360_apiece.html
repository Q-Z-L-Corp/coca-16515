apiece 
英[əˈpi:s] 美[əˈpis]
 
adv.每个；每件；每人；各自地；
 "

ADV
每人
If people have a particular number of things apiece, they have that number each.
例：He and I had two fish apiece...他和我各有两条鱼。
例：The World Series between the Atlanta Braves and Toronto Blue Jays is tied at one game apiece.世界系列赛上，亚特兰大勇士队和多伦多蓝鸟队双方各赢一场，战成平局。

ADV
每件；每个
If a number of similar things are for sale at a certain price apiece, that is the price for each one of them.
例：Entire roast chickens were sixty cents apiece.烤全鸡每只60美分。

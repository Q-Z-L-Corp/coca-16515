noted 
英[ˈnəʊtɪd] 美[ˈnoʊtɪd]
 
adj.闻名的，著名的；[乐]附有乐谱的；v.注意，记下；“note”的过去式和过去分词；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
著名的;知名的
To be noted for something you do or have means to be well-known and admired for it.
例：...a television programme noted for its attacks on organised crime...以抨击有组织犯罪而知名的电视节目
例：Lawyers are not noted for rushing into change.律师并非以仓促求变著称。

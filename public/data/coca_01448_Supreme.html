Supreme 
英[su:ˈpri:m] 美[sʊˈprim]
 
adj.最高的；至高的，无上的；最重要的；（程度）很大的；
 "

ADJ
最高的;至高无上的
Supreme is used in the title of a person or an official group to indicate that they are at the highest level in a particular organization or system.
例：MacArthur was Supreme Commander for the allied powers in the Pacific.麦克阿瑟将军曾是太平洋战区盟军的最高统帅。
例：...the Supreme Court.最高法院

ADJ
最大的;最多的
You use supreme to emphasize that a quality or thing is very great.
例：Her approval was of supreme importance...她的认可是至关重要的。
例：The lady conspired to seize supreme power.该女士图谋攫取最高权力。

VERB
闲逛;漫步
If you saunter somewhere, you walk there in a slow, casual way.
例：We watched our fellow students saunter into the building...我们看着同学们悠闲地走进了大楼。
例：He sauntered along the river to the mill.他沿着河边漫步走到磨坊。

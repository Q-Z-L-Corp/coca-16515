progression 
英[prəˈgreʃn] 美[prəˈɡrɛʃən]

N-COUNT
进展；发展；演变
A progression is a gradual development from one state to another.
例：Both drugs slow the progression of HIV, but neither cures the disease...这两种药物都可以延缓艾滋病病情发展，但是都不能治愈这种疾病。
例：I think they saw it as a natural progression for me.我想他们认为这对我来说是顺理成章的。

N-COUNT
（事件的）一连串，一系列，连续
A progression of things is a number of things which come one after the other.
例：...a progression of habitats from dry meadows through marshes to open water.从干燥的草地到沼泽地再到开阔的水面的一系列栖息地

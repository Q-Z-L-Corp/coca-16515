clumsily 
英['klʌmzɪlɪ] 美[ˈklʌmzɪlɪ]
 
adv.笨拙地；粗陋地，形状难看地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
笨拙的;不灵活的
A clumsy person moves or handles things in a careless, awkward way, often so that things are knocked over or broken.
例：I'd never seen a clumsier, less coordinated boxer...我从没见过更笨拙、动作更不协调的拳击手。
例：Unfortunately, I was still very clumsy behind the wheel of the jeep.令人遗憾的是我开起吉普来还是笨手笨脚。

ADJ-GRADED
(行为或措词)不得体的，拙劣的
A clumsy action or statement is not skilful or is likely to upset people.
例：The action seemed a clumsy attempt to topple the Janata Dal government...这一试图推翻人民党政府的行动似乎缺乏策略。
例：He denied the announcement was clumsy and insensitive.他否认声明不得体、不近人情。

ADJ-GRADED
(设计或外表)粗糙的，难用的;设计欠佳的
An object that is clumsy is not neat in design or appearance, and is often awkward to use.
例：The keyboard is a large and clumsy instrument as far as portable computers are concerned...就手提电脑而言，键盘就显得大而笨重了。
例：It was a clumsy looking aeroplane.那架飞机样子很难看。

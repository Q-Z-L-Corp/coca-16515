inducement 
英[ɪnˈdju:smənt] 美[ɪnˈdusmənt]
 
n.诱导，劝诱；诱因，动机；[法]引言部分；刺激物；
 "

N-COUNT
诱惑;引诱物
If someone is offered an inducement to do something, they are given or promised gifts or benefits in order to persuade them to do it.
例：They offer every inducement to foreign businesses to invest in their states...他们为外国企业提供种种优厚条件，以吸引这些企业到他们所在的州投资。
例：Various inducements are offered to encourage employees to wear safety clothing.他们提供各种奖励以鼓励员工穿安全服。

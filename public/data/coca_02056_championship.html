N-COUNT
锦标赛;冠军赛
A championship is a competition to find the best player or team in a particular sport.
例：...the world chess championship.国际象棋世界冠军赛

N-SING
冠军头衔;冠军称号;第一名
The championship refers to the title or status of being a sports champion.
例：He went on to take the championship...他继而夺取冠军宝座。
例：This season I expect us to retain the championship and win the European Cup.这个赛季我期待我们能够保住冠军头衔，赢得欧洲杯。

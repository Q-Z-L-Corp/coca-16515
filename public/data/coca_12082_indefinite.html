ADJ
期限不定的;无限期的
If you describe a situation or period as indefinite, you mean that people have not decided when it will end.
例：The trial was adjourned for an indefinite period.审判被无限期推迟。
例：...an indefinite strike by government workers.公务人员的无限期罢工

ADJ
不明确的;不清楚的
Something that is indefinite is not exact or clear.
例：...at some indefinite time in the future.在未来的某个时间
例：...a handsome woman of indefinite age.年龄不明的端庄女子

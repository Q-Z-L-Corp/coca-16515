ADV
完全地;完整地
You use wholly to emphasize the extent or degree to which something is the case.
例：While the two are only days apart in age they seem to belong to wholly different generations...尽管这两人年龄上只相差几天，他们却好像是属于完全不同时代的人。
例：For urban areas this approach was wholly inadequate...对于市区来说，这种办法根本不会奏效。

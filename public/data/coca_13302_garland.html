garland 
英[ˈgɑ:lənd] 美[ˈgɑrlənd]
 
n.花环，花冠，花圈；[建]华饰；〈古〉诗歌选集；胜利和荣誉的象征；v.戴花环；把…做成花环；给…饰以花环；
 "

N-COUNT
花环；花冠；环状花饰
A garland is a circular decoration made from flowers and leaves. People sometimes wear garlands of flowers on their heads or around their necks.
例：They wore garlands of summer flowers in their hair.他们头戴用夏季鲜花编成的花环。

VERB
用花环装饰；给…戴上花冠（或饰以花环）
If people, places, or objects are garlanded, people hang garlands or similar decorations around them.
例：Players and officials were garlanded with flowers...运动员和官员们戴着花环。
例：The wagons were decorated with flowers and pulled by garlanded horses.马车都用鲜花装点，由佩戴花环的几匹马拉着。

ADJ
心理的；精神的
Psychological means concerned with a person's mind and thoughts.
例：John received constant physical and psychological abuse from his father...约翰不断遭到父亲身体和心理上的双重虐待。
例：Robyn's loss of memory is a psychological problem, rather than a physical one.罗宾的失忆是心理问题，而非生理问题。

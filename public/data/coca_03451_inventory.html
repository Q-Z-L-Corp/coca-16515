N-COUNT
详细目录;清单
An inventory is a written list of all the objects in a particular place.
例：Before starting, he made an inventory of everything that was to stay.开始之前,他把所有要留下的东西列了详细的清单。

储备;存货

例：...one inventory of twelve sails for each yacht.每艘快艇 12 张帆的储备

ADJ-GRADED
不睡觉的;不眠的
A sleepless night is one during which you do not sleep.
例：I have sleepless nights worrying about her.我好几个晚上都睡不着，一直担心她。

ADJ
睡不着的;失眠的
Someone who is sleepless is unable to sleep.
例：A sleepless baby can seem to bring little reward.宝宝睡不着觉似乎没有任何好处。

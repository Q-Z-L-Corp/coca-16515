ADJ
未说明的；不明确的
You say that something is unspecified when you are not told exactly what it is.
例：The government said an unspecified number of bandits were killed...政府称已歼灭若干路匪。
例：He was arrested on unspecified charges.他遭到逮捕，但未透露具体罪名。

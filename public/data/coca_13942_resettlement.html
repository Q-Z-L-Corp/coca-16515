N-UNCOUNT
重新定居;移居;重新安置
Resettlement is the process of moving people to a different place to live, because they are no longer allowed to stay in the area where they used to live.
例：Only refugees are eligible for resettlement abroad.只有难民才符合移民国外的条件。
例：...the forcible resettlement of villagers from the countryside into towns.强制村民从农村搬迁到城市

VERB
绞接(绳子);粘接(胶片、磁带等)
If you splice two pieces of rope, film, or tape together, you join them neatly at the ends so that they make one continuous piece.
例：He taught me to edit and splice film...他教我剪辑和粘接胶片。
例：The film will be spliced with footage of Cypress Hill to be filmed in America.这部电影要和将在美国拍摄的柏树山乐队的音乐片段粘接在一起。

V-PASSIVE
结婚
When two people get spliced, they get married.
例：An old friend of mine, newly spliced, recently invited me to dinner in his new marital home.我的一位刚结婚不久的老朋友最近邀请我到他的婚房去吃晚饭。

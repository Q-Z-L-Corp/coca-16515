drapery 
英[ˈdreɪpəri] 美['dreɪpərɪ]
 
n.布料及服装业或所经售的货物；打褶悬挂的布等；adj.悬有（褶形）布帘的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(打褶悬挂的)织物;窗帘;衣物
You can refer to cloth, curtains, or clothing hanging in folds as drapery or draperies .
例：In the dining-room the draperies create an atmosphere of elegance.在餐厅里，打褶装饰物能营造雅致的氛围。

N-UNCOUNT
布料，布匹
Drapery is cloth that you buy in a shop.
例：My mother ran a couple of drapery shops.我母亲经营着几家布料店。

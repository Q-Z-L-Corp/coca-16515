ADJ
无端的；无正当理由的
If you describe something as unwarranted, you are critical of it because there is no need or reason for it.
例：Any attempt to discuss the issue of human rights was rejected as an unwarranted interference in the country's internal affairs...任何讨论人权问题的尝试都会被以无端干预该国内政为由加以拒绝。
例：He accused the police of using unwarranted brutality.他指控警方无端施暴。

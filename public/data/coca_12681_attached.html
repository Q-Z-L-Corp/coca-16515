attached 
英[əˈtætʃt] 美[əˈtætʃt]
 
adj.附加的，附属的；依恋的；爱慕的；v.贴上，系，附上( attach的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
非常喜欢…的，热爱…的
If you are attached to someone or something, you like them very much.
例：She is very attached to her family and friends.她非常热爱家人和朋友。

ADJ
（通常只是短时间）从属于…的，为…工作的
If someone is attached to an organization or group of people, they are working with them, often only for a short time.
例：Ford was attached to the battalion's first line of transport.福特曾在该营的第一运输队服役。

ADJ
隶属于…的;附属的
If one organization or institution is attached to a larger organization, it is part of that organization and is controlled and run by it.
例：At one time the schools were mainly attached to the church.这些学校曾一度归教会主管。
例：...a think-tank, attached to the Academy of Sciences.为科学院出谋划策的智囊团

ADJ-GRADED
具有异国情调的;外来的;奇异的
Something that is exotic is unusual and interesting, usually because it comes from or is related to a distant country.
例：...brilliantly coloured, exotic flowers...色彩斑斓的奇异花卉
例：She flits from one exotic location to another.她辗转于异国他乡。

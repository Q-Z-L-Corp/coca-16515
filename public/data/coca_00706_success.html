success 
英[səkˈses] 美[səkˈsɛs]
 
n.成功，成就；好成绩，好结果；成功的人（或物）；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
成功;胜利;成就
Success is the achievement of something that you have been trying to do.
例：It's important for the long-term success of any diet that you vary your meals.如果想要成功做到长期节食，很重要的一点是要膳食多样化。
例：...the success of European business in building a stronger partnership between management and workers.欧洲企业在劳资双方之间建立更牢固的伙伴关系上所取得的成功

N-UNCOUNT
(在某一领域的)成功，成名
Success is the achievement of a high position in a particular field, for example in business or politics.
例：Nearly all of the young people interviewed believed that work was the key to success.几乎所有被采访的年轻人都认为工作是成功的关键。

N-UNCOUNT
(某事所取得的)成功，良好表现
The success of something is the fact that it works in a satisfactory way or has the result that is intended.
例：Most of the cast was amazed by the play's success...大多数演员都为该剧大获成功而感到惊奇。
例：Enthused by the success of the first exhibition, its organisers are hoping to repeat the experience.由于首展告捷，组织者大受鼓舞，希望能再创佳绩。

N-COUNT
成功的人(或事物)
Someone or something that is a success achieves a high position, makes a lot of money, or is admired a great deal.
例：The jewellery was a great success...这些珠宝大获赞誉。
例：We hope it will be a commercial success.我们希望它能取得商业上的成功。

paste 
英[peɪst] 美[pest]
 
n.面团；糨糊；肉（或鱼等）酱（作涂抹料或烹饪用）；（制作人造宝石的）铅质玻璃；vt.粘贴，张贴；以…覆盖于；
 "

N-MASS
糊糊；糨糊
Paste is a soft, wet, sticky mixture of a substance and a liquid, which can be spread easily. Some types of paste are used to stick things together.
例：He then sticks it back together with flour paste...然后他用面糊把它重新粘了起来。
例：Blend a little milk with the custard powder to form a paste.往蛋奶沙司粉里加入一点儿牛奶，搅成糊糊。

N-MASS
（肉、水果或蔬菜弄碎做成的）酱
Paste is a soft smooth mixture made of crushed meat, fruit, or vegetables. You can, for example, spread it onto bread or use it in cooking.
例：...tomato paste.番茄酱
例：...fish paste sandwiches.鱼酱三明治

VERB
粘贴；黏合
If you paste something on a surface, you put glue or paste on it and stick it on the surface.
例：...pasting labels on bottles...把商标贴在瓶子上
例：Activists pasted up posters criticizing the leftist leaders.激进分子张贴起海报批评左翼领袖。

N-UNCOUNT
（制造人造宝石的）铅玻璃
Paste is a hard shiny glass that is used for making imitation jewellery.
例：...paste emeralds.铅玻璃翡翠

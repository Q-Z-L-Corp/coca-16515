lad 
英[læd] 美[læd]
 
n.少年，小伙子；〈口〉伙伴，老朋友；〈英〉管赛马房的马夫；〈俚〉情人；
 "

N-COUNT; N-VOC
小伙子；年轻人；少年；男孩
A lad is a young man or boy.
例：When I was a lad his age I would laugh at the strangest things...我是他那样的小毛孩时，莫明其妙地就会发笑。
例：Come along, lad. Time for you to get home.来吧，小伙子。你该回家了。

N-PLURAL
伙计；兄弟；哥们儿
Some men refer to their male friends or colleagues as the lads .
例：...a drink with the lads...与哥们儿一块喝一杯
例：The lads don't join the union because they're frightened of being victimized.那几个伙计没加入工会是因为害怕成为牺牲品。

globalization 
英[ˌgləʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃn] 美[ˌgloʊbələˈzeɪʃn]
 
n.全球化，全球性；
 "

V-ERG
（使)全球化
When industry globalizes or is globalized, companies from one country link with companies from another country in order to do business with them.
例：As the world becomes more complex, some things do, of course, standardize and globalize...随着世界变得日益复杂，某些事物确实理所当然地规范化和全球化了。
例：Companies will come together because of the sheer costs involved in globalising their businesses.由于业务全球化的成本太高，公司将进行合并。

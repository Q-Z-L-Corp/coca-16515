convention 
英[kənˈvenʃn] 美[kənˈvɛnʃən]
 
n.会议；全体与会者；国际公约；惯例，习俗，规矩；
 "

N-VAR
习俗;惯例;常规
A convention is a way of behaving that is considered to be correct or polite by most people in a society.
例：It's just a social convention that men don't wear skirts...男人不穿裙子只是一种社会习俗。
例：Despite her wish to defy convention, she had become pregnant and married at 21.尽管她不想遵循传统习俗，但她还是在21岁的时候就怀孕并且结婚了。

N-COUNT
(艺术、文学、戏剧中的)传统做法，传统风格
In art, literature, or the theatre, a convention is a traditional method or style.
例：We go offstage and come back for the convention of the encore.按照加演的传统做法，我们走到后台后又再次返场。
例：...the stylistic conventions of Egyptian art.埃及艺术的传统风格

N-COUNT
(国家或组织间的)公约，协定，协约
A convention is an official agreement between countries or groups of people.
例：...the UN convention on climate change.联合国有关气候变化的公约
例：...the Geneva convention.日内瓦公约

N-COUNT
(组织、政治团体的)大会，大型会议
A convention is a large meeting of an organization or political group.
例：...the annual convention of the Society of Professional Journalists.职业新闻工作者协会年度大会
例：...the Republican convention.共和党代表大会

inhuman 
英[ɪnˈhju:mən] 美[ɪnˈhjumən]
 
adj.非人的，不近人情的；残忍的，野蛮的；无人性的；不人道；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不人道的;野蛮的;残酷的
If you describe treatment or an action as inhuman, you mean that it is extremely cruel.
例：The detainees are often held in cruel and inhuman conditions...被拘留者的关押环境常常恶劣严酷、很不人道。
例：The barbaric slaughter of whales is unnecessary and inhuman.对鲸的野蛮屠杀既不必要也不人道。

ADJ-GRADED
非人类的;怪异可怕的
If you describe someone or something as inhuman, you mean that they are strange or bad because they do not seem human in some way.
例：...inhuman shrieks that chilled my heart.让我心惊胆寒的、异常恐怖的尖叫声

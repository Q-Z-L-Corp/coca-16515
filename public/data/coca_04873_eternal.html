eternal 
英[ɪˈtɜ:nl] 美[ɪˈtɜrnl]
 
adj.永恒的，永久的；似乎不停的，没完没了的；不朽的；永生的；n.永恒的事物；Eternal 上帝，与定冠词the 连用；
 "

ADJ
永久的;永恒的;永远的
Something that is eternal lasts for ever.
例：Whoever believes in Him shall have eternal life.所有相信上帝的人都会获得永生。
例：...the quest for eternal youth.对永葆青春的追求

ADJ
无休止的;没完没了的
If you describe something as eternal, you mean that it seems to last for ever, often because you think it is boring or annoying.
例：In the background was that eternal hum.背景音是没完没了的嗡嗡声。

ADJ
（真理、价值、问题等）永恒的，不朽的
Eternal truths, values, and questions never change and are believed to be always true and to be relevant in all situations.
例：...the eternal truths of the Indian subcontinent.印度次大陆的永恒真理
例：...the eternal question.永恒的问题

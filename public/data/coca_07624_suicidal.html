suicidal 
英[ˌsu:ɪˈsaɪdl] 美[ˌsuɪˈsaɪdl]
 
adj.（想）自杀的，自杀性的；自我毁灭的，自取灭亡的；于己不利；
 "

ADJ
有自杀倾向的;想自杀的
People who are suicidal want to kill themselves.
例：I was suicidal and just couldn't stop crying...我当时想自杀，一直哭个不停。
例：Her suicidal tendencies continued for several more weeks.她的自杀倾向又持续了几周。

ADJ
自取灭亡的;有致命危险的
If you describe an action or behaviour as suicidal, you mean that it is very dangerous.
例：They realized it would be suicidal to resist in the face of overwhelming military superiority.他们意识到面对力量相差如此悬殊的军队，抵抗无异于自取灭亡。
例：...the suicidal bicycle rickshaws of New Delhi.新德里非常危险的人力车

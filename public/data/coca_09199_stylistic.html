ADJ
(写作、音乐或艺术作品)风格上的，创作技巧的
Stylistic describes things relating to the methods and techniques used in creating a piece of writing, music, or art.
例：There are some stylistic elements in the statue that just don't make sense.这座雕像上的一些风格元素让人完全捉摸不透。

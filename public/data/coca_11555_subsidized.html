subsidized 
英[] 美[]
 
v.给…津贴或补贴，资助或补助…( subsidize的过去式和过去分词 )；
 "

VERB
资助;补助;给…发津贴
If a government or other authority subsidizes something, they pay part of the cost of it.
例：Around the world, governments have subsidized the housing of middle and upper-income groups...全世界各国政府都为中高收入群体提供过住房补贴。
例：At the moment they are existing on pensions that are subsidised by the government.此刻他们正依靠政府发放的养老金维持生计。

VERB
(政府)资助，出资支持
If a government subsidizes an industry, they provide money so that the industry can continue.
例：The government continues to subsidize the production of eggs and beef.政府继续出资扶持鸡蛋和牛肉的生产。
例：...a government decision to subsidise coal mining.政府资助煤矿开采的决定

N-COUNT
(尤指共患难的)朋友
Your comrades are your friends, especially friends that you share a difficult or dangerous situation with.
例：Unlike so many of his comrades he survived the war.和许多战友不同，他在战争中幸存下来。

N-TITLE; N-VOC; N-COUNT
同志(社会主义者或共产主义者之间的称呼，尤其在会议中)
Socialists or communists sometimes call each other comrade, especially in meetings.
例：The Party's authority, comrades, will be put to a serious test.同志们，党的权威将受到严重的考验。

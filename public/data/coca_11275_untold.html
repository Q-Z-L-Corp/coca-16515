untold 
英[ˌʌnˈtəʊld] 美[ˌʌnˈtoʊld]
 
adj.未说过的；未透露的；说不尽的；未叙述的，没有说到的；
 "

ADJ
（强调糟糕或令人不快的程度）无以名状的，难以估量的
You can use untold to emphasize how bad or unpleasant something is.
例：The demise of the industry has caused untold misery to thousands of hard-working tradesmen...该行业的没落给数以千计辛苦经营的商贩带来了难言的苦楚。
例：This might do untold damage to her health.这也许会给她的健康带来难以估量的伤害。

ADJ
数不胜数的;不计其数的
You can use untold to emphasize that an amount or quantity is very large, especially when you are not sure how large it is.
例：...the nation's untold millions of anglers.该国数不清的钓鱼爱好者
例：...the glittering prospect of untold riches.赚取巨额财富的美好前景

abstraction 
英[æbˈstrækʃn] 美[æbˈstrækʃən, əb-]
 
n.抽象；出神；抽象概念；抽象化；

N-VAR
抽象概念；抽象名称
An abstraction is a general idea rather than one relating to a particular object, person, or situation.
例：Is it worth fighting a big war, in the name of an abstraction like sovereignty?在主权之类抽象概念的名义下打一场大战是否值得呢？

N-UNCOUNT
出神；心不在焉
Abstraction is the state of being very deep in thought.
例：Andrew noticed her abstraction and asked, 'What's bothering you?'安德鲁注意到她在愣神，便问道：“你怎么啦？”

N-UNCOUNT
（艺术的）抽象主义
Abstraction is the use of shapes and patterns in art.

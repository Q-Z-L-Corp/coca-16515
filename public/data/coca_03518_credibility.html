N-UNCOUNT
信用;可信性;可靠性
If someone or something has credibility, people believe in them and trust them.
例：The police have lost their credibility...警方已经失去了公众的信任。
例：The president will have to work hard to restore his credibility.总统将不得不努力使公众恢复对他的信任。

illiterate 
英[ɪˈlɪtərət] 美[ɪˈlɪtərɪt]
 
adj.目不识丁的，文盲的；n.目不识丁者；文盲；无知；
 "

ADJ
文盲的;不会读写的
Someone who is illiterate does not know how to read or write.
例：A large percentage of the population is illiterate.文盲人口占有相当高的比例。

ADJ
(音乐、科技、经济上)知之甚少的,接近无知的
If you describe someone as musically, technologically, or economically illiterate, you mean that they do not know much about music, technology, or economics.
例：Many senior managers are technologically illiterate.许多高级经理都对技术知之甚少。

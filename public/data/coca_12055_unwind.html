unwind 
英[ˌʌnˈwaɪnd] 美[ʌnˈwaɪnd]
 
vt.& vi.展开，从缠绕中解开；（使）心情轻松；vi.放松，解开，松开；
 "

VERB
放松；减压
When you unwind, you relax after you have done something that makes you tense or tired.
例：It helps them to unwind after a busy day at work...这有助于他们忙碌一天后放松自己。
例：Singing is a nice way of unwinding.唱歌是一种不错的减压方法。

V-ERG
解开；展开
If you unwind a length of something that is wrapped round something else or round itself, you loosen it and make it straight. You can also say that it unwinds .
例：One of them unwound a length of rope from around his waist...其中一人把绑在腰间的一条绳子解下来。
例：The thread unwound a little more.线又松开了一些。

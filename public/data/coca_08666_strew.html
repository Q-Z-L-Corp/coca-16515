strew 
英[stru:] 美[stru]
 
vt.撒在…上；散落于；点缀；撒满；
 "

VERB
撒;散播;在…上布满(或撒满)
To strew things somewhere, or to strew a place with things, means to scatter them there.
例：The racoons knock over the rubbish bins in search of food, and strew the contents all over the ground...浣熊弄翻垃圾箱找吃的，把垃圾弄得满地都是。
例：An elderly woman was strewing the floor with French chalk so that the dancing shoes would not slip...一位老妇人正在往地板上撒滑石粉以防止舞鞋打滑。

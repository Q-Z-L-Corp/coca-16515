interlocking

adj.联锁的；v.<计>互锁设备( interlock的现在分词 )；双螺纹针织品；

V-RECIP-ERG
(使)连锁;(使)相互扣住
Things that interlock or are interlocked go between or through each other so that they are linked.
例：The parts interlock...各部分相互扣住。
例：Interlock your fingers behind your back...把手十指交错放到背后。

V-RECIP-ERG
(使)密切联系
If systems, situations, or plans are interlocked or interlock, they are very closely connected.
例：The problems of Israel, Lebanon, and the Gulf were tightly interlocked...以色列、黎巴嫩与海湾的问题一环扣一环。
例：The tragedies begin to interlock...悲剧开始相互交叠。

hard-pressed 
英[ˌhɑ:dˈprest] 美[ˈhɑrdˈprɛst]
 
adj.处于困境的，遭受强大压力的；经济困难的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
处于困境的;经济窘迫的;承受高压的
If someone is hard-pressed, they are under a great deal of strain and worry, usually because they have not got enough money.
例：The region's hard-pressed consumers are spending less on luxuries.该地区的消费者经济窘迫，在奢侈品上的开销比以前少了。

ADJ-GRADED
很难做到的;（做…）有困难的
If you will be hard-pressed to do something, you will have great difficulty doing it.
例：This year the airline will be hard-pressed to make a profit.今年该航空公司将很难赢利。

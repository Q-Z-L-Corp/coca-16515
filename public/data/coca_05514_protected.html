Protected is used to describe animals, plants, and areas of land which the law does not allow to be destroyed, harmed, or damaged.
例：In England, thrushes are a protected species so you will not find them on any menu.在英格兰，歌鸫是受保护物种，因此你在任何菜单上都找不到。

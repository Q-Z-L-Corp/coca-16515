licensed 
英[ˈlaɪsnst] 美[ˈlaɪsnst]
 
adj.得到许可的；v.许可，颁发执照(license的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ
得到许可的；领有执照的
If you are licensed to do something, you have official permission from the government or from the authorities to do it.
例：There were about 250 people on board, about 100 more than the ferry was licensed to carry.渡船上大约有250名乘客，超出了核定载客量约100名。
例：...a licensed doctor.领有执照的医生

ADJ
获准拥有（或使用）的
If something that you own or use is licensed, you have official permission to own it or use it.
例：While searching the house they discovered an unlicensed shotgun and a licensed rifle.搜查房子时，他们找到了一把无牌猎枪和一把有牌步枪。

ADJ
领有售酒执照的；特许销售酒类的
If a place such as a restaurant or hotel is licensed, it has been given a licence to sell alcoholic drinks.
例：...licensed premises...领有售酒执照的经营场所
例：The restaurant is licensed, offering draught beers, wines, spirits and soft drinks.这家餐馆有酒类经营执照，提供扎啤、葡萄酒、烈性酒和汽水。

ready-made 
英[ˌredi:ˈmeɪd] 美[ˌrɛdiˈmed]
 
adj.现成的；做好的；事前已想好了的；n.已做好的东西；
 "

ADJ
现成的;预制的
If something that you buy is ready-made, you can use it immediately, because the work you would normally have to do has already been done.
例：We rely quite a bit on ready-made meals — they are so convenient...我们很是依赖现成食物——它们真的很方便。
例：You can buy it ready-made at Chinese groceries...在中国杂货店里你可以买到现成的。

ADJ
方便的;实用的;有用的
Ready-made means extremely convenient or useful for a particular purpose.
例：Those wishing to study urban development have a ready-made example on their doorstep...那些想要研究城市发展的人眼下就有一个十分现成的范例。
例：It provides perfect strangers with a ready-made and infinitely adaptable topic of conversation.它给完全陌生的人提供了一个方便灵活的聊天话题。

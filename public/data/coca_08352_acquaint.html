acquaint 
英[əˈkweɪnt] 美[əˈkwent]
 
vt.使熟悉；使认识；把某事通知某人，告知；
 "

VERB
使认识;介绍;使了解
If you acquaint someone with something, you tell them about it so that they know it. If you acquaint yourself with something, you learn about it.
例：...efforts to acquaint the public with their rights under the new law...为使公众熟悉新法律所赋予的权利而作出的努力
例：I want to acquaint myself with your strengths and weaknesses.我想知道你有哪些长处以及不足。

challenging

adj.挑战性的；引起兴趣的；令人深思的；v.挑战，质疑(challenge的现在分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(任务或工作)艰巨的，具有挑战性的，要求高的
A challenging task or job requires great effort and determination.
例：Mike found a challenging job as a computer programmer...迈克找到一份做计算机程序员的富有挑战性的工作。
例：I'm ready to do all those things which are more challenging.我愿意接受更大的挑战。

ADJ-GRADED
挑战性的;挑衅的
If you do something in a challenging way, you seem to be inviting people to argue with you or compete against you in some way.
例：Mona gave him a challenging look.莫娜挑衅地看了他一眼。

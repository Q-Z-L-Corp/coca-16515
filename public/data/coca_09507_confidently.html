confidently 
英['kɒnfədəntlɪ] 美[ˈkɑnfədəntlɪ]
 
adv.确信地，肯定地；自信地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
坚信的;有信心的
If you are confident about something, you are certain that it will happen in the way you want it to.
例：I am confident that everything will come out right in time...我坚信一切终究都会好起来。
例：Mr Ryan is confident of success...瑞安先生坚信一定会成功。

ADJ-GRADED
自信的;充满信心的
If a person or their manner is confident, they feel sure about their own abilities, qualities, or ideas.
例：In time he became more confident and relaxed...过了一段时间，他变得更加自信、更为放松。
例：She is a confident woman who is certain of her views.她是一位充满自信的女性，对自己的观点深信不疑。

ADJ-GRADED
肯定的;有把握的
If you are confident that something is true, you are sure that it is true. A confident statement is one that the speaker is sure is true.
例：She is confident that everybody is on her side...她确信所有人都站在她这边。
例：'Bet you I can', comes the confident reply.“我肯定行。”传来一个很有把握的回答。

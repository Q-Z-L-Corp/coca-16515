genteel 
英[dʒenˈti:l] 美[dʒɛnˈtil]

ADJ-GRADED
彬彬有礼的；有教养的；上流社会的；装出绅士派头的
A genteel person is respectable and well-mannered, and comes or seems to come from a high social class.
例：It was a place to which genteel families came in search of health and quiet.这是上流社会家庭寻求健康和静谧的场所。
例：...two maiden ladies with genteel manners and voices.两位举止文雅、嗓音轻柔的少女

ADJ-GRADED
（地方）幽静的,古朴单调的
A genteel place or area is quiet and traditional, but may also be old-fashioned and dull.
例：...the genteel towns of Winchester and Chichester.古朴幽静的温彻斯特和奇切斯特

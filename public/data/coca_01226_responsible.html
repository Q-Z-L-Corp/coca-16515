responsible 
英[rɪˈspɒnsəbl] 美[rɪˈspɑnsəbl]
 
adj.尽责的；承担责任；负有责任的；懂道理的；
 "

ADJ
负有责任的;应承担责任的
If someone or something is responsible for a particular event or situation, they are the cause of it or they can be blamed for it.
例：He still felt responsible for her death...他仍然觉得自己对她的死负有责任。
例：I want you to do everything you can to find out who's responsible.我希望你能尽一切可能找出责任人。

ADJ
对(某事物)负责的
If you are responsible for something, it is your job or duty to deal with it and make decisions relating to it.
例：...the minister responsible for the environment...负责环境工作的部长
例：The man responsible for finding the volunteers is Dr. Charles Weber.负责寻找志愿者的人是查尔斯·韦伯博士。

ADJ
对(某人或某团体)负责的
If you are responsible to a person or group, they have authority over you and you have to report to them about what you do.
例：I'm responsible to my board of directors...我对董事会负责。
例：The government will be responsible to the President alone.政府只对总统一人负责。

ADJ-GRADED
负责的;有责任心的;可靠的
Responsible people behave properly and sensibly, without needing to be supervised.
例：He's a very responsible sort of person...他是个非常有责任心的人。
例：He feels that the media should be more responsible in what they report.他觉得媒体应该对报道的内容更负责任些。

ADJ-GRADED
责任重大的
Responsible jobs involve making important decisions or carrying out important tasks.
例：I work in a government office. It's a responsible position, I suppose, but not very exciting...我在政府机关工作。我想那是一个责任重大的职位，但并不是很有意思。
例：They have been demoted to less responsible jobs.他们被降职去做不太重要的工作。

ADJ
世界著名的;举世闻名的
Someone or something that is world-famous is known about by people all over the world.
例：...the world-famous Hollywood Bowl...举世闻名的好莱坞露天剧场
例：Harefield Hospital has become world-famous for its pioneering heart transplant surgery.哈瑞福德医院以其开创性的心脏移植手术闻名于世。

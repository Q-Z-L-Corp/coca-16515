正当理由；合理的解释
A justification for something is an acceptable reason or explanation for it.
例：To me the only justification for a zoo is educational...对我来说，动物园存在的唯一正当理由是其教育作用。
例：I knew from the beginning that there was no justification for what I was doing.我从一开始就知道我的所作所为毫无道理。

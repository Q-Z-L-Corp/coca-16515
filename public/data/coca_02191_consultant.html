N-COUNT
高级顾问医师;会诊医师
A consultant is an experienced doctor with a high position, who specializes in one area of medicine.
例：Shirley's brother is now a consultant heart surgeon in Sweden.雪利的兄弟现在在瑞典做心脏外科高级顾问医师。

N-COUNT
顾问
A consultant is a person who gives expert advice to a person or organization on a particular subject.
例：He was a consultant to the Swedish government.他是瑞典政府顾问。
例：...a team of management consultants sent in to reorganise the department.派来对部门进行重组的管理顾问工作组

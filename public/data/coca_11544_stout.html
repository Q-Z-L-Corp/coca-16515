stout 
英[staʊt] 美[staʊt]
 
adj.强壮的；结实的；勇敢的；坚定的；n.烈性黑啤酒；胖子；特大号；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
肥胖的;肥壮的
A stout person is rather fat.
例：He was a tall, stout man with gray hair.他是个又高又胖、头发灰白的男子。

ADJ-GRADED
结实的;厚实的;粗壮的
Stout shoes, branches, or other objects are thick and strong.
例：I hope you've both got stout shoes...我希望你们俩都有结实的鞋子。
例：The old man picked up a stout stick that lay by his feet.老头捡起脚边的一根粗棍子。

ADJ-GRADED
(行动、态度或信念)坚定的，坚决的，强有力的
If you use stout to describe someone's actions, attitudes, or beliefs, you approve of them because they are strong and determined.
例：He produced a stout defence of the car business...他为汽车业进行了坚决的辩护。
例：The invasion was held up by unexpectedly stout resistance.入侵行动因遭遇到了出乎意料的顽强抵抗而受阻。

N-COUNT
（尤指不祥的）先兆，预兆
Something that is a harbinger of something else, especially something bad, is a sign that it is going to happen.
例：The November air stung my cheeks, a harbinger of winter.11月的空气刺痛了我的脸颊，预示着冬天就要来临。

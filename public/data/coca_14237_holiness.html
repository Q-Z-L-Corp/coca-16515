holiness 
英[ˈhəʊlinəs] 美[ˈhoʊlinəs]
 
n.神圣；（对罗马教皇的尊称）陛下，圣座；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
神圣
Holiness is the state or quality of being holy.
例：The holiness of God reveals one's own sin...上帝的神圣揭示世人自身的罪孽。
例：We were immediately struck by this city's holiness.我们立即就被这座城市的神圣氛围所打动。

N-VOC
（对天主教教皇或某些其他宗教领袖的尊称）陛下，圣座
You say Your Holiness or His Holiness when you address or refer respectfully to the Pope or to leaders of some other religions.
例：The President received His Holiness at the White House.总统在白宫接见了教皇陛下。

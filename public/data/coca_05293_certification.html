certification 
英[ˌsɜ:tɪfɪˈkeɪʃn] 美[ˌsɜrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn]
 
n.证明，鉴定，证书；
 "

VERB
证明;出具证明;证实
If someone in an official position certifies something, they officially state that it is true.
例：...if the president certified that the project would receive at least $650m from overseas sources...如果总统证明这个工程将获得至少6.5亿美元的海外资金
例：The National Election Council is supposed to certify the results of the election...全国选举委员会应对选举结果出具证明。

VERB
给…颁发合格（或结业）证书
If someone is certified as a particular kind of worker, they are given a certificate stating that they have successfully completed a course of training in their profession.
例：They wanted to get certified as divers.他们想拿到潜水员资格证。
例：...a certified accountant...持证会计

nip 
英[nɪp] 美[nɪp]
 
vt.& vi.夹，捏，掐；阻止；冻伤；剪断；vt.伤害；摧残；vi.快走；掐去；（比赛或竞赛中）势均力敌；将…扼杀在萌芽状态；n.啃咬；（酒等的）一口，少量；寒冷；寒意；
 "

VERB
迅速前往;急忙赶往
If you nip somewhere, usually somewhere nearby, you go there quickly or for a short time.
例：Should I nip out and get some groceries?...要我赶紧出去一趟买些食品吗？
例：Wayne is always nipping down to the corner shop for him...韦恩总是一溜烟跑到街角商店找他。

VERB
轻咬;轻掐;轻捏
If an animal or person nips you, they bite you lightly or squeeze a piece of your skin between their finger and thumb.
例：I have known cases where dogs have nipped babies...我知道有一些狗咬婴儿的事情。
例：He nipped Billy's cheek with two rough fingers.他用两个粗糙的手指捏了捏比利的脸蛋。

N-COUNT
(烈性酒的)一小口，一呷，少量
A nip is a small amount of a strong alcoholic drink.
例：She had a habit of taking an occasional nip from a flask of cognac.她有偶尔喝一小口干邑的习惯。

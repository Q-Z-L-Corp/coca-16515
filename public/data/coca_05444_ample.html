ADJ-GRADED
大量的；充裕的；丰富的
If there is an ample amount of something, there is enough of it and usually some extra.
例：There'll be ample opportunity to relax, swim and soak up some sun...将会有充足的机会去放松、游泳和晒太阳。
例：The design of the ground floor created ample space for a good-sized kitchen.底层的设计为建一个大厨房提供了充足的空间。

ADJ-GRADED
（身材）丰满的
If you describe someone's figure as ample, you mean that they are large in a pleasant or attractive way.
例：...a young mother with a baby resting against her ample bosom.丰满的怀里抱着婴儿的年轻妈妈

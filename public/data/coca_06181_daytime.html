 "

N-SING
白天;日间
The daytime is the part of a day between the time when it gets light and the time when it gets dark.
例：In the daytime he stayed up in his room, sleeping, or listening to music...他白天呆在自己的屋子里睡觉或听音乐。
例：Please give a daytime telephone number...请留一个日间的电话号码。

ADJ
(电视或广播节目周一至周五)白天播出的，日间播放的
Daytime television and radio is broadcast during the morning and afternoon on weekdays.
例：...ITV's new package of daytime programmes.独立电视台的新一整套日间档节目

write 
英[raɪt] 美[raɪt]
 
vt.& vi.写；写信；写作；作曲；
 "

VERB
写;书写;写字
When you write something on a surface, you use something such as a pen or pencil to produce words, letters, or numbers on the surface.
例：If you'd like one, simply write your name and address on a postcard and send it to us...如果你想要一个的话，只要把姓名、住址写在一张明信片上寄给我们就行。
例：They were still trying to teach her to read and write...他们还在试图教她读书写字。

VERB
写(书、诗等);创作(歌曲)
If you write something such as a book, a poem, or a piece of music, you create it and record it on paper or perhaps on a computer.
例：I had written quite a lot of orchestral music in my student days...我在学生时代创作过很多管弦乐作品。
例：Finding a volunteer to write the computer program isn't a problem...找个志愿者编这个计算机程序不成问题。

VERB
写作，著述（通常以出版为目的）
Someone who writes creates books, stories, or articles, usually for publication.
例：Jay wanted to write...杰伊想要写作。
例：She writes for many papers, including the Sunday Times...她给包括《星期日泰晤士报》在内的很多报纸撰稿。

VERB
写(信);给…写信(美国英语亦可用 write someone)
When you write to someone or write them a letter, you give them information, ask them something, or express your feelings in a letter. In American English, you can also write someone.
例：Many people have written to me on this subject...很多人给我写信探讨这个问题。
例：She had written him a note a couple of weeks earlier...她几周前给他写过一个便条。

VERB
(在信、书或文章中)写道，说道
If someone writes that something is the case, they say it in a letter, book, or article.
例：'Some six months later,' Freud writes, 'Hans had got over his jealousy.'...“大约6个月后，”弗洛伊德写道，“汉斯克服了他的嫉妒心理。”
例：A few days later he wrote that he had hopes of a staff job.几天后，他写信说他有望得到一份行政工作。

VERB
开具(支票、收据、处方等)
When someone writes something such as a cheque, receipt, or prescription, they put the necessary information on it and usually sign it.
例：Snape wrote a receipt with a gold fountain pen...斯内普用金笔开了一张收据。
例：I'll write you a cheque in a moment.我过会儿给你开张支票。

VERB
(往计算机或磁盘)录入，写入
If you write to a computer or a disk, you record data on it.
例：You should write-protect all disks that you do not usually need to write to.你应该对你通常不需要写入内容的磁盘进行写保护。

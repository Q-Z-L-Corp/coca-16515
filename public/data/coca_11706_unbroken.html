ADJ
连续不断的；未被打断的；完整的；未破损的
If something is unbroken, it is continuous or complete and has not been interrupted or broken.
例：...an unbroken run of 38 match wins...38场连胜
例：We've had ten days of almost unbroken sunshine...我们这儿连续10天差不多都是阳光明媚。

sortie 
英[ˈsɔ:ti] 美[ˈsɔrti]
 
n.突围，突击；出击部队；（飞机出动的）架次；vt.突围，突袭；
 "

N-COUNT
短程旅行(尤指去陌生地方)
A sortie is a brief trip away from your home base, especially a trip to an unfamiliar place.
例：From here we plan several sorties into the countryside on foot.我们计划了几次从这里出发徒步去乡间的短途旅行。
例：...little sorties into antique shops.去古董店小逛

N-COUNT
(军队的)出击，袭击
If a military force makes a sortie, it leaves its own position and goes briefly into enemy territory to make an attack.
例：His men made a sortie to Guazatan and took a prisoner...他的士兵袭击了瓜扎坦，擒获了一名俘虏。
例：They flew 2,700 sorties in a day and didn't lose a single plane.他们一天出动了 2,700 架次飞机，而且没有损失一架。

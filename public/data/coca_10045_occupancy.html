occupancy

n.占有，占领；居住；[物]占有率；占据期间；

N-UNCOUNT
（通常指一段固定时间内对房屋、土地等的）占用
Occupancy is the act of using a room, building, or area of land, usually for a fixed period of time.
例：Hotel occupancy has been as low as 40%...酒店入住率最低达到40％。
例：Tour operators report low occupancy rates.旅游业经营者反映客房入住率低。

far-reaching 
英[fɑ: 'ri:tʃɪŋ] 美[ˈfɑrˈritʃɪŋ]
 
adj.深远的，广泛的，深至远处的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
影响深远的;意义重大的
If you describe actions, events, or changes as far-reaching, you mean that they have a very great influence and affect a great number of things.
例：The economy is in danger of collapse unless far-reaching reforms are implemented.如果不实施影响深远的改革，经济就面临崩溃的危险。

interested 
英[ˈɪntrəstɪd] 美[ˈɪntrɪstɪd, -tərɪstɪd, -təˌrɛstɪd]
 
adj.感兴趣的；拥有权益的；有成见的；v.使…感兴趣(interest的过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
感兴趣的;有兴趣的;关心的
If you are interested in something, you think it is important and want to learn more about it or spend time doing it.
例：I thought she might be interested in Paula's proposal...我觉得她对葆拉的提议可能会感兴趣。
例：I think the young man is getting interested in gardening...我认为这个年轻人开始对园艺产生兴趣了。

ADJ
有利害关系的;利益相关的;当事的
An interested party or group of people is affected by or involved in a particular event or situation.
例：All the interested parties eventually agreed to the idea.所有利益相关方最终都同意了这一想法。

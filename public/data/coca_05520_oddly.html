ADV
奇怪的是；说来也怪
You use oddly to indicate that what you are saying is true, but that it is not what you expected.
例：He said no and seemed oddly reluctant to talk about it...他说没有，而且奇怪的是似乎不愿谈及此事。
例：Oddly, Emma says she never considered her face was attractive...奇怪的是，埃玛说她从来都不认为自己长得好看。

V-ERG
（给飞机或其他交通工具）（再）加油;（给…）续燃料
When an aircraft or other vehicle refuels or when someone refuels it, it is filled with more fuel so that it can continue its journey.
例：His plane stopped in France to refuel...他的飞机经停法国续加燃油。
例：The airline's crew refuelled the plane.航空公司的机务人员给飞机加了油。

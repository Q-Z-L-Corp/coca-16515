N-COUNT
深长的伤口（或切口）
A gash is a long, deep cut in your skin or in the surface of something.
例：There was an inch-long gash just above his right eye.就在他右眼的上方有一条一英寸长的伤口。

VERB
划伤；砍伤
If you gash something, you accidentally make a long and deep cut in it.
例：He gashed his leg while felling trees.他砍树的时候砍伤了腿。

trot 
英[trɒt] 美[trɑt]
 
n.马的小跑；人快步走；摇摇摆摆学步的孩子；〈古〉老太婆；vi.小跑；快速前进；赶紧；vt.使小跑；使快步走；
 "

VERB
快步走;小步跑
If you trot somewhere, you move fairly fast at a speed between walking and running, taking small quick steps.
例：I trotted down the steps and out to the shed...我小步跑下台阶，奔向外面的小屋。
例：They trotted along behind him…他们跟在他身后疾走。

VERB
(马等)小跑;骑马小跑
When an animal such as a horse trots, it moves fairly fast, taking quick small steps. You can also say that the rider of the animal is trotting .
例：Alan took the reins and the small horse started trotting...艾伦抓住缰绳，小马开始慢跑起来。
例：Pete got on his horse and started trotting across the field.皮特跨上马，开始在田野间小跑起来。

PHRASE
接连地;持续地
If something happens several times on the trot, it happens that number of times without a break.
例：She lost five games on the trot...她接连输了 5 场比赛。
例：Sales of new cars rose in May for the second month on the trot.5 月份新车的销售量连续第二个月攀升。

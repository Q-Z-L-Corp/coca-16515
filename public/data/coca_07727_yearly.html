ADJ
一年一度的;每年一次的
A yearly event happens once a year or every year.
例：The seven major industrial countries will have their yearly meeting in London.7个主要工业国家将在伦敦举行一年一度的会议。

ADJ
年度的;一年的
You use yearly to describe something such as an amount that relates to a period of one year.
例：In Holland, the government sets a yearly budget for health care.在荷兰，政府会制定医疗保健部门的年度预算。

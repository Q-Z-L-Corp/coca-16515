N-UNCOUNT
(圆、地方、圆状物的)周长
The circumference of a circle, place, or round object is the distance around its edge.
例：...a scientist calculating the earth's circumference...计算地球周长的科学家
例：The island is 3.5 km in circumference.这个岛周长3.5千米。

ADJ
外太空的
Cosmic means occurring in, or coming from, the part of space that lies outside Earth and its atmosphere.
例：...cosmic radiation.外太空的辐射
例：...cosmic debris.外太空垃圾

ADJ
宇宙的
Cosmic means belonging or relating to the universe.
例：...the cosmic laws governing our world.支配我们世界的宇宙规律
例：...humanity's place in the cosmic order of things.在宇宙万物的秩序中人类的位置

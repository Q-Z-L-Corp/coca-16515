 "

ADV
先前；以前
Previously means at some time before the period that you are talking about.
例：Guyana's railways were previously owned by private companies...圭亚那的铁路以前是归私有企业所有的。
例：The contract was awarded to a previously unknown company...合同签给了一个之前并不知名的公司。

ADV
（一段时间）以前
You can use previously to say how much earlier one event was than another event.
例：He had first entered the House 12 years previously...12年前，他首次成为议员。
例：She had rented the flat from the council some fourteen months previously.大约14个月以前，她从地方政府那里租下了这套公寓。

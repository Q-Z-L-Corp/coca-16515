ADJ-GRADED
极其荒谬的；愚蠢至极的
If you describe something as preposterous, you mean that it is extremely unreasonable and foolish.
例：The whole idea was preposterous.整个想法都荒唐透顶。
例：...their preposterous claim that they had unearthed a plot.他们无比愚蠢地声称自己发现了一个阴谋

detriment 
英[ˈdetrɪmənt] 美[ˈdɛtrəmənt]
 
n.损害，伤害；造成损害的事物；
 "

PHRASE
对…有害;有损于
If something happens to the detriment of something or to a person's detriment, it causes harm or damage to them.
例：Children spend too much time on schoolwork, to the detriment of other activities.孩子把太多的时间用于做作业，影响了他们参加其他活动。

PHRASE
对…无害;无损于
If something happens without detriment to a person or thing, it does not harm or damage them.
例：These difficulties have been overcome without detriment to performance.这些困难都已在不影响业绩的情况下得到了克服。

academy 
英[əˈkædəmi] 美[əˈkædəmi]
 
n.专科学校；学会，学院；一般的高等教育；私立学校，学术团体；
 "

N-COUNT
专科院校;学院;(美国的)私立中学
Academy is sometimes used in the names of schools and colleges, especially those specializing in particular subjects or skills, or private high schools in the United States.
例：...the Royal Academy of Music.皇家音乐学院
例：...her experience as a police academy instructor.她在警校当教官的经历

N-IN-NAMES
（某一领域的）学会，研究院
Academy appears in the names of some societies formed to improve or maintain standards in a particular field.
例：...the American Academy of Psychotherapists...美国心理治疗师学会
例：The British Academy of Film and Television Arts.英国电影和电视艺术学院

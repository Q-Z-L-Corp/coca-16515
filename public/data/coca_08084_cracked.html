cracked 
英[krækt] 美[krækt]
 
adj.有裂缝的；声音沙哑的；精神失常的；v.破裂；崩溃(crack的过去分词)；发沙哑声；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
破裂的;有裂纹的
An object that is cracked has lines on its surface because it is damaged.
例：The ceiling was grey and cracked.天花板是灰色的，而且有裂纹。
例：...a cracked mirror.破裂的镜子

ADJ-GRADED
(嗓音)沙哑的，颤抖的;(律音)刺耳的，不齐的
A cracked voice or a cracked musical note sounds rough and unsteady.
例：When he spoke, his voice was hoarse and cracked.他说话时声音沙哑颤抖。

ADJ-GRADED
(行为或想法)奇怪的，错乱的
If you say that someone is cracked, you think that their behaviour or ideas are very strange.

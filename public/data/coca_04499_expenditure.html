expenditure 
英[ɪkˈspendɪtʃə(r)] 美[ɪkˈspɛndətʃɚ]
 
n.花费，支出；费用，经费；（尤指金钱的）支出额；（精力、时间、材料等的）耗费；
 "

N-VAR
花费;开销;支出
Expenditure is the spending of money on something, or the money that is spent on something.
例：Policies of tax reduction must lead to reduced public expenditure...减税政策必然导致公共支出的削减。
例：They should cut their expenditure on defence...他们应该缩减国防开支。

N-UNCOUNT
（时间、精力等的）消耗，花费
Expenditure of something such as time or energy is the using of that thing for a particular purpose.
例：The financial rewards justified the expenditure of effort.经济上的回报证明付出的努力是值得的。

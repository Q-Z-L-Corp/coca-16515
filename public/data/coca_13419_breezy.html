ADJ-GRADED
随意的；乐天的；自信的
If you describe someone as breezy, you mean that they behave in a casual, cheerful, and confident manner.
例：...his bright and breezy personality...他开朗活泼的个性
例：Mona tried to sound breezy.莫娜竭力使自己的声音听上去轻松随意。

ADJ-GRADED
明快的；生动的；活泼的
If you describe something as breezy, especially music or clothing, you mean that it is bright, lively, and cheerful.
例：This album is bright, breezy and playful.这张专辑音乐轻松明快，趣味十足。

ADJ-GRADED
（天气）有微风的
When the weather is breezy, there is a fairly strong but pleasant wind blowing.
例：The day was breezy and warm.这一天微风习习，暖洋洋的。

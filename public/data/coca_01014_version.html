 "

N-COUNT
变化形式;变体;版本
A version of something is a particular form of it in which some details are different from earlier or later forms.
例：...an updated version of his book...他的书的最新版本
例：Ludo is a version of an ancient Indian racing game...鲁多是由一种古印度竞赛游戏演变而来。

N-COUNT
（某人对某事件区别于他人的）描述，说法
Someone's version of an event is their own description of it, especially when it is different to other people's.
例：Yesterday afternoon the White House put out a new version of events...昨天下午，白宫对事件给出了另一种说法。
例：She and her friends wanted to go public with their version of the incident...她和朋友们想把他们看到的事情经过公之于众。

VERB
否决；驳回；宣布…无效
If someone in authority overrules a person or their decision, they officially decide that the decision is incorrect or not valid.
例：In 1991, the Court of Appeal overruled this decision...1991年，上诉法院驳回了这一判决。
例：I told them it was a lousy idea, but I was overruled.我告诉他们这个主意糟糕透顶，但是他们却拒不听从。

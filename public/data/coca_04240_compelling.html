compelling 
英[kəmˈpelɪŋ] 美[kəmˈpɛlɪŋ]
 
adj.引人入胜的；扣人心弦的；非常强烈的；不可抗拒的；v.强迫，使不得不( compel的现在分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人信服的;有说服力的
A compelling argument or reason is one that convinces you that something is true or that something should be done.
例：Factual and forensic evidence makes a suicide verdict the most compelling answer to the mystery of his death...事实证据和法庭证据使得自杀成为解开他死因之谜的最令人信服的答案。
例：My second and more compelling reason for going to Dearborn was to see the Henry Ford Museum.我去迪尔伯恩的第二个原因，也是更令人信服的原因,是为了参观亨利·福特博物馆。

ADJ-GRADED
非常有趣的;很有吸引力的
If you describe something such as a film or book, or someone's appearance, as compelling, you mean you want to keep looking at it or reading it because you find it so interesting.
例：...a frighteningly violent yet compelling film...有可怕的暴力镜头但却引人入胜的电影
例：Her eyes were her best feature, wide-set and compelling.她的眼睛是她五官中长得最好的，大而迷人。

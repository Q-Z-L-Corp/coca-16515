disintegration 
英[dɪsˌɪntɪ'ɡreɪʃn] 美[dɪsˌɪntɪˈɡreʃən]
 
n.瓦解；崩溃；<物>裂变；蜕变；
 "

VERB
崩溃;瓦解
If something disintegrates, it becomes seriously weakened, and is divided or destroyed.
例：During October 1918 the Austro-Hungarian Empire began to disintegrate.在 1918 年 10 月间，奥匈帝国开始瓦解。

VERB
碎裂;分裂;粉碎
If an object or substance disintegrates, it breaks into many small pieces or parts and is destroyed.
例：At 420mph the windscreen disintegrated.挡风玻璃在时速420英里时破碎。

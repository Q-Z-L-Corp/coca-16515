N-COUNT
建筑师;设计师
An architect is a person who designs buildings.

N-COUNT
（景观美化、铁路等大型项目的）规划者，设计者
You can use architect to refer to a person who plans large projects such as landscaping or railways.
例：...Merrick Denton-Thompson, the landscape architect for Hampshire county council.梅里克·登顿-汤普森，汉普郡议会的园林设计师
例：...Paul Andreu, chief architect of French railways.保罗·安德鲁，法国铁路的总设计师

N-COUNT
（想法、事件或组织的）发起者，创立者
The architect of an idea, event, or institution is the person who invented it or made it happen.
例：...Russia's chief architect of economic reform.俄罗斯经济改革的总规划师

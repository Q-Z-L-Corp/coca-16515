decrease 
英[dɪˈkri:s] 美[dɪˈkris]
 
n.减少，减小；减少量；vi.&vt.减少，减小；
 "

V-ERG
(使)减少;(使)减小;(使)降低
When something decreases or when you decrease it, it becomes less in quantity, size, or intensity.
例：Population growth is decreasing by 1.4% each year...人口增长率正在以每年 1.4% 的速度下降。
例：The number of independent firms decreased from 198 to 96...独立公司的数量从 198 个减少到了 96个。

N-COUNT
减少;减小;降低
A decrease in the quantity, size, or intensity of something is a reduction in it.
例：...a decrease in the number of young people out of work...失业青年人数的减少
例：Bank base rates have fallen from 10 per cent to 6 per cent — a decrease of 40 per cent.银行的基本利率已从 10% 降到了 6%，降幅高达 40%。

restitution 
英[ˌrestɪˈtju:ʃn] 美[ˌrestɪˈtuʃn]
 
n.归还原主；恢复原状；（尤指用钱）赔偿；补偿；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
归还;赔偿;偿还
Restitution is the act of giving back to a person something that was lost or stolen, or of paying them money for the loss.
例：The victims are demanding full restitution...受害人要求全额赔偿。
例：We have asked that they rehire the people that were fired and that they make restitution to them.我们已经要求他们重新雇用那些被开除的人，并对他们进行补偿。

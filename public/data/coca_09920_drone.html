drone 
英[drəʊn] 美[droʊn]
 
n.雄蜂；嗡嗡声；持续低音；无人驾驶飞机；v.嗡嗡叫；嗡嗡响；絮絮叨叨地说；
 "

VERB
嗡嗡作响;嗡嗡叫
If something drones, it makes a low, continuous, dull noise.
例：Above him an invisible plane droned through the night sky.在他头顶上一架隐形飞机嗡嗡地飞过夜空。
例：...a virtually non-stop droning noise in the background.背景中几乎从未间断的嗡嗡声

VERB
(人)絮絮叨叨地说,喋喋不休地说
If you say that someone drones, you mean that they keep talking about something in a boring way.
例：Chambers' voice droned, maddening as an insect around his head...钱伯斯一直在他耳边嗡嗡地说个不停，他都快疯了。
例：The droning murmur of the doctor's voice in the bedroom had ceased.卧室里医生喋喋不休的低语声已经停下了。

N-COUNT
游手好闲者;寄生虫
People who do not contribute anything to society or to an organization are sometimes described as drones .
例：A few are dim-witted drones, but most are talented, frustrated, wasted people.有几个是愚笨的寄生虫,但是大多数是有才能却不得志而自暴自弃的人。

N-COUNT
雄蜂
A drone is a male bee.

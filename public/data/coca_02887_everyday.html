ADJ
每天（发生）的;平常的;普通的
You use everyday to describe something which happens or is used every day, or forms a regular and basic part of your life, so it is not especially interesting or unusual.
例：In the course of my everyday life, I had very little contact with teenagers.在我的日常生活中，我很少和青少年打交道。
例：...opportunities for improving fitness in your everyday routine.在日常生活中改善健康的机会

N-VAR
狂热;狂乱;疯狂
Frenzy or a frenzy is great excitement or wild behaviour that often results from losing control of your feelings.
例：'Get out!' she ordered in a frenzy...“滚出去!”她狂怒地命令道。
例：The country was gripped by a frenzy of nationalism.这个国家笼罩在民族主义的狂热中。

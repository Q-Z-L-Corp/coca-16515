countryman 
英[ˈkʌntrimən] 美[ˈkʌntrimən]

 "

N-COUNT
同胞;同国人;同乡
Your countrymen are people from your own country.
例：He lost last year's final to fellow countryman Michael Stich.他在去年的决赛中输给了自己的同胞迈克尔·施蒂希。

N-COUNT
乡下人;农村人
A countryman is a person who lives in the country rather than in a city or a town.
例：He had the red face of a countryman.他有着乡下人的红脸庞。

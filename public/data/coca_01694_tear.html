tear 
英[teə(r)] 美[ter ]
 
v.撕裂；拉掉；挣开；拉伤；n.破洞；裂口；
 "

N-COUNT
眼泪;泪水;泪珠
Tears are the drops of salty liquid that come out of your eyes when you are crying.
例：Her eyes filled with tears...她双眼噙满泪水。
例：I just broke down and wept with tears of joy...我一下子禁不住喜极而泣。

N-PLURAL
哭泣
You can use tears in expressions such as in tears ,burst into tears, and close to tears to indicate that someone is crying or is almost crying.
例：He was in floods of tears on the phone...他在电话里失声痛哭。
例：She burst into tears and ran from the kitchen...她突然哭了，跑出了厨房。

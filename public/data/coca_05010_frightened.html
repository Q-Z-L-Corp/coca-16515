ADJ-GRADED
受惊的;害怕的
If you are frightened, you are anxious or afraid, often because of something that has just happened or that you think may happen.
例：She was frightened of flying...她害怕坐飞机。
例：Miriam was too frightened to tell her family what had happened.米丽娅姆太害怕了，不敢告诉家人发生了什么事。

angled

N-COUNT
角；角度
An angle is the difference in direction between two lines or surfaces. Angles are measured in degrees.
例：The boat is now leaning at a 30 degree angle.船正以30度角倾斜。

N-COUNT
（两条线或两个平面相交的）夹角，边角
An angle is the shape that is created where two lines or surfaces join together.
例：...the angle of the blade.刀口的夹角
例：...brackets to adjust the steering wheel's angle.用于调整方向盘角度的支架

N-COUNT
角度
An angle is the direction from which you look at something.
例：Thanks to the angle at which he stood, he could just see the sunset...由于站的位置视角好，他刚好能看见日落。
例：His face will be discreetly concealed by camera angles.通过调整照相机的角度，他的脸将被巧妙地遮住。

N-COUNT
角度；观点；立场；侧重点；倾向性
You can refer to a way of presenting something or thinking about it as a particular angle .
例：We had to do the scene over and over again, from different angles...我们不得不翻来覆去地从不同的角度拍这个场面。
例：He was considering the idea from all angles.他正从各个角度考虑这个想法。

VERB
（间接迂回地）猎取，谋取，博取
If someone is angling for something, they are trying to get it without asking for it directly.
例：It sounds as if he's just angling for sympathy.听起来好像他只是在博取同情。

V-ERG
（使）朝向；（使）转向
If you angle something or if it angles in a particular direction, it faces or points in that direction.
例：You can open the slats for a bright light or angle them for more shade...可以调节百叶窗使光线亮一些，也可以暗一些。
例：The path angled downhill and northwards...小路向山下伸展，然后向北延伸。

PHRASE
成角度的;歪的;斜的
If something is at an angle, it is leaning in a particular direction so that it is not straight, horizontal, or vertical.
例：An iron bar stuck out at an angle...一根铁棍斜伸出来。
例：The wall's at a slight angle.这面墙有点歪。

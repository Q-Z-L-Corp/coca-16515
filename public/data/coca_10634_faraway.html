faraway 
英[ˈfɑ:rəweɪ] 美[ˈfɑrəˈwe]
 
adj.遥远的；恍惚的；辽；偏远；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
远的;遥远的;远方的
A faraway place is a long distance from you or from a particular place.
例：They have just returned from faraway places with wonderful stories to tell.他们刚从遥远的地方回来，有很多精彩的故事要讲。
例：...photographs of a far away country.遥远国度的照片

ADJ-GRADED
思绪飘远的;心不在焉的
If you describe someone or their thoughts as faraway, you mean that they are thinking about something that is very different from the situation around them.
例：She smiled with a faraway look in her eyes.她微笑着，眼神缥缈恍惚。

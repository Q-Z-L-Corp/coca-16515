N-UNCOUNT
食物;营养;养料
Sustenance is food or drink which a person, animal, or plant needs to remain alive and healthy.
例：The state provided a basic quantity of food for daily sustenance, but little else.国家提供基本的日常食物供应，但除此之外就没什么了。

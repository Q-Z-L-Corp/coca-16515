fascination 
英[ˌfæsɪˈneɪʃn] 美[ˌfæsəˈneʃən]
 
n.魅力；迷恋；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
入迷;着迷;酷爱
Fascination is the state of being greatly interested in or delighted by something.
例：I've had a lifelong fascination with the sea and with small boats.我一生都对大海和小船着迷。

N-COUNT
魅力;令人着迷的事物
A fascination is something that fascinates people.
例：...a series focusing on the fascinations of the British Museum.主要讲述大英博物馆魅力的系列节目

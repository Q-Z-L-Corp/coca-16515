high-powered 
英[ˈhaiˈpauəd] 美[ˈhaɪˈpaʊəd]
 
adj.高性能的；精力充沛的；大功率的；马力大的；

ADJ-GRADED
（机器、设备等）大功率的，高效的
A high-powered machine or piece of equipment is very powerful and efficient.
例：...high powered lasers.大功率激光器
例：...high-powered binoculars.高倍双筒望远镜

ADJ-GRADED
（人）身居要职的，肩负重任的;（职位）重要的，需要很强能力的
Someone who is high-powered or has a high-powered job has a very important and responsible job which requires a lot of ability.
例：...a high-powered lawyer...精明强干的律师
例：I had a very high-powered senior job in publishing.我在出版界担任高层要职。

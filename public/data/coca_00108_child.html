child 
英[tʃaɪld] 美[tʃaɪld]

 "

N-COUNT
小孩;孩子;儿童
A child is a human being who is not yet an adult.
例：When I was a child I lived in a country village...我小时候生活在一个小乡村里。
例：He's just a child.他还只是个孩子。

N-COUNT
儿女;子女
Someone's children are their sons and daughters of any age.
例：How are the children?...孩子们都好吧？
例：His children have left home...他的子女都已离家独立生活了。

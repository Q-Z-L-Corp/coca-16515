ADJ
国际的;国际上的
International means between or involving different countries.
例：...an international agreement against exporting arms to that country.禁止向该国出口武器的国际协定
例：...Kuwait International Airport.科威特国际机场

N-COUNT
国际比赛
In sport, an international is a game that is played between teams representing two different countries.
例：...the midweek international against England.周三对抗英格兰的国际比赛

N-COUNT
国际比赛选手;国家队队员
An international is a member of a country's sports team.
例：...a former England international.前英格兰国手

VERB
比…快；超过；胜过
To outpace someone or something means to perform a particular action faster or better than they can.
例：These hovercraft can easily outpace most boats...这些气垫船在速度上能轻而易举地超过大多数船只。
例：The Japanese economy will continue to outpace its foreign rivals for years to come...在未来几年里，日本的经济发展将继续快过其国外的对手。

colossal 
英[kəˈlɒsl] 美[kəˈlɑsl]
 
adj.巨大的；<口语>异常的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
巨大的;庞大的
If you describe something as colossal, you are emphasizing that it is very large.
例：There has been a colossal waste of public money...一直存在巨大的公款浪费。
例：The task they face is colossal.他们面临的任务十分艰巨。

N-COUNT
(电影、小说、戏剧中的)闪回，倒叙
In a film, novel, or play, a flashback is a scene that returns to events in the past.
例：There is even a flashback to the murder itself.甚至有一段关于谋杀本身的闪回。

N-COUNT
(往事的)突然重现，闪回，再现
If you have a flashback to a past experience, you have a sudden and very clear memory of it.
例：He has recurring flashbacks to the night his friends died.他反复想起朋友们死去的那个夜晚的情形。

consequently 
英[ˈkɒnsɪkwəntli] 美[ˈkɑnsəkwentli]
 
adv.所以，因此；因此，因而；终于，这样；合乎逻辑的推论是；
 "

ADV
所以;结果
Consequently means as a result.
例：Grandfather Dingsdale had sustained a broken back while working in the mines. Consequently, he spent the rest of his life in a wheelchair...丁斯戴尔爷爷在矿井里工作时被压断了脊骨，结果，下半辈子都与轮椅为伴了。
例：Relations between the two companies had, consequently, never been close.因此，两家公司之间的关系一直都不太亲密。

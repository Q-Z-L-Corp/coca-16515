N-COUNT
（网球场、羽毛球场、篮球场等的）底线
The baseline of a tennis, badminton, or basketball court is one of the lines at each end of the court that mark the limits of play.
例：Martinez, when she served, usually stayed on the baseline.马丁内兹发球的时候通常站在底线处。

N-COUNT
（棒球场的）垒线
In baseball, the baseline is the line that a player must not cross when running between bases.

N-COUNT
基线；基准
A baseline is a value or starting point on a scale with which other values can be compared.
例：You'll need such information to use as a baseline for measuring progress.你会需要这些信息作为衡量进展的基准。

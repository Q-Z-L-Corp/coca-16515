enlightenment 
英[ɪnˈlaɪtnmənt] 美[ɛnˈlaɪtnmənt]
 
n.启迪，启发；教化，开导；启蒙运动；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
启发;教化;开明;开通
Enlightenment means the act of enlightening or the state of being enlightened.
例：Stella had a moment of enlightenment.斯特拉豁然开朗。

N-UNCOUNT
（佛教的）般若，觉悟，智慧
In Buddhism, enlightenment is a final spiritual state in which everything is understood and there is no more suffering or desire.
例：...a sense of deep peace and spiritual enlightenment.深深的平和感和精神觉悟

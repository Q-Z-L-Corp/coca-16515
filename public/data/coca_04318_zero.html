NUM
（数字）0
Zero is the number 0.
例：Visibility at the city's airport came down to zero, bringing air traffic to a standstill.该市机场的能见度降为零，空中交通陷入了停顿。
例：...a scale ranging from zero to seven.从0到7的范围

N-UNCOUNT
（气温的）零度
Zero is a temperature of 0˚. It is freezing point on the Centigrade and Celsius scales, and 32˚ below freezing point on the Fahrenheit scale.
例：It's a sunny late winter day, just a few degrees above zero...那是晚冬阳光明媚的一天，温度只有零上几度。
例：That night the mercury fell to thirty degrees below zero.那天晚上温度计显示气温降到了零下30度。

ADJ
毫无;没有
You can use zero to say that there is none at all of the thing mentioned.
例：This new ministry was being created with zero assets and zero liabilities.这个新部委正在筹建，既无资产也无负债。
例：...zero inflation...零通货膨胀率

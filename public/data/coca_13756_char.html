V-ERG
(将…)略微烧焦;(把…)烤煳
If food chars or if you char it, it burns slightly and turns black as it is cooking.
例：Toast hazelnuts on a baking sheet until the skins char...在烘板上将榛子烤至表皮微煳。
例：Halve the peppers and char the skins under a hot grill.将甜椒剖成两半，在滚热的烤架上烤至外壳微焦。

VERB
当清洁工
If a woman chars for someone, she works as their cleaner.
例：I'm calling round at the Rodings to ask if Mrs Higgins will char for you.我将去一趟罗丁家，打听希金斯夫人愿不愿意为你当清洁工。

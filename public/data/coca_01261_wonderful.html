ADJ-GRADED
精彩的;绝妙的;极好的
If you describe something or someone as wonderful, you think they are extremely good.
例：The cold, misty air felt wonderful on his face...雾蒙蒙的寒冷空气使他脸上感觉很舒爽。
例：It's wonderful to see you...见到你真好。

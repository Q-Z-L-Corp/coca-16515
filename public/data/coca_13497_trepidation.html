trepidation 
英[ˌtrepɪˈdeɪʃn] 美[ˌtrɛpɪˈdeʃən]
 
n.害怕；不安；震颤；颤抖；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
惊恐;惶恐;焦虑;不安
Trepidation is fear or anxiety about something that you are going to do or experience.
例：It was with some trepidation that I viewed the prospect of cycling across Uganda.我带着些许惶恐考虑了一下骑自行车周游乌干达的可能性。

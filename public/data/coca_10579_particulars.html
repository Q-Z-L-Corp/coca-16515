N-PLURAL
细节；详情
The particulars of something or someone are facts or details about them which are written down and kept as a record.
例：You will find all the particulars in Chapter 9...你在第9章会找到所有细节。
例：The nurses at the admission desk asked her for particulars.住院接待处的护士询问了她的详细情况。

ADJ
每小时一次的;每小时的
An hourly event happens once every hour.
例：He flipped on the radio to get the hourly news broadcast.他打开收音机收听整点新闻。

ADJ
（薪酬）按钟点计算的
Your hourly earnings are the money that you earn in one hour.
例：They have little prospect of finding new jobs with the same hourly pay.他们不太可能找到时薪和原来一样的新工作。

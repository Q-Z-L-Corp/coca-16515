appropriation 
英[əˌprəʊpriˈeɪʃn] 美[əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃn]
 
n.挪用；拨款；批准支出；
 "

N-COUNT
拨款
An appropriation is an amount of money that a government or organization reserves for a particular purpose.
例：The government raised defence appropriations by 12 per cent.政府将国防拨款提高了12%。

N-UNCOUNT
私占；侵吞；盗用；挪用
Appropriation of something that belongs to someone else is the act of taking it, usually without having the right to do so.
例：Other charges include fraud and illegal appropriation of land.其他指控包括欺诈罪和非法侵占土地罪。

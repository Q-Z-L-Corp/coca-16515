ADJ
一定会赢的;稳操胜券的;万无一失的
A win-win situation is one where you are certain to do well or be successful.
例：It is surprising that it has taken people so long to take advantage of what is a win-win opportunity.真奇怪，人们居然用了这么长时间才把握住一个万无一失的机会。

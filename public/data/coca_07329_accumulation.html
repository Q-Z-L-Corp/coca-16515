accumulation 
英[əˌkju:mjə'leɪʃn] 美[əˌkjumjəˈleʃən]
 
n.积累；堆积物；累积量；
 "

N-COUNT
堆积物；聚积物
An accumulation of something is a large number of things which have been collected together or acquired over a period of time.
例：...an accumulation of experience and knowledge.经验和知识的积累
例：...accumulations of dirt.积尘

N-UNCOUNT
堆积;积累;积聚
Accumulation is the collecting together of things over a period of time.
例：...the accumulation of capital and the distribution of income...资本的积累和收入的分配
例：The rate of accumulation decreases with time.积累的速度随着时间变慢。

between 
英[bɪˈtwi:n] 美[bɪˈtwin]
 
prep.在…之间；私下，暗中；在…中任择其一；来往于…之间；adv.当中，中间；
 "

PREP
在…之间；处于…中间
If something is between two things or is in between them, it has one of the things on one side of it and the other thing on the other side.
例：She left the table to stand between the two men...她离开桌子，站在了那两个男人之间。
例：Charlie crossed between the traffic to the far side of the street.查利从车流中间穿过，到了街对面。

PREP
在（两地）之间（穿梭）
If people or things travel between two places, they travel regularly from one place to the other and back again.
例：I spent a lot of time in the early Eighties travelling between London and Bradford.八十年代初，我有很多时间穿梭于伦敦和布拉德福德之间。

PREP
在（两个人、群体或事物）之间
A relationship, discussion, or difference between two people, groups, or things is one that involves them both or relates to them both.
例：I think the relationship between patients and doctors has got a lot less personal...我认为医患关系变得越来越缺乏人情味。
例：There have been intensive discussions between the two governments in recent days...近几天，双方政府一直在进行紧张的磋商。

PREP
挡在…之间
If something stands between you and what you want, it prevents you from having it.
例：His sense of duty often stood between him and the enjoyment of life.他的责任感经常让他无法好好享受生活。

PREP
（数量、年龄等）介于…之间
If something is between two amounts or ages, it is greater or older than the first one and smaller or younger than the second one.
例：Increase the amount of time you spend exercising by walking between 15 and 20 minutes...步行15至20分钟，增加一下你的运动时间。
例：Amsterdam is fun — a third of its population is aged between 18 and 30.阿姆斯特丹非常有趣——它有1/3的人口年龄介于18到30岁之间。

PREP
（时间上）在…之间，介于…之间
If something happens between or in between two times or events, it happens after the first time or event and before the second one.
例：The canal was built between 1793 and 1797...运河开凿于1793到1797年间。
例：Berlin was well known for its good living in between the two world wars.在两次世界大战之间，柏林生活的优裕是众所周知的。

PREP
在…之间（作出选择）
If you must choose between two or more things, you must choose just one of them.
例：Students will be able to choose between English, French and Russian as their first foreign language.学生可以在英语、法语和俄语中间选择一门作为第一外语。

PREP
总共；总计
If people or places have a particular amount of something between them, this is the total amount that they have.
例：The three sites employ 12,500 people between them...这三处共雇用了12,500名员工。
例：Between them, they train over fifty horses in Lambourn.他们在兰伯恩总共驯了五十多匹马。

PREP
由…分担；由…分享
When something is divided or shared between people, they each have a share of it.
例：His company was bought out by Hogg Robinson for £3.5m, divided between five partners...他的公司被霍格·鲁宾逊公司以350万英镑的价格收购，分给了五位合伙人。
例：There is only one bathroom shared between eight bedrooms.八间卧室共用一间浴室。

PHRASE
你我之间（的秘密）；只限于你我之间；我俩私下说说
When you introduce a statement by saying 'between you and me' or 'between ourselves', you are indicating that you do not want anyone else to know what you are saying.
例：Between you and me, though, it's been awful for business...不过你我之间说说，这事儿办得真不怎么样。
例：Between ourselves, I know he wants to marry her.我们私下说说，我知道他想娶她。

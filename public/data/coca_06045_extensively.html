extensively 
英[ɪk'stensɪvlɪ] 美[ɪkˈstɛnsɪvlɪ]
 
adv.广大地，广泛地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
广大的;广阔的
Something that is extensive covers or includes a large physical area.
例：...an extensive tour of Latin America...在拉丁美洲广泛游历
例：When built, the palace and its grounds were more extensive than the city itself.建成时，宫殿及其庭园比城市本身面积还要大。

ADJ-GRADED
广泛的;全面的;包罗万象的
Something that is extensive covers a wide range of details, ideas, or items.
例：Developments in South Africa receive extensive coverage in The Sunday Telegraph...《星期日电讯报》全面报道了南非的发展。
例：...the extensive research into public attitudes to science...针对公众科学观的广泛调查

ADJ-GRADED
巨大的；大量的
If something is extensive, it is very great.
例：The blast caused extensive damage, shattering the ground-floor windows...爆炸造成了巨大的破坏，震碎了一楼的窗户。
例：The security forces have extensive powers of search and arrest...安全部队拥有搜查和逮捕的至高权力。

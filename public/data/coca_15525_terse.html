terse 
英[tɜ:s] 美[tɜrs]
 
adj.（说话、文笔等）精练的，简洁的；扼要的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（声明、评论）简短的,短促的,无礼的
A terse statement or comment is brief and unfriendly.
例：He issued a terse statement, saying he is discussing his future with colleagues before announcing his decision on Monday...他发表了简短声明，称他正在和同事讨论他的未来，并将于星期一宣布他的决定。
例：His tone was terse as he asked the question.他提问时语气生硬。

cycling 
英[ˈsaɪklɪŋ] 美['saɪklɪŋ]
 
n.骑脚踏车兜风，骑脚踏车消遣；
 "

VERB
骑(自行车)
If you cycle, you ride a bicycle.
例：He cycled to Ingwold...他骑车去英格伍德。
例：Britain could save £4.6 billion a year in road transport costs if more people cycled...如更多的人骑自行车，英国一年能在道路交通上节省46亿英镑的开销。

N-COUNT
自行车
A cycle is a bicycle.
例：...an eight-mile cycle ride.骑自行车8英里

N-COUNT
摩托车
A cycle is a motorcycle.

N-COUNT
循环；周期
A cycle is a series of events or processes that is repeated again and again, always in the same order.
例：...the life cycle of the plant...植物的生命周期
例：The figures marked the final low point of the present economic cycle...这些数据显示了目前经济周期的最低点。

N-COUNT
(运转的)一周，一圈;一个工作循环
A cycle is a single complete series of movements in an electrical, electronic, or mechanical process.
例：...10 cycles per second.每秒10转

N-COUNT
组歌;组诗
A cycle is a series of songs or poems that are intended to be performed or read one after the other.
例：...Wagner's Ring cycle.瓦格纳的《指环》组歌

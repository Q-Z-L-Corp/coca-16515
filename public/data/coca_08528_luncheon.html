luncheon 
英[ˈlʌntʃən] 美[ˈlʌntʃən]
 
n.午餐；午餐会；午宴；两餐之间吃的一点食物；
 "

N-COUNT
午宴
A luncheon is a formal lunch, for example to celebrate an important event or to raise money for charity.
例：Earlier this month, a luncheon for former UN staff was held in Vienna.本月早些时候在维也纳设午宴招待了前联合国工作人员。

N-VAR
午餐；午饭
Luncheon is the meal that you eat in the middle of the day. It is usually called lunch.
例：Promptly at one, luncheon was served...午饭一到一点钟就上桌了。
例：I have a luncheon engagement.我午饭有约。

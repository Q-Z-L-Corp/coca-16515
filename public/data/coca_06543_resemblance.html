N-VAR
相似之处;类似之处
If there is a resemblance between two people or things, they are similar to each other.
例：There was a remarkable resemblance between him and Pete...他和皮特之间有着惊人的相似之处。
例：Our tour prices bore little resemblance to those in the holiday brochures.我们的旅游报价和那些度假手册里的价格相去甚远。

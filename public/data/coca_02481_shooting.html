shooting 
英[ˈʃu:tɪŋ] 美[ˈʃutɪŋ]
 
n.射击，发出；投篮；摄影；射杀，枪杀；v.shoot的现在分词；
 "

N-COUNT
枪击事件
A shooting is an occasion when someone is killed or injured by being shot with a gun.
例：Two more bodies were found nearby after the shooting...枪击事件后附近又发现了两具尸体。
例：A drug-related gang war led to a series of shootings in the city.一起与毒品有关的帮派火并引发了该市一连串的枪击事件。

N-UNCOUNT
狩猎;打猎
Shooting is hunting animals with a gun as a leisure activity.
例：Grouse shooting begins in August.松鸡狩猎开始于 8 月。

N-UNCOUNT
(电影的)拍摄，摄制
The shooting of a film is the act of filming it.
例：Ingrid was busy learning her lines for the next day's shooting...英格丽德正忙着背诵第二天戏份的台词。
例：The shooting of The Maltese Falcon proceeded without a hitch.《马耳他之鹰》的拍摄进展顺利。

awakening 
英[əˈweɪkənɪŋ] 美[ə'weɪkənɪŋ]
 
adj.觉醒中的；唤醒的；觉悟的；（兴趣等）正在产生的；n.觉醒；被激起；意识；觉悟；v.（使）醒(awaken的现在分词)；（使）意识到；
 "

N-COUNT
觉悟；觉醒；醒悟
The awakening of a feeling or realization is the start of it.
例：...the awakening of national consciousness in people.民众中国民意识的觉醒
例：...a young woman's sexual awakening.少女的性萌动

PHRASE
（对不愉快之事的）猛然觉醒
If you have a rude awakening, you are suddenly made aware of an unpleasant fact.
例：It was a rude awakening to learn after I left home that I wasn't so special anymore.离家后我才猛然意识到自己不再有什么特别。

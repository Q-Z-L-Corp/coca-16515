intersperse 
英[ˌɪntəˈspɜ:s] 美[ˌɪntərˈspɜrs]
 
vt.散布，散置；点缀；
 "

VERB
散布;散置;点缀
If you intersperse one group of things with another or among another, you put or include the second things between or among the first things.
例：Originally the intention was to intersperse the historical scenes with modern ones.最初是想用现代景观点缀历史景观。
例：...skillfully interspersing jokes and gossipy anecdotes among his instructions.他的讲课中巧妙穿插的笑话和趣闻闲谈

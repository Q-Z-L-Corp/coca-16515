N-COUNT
指示物;指标
An indicator is a measurement or value which gives you an idea of what something is like.
例：...vital economic indicators, such as inflation, growth and the trade gap...重要的经济指标,例如通货膨胀、增长与贸易逆差
例：The number of wells is a fair indicator of the demand for water.水井的数量能较准确地反映水需求量的大小。

ADJ
魔鬼（似）的;恶魔（般）的;邪恶的
Demonic means coming from or belonging to a demon or being like a demon.
例：...demonic forces.邪恶力量
例：...a demonic grin.狞笑

ADJ
(精力、决心、能力等)超乎常人的
If someone has demonic energy, drive, or abilities, they are more energetic, determined, or clever than most people.
例：...a demonic drive to succeed.想要成功的决心超乎常人

lumber 
英[ˈlʌmbə(r)] 美[ˈlʌmbɚ]
 
n.木材；隆隆声；废物；vt.& vi.砍伐；笨重地行进；胡乱堆积；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
木材；方材
Lumber consists of trees and large pieces of wood that have been roughly cut up.
例：It was made of soft lumber, spruce by the look of it...其制作材料是软木，看起来像是云杉。
例：He was going to have to purchase all his lumber at full retail price.他将不得不把所有木材按零售价分文不少地买下来。

VERB
（缓慢而笨拙地）挪动，移动
If someone or something lumbers from one place to another, they move there very slowly and clumsily.
例：He turned and lumbered back to his chair...他转过身来，蹒跚着走回到椅子边坐下。
例：The truck lumbered across the parking lot toward the road...卡车隆隆地驶过停车场准备上路。

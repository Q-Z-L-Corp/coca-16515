discern 
英[dɪˈsɜ:n] 美[dɪˈsɜrn]
 
vt.看出；理解，了解；识别，辨别；vi.辨明，分清；
 "

VERB
觉察出;知道;了解;辨明
If you can discern something, you are aware of it and know what it is.
例：You need a long series of data to be able to discern such a trend...你需要一长串的数据才能看出这一趋势。
例：It was hard to discern why this was happening.很难弄清楚这为什么会发生。

VERB
依稀看出;分辨出
If you can discern something, you can just see it, but not clearly.
例：Below the bridge we could just discern a narrow, weedy ditch.我们仅能依稀辨出桥下一条杂草丛生的窄沟。

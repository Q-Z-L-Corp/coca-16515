 "

NUM
百万
A million or one million is the number 1,000,000.
例：Up to five million people a year visit the county...每年参观这个县的人多达500万。
例：Profits for 1999 topped £100 million.1999年的利润超过了1亿英镑。

QUANT-PLURAL
数百万的；大量的；数不清的;不计其数的
If you talk about millions of people or things, you mean that there is a very large number of them but you do not know or do not want to say exactly how many.
例：The programme was viewed on television in millions of homes.无数家庭通过电视收看了这个节目。

V-ERG
（使）两极化；（使）分化；（使）对立
If something polarizes people or if something polarizes, two separate groups are formed with opposite opinions or positions.
例：Missile deployment did much to further polarize opinion in Britain...在英国，导弹部署极大地导致了意见进一步分化。
例：As the car rental industry polarizes, business will go to the bigger companies...随着汽车租赁业的两极分化，生意将流向较大的公司。

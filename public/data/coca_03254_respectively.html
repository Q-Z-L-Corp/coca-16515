ADV
分别地;各自地
Respectively means in the same order as the items that you have just mentioned.
例：Their sons, Ben and Jonathan, were three and six respectively...他们的儿子，本和乔纳森，分别是3岁和6岁。
例：They finished first and second respectively.他们分获一二名。

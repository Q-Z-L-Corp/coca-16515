northwest 
英['nɔ:θ'west] 美[nɔrθˈwɛst, nɔrˈwɛst]
 
n.西北，西北方向；西北部；adj.在西北的；西北部的；向西北的；（风、光线）来自西北方的；adv.自西北地；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
西北;西北方
The north-west is the direction which is halfway between north and west.
例：...Ushant, five miles out to the north-west.西北方向5英里外的阿申特岛

N-SING
西北部;西北部地区
The north-west of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the north-west.
例：Labour took its pre-election campaign to the North-West.工党在西北部地区展开了其选举前的竞选活动。
例：...the extreme north-west of South America.南美洲的最西北部

ADV
向西北
If you go north-west, you travel towards the north-west.
例：Take the narrow lane going north-west parallel with the railway line.走和铁路线平行的那条西北向的窄巷。

ADV
在西北
Something that is north-west of a place is positioned to the north-west of it.
例：This was situated to the north-west of the town, a short walk from the railway station.这里地处镇子的西北方，离火车站仅有一小段步行距离。

ADJ-GRADED
在西北的;西北部的
The north-west part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the north-west.
例：...the North-West Regional Health Authority.西北地区卫生局
例：...the north-west coast of the United States.美国的西北海岸

ADJ
(风)从西北方吹来的;西北风的
A north-west wind is a wind that blows from the north-west.
例：A brisk north-west wind swept across the region.寒冷干燥的西北风横扫了这个地区。

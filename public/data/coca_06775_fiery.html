fiery 
英[ˈfaɪəri] 美[ˈfaɪri]
 
adj.激烈的；火似的；火热的；易燃烧的；
 "

ADJ
熊熊燃烧的;含火的
If you describe something as fiery, you mean that it is burning strongly or contains fire.
例：A helicopter crashed in a fiery explosion in Vallejo.一架直升机在瓦列霍爆炸起火并坠毁。

ADJ-GRADED
(颜色)火一般的，如火的
You can use fiery for emphasis when you are referring to bright colours such as red or orange.
例：A large terracotta pot planted with Busy Lizzie provides a fiery bright red display.一个栽种了凤仙花的大赤陶罐里开出了明艳如火的花朵。

ADJ-GRADED
(食物或饮料)辛辣的，刺激的
If you describe food or drink as fiery, you mean that it has a very strong hot or spicy taste.
例：A fiery combination of chicken, chillies and rice.把鸡肉、辣椒和米饭和在一起、口味辛辣的搭配

ADJ-GRADED
(感情、尤指愤怒)激烈的，暴烈的
If you describe someone as fiery, you mean that they express very strong emotions, especially anger, in their behaviour or speech.
例：She was a fiery, brilliant and unyielding intellectual and politician...她是个充满激情、才华卓越、毫不妥协的知识分子和政治家。
例：She had a fiery temper and liked to get her own way.她脾气火爆，喜欢我行我素。

subway

 "

N-COUNT
地铁
A subway is an underground railway.
例：...the Bay Area Rapid Transit subway system...旧金山海湾地区地铁捷运系统
例：I don't ride the subway late at night.我深夜不坐地铁。

N-COUNT
(穿越马路、铁路的)地下通道，地下人行道
A subway is a passage underneath a busy road or a railway track for people to walk through.
例：The majority of us feel worried if we walk through a subway.大多数人在走地下通道时都感到害怕。

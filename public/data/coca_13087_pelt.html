pelt 
英[pelt] 美[pɛlt]
 
vt.（连续地）投掷；连续攻击；剥皮；vi.（连续地）投掷；（雨等）急降；开火；<口>迅猛前进；n.毛皮；抨击，打击；投掷；
 "

N-COUNT
（可用来制衣或制毯的）毛皮
The pelt of an animal is its skin, which can be used to make clothing or rugs.
例：...a bed covered with beaver pelts.铺着海狸毛皮的床
例：...rapidly diminishing suppliers of furs and pelts.数量锐减的毛皮供应商

VERB
向…投掷
If you pelt someone with things, you throw things at them.
例：Some of the younger men began to pelt one another with snowballs...一些年轻人开始相互投掷雪球。
例：Crowds started to pelt police cars with stones.人群开始向警车扔石块。

VERB
倾泻；倾盆而下
If the rain is pelting down, or if it is pelting with rain, it is raining very hard.
例：The rain now was pelting down...此刻大雨滂沱。
例：It's pelting with rain...大雨倾盆。

VERB
快速奔跑；飞奔
If you pelt somewhere, you run there very fast.
例：Without thinking, she pelted down the stairs in her nightgown.她想都没想，穿着睡衣就飞奔下了楼。

PHRASE
全速；飞快地
If you do something full pelt or at full pelt, you do it very quickly indeed.
例：Alice leapt from the car and ran full pelt towards the emergency room...艾丽斯跳下车，拼命奔向急诊室。
例：He drove his car through the gates at full pelt.他驾车急速通过大门。

VERB
批评;指责;批判
If you criticize someone or something, you express your disapproval of them by saying what you think is wrong with them.
例：His mother had rarely criticized him or any of her children...他母亲很少指责他或她的任何孩子。
例：The minister criticised the police for failing to come up with any leads...部长批评警方没能找到任何线索。

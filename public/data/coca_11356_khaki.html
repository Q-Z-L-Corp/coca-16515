khaki 
英[ˈkɑ:ki] 美[ˈkæki]
 
n.卡其布；土黄色；卡其布军服；卡其布裤子；adj.卡其色的；土黄色的；卡其布做的；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（尤指用于做军装的）卡其布
Khaki is a strong material of a greenish brown colour, used especially to make uniforms for soldiers.
例：On each side of me was a figure in khaki.我两边各有一个穿卡其布服装的人。

COLOUR
暗绿色的；黄褐色的
Something that is khaki is greenish brown in colour.
例：He was dressed in khaki trousers.他穿着暗绿色的裤子。

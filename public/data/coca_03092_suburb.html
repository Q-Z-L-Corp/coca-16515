suburb 
英[ˈsʌbɜ:b] 美[ˈsʌbɜrb]
 
n.郊区，城郊；边缘；附近，周围；
 "

N-COUNT
城郊;郊区
A suburb of a city or large town is a smaller area which is part of the city or large town but is outside its centre.
例：Anna was born in 1923 in Ardwick, a suburb of Manchester.安娜于1923年出生在曼彻斯特的郊区阿德维克。
例：...the north London suburbs of Harrow, Barnet and Enfield.伦敦北部郊区哈罗区、巴尼特区和恩菲尔德区。

N-PLURAL
城郊住宅区
If you live in the suburbs, you live in an area of houses outside the centre of a large town or city.
例：His family lived in the suburbs.他家住在城郊。
例：...Bombay's suburbs.孟买城郊住宅区

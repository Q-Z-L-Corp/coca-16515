batter 
英[ˈbætə(r)] 美[ˈbætɚ]
 
vt.& vi.连续猛击；捣碎，打烂；磨损；使内倾，使倾斜；n.（铅字的）磨损，毁损；（用鸡蛋、牛奶、面粉等调成的）糊状物；击球员，击球手；墙面的倾斜；
 "

VERB
殴打；痛打；毒打
If someone is battered, they are regularly hit and badly hurt by a member of their family or by their partner.
例：...evidence that the child was being battered.这个孩子被家人毒打的证据
例：...boys who witness fathers battering their mothers.目睹父亲殴打母亲的男孩们

VERB
（用拳头或重物）多次重击，连续猛打（某人）
To batter someone means to hit them many times, using fists or a heavy object.
例：He battered her around the head...他连续重击她的头部。
例：A karate expert battered a man to death...一位空手道高手将一名男子毒打致死。

VERB
（风、雨或暴风雪等）肆虐，严重破坏
If a place is battered by wind, rain, or storms, it is seriously damaged or affected by very bad weather.
例：The country has been battered by winds of between fifty and seventy miles an hour.该国遭受到时速高达50至70英里的大风袭击，破坏严重。
例：...a storm that's been battering the Northeast coastline.在东北海岸肆虐的暴风雨

VERB
（用拳头或重物）多次重击,连续猛打（某物）
If you batter something, you hit it many times, using your fists or a heavy object.
例：They were battering the door, they were breaking in...他们正在使劲捶门，就要闯进来了。
例：Batter the steaks flat.把牛排捶扁。

N-VAR
（面粉、鸡蛋和牛奶和成的）糊，面糊
Batter is a mixture of flour, eggs, and milk that is used in cooking.
例：...pancake batter.做煎饼的面糊
例：...fish in batter.裹着面糊的鱼

N-COUNT
（棒球和垒球等运动中的）击球员，击球手
In sports such as baseball and softball, a batter is a person who hits the ball with a wooden bat.
例：...batters and pitchers.击球手和投球手们

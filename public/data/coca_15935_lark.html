lark 
英[lɑ:k] 美[lɑrk]
 
n.云雀，百灵鸟；<口>玩笑，嬉戏；vi.捉云雀；嬉戏；
 "

N-COUNT
百灵鸟；云雀
A lark is a small brown bird which makes a pleasant sound.

N-COUNT
（可能带有淘气或危险性质的）玩乐，玩耍，嬉戏
If you say that doing something is a lark, you mean that it is fun, although perhaps naughty or dangerous.
例：The children thought it was a great lark...孩子们以为这很好玩。
例：The chances are that the thief will be under 21 and doing it for a lark...偷东西的很可能是个不到21岁的年轻人，干这事儿只是贪玩。

N-COUNT
（用于幽默地表示好笑、愚蠢或多余）演戏这玩意儿/耍笔杆子这一行
You use lark in expressions such as this acting lark and the writing lark to indicate humorously that you think an activity or job is amusing, foolish, or unnecessary.
例：He got the part, and eventually decided the acting lark wasn't half bad.他赢得了那个角色，一番考虑后觉得演戏这玩意儿感觉也还不错。

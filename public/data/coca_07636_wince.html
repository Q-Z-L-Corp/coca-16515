wince 
英[wɪns] 美[wɪns]
 
vi.赶紧避开；畏缩，退避；n.（由于疼痛，吃惊等）畏缩，退缩；[印]（洗染布时用的）绞车，六角绞盘；
 "

VERB
(因疼痛或因看到、听到或记起不快之事而)皱起脸，皱眉蹙额
If you wince, the muscles of your face tighten suddenly because you have felt a pain or because you have just seen, heard, or remembered something unpleasant.
例：Every time he put any weight on his left leg he winced in pain...只要左腿一受力，他就会疼得龇牙咧嘴。
例：He winced at the thought of dining with Camilla...一想到要跟卡米拉一起吃饭，他皱起了眉头。

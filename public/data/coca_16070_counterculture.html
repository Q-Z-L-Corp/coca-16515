N-VAR
反主流文化;反正统文化
Counter-culture is a set of values, ideas, and ways of behaving that are completely different from those of the rest of society.
例：...a history of British counter-culture.英国反主流文化的历史

N-COUNT
反主流文化群体
A counter-culture is a group in society whose values, ideas, and ways of behaving are completely different from those of the rest of society.
例：Not everyone joined the counterculture, not everyone demonstrated, dropped out, took drugs, or dodged the draft.不是所有人都加入反主流文化群体，不是所有人都示威游行，退学，吸毒，或逃避服兵役。

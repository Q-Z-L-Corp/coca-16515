ADJ
不存在的
If you say that something is non-existent, you mean that it does not exist when you feel that it should.
例：Hygiene was non-existent: no running water, no bathroom...卫生根本谈不上：没有自来水、没有卫生间。
例：You'll take everything you're offered yet your own generosity is virtually non-existent.你倒是来者不拒，照单全收，可你自己实际上却一毛不拔。

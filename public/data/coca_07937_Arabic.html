N-UNCOUNT
阿拉伯语
Arabic is a language that is spoken in the Middle East and in parts of North Africa.

ADJ
阿拉伯语的；阿拉伯文字的；阿拉伯文化的
Something that is Arabic belongs or relates to the language, writing, or culture of the Arabs.
例：...a large tapestry with swirling Arabic script.绘有螺旋形阿拉伯字母的大幅花毯
例：...the development of modern Arabic literature.现代阿拉伯文学的发展

ADJ
（数字）阿拉伯的
An Arabic numeral is one of the written figures such as 1, 2, 3, or 4.
例：The clock is available with either Roman or Arabic numerals.这款钟有带罗马数字的，也有带阿拉伯数字的。

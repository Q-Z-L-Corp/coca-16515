ADJ
有关的;有联系的
If one thing is connected with another, there is a link or relationship between them.
例：Have you ever had any skin problems connected with exposure to the sun?...你曾因日晒出现过皮肤问题吗？
例：The dispute is not directly connected to the negotiations...这一争端与谈判没有直接联系。

demonstration 
英[ˌdemənˈstreɪʃn] 美[ˌdɛmənˈstreʃən]
 
n.游行示威；示范；证明；表露；
 "

N-COUNT
示威;示威游行（或集会）
A demonstration is a march or gathering which people take part in to show their opposition to something or their support for something.
例：Riot police broke up a demonstration by students.防暴警察驱散了示威的学生。
例：...mass demonstrations.群众性示威

N-COUNT
示范;演示
A demonstration of something is a talk by someone who shows you how to do it or how it works.
例：...a cookery demonstration.烹饪示范
例：...demonstrations of new products.新产品的演示

N-COUNT
证明;论证;表明;说明
A demonstration of a fact or situation is a clear proof of it.
例：This is a clear demonstration of how technology has changed...这清楚地表明了技术已有了怎样的进步。
例：This was a very practical demonstration of why the Army trained people to be disciplined.这很实际地说明了为什么军队要将士兵训练得遵规守纪。

N-COUNT
(特质的)显示;(情感的)表露
A demonstration of a quality or feeling is an expression of it.
例：There's been no public demonstration of opposition to the President.没有人对总统公开表示反对。
例：...physical demonstrations of affection.身体上的亲昵

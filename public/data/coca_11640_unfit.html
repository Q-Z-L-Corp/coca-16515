unfit 
英[ʌnˈfɪt] 美[ʌnˈfɪt]
 
adj.不适宜的，不适当的；体格不佳的，不健康的；vt.使不相宜，使不合格；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（人因缺乏锻炼而）不健康的，不强健的
If you are unfit, your body is not in good condition because you have not been taking regular exercise.
例：Many children are so unfit they are unable to do even basic exercises.许多孩子非常不健康，连基本的健身锻炼都无法完成。

ADJ-GRADED
（人因伤病而）不适宜的
If someone is unfit for something, he or she is unable to do it because of injury or illness.
例：He had a third examination and was declared unfit for duty...他接受了第三次检查，被告知不宜上班。
例：Mr Abel's doctor has said he is unfit to travel.埃布尔先生的医生说他不宜出行。

ADJ-GRADED
不合适的；不合格的；不胜任的
If you say that someone or something is unfit for a particular purpose or job, you are criticizing them because they are not good enough for that purpose or job.
例：Existing houses are becoming totally unfit for human habitation...现有的房子正变得完全不适合住人。
例：They were utterly unfit to govern America...他们完全没有能力治理美国。

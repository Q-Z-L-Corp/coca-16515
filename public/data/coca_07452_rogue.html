rogue 
英[rəʊg] 美[roʊg]
 
n.流氓，无赖；调皮捣蛋的人人；离群的野兽；vt.欺骗；[农]去劣；vi.[农]淘汰劣种；流浪；
 "

N-COUNT
无赖;流氓;恶棍
A rogue is a man who behaves in a dishonest or criminal way.
例：Mr Ward wasn't a rogue at all.沃德先生根本不是恶棍。

N-COUNT
捣蛋鬼;小淘气;小坏蛋
If a man behaves in a way that you do not approve of but you still like him, you can refer to him as a rogue .
例：...Falstaff, the loveable rogue.福斯塔夫，那个可爱的捣蛋鬼

ADJ
(人或事物)不寻常的，破坏性的
A rogue element is someone or something that behaves differently from others of its kind, often causing damage.
例：Computer systems throughout the country are being affected by a series of mysterious rogue programs, known as viruses...全国的计算机系统都感染了一系列诡异的流氓程序，即病毒。
例：The rogue male is not a twentieth-century phenomenon.男性不安分守己并非20世纪特有的现象。

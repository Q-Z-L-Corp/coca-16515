VERB
使正式就任;赋予头衔;给予职位
If someone is inducted into a particular job, rank, or position, they are given the job, rank, or position in a formal ceremony.
例：Six new members have been inducted into the Provincial Cabinet...6 名新成员已经正式加入省内阁班子。
例：She inducts Nina into the cult.她诱导尼娜加入这一邪教。

VERB
征召…入伍
If someone is inducted into the army, they are officially made to join the army.
例：In December he was inducted into the army.12 月他应征入伍了。

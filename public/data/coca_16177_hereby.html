ADV
（正式场合用语）特此，兹，谨
You use hereby when officially or formally saying what you are doing.
例：I hereby sentence you for life after all the charges against you have been proven true...鉴于对你的所有指控都证明属实，我特此宣判你无期徒刑。
例：You are hereby appointed Sub-Lieutenant RNVR of HMS Tartar...你被任命为皇家海军舰艇“鞑靼人”号的皇家海军志愿后备队中尉。

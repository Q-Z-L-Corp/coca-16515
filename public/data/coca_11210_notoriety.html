N-UNCOUNT
声名狼藉;臭名昭著
To achieve notoriety means to become well-known for something bad.
例：He achieved notoriety as chief counsel to President Nixon in the Watergate break-in.他因在水门事件中担任尼克松总统的首席法律顾问而声名狼藉。

ADV
略微;稍微
Slightly means to some degree but not to a very large degree.
例：His family then moved to a slightly larger house...然后他全家搬到了一个稍微大一点的房子里。
例：They will be slightly more expensive but they last a lot longer...它们的价钱要贵一些，可是更加耐用。

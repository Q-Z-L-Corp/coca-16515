summon 
英[ˈsʌmən] 美[ˈsʌmən]
 
vt.传唤，召唤；鼓起（勇气）；传讯（出庭）；[军]劲降，招降；
 "

VERB
召唤;传唤;召见
If you summon someone, you order them to come to you.
例：Howe summoned a doctor and hurried over...豪叫了医生，然后匆忙赶了过来。
例：Suddenly we were summoned to the interview room...突然我们被叫进了面试的房间。

VERB
鼓起(勇气);振作;使出(力气)
If you summon a quality, you make a great effort to have it. For example, if you summon the courage or strength to do something, you make a great effort to be brave or strong, so that you will be able to do it.
例：It took her a full month to summon the courage to tell her mother.她用了整整一个月才鼓足勇气告诉了妈妈。

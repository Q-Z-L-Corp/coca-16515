ORD
第八的
The eighth item in a series is the one that you count as number eight.
例：...the eighth prime minister of India.印度第八任总理

FRACTION
八分之一
An eighth is one of eight equal parts of something.
例：The Kuban produces an eighth of Russia's grain, meat and milk.库班地区出产俄罗斯1/8的粮食、肉类和牛奶。

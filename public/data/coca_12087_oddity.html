oddity 
英[ˈɒdəti] 美[ˈɑdəti]
 
n.奇特，奇异；怪癖；怪人，怪事；
 "

N-COUNT
怪人；怪事
An oddity is someone or something that is very strange.
例：Losing my hair made me feel an oddity...脱发使我觉得自己是个怪人。
例：Carlson noticed another oddity; his plant had bloomed twice.卡尔森注意到另外一件怪事：他的植物开了两次花。

N-COUNT
奇怪；古怪；奇特
The oddity of something is the fact that it is very strange.
例：...the oddities of the Welsh legal system.威尔士法律体系的奇特之处

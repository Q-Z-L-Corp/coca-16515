N-COUNT
要人;贵宾
A VIP is someone who is given better treatment than ordinary people because they are famous, influential, or important. VIP is an abbreviation for (缩略=) 'very important person'.
例：...such VIPs as Prince Charles and Richard Nixon...查尔斯王子和理查德·尼克松这样的贵宾
例：She waited in the VIP lounge.她在贵宾休息室里等候着。

joy 
英[dʒɔɪ] 美[dʒɔɪ]
 
n.喜悦，快乐，高兴；令人高兴的事（或人）；成功；好运；vi.因…而高兴；vt.高兴，使快乐；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
欢欣；愉快；喜悦
Joy is a feeling of great happiness.
例：Salter shouted with joy.索尔特欣喜地叫喊着。
例：...tears of joy.喜悦的泪水

N-COUNT
令人高兴的事（或人）；乐事；乐趣
A joy is something or someone that makes you feel happy or gives you great pleasure.
例：One can never learn all there is to know about cooking, and that is one of the joys of being a chef...钻研烹饪之道是永无止境的，这也是当厨师的乐趣之一。
例：It was a joy to see her looking so well.看到她气色这么好真叫人高兴。

N-UNCOUNT
成功；好运
If you get no joy, you do not have success or luck in achieving what you are trying to do.
例：They expect no joy from the vote itself...他们对投票本身不抱任何希望。
例：If you don't get any joy, get in touch with your local councillor.如果你没得到任何结果，请和本地议员联系。

PHRASE
欢欣鼓舞；欣喜若狂
If you say that someone is jumping for joy, you mean that they are very pleased or happy about something.
例：He jumped for joy on being told the news.得知这个消息，他高兴得手舞足蹈。

fable 
英[ˈfeɪbl] 美[ˈfebəl]
 
n.寓言，童话；传说；无稽之谈；人人谈论的话题；v.讲故事，编寓言；虚构，杜撰；煞有介事地讲；
 "

N-VAR
寓言
A fable is a story which teaches a moral lesson. Fables sometimes have animals as the main characters.
例：...the fable of the tortoise and the hare...龟兔赛跑的寓言
例：Each tale has the timeless quality of fable.每个故事都有其永恒的寓意。

N-VAR
谣传;无稽之谈
You can describe a statement or explanation that is untrue but that many people believe as fable .
例：Is reincarnation fact or fable?转世轮回是确有其事还是无稽之谈？
例：...little-known horticultural facts and fables.有关园艺的鲜为人知的事实和谬传

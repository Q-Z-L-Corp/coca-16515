density 
英[ˈdensəti] 美[ˈdɛnsɪti]
 
n.密度；稠密，浓厚；[物]浓度，比重；愚钝；
 "

N-VAR
密度;稠密;密集
Density is the extent to which something is filled or covered with people or things.
例：...a law which restricts the density of housing...限制房屋密集程度的法规
例：The region has a very high population density.该地区的人口密度很高。

N-VAR
（物质或物体的）密度
In science, the density of a substance or object is the relation of its mass or weight to its volume.
例：Jupiter's moon Io, whose density is 3.5 grams per cubic centimetre, is all rock.密度为每立方厘米 3.5 克的木星卫星木卫一上遍布岩石。

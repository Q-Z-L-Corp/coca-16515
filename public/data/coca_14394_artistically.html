artistically 
英[ɑ:'tɪstɪklɪ] 美[ɑ'tɪstɪklɪ]
 
adv.有艺术地，在艺术上；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有艺术天赋的;有艺术鉴赏力的
Someone who is artistic is good at drawing or painting, or arranging things in a beautiful way.
例：They encourage boys to be sensitive and artistic...他们鼓励培养男孩子的敏感和艺术气质。
例：Mary's got it all so nice — you remember how artistic she always was with colors.玛丽把一切弄得都很漂亮——你还记得她以前总是对色彩很有艺术品位吧。

ADJ
艺术的;艺术家的
Artistic means relating to art or artists.
例：...the campaign for artistic freedom.艺术自由运动
例：...their 1,300 year old artistic traditions.他们1,300年的艺术传统

ADJ-GRADED
精美的；富有艺术性的
An artistic design or arrangement is beautiful.
例：...an artistic arrangement of stone paving.石子路的精美图案

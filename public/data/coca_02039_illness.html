illness 
英[ˈɪlnəs] 美[ˈɪlnɪs]
 
n.疾病；某种具体疾病；不健康；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
疾病;患病(期)
Illness is the fact or experience of being ill.
例：If your child shows any signs of illness, take her to the doctor...如果您的孩子出现任何患病症状,带她去看医生。
例：Mental illness is still a taboo subject.精神疾病仍然是禁忌话题。

N-COUNT
(特指某种)疾病
An illness is a particular disease such as measles or pneumonia.
例：She returned to her family home to recover from an illness.她回家养病来了。

nursery 
英[ˈnɜ:səri] 美[ˈnɜrsəri]
 
n.婴儿室，幼儿园；苗圃；临时托儿所；温床，滋生地；
 "

N-COUNT
托儿所
A nursery is a place where children who are not old enough to go to school are looked after.
例：This nursery will be able to cater for 29 children...这家托儿所能照料29个孩子。
例：Her company ran its own workplace nursery.她的公司开办了内部的托儿所。

N-VAR
幼儿园;幼稚园
Nursery is a school for very young children.
例：An affordable nursery education service is an essential basic amenity.人们能负担得起的幼儿园教育是一项基本的社会福利。
例：...a nursery teacher.幼儿园老师

N-COUNT
育儿室;儿童房
A nursery is a room in a family home in which the young children of the family sleep or play.
例：He has painted murals in his children's nursery.他在孩子们的儿童房墙壁上画上了画。

N-COUNT
苗圃
A nursery is a place where plants are grown in order to be sold.
例：The garden, developed over the past 35 years, includes a nursery.这个有35年历史的花园里有一个苗圃。

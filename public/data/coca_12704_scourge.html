N-COUNT
灾难;祸害
A scourge is something that causes a lot of trouble or suffering to a group of people.
例：...the best chance in 20 years to end the scourge of terrorism...20 年来消灭恐怖主义祸害的最佳机会
例：Drugs are a scourge that is devastating our society.毒品是破坏我们社会的一大祸害。

VERB
使痛苦;危害;折磨
If something scourges a place or group of people, it causes great pain and suffering to people.
例：Economic anarchy scourged the post-war world.经济混乱使得战后的世界陷入水深火热之中。

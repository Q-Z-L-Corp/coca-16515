right 
英[raɪt] 美[raɪt]
 
adv.立刻，马上；向右，右边；恰当地；一直；adj.右方的；正确的；合适的；好的，正常的；n.正确，正当；右边；权利；右手；vt.纠正；扶直，使正；整理；补偿；vi.（船舶等）复正，恢复平稳；
 "

ADJ
对的;正确的;符合事实的
If something is right, it is correct and agrees with the facts.
例：That's absolutely right...那绝对正确。
例：Clocks never told the right time...钟表显示的时间从来都不准确。

ADJ
合适的;恰当的;准确的
If you do something in the right way or in the right place, you do it as or where it should be done or was planned to be done.
例：Walking, done in the right way, is a form of aerobic exercise...走路，如果方法正确的话，也是一种有氧健身运动。
例：They have computerized systems to ensure delivery of the right pizza to the right place...他们已经实现了系统的计算机化，以保证比萨的送货准确无误。

ADJ
(地方)为大众接受的;(人)正派的，体面的
If you say that someone is seen in all the right places or knows all the right people, you mean that they go to places which are socially acceptable or know people who are socially acceptable.
例：He was always to be seen in the right places...他总是会出现在一些体面的地方。
例：Through his father, he had met all the right people.他通过父亲认识了很多正派的人。

ADJ
(说法或想法)正确的，对的
If someone is right about something, they are correct in what they say or think about it.
例：Ron has been right about the result of every General Election but one...罗恩对于历届大选结果都预测对了，只有一次除外。
例：Is that true? Was she right?...那是真的吗？她说得没错吗？

ADJ
(选择、行动、决定等)正确的，最好的，最合适的
If something such as a choice, action, or decision is the right one, it is the best or most suitable one.
例：She'd made the right choice in leaving New York...她离开纽约的决定是正确的。
例：The right decision was made, but probably for the wrong reasons...作出的决定是正确的，但理由可能是错误的。

ADJ
正常的;没有问题的;令人满意的
If something is not right, there is something unsatisfactory about the situation or thing that you are talking about.
例：Ratatouille doesn't taste right with any other oil...普罗旺斯杂烩用其他任何油炒出来的味道都不正宗。
例：The name Sue Anne never seemed quite right to Molly...莫莉总觉得休·安妮这个名字不太好听。

ADJ
对的;正当的;有理的;合乎道德的
If you think that someone was right to do something, you think that there were good moral reasons why they did it.
例：You were right to do what you did, under the circumstances...在这种情况下，你做的是对的。
例：The president was absolutely right in ordering the bombing raid.总统下令发动空袭是绝对合乎道义的。

ADJ
(活动或行动)符合道德的，正当的
Right is used to refer to activities or actions that are considered to be morally good and acceptable.
例：It's not right, leaving her like this...就这样离开她是不对的。
例：Fox hunting is popular among some people in this country. It doesn't make it right though...这个国家有些人喜欢猎狐，但是这并不能使其变得名正言顺。

VERB
扭转;矫正;(使)恢复正常
If you right something or if it rights itself, it returns to its normal or correct state, after being in an undesirable state.
例：They recognise the urgency of righting the economy...他们认识到了复苏经济的紧迫性。
例：Your eyesight rights itself very quickly.你的视力恢复得非常快。

VERB
纠正;改正;弥补
If you right a wrong, you do something to make up for a mistake or something bad that you did in the past.
例：We've made progress in righting the wrongs of the past...我们在纠正过去的错误方面取得了进展。
例：Having spent 25 years righting his own mistakes, he is anxious that children should not waste opportunities.他花了25年的时间弥补自己的过失，因此他非常渴望孩子们不要错失良机。

VERB
把…扶正;把…摆正;(使)直立
If you right something that has fallen or rolled over, or if it rights itself, it returns to its normal upright position.
例：He righted the yacht and continued the race...他调正了快艇，继续比赛。
例：The helicopter turned at an awful angle before righting itself.直升机在恢复平稳之前倾斜得很厉害。

ADJ
(材料)正面的
The right side of a material is the side that is intended to be seen and that faces outwards when it is made into something.

PHRASE
进展顺利;发展正常
If you say that things are going right, you mean that your life or a situation is developing as you intended or expected and you are pleased with it.
例：I can't think of anything in my life that's going right...我想不起我的生活中有任何一帆风顺的事情。
例：I was pleased with my performance on Saturday — everything went right.我对自己星期六的表现挺满意——一切顺利。

PHRASE
(道德上或法律上)有理的，合理的，正确的，说得通的
If someone has behaved in a way which is morally or legally right, you can say that they are in the right. You usually use this expression when the person is involved in an argument or dispute.
例：She wasn't entirely in the right...她并非完全占理。
例：Legally, the local tax office is in the right.从法律的角度来说，当地税务局的做法是无可厚非的。

PHRASE
纠正;更正
If you put something right, you correct something that was wrong or that was causing problems.
例：We've discovered what's gone wrong and are going to put it right.我们已经发现了存在的问题，准备加以纠正。

PHRASE
白马王子;梦中情人;理想的伴侣
You can use Mr Right ,Miss Right, or Ms Right to talk about the kind of person that you imagine you will marry or spend the rest of your life with.
例：She confesses to having trouble finding Mr Right.她承认找不到自己的白马王子。
例：...bachelors searching for Ms Right.寻找真命天女的单身汉们

abound 
英[əˈbaʊnd] 美[əˈbaʊnd]
 
vi.丰富，盛产；非常多，大量存在；充满；
 "

VERB
大量存在;盛产;充足
If things abound, or if a place abounds with things, there are very large numbers of them.
例：Stories abound about when he was in charge...关于他在任时的传闻多不胜数。
例：Venice abounds in famous hotels...威尼斯有许多知名的酒店。

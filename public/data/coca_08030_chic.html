chic 
英[ʃi:k] 美[ʃik]
 
adj.<法>漂亮的，时髦的，潇洒的；别致的；n.高雅，雅致；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
时髦的;别致的
Something or someone that is chic is fashionable and sophisticated.
例：Her gown was very French and very chic.她的礼服颇具法国风情，非常别致。

N-UNCOUNT
时尚;雅致;别致
Chic is used to refer to a particular style or to the quality of being chic.
例：...French designer chic...法国设计时尚
例：He radiates charm and chic.他魅力四射，风度翩翩。

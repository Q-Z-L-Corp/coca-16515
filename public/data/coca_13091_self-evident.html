ADJ-GRADED
不证自明的;不言而喻的;明摆着的
A fact or situation that is self-evident is so obvious that there is no need for proof or explanation.
例：It is self-evident that we will never have enough resources to meet the demand...不言而喻，我们永远都不会有足够的资源来满足需求。
例：The implications for this country are self-evident.对该国的影响显而易见。

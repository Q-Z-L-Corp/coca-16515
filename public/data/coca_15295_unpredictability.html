unpredictability 
英[ˌʌnprɪˌdɪktə'bɪlətɪ] 美[ˌʌnprɪˌdɪktə'bɪlətɪ]
 
不可预测性，不可预知性；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
无法预言的；不可预测的；难以预料的
If you describe someone or something as unpredictable, you mean that you cannot tell what they are going to do or how they are going to behave.
例：He is utterly unpredictable.他是个高深莫测的人。
例：...Britain's notoriously unpredictable weather.众所周知，英国的天气一向变幻莫测

sale 
英[seɪl] 美[sel]
 
n.卖，出卖；销售额，销售；销路；拍卖；
 "

N-SING
卖;销售
The sale of goods is the act of selling them for money.
例：Efforts were made to limit the sale of alcohol.过去曾采取措施限制酒的销售。
例：...a proposed arms sale to Saudi Arabia...拟议对沙特阿拉伯出售武器

N-PLURAL
销售额;销售量
The sales of a product are the quantity of it that is sold.
例：The newspaper has sales of 1.72 million.该报纸有 172 万份的销量。
例：...the huge Christmas sales of computer games.电脑游戏在圣诞期间的巨额销量

N-PLURAL
(公司产品的)销售工作
The part of a company that deals with sales deals with selling the company's products.
例：Until 1983 he worked in sales and marketing...1983 年之前，他一直在市场营销部工作。
例：She was their Dusseldorf sales manager.她是他们驻杜塞尔多夫的销售部经理。

N-COUNT
减价出售;大减价;贱卖
A sale is an occasion when a shop sells things at less than their normal price.
例：...a pair of jeans bought half-price in a sale...打折时半价买的一条牛仔裤
例：Many stores have started their January sales a month early.许多商店提前一个月就开始了一月份的特价促销活动。

N-COUNT
拍卖;拍卖会
A sale is an event when goods are sold to the person who offers the highest price.
例：The painting was bought by dealers at the Christie's sale.这幅油画在佳士得的拍卖会上被交易商拍走。

PHRASE
供出售;待售
If something is for sale, it is being offered to people to buy.
例：His former home is for sale at £495,000...他的旧居要以 49.5 万英镑的价格出售。
例：There was a Leica camera for sale in the window...橱窗里有架莱卡相机待售。

PHRASE
出售;上市
Products that are on sale can be bought in shops.
例：English textbooks and dictionaries are on sale everywhere...英语教材和词典到处都有卖的。
例：All tickets go on sale this Friday.所有票在本星期五开始销售。

PHRASE
折价销售;减价出售
If products in a shop are on sale, they can be bought for less than their normal price.
例：He bought a sports jacket on sale at Gowings Men's Store.他在高英斯男装店买了一件打折的运动上衣。

PHRASE
供出售;待售;转让
If a property or company is up for sale, its owner is trying to sell it.
例：The castle has been put up for sale.该城堡已经对外出售。

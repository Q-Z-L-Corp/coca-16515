ADJ-GRADED
可辨认的;可识别的
Something or someone that is identifiable can be recognized.
例：In the corridor were four dirty, ragged bundles, just identifiable as human beings...走廊里有4团脏兮兮、破破烂烂的东西，依稀可辨是人。
例：Stan Dean, easily identifiable by his oddly-shaped hat, sat in a doorway.斯坦·迪安坐在门口，他那顶奇形怪状的帽子一下子就让人认出他来。

technicality 
英[ˌteknɪˈkæləti] 美[ˌtɛknɪˈkælɪti]
 
n.专门性，学术性，技术性；术语；专门事项；
 "

N-PLURAL
(过程、活动的)技术细节，详细步骤
The technicalities of a process or activity are the detailed methods used to do it or to carry it out.
例：...the technicalities of classroom teaching.课堂教学的具体步骤

N-COUNT
(尤指法律上的)技术性细则
A technicality is a point, especially a legal one, that is based on a strict interpretation of the law or of a set of rules.
例：The earlier verdict was overturned on a legal technicality.由于诉讼程序上的一个技术细则，原先的判决被推翻了。

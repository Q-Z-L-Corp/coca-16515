soup 
英[su:p] 美[sup]
 
n.汤，羹；浓雾；硝化甘油炸药；困境；vt.加大马力；
 "

N-MASS
汤;羹
Soup is liquid food made by boiling meat, fish, or vegetables in water.
例：...home-made chicken soup.家常的鸡汤

PHRASE
处于困境;处境尴尬
If you say that someone is in the soup, you mean they are in trouble.
例：She has a knack of landing herself right in the soup.她老是让自己的处境很尴尬。

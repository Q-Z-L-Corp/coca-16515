ADJ
（尤指娱乐节目）深夜的，午夜的，半夜的
Late-night is used to describe events, especially entertainments, that happen late in the evening or late at night.
例：...John Peel's late-night show on BBC Radio One.约翰·皮尔在英国广播公司一台主持的深夜节目
例：...late-night drinking parties.深夜饮酒派对

ADJ
营业到深夜的
Late-night is used to describe services that are available late at night and do not shut when most commercial activities finish.
例：Saturday night was a late-night shopping night.周六晚上购物可以持续到深夜。
例：...late-night trains.午夜列车

VERB
（在数量上）压倒，比…多
If one group of people or things outnumbers another, the first group has more people or things in it than the second group.
例：...a town where men outnumber women four to one...男性比女性多3倍的城镇
例：Donkeys outnumber cars in this landscape of tiny stonewalled fields.在这片以石墙隔出巴掌大的农田的乡村，驴子比汽车还多。

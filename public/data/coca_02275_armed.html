ADJ
携带武器（通常为枪支）的
Someone who is armed is carrying a weapon, usually a gun.
例：City police said the man was armed with a revolver.市警方称那名男子携带了一支左轮手枪。
例：...a barbed-wire fence patrolled by armed guards...有武装警卫巡逻的带刺铁丝网围栏

ADJ
使用武器的
An armed attack or conflict involves people fighting with guns or carrying weapons.
例：I call on everyone to renounce the use of violence and armed struggle...我呼吁每个人都放弃使用暴力和武装斗争。
例：They had been found guilty of armed robbery.他们被判犯有持械抢劫罪。

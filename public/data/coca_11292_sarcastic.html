ADJ-GRADED
讽刺的;嘲笑的;挖苦的
Someone who is sarcastic says or does the opposite of what they really mean in order to mock or insult someone.
例：She poked fun at people's shortcomings with sarcastic remarks.她冷嘲热讽地拿别人的缺点开玩笑。

affiliated 
英[əˈfɪlieɪtɪd] 美[]
 
v.使隶属于（ affiliate的过去式和过去分词）；接纳；使发生联系；adj.隶属的；
 "

ADJ
隶属的；附属的
If an organization is affiliated with another larger organization, it is officially connected with the larger organization or is a member of it.
例：There are 73 unions affiliated to the Trades Union Congress.共有73家工会隶属于英国职工大会。
例：...the United Nations and its affiliated organisations.联合国及其附属机构

ADJ
（专业人员如医生或律师）与…有正式关系的，为…工作的
If a professional person, such as a lawyer or doctor, is affiliated with an organization, they are officially connected with that organization or do some official work for it.
例：He will remain affiliated with the firm as a special associate director.他将作为特别副经理继续为该公司工作。
例：...our affiliated members.我们所有的正式成员

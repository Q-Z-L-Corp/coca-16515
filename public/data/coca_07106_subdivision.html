N-COUNT

A subdivision is an area, part, or section of something which is itself a part of something larger.
例：Months are a conventional subdivision of the year.通常可将一年再按月细分。

A subdivision is an area of land for building houses on.
例：Rammick lives high on a ridge in a 400-home subdivision.拉米克住在高耸的山脊上一片有 400 户人家的地方。

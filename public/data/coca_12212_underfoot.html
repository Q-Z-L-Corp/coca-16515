underfoot 
英[ˌʌndəˈfʊt] 美[ˌʌndərˈfʊt]

 "

ADV
在脚下；在地上
You describe something as being underfoot when you are standing or walking on it.
例：...a room, high and square with carpet underfoot and tapestries on the walls...一个高高的四方房间，地上铺有地毯，墙上挂着挂毯
例：It was still wet underfoot.地上仍是湿的。

ADV
踩在脚下；践踏
If you trample or crush something underfoot, you spoil or destroy it by stepping on it.
例：Morgan dropped his cigarette and crushed it underfoot.摩根把香烟丢到地上，用脚踩灭。
例：...half-ripe apples that were being trampled underfoot.被踩踏的半熟的苹果

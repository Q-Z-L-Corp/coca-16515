soothe 
英[su:ð] 美[suð]
 
vt.安慰；缓和；使平静；减轻痛苦；
 "

VERB
安抚;抚慰;使平静
If you soothe someone who is angry or upset, you make them feel calmer.
例：He would take her in his arms and soothe her...他会把她揽在怀中抚慰她。
例：It did not take long for the central bank to soothe investors' fears.中央银行很快便消除了投资者的担忧。

VERB
减轻，缓和(身体的痛处或不适)
Something that soothes a part of your body where there is pain or discomfort makes the pain or discomfort less severe.
例：...body lotion to soothe dry skin.滋润干燥皮肤的润肤液

availability 
英[əˌveɪlə'bɪlətɪ] 美[əˌveləˈbɪlətɪ]
 
n.有效；有益；可利用性；可得到的东西（或人）；
 "

ADJ
能找到的；可获得的
If something you want or need is available, you can find it or obtain it.
例：Since 1978, the amount of money available to buy books has fallen by 17%...自1978年以来，可用于采购图书的经费已经减少了17％。
例：There are three small boats available for hire...有3艘小船可供出租。

ADJ
有空的;有暇的
Someone who is available is not busy and is therefore free to talk to you or to do a particular task.
例：Mr Leach is on holiday and was not available for comment.利奇先生休假了，不方便作出评论。

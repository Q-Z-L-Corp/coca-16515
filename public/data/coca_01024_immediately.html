immediately 
英[ɪˈmi:diətli] 美[ɪˈmidiɪtli]
 
adv.立即，马上；直接地；紧接地；conj.一…就，即刻；
 "

ADV
立即;马上
If something happens immediately, it happens without any delay.
例：He immediately flung himself to the floor...他立即飞身扑到地上。
例：Ingrid answered Peter's letter immediately.英格丽德立即给彼得回了信。

ADV
即刻(可见或明了的)
If something is immediately obvious, it can be seen or understood without any delay.
例：The cause of the accident was not immediately apparent.事故的起因并非一目了然。

ADV
直接地
Immediately is used to indicate that someone or something is closely and directly involved in a situation.
例：The man immediately responsible for this misery is the province's governor...对这种不幸境况负有直接责任的人是该省的省长。
例：We had the people immediately concerned in the plot.我们让故事情节与人们息息相关。

ADV
紧接地;紧靠地;贴近地
Immediately is used to emphasize that something comes next, or is next to something else.
例：They wish to begin immediately after dinner...他们希望饭后马上就开始。
例：She always sits immediately behind the driver...她总是坐在司机驾驶位置的正后方。

CONJ-SUBORD
一…(就…)
If one thing happens immediately something else happens, it happens after that event, without any delay.
例：Immediately I've done it I feel completely disgusted with myself.我一做完那件事就对自己感到十分厌恶。

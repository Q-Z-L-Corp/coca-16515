ADJ-GRADED
不加区别的;不加选择的
If you describe an action as indiscriminate, you are critical of it because it does not involve any careful thought or choice.
例：The indiscriminate use of fertilisers is damaging to the environment.乱用化肥会破坏环境。
例：...the indiscriminate killing of refugees.对难民不分青红皂白的杀戮

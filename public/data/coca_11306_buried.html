buried 
英['berɪd] 美['berɪd]
 
v.埋葬( bury的过去式和过去分词 )；掩埋；原谅；沉溺于；
 "

VERB
掩埋；埋藏
To bury something means to put it into a hole in the ground and cover it up with earth.
例：They make the charcoal by burying wood in the ground and then slowly burning it.他们通过把木材埋到地下缓慢燃烧来获取木炭。
例：...squirrels who bury nuts and seeds.掩埋坚果和种子的松鼠

VERB
埋葬；安葬
To bury a dead person means to put their body into a grave and cover it with earth.
例：...soldiers who helped to bury the dead in large communal graves...帮忙把死者安葬在大型公共墓地的士兵们
例：I was horrified that people would think I was dead and bury me alive...想到人们会认为我死了并把我活埋，我觉得恐惧万分。

VERB
失去（亲人）
If someone says they have buried one of their relatives, they mean that one of their relatives has died.
例：He had buried his wife some two years before he retired.大概在他退休前两年，他妻子去世了。

VERB
掩藏；埋藏
If you bury something under a large quantity of things, you put it there, often in order to hide it.
例：She buried it under some leaves...她把它藏在一堆树叶下面。
例：I was looking for my handbag, which was buried under a pile of old newspapers.我正在找我那被埋在一堆旧报纸下面的手提包。

VERB
覆盖；掩埋；把…埋在下面
If something buries a place or person, it falls on top of them so that it completely covers them and often harms them in some way.
例：Latest reports say that mud slides buried entire villages...最新报道说泥石流掩埋了整个整个的村庄。
例：Their house was buried by a landslide...他们的房子在一次塌方中被掩埋。

VERB
（因不高兴而）把（头或脸）贴（或靠）在…
If you bury your head or face in something, you press your head or face against it, often because you are unhappy.
例：She buried her face in the pillows ...她把脸埋进枕头里。
例：He held her closely, burying his head against her shoulder.他紧紧地抱住她，把头靠在她的肩上。

VERB
深藏；深埋
If something buries itself somewhere, or if you bury it there, it is pushed very deeply in there.
例：The missile buried itself deep in the grassy hillside...导弹深深地埋在杂草丛生的山坡上。
例：He stood on the sidewalk with his hands buried in the pockets of his dark overcoat.他站在人行道上，双手插在黑色外套的口袋里。

VERB
掩藏（感情）；抛开（记忆）
If you bury a feeling, you try not to show it. If you bury a memory, you try to forget it.
例：When we feel anger, we bury the emotion and feel guilty instead...当我们感到愤怒时，压抑这种情绪反而会觉得内疚。
例：It is time to bury our past misunderstandings.是我们捐弃前嫌的时候了。

VERB
藏身于；埋头于；专心于
If you bury yourself in a place or in an activity such as your work, you spend all your time in that place or doing that activity, usually because you want to forget about things.
例：His reaction was to withdraw, to bury himself in work.他的反应是抽身而退，埋头于工作。
例：...the popular image of writers burying themselves in the country in order to write.常见的退隐乡间写作的作家形象

VERB
沉浸于；专心致志于
If you bury your head in something such as a book or newspaper, or bury yourself in it, you look at it closely and concentrate very hard on it.
例：My father buried his head in his newspaper...我父亲专心致志地看他的报纸。
例：He buried himself in his detective story again.他又沉浸在他的侦探故事里了。

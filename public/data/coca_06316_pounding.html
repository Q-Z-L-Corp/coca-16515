N-COUNT
重创；严重损坏；重伤
If someone or something takes a pounding, they are severely injured or damaged.
例：Sarajevo took one of its worst poundings in weeks.萨拉热窝遭受了几个星期以来最严重的一次破坏。

N-COUNT
惨败；大败
If a person or team gets a pounding, they are severely defeated.
例：The prospects are that he will give opponents a thorough pounding.他很有可能使对手们一败涂地。

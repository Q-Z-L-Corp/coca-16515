gang 
英[gæŋ] 美[ɡæŋ]
 
n.帮派，团体；盗贼等的)一帮；（工具，机械等的）一套；〈美俚〉棒球队；vt.使结成一帮；成群地袭击；vi.结伙行动；
 "

N-COUNT
一帮，一伙（尤指滋事的年轻人）
A gang is a group of people, especially young people, who go around together and often deliberately cause trouble.
例：During the fight with a rival gang he lashed out with his flick knife...在与敌对帮派的打斗中，他挥舞着弹簧折刀一通猛刺。
例：Gang members were behind a lot of the violence...很多暴力事件是流氓团伙成员暗中策划的。

N-COUNT
一帮，一伙（罪犯）
A gang is a group of criminals who work together to commit crimes.
例：Police were hunting for a gang who had allegedly stolen fifty-five cars.警方正在追捕一个被控盗窃了55辆汽车的犯罪团伙。
例：...an underworld gang.地下犯罪团伙

N-SING
一伙（常聚会的朋友）
The gang is a group of friends who frequently meet.
例：Come on over, we've got lots of the old gang here.过来吧，好多老朋友都在这儿。

N-COUNT
一群，一队，一组（一起干体力活的人）
A gang is a group of workers who do physical work together.
例：...a gang of labourers.一群工人

N-UNCOUNT
（为军队或其他组织的）采购，购买
Procurement is the act of obtaining something such as supplies for an army or other organization.
例：Russia was cutting procurement of new weapons 'by about 80 per cent', he said.他说，俄罗斯新武器的采购量将削减“大约80％”。

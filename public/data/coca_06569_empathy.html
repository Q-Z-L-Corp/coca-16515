empathy 
英[ˈempəθi] 美[ˈɛmpəθi]
 
n.<心>移情作用；同感，共鸣；神入；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
同感;同情;共鸣
Empathy is the ability to share another person's feelings and emotions as if they were your own.
例：Having begun my life in a children's home I have great empathy with the little ones.由于从小生活在儿童院，我对小家伙们产生了强烈的共鸣。

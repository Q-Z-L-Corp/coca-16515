widely 
英[ˈwaɪdli] 美[ ˈwaɪdlɪ]
 
adv.广泛地；普遍地；到处；大量地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
宽的;宽阔的;宽广的
Something that is wide measures a large distance from one side or edge to the other.
例：All worktops should be wide enough to allow plenty of space for food preparation.所有的厨房操作台都应该足够宽大，为制作食物提供足够的空间。
例：...a wide-brimmed sunhat.宽檐遮阳帽

ADJ-GRADED
（笑容）大大的，灿烂的
A wide smile is one in which your mouth is stretched because you are very pleased or amused.
例：It brought a wide smile to his face and laughter to his eyes...这让他眉开眼笑。
例：His face broke into a wide grin.他脸上绽放出灿烂的笑容。

ADJ
张开很大的;完全张开的
If you open or spread something wide, you open or spread it as far as possible or to the fullest extent.
例：Open your mouth wide...张大你的嘴。
例：'It was huge,' he announced, spreading his arms wide...“它很大很大”，他边说边伸开手臂比划着。

ADJ
…宽的;宽度为…的
You use wide to talk or ask about how much something measures from one side or edge to the other.
例：...a corridor of land 10 kilometres wide...10公里宽的陆地走廊
例：The road is only one track wide.这条路只有一个车道那么宽。

ADJ-GRADED
范围广的;包容的;广泛的
You use wide to describe something that includes a large number of different things or people.
例：The brochure offers a wide choice of hotels, apartments and holiday homes...这本小册子提供了很多宾馆、公寓、度假屋供选择。
例：The proposed constitution gives him much wider powers than his predecessor.宪法提案赋予他比前任大得多的权力。

ADJ-GRADED
广泛的;广为流传的;广为人知的
You use wide to say that something is found, believed, known, or supported by many people or throughout a large area.
例：The case has attracted wide publicity...这个案子吸引了广泛的关注。
例：I suspect this book will have the widest appeal of all...我猜想这本书会引起大家最广泛的兴趣。

ADJ-GRADED
(差距等)巨大的
A wide difference or gap between two things, ideas, or qualities is a large difference or gap.
例：Research shows a wide difference in tastes around the country...研究表明全国各地的口味大有不同。
例：There are wide variations caused by different academic programme structures.不同的教学项目结构造成了巨大的差异。

ADJ-GRADED
更大的;更多的;更广泛的
Wider is used to describe something which relates to the most important or general parts of a situation, rather than to the smaller parts or to details.
例：He emphasised the wider issue of superpower cooperation...他强调了超级大国合作这一更重要的议题。
例：Oakley locates housework in the wider context of economic, social and political structures.奥克利把家务置于更广泛的经济、社会和政治结构背景中。

ADJ-GRADED
未击中目标的;未打中的
If something such as a shot or punch is wide, it does not hit its target but lands to the right or left of it.
例：The shot was wide anyway...不管怎样，这枪没打中。
例：Nearly half the missiles landed wide.有近一半的导弹落点出现偏差。

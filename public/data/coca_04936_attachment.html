attachment 
英[əˈtætʃmənt] 美[əˈtætʃmənt]
 
n.（用电子邮件发送的）附件，附属物；依恋，依附；扣押财产<法>逮捕，扣押（人，财产）；
 "

N-VAR
喜欢;爱慕;忠诚
If you have an attachment to someone or something, you are fond of them or loyal to them.
例：As a teenager she formed a strong attachment to one of her teachers...少女时代的她曾迷上了自己的一个老师。
例：Mother and child form a close attachment.母子情深。

N-COUNT
（机器的）附件，附加装置
An attachment is a device that can be fixed onto a machine in order to enable it to do different jobs.
例：Some models come with attachments for dusting.一些模型上附带了除尘器。

N-VAR
（临时的）隶属，委派
If someone is on attachment to another company, department, or place, they are working there temporarily.
例：During her course she worked on attachment for six months at Kew Gardens...在上学期间，她曾临时到基尤植物园工作了6个月。
例：Before coming on my attachment to a City law firm, I spent a year at Strasbourg University.在被临时派往伦敦的一家律师事务所工作前，我在斯特拉斯堡大学学习了一年。

N-COUNT
（文件）附件
An attachment is an extra document that is added to another document.
例：Justice Fitzgerald included a 120-page discussion paper as an attachment to the annual report.在年度报告上，法官菲茨杰拉德附上了一篇长达120页的讨论稿。

N-COUNT
（邮件）附件
In computing, an attachment is a file which is attached separately to a message that you send to someone.
例：When you send an e-mail you can also send a sound or graphic file as an attachment.在发送电子邮件时，你也可以附上一份声音或图片文件。

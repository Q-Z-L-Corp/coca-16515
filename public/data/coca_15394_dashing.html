ADJ-GRADED
潇洒的;有风度的;时髦的;漂亮的
A dashing person or thing is very stylish and attractive.
例：He was the very model of the dashing RAF pilot...他正是风度翩翩的英国皇家空军飞行员的典范。
例：Two elegant Scotsmen travelling together wore dashing kilts at dinner.两名结伴旅行的优雅苏格兰男子就餐时穿了潇洒的苏格兰方格呢短裙。

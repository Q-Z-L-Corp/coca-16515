QUANT
四分之三
Three-quarters is an amount that is three out of four equal parts of something.
例：Three-quarters of the country's workers took part in the strike...该国有 3/4 的工人参加了这次罢工。
例：It took him about three-quarters of an hour.那花了他大概 45 分钟。

rely 
英[rɪˈlaɪ] 美[rɪˈlaɪ]
 
vi.依靠，倚赖，仗恃；信任，信赖；
 "

VERB
依赖;依靠
If you rely on someone or something, you need them and depend on them in order to live or work properly.
例：They relied heavily on the advice of their professional advisers...他们非常依赖职业咨询师提供的建议。
例：The Association relies on member subscriptions for most of its income.这个协会的收入大部分来自会员的入会费。

VERB
信赖;信任
If you can rely on someone to work well or to behave as you want them to, you can trust them to do this.
例：I know I can rely on you to sort it out...我相信你会把它解决好的。
例：The Red Cross are relying on us.红十字会信赖我们。

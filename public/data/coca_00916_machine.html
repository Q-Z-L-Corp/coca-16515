machine 
英[məˈʃi:n] 美[məˈʃin]
 
n.机器，机械；机械般的人；核心；vt.用机器制造，用机器加工；vi.被机器切削；
 "

N-COUNT
机器;机械
A machine is a piece of equipment which uses electricity or an engine in order to do a particular kind of work.
例：I put the coin in the machine and pulled the lever...我把硬币投入机器，拉动控制杆。
例：The machine can be remotely operated and monitored.这台机器可以远程操作和监控。

VERB
用机器制造;用机器加工
If you machine something, you make it or work on it using a machine.
例：The material is machined in a factory...这种材料在一家工厂里用机器加工成型。
例：All parts are machined from top grade, high tensile aluminium.所有零件都是用顶级高强度铝加工而成。

N-COUNT
大型机构;严密组织
You can use machine to refer to a large and well-controlled system or organization.
例：...Nazi Germany's military machine...纳粹德国的军事机构
例：He has put the party publicity machine behind another candidate.他为另一位参选者在幕后组织政党宣传。

N-COUNT
（由于工作过于劳累或乏味）机械般工作的人，做工作不动脑筋的人
If you say that someone is a machine, you mean that they are so tired or bored that they do their work without thinking.
例：I think I have got to stop or I might turn into a machine...我想我得停下来了，否则我就会变成一台机器。
例：He has dedicated himself to his work and become just a writing machine.他忘我地投入工作，就像是一台写作机器。

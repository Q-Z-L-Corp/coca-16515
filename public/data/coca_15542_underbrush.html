N-UNCOUNT
（森林里林木下的）下层灌丛，灌木丛
Underbrush consists of bushes and plants growing close together under trees in a forest.
例：...the cool underbrush of the rain forest...雨林里使人感到凉爽的下层灌丛
例：The trail was steep and thick with underbrush.那条小路非常陡峭，而且长满了灌木丛。

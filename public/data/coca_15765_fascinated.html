fascinated 
英[ˈfæsɪneɪtɪd] 美[]
 
adj.着迷的；被深深吸引的；v.使着迷；使陶醉；

ADJ-GRADED
极感兴趣的;入迷的
If you are fascinated by something, you find it very interesting and attractive, and your thoughts tend to concentrate on it.
例：I sat on the stairs and watched, fascinated...我坐在楼梯上出神地观望。
例：A new generation of scientists became fascinated by dinosaurs.新一代科学家对恐龙研究着了迷。

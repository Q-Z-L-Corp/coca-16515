transmission 
英[trænsˈmɪʃn] 美[trænsˈmɪʃən, trænz-]
 
n.播送；传送；（电台或电视）信息；传动装置；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
传送;传递;传播;传染
The transmission of something is the passing or sending of it to a different person or place.
例：Heterosexual contact is responsible for the bulk of HIV transmission.艾滋病病毒大半是经异性性接触传播的。
例：…the fax machine and other forms of electronic data transmission.传真机及其他形式的电子数据传输

N-UNCOUNT
(电视、广播节目的)播送，播放
The transmission of television or radio programmes is the broadcasting of them.

N-COUNT
(电视、广播播送的)节目
A transmission is a broadcast.

N-VAR
(汽车等的)传动装置，变速器
The transmission on a car or other vehicle is the system of gears and shafts by which the power from the engine reaches and turns the wheels.
例：The car was fitted with automatic transmission.这辆车装配了自动变速器。
例：...a four-speed manual transmission.四挡手动变速器

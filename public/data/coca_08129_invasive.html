invasive 
英[ɪnˈveɪsɪv] 美[ɪnˈvesɪv]
 
adj.侵略性的，侵害的；攻击性的；扩散性的，蔓延性的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
侵袭的;扩散的
You use invasive to describe something undesirable which spreads very quickly and which is very difficult to stop from spreading.
例：They found invasive cancer during a routine examination.他们在常规体检中发现了扩散性的癌变。

ADJ-GRADED
开刀的;手术的
An invasive medical procedure involves operating on a patient or examining the inside of their body.
例：Many people find the idea of any kind of invasive surgery unbearable.很多人认为任何开刀手术都不堪忍受。

N-SING
混乱;骚乱
A tumult is a state of great confusion or excitement.
例：A tumult of feelings inside her fought for supremacy.她内心百感交集。
例：...the recent tumult in global financial markets.最近全球金融市场的混乱

N-SING
吵闹;喧哗
A tumult is a lot of noise made by a crowd of people.
例：Round one ends, to a tumult of whistles, screams and shouts.第一局比赛结束了，口哨声、尖叫声与呼喊声一片喧哗。

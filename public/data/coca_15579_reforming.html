V-ERG
（使）改组;重新组成
When an organization, group, or shape re-forms, or when someone re-forms it, it is created again after a period during which it did not exist or existed in a different form.
例：The official trades union council voted to disband itself and re-form as a confederation...这个官方的工会委员会投票决定解体然后重新组成联盟。
例：The 40-year-old singer reformed his band.这个40岁的歌手重组了他的乐队。

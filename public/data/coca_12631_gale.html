N-COUNT

N-COUNT
一阵（大笑声）
You can refer to the loud noise made by a lot of people all laughing at the same time as a gale of laughter or gales of laughter.
例：This was greeted with gales of laughter from the audience.观众对此报以阵阵笑声。

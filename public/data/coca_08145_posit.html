VERB
假定；假设
If you posit something, you suggest or assume it as the basis for an argument or calculation.
例：Several writers have posited the idea of a universal consciousness...有几个作者都假设存在普遍意识。
例：Callahan posits that chemical elements radiate electromagnetic signals.卡拉汉假定化学元素放射电磁信号。

quartet 
英[kwɔ:ˈtet] 美[kwɔrˈtet]
 
n.四重唱（奏）曲；四重唱（奏）组；四件一套；
 "

N-COUNT-COLL
四重奏乐团;四重唱组合
A quartet is a group of four people who play musical instruments or sing together.
例：...a string quartet.四重奏弦乐团
例：...a quartet of singers.四重唱组合

N-COUNT
四重奏曲;四重唱曲
A quartet is a piece of music for four instruments or four singers.

N-COUNT
四人组;四件套；四个一组
A quartet of people or things is a group or set of four people or things.
例：...a quartet of books.一套4本的书
例：...a quartet of local women in their mid-forties.由4名45岁左右的当地妇女组成的小组

fraught 
英[frɔ:t] 美[frɔt]
 
adj.充满的，满载的；担心的，忧虑的；n.货物，装载物；vt.装货于；
 "

ADJ
充满…的;…重重的
If a situation or action is fraught with problems or risks, it is filled with them.
例：The earliest operations employing this technique were fraught with dangers.最早使用这种技术的手术充满了危险。

ADJ-GRADED
令人焦虑的;困难的;让人担忧的
If you say that a situation or action is fraught, you mean that it is worrying or difficult.
例：It has been a somewhat fraught day.这是让人有些担忧的一天。

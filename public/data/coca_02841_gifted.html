ADJ-GRADED
有天赋的；有天才的；有天资的
Someone who is gifted has a natural ability to do something well.
例：...one of the most gifted players in the world...世界上最有天赋的运动员之一
例：He was witty, amusing and gifted with a sharp business brain.他机智诙谐，具有敏锐的商业头脑。

ADJ-GRADED
（儿童）天资聪颖的

例：...a state program for gifted children.国家培养天才儿童的方案

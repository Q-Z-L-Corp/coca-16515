动画片绘制；卡通片制作
Animation is the process of making films in which drawings or puppets appear to move.
例：The films are a mix of animation and full-length features.这些电影将动画制作和长篇故事片融为一体。
例：...computer animation.电脑动画制作

N-COUNT
动画片
An animation is a film in which drawings or puppets appear to move.
例：This film is the first British animation sold to an American network.这是第一部出售给美国电视网的英国动画片。

N-UNCOUNT
生气；活泼；热烈
Someone with animation shows liveliness in the way that they speak, look, or behave.
例：They both spoke with animation.他们兴致勃勃地说着。

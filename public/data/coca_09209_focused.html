ADJ-GRADED
有明确目标的;注意力集中的
If you describe someone or something as focused, you approve of the fact that they have a clear and definite purpose.
例：I spent the next year just wandering. I wasn't focused...接下来的一年我一直在闲逛。没有什么明确的目标。
例：The band's third album seems more disciplined and focussed...这个乐队的第三张专辑似乎表现更为节制、基调更为清晰。

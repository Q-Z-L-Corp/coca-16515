ADJ-GRADED
橡胶似的;有弹性的
Something that is rubbery looks or feels soft or elastic like rubber.
例：She had the most rubbery face...她表情极为丰富。
例：The mask is left on for about 15 minutes while it sets to a rubbery texture.面膜敷上后等约15分钟，待其变成橡胶状质地。

ADJ-GRADED
(肉等食物)难嚼的，坚韧的
Food such as meat that is rubbery is difficult to chew.

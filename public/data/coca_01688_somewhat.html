ADV
有点儿；有些；有几分
You use somewhat to indicate that something is the case to a limited extent or degree.
例：He concluded that Oswald was somewhat abnormal...他断定奥斯瓦尔德有点不正常。
例：He explained somewhat unconvincingly that the company was paying for everything...他解释说公司会支付一切费用，这个说法有点令人不能信服。

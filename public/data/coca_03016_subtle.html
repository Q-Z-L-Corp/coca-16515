subtle 
英[ˈsʌtl] 美[ˈsʌtl]
 
adj.微妙的；敏感的；狡猾的；巧妙的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不易察觉的;不明显的;微妙的
Something that is subtle is not immediately obvious or noticeable.
例：...the slow and subtle changes that take place in all living things...发生在所有生物身上的缓慢而细微的变化
例：Intolerance can take subtler forms too.过敏反应也可能会表现得很不明显。

ADJ-GRADED
机智的;机巧的;狡猾的
A subtle person cleverly uses indirect methods to achieve something.
例：I even began to exploit him in subtle ways...我甚至开始巧妙地利用他。
例：He is a subtle character, you know.你知道他为人狡猾。

ADJ-GRADED
清淡的;隐约的;柔和的
Subtle smells, tastes, sounds, or colours are pleasantly complex and delicate.
例：...subtle shades of brown.淡棕色
例：...delightfully subtle scents.怡人的淡淡香气

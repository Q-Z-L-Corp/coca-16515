adj.不可战胜的，不能征服的；无敌的；无攻不克；无前；

ADJ-GRADED
不可战胜的;所向披靡的
If you describe an army or sports team as invincible, you believe that they cannot be defeated.
例：When Sotomayor is on form he is virtually invincible.索托马约尔状态好时几乎无人可敌。

ADJ
(信念、态度)不可动摇的，不可改变的
If someone has an invincible belief or attitude, it cannot be changed.
例：He also had an invincible faith in the medicinal virtues of garlic.他同时也对大蒜的医学功效深信不疑。

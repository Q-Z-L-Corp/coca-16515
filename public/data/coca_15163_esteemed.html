ADJ-GRADED
受尊敬的;受敬重的;受仰慕的
You use esteemed to describe someone who you greatly admire and respect.
例：He was esteemed by his neighbours...邻居们很敬重他。
例：It is indeed an honour to serve my country in such an esteemed position.在如此受人尊敬的岗位上为我的国家服务的确是一种荣誉。

downside 
英[ˈdaʊnsaɪd] 美[ˈdaʊnˌsaɪd]
 
n.下降趋势；负面，消极面；adj.下降趋势的；
 "

N-SING
负面;阴暗面;缺点
The downside of a situation is the aspect of it which is less positive, pleasant, or useful than its other aspects.
例：The downside of this approach is a lack of clear leadership...这个办法的缺点是没有明确的领导。
例：There is a downside to it: the more she remembers, the more bitter she feels about what has happened.这有个不利之处:她记起的越多，对发生的一切就会觉得越痛苦。

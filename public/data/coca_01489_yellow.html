yellow 
英[ˈjeləʊ] 美[ˈjeloʊ]
 
adj.黄色的；黄皮肤的；黄种人的；胆怯的；vt.& vi.（使某物）变黄或发黄；n.黄色；黄种人；黄色颜料；
 "

COLOUR
黄;黄色
Something that is yellow is the colour of lemons, butter, or the middle part of an egg.
例：The walls have been painted bright yellow...墙壁漆成了亮黄色。
例：Kim opted for cooler blues and yellows in the master bedroom.金为主卧室挑选了偏冷色调的蓝色和黄色。

VERB
（通常因陈旧）变黄，发黄
If something yellows, it becomes yellow in colour, often because it is old.
例：The flesh of his cheeks seemed to have yellowed...他似乎有些面色发黄。
例：She sat scanning the yellowing pages.她坐着浏览发黄的书页。

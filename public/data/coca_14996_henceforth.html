ADV
从今以后;此后
Henceforth means from this time onwards.
例：Henceforth, parties which fail to get 5% of the vote will not be represented in parliament...从今以后，选票数量未达到总数5%的党派将不能在议会里占有席位。
例：We were finally released with a formal warning that we were henceforth barred from the base.我们被正式警告从此不得进入基地后终于获得了释放。

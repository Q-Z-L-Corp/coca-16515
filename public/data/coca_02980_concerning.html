concerning 
英[kənˈsɜ:nɪŋ] 美[kənˈsɜrnɪŋ]
 
prep.（表示论及）关于，就…而论；v.“concern”的现在分词；涉及；关心；顾虑，忧虑；
 "

PREP
关于
You use concerning to indicate what a question or piece of information is about.
例：For more information concerning the club contact I. Coldwell.咨询俱乐部详情，请与I.科德维尔联系。
例：...various questions concerning pollution and the environment.有关污染和环境的各种问题

ADJ
让人不安的;令人担忧的
If something is concerning, it causes you to feel concerned about it.
例：It is particularly concerning that he is working for non-British companies while advising on foreign policy.让人尤为不安的是，他在提供外交政策咨询的同时却又供职于一些外国公司。

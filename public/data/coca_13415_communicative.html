communicative 
英[kəˈmju:nɪkətɪv] 美[kəˈmjunɪkeɪtɪv]
 
adj.爱说话的，好社交的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
健谈的;善于言谈的
Someone who is communicative talks to people, for example about their feelings, and tells people things.
例：She has become a lot more tolerant and communicative.她变得宽容多了，也爱说话了。

ADJ-GRADED
交际能力的;表达能力的
Communicative means relating to the ability to communicate.
例：...the notion of communicative competence...交际能力的概念
例：We have a very communicative approach to teaching languages.我们在语言教学中非常强调交际教学法。

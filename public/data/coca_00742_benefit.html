benefit 
英[ˈbenɪfɪt] 美[ˈbɛnəfɪt]
 
n.利益，好处；救济金，津贴；义演，义卖；恩惠，恩泽；vt.有益于，有助于；使受益；得益，受益；
 "

N-VAR
好处；益处；裨益
The benefit of something is the help that you get from it or the advantage that results from it.
例：Each family farms individually and reaps the benefit of its labor...各家经营各自的农场，收获各自的劳动成果。
例：I'm a great believer in the benefits of this form of therapy...我坚信这种治疗形式非常有益。

N-UNCOUNT
（对…有）好处，帮助
If something is to your benefit or is of benefit to you, it helps you or improves your life.
例：This could now work to Albania's benefit...这种情况目前对阿尔巴尼亚有利。
例：I hope what I have written will be of benefit to someone else who may feel the same way.我希望我所写的内容能对其他和我有同感的人有所裨益。

V-ERG
获益；对…有益；有利于
If you benefit from something or if it benefits you, it helps you or improves your life.
例：Both sides have benefited from the talks.双方都从谈判中获益。
例：...a variety of government programs benefiting children.有益于儿童的各种政府项目

N-UNCOUNT
有利条件；优势
If you have the benefit of some information, knowledge, or equipment, you are able to use it so that you can achieve something.
例：Steve didn't have the benefit of a formal college education...史蒂夫没能接受正规的大学教育。
例：With the benefit of hindsight, it is clear we ought to have done more...事后看来，我们显然本该做得更多。

N-VAR
（政府提供给穷人、病人和失业人员的）救济金，补助金
Benefit is money that is given by the government to people who are poor, ill, or unemployed.
例：...the removal of benefit from school-leavers...取消辍学者的补助金
例：I was told that in order to get benefit payments I would have to answer some questions.我得知要领补助，就得回答一些问题。

N-COUNT
慈善活动
A benefit, or a benefit concert or dinner, is an event that is held in order to raise money for a particular charity or person.
例：I am organising a benefit gig in Bristol to raise these funds.我要在布里斯托尔组织一场义演来募集这些资金。

PHRASE
暂且相信；姑且信之
If you give someone the benefit of the doubt, you treat them as if they are telling the truth or as if they have behaved properly, even though you are not sure that this is the case.
例：At first I gave him the benefit of the doubt...起初，我姑且相信了他。
例：Shalford is entitled to the benefit of the doubt.沙尔福德暂且获得了信任。

PHRASE
为了…的利益；为…好
If you say that someone is doing something for the benefit of a particular person, you mean that they are doing it for that person.
例：You need people working for the benefit of the community...你们需要为社区谋利益的人。
例：He doesn't have to go through this elaborate display for my benefit!他不必为了我作这么详细的展示！

harmonious 
英[hɑ:ˈməʊniəs] 美[hɑrˈmoʊniəs]
 
adj.和谐的，融洽的；协调的；音调优美的；悦耳的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（关系、协定、讨论）融洽的，和睦的，友好的
A harmonious relationship, agreement, or discussion is friendly and peaceful.
例：Their harmonious relationship resulted in part from their similar goals...他们关系融洽的部分原因是他们有着相似的目标。
例：It is a harmonious community where pupils are happy and industrious.这是一个和睦的集体，学生们开心玩耍，用功读书。

ADJ-GRADED
（各组成部分）和谐的，协调的
Something that is harmonious has parts which go well together and which are in proportion to each other.
例：The architecture is harmonious and no building is over five or six floors high.这里的建筑风格和谐统一，没有哪座楼房高于五六层。
例：...a harmonious balance of mind, body, and spirit.头脑、身体和心灵的协调

ADJ-GRADED
（音调）和谐的，悦耳的
Musical notes that are harmonious produce a pleasant sound when played together.
例：...the mysterious skill involved in producing harmonious sounds.发出悦耳声音的神秘技巧

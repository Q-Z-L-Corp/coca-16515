extinguish 
英[ɪkˈstɪŋgwɪʃ] 美[ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ]
 
vt.熄灭（火）；使（希望、爱情等）不复存在；偿清；
 "

VERB
熄灭;扑灭
If you extinguish a fire or a light, you stop it burning or shining.
例：It took about 50 minutes to extinguish the fire...大约花了50分钟才把火扑灭。
例：The lights are extinguished as soon as the news conference is over.新闻发布会一结束，灯光就熄灭了。

VERB
消除;使破灭
If something extinguishes a feeling or idea, it destroys it.
例：The message extinguished her hopes of Richard's return.那条消息使她对理查德归来的希望彻底破灭。

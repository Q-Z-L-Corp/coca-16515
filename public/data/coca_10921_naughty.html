naughty 
英[ˈnɔ:ti] 美[ˈnɔti]
 
adj.顽皮的；不听话的；淘气的；下流的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
顽皮的;淘气的;不听话的
If you say that a child is naughty, you mean that they behave badly or do not do what they are told.
例：Girls, you're being very naughty...姑娘们，你们真是淘气。
例：You naughty boy, you gave me such a fright.你这调皮鬼，吓了我一跳。

ADJ-GRADED
(书籍、照片、词语等)不规矩的，淫猥的，下流的
You can describe books, pictures, or words, as naughty when they are slightly rude or related to sex.
例：You know what little boys are like with naughty words.你知道小男孩们说下流话是什么样子。
例：...saucy TV shows, crammed full of naughty innuendo.充斥着淫猥暗示的下流电视节目

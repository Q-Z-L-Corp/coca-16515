ADV
在附近;在近处
If something is nearby, it is only a short distance away.
例：He might easily have been seen by someone who lived nearby...住在附近的人很可能有谁见过他。
例：He spoke softly to a couple standing nearby...他轻声和站在旁边的一对夫妇说话。

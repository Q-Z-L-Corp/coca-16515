marriage 
英[ˈmærɪdʒ] 美[ˈmærɪdʒ]
 
n.结婚；婚姻生活；密切结合；合并；
 "

N-COUNT
婚姻
A marriage is the relationship between a husband and wife.
例：In a good marriage, both husband and wife work hard to solve any problems that arise...美满的婚姻中，夫妻双方会共同努力去解决出现的任何问题。
例：When I was 35 my marriage broke up...35岁时我的婚姻破裂了。

N-VAR
结婚;婚礼
A marriage is the act of marrying someone, or the ceremony at which this is done.
例：I opposed her marriage to Darryl.我反对她嫁给达里尔。

N-UNCOUNT
婚姻生活;已婚状态
Marriage is the state of being married.
例：Marriage might not suit you...婚姻也许并不适合你。
例：In twenty years of marriage he has only taken two proper vacations.结婚二十年里，他只像样地度过两次假。

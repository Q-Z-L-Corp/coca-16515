VERB
（在记忆或想象中）再次体验，回味，重温
If you relive something that has happened to you in the past, you remember it and imagine that you are experiencing it again.
例：There is no point in reliving the past...回味过去没有任何意义。
例：Last night he relived his terrifying ordeal.昨夜他又一次体验了那种可怕的折磨。

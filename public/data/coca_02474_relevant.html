ADJ-GRADED
相关的;相应的;有重要性的
Something that is relevant to a situation or person is important or significant in that situation or to that person.
例：We are trying to make politics more relevant to younger people.我们试图使政治活动更加贴近年轻人。
例：We have passed all relevant information on to the police.我们已经把所有相关信息都告知了警方。

ADJ
适当的;合适的;确切的
The relevant thing of a particular kind is the one that is appropriate.
例：Make sure you enclose all the relevant certificates.要保证把所有相关证书都放在里面。

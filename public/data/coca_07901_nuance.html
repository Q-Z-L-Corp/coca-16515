N-VAR
(声音、感受、外貌或意义的)细微差别
A nuance is a small difference in sound, feeling, appearance, or meaning.
例：We can use our eyes and facial expressions to communicate virtually every subtle nuance of emotion there is.我们用眼睛和面部表情几乎就能传达出情感上的每一丝微妙变化。

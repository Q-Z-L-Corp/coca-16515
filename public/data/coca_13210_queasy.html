adj.（指人）想呕吐的；感到恶心的；易反胃的；（指良心等）感到不自在的；

ADJ-GRADED
恶心的;想呕吐的
If you feel queasy or if you have a queasy stomach, you feel rather ill, as if you are going to be sick.
例：He was very prone to seasickness and already felt queasy.他快晕船了，已经感到恶心了。

ADJ-GRADED
忧虑的；不安的;担心的
If you feel queasy about something, you are a little worried about it.
例：Some people feel queasy about how their names and addresses have been obtained.一些人为自己的姓名和地址是如何被泄露出去的感到不安。

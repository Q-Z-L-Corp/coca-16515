（用于书面语中表示发音非正式的your）你的，你们的
Yer is used in written English to represent the word 'your' when it is pronounced informally.
例：Mister, can we 'elp to carry yer stuff in?先生，要我们帮您把行李拿进去吗？

（用于书面语中表示发音非正式的you）你，你们
Yer is used in written English to represent the word 'you' when it is pronounced informally.
例：I bloody told yer it would sell.我早就告诉过你它会卖出去的。

obtain 
英[əbˈteɪn] 美[əbˈtein]
 
vt.获得，得到；流行；买到，达到（目的）；vi.通行，通用；流行；存在；
 "

VERB
得到；获得;实现
To obtain something means to get it or achieve it.
例：Evans was trying to obtain a false passport and other documents...埃文斯正试图取得一本假护照和其他证件。
例：The perfect body has always been difficult to obtain.完美的身材总是很难拥有。

VERB
存在；流行；通行
If a situation obtains, it exists.
例：The longer this situation obtains, the more extensive the problems become.这种局面持续的时间越长，问题波及的范围就越广。

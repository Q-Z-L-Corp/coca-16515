requirement

N-COUNT
要求;需求;必要条件
A requirement is a quality or qualification that you must have in order to be allowed to do something or to be suitable for something.
例：Its products met all legal requirements...它的产品符合所有的法律要求。
例：Graduate status is the minimum requirement for entry to the teaching profession...研究生学历是从事教学工作的最低要求。

N-COUNT
必需品;需要的东西
Your requirements are the things that you need.
例：Variations of this programme can be arranged to suit your requirements.本方案可以进行调整，以满足您的需求。
例：...a packaged food which provides 100 percent of your daily requirement of one vitamin.能够百分之百满足人体对于一种维生素每日所需的包装食品

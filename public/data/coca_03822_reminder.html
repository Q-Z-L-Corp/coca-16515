reminder 
英[rɪˈmaɪndə(r)] 美[rɪˈmaɪndər]
 
n.令人回忆起…的东西；提醒…的东西；（告知该做某事的）通知单；提示信；
 "

N-COUNT
提示;使人回忆起某事的事物
Something that serves as a reminder of another thing makes you think about the other thing.
例：The British are about to be given a sharp reminder of what fighting abroad really means...有些事将很快让英国人清楚意识到海外作战究竟意味着什么。
例：Violence has broken out in the capital, a stark reminder that the religious tensions are refusing to go away.首都爆发了暴力事件，这给了人们一个严酷的提醒——宗教紧张局势还在持续。

N-COUNT
缴费单;催缴单;催缴通知
A reminder is a letter or note that is sent to tell you that you have not done something such as pay a bill or return library books.
例：...the final reminder for the gas bill.最后一次煤气催缴通知单

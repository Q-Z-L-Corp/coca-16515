N-COUNT
杀人者；杀手
A killer is a person who has killed someone, or who intends to kill someone.
例：The police are searching for his killers...警方正在搜捕杀害他的凶手。
例：He's a psychopath, a killer.他是个精神变态杀人者。

N-COUNT
导致死亡的事物
You can refer to something that causes death or is likely to cause death as a killer .
例：Heart disease is the biggest killer of men in most developed countries.在多数发达国家，心脏病是导致人们死亡的头号杀手。

ADJ
不容忽视的;不可避免的;必然发生的
If you describe a fact, situation, or activity as inescapable, you mean that it is difficult not to notice it or be affected by it.
例：The economic logic of reform is inescapable...改革有其必然的经济学逻辑。
例：A sense of imminent doom was inescapable.一种即将来临的毁灭感无可逃避。

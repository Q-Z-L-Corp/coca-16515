热烈欢迎；热烈鼓掌
An ovation is a large amount of applause from an audience for a particular performer or speaker.
例：They had lost by a wide margin, but their supporters gave them a defiant, loyal ovation.他们以较大的差距败北，但支持者们还是给予了热烈的掌声，以示对对手的不服和对他们的忠诚。

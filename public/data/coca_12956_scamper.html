VERB
（步子小而轻地）奔跑，疾走;蹦蹦跳跳
When people or small animals scamper somewhere, they move there quickly with small, light steps.
例：Children scampered off the yellow school bus and into the playground...孩子们欢快地跑下黄色的校车，冲进运动场。
例：The flash sent the foxes scampering away.闪光惊得狐狸四处逃窜。

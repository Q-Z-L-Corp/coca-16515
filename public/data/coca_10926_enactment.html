enactment 
英[ɪˈnæktmənt] 美[ɛnˈæktmənt]
 
n.（法律的）制定，颁布；法律，法规；扮演；
 "

N-VAR
(法律的)制定，通过
The enactment of a law is the process in a parliament or other law-making body by which the law is agreed upon and made official.
例：We support the call for the enactment of a Bill of Rights.我们支持要求通过《权利法案》的呼声。

N-VAR
表演;演出;上演
The enactment of a play or story is the performance of it by an actor or group of actors.
例：The main building was also used for the enactment of mystery plays.主楼还用于神秘剧的演出。

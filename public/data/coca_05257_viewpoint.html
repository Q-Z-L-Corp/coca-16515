viewpoint 
英[ˈvju:pɔɪnt] 美[ˈvjuˌpɔɪnt]
 
n.观点，意见，角度；视角；<物>视点；
 "

N-COUNT
观点;看法；角度
Someone's viewpoint is the way that they think about things in general, or the way they think about a particular thing.
例：The novel is shown from the girl's viewpoint...这部小说是从这个女孩的视角来叙述的。
例：To include as many viewpoints as possible, the editor reserves the right to shorten letters.为了能包括尽可能多的观点，编辑保留对信件进行删节的权力。

N-COUNT
视角;角度；观察点
A viewpoint is a place from which you can get a good view of something.
例：You have to know where to stand for a good viewpoint.你得知道站在哪里观察角度比较理想。

reveal 
英[rɪˈvi:l] 美[rɪˈvil]
 
vt.显露；揭露；泄露；[神]启示；n.揭示，展现；[建]门侧，窗侧；
 "

VERB
显示;表明;透露
To reveal something means to make people aware of it.
例：She has refused to reveal the whereabouts of her daughter...她不肯透露她女儿的行踪。
例：A survey of the British diet has revealed that a growing number of people are overweight...对英国人饮食进行的一项调查显示有越来越多的人超重。

VERB
揭露;展示;使显露
If you reveal something that has been out of sight, you uncover it so that people can see it.
例：A grey carpet was removed to reveal the original pine floor.一块灰色的地毯被揭开，露出了原来的松木地板。

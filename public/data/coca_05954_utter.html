utter 
英[ˈʌtə(r)] 美[ˈʌtɚ]
 
vt.发出声音；说，讲；出版（书籍）等；[法]流通使用；adj.彻底的，完全的，绝对的；
 "

VERB
发出（声音）；说；讲
If someone utters sounds or words, they say them.
例：He uttered a snorting laugh...他嗤笑了一声。
例：They departed without uttering a word.他们一言不发地离开了。

ADJ
完全的；彻底的；十足的
You use utter to emphasize that something is great in extent, degree, or amount.
例：This, of course, is utter nonsense.这当然是一派胡言。
例：...this utter lack of responsibility...这种完全没有责任感的表现

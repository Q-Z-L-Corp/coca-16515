abdicate 
英[ˈæbdɪkeɪt] 美[ˈæbdɪˌket]
 
vt.放弃（职责、权力等）；vi.退位，逊位；adj.可以放弃的；放弃权力的，退位的；n.放弃权力者，退位者；
 "

VERB
退位;逊位
If a king or queen abdicates, he or she gives up being king or queen.
例：The last French king was Louis Philippe, who abdicated in 1848.法国末代国王是1848年逊位的路易·菲力普。

VERB
放弃（责任）；失（职）
If you say that someone has abdicated responsibility for something, you disapprove of them because they have refused to accept responsibility for it any longer.
例：Many parents simply abdicate all responsibility for their children.许多父母对孩子简直是完全撒手不管。

Rung is the past participle of ring .

N-COUNT
梯级;横档
The rungs on a ladder are the wooden or metal bars that form the steps.
例：I swung myself onto the ladder and felt for the next rung.我纵身跳到梯子上，摸索着下一根横档。

N-COUNT
(事业、组织、进程中的)级别，等级，地位
If you reach a particular rung in your career, in an organization, or in a process, you reach that level in it.
例：I first worked with him in 1971 when we were both on the lowest rung of our careers...我第一次和他共事是在1971年，那时我们的事业都刚刚起步。
例：There has never been a better time to get on the first rung of the property ladder.迈出置业第一步的最佳时机莫过于此。

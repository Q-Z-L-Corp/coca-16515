fluidity 
英[fluˈɪdəti] 美[fluˈɪdəti]
 
n.流动性；流质；变移性；流度；
 "

N-MASS
液体;流质
A fluid is a liquid.
例：The blood vessels may leak fluid, which distorts vision...血管可能会渗漏，造成视力模糊。
例：Make sure that you drink plenty of fluids.务必保证足够的水分摄入。

ADJ-GRADED
(动作、线条或设计)流畅优美的，优雅自然的
Fluid movements or lines or designs are smooth and graceful.
例：The forehand stroke should be fluid and well balanced.正手击球应该保证动作流畅和平稳。
例：...long, fluid dresses.线条优雅流畅的长裙

ADJ-GRADED
不稳定的;不确定的
A situation that is fluid is unstable and is likely to change often.
例：The situation is extremely fluid and it can be changing from day to day.情况极不稳定，每天都有可能变化。

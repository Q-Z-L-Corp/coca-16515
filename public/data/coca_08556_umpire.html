N-COUNT
（体育比赛或竞赛中的）裁判，裁判员
An umpire is a person whose job is to make sure that a sports match or contest is played fairly and that the rules are not broken.
例：The umpire's decision is final.裁判的裁定是不可更改的。

VERB
在…中担任裁判
To umpire means to be the umpire in a sports match or contest.
例：He umpired baseball games...他担任棒球比赛的裁判。
例：He umpired for school football matches until he was in his late 50s.他担任校际足球比赛的裁判一直到年近花甲。

billow 
英[ˈbɪləʊ] 美[ˈbɪloʊ]
 
n.巨浪，如波涛滚滚而来之物；vi.在波涛中翻滚；扬起，鼓起；
 "

VERB
（布制品）鼓起
When something made of cloth billows, it swells out and moves slowly in the wind.
例：The curtains billowed in the breeze...窗帘在微风中鼓了起来。
例：Her pink dress billowed out around her.她粉红色的连衣裙鼓了起来。

VERB
（烟或云）翻滚，滚滚向上
When smoke or cloud billows, it moves slowly upwards or across the sky.
例：...thick plumes of smoke billowing from factory chimneys...工厂烟囱冒出的滚滚浓烟
例：Steam billowed out from under the bonnet.引擎盖下喷出滚滚的蒸汽。

N-COUNT
腾起的一大团（烟、尘等）
A billow of smoke or dust is a large mass of it rising slowly into the air.
例：...smoke stacks belching billows of almost solid black smoke.喷出滚滚黑烟的烟囱

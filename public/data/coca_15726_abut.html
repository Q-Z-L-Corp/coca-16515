VERB
邻接；毗连；紧靠
When land or a building abuts something or abuts on something, it is next to it.
例：One edge of the garden abutted on an old entrance to the mine...花园的一侧紧靠着矿井的旧入口。
例：He was born in 1768 in the house abutting our hotel.他于1768年出生于我们旅馆旁边的一幢房子里。

A miscellaneous group consists of many different kinds of things or people that are difficult to put into a particular category.
例：...a hoard of miscellaneous junk...收藏的一堆乱七八糟的破烂
例：They eat a lot of meats and dairy foods, along with a lot of miscellaneous items that don't fall into any group.他们吃了大量的肉和奶制品，还有许多不知道该归为什么类别的杂七杂八的东西。

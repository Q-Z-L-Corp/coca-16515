lukewarm 
英[ˌlu:kˈwɔ:m] 美[ˌlukˈwɔrm]
 
adj.冷淡的；微温的；不热心的；温和；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（尤指液体）微温的，微热的，不冷不热的
Something, especially a liquid, that is lukewarm is only slightly warm.
例：Wash your face with lukewarm water...用温水洗脸。
例：The coffee was weak and lukewarm.咖啡寡淡无味，温乎乎的。

ADJ-GRADED
缺乏兴趣的；不热情的；冷淡的
If you describe a person or their attitude as lukewarm, you mean that they are not showing much enthusiasm or interest.
例：Economists have never been more than lukewarm towards him...经济学家对他向来态度冷淡。
例：The study received a lukewarm response from the Home Secretary.内政大臣对这项研究反应不是很热烈。

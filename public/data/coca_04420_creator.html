creator

 "

N-COUNT
创造者;创作者;发明者
The creator of something is the person who made it or invented it.
例：...Ian Fleming, the creator of James Bond...詹姆斯·邦德这一形象的创作者伊恩·弗莱明
例：I have always believed that a garden dies with its creator.我一直认为花园的灵魂是与其创造者共存亡的。

N-PROPER
上帝;造物主
God is sometimes referred to as the Creator .
例：This was the first object placed in the heavens by the Creator.这是上帝安放在天堂里的第一件东西。

ADV-GRADED
明显地;显然
Decidedly means to a great extent and in a way that is very obvious.
例：Sometimes he is decidedly uncomfortable at what he sees on the screen...他有时会对屏幕上出现的画面明显感到不自在。
例：Representatives of the other branches adopted a decidedly different view...其他分部的代表持截然不同的观点。

COCA TOP 20000 words - aim 




aim 
英[eɪm] 美[em]
 
vt.瞄准；对准；踢向；挥向；vi.旨在；以…为目标；致力于；决定；n.目的；目标；瞄准；
 "

VERB
致力于;旨在;以…为目标
If you aim for something or aim to do something, you plan or hope to achieve it.
例：He is aiming for the 100 metres world record...他志在打破100米世界纪录。
例：Businesses will have to aim at long-term growth.企业必须以长期增长为目标。

N-COUNT
目的;目标
The aim of something that you do is the purpose for which you do it or the result that it is intended to achieve.
例：The aim of the festival is to increase awareness of Hindu culture and traditions.该节庆日的目的在于增强人们对印度文化和传统的认识。
例：...a research programme that has largely failed to achieve its principal aims.在较大程度上未能实现其主要目标的研究项目

V-PASSIVE
旨在;目的是
If an action or plan is aimed at achieving something, it is intended or planned to achieve it.
例：The new measures are aimed at tightening existing sanctions.新措施旨在加强现行的制裁。
例：...talks aimed at ending the war.旨在结束战争的谈判

VERB
决定;想要
If you aim to do something, you decide or want to do it.
例：Are you aiming to visit the gardens?...你想去逛公园吗？
例：I didn't aim to get caught.我并不想被抓住。

VERB
（行为、言论）针对，指向
If your actions or remarks are aimed at a particular person or group, you intend that the person or group should notice them and be influenced by them.
例：His message was aimed at the undecided middle ground of Israeli politics...他所传达的信息针对的是以色列政局中尚未表态的中间派。
例：Advertising aimed at children should be curbed.针对儿童的广告应受到限制。

VERB
瞄准；对准
If you aim a weapon or object at something or someone, you point it towards them before firing or throwing it.
例：He was aiming the rifle at Wade.他将步枪瞄准了韦德。
例：...a missile aimed at the arms factory...瞄准军工厂的导弹

N-SING
瞄准，对准（指技巧或动作）
Your aim is your skill or action in pointing a weapon or other object at its target.
例：He stood with the gun in his right hand and his left hand steadying his aim.他站立着，右手举枪，左手稳住枪对准目标。

VERB
踢向;挥（拳）向
If you aim a kick or punch at someone, you try to kick or punch them.
例：They aimed kicks at his shins.他们踢向他的小腿。

PHRASE
瞄准;对准
When you take aim, you point a weapon or object at someone or something, before firing or throwing it.
例：She had spotted a man with a shotgun taking aim.她发现一个正持猎枪瞄准的男子。

PHRASE
激烈地批评
If you take aim at someone or something, you criticize them strongly.
例：Republican strategists are taking particular aim at Democratic senators.共和党的战略家正尖锐地攻击民主党的参议员。

amused 
英[əˈmju:zd] 美[]
 
adj.被逗笑的；愉快的；顽皮的；v.“amuse”的过去式和过去分词；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
被逗乐的
If you are amused by something, it makes you want to laugh or smile.
例：Sara was not amused by Franklin's teasing...被富兰克林揶揄之后，萨拉的脸上全无笑意。
例：We were amused to see how assiduously the animal groomed its fur.看着这只动物如此认真地梳理自己的毛，我们被逗乐了。

PHRASE
使…快乐;为…提供消遣
If you keep someone amused, you find things to do which stop them getting bored.
例：Having pictures to colour will keep children amused for hours...有画可用来涂色会让孩子们玩很长时间。
例：Archie kept us amused with his stories.阿奇一直给我们讲故事来逗我们开心。

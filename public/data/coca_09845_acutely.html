acutely 
英[əˈkju:tli] 美[əˈkjuːtli]
 
adv.尖锐地；剧烈地；
 "

ADV-GRADED
深深地（感觉到），强烈地(意识到)
If you feel or notice something acutely, you feel or notice it very strongly.
例：He was acutely aware of the odour of cooking oil...他闻到一股浓浓的油烟味。
例：Those tensions have been felt most acutely by women.最能深切感受到这种紧张氛围的是女性。

ADV-GRADED
（感觉、性质）极其
If a feeling or quality is acutely unpleasant, it is extremely unpleasant.
例：It was an acutely uncomfortable journey back to London.那是一次极其痛苦的返回伦敦的旅行。

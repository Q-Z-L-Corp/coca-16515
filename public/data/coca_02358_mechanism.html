mechanism 
英[ˈmekənɪzəm] 美[ˈmɛkəˌnɪzəm]
 
n.[生]机制，机能，[乐]机理；（机械）结构，机械装置[作用]，（故事的）结构；[艺]手法，技巧，途径；机械作用；
 "

N-COUNT
机械装置;机件
In a machine or piece of equipment, a mechanism is a part, often consisting of a set of smaller parts, which performs a particular function.
例：...the locking mechanism...锁定装置
例：A bomb has been detonated by a special mechanism.炸弹由一个专门装置引爆。

N-COUNT
方法;途径;程序;机制
A mechanism is a special way of getting something done within a particular system.
例：There's no mechanism for punishing arms exporters who break the rules.对违规的武器出口商实施惩罚尚无执行机制。
例：...the clumsy and ineffective mechanism of price controls.方法笨拙、效率低下的价格控制机制

N-COUNT
(人为生存、对付困境的)机制，行为方式
A mechanism is a part of your behaviour that is automatic and that helps you to survive or to cope with a difficult situation.
例：...a survival mechanism, a means of coping with intolerable stress.一种生存机制，一种应对无法承受的压力的方法

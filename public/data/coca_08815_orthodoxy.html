orthodoxy 
英[ˈɔ:θədɒksi] 美[ˈɔrθədɑksi]
 
n.正统；正教，信奉正教；正统派的观念[学说，做法等]；正统性；
 "

N-VAR
正统观念；普遍接受的观念
An orthodoxy is an accepted view about something.
例：These ideas rapidly became the new orthodoxy in linguistics...这些观点迅速成为语言学中新的正统观念。
例：He broke from prevailing orthodoxies and asked the awkward questions...他抛开盛行的正统观念，问了一些难以回答的问题。

N-UNCOUNT
（宗教、政党或哲学的）传统看法，正统信仰
The old, traditional beliefs of a religion, political party, or philosophy can be referred to as orthodoxy.
例：...the latest step in a purge of liberals and a return to political orthodoxy.肃清自由派、回归政治正统理念的最新举措
例：...a conflict between Nat's religious orthodoxy and Rube's belief that his mission is to make money.纳特坚守宗教传统信仰与鲁布以赚钱为己任之间的冲突

humidity 
英[hju:ˈmɪdəti] 美[hjuˈmɪdɪti]
 
n.（空气中的）湿度；潮湿，高温潮湿；湿热；[物]湿度；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
潮湿
You say there is humidity when the air feels very heavy and damp.
例：The heat and humidity were insufferable.高温和潮湿让人难以忍受。

N-UNCOUNT
（空气）湿度
Humidity is the amount of water in the air.
例：The humidity is relatively low.湿度相对较低。

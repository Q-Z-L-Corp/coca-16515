ADJ
非全日制的；兼职的
If someone is a part-time worker or has a part-time job, they work for only part of each day or week.
例：Many businesses are cutting back by employing lower-paid part-time workers...很多企业通过雇用薪金低廉的兼职工人来减少开支。
例：Part-time work is generally hard to find...一般说来，兼职工作很难找。

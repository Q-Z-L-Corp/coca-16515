stain 
英[steɪn] 美[sten]
 
vi.弄脏；污染；被玷污；vt.玷污，染污；败坏（名声）；给…染色；n.污点；瑕疵；着色剂；色斑；
 "

N-COUNT
污迹;污渍
A stain is a mark on something that is difficult to remove.
例：Remove stains by soaking in a mild solution of bleach.用温和的漂白剂浸泡以去除污迹。
例：...a black stain.黑色污迹

VERB
在…上留下污迹;玷污;染污
If a liquid stains something, the thing becomes coloured or marked by the liquid.
例：Some foods can stain the teeth, as of course can smoking.某些食物会在牙齿上留下污渍，吸烟当然也会。

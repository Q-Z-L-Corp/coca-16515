brigade 
英[brɪˈgeɪd] 美[brɪˈɡeɪd]
 
n.旅；vt.把…编成旅；把…编成队；
 "

N-COUNT-COLL
旅
A brigade is one of the groups which an army is divided into.
例：...the men of the Seventh Armoured Brigade.第七装甲旅的士兵们

N-SING
（信奉某事或具有共同特点的）一帮人，族
You can use brigade to refer to a group of people who believe strongly in a particular thing or who share a particular characteristic.
例：...the healthy eating brigade.健康饮食族
例：...the black T-shirt brigade.黑T恤一族

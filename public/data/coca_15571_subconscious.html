subconscious 
英[ˌsʌbˈkɒnʃəs] 美[ˌsʌbˈkɑnʃəs]
 
adj.下意识的；潜意识的；
 "

N-SING
潜意识;下意识
Your subconscious is the part of your mind that can influence you or affect your behaviour even though you are not aware of it.
例：...the hidden power of the subconscious...潜意识的潜在力量
例：The memory of it all was locked deep in my subconscious.关于它的一切记忆都深植于我的潜意识里。

ADJ
下意识的;潜意识的
A subconscious feeling or action exists in or is influenced by your subconscious.
例：He caught her arm in a subconscious attempt to detain her.他下意识地抓住她的胳膊想挽留她。
例：...a subconscious cry for affection.潜意识里渴望得到关爱

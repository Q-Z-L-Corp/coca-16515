Worn is the past participle of wear .

ADJ-GRADED
破旧的；磨损的;陈旧的
Worn is used to describe something that is damaged or thin because it is old and has been used a lot.
例：Worn rugs increase the danger of tripping...磨损的小地毯会使人更容易绊倒。
例：Most of the trek is along worn paths.大部分时候都是沿着被人踩得坑坑洼洼的小路跋涉。

ADJ-GRADED
憔悴的；疲惫的;苍老的
If someone looks worn, they look tired and old.
例：She was looking very haggard and worn.她看上去非常憔悴和疲惫。

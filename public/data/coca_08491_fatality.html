fatality 
英[fəˈtæləti] 美[feˈtælɪti, fə-]
 
n.灾祸；死亡（事故）；宿命；致命性；

N-COUNT
(事故或暴力导致的)死亡
A fatality is a death caused by an accident or by violence.
例：Drunk driving fatalities have declined more than 10 percent over the past 10 years.酒后驾车死亡人数在过去 10 年中下降了 10%以上。

N-UNCOUNT
宿命感;宿命论
Fatality is the feeling or belief that human beings cannot influence or control events.
例：...with a feeling of fatality.怀着宿命感

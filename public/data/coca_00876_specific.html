specific 
英[spəˈsɪfɪk] 美[spɪˈsɪfɪk]
 
adj.明确的；特种的；具体的；[免疫学]特效的；n.特效药；特性；细节；显著的性质，特性；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
(部位、问题、题目等)特定的，特别的
You use specific to refer to a particular fixed area, problem, or subject.
例：Massage may help to increase blood flow to specific areas of the body...按摩有助于增加身体特定部位的血液流量。
例：There are several specific problems to be dealt with...有几个特定问题需要解决。

ADJ-GRADED
（表达）明确的，确切的，具体的
If someone is specific, they give a description that is precise and exact. You can also use specific to describe their description.
例：I asked him to be more specific...我要求他说得更具体些。
例：These nerve centres generate rhythmic movements; or to be more specific, rhythmic stomach movements...这些神经中心产生有节奏的运动，或者更确切地说，是有节奏的肠胃蠕动。

ADJ
特有的;独特的;只与…有关的
Something that is specific to a particular thing is connected with that thing only.
例：Send your resume with a cover letter that is specific to that particular job.寄出你的简历并附上一份针对那个特定工作岗位的附函。

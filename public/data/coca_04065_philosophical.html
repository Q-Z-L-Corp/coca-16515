ADJ
哲学的
Philosophical means concerned with or relating to philosophy.
例：He was not accustomed to political or philosophical discussions.他不习惯政治或哲学讨论。

ADJ-GRADED
豁达的；处之泰然的
Someone who is philosophical does not get upset when disappointing or disturbing things happen.
例：Lewis has grown philosophical about life.对于生活，刘易斯变得豁达洒脱了。

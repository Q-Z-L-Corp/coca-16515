freezing 
英[ˈfri:zɪŋ] 美[]
 
adj.严寒的，冰冻的；极冷的；使人打冷颤的；冷冻用的；v.使结冰(freeze的现在分词)；冷冻贮藏，冷藏；冻结（资金、银行账户等）；使冻伤；n.冻结，结冰；（物价、工资等的）冻结；[电影]定格，保持不动；
 "

ADJ
极冷的;冻得彻骨的
If you say that something is freezing or freezing cold, you are emphasizing that it is very cold.
例：The cinema was freezing.电影院太冷了。
例：...a freezing January afternoon.1月份的一个滴水成冰的下午

ADJ
(人)感到极冷的，冻得要命的
If you say that you are freezing or freezing cold, you emphasizing that you feel very cold.
例：'You must be freezing,' she said.“你一定冻坏了，”她说。

N-UNCOUNT
同 freezing point
Freezing means the same as freezing point .
例：It's 15 degrees below freezing.温度为零下 15 度。

VERB
打开…的包裹物;拆开…的包装
When you unwrap something, you take off the paper, plastic, or other covering that is around it.
例：I untied the bow and unwrapped the small box...我解开蝴蝶结，拆开小盒子的包装。
例：Vacuum-packed ham slices should be unwrapped 30 minutes before serving.真空包装的火腿片应在食用前30分钟把包装除去。

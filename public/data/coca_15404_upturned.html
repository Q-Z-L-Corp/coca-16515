ADJ
朝上的；朝上翘的；向上翻的
Something that is upturned points upwards.
例：...the rain splashing down on her upturned face.溅落到她仰起的脸上的雨水
例：...his eyes closed and his palms upturned.他闭上眼睛，掌心向上。

ADJ
翻转的；倒置的
Something that is upturned is upside down.
例：...upturned buckets...倒扣的水桶
例：He clung to the upturned boat, screaming for help.他紧紧抓着住倾覆的小船，高呼救命。

raw 
英[rɔ:] 美[rɔ]
 
adj.生的，未加工的；无经验的；新近完成的；发炎的，疼痛的；vt.擦伤；使赤身露体；n.（身体的）擦伤部位；红肿部位；半成品；原料；
 "

ADJ
未经加工的;天然状态的
Raw materials or substances are in their natural state before being processed or used in manufacturing.
例：We import raw materials and energy and export mainly industrial products.我们进口原材料和能源，主要出口工业产品。
例：...two ships carrying raw sugar from Cuba.两艘来自古巴装有粗糖的船

ADJ
（食物）生的，生食的，未熟的
Raw food is food that is eaten uncooked, that has not yet been cooked, or that has not been cooked enough.
例：...a popular dish made of raw fish...受欢迎的生鱼菜肴
例：This versatile vegetable can be eaten raw or cooked...这种蔬菜有多种吃法，既可生吃也可烹调后食用。

ADJ-GRADED
露肉的;擦破皮的
If a part of your body is raw, it is red and painful, perhaps because the skin has come off or has been burnt.
例：...the drag of the rope against the raw flesh of my shoulders...绳子摩擦着我蹭破了皮的肩头
例：Her feet hurt and her hands were rubbed raw from unaccustomed work.这种活儿她根本做不习惯，双脚疼痛，双手也蹭破了皮。

ADJ-GRADED
（情感）不加掩饰的，原始的，朴实的
Raw emotions are strong basic feelings or responses which are not weakened by other influences.
例：...the raw passions of nationalism...朴实的民族主义热情
例：Her grief was still raw and he did not know how to help her.她仍然沉浸在悲伤中，他不知道如何帮她才好。

ADJ-GRADED
简单的;强大的;真实的
If you describe something as raw, you mean that it is simple, powerful, and real.
例：...the raw power of instinct.本能的强大力量
例：...the raw vitality of his earlier painting.他早期绘画的强大生命力

ADJ
（信息）未经处理的，原始的
Raw data is facts or information that has not yet been sorted, analysed, or prepared for use.
例：Analyses were conducted on the raw data.对原始数据进行了分析。
例：...a statistical model that fully adjusts the census's raw figures.全面调整这项普查的原始数据的统计模型

ADJ-GRADED
没有经验的;不熟练的
If you describe someone in a new job as raw, or as a raw recruit, you mean that they lack experience in that job.
例：...replacing experienced men with raw recruits...用新手替代有经验的人
例：Davies is still raw but his potential shows.戴维斯还是新人，但已经显示出了他的潜力。

ADJ-GRADED
（天气）生冷的，阴冷的
Raw weather feels unpleasantly cold.
例：Once they cleared the housetops, the wind was raw and biting.他们清扫完屋顶后，寒风刺骨。
例：...a raw December morning.12月一个阴冷的早上

ADJ
（污水）未经处理的
Raw sewage is sewage that has not been treated to make it cleaner.
例：...contamination of bathing water by raw sewage.洗澡用水被未经处理的污水污染

PHRASE
不公平待遇
If you say that you are getting a raw deal, you mean that you are being treated unfairly.
例：They feel that Quebec is getting a raw deal...他们觉得魁北克省受到了不公平的待遇。
例：I think women have a raw deal.我认为妇女受到了不公正的待遇。

PHRASE
未开化的；原生态的；处于蒙昧状态的；原原本本的
You use in the raw to describe something that is in its true state, and has not been made civilized or respectable.
例：This is nature in the raw...这是自然的原生态。
例：He also wanted to see Bangladesh in the raw...他也想看看原生态的孟加拉国是什么样子。

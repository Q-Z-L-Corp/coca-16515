penetrating 
英[ˈpenɪtreɪtɪŋ] 美[ˈpɛnɪˌtretɪŋ]
 
adj.敏锐的；有洞察力的；聪明的；响亮的；v.贯穿，穿过( penetrate的现在分词)；刺入；了解；渗透；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
（声音）响亮的，刺耳的，尖厉的
A penetrating sound is loud and usually high-pitched.
例：Mary heard the penetrating bell of an ambulance...玛丽听到了刺耳的救护车警铃声。
例：Her voice was nasal and penetrating.她说话带有鼻音，很刺耳。

ADJ-GRADED
（目光）锐利的，犀利的，有洞察力的
If someone gives you a penetrating look, it makes you think that they know what you are thinking.
例：He gazed at me with a sharp, penetrating look that made my heart pound.他的目光敏锐而犀利，看得我心里怦怦直跳。
例：...dark penetrating eyes.锐利的黑眼睛

ADJ-GRADED
（头脑）敏锐的，敏捷的
Someone who has a penetrating mind understands and recognizes things quickly and thoroughly.
例：...a thoughtful, penetrating mind...缜密而敏锐的头脑
例：He never stopped asking penetrating questions.他一直在问一些尖锐的问题。

ADJ-GRADED
悠然自得的；放松随和的
If you describe someone as laid-back, you mean that they behave in a calm relaxed way as if nothing will ever worry them.
例：Nothing worried him, he was really laid back...他万事无忧，日子过得十分悠游自在。
例：Everyone here has a really laid-back attitude.这里的每个人都是一副优哉游哉的态度。

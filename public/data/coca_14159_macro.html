macro 
英[ˈmækrəʊ] 美[ˈmækroʊ]
 
n.[自]宏指令；巨（计算机术语）；adj.巨大的；大量使用的；特别突出的；极厚的；
 "

ADJ
广大的；宏观的
You use macro to indicate that something relates to a general area, rather than being detailed or specific.
例：...coordinated programmes of regulation of the economy both at the macro level and at the micro level.宏观与微观手段相协调的经济调控计划

ADJ
(摄影用)微距的
In photography and film, macro lenses and other devices are used for photographing or filming things at very close range.
例：He photographed this using a macro lens...他用微距镜拍下了这个。
例：Zooming is not possible while in macro mode.在微距模式下不可能实现变焦。

N-COUNT
宏；宏命令；宏指令
A macro is a shortened version of a computer command which makes the computer carry out a set of actions.

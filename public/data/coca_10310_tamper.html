tamper 
英[ˈtæmpə(r)] 美[ˈtæmpɚ]
 
vt.窜改；vi.篡改；（用不正当手段）影响，干预；瞎摆弄；贿赂；n.夯，夯具；捣乱者；填塞者；反射器；
 "

VERB
干预;篡改
If someone tampers with something, they interfere with it or try to change it when they have no right to do so.
例：I don't want to be accused of tampering with the evidence...我不希望被人控告篡改证据。
例：He found his computer had been tampered with.他发现他的电脑被人动过手脚。

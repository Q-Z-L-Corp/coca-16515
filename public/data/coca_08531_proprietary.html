proprietary 
英[prəˈpraɪətri] 美[prəˈpraɪəteri]
 
adj.专有的，专利的；所有（人）的；（商品）专卖的；n.所有权，所有物；所有人；专卖药品；独家制造（及销售）的产品；
 "

ADJ
专利的；专卖的
Proprietary substances or products are sold under a trade name.
例：...some proprietary brands of dog food...一些狗粮的专利品牌
例：We had to take action to protect the proprietary technology.我们必须采取措施保护专利技术。

ADJ-GRADED
（态度）以所有者自居的
If someone has a proprietary attitude towards something, they act as though they own it.
例：Directors weren't allowed any proprietary airs about the product they made.主管们不允许对他们生产的产品以所有者自居。

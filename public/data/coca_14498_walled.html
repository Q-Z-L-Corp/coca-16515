ADJ
有围墙的;有城墙的
If an area of land or a city is walled, it is surrounded or enclosed by a wall.
例：The city was walled and built upon a rock.这座城市建在岩石上，四周筑有城墙。
例：...a walled rose garden.带围墙的玫瑰花园

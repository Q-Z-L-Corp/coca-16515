ability 
英[əˈbɪləti] 美[əˈbɪlɪti]
 
n.能力，资格；能耐，才能；
 "

N-SING
能力
Your ability to do something is the fact that you can do it.
例：The public never had faith in his ability to handle the job...公众从来不相信他有能力胜任这一职位。
例：He has the ability to bring out the best in others.他能做到扬人之长。

N-VAR
才能;技能;本领
Your ability is the quality or skill that you have which makes it possible for you to do something.
例：Her drama teacher spotted her ability...她的戏剧老师发现了她的才能。
例：I have confidence in the ability of the players...我对于球员们的能力充满信心。

PHRASE
尽…所能地；竭尽全力地
If you do something to the best of your abilities or to the best of your ability, you do it as well as you can.
例：I take care of them to the best of my abilities...我尽我所能地照顾他们。
例：They tackled them to the best of their ability.他们竭尽全力给予解决。

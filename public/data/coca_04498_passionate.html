passionate 
英[ˈpæʃənət] 美[ˈpæʃənɪt]
 
adj.热烈的；激昂的；易怒的；易被情欲所支配的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
热情的；狂热的
A passionate person has very strong feelings about something or a strong belief in something.
例：...his passionate commitment to peace...他热情满怀地献身于和平事业
例：I'm a passionate believer in public art...我是公共艺术的狂热信徒。

ADJ-GRADED
热恋的；情欲强烈的
A passionate person has strong romantic or sexual feelings and expresses them in their behaviour.
例：...a beautiful, passionate woman of twenty-six.美丽而炽烈的26岁女子
例：...the story of a passionate love affair...热恋故事

affectionate 
英[əˈfekʃənət] 美[əˈfɛkʃənɪt]
 
adj.深情的，挚爱的；慈爱的；有感情的；亲热；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
充满深情的；满怀柔情的；表示爱的
If you are affectionate, you show your love or fondness for another person in the way that you behave towards them.
例：They seemed devoted to each other and were openly affectionate...他们看起来非常恩爱，柔情蜜意溢于言表。
例：She gave me a very long and affectionate hug.她满怀深情地久久拥抱了我。

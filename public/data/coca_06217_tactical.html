tactical 
英[ˈtæktɪkl] 美[ˈtæktɪkəl]
 
adj.战术的；策略上的；巧妙设计的；有谋略的；
 "

ADJ
(行动、计划)战术上的，策略性的
You use tactical to describe an action or plan which is intended to help someone achieve what they want in a particular situation.
例：It's not yet clear whether the Prime Minister's resignation offer is a serious one, or whether it's simply a tactical move...现在还不清楚首相提出辞职是认真的，还是仅仅作为一种策略。
例：The security forces had made a tactical withdrawal from the area.安全部队已从该地区战术性撤军。

ADJ
(武器、军队)战术的，作战的
Tactical weapons or forces are those which a military leader can decide for themselves to use in a battle, rather than waiting for a decision by a political leader.
例：They have removed all tactical nuclear missiles that could strike Europe.他们已经拆除所有能够袭击欧洲的战术核导弹。
例：...U.S. tactical air fighter squadrons.美国战术空中战斗机中队

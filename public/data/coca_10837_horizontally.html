horizontally

 "

ADJ
水平的;横的
Something that is horizontal is flat and level with the ground, rather than at an angle to it.
例：The board consists of vertical and horizontal lines...板上画着横纵的线条。
例：Swing the club back until it is horizontal.将球杆向后挥至水平位置。

N-COUNT
水平线;横线;水平结构
A horizontal is a line or structure that is horizontal.
例：The undulating planes of the countryside provide relief from the hard horizontals and verticals of the urban scene.乡村起伏不平的地势使人们摆脱了城市刻板的横平竖直景象。

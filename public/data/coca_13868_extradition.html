extradition 
英[ˌekstrə'dɪʃn] 美[ˌɛkstrəˈdɪʃən]
 
n.（根据条约或法令对逃犯等的）引渡；
 "

VERB
引渡
If someone is extradited, they are officially sent back to their own or another country to be tried for a crime that they have been accused of.
例：He was extradited to Britain from the Irish Republic to face explosives charges...他被从爱尔兰共和国引渡到英国，面临爆炸罪的指控。
例：The authorities refused to extradite him.当局拒绝引渡他。

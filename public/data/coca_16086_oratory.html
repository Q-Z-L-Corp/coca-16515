N-UNCOUNT
演讲术；雄辩术
Oratory is the art of making formal speeches which strongly affect people's feelings and beliefs.
例：He displayed determination as well as powerful oratory.他既展示出震撼人心的雄辩术，又显露了矢志不移的决心。

N-COUNT
（基督徒做礼拜的）祈祷室，小礼拜堂
An oratory is a room or building where Christians go to pray.
例：The wedding will be at the Brompton Oratory next month.婚礼将于下月在布朗普顿礼拜堂举行。

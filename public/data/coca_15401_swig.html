VERB
大口喝;畅饮
If you swig a drink, you drink it from a bottle or cup quickly and in large amounts.
例：I swigged down two white wines...我把两杯白葡萄酒一饮而尽。
例：He was still hanging around, swigging the Coke out of the can.他还在闲逛，拿着一罐可乐大口大口地喝着。

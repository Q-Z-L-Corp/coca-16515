commensurate 
英[kəˈmenʃərət] 美[kəˈmɛnsərɪt, -ʃə-]
 
adj.（在时间和空间上）相等的；相称的，相当的；
 "

ADJ
(比例)相当的，相称的
If the level of one thing is commensurate with another, the first level is in proportion to the second.
例：Employees are paid salaries commensurate with those of teachers...给员工的薪水与老师拿的薪水相当。
例：Managers saw a commensurate fall in their revenues.经理们发现自己的收入相应地减少了。

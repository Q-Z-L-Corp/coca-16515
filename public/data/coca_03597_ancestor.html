ancestor 
英[ˈænsestə(r)] 美[ˈænˌsɛstɚ]
 
n.祖先，祖宗；被继承人；原型；（动物的）原种，先祖；
 "

N-COUNT
祖宗;祖先
Your ancestors are the people from whom you are descended.
例：...our daily lives, so different from those of our ancestors...与我们祖先的生活有着天壤之别的我们的日常生活
例：He could trace his ancestors back seven hundred years.他的先祖可以上溯到700年前。

N-COUNT
原种；原型
An ancestor of something modern is an earlier thing from which it developed.
例：The direct ancestor of the modern cat was the Kaffir cat of ancient Egypt...现代猫的直系原种是古埃及的卡菲尔猫。
例：The immediate ancestor of rock 'n' roll is rhythm-and-blues.摇滚乐直接源自节奏布鲁斯。

hateful 
英[ˈheɪtfl] 美[ˈhetfəl]
 
adj.可恶的，十分讨厌的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
极坏的;可恶的;令人讨厌的
Someone or something that is hateful is extremely bad or unpleasant.
例：I'm sorry. That was a hateful thing to say.对不起，我不该说那么难听的话。

ADJ-GRADED
心怀仇恨的;充满憎恨的
Someone who is hateful hates someone else.
例：These are not necessarily hateful, malicious people.这些人不一定心怀仇恨，恶意伤人。
例：...a lying, hateful and racist campaign.充满谎言、仇恨和种族主义情绪的运动

imaginative 
英[ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv] 美[ɪˈmædʒənətɪv, -ˌnetɪv]
 
adj.富于想像力的，运用想像力的；想象或创造出来的；沉溺于想象的；虚假的；

ADJ-GRADED
富于想象力的;有独创性的
If you describe someone or their ideas as imaginative, you are praising them because they are easily able to think of or create new or exciting things.
例：...an imaginative writer.想象力丰富的作家
例：...hundreds of cooking ideas and imaginative recipes...成百上千个烹饪点子和富有创意的食谱

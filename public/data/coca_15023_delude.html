delude 
英[dɪˈlu:d] 美[dɪˈlud]
 
vt.欺骗，哄骗；
 "

VERB
欺骗，哄骗(自己)
If you delude yourself, you let yourself believe that something is true, even though it is not true.
例：The President was deluding himself if he thought he was safe from such action...总统如果认为这样的行为不会威胁到他，那他就是在自欺欺人。
例：We delude ourselves that we are in control...我们骗自己说我们掌控着局面。

VERB
欺骗，哄骗(他人)
To delude someone into thinking something means to make them believe what is not true.
例：Television deludes you into thinking you have experienced reality, when you haven't...电视给人一种经历现实的错觉，而实际上并没有。
例：He had been unwittingly deluded by their mystical nonsense.他不知不觉被他们故弄玄虚的胡说八道给骗了。

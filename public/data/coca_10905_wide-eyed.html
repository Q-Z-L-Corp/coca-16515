wide-eyed 
英[ˈwaɪdˌaɪd] 美[ˈwaɪdˌaɪd]
 
adj.睁大眼睛的，吃惊的，天真的；天真的；吃惊的；
 "

ADJ
天真的;不懂世故的
If you describe someone as wide-eyed, you mean that they are inexperienced and innocent, and may be easily impressed.
例：Her wide-eyed innocence soon exposes the pretensions of the art world.她的天真无邪很快就让艺术界的自命不凡显露无遗。
例：...a wide-eyed boy ready to explore.迫切想要探索这个世界的天真男孩

ADJ
(尤指因惊讶或恐惧而)睁大眼睛的
If you describe someone as wide-eyed, you mean that their eyes are more open than usual, especially because they are surprised or frightened.
例：She is wide-eyed with astonishment.她吃惊地睁大了眼睛。
例：...an expression of wide-eyed amazement.瞠目结舌的表情

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指战争中的)屠杀,残杀
Carnage is the violent killing of large numbers of people, especially in a war.
例：...his strategy for stopping the carnage in Kosovo.他提出的制止科索沃大屠杀的策略
例：...the carnage of motorway accidents.高速公路车祸导致的大量人员死亡

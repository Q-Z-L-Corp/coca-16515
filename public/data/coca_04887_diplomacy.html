diplomacy 
英[dɪˈpləʊməsi] 美[dɪˈploʊməsi]
 
n.外交；外交手腕，交际手段；外交使团；处世之道；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
外交
Diplomacy is the activity or profession of managing relations between the governments of different countries.
例：Today's Security Council resolution will be a significant success for American diplomacy...今天的安理会决议将是美国外交取得的一个重大成功。
例：The talks have now gone into a stage of quiet diplomacy.会谈现在已经进入了“温和外交”阶段。

N-UNCOUNT
(处理人际关系等的)手腕，策略，手段
Diplomacy is the skill of being careful to say or do things which will not offend people.
例：He stormed off in a fury, and it took all Minnelli's powers of diplomacy to get him to return.他愤然离去，米内利使出了浑身解数才把他劝了回来。

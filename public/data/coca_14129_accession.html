accession 
英[ækˈseʃn] 美[ækˈsɛʃən]
 
n.增加；就职；增加物；（对同盟、国际条约等的）正式加入；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
（君主、帝王的）登基，即位；（国家统治者的）就职，就任
Accession is the act of taking up a position as the ruler of a country.
例：...the 50th anniversary of the Queen's accession to the throne.女王登基50周年庆典

N-UNCOUNT
正式加入
A country's accession to a group of countries or to an association is its joining that group of countries or association.
例：...Namibia's accession to the Lome convention.纳米比亚正式加入《洛美协定》
例：...a ballot over the issue of Kashmir's accession to India.对克什米尔是否并入印度的表决

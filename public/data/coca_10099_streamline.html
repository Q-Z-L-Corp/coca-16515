VERB
使(机构或过程)效率更高;精简
To streamline an organization or process means to make it more efficient by removing unnecessary parts of it.
例：They're making efforts to streamline their normally cumbersome bureaucracy...他们正努力精简本来繁冗复杂的官僚体制。

listener 
英[ˈlɪsənə(r)] 美[ˈlɪsənər]
 
n.听众，倾听者，收听者；〈俚〉耳朵；
 "

N-COUNT
（电台广播的）收听者，听众之一
A listener is a person who listens to the radio or to a particular radio programme.
例：I'm a regular listener to her show...我经常收听她的节目。
例：Each week Dr Buczacki chooses a listener's question and gives a detailed answer.每个星期布柴齐医生都会选取一位听众的问题，给予详细回答。

N-COUNT
倾听他人说话的人
If you describe someone as a good listener, you mean that they listen carefully and sympathetically to you when you talk, for example about your problems.
例：Dr Brian was a good listener...布莱恩医生会耐心倾听他人说话。
例：If you can be a sympathetic listener, it may put your own problems in perspective.如果你能充满同情心地倾听别人的说话，也许就能够正确地认识自己的问题。

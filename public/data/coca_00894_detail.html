detail 
英[ˈdi:teɪl] 美[dɪˈteɪl]
 
n.详述；（照片、绘画等的）细部；细目，琐碎；各种细节；vt.详述，仔细汇报；选派；清晰地说明；给于细部装饰；
 "

N-COUNT
细节
The details of something are its individual features or elements.
例：The details of the plan are still being worked out...计划的细节仍在制订中。
例：No details of the discussions have been given...没有透露讨论的细节。

N-PLURAL
详情;信息;资料
Details about someone or something are facts or pieces of information about them.
例：See the bottom of this page for details of how to apply for this exciting offer...有关如何申请这一令人兴奋的工作的详情请参见本页底部。
例：Full details will be announced soon.具体信息很快就会公布。

N-COUNT
枝节;末节;琐事
A detail is a minor point or aspect of something, as opposed to the central ones.
例：Only minor details now remain to be settled.现在仅剩一些细枝末节的问题需要解决。

N-UNCOUNT
(难以觉察的)细微之处
You can refer to the small features of something which are often not noticed as detail .
例：We like his attention to detail and his enthusiasm.我们喜欢他的细心和热情。

N-COUNT
(画等的)细部
A detail of a picture is a part of it that is printed separately and perhaps made bigger, so that smaller features can be clearly seen.

VERB
详述;详细列举（或说明）
If you detail things, you list them or give information about them.
例：The report detailed the human rights abuses committed during the war.这份报告详述了战争期间所发生的践踏人权的情况。

VERB
派遣;指示
If someone is detailed to do a task or job, they are officially ordered to do it.
例：He detailed a constable to take it to the Incident Room.他派了一名警察把它送到重案调查室。

PHRASE
详细叙述;逐一说明
If someone does not go into details about a subject, or does not go into the detail, they mention it without explaining it fully or properly.
例：He said he had been in various parts of Britain but did not go into details...他说曾去过英国的许多地方，但没有细讲。
例：Neither of them were prepared to go into the detail of their talks.他们俩都没准备要详细说明他们会谈的情况。

PHRASE
详细地;全面地;仔细地
If you examine or discuss something in detail, you do it thoroughly and carefully.
例：We examine the wording in detail before deciding on the final text.我们彻底仔细地检查了措词后才最终定稿。

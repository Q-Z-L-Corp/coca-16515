inward 
英[ˈɪnwəd] 美[ˈɪnwərd]
 
adj.向内的；内部的；本质上的；亲密的；adv.向内；内心里；n.内部；内脏；
 "

ADJ
(思想、感情)内在的,内心的
Your inward thoughts or feelings are the ones that you do not express or show to other people.
例：I sighed with inward relief.我心里舒了一口气。
例：...a glow of inward satisfaction.内心强烈的满足感

ADJ
向内的;向中心的
An inward movement is one towards the inside or centre of something.
例：...a sharp, inward breath like a gasp...像倒抽气一样猛地吸入一口气
例：The athlete takes off from one leg from an inward twist.这名运动员向内转体一周单腿起跳。

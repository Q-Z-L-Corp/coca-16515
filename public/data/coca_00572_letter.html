N-COUNT
信;信函
If you write a letter to someone, you write a message on paper and send it to them, usually by post.
例：I had received a letter from a very close friend.我收到一个很要好的朋友的来信。
例：...a letter of resignation...辞职信

字母
Letters are written symbols which represent one of the sounds in a language.
例：...the letters of the alphabet.字母表中的字母
例：...the letter E.字母E

PHRASE
（钻）法律字眼
If you say that someone keeps to the letter of the law, you mean that they act according to what is actually written in the law, rather than according to the general principles of it, especially when you disapprove of this.
例：They had stuck to the letter of the law and in my view they should be ashamed.他们死抠法律字眼。依我看他们应该感到惭愧。

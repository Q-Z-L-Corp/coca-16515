ADJ-GRADED
非典型的
Someone or something that is atypical is not typical of its kind.
例：The economy of the province was atypical because it was particularly small...该省的经济比较特殊，因为它的规模特别小。
例：He was an atypical English schoolboy.他跟一般的英国男学生不太一样。

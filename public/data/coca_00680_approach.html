approach 
英[əˈprəʊtʃ] 美[əˈproʊtʃ]
 
vt.& vi.接近，走近，靠近；vt.接近；着手处理；使移近；试图贿赂（或影响，疏通）；n.方法；途径；接近；vi.靠近；
 "

VERB
靠近;接近;走近
When you approach something, you get closer to it.
例：He didn't approach the front door at once...他没有马上走向前门。
例：When I approached, they grew silent...当我走近时，他们就不说话了。

N-COUNT
路径;途径;道路
An approach to a place is a road, path, or other route that leads to it.
例：The path serves as an approach to the boat house.这条小路通往船屋。

VERB
与…接洽；找…商量
If you approach someone about something, you speak to them about it for the first time, often making an offer or request.
例：When Chappel approached me about the job, my first reaction was of disbelief...当查普尔为这份工作找我商量时，我的第一反应是不相信。
例：He approached me to create and design the restaurant...他请我建造并设计该饭店。

VERB
探讨；处理；对待
When you approach a task, problem, or situation in a particular way, you deal with it or think about it in that way.
例：The Bank has approached the issue in a practical way...银行已经实际解决了这个问题。
例：Employers are interested in how you approach problems.雇主们对你如何处理问题感兴趣。

N-COUNT
方法;态度;手段
Your approach to a task, problem, or situation is the way you deal with it or think about it.
例：We will be exploring different approaches to gathering information.我们将探索收集信息的不同方法。
例：...the adversarial approach of the British legal system.英国法律制度采用的辩论式审判模式

VERB
临近；来临
As a future time or event approaches, it gradually gets nearer as time passes.
例：As autumn approached, the plants and colours in the garden changed.秋天临近，花园中的草木和颜色也发生了变化。
例：...the approaching crisis.步步逼近的危机

VERB
接近（未来时间或事件）
As you approach a future time or event, time passes so that you get gradually nearer to it.
例：We approach the end of the year with the economy slowing and little sign of cheer.接近年末时，我们面临经济迟滞、处处萧条的局面。

VERB
近似；接近于
If something approaches a particular level or state, it almost reaches that level or state.
例：Oil prices have approached their highest level for almost ten years...石油价格已接近近10年中的最高水平。
例：Mansell will race at average speeds approaching 200mph.曼塞尔将以接近每小时200英里的平均车速参赛。

spatter 
英[ˈspætə(r)] 美[ˈspætɚ]
 
vt.& vi.溅，洒落；n.泼；洒、溅等的声音；
 "

V-ERG
溅;洒
If a liquid spatters a surface or you spatter a liquid over a surface, drops of the liquid fall on an area of the surface.
例：He stared at the rain spattering on the glass...他凝视着雨点溅落在玻璃上。
例：Gently turn the fish, being careful not to spatter any hot butter on yourself...轻轻地把鱼翻过来，小心别把滚烫的黄油溅到自己身上。

hostility 
英[hɒˈstɪləti] 美[hɑˈstɪləti]
 
n.敌意，敌对状态；战争行动；愤怒反对，愤怒反抗；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
敌意;对抗;敌对行为
Hostility is unfriendly or aggressive behaviour towards people or ideas.
例：The last decade has witnessed a serious rise in the levels of racism and hostility to Black and ethnic groups...过去十年里，对黑人及少数族裔的种族歧视和敌对行为变得日益猖獗。
例：Christabel looked at Ron with open hostility.克丽丝特布尔带着公然的敌意看着罗恩。

N-UNCOUNT
反对;抵制
Your hostility to something you do not approve of is your opposition to it.
例：There is hostility among traditionalists to this method of teaching history.传统主义者反对这种讲授历史的方法。

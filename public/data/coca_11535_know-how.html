N-UNCOUNT
（尤指专业或实用的）技巧，技能
Know-how is knowledge of the methods or techniques of doing something, especially something technical or practical.
例：He hasn't got the know-how to run a farm.他没有经营农场的专业知识。
例：...technical know-how.专业技术知识

glue 
英[glu:] 美[ɡlu]
 
vt.胶合；紧附于；n.胶水；胶粘物；粘聚力；
 "

N-MASS
胶；胶水
Glue is a sticky substance used for joining things together, often for repairing broken things.
例：...a tube of glue.一管胶水
例：...high quality glues.优质胶水

VERB
用胶水粘
If you glue one object to another, you stick them together using glue.
例：Glue the fabric around the window...用胶水把布料粘在窗户周围。
例：The material is cut and glued in place...材料被剪切下来并粘贴到合适位置。

V-PASSIVE
完全吸引；使专注于
If you say that someone is glued to something, you mean that they are giving it all their attention.
例：They are all glued to the Olympic Games.他们都被奥运会吸引住了。

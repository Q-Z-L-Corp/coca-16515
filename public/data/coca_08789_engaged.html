engaged 
英[ɪnˈgeɪdʒd] 美[ɛnˈɡedʒd]
 
adj.（指厕所等）有人用的；已订婚的；（指电话）占线的；忙碌的；v.与某人订婚；保证，约定；从事( engage的过去式和过去分词)；吸引或引起（注意、兴趣等）；
 "

ADJ
从事…的;忙于…的
Someone who is engaged in or engaged on a particular activity is doing that thing.
例：They found the three engaged in target practice.他们发现那3个人在练习打靶。
例：...the various projects he was engaged on.他参与的各种项目

ADJ
订婚的
When two people are engaged, they have agreed to marry each other.
例：We got engaged on my eighteenth birthday...在我18岁生日那天我们订婚了。
例：He was engaged to Miss Julia Maria Boardman.他和朱莉娅·玛丽亚·博德曼小姐订婚了。

ADJ
（电话）占线的
If a telephone or a telephone line is engaged, it is already being used by someone else so that you are unable to speak to the person you are phoning.
例：The line is engaged...电话占线。
例：We tried to call you back but you were engaged.我们试着给你回电，但是你正在通话中。

ADJ
（公共厕所）被占用的，使用中的
If a public toilet is engaged, it is already being used by someone else.

wording 
英[ˈwɜ:dɪŋ] 美[ˈwɜrdɪŋ]
 
n.用词，措辞；v.用词语表达，讲话(word的ing形式)；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
措辞;用词
The wording of a piece of writing or a speech are the words used in it, especially when these are chosen to have a particular effect.
例：The two sides failed to agree on the wording of a final report...双方未能就最终报告的措辞达成一致。
例：The wording is so vague that no one actually knows what it means.措辞十分模糊，谁也不知道究竟是什么意思。

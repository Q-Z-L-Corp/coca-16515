premiere 
英[ˈpremieə(r)] 美[prɪˈmɪr]
 
n.首映；vi.&vt.初次公演，首次露面；adj.首要的，至上的；
 "

N-COUNT
首映；首演；初次公演
The premiere of a new play or film is the first public performance of it.
例：A new Czechoslovak film has had its premiere at the Karlovy Vary film festival.一部捷克斯洛伐克新电影在卡罗维发利电影节上首映。
例：...a royal film premiere.盛大的电影首映

V-ERG
（使）首映；（使）首演；（使）初次公演
When a film or show premieres or is premiered, it is shown to an audience for the first time.
例：The documentary premiered at the Jerusalem Film Festival...这部纪录片在耶路撒冷电影节首映。
例：The opera is due to be premiered by ENO next year.这部歌剧将于明年在英国国家歌剧院首演。

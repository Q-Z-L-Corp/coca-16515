N-UNCOUNT
维修；保养；养护
The upkeep of a building or place is the work of keeping it in good condition.
例：The money will be used for the estate's upkeep...这笔钱将用于物业维护。
例：The maintenance department is responsible for the general upkeep of the park.维护部负责公园的一般养护。

N-UNCOUNT
赡养；供养；（公共事业的）维持
The upkeep of a group of people or services is the process of providing them with the things that they need.
例：He offered to pay £100 a month towards his son's upkeep.他愿意每月付100英镑作为儿子的抚养费。
例：...subsidies for the upkeep of kindergartens and orphanages.用于维持幼儿园和孤儿院日常运转的补助

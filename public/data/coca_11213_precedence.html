N-UNCOUNT
优先；优先权
If one thing takes precedence over another, it is regarded as more important than the other thing.
例：Have as much fun as possible at college, but don't let it take precedence over work...在大学要尽情娱乐，但不要把它看得比学习还重要。
例：As the King's representative he took precedence over everyone else on the island.作为国王的代表，他比岛上其他任何人的地位都要高。

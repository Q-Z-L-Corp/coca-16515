N-COUNT
生命线；命脉
A lifeline is something that enables an organization or group to survive or to continue with an activity.
例：Information about the job market can be a lifeline for those who are out of work...对于那些失业的人而言，就业信息可谓是他们的生命线。
例：The orders will throw a lifeline to Britain's shipyards.这些订单将给英国的造船厂注入一线生机。

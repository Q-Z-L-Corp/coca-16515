N-SING
(尤指作为度假地的)海边，海滨
You can refer to an area that is close to the sea, especially one where people go for their holidays, as the seaside .
例：I went to spend a few days at the seaside...我去海滨玩了几天。
例：The town was Redcar, a seaside resort on the Cleveland coast.那个城镇叫雷德卡，是克利夫兰海岸上的一个海滨胜地。

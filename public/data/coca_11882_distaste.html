N-UNCOUNT
不喜欢;反感;厌恶
If you feel distaste for someone or something, you dislike them and consider them to be unpleasant, disgusting, or immoral.
例：Roger looked at her with distaste...罗杰厌恶地看着她。
例：He professed a distaste for everything related to money.他声称厌恶与金钱有关的一切。

propagation 
英[ˌprɒpə'ɡeɪʃn] 美[ˌprɑpəˈɡeʃən]
 
n.传播，传输，蔓延，扩展，波及深度；〔生〕繁殖法，〔地〕传导；宣传；培养；
 "

VERB
宣传；传播；使普及
If people propagate an idea or piece of information, they spread it and try to make people believe it or support it.
例：They propagated political doctrines which promised to tear apart the fabric of British society.他们鼓吹能颠覆英国社会结构的政治学说。

VERB
繁殖，培植（植物）
If you propagate plants, you grow more of them from the original ones.
例：The easiest way to propagate a vine is to take hardwood cuttings...栽培葡萄最简便的方法就是硬条扦插。
例：The pasque flower can be propagated from seed.欧白头翁可以通过种子进行繁殖。

timber 
英[ˈtɪmbə(r)] 美[ˈtɪmbɚ]
 
n.木材，木料；（用于建筑或制作物品的）树木；用材林，林场；素质；vt.用木料支撑；备以木材；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
木材;原木;树木；林木
Timber is wood that is used for building houses and making furniture. You can also refer to trees that are grown for this purpose as timber .
例：These Severn Valley woods have been exploited for timber since Saxon times.从撒克逊时代起就开始采伐塞文山谷的这些树林获取木料。
例：...a single-story timber building.木质平房

N-COUNT
(造船或建屋用的)栋木，大梁
The timbers of a ship or house are the large pieces of wood that have been used to build it.
例：...a bird nestling in the timbers of the roof.栖息于屋顶横梁上的鸟

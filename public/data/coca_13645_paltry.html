ADJ-GRADED
(数量)微小的，微不足道的
A paltry amount of money or of something else is one that you consider to be very small.
例：...a paltry fine of £50...50英镑的小额罚款
例：They suffered an electoral catastrophe, winning a paltry 3 seats.他们在选举中惨败，仅获得微不足道的3个席位。

ADJ-GRADED
可鄙的；卑贱的；不重要的
You can use paltry to describe something or someone that you consider to be small or unimportant.
例：The parents had little interest in paltry domestic concerns.那些家长对家里鸡毛蒜皮的小事没什么兴趣。

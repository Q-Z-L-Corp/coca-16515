coincide 
英[ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd] 美[ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd]
 
vi.与…一致；想法、意见等相同；相符；极为类似；
 "

V-RECIP
巧合;同时发生
If one event coincides with another, they happen at the same time.
例：The exhibition coincides with the 50th anniversary of his death...展览恰好在他逝世50周年之际举行。
例：Although his mental illness had coincided with his war service it had not been caused by it...虽然他的精神病刚好在他参战期间发作，但并不是由此引起的。

V-RECIP
(想法、利益等)一致，相符
If the ideas or interests of two or more people coincide, they are the same.
例：The kids' views on life don't always coincide, but they're not afraid of voicing their opinions...孩子们的人生观并不总是一致，但他们敢于表达自己的观点。
例：...a case in which public and private interests coincide...一个公众利益和私人利益一致的例子

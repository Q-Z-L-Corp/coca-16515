N-VAR
使用不当;误用;滥用；盗用
The misuse of something is incorrect, careless, or dishonest use of it.
例：...the misuse of power and privilege...盗用权力和特权
例：...a misuse of public funds...滥用公共基金

VERB
对…使用不当;误用;滥用;盗用
If someone misuses something, they use it incorrectly, carelessly, or dishonestly.
例：You are protected instantly if a thief misuses your credit card...如果小偷盗用你的信用卡，你的卡会立即受到保护。
例：She misused her position in the appointment of 26,000 party supporters to government jobs.她滥用职权，任命了26,000名党羽在政府部门工作。

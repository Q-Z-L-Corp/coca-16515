reassemble 
英[ˌri:əˈsembl] 美[ˌriəˈsɛmbəl]
 
v.重新组装；重新装配；重新召集；
 "

VERB
重组；重新整合；重新装配
If you reassemble something, you put it back together after it has been taken apart.
例：We will now try to reassemble pieces of the wreckage...我们现在会尽量把残骸重新组合起来。
例：The table had to be taken apart and hoisted through the window before it was reassembled.桌子得被拆开，通过窗户吊进来，然后再重新组装。

V-ERG
重新召集；重新集合
If a group of people reassembles or if you reassemble them, they gather together again in a group.
例：We shall reassemble in the car park in thirty minutes...我们30分钟后在停车场再碰面。
例：Mr Lucas reassembled his team in September.卢卡斯先生在9月重新召集了他的队伍。

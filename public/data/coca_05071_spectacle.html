spectacle 
英[ˈspektəkl] 美[ˈspɛktəkəl]
 
n.眼镜；奇观，壮观；光景，景象；表演，场面；
 "

N-PLURAL
眼镜
Glasses are sometimes referred to as spectacles .
例：He looked at me over the tops of his spectacles.他从眼镜框的上方看了看我。
例：...thick spectacle frames.厚厚的眼镜框

N-COUNT
奇异的景象;有趣的场面
A spectacle is a strange or interesting sight.
例：It was a spectacle not to be missed.这是个不容错过的奇观。

N-VAR
盛大的演出;壮观的场面
A spectacle is a grand and impressive event or performance.
例：94,000 people turned up for the spectacle.94,000 人参加了这一盛大活动。
例：...a director passionate about music and spectacle.对音乐和盛大演出充满激情的指挥

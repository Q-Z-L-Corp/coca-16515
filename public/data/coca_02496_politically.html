politically

 "

ADJ
政治的；政治上的
Political means relating to the way power is achieved and used in a country or society.
例：All other political parties there have been completely banned...那里其他所有政党已被彻底取缔。
例：The Canadian government is facing another political crisis.加拿大政府面临着又一场政治危机。

ADJ-GRADED
对政治感兴趣的；从事政治的
Someone who is political is interested or involved in politics and holds strong beliefs about it.
例：Oh I'm not political, I take no interest in politics...噢，我不关心政治，对政治一点也不感兴趣。
例：This play is very political.这部戏政治味太浓。

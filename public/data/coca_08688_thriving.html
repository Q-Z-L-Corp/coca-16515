thriving 
英[θraɪvɪŋ] 美[θraɪvɪŋ]
 
adj.兴旺的，旺盛的，繁荣的；v.兴盛，兴隆( thrive的现在分词 )；长得健壮；
 "

VERB
欣欣向荣;茁壮成长;兴旺发达
If someone or something thrives, they do well and are successful, healthy, or strong.
例：Today his company continues to thrive...今天，他的公司继续蓬勃发展。
例：Lavender thrives in poor soil.薰衣草在贫瘠的土壤中茁壮生长。

VERB
(尤指在别人觉得困难的时候)以…为乐，可以出色应对
If you say that someone thrives on a particular situation, you mean that they enjoy it or that they can deal with it very well, especially when other people find it unpleasant or difficult.
例：Many people thrive on a stressful lifestyle...有许多人喜欢充满压力的生活方式。
例：Creative people are usually very determined and thrive on overcoming obstacles.富有创造力的人一般都意志非常坚定，能不断克服困难，事业蒸蒸日上。

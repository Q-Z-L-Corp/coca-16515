N-COUNT
一束，一缕(头发等)
A tendril is something light and thin, for example a piece of hair which hangs loose and is away from the main part.
例：Tendrils of hair strayed to the edge of her pillow.她的几缕头发散落在枕边。

N-COUNT
(攀缘植物的)卷须, 蔓
Tendrils are thin stems which grow on some plants so that they can attach themselves to supports such as walls or other plants.

dart 
英[dɑ:t] 美[dɑrt]
 
vt.投掷，投射；使迅速突然移动；vi.向前冲，飞奔；n.飞镖，标枪；急驰，飞奔；（虫的）螯；飞快的移动；
 "

VERB
飞奔;猛冲
If a person or animal darts somewhere, they move there suddenly and quickly.
例：Ingrid darted across the deserted street...英格丽德飞奔过空无一人的街道。
例：The girl turned and darted away through the trees.女孩转身穿过树林飞速跑开了。

VERB
瞥，急扫（一眼)
If you dart a look at someone or something, or if your eyes dart to them, you look at them very quickly.
例：She darted a sly sideways glance at Bramwell...她狡黠地从眼角瞥了布拉姆韦尔一眼。
例：The conductor's eyes darted to Wilfred, then fixed on Michael again.乐队指挥飞快地扫了威尔弗雷德一眼，然后视线又落回到迈克尔身上。

N-COUNT
镖;飞镖
A dart is a small, narrow object with a sharp point which can be thrown or shot.
例：Markov died after being struck by a poison dart.马尔科夫身中毒镖而亡。

N-UNCOUNT
掷镖游戏;飞镖游戏
Darts is a game in which you throw darts at a round board which has numbers on it.

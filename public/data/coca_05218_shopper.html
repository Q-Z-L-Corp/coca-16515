shopper 
英[ˈʃɒpə(r)] 美[ˈʃɑpə(r)]
 
n.（商店的）顾客，买东西的人；
 "

N-COUNT
商店;店铺
A shop is a building or part of a building where things are sold.
例：...health food shops.健康食品商店
例：...a record shop...音像店

VERB
购物;去商店买东西
When you shop, you go to shops and buy things.
例：He always shopped at the Co-op.他一直在合作社买东西。
例：...some advice that's worth bearing in mind when shopping for a new carpet.买新地毯时值得参考的一些建议

N-COUNT
(提供某种服务的)店，店面
You can refer to a place where a particular service is offered as a particular type of shop .
例：...the barber shop where Rodney sometimes had his hair cut.罗德尼有时去理发的那家理发店
例：...betting shops.彩票销售点

N-COUNT
工厂;工场;作坊;车间
You can refer to a place where things are made or done as a particular kind of shop .
例：...the blacksmith's shop.铁匠作坊
例：...a repair shop.修理厂

VERB
(向警察)告发
If you shop someone, you report them to the police for doing something illegal.
例：His father was so disgusted to discover his son was dealing drugs he shopped him to police...他父亲发现儿子在贩毒时深感憎恶，就向警察告发了他。
例：Fraudsters are often shopped by honest friends and neighbours.诈骗犯经常会被正直守法的朋友和邻居告发。

PHRASE
到处;各处
If something is happening all over the shop, it is happening in many different places or throughout a wide area.
例：This gave them the freedom to make trouble all over the shop without fear of retribution.这使他们可以肆意到处惹是生非，而不用担心受到惩罚。

PHRASE
开业;开张
If you set up shop, you start a business.
例：He set up shop as an independent PR consultant...他自己开公司，做起了独立公共关系顾问。
例：He has just set up shop in Cherbourg.他刚刚在瑟堡开业不久。

PHRASE
(商店、办事处、公司)休息，下班，打烊，停业，歇业
When a shop, office, or firm shuts up shop, it stops doing business and closes, either at the end of the day or permanently.
例：If they had been faced with the bill they'd have shut up shop and fled the country.他们要是看到这账单，早就关门歇业逃到国外去了。

PHRASE
谈论本行工作;三句话不离本行
If you say that people are talking shop, you mean that they are talking about their work, and this is boring for other people who do not do the same work.
例：If you hang around with colleagues all the time you just end up talking shop.如果整天和同事泡在一起的话，你最终就会变得开口闭口都是工作。

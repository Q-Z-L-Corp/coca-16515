capitulate 
英[kəˈpɪtʃuleɪt] 美[kəˈpɪtʃəˌlet]
 
vi.认输，屈服；屈从，停止反抗；有条件投降；让步；
 "

VERB
不再反抗;屈服
If you capitulate, you stop resisting and do what someone else wants you to do.
例：The club eventually capitulated and now grants equal rights to women...那家俱乐部最终还是让了步,现在赋予女性以平等权利。
例：In less than two hours Cohen capitulated to virtually every demand.还不到两个小时,科恩就屈服了,接受了几乎一切要求。

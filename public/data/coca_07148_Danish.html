ADJ
丹麦的;丹麦人的;丹麦语（或文化）的
Danish means belonging or relating to Denmark, its people, its language, or culture.
例：...the Danish coast...丹麦海岸
例：He was the son of a Danish father and a Venezuelan mother.他的父亲是丹麦人，母亲是委内瑞拉人。

N-UNCOUNT
丹麦语
Danish is the language spoken in Denmark.

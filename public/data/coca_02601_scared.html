scared 
英[skeəd] 美[skerd]
 
adj.恐惧的；惊恐的；担心的；v.使害怕（scare的过去分词）；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
惊恐的;恐惧的;害怕的
If you are scared of someone or something, you are frightened of them.
例：I'm certainly not scared of him...我当然不怕他。
例：I was too scared to move...我吓得动弹不得。

ADJ-GRADED
担心的;害怕的;焦虑的
If you are scared that something unpleasant might happen, you are nervous and worried because you think that it might happen.
例：I was scared that I might be sick...我担心自己可能病了。
例：He was scared of letting us down.他怕让我们失望。

PHRASE
非常恐惧的;极度害怕的;吓得要死的;吓傻的
If you are scared to death or scared stiff, you are extremely scared.
例：In high school I was scared to death of you...高中时我怕你怕得要命。
例：He's scared stiff of the relationship breaking down.他非常害怕关系破裂。

pernicious 
英[pəˈnɪʃəs] 美[pərˈnɪʃəs]
 
adj.很有害的，恶性的，致命的，险恶的；引起巨大伤害的；毁灭性的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有害的；恶性的
If you describe something as pernicious, you mean that it is very harmful.
例：I did what I could, but her mother's influence was pernicious...我已尽力了，但是她母亲贻害颇深。
例：There is a pernicious culture of excellence: everything has to be not merely good but the best.对于优秀存在着一种认识的误区：一切都务求最好。

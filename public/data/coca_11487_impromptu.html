ADJ
即兴的;事先无准备的
An impromptu action is one that you do without planning or organizing it in advance.
例：This afternoon the Palestinians held an impromptu press conference...今天下午巴勒斯坦方面临时召开了记者招待会。
例：The children put on an impromptu concert for the visitors.孩子们为来访者即兴献上了一场音乐会。

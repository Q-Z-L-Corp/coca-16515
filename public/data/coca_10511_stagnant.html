stagnant 
英[ˈstægnənt] 美[ˈstæɡnənt]
 
adj.不流动的，停滞的；污浊的；不景气的；迟钝的，呆笨的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
停滞不前的;不发展的;无变化的
If something such as a business or society is stagnant, there is little activity or change.
例：He is seeking advice on how to revive the stagnant economy...他正在就如何振兴低迷的经济征求意见。
例：Mass movements are often a factor in the awakening and renovation of stagnant societies.群众性运动经常是能唤醒和振兴停滞不前的社会的一个因素。

ADJ
(水)不流动而污浊的
Stagnant water is not flowing, and therefore often smells unpleasant and is dirty.

N-UNCOUNT
（射出的）枪弹
Gunshot is used to refer to bullets that are fired from a gun.
例：They had died of gunshot wounds.他们死于枪伤。
例：...avoiding the volleys of gunshot.躲开一排排子弹

N-COUNT
枪炮射击；枪炮声
A gunshot is the firing of a gun or the sound of a gun being fired.
例：They heard thousands of gunshots...他们听到密集的枪炮声。
例：A balloon popped, sounding like a gunshot.一只气球爆裂了，听着像是枪响。

passport 
英[ˈpɑ:spɔ:t] 美[ˈpæspɔrt]
 
n.护照，通行证；手段；
 "

N-COUNT
护照
Your passport is an official document containing your name, photograph, and personal details, which you need to show when you enter or leave a country.
例：You should take your passport with you when changing money.兑换货币时应该带着护照。
例：...a South African businessman travelling on a British passport.持英国护照旅行的南非商人

N-COUNT
进身之阶；（获取成功或幸福的）途径，手段
If you say that a thing is a passport to success or happiness, you mean that this thing makes success or happiness possible.
例：Victory would give him a passport to the riches he craves...胜利将使他有机会获得自己梦寐以求的财富。
例：If the interview goes well it could be the passport to an exciting new career.面试顺利的话，将获得令人兴奋的新工作。

cloth 
英[klɒθ] 美[klɔθ]
 
n.布，织物；一块布；用作某种用途的布，桌布，擦布，揩布；（统称）牧师；
 "

N-MASS
(尤用于制衣的)布，织物，布料
Cloth is fabric which is made by weaving or knitting a substance such as cotton, wool, silk, or nylon. Cloth is used especially for making clothes.
例：She began cleaning the wound with a piece of cloth.她开始用一块布清洗伤口。

N-COUNT
(用于特定用途的)布
A cloth is a piece of cloth which you use for a particular purpose, such as cleaning something or covering something.
例：Clean the surface with a damp cloth.用湿布清洁表面。
例：...a tray covered with a cloth.盖着布的托盘

N-SING
(统称)牧师，神父
The cloth is sometimes used to refer to Christian priests and ministers.
例：I've got as much respect for the cloth as the next man.我和任何人一样都对神父充满敬意。
例：...a man of the cloth.一名教士

troubled 
英[ˈtrʌbld] 美[]
 
adj.焦虑的；担忧的；混乱的；存在诸多问题的；v.费神，费心( trouble的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
苦恼的;烦恼的;忧虑的
Someone who is troubled is worried because they have problems.
例：Rose sounded deeply troubled...罗斯听起来颇为烦恼。
例：She was conscious of a troubled expression on Ann's face.她注意到了安脸上露出了苦恼的表情。

ADJ-GRADED
问题多的;动乱的;混乱的
A troubled place, situation, organization, or time has many problems or conflicts.
例：There is much we can do to help this troubled country...我们可以做很多事情来帮助这个动荡不安的国家。
例：Anyone who has been in a troubled relationship will sympathise with Sue…那些曾经恋爱不顺利的人都会同情休。

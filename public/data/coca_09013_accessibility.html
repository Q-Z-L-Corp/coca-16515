accessibility 
英[əkˌsesə'bɪlətɪ] 美[əkˌsesə'bɪlətɪ]
 
n.易接近，可到达；学习资料利用率；可诣达性；可达性；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
易到达的;易进入的;易够到的
If a place or building is accessible to people, it is easy for them to reach it or get into it. If an object is accessible, it is easy to reach.
例：The Centre is easily accessible to the general public...该中心普通大众可随意进入。
例：The premises are wheelchair accessible...该场所轮椅可以自由出入。

ADJ-GRADED
易使用的;易得到的
If something is accessible to people, they can easily use it or obtain it.
例：The legal aid system should be accessible to more people...法律援助制度应该面向更多人。
例：This device helps make virtual reality a more usable and accessible technology.这一装置有助于使虚拟现实成为更可用、更便利的技术。

ADJ-GRADED
可以理解的;易懂的
If you describe a book, painting, or other work of art as accessible, you think it is good because it is simple enough for people to understand and appreciate easily.
例：...literary books that are accessible to a general audience.一般读者都能欣赏的文学作品
例：...their reputation for providing exciting and accessible theatre for young people.他们在为年轻人奉献喜闻乐见的精彩戏剧方面是出了名的

conserve 
英[kənˈsɜ:v] 美[kənˈsɜrv]
 
vt.保护，保藏，保存；[化，物]使守恒；将…做成蜜饯；n.果酱，蜜饯；
 "

VERB
节约;节省
If you conserve a supply of something, you use it carefully so that it lasts for a long time.
例：The republic's factories have closed for the weekend to conserve energy.为了节约能源，该共和国的工厂周末不开工。

VERB
保护;保存
To conserve something means to protect it from harm, loss, or change.
例：...a big increase in US aid to help developing countries conserve their forests.美国为帮助发展中国家保护森林所提供的援助的大幅增长
例：...the Government-funded body responsible for conserving historic buildings.政府资助的历史建筑保护机构

N-MASS
(含有大块或整块水果的)蜜饯，果酱

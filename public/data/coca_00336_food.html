food 
英[fu:d] 美[fud]
 
n.食物，食品；粮食；养料；资料；
 "

N-MASS
食物;食品
Food is what people and animals eat.
例：Enjoy your food.用餐愉快。
例：...supplies of food and water.食物和水的供给

PHRASE
(常指因生病)不想吃东西，厌食
If you are off your food, you do not want to eat, usually because you are ill.
例：It's not like you to be off your food.胃口不好，这可不像你了。

PHRASE
引人深思;发人深省
If you give someone food for thought, you make them think carefully about something.
例：Lord Fraser's speech offers much food for thought...弗雷泽勋爵的讲演发人深省。
例：Developments in your career may give you food for thought.事业上的发展可能会引发你很多的思考。

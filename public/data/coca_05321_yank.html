yank 
英[jæŋk] 美[jæŋk]
 
vt.& vi.快而有力地拉，急拉；[俚语] 突然地移动；n.突然的猛拉；急抽；
 "

VERB
猛拉;猛拽
If you yank someone or something somewhere, you pull them there suddenly and with a lot of force.
例：She yanked open the drawer...她猛地拉开了抽屉。
例：She yanked the child back into the house...她使劲儿把那个孩子拽回了房子里。

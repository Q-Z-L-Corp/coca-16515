main 
英[meɪn] 美[men]
 
adj.主要的，最重要的；全力的；n.最主要的部分，重点；主要管道；体力；[航海]公海；
 "

ADJ
主要的;最重要的
The main thing is the most important one of several similar things in a particular situation.
例：...one of the main tourist areas of Amsterdam...阿姆斯特丹的主要旅游区之一
例：My main concern now is to protect the children...我现在最关心的就是要保护好孩子们。

PHRASE
大体上;基本上
If you say that something is true in the main, you mean that it is generally true, although there may be exceptions.
例：Tourists are, in the main, sympathetic people...大体说来，游客们都富有同情心。
例：In the main, children are taboo in the workplace.工作场所基本上禁止儿童进入。

N-COUNT
(室内煤气、自来水或污水的)总管道，主管道
The mains are the pipes which supply gas or water to buildings, or which take sewage away from them.
例：...the water supply from the mains...总管道的自来水供应
例：The capital has been without mains water since Wednesday night.从周三晚上开始，首都就停水了。

N-PLURAL
电力线;电源
The mains are the wires which supply electricity to buildings, or the place where the wires end inside the building.
例：...amplifiers which plug into the mains...接通电源的扩音器
例：Make sure plugs are disconnected from the mains...确保插头与电源断开。

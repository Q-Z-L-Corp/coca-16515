trend 
英[trend] 美[trɛnd]
 
n.走向；趋向；时尚，时髦；vi.倾向；趋势；
 "

N-COUNT
趋势;倾向;动向
A trend is a change or development towards something new or different.
例：This is a growing trend.这是日益显著的趋势。
例：...a trend towards part-time employment.倾向于兼职就业的趋势

N-COUNT
规范;风尚;时尚
To set a trend means to do something that becomes accepted or fashionable, and that a lot of other people copy.
例：The record has already proved a success and may well start a trend.该唱片已证明大获成功，并且很可能会引导潮流。

ADJ
无人驾驶的；无人操纵的
Unmanned vehicles such as spacecraft do not have any people in them and operate automatically or are controlled from a distance.
例：...a special unmanned spacecraft.一架特别的无人驾驶飞行器
例：...unmanned rockets.自控火箭

ADJ
无人(工作)的
If a place is unmanned, there is nobody working there.
例：The fare from the last unmanned station is probably less than a pound...从上一个无人站过来的费用可能还不到1镑。
例：Unmanned post offices meant millions of letters went unsorted.邮局无人工作意味着许多信件无人分拣。

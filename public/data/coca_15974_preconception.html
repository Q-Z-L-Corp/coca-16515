N-COUNT
事先形成的看法；先入之见；成见
Your preconceptions about something are beliefs formed about it before you have enough information or experience.
例：Did you have any preconceptions about the sort of people who did computing?...你对从事计算机工作的人有什么成见吗？
例：He did not allow his preconceptions to compromise his scientific work.他不允许自己的先入之见破坏自己的科学工作。

VERB
拥护；支持；维护
If you uphold something such as a law, a principle, or a decision, you support and maintain it.
例：Our policy has been to uphold the law...拥护法律是我们一贯的方针。
例：It is the responsibility of every government to uphold certain basic principles.各政府均有责任拥护某些基本原则。

VERB
维持，坚持（原判）
If a court of law upholds a legal decision that has already been made, it decides that it was the correct decision.
例：The crown court, however, upheld the magistrate's decision.然而，最高法院维持了地方法院的判决。

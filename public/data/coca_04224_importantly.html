ADJ-GRADED
重要的;必要的;非常有价值的
Something that is important is very significant, is highly valued, or is necessary.
例：Her sons are the most important thing in her life...她生命中最重要的是她的儿子们。
例：The planned general strike represents an important economic challenge to the government...这场有计划的总罢工是政府面临的一个重大经济挑战。

ADJ-GRADED
有声望的;有影响的;显要的
Someone who is important has influence or power within a society or a particular group.
例：He was the most important person on the island.他是岛上最有声望的人。
例：...an important figure in the media world.传媒界很有影响力的人物

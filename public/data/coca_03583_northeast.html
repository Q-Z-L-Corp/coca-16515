northeast 
英['nɔ:θ'i:st] 美[nɔrθˈist, nɔrˈist]
 
n.东北，东北方向；东北部；adj.在东北的；东北部的；向东北的；（风、光线）来自东北方向的；adv.自东北地；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
东北;东北方
The north-east is the direction which is halfway between north and east.
例：The land to the north-east fell away into meadows.陆地向东北方向倾斜延伸成了牧场。

N-SING
东北部;东北部地区
The north-east of a place, country, or region is the part which is in the north-east.
例：The north-east, with 60 million people, is the most densely populated part of the United States...有6,000万人口的东北部是美国人口最稠密的地区。
例：They're all from Newcastle in the North East of England.他们都来自英格兰东北部的纽卡斯尔。

ADV
向东北
If you go north-east, you travel towards the north-east.
例：The streets were jammed with slow moving traffic, army convoys moving north-east.军车队向东北方向行驶，致使街道上车流缓慢，交通拥挤。

ADV
在东北
Something that is north-east of a place is positioned to the north-east of it.
例：This latest attack was at Careysburg, twenty miles north-east of the capital, Monrovia.最近的这次袭击发生在首都蒙罗维亚东北20英里处的凯里斯堡。

ADJ
在东北的;东北部的;向东北的;面朝东北的
The north-east edge, corner, or part of a place is the part which is towards the north-east.
例：These islands of the north-east coast can only be reached in small boats.东北部沿海的这些岛屿只有坐小船才能抵达。
例：...Waltham Abbey on the north-east outskirts of London.位于伦敦东北市郊的沃尔瑟姆修道院

ADJ
(风)从东北方吹来的
A north-east wind is a wind that blows from the north-east.
例：By 9.15 a bitter north-east wind was blowing.9点15分时刮起了寒冷的东北风。

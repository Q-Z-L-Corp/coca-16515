leather 
英[ˈleðə(r)] 美[ˈlɛðɚ]
 
n.皮，皮革；皮革制品；vt.用皮革包盖；（口）（用皮带等）抽打；制成皮，蒙上皮；adj.皮的，皮革的，皮革制的；
 "

N-MASS
皮革
Leather is treated animal skin which is used for making shoes, clothes, bags, and furniture.
例：He wore a leather jacket and dark trousers.他身穿皮夹克和深色裤子。
例：...an impressive range of upholstered furniture, in a choice of fabrics and leathers.琳琅满目的覆面家具，有多种布料和皮革可供选择

N-PLURAL
（尤指骑摩托车者穿的）皮外套，皮衣
Leathers are leather clothes such as jackets and trousers, especially those worn by motorcyclists.
例：...a couple of youths in motorcyclists' black leathers.两个穿黑色机车皮衣的年轻人

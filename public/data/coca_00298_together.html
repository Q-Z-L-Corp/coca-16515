together 
英[təˈgeðə(r)] 美[təˈɡɛðɚ]
 
adv.同时；在一起；一致地；不间断地；adj.稳定可靠的；做事有效率的；
 "

ADV
一起；共同
If people do something together, they do it with each other.
例：We went on long bicycle rides together...我们一起骑自行车长途旅行。
例：He and I worked together on a book…我和他合写了一本书。

ADV
(接触、结合)到一起
If things are joined together, they are joined with each other so that they touch or form one whole.
例：Mix the ingredients together thoroughly...把配料充分混合。
例：She clasped her hands together on her lap...她十指紧扣，放在膝上。

ADV
(位置)在一起，紧挨地
If things or people are situated together, they are in the same place and very near to each other.
例：The trees grew close together...树木密密地长在一起。
例：Ginette and I gathered our things together...我和吉内特把东西收拢好。

ADV
(团结)在一起
If a group of people are held or kept together, they are united with each other in some way.
例：He has done enough to pull the party together...他为使政党内部团结一致已经做得够多了。
例：I want us all to be a happy family together…我希望我们大家都能在一起，成为一个幸福的家庭。

ADJ
(两人)结婚的，有性关系(或恋爱关系)的
If two people are together, they are married or having a sexual relationship with each other.
例：We were together for five years...我们共同生活了 5 年。
例：Towards the end of our time together he was impossible…到我们在一起的最后那段时间，他简直令人难以忍受。

ADV
同时;一齐
If two things happen or are done together, they happen or are done at the same time.
例：Three horses crossed the finish line together...3 匹马同时越过终点线。
例：'Yes,' they said together.“是的，”他们齐声说。

ADV
合计地;总合地
You use together when you are adding two or more amounts or things to each other in order to consider a total amount or effect.
例：The two main right-wing opposition parties together won 29.8 per cent…两大右翼反对党合计赢得 29.8% 的选票。
例：The companies have together spent £300 million…这些公司总计支出 3 亿英镑。

PHR-RECIP
相配;不可分割
If you say that two things go together, or that one thing goes together with another, you mean that they go well with each other or cannot be separated from each other.
例：I can see that some colours go together and some don't...我看得出有些颜色很相配，有些就不行。
例：Winckelmann declared that art and freedom went together…温克尔曼宣称艺术与自由不可分割。

ADJ-GRADED
自信的;精干的;清醒的
If you describe someone as together, you admire them because they are very confident, organized, and know what they want.
例：She was very headstrong, and very together...她非常倔强，又非常精干。
例：I know on the surface I appear to be quite a together person…我知道表面上我似乎自信能干。

PREP-PHRASE
连同…一起;还有;加之
You use together with to mention someone or something else that is also involved in an action or situation.
例：Every month we'll deliver the very best articles, together with the latest fashion and beauty news...每个月我们都登载最好的文章，还有最新的时尚美容资讯。
例：A famine started which, together with the war, carried away millions of lives…一场饥荒开始了，再加上战争，夺走了几百万条生命。

ADV
（买卖）通过私人地
If you buy or sell something privately, you buy it from or sell it to another person directly, rather than in a shop or through a business.
例：The whole process makes buying a car privately as painless as buying from a garage...整个过程使得从私人手中购买汽车与从汽车库购买一样方便。
例：A great deal of food is distributed and sold privately without ever reaching the shops.很多食物都是通过私人渠道直接配送和销售，根本没有进过商店。

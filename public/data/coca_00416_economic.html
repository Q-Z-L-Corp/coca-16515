economic 
英[ˌi:kəˈnɒmɪk] 美[ˌikəˈnɑmɪk]
 
adj.经济的；经济学的；合算的；有经济效益的；
 "

ADJ
经济的;经济上的
Economic means concerned with the organization of the money, industry, and trade of a country, region, or society.
例：...Poland's radical economic reforms...波兰彻底的经济改革
例：The pace of economic growth is picking up.经济增长的步伐正在加快。

ADJ-GRADED
有利可图的;赚钱的
If something is economic, it produces a profit.
例：The new system may be more economic but will lead to a decline in programme quality.新系统可能更合算，但是会降低程序质量。

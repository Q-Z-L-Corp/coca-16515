designer 
英[dɪˈzaɪnə(r)] 美[dɪˈzaɪnə]
 
n.设计师；设计者；构思者；adj.由专门设计师设计的；标有设计师名字的；时尚的；时髦的；
 "

N-COUNT
设计师;设计者
A designer is a person whose job is to design things by making drawings of them.
例：Carolyne is a fashion designer.卡罗琳是一名时装设计师。

ADJ
(服装、品牌等)由著名设计师设计的，标有设计师姓名的
Designer clothes or designer labels are expensive, fashionable clothes made by a famous designer, rather than being made in large quantities in a factory.
例：He wears designer clothes and drives an antique car...他穿着著名设计师设计的服装，开着一部古董车。
例：People want to buy designer labels for snob value.人们想买名牌是为了满足虚荣心。

ADJ
时尚的;时髦的
You can use designer to describe things that are worn or bought because they are fashionable.
例：Tousled hair and designer stubble are chic...乱蓬蓬的头发和时兴的胡茬都很新潮。
例：Designer beers and trendy wines have replaced the good old British pint.时尚的啤酒和流行的葡萄酒已经取代了老牌的英国啤酒。

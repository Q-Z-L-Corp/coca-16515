heresy 
英[ˈherəsi] 美[ˈhɛrɪsi]
 
n.异端邪说；异教；异端；
 "

N-VAR
（与大多数人的观点不合的）异端，邪说
Heresy is a belief or action that most people think is wrong, because it disagrees with beliefs that are generally accepted.
例：It might be considered heresy to suggest such a notion.提出这样一个观点可能会被视为异端邪说。

N-VAR
（与某种宗教的教义严重不合的）异教，异端，邪说
Heresy is a belief or action which seriously disagrees with the principles of a particular religion.
例：He said it was a heresy to suggest that women should not conduct services.他说认为女性不应该主持宗教仪式的观点是一种异端邪说。

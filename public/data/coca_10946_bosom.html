bosom 
英[ˈbʊzəm] 美[ˈbʊzəm, ˈbuzəm]
 
n.胸部，乳房；衣服的胸襟；家庭般的温暖；胸怀；vt.怀抱；把…藏在心中；adj.心爱的；亲密的；

N-COUNT
（女子的）乳房
A woman's breasts are sometimes referred to as her bosom or her bosoms.
例：...a large young mother with a baby resting against her ample bosom.让怀中婴儿靠在她丰满胸部上的身材高大的年轻母亲

N-SING
怀抱；关怀
If you are in the bosom of your family or of a community, you are among people who love, accept, and protect you.
例：Joan was delighted to welcome her boyfriend into the bosom of her large, close-knit family.琼十分高兴地欢迎她的男朋友加入她亲密无间的大家庭。

ADJ
（朋友）知心的，亲密的
A bosom friend is a friend who you know very well and like very much indeed.
例：They were bosom friends...他们是知心好友。
例：Sakota was her cousin and bosom pal.沙科塔是她的表妹和亲密伙伴。

PHRASE
接纳；关怀；关爱
If you take someone or something to your bosom, you accept them and treat or regard them with great affection.
例：We, at least, have taken her to our bosom.至少我们对她是关爱有加的。

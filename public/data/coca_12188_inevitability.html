inevitability 
英[ɪnˌevɪtə'bɪlətɪ] 美[ɪnˌevɪtə'bɪlətɪ]
 
n.必然性；不可避免性；
 "

N-VAR
不可避免;无法规避;必然发生
The inevitability of something is the fact that it is certain to happen and cannot be prevented or avoided.
例：We are all bound by the inevitability of death...我们早晚都会有一死。
例：Success is an inevitability for us.胜利对我们来说是必然的。

festive 
英[ˈfestɪv] 美[ˈfɛstɪv]
 
adj.节日的，过节似的；喜庆的；欢乐的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
喜庆的;欢乐的;充满节日气氛的
Something that is festive is special, colourful, or exciting, especially because of a holiday or celebration.
例：The town has a festive holiday atmosphere...镇子上充盈着一种节日的喜庆气氛。
例：The Captain's Party on Saturday evening is the cruise's most festive event.周六晚上的船长晚会是游轮上最喜庆的活动。

ADJ
与节日有关的;与节庆有关的;(尤指)与圣诞节有关的
Festive means relating to a holiday or celebration, especially Christmas.
例：The factory was due to shut for the festive period...节日期间工厂将关门。
例：The government's armed forces have halved their strength for the festive break.由于节日休假，政府武装部队人数减半。

n.猪油；vt.润色；涂猪油于；将肥猪肉片嵌于（牛肉或禽肉）中；（在讲话或文章中）夹杂大量（某类词语）；

N-UNCOUNT
猪油
Lard is soft white fat obtained from pigs. It is used in cooking.
例：...lard or beef fat.猪油或牛油

VERB
（在言谈或文章中）充斥，大量夹杂（某类词语）
If speech or writing is larded with particular types of words, it contains a lot of them or too many of them.
例：Official speeches in recent days have been larded with promises of democracy.近日官方在讲话中满口对民主的承诺。
例：...a long phone call, larded with 'darlings' and a sickening amount of baby-talk.一句一个“亲爱的”且充斥着令人反胃的儿语情话的电话粥

伯父；叔叔；舅舅；姑父；姨父
Someone's uncle is the brother of their mother or father, or the husband of their aunt.
例：My uncle was the mayor of Memphis...我伯父是孟菲斯市市长。
例：A telegram from Uncle Fred arrived...弗雷德舅舅发的一封电报来了。

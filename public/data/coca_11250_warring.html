warring 
英[ˈwɔ:rɪŋ] 美['wɔrɪŋ]
 
adj.交战的，进行战争的；敌对的；冲突的；势不两立的；n.战斗，战争；v.进行战争(war的现在分词形式)；

ADJ
交战的;敌对的;冲突的
Warring is used to describe groups of people who are involved in a conflict or quarrel with each other.
例：The warring factions have not yet turned in all their heavy weapons.敌对双方尚未将其重型武器悉数上缴。
例：...warring husbands and wives.打打闹闹的夫妻

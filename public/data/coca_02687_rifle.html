rifle 
英[ˈraɪfl] 美[ˈraɪfəl]
 
n.步枪；来福枪；vt.& vi.快速搜寻；匆忙翻找 ；偷窃；盗取；
 "

N-COUNT
来复枪;步枪
A rifle is a gun with a long barrel.
例：They shot him at point blank range with an automatic rifle...他们用自动步枪近距离开枪射杀了他。
例：Neighbours heard the sound of rifle fire and alerted the police.邻居们听到了步枪声，就报了警。

VERB
快速翻找;搜寻
If you rifle through things or rifle them, you make a quick search among them in order to find something or steal something.
例：I discovered my husband rifling through the filing cabinet...我发现我丈夫正在档案柜里匆匆翻找着什么东西。
例：The men rifled through his clothing and snatched the wallet...那些人匆匆翻了翻他的衣服，然后一把抢走了钱包。

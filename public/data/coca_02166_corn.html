corn

N-UNCOUNT
麦类作物;谷物;谷粒
Corn is used to refer to crops such as wheat and barley. It can also be used to refer to the seeds from these plants.
例：...fields of corn...麦田
例：He filled the barn to the roof with corn.他将谷仓装满了谷物。

N-UNCOUNT
同maize
Corn is the same as maize .
例：...rows of corn in an Iowa field.艾奥瓦州农田里成排的玉米

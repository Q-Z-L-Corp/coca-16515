eventual 
英[ɪˈventʃuəl] 美[ɪˈvɛntʃuəl]
 
adj.（事件）最终发生的，结果的；可能的；终于的；
 "

ADJ
最后发生的;最终的
You use eventual to indicate that something happens or is the case at the end of a process or period of time.
例：There are many who believe that civil war will be the eventual outcome of the racial tension in the country...有很多人相信，该国种族关系的紧张最终将导致内战。
例：The eventual aim is reunification.最终目标是实现统一。

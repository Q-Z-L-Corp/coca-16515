VERB
过多（或过度）使用；滥用
If someone overuses something, they use more of it than necessary, or use it more often than necessary.
例：A nasal spray or drops would ease your symptoms, but don't overuse them or further congestion could result...鼻腔喷剂或滴剂能减轻症状，但请勿使用过频，否则鼻塞可能会加重。
例：Don't overuse heated appliances on you hair.不要过多地使用电热器具弄头发。

VERB
滥用（词语等）；使成为陈词滥调
If you say that people overuse a word or idea, you mean that they use it so often that it no longer has any real meaning or effect.
例：Which words or phrases do you most overuse?你最为常用的词或词组有哪些？

ADJ
（尤指一国）货币的，金融的
Monetary means relating to money, especially the total amount of money in a country.
例：Some countries tighten monetary policy to avoid inflation...一些国家实行紧缩银根的货币政策，以避免通货膨胀。
例：The courts will be asked to place a monetary value on his unfinished career.将要求法院对他未竟的事业进行资产评估。

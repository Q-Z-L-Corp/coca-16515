N-COUNT
（政治）议题
You can refer to the political issues which are important at a particular time as an agenda.
例：Does television set the agenda on foreign policy?...电视舆论对外交政策有影响吗？
例：Many of the coalition members could have their own political agendas...许多联盟成员可能会有他们自己的政治议题。

N-COUNT
议事日程
An agenda is a list of the items that have to be discussed at a meeting.
例：This is sure to be an item on the agenda next week...这一定会成为下星期议事日程上的一项议题。
例：High on the agenda of tomorrow's meeting will be the turmoil in Japan.日本发生的骚乱将是明天会议的首要议题。

N-COUNT
必然的想法(或结果);直接推论
A corollary of something is an idea, argument, or fact that results directly from it.
例：The number of prisoners increased as a corollary of the government's determination to combat violent crime.政府决心打击暴力犯罪，直接导致了囚犯数量的增多。

servant 
英[ˈsɜ:vənt] 美[ˈsɜrvənt]
 
n.仆人，佣人，雇工；[美]奴仆，奴隶；官吏，公务员，服务员；随员，献身…的人；
 "

N-COUNT
仆人;用人
A servant is someone who is employed to work at another person's home, for example as a cleaner or a gardener.

N-COUNT
提供服务的人(或事物);可以利用的人(或事物)
You can use servant to refer to someone or something that provides a service for people or can be used by them.
例：Like any other public servants, police must respond to public demand...和其他所有公务员一样，警察必须服务于公众的需求。
例：The question is whether technology is going to be our servant or our master.问题是科技将为我们所用还是要驾驭我们。

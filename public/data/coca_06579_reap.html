reap 
英[ri:p] 美[rip]
 
vt.& vi.收割；获得；得到（报偿）；
 "

VERB
获得；收获；得到；取得
If you reap the benefits or the rewards of something, you enjoy the good things that happen as a result of it.
例：You'll soon begin to reap the benefits of being fitter...你很快就会体会到身体更健康带来的好处。
例：We are not in this to reap immense financial rewards.我们从事这一行不是为了获得巨大的经济回报。

VERB
收割，收获（农作物）
To reap crops means to cut them down and gather them.
例：The painting depicted a group of peasants reaping a harvest of fruits and vegetables.这幅画描绘了一群农民收获水果和蔬菜的情景。

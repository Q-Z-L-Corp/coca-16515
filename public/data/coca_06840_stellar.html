ADJ
星的;星球的
Stellar is used to describe anything connected with stars.
例：A stellar wind streams outward from the star.从那颗恒星上不断吹出恒星风。

ADJ-GRADED
杰出的;优秀的;出色的
A stellar person or thing is considered to be very good.
例：...a stellar education at Eton and Oxford...伊顿和牛津的精英教育
例：The French companies are registering stellar profits.法国各家公司都获利丰厚。

smoking 
英[ˈsməʊkɪŋ] 美[ˈsmoʊkɪŋ]
 
n.吸烟，抽烟；v.冒烟( smoke的现在分词)；抽烟；用烟熏制；吸（烟）；

N-UNCOUNT
吸烟;抽烟
Smoking is the act or habit of smoking cigarettes, cigars, or a pipe.
例：Smoking is now banned in many places of work.现在许多工作场所禁止吸烟。
例：...a no-smoking area.禁烟区

ADJ
允许吸烟的
A smoking area is intended for people who want to smoke.
例：...the decision to scrap smoking compartments on Kent trains.取消肯特列车上的吸烟车厢的决定

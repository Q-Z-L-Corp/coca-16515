avenue 
英[ˈævənju:] 美[ˈævənu]
 
n.林荫路；大街；途径，手段；〈美〉（南北向）街道；
 "

N-IN-NAMES
（有时用于街名）大街，街道（书面缩略形式为Ave.）
Avenue is sometimes used in the names of streets. The written abbreviation Ave. is also used.
例：...the most expensive stores on Park Avenue.帕克大街上最贵的商店

N-COUNT
大道；（尤指）林阴大道
An avenue is a wide, straight road, especially one with trees on either side.

N-COUNT
方法;途径
An avenue is a way of getting something done.
例：Talbot was presented with 80 potential avenues of investigation...给塔尔博特提出了80种进行调查的可能途径。
例：There is another avenue to pursue — it involves further negotiations.还有一个办法可用，但需要进一步的磋商。

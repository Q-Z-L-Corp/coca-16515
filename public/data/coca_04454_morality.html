morality 
英[məˈræləti] 美[məˈrælɪti, mɔ-]
 
n.道德；道德准则；品德高尚的行为；道德观；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
道德；道义;伦理
Morality is the belief that some behaviour is right and acceptable and that other behaviour is wrong.
例：...standards of morality and justice in society.社会道德和正义的标准
例：...an effort to preserve traditional morality.维护传统道德的努力

N-COUNT
道义准则;道德规范;伦理学
A morality is a system of principles and values concerning people's behaviour, which is generally accepted by a society or by a particular group of people.
例：...a morality that is sexist.存在性别歧视的道德规范
例：...communities and their shared moralities.社区和其共同的道义准则

N-UNCOUNT
道德性；道德
The morality of something is how right or acceptable it is.
例：...the arguments about the morality of blood sports.有关猎杀动物行为是否道德的争论

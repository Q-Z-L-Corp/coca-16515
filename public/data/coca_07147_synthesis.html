synthesis 
英[ˈsɪnθəsɪs] 美[ˈsɪnθɪsɪs]
 
n.综合；<化>合成；综合体；综合推理；
 "

N-COUNT
综合;结合;结合体
A synthesis of different ideas or styles is a mixture or combination of these ideas or styles.
例：His novels are a rich synthesis of Balkan history and mythology...他的小说融合了大量巴尔干半岛的历史和神话故事。
例：Her synthesis of feminism and socialism ran counter to all other historical currents.她将女权主义和社会主义的结合与所有其他的历史潮流背道而驰。

N-VAR
(通过化学或生物反应进行的)合成
The synthesis of a substance is the production of it by means of chemical or biological reactions.
例：...the genes that regulate the synthesis of these compounds...控制这些化合物合成的基因
例：This kind of lighting encourages vitamin D synthesis in the skin.这种光照会促进皮肤中维生素 D 的合成。

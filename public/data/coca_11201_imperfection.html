imperfection 
英[ˌɪmpəˈfekʃn] 美[ˌɪmpərˈfekʃn]
 
n.不完美；缺点，瑕疵；
 "

N-VAR
缺点;弱点;缺陷
An imperfection in someone or something is a fault, weakness, or undesirable feature that they have.
例：By his own statement, there have been imperfections in his judgment.据他自己声称,他的判断存在不足之处。
例：I was obsessed by my physical imperfections.我的身体缺陷令我很苦恼。

N-COUNT
污点;瑕疵
An imperfection in something is a small mark or damaged area which may spoil its appearance.
例：...imperfections in the cloth.衣料上的瑕疵

powerful 
英[ˈpaʊəfl] 美[ˈpaʊərfl]
 
adj.强大的；权力大的；（药）有效地；adv.很；非常；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有权力的；有势力的；有影响力的
A powerful person or organization is able to control or influence people and events.
例：You're a powerful man — people will listen to you.你是有影响力的人，大家会听你的。
例：...Russia and India, two large, powerful countries.两大强国，俄罗斯和印度

ADJ-GRADED
强壮的；强健的
You say that someone's body is powerful when it is physically strong.
例：Hans flexed his powerful muscles...汉斯活动了一下他那强健的肌肉。
例：It's such a big powerful dog.它是一条如此强壮的大狗。

ADJ-GRADED
（机器）大功率的；强效的；高效能的
A powerful machine or substance is effective because it is very strong.
例：The more powerful the car the more difficult it is to handle.汽车的功率越大，就越难驾驭。
例：...powerful computer systems...功能强大的计算机系统

ADJ-GRADED
（气味）强烈的，浓的
A powerful smell is very strong.
例：There was a powerful smell of stale beer.有一种呛人的过期啤酒味。
例：...tiny creamy flowers with a powerful scent.散发浓香的淡黄色小花

ADJ-GRADED
（声音）响亮的，洪亮的
A powerful voice is loud and can be heard from a long way away.
例：At that moment Mrs. Jones's powerful voice interrupted them, announcing a visitor.就在那一刻，琼斯夫人响亮的声音打断了他们，说有人来访。

ADJ-GRADED
（文章、演讲或艺术品）极具感染力的，有震撼力的
You describe a piece of writing, speech, or work of art as powerful when it has a strong effect on people's feelings or beliefs.
例：...Bleasdale's powerful 11-part drama about a corrupt city leader.布利兹戴尔创作的关于一个腐败市领导的极具感染力的电视剧，共有11集
例：...one of the world's most powerful and moving operas, Verdi's 'Otello'.威尔第创作的《奥泰罗》，世界最具感染力、最动人的歌剧之一

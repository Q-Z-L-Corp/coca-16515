V-ERG
（使）团结；（使）联合
If a group of people or things unite or if something unites them, they join together and act as a group.
例：The two parties have been trying to unite since the New Year...这两个党从新年开始就试图联合。
例：The vast majority of nations have agreed to unite their efforts to bring peace.绝大多数国家都同意团结起来为实现和平而努力。

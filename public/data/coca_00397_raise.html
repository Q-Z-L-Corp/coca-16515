raise 
英[reɪz] 美[rez]
 
v.提升；增加；养育；筹集；n.提升；增加；高处；举起；
 "

VERB
举起；抬高
If you raise something, you move it so that it is in a higher position.
例：He raised his hand to wave...他举手挥动起来。
例：She went to the window and raised the blinds...她走到窗前，拉起了百叶窗。

VERB
升起，举起（旗帜）
If you raise a flag, you display it by moving it up a pole or into a high place where it can be seen.
例：They had raised the white flag in surrender...他们举白旗投降了。
例：At midnight, the German flag will be raised over the Reichstag.半夜时，德国国旗将在德国国会大厦升起。

VERB
起身；站立
If you raise yourself, you lift your body so that you are standing up straight, or so that you are no longer lying flat.
例：He raised himself into a sitting position...他坐了起来。
例：She raised herself on one elbow.她用一只胳膊肘撑起身体。

VERB
增加；提高
If you raise the rate or level of something, you increase it.
例：The Republic of Ireland is expected to raise interest rates...爱尔兰共和国预计会提高利率。
例：Two incidents in recent days have raised the level of concern.最近发生的两起事件引发了更多人的关注。

VERB
提高（水平）；改善
To raise the standard of something means to improve it.
例：...a new drive to raise standards of literacy in Britain's schools.提高英国学校文化水平的新运动

VERB
（常指因发怒而）提高（嗓门）
If you raise your voice, you speak more loudly, usually because you are angry.
例：Don't you raise your voice to me, Henry Rollins!...别冲我叫嚷，亨利·罗林斯！
例：Anne raised her voice in order to be heard.为了让人能听得见，安妮提高了嗓门。

N-COUNT
加薪；提薪
A raise is an increase in your wages or salary.
例：Within two months Kelly got a raise.不到两个月，凯利就获得了加薪。

VERB
（为慈善组织、机构等）募捐，筹（款）
If you raise money for a charity or an institution, you ask people for money which you collect on its behalf.
例：...events held to raise money for Help the Aged...为“帮助老年人”活动而发起的募捐活动
例：All funds raised will be used by Children With Leukaemia.所有募集到的资金都将用于“白血病患儿”组织。

VERB
筹集，筹措（资金）
If a person or company raises money that they need, they manage to get it, for example by selling their property or by borrowing.
例：They raised the money to buy the house and two hundred acres of grounds.他们筹钱来购买房屋及200英亩的土地。

VERB
引起;唤起;激起
If an event raises a particular emotion or question, it makes people feel the emotion or consider the question.
例：The agreement has raised hopes that the war may end soon...那项协议使人们感到战争有望很快结束。
例：The accident again raises questions about the safety of the plant.这起事故再一次引发了人们对于工厂安全性的质疑。

VERB
提起，提出（问题、反对等）
If you raise a subject, an objection, or a question, you mention it or bring it to someone's attention.
例：In the meeting Mrs. Ashrawi raised the three main concerns that the Palestinians had...会议上，阿什拉维夫人提出了巴勒斯坦人最为关注的三个问题。
例：He had been consulted and had raised no objections.事先征求了他的意见，他并没有提出异议。

VERB
养育；抚养
Someone who raises a child looks after it until it is grown up.
例：My mother was an amazing woman. She raised four of us kids virtually singlehandedly.我的母亲是位了不起的女性。她几乎是一个人把我们4个孩子带大的。
例：...the house where she was raised.她在其间长大的那幢房子

VERB
饲养；种植
If someone raises a particular type of animal or crop, they breed that type of animal or grow that type of crop.
例：He raises 2,000 acres of wheat and hay.他种植了2,000英亩的小麦和牧草。
例：...a perfectly cooked farm-raised chicken.精心烹制的农场养的鸡

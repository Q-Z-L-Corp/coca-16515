straight 
英[streɪt] 美[stret]
 
adj.直的；连续的；直率的；整齐的；adv.直地；直接地；坦率地；立即；n.直线；直线部分；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
直的;笔直的
A straight line or edge continues in the same direction and does not bend or curve.
例：Keep the boat in a straight line...让船保持直线航行。
例：Using the straight edge as a guide, trim the cloth to size...用直尺比照着，将布料裁剪成合适的尺寸。

ADJ-GRADED
(头发)直的，不卷曲的
Straight hair has no curls or waves in it.
例：Grace had long straight dark hair which she wore in a bun.格雷斯把一头长长的黑色直发盘成发髻。

ADV
笔直地;径直地
You use straight to indicate that the way from one place to another is very direct, with no changes of direction.
例：The ball fell straight to the feet of Klinsmann...球直接落到了克林斯曼的脚上。
例：He finished his conversation and stood up, looking straight at me...他说完话站起来，直视着我。

ADV
立刻;马上
If you go straight to a place, you go there immediately.
例：As always, we went straight to the experts for advice...像往常一样，我们马上去找专家咨询。
例：We'll go to a meeting in Birmingham and come straight back.我们将去伯明翰参加会议，然后马上回来。

ADJ-GRADED
直截了当的;直率的;坦诚的
If you give someone a straight answer, you answer them clearly and honestly.
例：What a shifty arguer he is, refusing ever to give a straight answer.他真是个狡诈的辩论家，总是拒绝给出直截了当的回答。

ADJ
连续的;不间断的
Straight means following one after the other, with no gaps or intervals.
例：They'd won 12 straight games before they lost.他们在输掉比赛之前已连续赢了12场。

ADJ
只涉及两人(或两物)的;(选择)非此即彼的
A straight choice or a straight fight involves only two people or things.
例：It's a straight choice between low-paid jobs and no jobs...只有两个选择：要么是报酬很低的工作，要么就是失业。
例：Each has several times beaten the other in a straight fight.双方都曾在单打比赛中击败过对方几次。

ADJ-GRADED
正经的;正统的;规矩本分的
If you describe someone as straight, you mean that they are normal and conventional, for example in their opinions and in the way they live.
例：Dorothy was described as a very straight woman, a very strict Christian who was married to her job.多萝西被形容成一个非常正统的女人，一个尽职尽责、非常虔诚的基督徒。

ADJ
异性恋的;非同性恋的
If you describe someone as straight, you mean that they are heterosexual rather than homosexual.
例：His sexual orientation was a lot more gay than straight...从他的性取向来看，他不像异性恋，更像个同性恋。
例：Marty of New York describes herself as a straight female.纽约的马蒂称自己不是女同性恋者。

ADJ
(尤指酒精饮料)纯的，不掺水的
A straight drink, especially an alcoholic drink, has not had another liquid such as water added to it.
例：...a large straight whiskey without ice...不加冰的大杯纯威士忌
例：Children should not drink fruit juices straight.儿童不应该喝纯果汁。

N-COUNT
(赛道的)直道(部分)
On a racetrack, a straight is a section of the track that is straight, rather than curved.
例：Our cars were clearly too slow along the straights...我们的赛车在直道上跑得显然太慢了。
例：I went to overtake him on the back straight on the last lap.最后一圈时我在非终点直道上超过了他。

PHRASE
把…弄清楚;明确
If you get something straight, you make sure that you understand it properly or that someone else does.
例：You need to get your facts straight...你需要讲清楚你的情况。
例：Let's get things straight. I didn't lunch with her.咱们把事情说清楚。我没有和她一起吃午饭。

PHRASE
改过自新;改邪归正;重新做人
If a criminal is going straight, they are no longer involved in crime.

PHRASE
走正路;过正当的生活
If something keeps people on the straight and narrow, it helps to keep them living an honest or healthy life.
例：All her efforts to keep him on the straight and narrow have been rewarded.她为了让他走正道所作出的一切努力都得到了回报。

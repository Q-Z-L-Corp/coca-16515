EXCLAM
（表示惊讶）天哪，啊呀
People sometimes say 'goodness' or 'my goodness' to express surprise.
例：Goodness, I wonder if he knows...天哪，不知道他是否晓得。
例：My goodness, he's earned millions in his career.我的天哪，他干那一行已挣了好几百万了。

N-UNCOUNT
善良；美德
Goodness is the quality of being kind, helpful, and honest.
例：He retains a faith in human goodness.他依然相信人是善良的。

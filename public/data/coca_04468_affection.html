affection 
英[əˈfekʃn] 美[əˈfɛkʃən]
 
n.喜爱，慈爱；情感或感情；意向；疾病，病情；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
喜爱；喜欢
If you regard someone or something with affection, you like them and are fond of them.
例：She thought of him with affection...她很想念他。
例：She had developed quite an affection for the place.她逐渐对这个地方钟爱有加。

N-PLURAL
爱慕；钟爱之情
Your affections are your feelings of love or fondness for someone.
例：The distant object of his affections is Caroline...他一厢情愿地爱慕卡罗琳。
例：Jack Russell has a special place in the affections of the cricketing public.杰克·拉塞尔在打板球的人心中有着特殊的地位。

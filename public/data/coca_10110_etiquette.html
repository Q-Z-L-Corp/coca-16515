 "

N-UNCOUNT
（尤指特定阶层的）礼仪，礼节;（尤指特定行业的）行为规范，规矩
Etiquette is a set of customs and rules for polite behaviour, especially among a particular class of people or in a particular profession.
例：This was such a great breach of etiquette, he hardly knew what to do.这是严重失礼的行为，他几乎手足无措。
例：...the rules of diplomatic etiquette.外交礼节的规则

ADJ-GRADED
喜欢感官享受的;耽于肉欲的
Someone or something that is sensual shows or suggests a great liking for physical pleasures, especially sexual pleasures.
例：He was a very sensual person...他是个登徒子。
例：Clothing doesn't need to be overt to be sensual.衣着没有必要过于性感。

ADJ-GRADED
肉欲的;愉悦感官的
Something that is sensual gives pleasure to your physical senses rather than to your mind.
例：It was an opera, very glamorous and very sensual.这场歌剧美轮美奂，声色俱佳,给感官以极大享受。
例：...sensual dance rhythms.给人以快感的舞蹈节奏

VERB
重新设置;重新调试
If you reset a machine or device, you adjust or set it, so that it is ready to work again or ready to perform a particular function.
例：As soon as you arrive at your destination, step out of the aircraft and reset your wristwatch.你一到达目的地，就走出飞机并重新设置手表时间。

VERB
(骨头)复位，重新接上
If a doctor resets a broken bone, they put it back into its correct position.
例：He is recovering from an operation to reset his arm.他做了一个手臂复位手术，正在恢复。

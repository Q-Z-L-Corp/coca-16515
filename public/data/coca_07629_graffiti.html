N-UNCOUNT-COLL
（在公共场所的）涂画，涂鸦
Graffiti is words or pictures that are written or drawn in public places, for example on walls or posters.
例：Buildings old and new are thickly covered with graffiti...新老建筑上都有密密麻麻的涂鸦。
例：There's no vandalism, no graffiti, no rubbish left lying about.没有损害公共财物的行为，也没有涂鸦和随处丢弃的垃圾。

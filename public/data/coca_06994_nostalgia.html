nostalgia 
英[nɒˈstældʒə] 美[nəˈstældʒə]
 
n.对往事的怀恋；怀旧；乡愁；怀乡病；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(尤指对极幸福时光的)怀念;怀旧;念旧
Nostalgia is an affectionate feeling you have for the past, especially for a particularly happy time.
例：He might be influenced by nostalgia for his happy youth...也许是对年轻时幸福时光的怀恋影响了他。
例：He discerned in the novel an air of Sixties nostalgia.他在小说里感受到一种20世纪60年代的怀旧氛围。

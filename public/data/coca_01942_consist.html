consist 
英[kənˈsɪst] 美[kənˈsɪst]
 
vi.由…组成；在于；符合；
 "

VERB
由…组成;由…构成
Something that consists of particular things or people is formed from them.
例：Breakfast consisted of porridge served with butter.早餐是麦片粥配佐餐黄油。
例：Her crew consisted of children from Devon and Cornwall.她的团队由来自德文郡和康沃尔郡的孩子们组成。

VERB
在于;存在于
Something that consists in something else has that thing as its main or only part.
例：His work as a consultant consisted in advising foreign companies on the siting of new factories.作为一名顾问，他的工作是为外国公司在新厂选址问题上提供建议。
例：...Baudelaire's idea that genius consists in the ability to summon up childhood.波德莱尔认为天才在于唤起童年的能力的观点

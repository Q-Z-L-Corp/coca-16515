cleverly 
英['klevəlɪ] 美[ˈklɛvɚlɪ]
 
adv.聪明地；灵巧地；机敏地；巧妙地；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
聪明的;聪颖的;智商高的
Someone who is clever is intelligent and able to understand things easily or plan things well.
例：He's a very clever man...他非常聪明。
例：My sister was always a lot cleverer than I was...我妹妹向来比我聪明许多。

ADJ-GRADED
(观点、书、发明)巧妙的，绝妙的
A clever idea, book, or invention is extremely effective and shows the skill of the people involved.
例：...a clever and gripping novel.构思巧妙、扣人心弦的小说
例：A colleague of mine in Milan devised the following very clever little experiment.我在米兰的一个同事设计了下面这个非常巧妙的小实验。

PHRASE
聪明过头的;过分聪明的
If someone is too clever by half, they are very clever and they show their cleverness in a way that annoys other people.
例：His many admirers describe him as clever: his enemies as too clever by half.崇拜他的人很多说他聪明;恨他的人则说他聪明过了头。

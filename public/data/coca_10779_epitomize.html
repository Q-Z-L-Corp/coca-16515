VERB
成为…的典范;作为…的缩影
If you say that something or someone epitomizes a particular thing, you mean that they are a perfect example of it.
例：Lyonnais cooking is epitomized by the so-called 'bouchons'.被称作bouchon的里昂小餐馆是里昂饮食的缩影。

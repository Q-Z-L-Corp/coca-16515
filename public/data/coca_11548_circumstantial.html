circumstantial 
英[ˌsɜ:kəmˈstænʃl] 美[ˌsɜrkəmˈstænʃl]
 
adj.（指描述）详细的；（指证据）有充分细节却无法证实的；
 "

ADJ
(证据)间接的;佐证的
Circumstantial evidence is evidence that makes it seem likely that something happened, but does not prove it.
例：Fast work by the police in Birmingham had started producing circumstantial evidence.快速开展工作的伯明翰警方已经开始发现间接证据。

ADJ
视情况而定的;与特定情况有关的
Something that is circumstantial is related to a particular circumstance.
例：The reasons for the project collapsing were circumstantial.工程垮掉系环境所迫。

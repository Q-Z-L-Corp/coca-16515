penchant 
英['pɒ̃ʃɒ̃] 美[ˈpɛntʃənt]
 
n.（强烈的）倾向，爱好，嗜好；
 "

N-SING
爱好；嗜好；偏好
If someone has a penchant for something, they have a special liking for it or a tendency to do it.
例：...a stylish woman with a penchant for dark glasses...喜欢戴墨镜的时髦女子
例：He had a penchant for playing jokes on people.他喜欢拿人开玩笑。

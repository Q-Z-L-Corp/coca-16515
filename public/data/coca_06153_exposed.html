ADJ-GRADED
暴露于风雨的;无遮蔽措施的
If a place is exposed, it has no natural protection against bad weather or enemies, for example because it has no trees or is on very high ground.
例：...an exposed hillside in Connecticut...康涅狄格一处暴露于风雨中的小山坡
例：This part of the west coast of Scotland is very exposed to Atlantic winds.苏格兰西海岸的这一段完全暴露在大西洋海风前。

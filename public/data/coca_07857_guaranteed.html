ADJ
有保证的；确定的
If you say that something is guaranteed to happen, you mean that you are certain that it will happen.
例：Reports of this kind are guaranteed to cause anxiety...这种报道势必会引发忧虑。
例：It's guaranteed that my colleagues think I'm deranged...同事们一定以为我疯了。

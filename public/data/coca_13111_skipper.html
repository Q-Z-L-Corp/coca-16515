skipper 
英[ˈskɪpə(r)] 美[ˈskɪpɚ]
 
n.船长；队长；机长；跳跃者；vt.担任船长、球队队长等；
 "

N-COUNT; N-VOC
船长
You can use skipper to refer to the captain of a ship or boat.
例：...the skipper of an English fishing boat...一艘英国渔船的船长
例：Gunfire, skipper!开火，船长！

N-COUNT
(体育运动队的)队长
You can use skipper to refer to the captain of a sports team.
例：The England skipper is confident.英格兰队的队长信心十足。

VERB
当…的队长(或船长)
To skipper a team or a boat means to be the captain of it.
例：He skippered the second Rugby XV...他担任了橄榄球 15 人联队的第二任队长。
例：The yacht was skippered by Pierre Mas.这艘游艇的船长是皮埃尔·马斯。

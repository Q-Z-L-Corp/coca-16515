N-UNCOUNT
无能;无力;不能
If you refer to someone's inability to do something, you are referring to the fact that they are unable to do it.
例：Her inability to concentrate could cause an accident.她无法集中精力可能会导致事故。
例：...the government's inability to provide basic services.政府无力提供基本的公共服务

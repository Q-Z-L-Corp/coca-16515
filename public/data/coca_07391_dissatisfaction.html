dissatisfaction 
英[ˌdɪsˌsætɪsˈfækʃn] 美[dɪsˌsætɪsˈfækʃən]
 
n.不满，不平；令人不满的事物；
 "

N-VAR
不满;不悦
If you feel dissatisfaction with something, you are not contented or pleased with it.
例：She has already expressed her dissatisfaction with this aspect of the policy...她对政策的这一方面已经表达了不满。
例：Low pay is the main cause of job dissatisfaction among teachers.低薪是教师对工作不满的主要原因。

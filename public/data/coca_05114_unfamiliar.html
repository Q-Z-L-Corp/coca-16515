ADJ-GRADED
不熟悉的；陌生的
If something is unfamiliar to you, you know nothing or very little about it, because you have not seen or experienced it before.
例：She grew many wonderful plants that were unfamiliar to me...她种了很多我不熟悉的奇妙植物。
例：I was alone in an unfamiliar city.我在一个陌生的城市里形单影只。

ADJ-GRADED
不熟知的；不了解的
If you are unfamiliar with something, it is unfamiliar to you.
例：She speaks no Japanese and is unfamiliar with Japanese culture.她不会说日语，也不了解日本文化。

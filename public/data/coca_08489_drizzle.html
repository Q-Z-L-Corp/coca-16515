drizzle 
英[ˈdrɪzl] 美['drɪzl]
 
vi.下蒙蒙细雨，下毛毛雨；n.蒙蒙细雨，毛毛雨；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
毛毛雨;蒙蒙细雨
Drizzle is light rain falling in fine drops.
例：The drizzle had now stopped and the sun was breaking through.毛毛雨现在已经停了,太阳正破云而出。

VERB
下毛毛雨;下蒙蒙细雨
If it is drizzling, it is raining very lightly.
例：Clouds had come down and it was starting to drizzle...云层压低,开始飘起了毛毛雨。
例：I walked home in the drizzling rain.我冒着毛毛雨走回了家。

VERB
(在食物上)滴，淋，洒
If you drizzle a liquid over food or drizzle food with a liquid, you pour a small quantity of the liquid all over the food.
例：Drizzle the remaining dressing over the duck and salad...将剩下的调料淋在鸭肉和色拉上。
例：Drizzle them with warmed extra virgin olive oil.将加热过的特级初榨橄榄油淋在上面。

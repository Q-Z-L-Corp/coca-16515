ADJ-GRADED
不感兴趣的；不关心的
If you are uninterested in something or someone, you do not want to know any more about them, because you think they have no special or exciting qualities.
例：I was so uninterested in the result that I didn't even bother to look at it.我对结果完全没兴趣，甚至都懒得看一眼。
例：...unhelpful and uninterested shop staff.既没用又冷漠的店员

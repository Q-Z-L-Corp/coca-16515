N-COUNT
（出售三明治或报纸等的）售货亭，小亭
A kiosk is a small building or structure from which people can buy things such as sandwiches or newspapers through an open window.
例：I was getting cigarettes at the kiosk.我正在售货亭买烟。

N-COUNT
公用电话亭
A kiosk or a telephone kiosk is a public telephone box.
例：He phoned me from a kiosk.他在公用电话亭给我打了电话。

N-UNCOUNT
(总称)座椅，座位
You can refer to the seats in a place as the seating .
例：The stadium has been fitted with seating for over eighty thousand spectators.这个体育场配备了8万多个观众座席。

N-UNCOUNT
(公共场所、正式场合的)座位安排(或设置)
The seating at a public place or a formal occasion is the arrangement of where people will sit.
例：She checked the seating arrangements before the guests filed into the dining-room.在客人们鱼贯进入餐厅之前，她核实了一遍座位安排。

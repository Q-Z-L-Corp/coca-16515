N-COUNT
(农场储存草料、谷物等的)筒仓
A silo is a tall round metal tower on a farm, in which grass, grain, or some other substance is stored.
例：Before silos were invented, cows gave less milk during winter because they had no green grass to eat.筒仓发明前，因为没有青草可以吃，奶牛在冬天产奶量减少。
例：...a grain silo.盛谷物的筒仓

N-COUNT
(核导弹等的)发射井
A silo is a specially built place underground where a nuclear missile is kept.

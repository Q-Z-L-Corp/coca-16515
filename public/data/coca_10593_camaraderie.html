camaraderie 
英[ˌkæməˈrɑ:dəri] 美[ˌkɑməˈrɑdəri]
 
n.同志之爱，友情；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
同志间的感情;友情
Camaraderie is a feeling of trust and friendship among a group of people who have usually known each other for a long time or gone through some kind of experience together.
例：...the family camaraderie in Italy.在意大利的家人之间的亲情
例：...the loyalty and camaraderie of the wartime Army.战时部队里的忠诚和战友情

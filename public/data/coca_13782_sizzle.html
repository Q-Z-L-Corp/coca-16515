VERB
(热油、脂肪等)发出咝咝声
If something such as hot oil or fat sizzles, it makes hissing sounds.
例：The sausages and burgers sizzled on the barbecue.香肠和牛肉饼在烤架上咝咝作响。
例：...a frying pan of sizzling oil.油烧得咝咝作响的平底锅

N-COUNT
少女;姑娘
A maiden is a young girl or woman.
例：...stories of noble princes and their brave deeds on behalf of beautiful maidens.高贵的王子和他们英雄救美的故事

ADJ
(航行、飞行等)初次的，首次的
The maiden voyage or flight of a ship or aircraft is the first official journey that it makes.
例：In 1912, the Titanic sank on her maiden voyage.1912年，“泰坦尼克”号在处女航中失事沉没。

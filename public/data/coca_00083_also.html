also 
英[ˈɔ:lsəʊ] 美[ˈɔlsoʊ]
 
adv.也；同样；并且；conj.另外；
 "

ADV
而且;还;此外
You can use also to give more information about a person or thing, or to add another relevant fact.
例：It is the work of Ivor Roberts-Jones, who also produced the statue of Churchill in Parliament Square...这是艾弗·罗伯茨-琼斯的作品，他还创作了议会广场上的丘吉尔塑像。
例：He is an asthmatic who was also anaemic...他是个曾患贫血症的哮喘患者。

ADV
也；亦
You can use also to indicate that something you have just said about one person or thing is true of another person or thing.
例：His father, also a top-ranking officer, had perished during the war...他的父亲，也是位高级军官，在战争中牺牲了。
例：We have been working very hard, and our families have also worked hard...我们一直很努力工作，并且我们的家人也是如此。

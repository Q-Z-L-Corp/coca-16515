slither 
英[ˈslɪðə(r)] 美[ˈslɪðɚ]
 
vi.滑动；滑行；vt.使滑动；n.滑动，滑行；
 "

VERB
(摇摇晃晃地)滑行，滑动
If you slither somewhere, you slide along in an uneven way.
例：Robert lost his footing and slithered down the bank.罗伯特失足滑下了河岸。

VERB
(蛇等)蜿蜒而行
If an animal such as a snake slithers, it moves along in a curving way.
例：The snake slithered into the water.蛇滑入了水中。

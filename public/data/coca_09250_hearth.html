hearth 
英[hɑ:θ] 美[hɑrθ]
 
n.灶台，炉边；（高炉的）炉膛，炉缸；〈比喻〉家庭；壁炉地面；
 "

N-COUNT
壁炉地面;壁炉炉床
The hearth is the floor of a fireplace, which sometimes extends into the room.
例：It was winter and there was a huge fire roaring in the hearth.时值冬天，壁炉里炉火熊熊。

PHRASE
温暖舒适的家庭生活
A person's home and family life can be referred to as their hearth and home .
例：...a man who leaves his hearth and home to labour as a miner in the inhospitable north.离开温暖舒适的家到荒凉的北方做矿工的男子

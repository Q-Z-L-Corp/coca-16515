indoctrination 
英[ɪnˌdɒktrɪ'neɪʃn] 美[ɪnˌdɑktrɪˈneʃən]
 
n.教导，教化；
 "

VERB
向…灌输(信仰等)
If people are indoctrinated, they are taught a particular belief with the aim that they will reject other beliefs.
例：They have been completely indoctrinated...他们完全被洗脑了。
例：I wouldn't say that she was trying to indoctrinate us.我不认为她在竭力向我们灌输一种信仰。

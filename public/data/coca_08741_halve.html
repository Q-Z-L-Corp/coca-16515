halve 
英[hɑ:v] 美[hæv]
 
vt.把…分成两半；把…减半；对分；平摊；
 "

V-ERG
把…减半;减到一半
When you halve something or when it halves, it is reduced to half its previous size or amount.
例：Dr Lee believes that men who exercise can halve their risk of cancer of the colon...李医生相信，锻炼身体能使患结肠癌的风险降低一半。
例：The work force has been halved in two years...劳动力在两年里减少了一半。

VERB
将…对半分;把…二等分
If you halve something, you divide it into two equal parts.
例：Halve the pineapple and scoop out the inside.将菠萝对半切开，挖出里面的果肉。

（half 的复数）
Halves is the plural of half.

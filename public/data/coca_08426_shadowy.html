shadowy 
英[ˈʃædəʊi] 美[ˈʃædoʊi]
 
adj.朦胧的；似影子的，模糊的；虚无的；影影绰绰；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
阴暗的;幽暗的;被阴影笼罩的
A shadowy place is dark or full of shadows.
例：I watched him from a shadowy corner.我躲在一个阴暗的角落里观察他。
例：...a broad, shadowy room.一间宽敞阴暗的房间

ADJ-GRADED
昏暗的;模糊的;蒙的
A shadowy figure or shape is someone or something that you can hardly see because they are in a dark place.
例：...a tall, shadowy figure silhouetted against the pale wall.灰白的墙上映出一个高大而模糊的人影
例：...the shadowy shape of a big barge loaded with logs.装满木材的巨大驳船的模糊轮廓

ADJ
鲜为人知的;神秘莫测的
You describe activities and people as shadowy when very little is known about them.
例：...the shadowy world of spies.间谍的神秘世界
例：...a shadowy group calling itself the People's Army.一个自称为“人民军队”的神秘组织

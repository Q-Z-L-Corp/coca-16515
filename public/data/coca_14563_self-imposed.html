ADJ
自己强加的;自愿承担的;自己规定的
A self-imposed restriction, task, or situation is one that you have deliberately created or accepted for yourself.
例：He returned home in the summer of 1974 after eleven years of self-imposed exile...经历了 11 年的自我流放后，他于1974 年夏回归故里。
例：Sometimes a well-defined job becomes cluttered with self-imposed tasks.有时一项职责明确的工作会因一些自愿承担的任务而乱作一团。

prototype 
英[ˈprəʊtətaɪp] 美[ˈproʊtətaɪp]
 
n.原型，雏形，蓝本；
 "

N-COUNT
原型；样品；样本
A prototype is a new type of machine or device which is not yet ready to be made in large numbers and sold.
例：Chris Retzler has built a prototype of a machine called the wave rotor.克里斯·雷茨勒已制造出了一种叫波转子的机器的样机。
例：...the first prototype aircraft.第一架飞机样机

N-COUNT
模范；典型
If you say that someone or something is a prototype of a type of person or thing, you mean that they are the first or most typical one of that type.
例：He was the prototype of the elder statesman.他是老一辈政治家的典型。

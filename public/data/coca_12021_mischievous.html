mischievous 
英[ˈmɪstʃɪvəs] 美[ˈmɪstʃəvəs]
 
adj.淘气的；恶作剧的；有害的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
调皮的;淘气的;恶作剧的
A mischievous person likes to have fun by playing harmless tricks on people or doing things they are not supposed to do.
例：She rocks back and forth on her chair like a mischievous child...她像个顽皮的孩子似的，坐在椅子上来回摇晃。
例：He's a little mischievous.他有一点淘气。

ADJ-GRADED
恶意的;不友善的;捣乱的
A mischievous act or suggestion is intended to cause trouble.
例：...a mischievous campaign by the press to divide the ANC...媒体恶意分裂非洲国民大会的运动
例：The Foreign Office dismissed the story as mischievous and false.外交部驳斥该报道为恶意报道，不符合事实。

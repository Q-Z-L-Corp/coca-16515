wholesale 
英[ˈhəʊlseɪl] 美[ˈhoʊlseɪl]
 
n.批发；趸售，大规模买卖；adj.批发的，整批卖的；大规模的；adv.大量地，大批地；广泛地，大规模地；vt.批发；vi.经营批发业；批发售出；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
批发;趸售
Wholesale is the activity of buying and selling goods in large quantities and therefore at cheaper prices, usually to shopkeepers who then sell them to the public.
例：Warehouse clubs allow members to buy goods at wholesale prices...仓储式会员店允许会员以批发价购买商品。
例：I am in the wholesale trade.我做批发生意。

ADV
用批发方式;以批发价
If something is sold wholesale, it is sold in large quantities and at cheaper prices, usually to shopkeepers.
例：The fabrics are sold wholesale to retailers, fashion houses, and other manufacturers.这些纺织品被批发给零售商、时装店和其他制造商。

ADJ
大规模的;大批的
You use wholesale to describe the destruction, removal, or changing of something when it affects a very large number of things or people.
例：They are only doing what is necessary to prevent wholesale destruction of vegetation...他们只是在采取必要措施避免大规模的植被破坏。
例：It was wholesale slaughter of women and children.那是对妇女和儿童的大规模屠杀。

mercifully 
英[ˈmɜ:sɪfəli] 美[ˈmɜrsɪfəli]

 "

ADV
幸运地;有福气地;幸好
You can use mercifully to show that you are glad that something good has happened, or that something bad has not happened or has stopped.
例：Mercifully, a friend came to the rescue...幸好一个朋友赶来解了围。
例：Bolivia has been mercifully free of large-scale, drug-related violence.幸运的是，玻利维亚没有发生过与毒品有关的大规模暴力事件。

dancer 
英[ˈdɑ:nsə(r)] 美[ˈdænsə(r)]
 
n.跳舞者，舞蹈演员；舞女；舞蹈家；
 "

N-COUNT
舞蹈演员;舞蹈家;跳舞者
A dancer is a person who earns money by dancing, or a person who is dancing.
例：His previous girlfriend was a dancer with the Royal Ballet...他的前女友是皇家芭蕾舞团的舞蹈演员。
例：The dancers began to walk away from the floor.跳舞的人们开始离开舞池。

N-COUNT
（出色或蹩脚的）舞者
If you say that someone is a good dancer or a bad dancer, you are saying how well or badly they can dance.
例：He was the best dancer in LA.他是洛杉矶舞跳得最好的人。

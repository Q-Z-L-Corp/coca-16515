barbecue 
英[ˈbɑ:bɪkju:] 美[ˈbɑrbɪkju]
 
n.烤架；户外烧烤；v.烧烤 ；
 "

N-COUNT
（户外烧烤用的）烤肉架
A barbecue is a piece of equipment which you use for cooking on in the open air.

N-COUNT
烧烤野餐
If someone has a barbecue, they cook food on a barbecue in the open air.

VERB
烧烤（尤指烤肉）
If you barbecue food, especially meat, you cook it on a barbecue.
例：Tuna can be grilled, fried or barbecued...金枪鱼可以用烤炉烤、油炸或者烧烤。
例：Here's a way of barbecuing corn-on-the-cob that I learned in the States.这是我在美国学的一种烤玉米的方法。

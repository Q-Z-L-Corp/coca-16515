ADJ-GRADED
古怪的;怪异的
If you say that someone is eccentric, you mean that they behave in a strange way, and have habits or opinions that are different from those of most people.
例：He is an eccentric character who likes wearing a beret and dark glasses.他人很怪，喜欢戴贝雷帽和墨镜。
例：...Mr Thomas, a businessman with eccentric views.托马斯先生，一个想法怪异的商人

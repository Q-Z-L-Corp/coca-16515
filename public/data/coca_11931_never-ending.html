ADJ
永无止境的;永无休止的;永恒的
If you describe something bad or unpleasant as never-ending, you are emphasizing that it seems to last a very long time.
例：...a never-ending series of scandals...一系列无休止的丑闻
例：The spiral of terrorism becomes never-ending.恐怖主义活动没完没了，不断升级。

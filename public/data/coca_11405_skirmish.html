N-COUNT
小规模战斗;接触战
A skirmish is a minor battle.
例：Border skirmishes between India and Pakistan were common.印度和巴基斯坦边境上常常发生小规模战斗。

V-RECIP
发生小冲突;进行小规模战斗;打斗
If people skirmish, they fight.
例：They were skirmishing close to the minefield now...他们当时正在雷区附近交火。
例：Police skirmished with youths on the estate last Friday.上周五警方与一些青年在住宅区发生了冲突。

N-COUNT
小争执;口角;争论;冲突
A skirmish is a short, sharp argument.
例：This difference in approach has led to several political skirmishes.这种方式上的差异已经导致了几场政治冲突。

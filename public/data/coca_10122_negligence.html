N-UNCOUNT
疏忽;失误;失职
If someone is guilty of negligence, they have failed to do something which they ought to do.
例：The soldiers were ordered to appear before a disciplinary council on charges of negligence.士兵们被控玩忽职守，被勒令接受纪律委员会的审查。

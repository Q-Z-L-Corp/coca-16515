miracle 
英[ˈmɪrəkl] 美[ˈmɪrəkəl]
 
n.奇迹，圣迹，神迹；令人惊奇的人（或事）；
 "

N-COUNT
奇迹;出人意料的事
If you say that a good event is a miracle, you mean that it is very surprising and unexpected.
例：It is a miracle no one was killed...出人意料的是，没有人死亡。
例：The Italian economic miracle has always been a mystery...意大利的经济奇迹一直是个谜。

ADJ
(药物或产品)神奇的，不可思议的
A miracle drug or product does something that was thought almost impossible.
例：...a miracle drug that is said to be a cure for Aids and cancer.据说可以治愈艾滋病和癌症的神药

N-COUNT
(上帝创造的)神迹，圣迹
A miracle is a wonderful and surprising event that is believed to be caused by God.
例：...the miracle of the Virgin Birth.童贞女生子的神迹
例：...Jesus's ability to perform miracles.耶稣创造奇迹的能力

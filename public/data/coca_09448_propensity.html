propensity 
英[prəˈpensəti] 美[prəˈpɛnsɪti]
 
n.倾向，习性，癖好，偏爱；
 "

N-COUNT
（性格上的）倾向；习性
A propensity to do something or a propensity for something is a natural tendency that you have to behave in a particular way.
例：Mr Bint has a propensity to put off decisions to the last minute...宾特先生习惯拖到最后时刻才作决定。
例：She hasn't reckoned on his propensity for violence.她不曾料到他有暴力倾向。

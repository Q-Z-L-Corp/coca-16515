flair 
英[fleə(r)] 美[fler]
 
n.天资；天分；眼光；鉴别力；
 "

N-SING
天资;天赋;天分
If you have a flair for a particular thing, you have a natural ability to do it well.
例：...a friend who has a flair for languages.有语言天赋的朋友

N-UNCOUNT
才华;资质;创意
If you have flair, you do things in an original, interesting, and stylish way.
例：Their work has all the usual punch, panache and flair you'd expect.他们的作品一如既往地引人入胜、气韵潇洒、新颖别致，不会让你失望。

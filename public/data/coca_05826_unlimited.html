ADJ
无限的；（数量）极大的
If there is an unlimited quantity of something, you can have as much or as many of that thing as you want.
例：An unlimited number of copies can still be made from the original...仍可以由原件复印出无数份副本来。
例：You'll also have unlimited access to the swimming pool...进泳池的次数是没有限制的。

ADJ-GRADED
毫无…的;没有…的
If you say that someone or something is devoid of a quality or thing, you are emphasizing that they have none of it.
例：I have never looked on a face that was so devoid of feeling...我从来没有见过一张如此面无表情的脸。
例：The skies are virtually devoid of birdlife.天空中几乎没有任何鸟的踪迹。

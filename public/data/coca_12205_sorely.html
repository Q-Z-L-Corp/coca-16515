ADV-GRADED
(用于强调情感或需要的强烈程度)很，非常
Sorely is used to emphasize that a feeling such as disappointment or need is very strong.
例：I for one was sorely disappointed.我本人就感到特别失望。
例：...the potential to earn sorely needed money for Britain from overseas orders...通过海外订单为英国赚取其迫切需要的资金的可能性

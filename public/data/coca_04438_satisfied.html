satisfied 
英[ˈsætɪsfaɪd] 美[ˈsætɪsˌfaɪd]
 
adj.满意的，满足的；清偿过的；确信的，毫无疑问的；v.使满意( satisfy的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
满意的;满足的;称心的
If you are satisfied with something, you are happy because you have got what you wanted or needed.
例：We are not satisfied with these results.我们对这些结果并不满意。
例：...satisfied customers.心满意足的顾客

ADJ-GRADED
确信的;信服的
If you are satisfied that something is true or has been done properly, you are convinced about this after checking it.
例：People must be satisfied that the treatment is safe.人们必须确信这种治疗方法是安全的。

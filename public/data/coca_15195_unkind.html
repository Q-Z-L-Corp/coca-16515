ADJ-GRADED
不仁慈的；不友善的；不厚道的；刻薄的
If someone is unkind, they behave in an unpleasant, unfriendly, or slightly cruel way. You can also describe someone's words or actions as unkind .
例：All last summer he'd been unkind to her...去年整个夏天他都对她很恶劣。
例：No one has an unkind word to say about him...所有人说起他来都没有一句坏话。

ADJ-GRADED
（遭遇）残忍的，无情的
If you describe something bad that happens to someone as unkind, you mean that they do not deserve it.
例：The weather was unkind to those pipers who played in the morning.坏天气对上午那些风笛吹奏者毫不容情。

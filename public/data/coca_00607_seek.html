seek 
英[si:k] 美[sik]
 
vt.寻找，探寻；追求，谋求；往或朝…而去；[废语]考察；vi.查找，查寻；找一找；
 "

VERB
寻找;物色
If you seek something such as a job or a place to live, you try to find one.
例：They have had to seek work as labourers...他们只好找体力活儿干。
例：Four people who sought refuge in the Italian embassy have left voluntarily...4 名在意大利大使馆寻求避难的人已经自愿离开了。

VERB
谋求;谋取;寻求
When someone seeks something, they try to obtain it.
例：The prosecutors have warned they will seek the death penalty...检控方律师已警告说他们会努力争取判处死刑。
例：Haemophiliacs are seeking compensation for being given contaminated blood.血友病患者们因为被输入了受到污染的血液而正在寻求赔偿。

VERB
请求(帮助);征求(意见)
If you seek someone's help or advice, you contact them in order to ask for it.
例：Always seek professional legal advice before entering into any agreement...在签订任何协定之前一定要先征求法律专业人士的意见。
例：On important issues, they seek a second opinion...在重要问题上，他们会征求别人的看法。

VERB
力图;想方设法
If you seek to do something, you try to do it.
例：He also denied that he would seek to annex the country...他还否认会试图吞并该国。
例：Moscow is seeking to slow the growth of Russian inflation.俄罗斯政府正力图遏制本国的通货膨胀。

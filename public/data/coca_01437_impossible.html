impossible 
英[ɪmˈpɒsəbl] 美[ɪmˈpɑsəbl]
 
adj.不可能的，做不到的；难以忍受的；不会有的，不能相信的；n.不可能；不可能的事；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不可能的;做不到的
Something that is impossible cannot be done or cannot happen.
例：It was impossible for anyone to get in because no one knew the password...因为没人知道密码,所以任何人都不可能进得去。
例：He thinks the tax is impossible to administer...他认为税金没法管理。

ADJ
极难应付的
An impossible situation or an impossible position is one that is very difficult to deal with.
例：The Government was now in an almost impossible position.政府现在几乎陷入了进退维谷的境地。

ADJ-GRADED
难以忍受的;让人受不了的
If you describe someone as impossible, you are annoyed that their bad behaviour or strong views make them difficult to deal with.
例：The woman is impossible, thought Frannie.弗兰妮想,这个女人真让人受不了。

nutty 
英[ˈnʌti] 美[ˈnʌti]
 
adj.多坚果的，产坚果的；坚果般的；<俚>古怪的，狂热的，发疯的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
有坚果味的;坚果般的
If you describe food as nutty, you mean that it tastes of nuts, has the texture of nuts, or is made with nuts.
例：...nutty butter cookies...坚果味牛油曲奇
例：Chick peas have a distinctive, delicious and nutty flavour.鹰嘴豆有独特可口的坚果风味。

ADJ-GRADED
发疯的;疯疯癫癫的;痴呆的
If you describe someone as nutty, you mean that their behaviour is very strange or foolish.
例：He looked like a nutty professor...他看上去像个疯疯癫癫的教授。
例：That's a nutty idea.这是个疯狂的想法。

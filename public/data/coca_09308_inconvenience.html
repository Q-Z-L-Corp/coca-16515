inconvenience 
英[ˌɪnkənˈvi:niəns] 美[ˌɪnkənˈvinjəns]
 
n.不方便；麻烦；为难之处；麻烦事；vt.给…带来不便；给…添麻烦；打扰；使为难；
 "

N-VAR
不便;麻烦;困难
If someone or something causes inconvenience, they cause problems or difficulties.
例：We apologize for any inconvenience caused during the repairs...我们为维修期间造成的任何不便道歉。
例：The practical inconveniences of long hair are negligible.长发在实际生活中产生的不便可以忽略不计。

VERB
给…带来不便;麻烦;打扰
If someone inconveniences you, they cause problems or difficulties for you.
例：He promised to be quick so as not to inconvenience them any further.他许诺尽快完成，以免给他们带来更多不便。

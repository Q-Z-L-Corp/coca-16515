mastery 
英[ˈmɑ:stəri] 美[ˈmæstəri]
 
n.精通，熟练；统治，控制；优势；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
精通;掌握
If you show mastery of a particular skill or language, you show that you have learned or understood it completely and have no difficulty using it.
例：He doesn't have mastery of the basic rules of grammar...他还没有完全掌握基本的语法规则。
例：He demonstrated his mastery of political manoeuvring.他表现出了高超的政治手段。

N-UNCOUNT
统治;控制
Mastery is power or control over something.
例：Mesopotamia was probably the first region of the world where humans gained mastery over major rivers.美索不达米亚地区可能是世界上人类最早征服主干河流的地区。

adj.过激的，极端的；n.过激论者，急进论者；
 "

PREFIX
（置于形容词之前构成形容词，用以强调）表示“超…的”，“极其…的”
Ultra- is added to adjectives to form other adjectives that emphasize that something or someone has a quality to an extreme degree.
例：...a wide range of ultra-modern equipment.一大批超现代设备
例：...an ultra-ambitious executive.雄心勃勃的主管

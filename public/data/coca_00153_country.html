country

N-COUNT
国;国家
A country is one of the political units which the world is divided into, covering a particular area of land.
例：Indonesia is the fourth most populous country in the world.印度尼西亚是世界上第四大人口大国。
例：...that disputed boundary between the two countries...两国之间存有争议的边境地区

N-SING
全国人民;国民;全民
The people who live in a particular country can be referred to as the country .
例：The country had confounded the pundits by electing a fourth-term Tory government...该国国民连续四次选举托利党来执掌政府，这令专家们困惑不已。
例：Seventy per cent of this country is opposed to blood sports.该国70%的人反对猎兽活动。

N-SING
乡下;乡村
The country consists of places such as farms, open fields, and villages which are away from towns and cities.
例：...a healthy life in the country...乡下的健康生活
例：She was cycling along a country road near Compiegne...她正骑车行进在贡比涅附近的乡村道路上。

N-UNCOUNT
(具有某种特征或与某名人有关的)地区，区域
A particular kind of country is an area of land which has particular characteristics or is connected with a particular well-known person.
例：Varese Ligure is a small town in mountainous country east of Genoa.瓦雷泽古雷是位于热那亚以东山区的一座小城。
例：...some of the best walking country in the Sierras...喜艾拉生态保护区内一些最适宜散步的地区

N-UNCOUNT
(源自美国南部的)乡村音乐
Country music is popular music from the southern United States.
例：For a long time I just wanted to play country music.有很长一段时间我只想弹奏乡村音乐。
例：...a famous country singer named Katie Cocker.一位名为凯蒂·科克尔的著名乡村乐歌手

PHRASE
穿过田野;不走大路
If you travel across country, you travel through country areas, avoiding major roads and towns.
例：From here we walked across country to Covington.我们从这儿走乡间小路去卡温顿。

PHRASE
长途;长距离;跨越地区地
If you travel across country, you travel a long distance, from one part of a country to another.
例：We've just moved all the way across country to begin a new life.我们刚刚经过长途的搬迁来这里开始一种新生活。

PHRASE
(政府首脑或政府)举行大选
If a head of government or a government goes to the country, they hold a general election.
例：The Prime Minister does not have to go to the country for another year.首相明年也不用举行大选。

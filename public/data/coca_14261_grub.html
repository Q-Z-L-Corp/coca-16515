grub 
英[grʌb] 美[ɡrʌb]
 
n.蛆，幼虫；食物；做苦工的人；邋遢人；vi.翻掘；搜寻；挖土；vt.挖掘寻找；将某物挖出；根除；费力查出；
 "

N-COUNT
（昆虫的）幼虫，蛆
A grub is a young insect which has just come out of an egg and looks like a short fat worm.

N-UNCOUNT
食物；吃的
Grub is food.
例：Get yourself some grub and come and sit down.你自己去盛些吃的，过来坐下吃。

VERB
翻找；搜寻
If you grub around, you search for something.
例：I simply cannot face grubbing through all this paper.我简直受不了要翻阅这么多文件。

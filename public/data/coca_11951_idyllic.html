idyllic 
英[ɪˈdɪlɪk] 美[aɪˈdɪlɪk]
 
adj.田园诗般的；牧歌的；质朴宜人的；平和欢畅的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
安恬的;田园诗般的;质朴宜人的
If you describe something as idyllic, you mean that it is extremely pleasant, simple, and peaceful without any difficulties or dangers.
例：...an idyllic setting for a summer romance...适于夏日恋曲的质朴宜人的环境
例：Married life was not as idyllic as he had imagined.婚姻生活并不像他想象的那么浪漫美好。

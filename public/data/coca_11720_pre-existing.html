ADJ
先前存在的；先于…而存在的
A pre-existing situation or thing exists already or existed before something else.
例：...the pre-existing tensions between the two countries.两国之间已经存在的紧张局势
例：...people who have been infected in the course of their NHS treatment for a pre-existing illness.因已患疾病接受国民健康保障治疗但在治疗过程中被感染的人

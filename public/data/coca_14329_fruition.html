N-UNCOUNT
实现;完成;取得成果
If something comes to fruition, it starts to succeed and produce the results that were intended or hoped for.
例：These plans take time to come to fruition...这些方案需要时间才能取得成果。
例：His hopes for a new political party have little chance of reaching fruition.他期盼出现一个新政党的愿望几乎不可能实现。

VERB
(通常因做错事而)使无资格，取消…的资格
When someone is disqualified, they are officially stopped from taking part in a particular event, activity, or competition, usually because they have done something wrong.
例：He was convicted of corruption, and will be disqualified from office for seven years...他被判犯有贪污罪，以后7年里将无资格担任公职。
例：The stewards conferred and eventually decided to disqualify us.负责人商议之后，最终决定取消我们的资格。

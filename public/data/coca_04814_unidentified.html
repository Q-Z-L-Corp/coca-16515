unidentified 
英[ˌʌnaɪˈdentɪfaɪd] 美[]
 
adj.不能辨认的；来路[身分]不明的；未经确认的；
 "

ADJ
未被认出的；未被识别的
If you describe someone or something as unidentified, you mean that nobody knows who or what they are.
例：He was shot this morning by unidentified intruders at his house.他今天早上在家中被身份不明的闯入者射杀。
例：...unidentified cancer-causing substances in the environment.环境中未知的致癌物质

ADJ
不愿透露姓名（或名称）的
If you use unidentified to describe people, groups, and organizations, you do not want to give their names.
例：...his claims, which were based on the comments of anonymous and unidentified sources.他的说法是基于不知姓名且身份不明的消息人士的话

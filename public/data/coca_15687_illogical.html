illogical 
英[ɪˈlɒdʒɪkl] 美[ɪˈlɑdʒɪkl]
 
adj.不合逻辑的；无意义的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
不合逻辑的;有悖逻辑的
If you describe an action, feeling, or belief as illogical, you are critical of it because you think that it does not result from a logical and ordered way of thinking.
例：It is illogical to oppose the repatriation of economic migrants...反对把经济移民遣送归国是不合逻辑的。
例：She may think your reaction is crazy or illogical, but she cannot deny what you are feeling.她可能觉得你的反应疯狂或者不合逻辑,但她不会否认你的感受。

expectant 
英[ɪkˈspektənt] 美[ɪkˈspɛktənt]
 
adj.期待的，预期的，期望的；n.[数]期望值；期待着；候选人；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
期待的;期望的
If someone is expectant, they are excited because they think something interesting is about to happen.
例：An expectant crowd gathered...满怀期待的人群聚在一起。
例：She turned to me with an expectant look on her face.她转身望着我，脸上带着期待的表情。

ADJ
即将当（妈妈或爸爸）的
An expectant mother or father is someone whose baby is going to be born soon.

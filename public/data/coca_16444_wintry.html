wintry 
英[ˈwɪntri] 美[ˈwɪntri]
 
adj.冬天的；冬天似的；寒冷的；风雪交加的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
冬天的;冬令的;寒冷的
Wintry weather is cold and has features that are typical of winter.
例：Wintry weather continues to sweep across Britain...寒冷的天气继续席卷整个英国。
例：A wintry wind was blowing.寒风呼啸。

ADJ-GRADED
(态度或行为)冷冰冰的，冷淡的
If you describe someone's attitude or behaviour as wintry, you mean that they seem very unfriendly.
例：He was, according to witnesses, extremely wintry with Her Royal Highness.据目击者称，他对女王殿下极其冷淡。

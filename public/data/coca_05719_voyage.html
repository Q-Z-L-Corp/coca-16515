voyage 
英[ˈvɔɪɪdʒ] 美[ˈvɔɪɪdʒ]
 
n.旅行，航海，航空；远行；旅程；vi.航行，旅行；vt.驶过，横越；
 "

N-COUNT
旅行;航行;航海;航天
A voyage is a long journey on a ship or in a spacecraft.
例：He aims to follow Columbus's voyage to the West Indies.他打算重走哥伦布到达西印度群岛的航行路线。
例：...the first space shuttle voyage to be devoted entirely to astronomy.首次完全以天文研究为目的的航天飞行

VERB
旅行;（尤指）航海旅行
To voyage to a place means to travel there, especially by sea.
例：The Greenpeace flagship is voyaging through the Arctic cold of the Barents Sea.绿色和平组织的旗舰正在穿越巴伦支海的北极寒冷地带。

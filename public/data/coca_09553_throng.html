throng 
英[θrɒŋ] 美[θrɔŋ]
 
n.人群，大群人；众多；vt.& vi.群集；蜂拥而至；挤满；
 "

N-COUNT
一大群人;人群
A throng is a large crowd of people.
例：An official pushed through the throng.一名官员费力地挤过人群。

VERB
(人群)涌向，蜂拥
When people throng somewhere, they go there in great numbers.
例：The crowds thronged into the mall.人群涌进大厅。
例：...the multitudes that throng around the Pope.拥在教皇周围的教众甚多

VERB
挤满;群集
If people throng a place, they are present there in great numbers.
例：They throng the beaches between late June and early August.6 月底到 8 月初，他们群聚在海滩上。

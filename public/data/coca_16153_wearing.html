 "

ADJ-GRADED
令人疲倦的;使人身心疲惫的
If you say that a situation or activity is wearing, you mean that it requires a lot of energy and makes you feel mentally or physically tired.
例：She finds the continual confrontation very wearing...她发现持续的对峙让人精疲力竭。
例：Being in demand can be rather wearing.受欢迎也可能是件累人的事。

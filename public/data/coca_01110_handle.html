handle 
英[ˈhændl] 美[ˈhændl]
 
n.（织物、毛皮等的）手感；手柄；举动；柄状物；vi.操作，操控；容易搬运；vt.用双手触摸、举起或握住；用手操作，操纵；处理或负责，管理；〈美〉买卖，经营；
 "

N-COUNT
（门上的）拉手，把手
A handle is a small round object or a lever that is attached to a door and is used for opening and closing it.
例：I turned the handle and found the door was open.我转了一下门把手，发现门开着。

N-COUNT
（工具、包、杯等的）柄，把手，提手
A handle is the part of an object such as a tool, bag, or cup that you hold in order to be able to pick up and use the object.
例：The handle of a cricket bat protruded from under his arm.一个板球拍柄从他的胳肢窝下面伸了出来。
例：...a broom handle.扫帚柄

VERB
有能力应付;（成功地）处理
If you say that someone can handle a problem or situation, you mean that they have the ability to deal with it successfully.
例：To tell the truth, I don't know if I can handle the job...说实话，我不知道自己是否能做好这份工作。
例：She cannot handle pressure...她承受不了压力。

VERB
（以…方式）应付，处理
If you talk about the way that someone handles a problem or situation, you mention whether or not they are successful in achieving the result they want.
例：I think I would handle a meeting with Mr. Siegel very badly...我觉得和西格尔先生会谈我可能会搞得一团糟。
例：She admitted to herself she didn't know how to handle the problem.她心里承认自己不知道该如何处理这个问题。

VERB
负责（某项工作）
If you handle a particular area of work, you have responsibility for it.
例：She handled travel arrangements for the press corps during the presidential campaign...她负责了总统竞选活动期间新闻报道团的行程安排。
例：The investigation is being handled by Scotland Yard's anti-terrorist branch.调查由伦敦警察厅反恐部门负责。

VERB
使用，操纵（武器、车辆等）;训练，使唤（动物）
When you handle something such as a weapon, vehicle, or animal, you use it or control it, especially by using your hands.
例：I had never handled an automatic.我从未用过自动手枪。

VERB
（车辆等容易）操纵，驾驶
If something such as a vehicle handles well, it is easy to use or control.
例：His ship had handled like a dream!他的船操纵起来非常灵便。

VERB
拿;抓;搬动
When you handle something, you hold it or move it with your hands.
例：Wear rubber gloves when handling cat litter.清理猫粪时要带上橡胶手套。

N-SING
（理解、解决问题的）方法，途径
If you have a handle on a subject or problem, you have a way of approaching it that helps you to understand it or deal with it.
例：When you have got a handle on your anxiety you can begin to control it.一旦了解了自己焦虑的原因，你就开始能控制它了。

PHRASE
大发脾气;勃然大怒
If you fly off the handle, you suddenly and completely lose your temper.
例：He flew off the handle at the slightest thing.他为一点小事就大发脾气。

shriek 
英[ʃri:k] 美[ʃrik]
 
vt.& vi.尖叫；引人注意；发出一种类似尖叫的声音；n.尖叫声；尖锐的声音：一种能使人联想起这种尖叫的声音；
 "

VERB
(因惊讶或疼痛等)尖叫;尖声笑
When someone shrieks, they make a short, very loud cry, for example because they are suddenly surprised, are in pain, or are laughing.
例：She shrieked and leapt from the bed...她尖叫一声从床上跳了起来。
例：Miranda shrieked with laughter.米兰达尖声大笑。

VERB
尖声说出;尖声喊出
If you shriek something, you shout it in a loud, high-pitched voice.
例：'Stop it! Stop it!' shrieked Jane...“停下！停下！”简尖叫道。
例：He was shrieking obscenities and weeping.他一边哭一边尖声咒骂着脏话。

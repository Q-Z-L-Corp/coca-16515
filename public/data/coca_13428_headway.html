headway 
英[ˈhedweɪ] 美[ˈhɛdˌwe]
 
n.前进；进展；航行速度；间隔时间；
 "

PHRASE
前进;取得进展
If you make headway, you progress towards achieving something.
例：He is not disappointed at the failure to make headway towards resolving their differences...他们之间的分歧依然未能解决，他对此并不感到沮丧。
例：There was concern in the city that police were making little headway in the investigation.警方在调查中未能取得什么进展，对此市民有些担心。

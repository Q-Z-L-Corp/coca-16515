V-RECIP-ERG
（使）成一体；（使）联合；（使）统一
If someone unifies different things or parts, or if the things or parts unify, they are brought together to form one thing.
例：A flexible retirement age is being considered by Ministers to unify men's and women's pension rights...大臣们正在考虑一个灵活的退休年龄机制，这样可以把男性和女性的养老金权益统一起来。
例：He said he would seek to unify the Conservative Party and win the next general election...他说他将寻求团结保守党，赢得下届大选。

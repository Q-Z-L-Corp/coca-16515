ADV
有时;不时;间或
You use sometimes to say that something happens on some occasions rather than all the time.
例：During the summer, my skin sometimes gets greasy...夏天我的皮肤有时候会出油。
例：Sometimes I think he dislikes me...有时候，我觉得他不喜欢我。

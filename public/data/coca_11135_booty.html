N-UNCOUNT
赃物；（尤指）战利品
Booty is a collection of valuable things stolen from a place, especially by soldiers after a battle.
例：Troops destroyed the capital and confiscated many works of art as war booty.军队摧毁了这座都城，并把大量艺术品收缴为战利品。

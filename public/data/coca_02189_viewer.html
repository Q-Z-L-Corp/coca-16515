viewer

n.电视观众；观看者，观察者；阅读器；看片机（把幻灯片等放大观看的装置）；
 "

N-COUNT
电视（节目）观众
Viewers are people who watch television, or who are watching a particular programme on television.
例：These programmes are each watched by around 19 million viewers every week.这些电视节目的观众各自都在每周1,900万左右。

N-COUNT
观看者;观察者
A viewer is someone who is looking carefully at a picture or other interesting object.
例：... the relationship between the art object and the viewer.艺术品与其观赏者之间的关系

N-COUNT
（看幻灯片的）观片器，看片器
A viewer is a device shaped like a box with a lens in one side. It is used for looking at transparent colour photographs.
例：This special viewer is simple to use and comes with 70 full-colour slides.这个特别的观片器操作简单，配有70张全彩幻灯片。

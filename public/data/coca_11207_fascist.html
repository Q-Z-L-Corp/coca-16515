fascist 
英['fæʃɪst] 美['fæʃɪst]
 
n.法西斯主义者；法西斯分子；adj.法西斯主义的；法西斯主义者的；法西斯党的；
 "

ADJ
法西斯主义的;法西斯的
You use fascist to describe organizations, ideas, or systems which follow the principles of fascism.
例：...an upsurge of support for extreme rightist, nationalist and fascist organisations.一股支持极右团体、民族主义者以及法西斯组织的狂潮
例：...the threatening nature of fascist ideology.法西斯意识形态的凶险本性

N-COUNT
极端分子;不容异见者
If you refer to someone as a fascist, you are expressing disapproval of the fact that they have extreme views on something, and do not tolerate alternative views.
例：...the so-called health fascists who would meddle in their lives and regulate their calorie intake.那些可能干涉他们生活、规定他们卡路里摄入量，人称健康法西斯的极端者

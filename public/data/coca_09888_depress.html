depress 
英[dɪˈpres] 美[dɪˈprɛs]
 
vt.压下，压低；使沮丧；使萧条；使跌价；
 "

VERB
使抑郁;使沮丧;使消沉
If someone or something depresses you, they make you feel sad and disappointed.
例：I must admit the state of the country depresses me...我必须承认国家的现状让我倍感沮丧。
例：I know he is too optimistic but I don't want to depress him.我知道他太过乐观了，但我不想令他灰心丧气。

VERB
降低;减少
If something depresses prices, wages, or figures, it causes them to become less.
例：The stronger U.S. dollar depressed sales.美元走强导致销售额下降。

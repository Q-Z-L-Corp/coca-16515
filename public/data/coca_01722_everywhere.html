everywhere 
英[ˈevriweə(r)] 美[ˈevriwer]
 
adv.到处，处处，无论什么地方；四下里；漫；匝地；
 "

ADV-INDEF
处处;四处;各处;到处
You use everywhere to refer to a whole area or to all the places in a particular area.
例：Working people everywhere object to paying taxes...各地的工薪族都反对缴税。
例：We went everywhere together...我们一起去各个地方。

ADV-INDEF
所经之地;所到之处
You use everywhere to refer to all the places that someone goes to.
例：Bradley is still accustomed to travelling everywhere in style...布拉德利仍然保持着每次出行必派头十足的习惯。
例：Everywhere he went he was introduced as the current United States Open Champion.他所到之处人们都介绍他是本届美国公开赛的冠军。

ADV-INDEF
（强调范围广）四处，到处
You use everywhere to emphasize that you are talking about a large number of places, or all possible places.
例：I saw her picture everywhere...我到处都可以看见她的照片。
例：I looked everywhere. I couldn't find him.我到处都找了，就是找不到他。

ADV-INDEF
遍地；到处
If you say that someone or something is everywhere, you mean that they are present in a place in very large numbers.
例：There were cartons of cigarettes everywhere...到处都是香烟盒。
例：Clothes were everywhere; hanging out of drawers, strewn across the floor, even spilling from the wastepaper basket.到处都是衣服：挂在抽屉上、扔在地上、甚至堆在废纸篓里。

woe 
英[wəʊ] 美[woʊ]
 
n.悲哀；悲伤；灾难，灾殃；苦恼；int.唉（表示痛苦，悲伤或悔恨）；咳；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
悲痛;悲哀;哀伤
Woe is very great sadness.
例：He listened to my tale of woe...他听我诉说悲伤的故事。
例：All around women wailed their woe or screamed abuse.四下里女人们呼天抢地，高声叫骂。

N-PLURAL
麻烦;问题;困难
You can refer to someone's problems as their woes .
例：He did not tell his relatives and friends about his woes.他没有告诉亲友自己的烦恼。

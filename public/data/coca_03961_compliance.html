N-UNCOUNT
遵守;服从;听从
Compliance with something, for example a law, treaty, or agreement means doing what you are required or expected to do.
例：Inspectors were sent to visit nuclear sites and verify compliance with the treaty...监督员被派去视察核武器基地并核实条约的遵守情况。
例：The company says it is in full compliance with US labor laws...这家公司说自己严格遵守美国的各项劳工法。

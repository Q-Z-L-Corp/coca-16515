ADJ-GRADED
不诚实的;靠不住的
If you say that a person or their behaviour is dishonest, you mean that they are not truthful or honest and that you cannot trust them.
例：You have been dishonest with me...你对我不诚实。
例：It would be dishonest not to present the data as fairly as possible.不把数据尽可能如实公开并非诚信之举。

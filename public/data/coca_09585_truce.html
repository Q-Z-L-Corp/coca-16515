N-COUNT
停战(协定);休战(协定)
A truce is an agreement between two people or groups of people to stop fighting or quarrelling for a short time.
例：The fighting of recent days has given way to an uneasy truce between the two sides...最近几天的交战结束了，双方达成了暂时的休战协议。
例：Let's call a truce.我们别争论了。

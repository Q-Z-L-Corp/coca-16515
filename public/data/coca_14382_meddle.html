VERB
管闲事;干涉;干预
If you say that someone meddles in something, you are criticizing the fact that they try to influence or change it without being asked.
例：Already some people are asking whether scientists have any right to meddle in such matters...已经有人在问科学家是否有权干预这类事情。
例：If only you hadn't felt compelled to meddle.要是你没有觉得非插手不可就好了。

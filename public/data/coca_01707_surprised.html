ADJ-GRADED
惊奇的;惊讶的;觉得奇怪的;感觉意外的
If you are surprised at something, you have a feeling of surprise, because it is unexpected or unusual.
例：This lady was genuinely surprised at what happened to her pet...这位女士对发生在自己宠物身上的事感到非常惊讶。
例：Chang seemed surprised to find the big living-room empty...张看到偌大的客厅里空无一人，似乎很惊讶。

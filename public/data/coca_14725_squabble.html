squabble 
英[ˈskwɒbl] 美[ˈskwɑbl]
 
vi.（为小事而）争论，吵嘴；搅乱（排好的铅字）；n.争吵，口角；
 "

V-RECIP
(为琐事)争吵，起争执
When people squabble, they quarrel about something that is not really important.
例：Mother is devoted to Dad although they squabble all the time...母亲很爱父亲，尽管他们总是为琐事拌嘴。
例：The children were squabbling over the remote-control gadget for the television...孩子们正在为抢夺电视机的遥控器而争吵。

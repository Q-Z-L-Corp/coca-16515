N-VAR
产量；输出量；排出量
Output is used to refer to the amount of something that a person or thing produces.
例：Manual workers need a good breakfast for high-energy output...体力劳动者身体消耗巨大，早餐要丰盛一些。
例：Government statistics show the largest drop in industrial output for ten years.政府统计数据显示这是10年来工业产量最大幅度的滑坡。

N-VAR
（计算机、文字处理器等的）输出信息，输出文件
The output of a computer or word processor is the information that it displays on a screen or prints on paper as a result of a particular program.
例：You run the software, you look at the output, you make modifications.运行该软件，查看输出结果并进行修正。

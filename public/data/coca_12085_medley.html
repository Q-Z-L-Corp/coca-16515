medley 
英[ˈmedli] 美[ˈmɛdli]
 
n.混杂物，混合物；杂录；[体]混合径赛；混战；adj.混合的，混杂的；拼凑的；
 "

N-COUNT
集成曲;集锦;音乐串烧
In music, a medley is a collection of different tunes or songs that are played one after the other as a single piece of music.
例：...a medley of traditional songs.传统歌曲集锦

N-COUNT
混合泳
In sport, a medley is a swimming race in which the four main strokes are used one after the other.
例：Japan won the Men's 200 metres Individual Medley.日本赢得了男子200米个人混合泳比赛。

N-COUNT
杂烩;什锦;混合物
A medley of different foods or other things is a mixture of them.
例：...a medley of four fish in a cream sauce...奶油四喜鱼
例：We communicated in a medley of foreign words and universal gestures.我们边说着外语，边打着通用的手势进行交流。

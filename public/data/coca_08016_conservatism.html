conservatism 
英[kənˈsɜ:vətɪzəm] 美[kənˈsɜrvətɪzəm]
 
n.保守主义；守旧性；〈英〉保守党（的主张]）；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
(政治)保守主义;保守党的政治主张
Conservatism is a political philosophy which believes that if changes need to be made to society, they should be made gradually. You can also refer to the political beliefs of a conservative party in a particular country as Conservatism .
例：...the philosophy of modern Conservatism.现代保守主义哲学

N-UNCOUNT
保守;守旧
Conservatism is unwillingness to accept changes and new ideas.
例：The conservatism of the literary establishment in this country is astounding...这个国家文学界的因循守旧令人震惊。
例：He began his professional life as an accountant, the very model of respectability and conservatism.他开始了会计师的职业生涯，一份典型的体面而保守的工作。

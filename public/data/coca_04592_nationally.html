nationally

 "

ADJ
国家的;全国的
National means relating to the whole of a country or nation rather than to part of it or to other nations.
例：Ruling parties have lost ground in national and local elections.执政党在全国和地区选举中均败北。
例：...major national and international issues.国内和国际重大问题

ADJ
国民的;民族的
National means typical of the people or customs of a particular country or nation.
例：...the national characteristics and history of the country...这个国家的民族特征和历史
例：Baseball is the national pastime.棒球是全民性的休闲运动。

N-COUNT
国民;国人
You can refer to someone who is legally a citizen of a country as a national of that country.
例：...a Sri-Lankan-born British national.出生于斯里兰卡的英国国民

deviate 
英[ˈdi:vieɪt] 美[ˈdiviˌet]
 
v.脱离，越轨，违背，误入歧途；使脱离常轨；n.脱离常轨的人；adj.脱离常轨的；
 "

VERB
偏离;背离
To deviate from something means to start doing something different or not planned, especially in a way that causes problems for others.
例：They stopped you as soon as you deviated from the script...一旦你偏离了剧本的内容，他们就会喊停。
例：He planned his schedule far in advance, and he didn't deviate from it...他提前很早就制订出了自己的进度表，然后从未偏离过它。

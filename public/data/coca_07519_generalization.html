N-VAR
概括；概论；归纳
A generalization is a statement that seems to be true in most situations or for most people, but that may not be completely true in all cases.
例：He is making sweeping generalisations to get his point across...为了让大家领会他的观点，他正在作全面的概括。
例：The evaluation of conduct involves some amount of generalization.对操行的评价会含有一些泛泛之论。

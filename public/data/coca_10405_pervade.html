VERB
贯穿；弥漫；渗透
If something pervades a place or thing, it is a noticeable feature throughout it.
例：The smell of sawdust and glue pervaded the factory...工厂里弥漫着锯屑和胶水的气味。
例：...the corruption that pervades every stratum of the country...渗透到社会各阶层的腐败

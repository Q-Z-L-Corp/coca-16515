merchant 
英[ˈmɜ:tʃənt] 美[ˈmɜrtʃənt]
 
n.商人；批发商；店主；零售商；adj.商人的；商业的；
 "

N-COUNT
(尤指外贸)商人，批发商
A merchant is a person who buys or sells goods in large quantities, especially one who imports and exports them.
例：Any knowledgeable wine merchant would be able to advise you.任何一个懂行的葡萄酒商人都可以给你提出建议。

N-COUNT
店主;商人
A merchant is a person who owns or runs a shop, store, or other business.
例：The family was forced to live on credit from local merchants.这家人不得不靠向当地商人赊账生活。

ADJ
海上货运的;商船的
Merchant seamen or ships are involved in carrying goods for trade.
例：There's been a big reduction in the size of the British merchant fleet in recent years.近些年来英国商船队伍的规模大为缩减。

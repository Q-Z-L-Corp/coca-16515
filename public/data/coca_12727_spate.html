spate 
英[speɪt] 美[spet]
 
n.大量；发洪水，（河流）暴涨；大雨；
 "

N-COUNT
大批，一连串，一阵(尤指令人不快之物)
A spate of things, especially unpleasant things, is a large number of them that happen or appear within a short period of time.
例：...the recent spate of attacks on horses.最近发生的一连串袭马事件
例：...the current spate of scandals.目前流传的大量谣言

PHRASE
(河水)猛涨，泛滥
When a river is in spate it contains a lot more water than usual and is flowing very fast.
例：The Thames was in spate, with flocks of Canada geese speeding downriver.泰晤士河河水暴涨，成群的加拿大雁迅速游向下游。

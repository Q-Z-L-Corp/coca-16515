untreated 
英[ˌʌnˈtri:tɪd] 美[ˌʌnˈtriːtɪd]
 
adj.未经处理的；未加工的；未浸渍的；未得到治疗的；
 "

ADJ
未经治疗的
If an injury or illness is left untreated, it is not given medical treatment.
例：If left untreated the condition may become chronic.这种病如果不治疗可能会发展成慢性病。
例：...the consequences of untreated tuberculosis.肺结核得不到治疗的后果

ADJ
（有害物质）未经处理的
Untreated materials, water, or chemicals are harmful and have not been made safe.
例：...the dumping of nuclear waste and untreated sewage.倾倒核废料和未经处理的污水

ADJ
（天然或原始物质）未经处理的，未加工过的
Untreated materials are in their natural or original state, often before being prepared for use in a particular process.
例：All the bedding is made of simple, untreated cotton...所有床上用品全部用未经处理的天然棉花制成。
例：In its untreated state the carbon fibre material is rather like cloth.原始的碳纤维材料很像布料。

N-VAR
巧合;巧事
A coincidence is when two or more similar or related events occur at the same time by chance and without any planning.
例：Mr. Berry said the timing was a coincidence and that his decision was unrelated to Mr. Roman's departure...贝里先生说这是时间上的巧合，他的决定与罗曼先生的离开没有关系。
例：...a string of amazing coincidences...一连串惊人的巧合

N-UNCOUNT
优柔寡断;无决断力;迟疑不决
If you say that someone suffers from indecision, you mean that they find it very difficult to make decisions.
例：After months of indecision, the government gave the plan the go-ahead on Monday...经过几个月的迟疑不决,政府在周一批准了这项计划。
例：The team has been plagued by indecision and internal divisions.优柔寡断与内部分裂深深困扰着该团队。

ADJ-GRADED
解释性的;说明性的
Explanatory statements or theories are intended to make people understand something by describing it or giving the reasons for it.
例：These statements are accompanied by a series of explanatory notes...这些陈述后面有一系列解释性注释。
例：The concept was later seen to have explanatory power.这一概念其后被认为具有阐释力。

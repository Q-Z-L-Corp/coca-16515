 "

VERB
想象;设想
If you imagine something, you think about it and your mind forms a picture or idea of it.
例：He could not imagine a more peaceful scene...他想象不出比这更祥和的景象。
例：She couldn't imagine living in a place like that...她无法想象住在那样一个地方。

VERB
猜测;猜想;认为
If you imagine that something is the case, you think that it is the case.
例：I imagine you're referring to Jean-Paul Sartre...我猜你说的是让－保罗·萨特。
例：We tend to imagine that the Victorians were very prim and proper...我们往往认为维多利亚时代的人们都一本正经,谨言慎行。

VERB
臆想;幻想
If you imagine something, you think that you have seen, heard, or experienced that thing, although actually you have not.
例：I realised that I must have imagined the whole thing.我意识到大概整件事都是我臆想出来的。

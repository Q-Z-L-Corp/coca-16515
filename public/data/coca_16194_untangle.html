untangle 
英[ˌʌnˈtæŋgl] 美[ʌnˈtæŋɡəl]
 
vt.解开（某物的）结；使不再打结；整顿；解决（纠纷等）；
 "

VERB
解开，解松（绳结）
If you untangle something that is knotted or has become twisted around something, you undo the knots in it or free it.
例：He was found desperately trying to untangle several reels of film.他被撞见拼命想要解开几盘缠在一起的胶卷。
例：...a light, non-sticky mousse which untangles hair and adds brilliant shine.一种能理顺头发、增加其光泽的轻柔、无黏性的摩丝

VERB
排解，解决，处理（混乱或复杂的局面）
f you untangle a confused or complicated situation, you make the different things involved clear, or put the situation right.
例：Lawyers and accountants began trying to untangle the complex affairs of the bank...律师和会计师开始着手梳理这家银行复杂的事务。
例：The problem took three hours to untangle.这个问题花了3个小时才解决。

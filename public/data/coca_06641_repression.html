repression 
英[rɪˈpreʃn] 美[rɪˈprɛʃən]
 
n.压抑；约束；抑制，镇压；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
镇压;压制;控制
Repression is the use of force to restrict and control a society or other group of people.
例：...a society conditioned by violence and repression.受暴力和镇压制约的社会
例：...the repressions of the 1930s.20世纪30年代的镇压

N-UNCOUNT
(情感，尤指性欲的)压抑，控制
Repression of feelings, especially sexual ones, is a person's unwillingness to allow themselves to have natural feelings and desires.
例：...the repression of his feelings about men.他对男人情感的压抑

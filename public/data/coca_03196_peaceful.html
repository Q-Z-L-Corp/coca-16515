peaceful

 "

ADJ-GRADED
和平的；不诉诸战争的
Peaceful activities and situations do not involve war.
例：He has attempted to find a peaceful solution to the Ossetian conflict...他试图寻求和平解决奥赛梯冲突的方案。
例：They emphasised that their equipment was for peaceful and not military purposes.他们强调他们的设备用于和平而非战争目的。

ADJ-GRADED
和平的；非暴力的；未引起骚乱的
Peaceful occasions happen without violence or serious disorder.
例：The farmers staged a noisy but peaceful protest outside the headquarters of the organization...农场主在该组织总部外进行了喧闹而和平的抗议。
例：Despite the violence that preceded the elections, reports say that polling was orderly and peaceful.虽然选举之前有过暴力事件，但有报道称投票过程秩序井然，波澜不惊。

ADJ-GRADED
平和的；爱好和平的
Peaceful people are not violent and try to avoid quarrelling or fighting with other people.
例：...warriors who killed or enslaved the peaceful farmers.杀害或奴役本分农民的武士

ADJ-GRADED
宁静的；平静的；无人打扰的
A peaceful place or time is quiet, calm, and free from disturbance.
例：...a peaceful Georgian house in the heart of Dorset...多塞特腹地一幢宁静的乔治王朝风格的房子
例：Mornings are usually quiet and peaceful in Hueytown.胡伊镇的早晨通常一片静谧祥和。

ADJ-GRADED
安详的；平和的；平静的
Someone who feels or looks peaceful feels or looks calm and free from worry.
例：I feel relaxed and peaceful...我感到放松而平和。
例：The animals look peaceful and happy.那些动物看起来气定神闲，怡然自得。

VERB
使商业化;以…牟利
If something is commercialized, it is used or changed in such a way that it makes money or profits, often in a way that people disapprove of.
例：It seems such a pity that a distinguished and honored name should be commercialized in such a manner...一个著名的、令人尊敬的名字被人用得如此商业化，似乎太让人遗憾了。
例：Federal agencies should commercialize research.各个联邦机构应该把研究商业化。

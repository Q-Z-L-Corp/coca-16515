discontinue 
英[ˌdɪskənˈtɪnju:] 美[ˌdɪskənˈtɪnju]
 
vt.& vi.（使）终止，中断，中止；
 "

VERB
终止;中断
If you discontinue something that you have been doing regularly, you stop doing it.
例：Do not discontinue the treatment without consulting your doctor.未经医嘱不得中断治疗。

VERB
停止，终止(生产)
If a product is discontinued, the manufacturer stops making it.
例：The Leica M2 was discontinued in 1967...徕卡 M2 型相机于 1967 年停产。
例：They have a huge selection of discontinued cookers.他们有大量绝版的精选炉灶。

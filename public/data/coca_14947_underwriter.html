underwriter 
英[ˈʌndəraɪtə(r)] 美[ˈʌndɚˌraɪtɚ]
 
n.保险业者；保险公司，保险商；承诺支付者；担保人；
 "

N-COUNT
保险商；承销商
An underwriter is someone whose job involves agreeing to provide money for a particular activity or to pay for any losses that are made.
例：If the market will not buy the shares, the underwriter buys them...如果市场上没有人买这些股票，承销商就会买下来。
例：Global 2000 became an underwriter for small farmers.“全球2000”成为了小农场主的承保公司。

N-COUNT
核保师（对投保项目进行风险评估并决定保险费率）
An underwriter is someone whose job is to judge the risks involved in certain activities and decide how much to charge for insurance.

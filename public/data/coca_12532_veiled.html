veiled 
英[veɪld] 美[veld]
 
adj.隐藏的；掩饰的；蒙着面纱的；声音哑的；v.veil的过去式和过去分词；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
间接表示的;含蓄的;不明言的
A veiled comment is expressed in a disguised form rather than directly and openly.
例：He made only a veiled reference to international concerns over human rights issues...他只是含蓄地提到了国际社会对人权问题的关注。
例：This last clause is a thinly-veiled threat to those who might choose to ignore the decree.这最后一项条款明显是对那些可能会无视法令之人的威慑。

ADJ
蒙面纱的;戴面罩的
A woman or girl who is veiled is wearing a veil.
例：A veiled woman gave me a kindly smile.一位戴面纱的女士朝我友好地微笑。

tile 
英[taɪl] 美[taɪl]
 
n.瓦片，瓷砖；空心砖；麻将牌；vt.用瓦片、瓷砖等覆盖；
 "

N-VAR
瓷砖;墙砖;地砖
Tiles are flat, square pieces of baked clay, carpet, cork, or other substance, which are fixed as a covering onto a floor or wall.
例：Amy's shoes squeaked on the tiles as she walked down the corridor...埃米走过走廊时，鞋子踩在地砖上嘎吱作响。
例：The cabins had linoleum tile floors.船舱地上铺着油毡。

N-VAR
瓦片
Tiles are flat pieces of baked clay which are used for covering roofs.
例：...a fine building, with a neat little porch and ornamental tiles on the roof.一幢精致的建筑，有齐整的小巧柱廊，屋顶上覆着装饰瓦

VERB
铺瓦于;贴地砖于
When someone tiles a surface such as a roof or floor, they cover it with tiles.
例：He wants to tile the bathroom...他想在浴室里铺瓷砖。
例：The terracotta tiled floor gives the place a wonderfully homely character.陶瓦地板使这个地方给人一种非常舒适温馨的感觉。

PHRASE
纵情玩乐，花天酒地(到深夜)
If someone has a night on the tiles or is out on the tiles, they go out in the evening, for example to a bar or a club, and do not return home until very late.
例：Charlotte was dressed for a night on the tiles...夏洛特为彻夜狂欢精心打扮了一番。
例：I used to be out on the tiles every night.我曾每晚都在外面花天酒地。

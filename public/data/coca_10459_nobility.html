nobility 
英[nəʊˈbɪləti] 美[noʊˈbɪləti]
 
n.贵族阶级；高贵的身分[出身]；高尚，崇高；庄严，雄伟；
 "

N-SING-COLL
贵族(阶层)
The nobility of a society are all the people who have titles and belong to a high social class.
例：They married into the nobility and entered the highest ranks of state administration...他们同贵族阶层联姻，进入了国家最高行政层。
例：Despite its lack of formal power the nobility was not powerless.尽管没有正式的权力，贵族阶层并非没有权势。

N-UNCOUNT
高尚的品质;高贵的举止
A person's nobility is their noble character and behaviour.
例：...his nobility of character, and his devotion to his country.他的高尚品格和对祖国的忠诚

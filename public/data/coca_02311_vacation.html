vacation 
英[vəˈkeɪʃn] 美[veˈkeʃən, və-]
 
n.假期，休假；[古] 空出，撤出，辞去；vi.度假；
 "

N-COUNT
（大学或美国大中小学的）假期
A vacation is a period of the year when universities and colleges, and in the United States also schools, are officially closed.
例：During his summer vacation he visited Russia...暑假期间他去俄罗斯了。
例：Did you have a lot of reading during the vacation?放假期间你读了很多书吗？

N-COUNT
假日;休假
A vacation is a period of time during which you relax and enjoy yourself away from home.
例：They planned a late summer vacation in Europe...他们打算夏末去欧洲度假。
例：We went on vacation to Puerto Rico.我们去了波多黎各度假。

N-UNCOUNT
休息日;假期
If you have a particular number of days' or weeks' vacation, you do not have to go to work for that number of days or weeks.
例：The French get five to six weeks' vacation a year.法国人每年有5到6周的假期。

VERB
（离家在某地）度假
If you are vacationing in a place away from home, you are on vacation there.
例：Myles vacationed in Jamaica...迈尔斯去了牙买加度假。
例：He was vacationing and couldn't be reached for comment.他当时正在度假，无法联系到他征求意见。

anyone 
英[ˈeniwʌn] 美[ˈɛniˌwʌn, -wən]
 
pron.任何人；任何一个；谁；
 "

PRON-INDEF
（用于否定句）任何人
You use anyone or anybody in statements with negative meaning to indicate in a general way that nobody is present or involved in an action.
例：I won't tell anyone I saw you here...我不会告诉任何人我在这儿见过你。
例：You needn't talk to anyone if you don't want to...不想说话的时候，你可以不说。

PRON-INDEF
（用于疑问句或条件从句）任何人
You use anyone or anybody in questions and conditional clauses to ask or talk about whether someone is present or doing something.
例：Why would anyone want that job?...为什么会有人想要那份工作呢？
例：How can anyone look sad at an occasion like this?...在这样的场合怎么会有人显得忧伤呢？

PRON-INDEF
（用于描述所谈论的人的词语前）…人
You use anyone or anybody before words which indicate the kind of person you are talking about.
例：I always had been the person who achieved things before anyone else at my age...我过去总是比同龄人要捷足先登。
例：It's not a job for anyone who is slow with numbers...这份工作不适合不擅长算术的人。

PRON-INDEF
（用于强调）任何人，随便哪个人
You use anyone or anybody to refer to a person when you are emphasizing that it could be any person out of a very large number of people.
例：Anyone could be doing what I'mdoing...我在做的事，谁都可能正在做。
例：Al Smith could make anybody laugh.阿尔·史密斯能把所有人逗乐。

PHRASE
重要人物;有影响的人物
You use anyone who is anyone and anybody who is anybody to refer to people who are important or influential.
例：It seems anyone who's anyone in business is going to the conference.似乎商界所有重要人物都将参加那个会议。

patch 
英[pætʃ] 美[pætʃ]
 
n.补丁，补片；眼罩；斑点；小块；vt.修补，拼凑；暂时遮掩一下；修理，平息（吵架等）；用美人斑装饰（脸）；vi.打补丁；
 "

N-COUNT
（表面上）与周围不同的部分
A patch on a surface is a part of it which is different in appearance from the area around it.
例：...the bald patch on the top of his head...他头顶上的秃斑
例：There was a small patch of blue in the grey clouds.灰色的云团中透出一小片蓝天。

N-COUNT
（种有某种植物或农作物的）小块土地
A patch of land is a small area of land where a particular plant or crop grows.
例：...a patch of land covered in forest.一小块林地
例：...the little vegetable patch in her backyard.她家后院中的一小块菜地

N-COUNT
补丁；补片
A patch is a piece of material which you use to cover a hole in something.
例：...jackets with patches on the elbows.肘部打着补丁的夹克
例：...trying to fix the flat tire by putting a patch on it.想打上一块补丁修好漏气的轮胎

N-COUNT
（保护受伤眼睛的）眼罩
A patch is a small piece of material which you wear to cover an injured eye.
例：She went to the hospital and found him lying down with a patch over his eye.她去了医院，发现他躺着，一只眼睛戴着眼罩。

VERB
补缀；修补
If you patch something that has a hole in it, you mend it by fastening a patch over the hole.
例：He and Walker patched the barn roof...他和沃克补好了谷仓顶。
例：One of the mechanics took off the damaged tyre, and took it back to the station to be patched.其中一个机修工卸下坏轮胎，把它带回维修站修补。

N-COUNT
补丁
A patch is a piece of computer program code written as a temporary solution for dealing with a virus in computer software and distributed by the makers of the original program.
例：Older machines will need a software patch to be loaded to correct the date.老些的机器需要下载软件补丁来修改日期。

PHRASE
一段艰难时期
If you have or go through a bad patch or a rough patch, you have a lot of problems for a time.
例：His marriage was going through a bad patch...他的婚姻当时麻烦不断。
例：The company I work for went through a rough patch.我所在的公司经历了一段困难时期。

PHRASE
比不上；远不及
If you say that someone or something is not a patch on another person or thing, you mean that they are not as good as that person or thing.
例：He's not a patch on the rest of the Cabinet...他远比不上内阁的其他成员。
例：Handsome, she thought, but not a patch on Alex.是帅，她想，不过比亚历克斯差远了。

ADJ-GRADED
妖娆的；性感的
If you describe a woman or something about her as luscious, you mean that you find her or this thing sexually attractive.
例：...a luscious young blonde...性感的金发女郎
例：What I like most about Gabby is her luscious lips!我最喜欢的是盖比那性感饱满的双唇！

ADJ-GRADED
甘美多汁的；美味的
Luscious food is juicy and very good to eat.
例：...a small apricot tree which bore luscious fruit.一棵结的果子甘甜多汁的小杏树

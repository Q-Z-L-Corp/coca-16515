emit 
英[iˈmɪt] 美[ɪˈmɪt]
 
vt.发出；发射；颁布；发表；
 "

VERB
发出，排放，散发（热、光、气或气味）
If something emits heat, light, gas, or a smell, it produces it and sends it out by means of a physical or chemical process.
例：The new device emits a powerful circular column of light.新设备发出明亮的圆形光柱。
例：...the amount of carbon dioxide emitted.二氧化碳排放量

VERB
发出（声音或噪音）
To emit a sound or noise means to produce it.
例：Polly blinked and emitted a long, low whistle.波莉眨了眨眼，吹了一声低沉而悠长的口哨。

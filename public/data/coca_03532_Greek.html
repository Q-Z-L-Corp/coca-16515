ADJ
希腊的
Greek means belonging or relating to Greece.
例：In the Greek general election, the New Democracy party has claimed victory.新民主党在希腊大选中获胜。
例：...the Greek army.希腊军队

N-COUNT
希腊人
A Greek is a person who comes from Greece.
例：He had looked through the house for the two Greeks.他搜遍了整个房子想要找到那两个希腊人。

N-UNCOUNT
希腊语
Greek is the language used in Greece.
例：I had to learn Greek.我不得不学习希腊语。

N-UNCOUNT
古希腊语
Greek or Ancient Greek was the language used in Greece in ancient times.
例：Teachers warn today that Latin, Greek and other classics will be squeezed out of school timetables.老师们今天发出警告说，拉丁语、古希腊语和其他古典文学将被挤出学校的课程表。

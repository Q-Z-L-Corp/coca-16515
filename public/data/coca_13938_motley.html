ADJ-GRADED
杂七杂八的;混杂的
You can describe a group of things as a motley collection if you think they seem strange together because they are all very different.
例：...a motley collection of vans, old buses, cattle-trucks, and even a fire engine...杂七杂八的一堆车，有运货车、旧公共汽车、运牛车，甚至救火车
例：The volunteers seem a motley crew.志愿者里似乎什么人都有。

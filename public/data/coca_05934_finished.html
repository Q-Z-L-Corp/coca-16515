finished 
英[ˈfɪnɪʃt] 美[ˈfɪnɪʃt]
 
adj.完成了的；技术高超的；精湛的；〈俚〉死了的，完蛋了的；v.结束，完成( finish的过去式和过去分词)；
 "

ADJ
不再做(…)的;(同…)不再打交道的;(对…)不再感兴趣的
Someone who is finished with something is no longer doing it or dealing with it or is no longer interested in it.
例：One suspects he will be finished with boxing.人们猜想他不会再打拳了。

ADJ
不复存在的;不再发生的
Something that is finished no longer exists or is no longer happening.
例：I go back on the dole when the shooting season's finished.狩猎季节过后，我又重靠领取救济金过活。

ADJ
垮台的;完蛋的;失势的;失效的
Someone or something that is finished is no longer important, powerful, or effective.
例：Her power over me is finished...她再也不能控制我了。
例：He confessed: 'I thought I was finished.'他承认道：“我以为自己已经完了。”

ADJ
(以某种方式的外观或装饰)最后呈现的
Something that is finished or finished off in a particular way has been given a particular appearance or decoration.
例：The dining room is finished in deep red...餐厅装饰成了深红色。
例：Each dress is beautifully finished off with piped seams and fitted underskirts.每件连衣裙最后线缝都滚了边，并且加上了合身的衬裙，看起来非常漂亮。

analyse 
英[ˈænəlaɪz] 美[ˈænəˌlaɪz]
 
vt.分析；分解；细察；na.“analyze”的变体；
 "

VERB
细察；分析
If you analyse something, you consider it carefully or use statistical methods in order to fully understand it.
例：McCarthy was asked to analyse the data from the first phase of trials of the vaccine...麦卡锡被要求对第一阶段疫苗试验的数据作出分析。
例：This book teaches you how to analyse what is causing the stress in your life.这本书教你分析生活压力形成的原因。

VERB
分析；分解；化验
If you analyse something, you examine it using scientific methods in order to find out what it consists of.
例：We haven't had time to analyse those samples yet...我们还没有时间分析那些样本。
例：They had their tablets analysed to find out whether they were getting the real drug or not.他们将药片拿去化验以查明他们得到的是否是真药。

travel 
英[ˈtrævl] 美[ˈtrævəl]
 
n.旅行；进行；移动；漫游；vi.旅行；传送；前进，行进；[篮球]走步；vt.经过，通过；游历；
 "

VERB
(常指长途)旅行，行进
If you travel, you go from one place to another, often to a place that is far away.
例：You had better travel to Helsinki tomorrow...你最好明天赶赴赫尔辛基。
例：Granny travelled down by train…奶奶是乘火车过来的。

N-UNCOUNT
(长途)旅行
Travel is the activity of travelling.
例：Information on travel in New Zealand is available at the hotel…新西兰的旅行信息可以在宾馆获取。
例：He detested air travel.他厌恶乘飞机旅行。

VERB
周游;走遍;在…游历
If you travel the world, the country, or the area, you go to many different places in the world or in a particular country or area.
例：Dr Ryan travelled the world gathering material for his book...瑞安博士周游世界为他的书搜集资料。
例：He has had to travel the country in search of work.他不得不走遍全国寻找工作。

VERB
(光、声音)传送，传播
When light or sound from one place reaches another, you say that it travels to the other place.
例：When sound travels through water, strange things can happen...当声音在水中传播时，可能会发生奇怪的现象。
例：Light travels at around 300,000,000 metres per second.光以每秒约 30 万公里的速度传播。

VERB
(消息)传播，流传
When news becomes known by people in different places, you can say that it travels to them.
例：News of his work traveled all the way to Asia...有关他研究成果的消息一路传到了亚洲。
例：Seems like news travels pretty fast around here.这里似乎消息传播得很快。

N-PLURAL
漫游;游历
Someone's travels are the journeys that they make to places a long way from their home.
例：He also collects things for the house on his travels abroad.他在国外旅游时也为家宅搜集物品作为摆设。

PHRASE
轻装旅行
If you travel light, you travel without taking much luggage.

PHRASE
(食品等货物)宜于长途运输，经得起长途运输
If goods such as food products travel well, they can be transported a long way without being damaged or their quality being spoiled.
例：Ripe fruit does not travel well, but unripe fruit can be transported worldwide.熟透的水果不宜长途运输，但未成熟的水果却可以运往全世界。

PHRASE
(思想、方法或样式)广为流传，盛行各地
If you say that an idea, a method, or a style travels well, you mean that it can be appreciated or used by people in several different countries, and not just in the country where it began.
例：That brand of humour generally travels well.那种风格的幽默通常在各地都喜闻乐见。

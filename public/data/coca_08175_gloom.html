gloom 
英[glu:m] 美[ɡlum]
 
n.昏暗，阴暗；忧郁；vi.变黑，变暗；忧郁，沮丧；vt.使变黑，变暗；使忧伤，沮丧；
 "

N-SING
阴暗；昏暗
The gloom is a state of near darkness.
例：...the gloom of a foggy November morning...11月份一个雾蒙蒙的早晨，天空昏暗阴沉
例：I was peering about me in the gloom.我在暗处端详着自己。

N-UNCOUNT
忧郁；愁闷；无望
Gloom is a feeling of sadness and lack of hope.
例：...the deepening gloom over the economy.对经济的忧虑日益加重

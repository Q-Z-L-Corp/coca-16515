ADJ
与经验有关的;由经验得来的
Experiential means relating to or resulting from experience.
例：Learning has got to be active and experiential.学习必须积极，而且要汲取经验。
例：...the rediscovery of the experiential path of religious truth.重新发现从经验中体会宗教真理的方法

kernel 
英[ˈkɜ:nl] 美[ˈkɜrnl]
 
n.核心；要点；谷粒；核；v.把…包在核内；
 "

N-COUNT
（坚果的）仁，核
The kernel of a nut is the part that is inside the shell.

N-COUNT
核心；中心；要点
The kernel of something is the central and most important part of it.
例：The kernel of that message was that peace must not be a source of advantage or disadvantage for anyone.那一信息的核心是和平绝不应该成为对任何人有利或不利的根源。

N-COUNT
一小部分
A kernel of something is a small element of it.
例：For all I know, there may be a kernel of truth in what he says.据我所知，他的话可能有一点儿道理。

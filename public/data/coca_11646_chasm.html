chasm 
英[ˈkæzəm] 美[ˈkæzəm]
 
n.峡谷；裂缝，断层；裂口；（感情等的）分歧；
 "

N-COUNT
石罅;地缝;冰缝
A chasm is a very deep crack in rock, earth, or ice.

N-COUNT
(事物或团体间的)显著差距，巨大分歧
If you say that there is a chasm between two things or between two groups of people, you mean that there is a very large difference between them.
例：...the chasm that divides the worlds of university and industry.将大学和企业两个世界分隔开的鸿沟
例：...the chasm between rich and poor in America.美国显著的贫富差距

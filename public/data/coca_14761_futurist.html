英['fju:tʃərɪst] 美['fjutʃərɪst]
 
n.未来主义者，未来派艺术家；
 "

N-COUNT
未来主义者;未来派艺术家;未来派作家
Futurists were artists and writers who were followers of futurism.

N-COUNT
预测未来的人;未来学家
A futurist is someone who makes predictions about what is going to happen, on the basis of facts about what is happening now.
例：Futurists agree that these trends will grow as telecommunications networks become cheaper and more efficient.未来学家一致认为，随着电信网络资费的降低和效率的提高，这些趋势将会越来越明显。

VERB
核实;查证;查清
If you verify something, you check that it is true by careful examination or investigation.
例：I verified the source from which I had that information...我核实了我所获消息的来源。
例：A clerk simply verifies that the payment and invoice amount match.职员只是核实付款和发票上的数额是否一致。

VERB
证实;证明
If you verify something, you state or confirm that it is true.
例：The government has not verified any of those reports...政府还没有证实那些报告中的任何一个。
例：I can verify that it takes about thirty seconds.我能证明，这大约要用30秒的时间。

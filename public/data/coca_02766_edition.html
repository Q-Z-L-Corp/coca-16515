edition 
英[ɪˈdɪʃn] 美[ɪˈdɪʃən]
 
n.版次，版本；（报纸、杂志的）一份；（广播、电视节目的）一期；（书、报、杂志等的）一版印刷总数；
 "

N-COUNT
版;版本
An edition is a particular version of a book, magazine, or newspaper that is printed at one time.
例：A paperback edition is now available at bookshops.平装本现在在书店可以买到。

N-COUNT
(书或报纸的)一次印刷量，版次
An edition is the total number of copies of a particular book or newspaper that are printed at one time.
例：The second edition was published only in America.第二版只在美国出版。

N-COUNT
（电视或广播节目的）一集，一期
An edition is a single television or radio programme that is one of a series about a particular subject.
例：They appeared on an edition of BBC2's Arena.他们在英国广播公司二台《竞技场》的一期节目中亮相。

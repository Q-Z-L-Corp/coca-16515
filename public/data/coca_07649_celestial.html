celestial 
英[səˈlestiəl] 美[səˈlestʃl]
 
adj.天的，天空的；n.天人，神仙；（指封建时代的）中国人，天朝之人；
 "

ADJ
天体的;天空的;天的
Celestial is used to describe things relating to heaven or to the sky.
例：Gravity governs the motions of celestial bodies.万有引力控制着天体的运动。
例：In the process of their careful watching and recording the celestial movements the Chinese provided valuable and interesting information for succeeding generations.古代中国人在仔细观察、认真记录天体运动的过程里，为后代留下了宝贵而又有趣的资料。

ADJ-GRADED
了不起的;美妙的;奇妙的
If you describe something as celestial, you mean that it is wonderful.
例：...a chocolate cake with an apricot filling and celestial effect on the taste buds.味道妙不可言的杏仁夹层巧克力蛋糕

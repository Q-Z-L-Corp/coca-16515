ADJ-GRADED
贵族的；贵族式的
Aristocratic means belonging to or typical of the aristocracy.
例：...a wealthy, aristocratic family...富有的贵族家庭
例：He laughed it off with aristocratic indifference.他带着贵族式的漠然对其一笑置之。

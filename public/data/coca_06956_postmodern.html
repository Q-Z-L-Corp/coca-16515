ADJ
后现代主义的；后现代派的
Post-modern is used to describe something or someone that is influenced by post-modernism.
例：Ripe with in-jokes, self-references, and post-modern metaphor, the movie questions a too-civilized world.这部电影驾轻就熟地借助圈内笑话、自我指认和后现代主义的隐喻，对过度文明的世界提出了质疑。
例：...post-modern architecture.后现代主义建筑

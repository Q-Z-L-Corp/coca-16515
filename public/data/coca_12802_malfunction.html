VERB
（机器）失灵，出故障；（人体器官）机能失常，出现功能障碍
If a machine or part of the body malfunctions, it fails to work properly.
例：The radiation can damage microprocessors and computer memories, causing them to malfunction...辐射会损坏微处理器和电脑内存，使之失灵。
例：Early physicians removed the malfunctioning organs.早年间的医生会把机能失常的器官摘除掉。

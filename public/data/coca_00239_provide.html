provide 
英[prəˈvaɪd] 美[prəˈvaɪd]
 
vt.& vi.提供，供给，供应；vt.规定；提供(+for)；装备；预备；vi.抚养，赡养(+for)；做准备；预约(for 或 against)；
 "

VERB
供给；提供；给予
If you provide something that someone needs or wants, or if you provide them with it, you give it to them or make it available to them.
例：I'll be glad to provide a copy of this...我很愿意提供它的一个副本。
例：They would not provide any details...他们不愿提供任何细节。

VERB
（法律或协议）规定
If a law or agreement provides that something will happen, it states that it will happen.
例：The treaty provides that, by the end of the century, the United States must have removed its bases...条约规定，到本世纪末，美国必须撤除其基地。
例：The Act provides that only the parents of a child have a responsibility for that child's financial support.法令规定只有孩子的父母才有责任为孩子提供经济支持。

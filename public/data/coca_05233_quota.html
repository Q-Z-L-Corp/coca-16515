quota 
英[ˈkwəʊtə] 美[ˈkwoʊtə]
 
n.（正式限定的）定量，定额；配额；指标；
 "

N-COUNT
定量;定额;配额
A quota is the limited number or quantity of something which is officially allowed.
例：The quota of four tickets per person had been reduced to two.每人可购买的票的限额已经由四张降至两张。

N-COUNT
(给予某团体的)限额，配额
A quota is a fixed maximum or minimum proportion of people from a particular group who are allowed to do something, such as come and live in a country or work for the government.
例：The bill would force employers to adopt a quota system when recruiting workers.该法案将迫使雇主在招聘工人时采取限额制度。

N-COUNT
配额;份额;指标
Someone's quota of something is their expected or deserved share of it.
例：They have the usual quota of human weaknesses, no doubt.毫无疑问，他们身上也有人所共有的弱点。

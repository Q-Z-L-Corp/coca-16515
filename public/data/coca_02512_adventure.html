adventure 
英[ədˈventʃə(r)] 美[ædˈvɛntʃɚ]
 
n.冒险活动；冒险经历；奇遇；
 "

N-COUNT
冒险；冒险经历；奇遇
If someone has an adventure, they become involved in an unusual, exciting, and rather dangerous journey or series of events.
例：I set off for a new adventure in the United States on the first day of the new year.新年第一天，我在美国开始了一次新的冒险。

N-UNCOUNT
冒险的刺激；大胆的开拓；勇敢的闯荡
Adventure is excitement and willingness to do new, unusual, or rather dangerous things.
例：Their cultural backgrounds gave them a spirit of adventure.他们的文化背景赋予了他们一种冒险精神。
例：...a feeling of adventure and excitement.惊险刺激的感觉

VERB
去（某地）探险
If you adventure somewhere, you go somewhere new, unusual, and exciting.
例：The group has adventured as far as the Austrian alps.该队伍的探险之旅远至奥地利的阿尔卑斯山。

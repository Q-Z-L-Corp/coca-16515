implausible 
英[ɪmˈplɔ:zəbl] 美[ɪmˈplɔzəbəl]
 
adj.难以置信的，似乎不合情理的，不太可能的；
 "

ADJ-GRADED
难以置信的;不像真实的
If you describe something as implausible, you believe that it is unlikely to be true.
例：I had to admit it sounded like an implausible excuse...我得承认那听起来像是个难以叫人相信的借口。
例：It seems implausible that the projects would have gone ahead without her backing.这些项目没有得到她的支持就开始进行了，这似乎不合情理。

sensibility 
英[ˌsensəˈbɪləti] 美[ˌsɛnsəˈbɪlɪti]
 
n.情感；敏感性，灵敏度；感觉，感性；感光性，感光度；
 "

N-UNCOUNT
感受力;感悟力;鉴赏力
Sensibility is the ability to experience deep feelings.
例：Everything he writes demonstrates the depth of his sensibility.他笔下的一切都显示出他深深的感悟。
例：...a man of sensibility.懂感情的男子

N-VAR
敏感;善感;感情
Someone's sensibility is their tendency to be influenced or offended by things.
例：He was unable to control his sensibility...他不能控制自己的过分敏感。
例：The challenge offended their sensibilities.这一质疑伤害了他们的感情。
